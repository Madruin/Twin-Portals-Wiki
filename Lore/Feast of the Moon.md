# Feast of the Moon

A holiday celebrated in [[Waterdeep]] featuring a parade through the streets. During the Feast of the Moon, [[SWOJ]] pursued [[Willifort Crowell]] and a spined devil carrying [[The Stone of Golorr]] in a cart chase down the [[High Road]], leading to their arrest by [[Captain Hyustus Stagget]].

## Key Appearances

- **[[S01E13 - Episode Thirteen|S01E13]]:** The [[High Road]] chase during the Feast of the Moon parade; [[SWOJ]] is arrested and taken to the [[Docks Ward Guardhouse]]
