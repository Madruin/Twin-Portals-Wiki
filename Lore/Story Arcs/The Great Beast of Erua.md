# The Great Beast of Erua

**Seasons:** 2
**Episodes:** [[S02E01 - Episode Thirty One (S02E01)|S02E01]] - [[S02E05 - Episode Thirty Five|S02E05]]
**Status:** Resolved

## Summary

The Great Beast of Erua was a colossal undead creature -- at least a hundred feet tall with four castle-like legs and exposed bones -- that lurched through the [[Shadowfell]]. Its scales were studded with hundreds of wish stones stolen from the [[Brighanox]], tiny mouse-like creatures who collected crystallized unfulfilled wishes. The wish stones fueled the Beast's movement, and a fortress built atop its spine served as a base of operations for one of [[Manshoon]]'s clones.

SWOJ's pursuit of Manshoon led them from the [[Feywild]] through [[The Way Inn]] on the border between planes and into the Shadowfell. There they encountered the [[Brighanox]], [[Shadar-kai]] servants of the [[Raven Queen]], and the horrifying [[Shadow Way Inn]] -- a replica of the real Way Inn staffed by podlings, plant-based copies of captured people. After discovering that their [[Jolly Gents]] companions [[Antidiluvian Jake]] and [[Ann Jane Flip-Flap]] had been replaced by podling duplicates, SWOJ fought through the plant creatures and their hairless cat swarms, then rode giant scarab beetles named Ga and Kra up the Beast's legs.

Inside the Beast's ribcage, the party found a laboratory, a teleportation circle, and pods containing the real captives -- including the real Jake, Ann Jane, [[Volothamp Geddarm|Volo]], and about fifteen others. [[Radissera]], a gnome inventor, revealed that his stolen invention -- the [[Dynamo of Possibility]] -- was powering both the Beast and the podling creation. SWOJ ascended through the Beast's trachea to its skull, where the malfunctioning Dynamo pulled them into a pocket dimension containing a miniature world. There they met [[Fetch]], a nine-year-old boy who was actually a fragment of Manshoon's soul representing his childhood innocence, and defeated the [[Body Taker]], a plant-based Manshoon clone who controlled the podlings.

The arc concluded when [[Rowan Crooks]] and the Jolly Gents arrived, capturing Fetch's soul in a crystal shard despite SWOJ's protests. [[Antidiluvian Jake]] teleported everyone back to [[Waterdeep]], where the party made the devastating discovery that three years had passed during their time in the Shadowfell -- the calendar now read 1496 DR instead of 1493.

## Resolution

The Body Taker clone was destroyed and the Dynamo of Possibility rendered inert. All captives were freed from the Beast's interior. Fetch merged with Teriador's soul and transported everyone out of the collapsing Dynamo, only to have Rowan Crooks capture his soul in a crystal shard. The return to Waterdeep revealed a three-year time skip (1493 to 1496 DR), dramatically changing the status quo: [[Taga]] (Scoot's mother) was bedridden, [[Nat]] and [[Jenks]] had grown into teenagers, Waterdeep was at war with [[Neverwinter]], and [[Renaer Neverember]] had gone missing.
