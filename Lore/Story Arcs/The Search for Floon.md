# The Search for Floon

**Seasons:** 1
**Episodes:** [[S01E01 - Episode One|S01E01]] - [[S01E03 - Episode Three|S01E03]]
**Status:** Resolved

## Summary

The campaign's inciting incident began when the famous chronicler [[Volothamp Geddarm|Volo]] hired the newly-met adventurers -- [[Scoot Sparkles]], [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]], [[Ylka Gralhund]], and [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] -- to find his missing friend [[Floon Blagmaar]]. Floon had been kidnapped after leaving the [[Skewered Dragon]] tavern, caught up in the escalating gang war between the [[Zhentarim]] and the [[Xanathar Guild]].

The party traced Floon to a [[Zhentarim]] warehouse on [[Candle Lane]], where they discovered the aftermath of a violent clash between the two factions. They fought kenku agents (including the memorable [[Pinker]]) and rescued [[Renaer Neverember]], who revealed that both factions were searching for the [[Stone of Golorr]] -- an artifact needed to locate a massive hoard of embezzled gold hidden by his father, [[Dagult Neverember]]. The kenku had taken Floon, mistaking him for Renaer.

Following the kenku's trail into the sewers beneath [[Waterdeep]], SWOJ confronted a mind flayer torturing Floon and defeated an intellect devourer. This encounter transported the party through a green portal into a psychic version of Waterdeep, where they met [[Zardos Zord]], explored [[Blue Alley]], and recovered a crystal unicorn before being returned to the Splintered Stair where Floon and Renaer awaited them. The search for Floon established the core relationships and mysteries that would drive the entire first season.

- [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] — Druid whose mysterious portal arrival in the City of the Dead foreshadowed her deeper story
- [[Volothamp Geddarm|Volo]] — Hired the party and set the adventure in motion
- [[Floon Blagmaar]] — The missing friend whose kidnapping drove the quest
- [[Renaer Neverember]] — Rescued alongside Floon; revealed the Stone of Golorr conspiracy

## Resolution

Floon was rescued from the sewers and reunited with Volo. As payment, Volo gifted the party the deed to [[Trollskull Manor]], launching the next phase of their adventures. The search also revealed the existence of the [[Stone of Golorr]] and [[Dagult Neverember]]'s hidden gold, setting up the central conflict of Season 1.
