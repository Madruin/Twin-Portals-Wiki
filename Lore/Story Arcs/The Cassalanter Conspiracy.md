# The Cassalanter Conspiracy

**Seasons:** 1-2
**Episodes:** [[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]] - [[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]] (primary); reappears in Season 2
**Status:** Resolved

## Summary

The Cassalanter family -- [[Victoro Cassalanter|Victoro]] and [[Amalia Cassalanter|Amalia]] -- were among Waterdeep's most powerful nobles, hiding a terrible secret: they had made a pact with [[Asmodeus]], Lord of Nessus, trading their children's souls for wealth and power. Their eldest son [[Osvaldo Cassalanter|Osvaldo]] had already been transformed into a chain devil, imprisoned in their attic. The pact demanded the souls of their remaining children, [[Terenzio Cassalanter|Terenzio]] and [[Elzerina Cassalanter|Elzerina]], unless a massive ransom of gold was paid.

SWOJ first encountered the Cassalanters through seemingly benign connections: a letter demanding 500% interest from [[House Wavesilver]], an invitation to a soiree, and [[Blossom Zuber]]'s confession that a tiefling from Cassalanter Villa gave her a devil ring that summoned scarecrows. At the Cassalanter party, Amalia hired SWOJ to find [[Dagult Neverember]]'s gold for 150 gold per day. That same night, [[Zylka]] (Ylka's doppelganger) crashed the ballroom with cultists wearing golden masks, and SWOJ discovered Osvaldo chained in the attic.

The conspiracy deepened as SWOJ learned the Cassalanters were allied with the [[Cult Of Asmodeus]], had connections to [[House Gralhund]], and possessed the third Eye of the [[The Stone of Golorr]]. Their majordomo [[Willifort Crowell]] was revealed to be a doppelganger agent. The climax came during the Founder's Day feast at Cassalanter Villa, where Victoro attempted a soul harvest using a charming cane while summoning devils. SWOJ, disguised as the [[Doom Raiders]] band, fought through spined devils and imps, called in dragon reinforcements ([[Melifarn]] and [[Aurinax]]), and confronted an avatar of Asmodeus himself in the temple beneath the villa.

[[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] signed a contract with Asmodeus to save the Cassalanter children: free six "brothers and sisters" (Jhesiyra, Rantantar, Mayrin, Trobriand, Muiral, and Arcturia) within 13 Founder's Days, or forfeit her own soul. The Cassalanters were sentenced to life in prison after Ylka delivered a legendary speech at their trial. Amalia later resurfaced in Season 2 as "Velia Shadowmantle," leading a [[Cult Of The Dragon]] cell and performing dracolich creation rituals.

## Resolution

The Cassalanters were exposed, defeated, and imprisoned. Their children Terenzio and Elzerina were placed under city protection and eventually joined the Trollskull Alley Irregulars. Xae's contract with Asmodeus set up the quest to free Halaster's apprentices that drove much of the subsequent campaign. Amalia's reappearance as Velia Shadowmantle in Season 2 showed the family's dark reach extended even from prison.
