# The Wall of the Faithless and the Radiant Citadel

**Seasons:** 2
**Episodes:** [[S02E25 - Episode Fifty Five LIVE (S02E25)|S02E25]] - [[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]
**Status:** Resolved (Season 2 Finale)

## Summary

The final arc of Season 2 saw SWOJ confront two intertwined threats: [[Amalia Cassalanter]]'s [[Cult of the Dragon]] cell performing dracolich creation rituals on captured dragons, and [[Manshoon]]'s forces seizing control of the [[Radiant Citadel]]. This arc brought together nearly every thread of the campaign -- the Cassalanter conspiracy, the hunt for Manshoon, the dragon allies of the [[Way of the Balanced Scale]], and [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]'s quest to free [[Halaster]]'s siblings -- into one sprawling climax.

The arc began after SWOJ defeated [[Rowan Crooks]] at [[Zentil Keep]] and passed the [[Raven Queen]]'s dream test. They learned that Amalia Cassalanter had resurfaced under the alias "[[Velia Shadowmantle]]," leading a Cult of the Dragon cell operating from [[Castle Nehri Tar]] in the [[Mere of Dead Men]]. The cult was capturing dragons from the Way of the Balanced Scale -- including [[Jack]] (silver dragon) and [[Jill]] / Iglemar (white dragon) -- and performing dracolich creation rituals, draining their souls into phylacteries.

SWOJ infiltrated Castle Nehri Tar, using baby otyughs as a distraction, and descended through its depths. They freed Jill from a cult trance, but witnessed Velia drain Jack's soul into a bronze phylactery. After recovering the [[Mask of Dragon Kind]], they escaped and regrouped. The campaign then shifted to the Radiant Citadel, where Manshoon's forces had taken control, imprisoning the Speakers in soul stones and installing soul pylons to drain the Citadel's essence. The two-headed dracolich created from the cult's rituals claimed the Dawn Incarnate crystals.

The liberation of the Radiant Citadel was the campaign's climactic sequence. SWOJ freed [[Zellopharn]] from imprisonment in the Preserve of the Ancestors, navigated the storm sewers (defeating "Old Salty," a giant crawfish from Sid Squid's restaurant that had grown enormous), and emerged at the Speakers' platform at the top of the Citadel. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] reactivated the Concord Jewels to send displaced civilizations home. They freed [[Sholeh]], the elder speaker revealed to be an ancient brass dragon in disguise, and fifteen other imprisoned speakers. In the climactic battle, [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] polymorphed into a Tyrannosaurus Rex and bit Amalia Cassalanter, while [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] communed with the [[Raven Queen]], receiving divine intervention -- a flock of spectral ravens that banished the two-headed dracolich and destroyed the top soul pylon.

The Wall of the Faithless itself was broken during these events, unleashing creatures from beyond -- giant frog-like beings that created "Bizarro SWOJ" illusions. SWOJ escaped aboard a Concord Jewel, fighting the frog creatures as the vessel warped through multiple dimensions before crash-landing in the [[Spiral Cavern]] on the Sword Coast, where Neverwinter soldiers, the Trollskull Regulars, and the Griffin Cavalry of Waterdeep waited to receive refugees.

## Resolution

The Cult of the Dragon was dismantled, the Radiant Citadel was liberated, and the two-headed dracolich was banished by the Raven Queen's divine intervention. SWOJ escaped aboard a Concord Jewel that crash-landed in the Spiral Cavern, where allied forces were waiting. The Season 2 finale saw SWOJ return to Waterdeep for a homecoming, with Scoot dealing with a frog curse from the creatures beyond the Wall, the party receiving a Guild charter, and attending a God's Night ceremony. [[Laeral Silverhand]] returned, and the campaign concluded its second season with SWOJ as established heroes of the Sword Coast.
