# Founders Day

A holiday celebrated in [[Waterdeep]] honoring the city's founders. The [[Cassalanter Family  Cult Of Asmodeus|Cassalanter family]] hosts a lavish masked ball and feast at [[Cassalanter Villa]] during Founders Day, which serves as a front for their cult activities.

## Key Events

- **[[S01E09 - Episode Nine|S01E09]]:** [[SWOJ]] attends the Founders Day masked ball at the [[Cassalanter Villa Ballroom]], where [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]'s father [[Grim Wavesilver]] confronts him
- **[[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]]:** The Cassalanters' Founders Day feast is revealed as a front for a devil-soul harvest; [[Victoro Cassalanter]] uses his [[Charming Cane]] and [[Rod of Rulership]] on the crowd; [[SWOJ]] battles devils and confronts [[Asmodeus]]'s avatar; [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] signs a contract with Asmodeus
