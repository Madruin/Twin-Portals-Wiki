# Zelifarn's Lair

A translucent, empty echo of [[Zelifarn]]'s dragon lair, accessed through one of the twin portals at the [[Deep Dragon Temple]]. Rather than the physical location beneath [[Deepwater Harbor]], this was a spiritual memory or psychic imprint of the lair -- confirming Zelifarn's deep ancestral connection to the temple and the bronze dragon lineage tied to the [[Breathless Elder]].

**Type:** Portal destination / spiritual echo
**First appearance:** [[S02E11 - Episode Forty One (S02E11)|S02E11]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

When SWOJ traveled through the twin portals at the [[Deep Dragon Temple]] during [[S02E11 - Episode Forty One (S02E11)|S02E11]], one of the two portals led to this location -- a translucent, empty version of [[Zelifarn]]'s lair. It appeared to be a memory or echo of the actual place rather than the real, physical [[Zelifarn's Underwater Lair|underwater lair]] in [[Deepwater Harbor]]. The space was devoid of treasure or any physical presence, existing only as a spiritual impression within the portal network.

The existence of this echo confirmed that Zelifarn's spirit was bound to the [[Deep Dragon Temple]] through the same ancient draconic lineage that connected him to [[Melifarn]] and the other bronze dragons afflicted by the [[Breathless Elder]]'s enchanted sleep. The other twin portal led to [[The Astral Sea]], and from there through [[The Wall]] into [[The Far Realm]] where the [[Breathless Elder]]'s corpse drifted with dragon spirits tethered to it.
