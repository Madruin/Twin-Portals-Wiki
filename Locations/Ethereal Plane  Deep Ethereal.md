# Ethereal Plane / Deep Ethereal

The vast, otherworldly expanse through which the [[Concord Jewel]] travels to reach the [[Radiant Citadel]]. The Deep Ethereal is an infinite auroral space filled with scintillating rainbows and strange phenomena, far removed from the Material Plane. It is home to the [[Radiant Citadel]] itself, which floats around the [[Auroral Diamond]], as well as dangerous entities like the [[Keening Gloam]] -- a churning maelstrom visible in the distance. Time flows at the same rate as on the Material Plane.

**Type:** Plane of Existence
**Connected to:** [[Radiant Citadel]], [[Keening Gloam]], [[Wall Of The Faithless]]

## Description

The Deep Ethereal is a plane of spirits and auroral light, distinct from the Border Ethereal that overlaps with the Material Plane. Travelers describe it as an infinite space of shimmering rainbows and scintillating colors. The [[Radiant Citadel]] floats here, built around the colossal [[Auroral Diamond]], connected to locations on [[Toril]] via the [[Concord Jewel|Concord Jewels]]. The [[Keening Gloam]] -- a vast, dark maelstrom -- lurks in the background, and the [[Ethereal Galleon]]'s enchanted sails can phase into this plane, though the ability barely functions with damaged masts. The [[Wall Of The Faithless]], [[Kelemvor]]'s domain for trapping faithless souls, is also reachable through the Deep Ethereal.

## Notable Events

- **[[S02E08 - Episode Thirty Eight (S02E08)|S02E08]]:** SWOJ travels through the Deep Ethereal aboard the [[Concord Jewel]] for the first time, passing through darkness and past the churning [[Keening Gloam]] before arriving at the [[Radiant Citadel]]. The citadel is revealed as a floating city built around the [[Auroral Diamond]], a hub for refugees connected to [[Toril]] by Concord Jewels.
- **[[S02E32 - Episode Sixty Two LIVE (S02E32)|S02E32]]:** SWOJ's fleet battles through the storm over the [[Moonhole]] and descends into the [[Spiral Cavern]]. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] takes over piloting the Concord Jewel from a cultist pilot, and the jewel transports everyone -- including the [[Ethereal Galleon]] and the wrecked [[Clockwork Kraken]] -- to the Deep Ethereal. The [[Radiant Citadel]] appears as a distant gleaming light with the [[Keening Gloam]] vast in the background.
- **[[S02E33 - Episode Sixty Three (S02E33)|S02E33]]:** SWOJ hurtles through the Deep Ethereal aboard the Concord Jewel with the Ethereal Galleon and salvaged Clockwork Kraken in tow. They dump empty soul stone crates overboard into the void. The [[Radiant Citadel]] comes into view at night, its normally luminous diamond glowing a sickly red, with two green conduit pylons visible and a half-formed dracolich circling the city.
- **[[S02E35 - Episode Sixty Five (S02E35)|S02E35]]:** The Radiant Citadel passes through the [[Keening Gloam]] while in the Deep Ethereal, causing [[The Raven Queen]]'s spectral ravens to fade as divine power cannot sustain in the interdimensional void. The citadel then exits the Gloam and arrives at the [[Wall Of The Faithless]].
