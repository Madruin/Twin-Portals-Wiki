# Zedford Falls

Zedford Falls is a small settlement in [[Prehistoric Waterdeep]] near the homestead of the [[Gorge Baileybrew|Bailey Brew]] family. The name is a direct reference to the town of Bedford Falls from the classic film *It's a Wonderful Life*, fitting the holiday special's theme of Christmas movie homages.

**Type:** settlement
**Region:** [[Prehistoric Waterdeep]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]

## Description

Zedford Falls is a modest dwarven settlement in the snowy wilderness of prehistoric Waterdeep. The Bailey Brew family describes it as their humble home, situated in a region of scattered dwarf communities that will eventually grow into the city of Waterdeep. The settlement exists during the same prehistoric era visited by SWOJ during the Winter Solstice holiday special, when the party was transported back in time by the [[Stone of Golorr]].

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]:** [[Gorge Baileybrew]] introduces SWOJ to the Bailey Brew homestead, describing it as their humble home by Zedford Falls; this is where the party first learns about the family and the threat facing them during the Winter Solstice
