# The Ash Tree Summit

A volcanic caldera at the mountain summit containing an enormous ash tree hundreds of feet tall. Four seasonal streams spiral around it, draining into the tree's heart. Lark's body rests in its crook. The werewolf and wererats feast here, corrupting the water with blood. Red ash berries hang from the branches.
