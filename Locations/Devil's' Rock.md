# Devil's Rock

Devil's Rock (also known as the Winged Rock or Winged Isle) is a wedge-shaped island in the [[Nameless Seas]], recognizable by two massive rocky outcrop spires flanking its peak that resemble either wings or devil horns. The island is the site of a burned-out abbey containing an aboleth shrine, and was believed to hold the hidden whirlpool entrance to the [[Deep Dragon Temple]] somewhere within its interior. SWOJ traveled here aboard the [[Hell Razor]] and later by rowboat from [[Driftwood]], braving skeletal pirates in the sand dunes and blade coral reefs to explore the ruins and locate the passage below.

**Type:** island
**Region:** [[Nameless Seas]]
**First appearance:** [[S02E09 - Episode Thirty Nine (S02E09)|S02E09]]

## Description

Devil's Rock rises from the sea as a large wedge, with the low end facing the water as rolling sand dunes stretching half a mile inland. The terrain gradually slopes upward into barren, craggy rock dotted with occasional trees and small rock lizards. The two signature spires jut outward at roughly thirty-degree angles from the island's peak, and when approached from the right angle at sunrise, the sun sits perfectly between them -- a striking and ominous sight.

The coastline is surrounded by blade coral, making close approach by ship extremely dangerous. Slimy hundred-foot cliffs line the island's flanks, while the dune area conceals things moving beneath the sand -- skeletal pirates who burst from the ground to attack visitors. A pirate crew from the Crimson Kraken was spotted hiding a small keel ship on the far side of the island, also hunting for the legendary treasure.

At the island's summit, between the two horn-like spires, sits a ruined stone abbey. The structure has been devastated -- massive holes blown through the roof and walls from above, with chunks of rock scattered dozens of feet from the building, suggesting destruction by something flying rather than cannon fire. Scorch marks are present but insufficient for dragon fire. Inside, the abbey's original purpose has been obscured by a makeshift shrine devoted to tentacled sea creatures ([[Aboleth]]). The shrine appears to have been built onto whatever the abbey originally was, constructed at its heart around a cross-shaped layout atypical of normal abbey architecture. A priestly-looking skeleton lies by the altar. Drawings on the shrine walls depict aboleths of various sizes, with one depicted at ten times the scale of the others at the top of the shrine.

The whirlpool entrance to the Deep Dragon Temple is located somewhere within the island itself rather than in the surrounding waters, a fact that surprised SWOJ and their allies. The party eventually located it deeper within the ruins.

## Notable Events

- **[[S02E09 - Episode Thirty Nine (S02E09)|S02E09]]:** SWOJ learns about Devil's Rock at [[Driftwood]] from pirates and locals. [[Long John McRon]] confirms he knows the location and that legends speak of a whirlpool and treasure there. [[Lark]] performs songs warning how dangerous the island is, inadvertently hyping up the pirate crowd. [[Xae]] flies through a seagull's eyes to scout the island from above, spotting the burned-out abbey, moving sand in the dunes, and an enemy pirate ship hiding on the far side.
- **[[S02E09 - Episode Thirty Nine (S02E09)|S02E09]]:** SWOJ arrives at the Winged Rock aboard a rowboat from the [[I-Catcher]], with [[Zardoz Zord]] and [[Mr. E]] providing transport. The island's namesake spires are immediately visible, and [[Mr. E]] recalls that [[Melifarn]] once fought aboleths in the Deep Dragon Temple hundreds of years ago. The party bids farewell to [[Jarlaxle]] (as Mr. E), who gives them a parting hug before shoving [[Scoot]] off the boat.
- **[[S02E10 - Episode Forty (S02E10)|S02E10]]:** SWOJ makes landfall on Devil's Rock and fights through skeletal pirates erupting from the sand dunes while cannon fire and shouting echo from the pirate ships behind them. They hike several hours uphill through worsening weather to reach the ruined abbey.
- **[[S02E10 - Episode Forty (S02E10)|S02E10]]:** Inside the abbey, SWOJ discovers the aboleth shrine built onto the original structure. The party examines the shrine's carvings depicting aboleths at various scales, finds the priestly skeleton by the altar, and notes a campsite fresher than the ancient ruins -- suggesting recent visitors. They search for a passage downward and eventually locate the whirlpool entrance to the [[Deep Dragon Temple]] within the island's interior.
