# Devil's' Rock

A wedge-shaped island in the Nameless Seas with two rocky horn-like spires flanking its peak, sand dunes on the lower shore (full of skeletons), and a burned-out temple/abbey at the top. The whirlpool entrance to the Deep Dragon Temple is believed to be inside the island. Also called the Winged Rock.
