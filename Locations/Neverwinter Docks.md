# Neverwinter Docks

The Neverwinter Docks are a cliff-side harbor and shipyard built into the western bluffs below [[Castle Never]], roughly sixty feet above the ocean. Connected to the city by wooden switchbacks carved into the rock face, the docks serve as both a military port and the construction site for [[Neverwinter]]'s airship fleet. Several caves extend into the mountainside from the dock level, including [[Quorlith The Starweaver|Corlith the Starweaver]]'s webbed lair. SWOJ departed from these docks aboard the [[Ethereal Galleon]] for the assault on the [[Moonhole|Spiral Cavern]].

**Type:** Docks / Shipyard
**Region:** [[Neverwinter]]
**First appearance:** [[S02E30 - Episode Sixty (S02E30)|S02E30]]

## Description

The docks occupy a series of ledges and platforms jutting from the cliff face on Neverwinter's western shore, where the city wall meets the sea. Wooden switchbacking scaffolds and stairways zigzag down from the castle to the dock level, connecting several natural cave openings in the mountainside. The harbor faces west, offering dramatic views of the sunset over the ocean.

During the war preparations of late Season 2, the docks were expanded into a dry-dock shipyard where three airships were constructed under [[Renaer Neverember]]'s direction: the [[Ethereal Galleon]] (a wooden galleon with gossamer sails capable of phasing into the Ethereal Plane), the Hammerhead Destroyer (shaped like a hammerhead shark with a massive battering ram), and the Clockwork Kraken (a steampunk vessel). [[Quorlith The Starweaver|Corlith the Starweaver]] descended from her usual perch in Castle Never's throne room to help build the ships, contributing spider silk stronger than steel cable that made them more maneuverable. The ships sat on elevated dry docks extending from the cliff face, tall enough that their masts poked above the cliff top.

One of the caves served as Corlith's nesting site, where she kept her eggs wrapped in silk webbing.

## Notable Events

- **[[S02E30 - Episode Sixty (S02E30)|S02E30]]:** SWOJ arrives at Neverwinter by dragon-back and sees three airships under construction at the cliff-side docks -- the Hammerhead Destroyer, the Ethereal Galleon, and the Clockwork Kraken. [[Renaer Neverember]] reveals that [[Quorlith The Starweaver|Corlith]] helped engineer all three ships. SWOJ chooses the Ethereal Galleon as their vessel for the assault on the [[Moonhole]]. Later, saboteurs detonate an explosion at the docks that damages the Ethereal Galleon's stern, blowing out its windows and tearing a hole in its hull.
- **[[S02E31 - Episode Sixty One (S02E31)|S02E31]]:** SWOJ rushes down the switchbacks to the burning Ethereal Galleon. While [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] and [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] organize fire-fighting crews, [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] hears frantic clicking from one of the caves and discovers [[Quorlith The Starweaver|Corlith]] in a panic -- the saboteur who set the ship fire also stole her eggs and fled deeper into the tunnels. SWOJ pursues through a collapsing cave passage, cornering the thief [[Krom]] at a dead end. Ylka polymorphs him into a cicada before he can use a volatile potion, and Scoot catches the falling vial mid-air while the eggs bounce safely to the ground. Corlith recovers her brood and patches the Ethereal Galleon with silk.
- **[[S02E31 - Episode Sixty One (S02E31)|S02E31]]:** Before departing, SWOJ meets [[Zardoz Zord]] on the wooden dock. He agrees to carry messages to [[Waterdeep]]'s masked lords and Blackstaff Tower requesting reinforcements. Allies gather at the docks for the launch: [[Squidly]], [[Fel'Rekt Lafeen]], [[Shaleheart]] (who crawls onto the ship's underside), [[Fioleth]] as quartermaster, and [[Urstul Floxxin]], a former enemy volunteering for redemption. Three ships depart from the docks with full crews as Neverwinter sends them off with music.
