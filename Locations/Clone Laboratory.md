# Clone Laboratory

A hidden laboratory deep in the caverns beneath [[Zhentil Keep]], accessible via a tunnel branching off from the [[Soul Engine]] chamber. The lab contained twelve human-sized clone tanks lining the walls, each holding a suspended copy of [[Rowan Crooks]] in various stages of development, connected by arcane machinery. [[Friar Truck]]'s private workstation occupied part of the space, where he kept detailed notes revealing the full conspiracy behind the Jolly Gents -- including the fact that Truck himself had manipulated the original Crooks into staging the doomed assault on Zhentil Keep. [[Scoot]] destroyed all twelve tanks using [[Friar Truck]]'s own mace.

**Type:** laboratory

## Description

The clone laboratory lies behind a large unlocked metal door at the end of a tunnel leading away from the [[Soul Engine]] chamber, in the direction [[Little Joan]] indicated as the location of the phylacteries. Inside, twelve back-to-back clone tanks line the walls, each containing a floating body of [[Rowan Crooks]] suspended in animation and connected by arcane machinery. The tanks hold a fluid that, when the power is cut, grows dark and cloudy. When a tank is smashed, the fluid spills out along with a rubbery, lifeless clone body that quickly begins to melt and dissolve.

Beyond the clone tanks, the room also functions as [[Friar Truck]]'s personal workstation, with a desk covered in books, notes, and logs. Shelves hold at least a hundred small metal objects -- phylacteries in the form of little spheres, tubes, and boxes similar to [[Antediluvian Jake]]'s copper orb. An altar in the center of the room is flanked by four pedestals bearing small glowing metal pieces that power some kind of arcane ritual. There is also a map of the island's basement showing concentric rings radiating from the [[Soul Engine]], with labeled locations including the phylactery laboratory, a river-in-roost, a print shop, and a post.

## Notable Events

- **[[S02E24 - Episode Fifty Four (S02E24)|S02E24]]:** SWOJ kicks open the metal door to find twelve clone tanks holding suspended copies of [[Rowan Crooks]]. [[Antediluvian Jake]] identifies them as clones rather than standard Jolly Gent phylactery bodies. [[Xae]] finds and pushes a purple button on a central device, shutting down the tanks and causing the fluid to go dark and cloudy. [[Scoot]] then systematically smashes all twelve tanks using [[Friar Truck]]'s mace, spilling clone bodies and fluid across the floor while [[Xae]] uses mold earth to create drainage. The party wades through dissolving clone residue as they search the room.
- **[[S02E24 - Episode Fifty Four (S02E24)|S02E24]]:** [[Lark]] (rolling 21 investigation) discovers [[Friar Truck]]'s recent notes near the door documenting that Crooks had just visited, taken the [[Hungering Shard]] and the [[Manshoon]] [[Soul Stone]]s. [[Ylka]] (rolling 26 investigation) finds Truck's private journal entries revealing the core conspiracy: Truck had convinced the original [[Rowan Crooks]] to stage the failed assault on [[Zhentil Keep]], manipulating him into his own downfall. A secret clone of Crooks was made that [[Manshoon]] inhabited after their fight. Additional notes detail Crooks' travels via teleportation to the [[Radiant Citadel]] and his plan to reach [[Kalimvoire's Wall]]. [[Lark]] loads phylacteries onto a cart, managing to collect fourteen before the party moves on.
- **[[S02E24 - Episode Fifty Four (S02E24)|S02E24]]:** [[Ylka]] also discovers a map of the underground complex on Truck's desk, revealing the layout of the caverns beneath the island with the [[Soul Engine]] at the center and various facilities radiating outward along the soul-chains.
