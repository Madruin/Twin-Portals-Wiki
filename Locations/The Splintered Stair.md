# The Splintered Stair

The Splintered Stair is a modest apartment in the [[Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]], located near [[Candle Lane]] on a main thoroughfare. It served as the shared home of [[Scoot Sparkles]] and [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] at the start of the campaign, and later became a crash pad for [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] as well. The apartment sits above a tavern or establishment of the same name, near a manhole that funnels street runoff from the surrounding cobblestone gutters.

**Type:** residence
**First appearance:** [[S01E01 - Episode One|Episode S01E01]]

## Description

The Splintered Stair is a humble dwelling in one of Waterdeep's rougher neighborhoods. [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] describes it as a "seedy place in the Docks Ward," a far cry from the noble Wavesilver household he ran away from. The apartment is accessible from a staircase and sits near a stone-lined ditch that channels rainwater and waste toward a manhole on the main thoroughfare, roughly a thousand feet from the end of [[Candle Lane]]. Despite its grim surroundings, the apartment has a certain lived-in charm -- [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] hangs posters on the walls after moving in, and [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] considers it a comfortable place to nap after a long day on the boats.
