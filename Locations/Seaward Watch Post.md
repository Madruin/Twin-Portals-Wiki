# Seaward Watch Post

The Seaward Watch Post is a [[City Watch]] station in the [[Sea Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]] with a prison in its basement. [[SWOJ]] visited here to report on events and to interrogate captured cultists.

**Type:** City Watch Station
**Ward:** [[Sea Ward]], [[Waterdeep]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E10 - Episode Ten|Episode S01E10]]

## Description

The Seaward Watch Post is a City Watch facility in Waterdeep's Sea Ward. It includes a basement prison where captured criminals and cultists are held awaiting trial. The station is staffed by City Watch guards and serves as a processing point for arrests in the ward.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E10 - Episode Ten|S01E10]]:** After the chaos at the [[Cassalanter Villa]] party -- where cultists attacked and were subdued by [[SWOJ]] and the noble guests -- [[Max]], the City Watch messenger, arrives with guards to cart away the unconscious cultists. The cultists are taken to the Seaward Watch Post and locked in the prison below. [[Terry]] later escorts [[SWOJ]] to the watch post so they can follow up on the captured cultists. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] is particularly interested in interrogating a captured female cultist who appeared unable to open her mouth to speak.
