# Lady Lissandra Never's Tomb

The simple, unadorned crypt of [[Lady Lissandra Never]], wife of [[Lord Halueth Never]] and co-founder of [[Neverwinter]], located in the crypts beneath [[Castle Never]]. The [[Crown of Neverwinter]] was discovered here on her skeleton.

**Type:** tomb
**Location:** Crypts beneath [[Castle Never]]
**First appearance:** Episode S02E18
**Total appearances:** 1 episodes

## Description

The tomb lies behind a silver-scaled door in the lower crypts of [[Castle Never]], opposite [[Lord Halueth Never's Tomb]] which is behind a gold-scaled door. In contrast to her husband's ornate crypt, [[Lady Lissandra Never]]'s resting place is simple and unadorned. It contains a single stone coffin made of the same material as the throne in the room above. A pair of chandeliers hang from the ceiling, lighting up magically with a silver glow when the room is opened. An inscription at the base of the coffin reads: "Lissandra Never of the waves of silver."

The simplicity of the tomb reflects a Last Crusade-style test -- the true relic was found not in the ornate crypt, but in the humble one. [[Lady Lissandra Never]] was a follower of [[Kelemvor]] (the god of neutral judgment) and was described as more pragmatic than her husband, yet beloved by the people of [[Neverwinter]].

## Notable Events

- During Episode S02E18, [[SWOJ]] was sent by [[Dagult Neverember]] to retrieve the [[Crown of Neverwinter]] from [[Castle Never]], with [[Renaer Neverember]] also independently asking them to find it.
- The [[Rhyming Raven]] hinted that the crown was "up" near the throne, but the party ultimately found a map leading them down to the crypts. [[Renaer Neverember|Renaer]]'s knowledge of history pointed them toward the Never family tombs as the likely resting place.
- The party debated which door to open -- silver (associated with [[Kelemvor]]) or gold (associated with [[Tyr]]). [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] opened the gold door to [[Lord Halueth Never's Tomb]], while [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] entered the silver door to this crypt.
- [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] made the connection between "waves of silver" and the party's own name -- the Silver Waves of Justice -- and convinced [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] to come help open this coffin.
- After struggling to slide the lid (it turned out to be a pull-lid installed facing the other direction), the party found [[Lady Lissandra Never]]'s skeleton wearing a mithril [[Crown of Neverwinter]] with a large silver gemstone and red and blue gems in a wavy pattern.
- As [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] lifted the crown, the ghost of [[Lady Lissandra Never]] rose from the skeleton with long wavy silver hair and hollow eyes, unleashing a devastating banshee wail that dropped [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] to zero hit points instantly.
