# Zuber Farm

Zuber Farm is a farm and vineyard in the [[Undercliff]] area south of [[Waterdeep]], located near [[Stoutfellow Farm]]. It is run by [[Blossom Zuber]], whose son [[Dasher Zuber]] went missing before [[SWOJ]] arrived to investigate a scarecrow attack in [[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]. The farm also has a bartender named [[Tula]]. The party camped at the farm overnight and later returned to deliver a farewell letter from Dasher and uncover a connection between Blossom and [[Cassalanter Villa]].

**Type:** Farm / Vineyard
**First appearance:** [[S01E07 - Episode Seven|Episode S01E07]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Description

Zuber Farm sits in [[Undercliff]], a rural area outside Waterdeep's walls to the south. It operates as both a farm and vineyard, neighboring [[Stoutfellow Farm]] run by [[Mary Stoutfellow]]. The property includes a tavern area staffed by the bartender [[Tula]]. When [[SWOJ]] first visited, the farm was plagued by animated scarecrows terrorizing the surrounding area, and [[Blossom Zuber]]'s son [[Dasher Zuber|Dasher]] had disappeared.

## Notable Events

- **Scarecrow investigation** ([[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]): [[SWOJ]] traveled to [[Undercliff]] on an [[Emerald Enclave]] quest delivered by [[Leah]]. They met [[Blossom Zuber]] and learned that her son [[Dasher Zuber|Dasher]] had gone missing. The party camped at the farm overnight and fought three animated scarecrows at night, using bioluminescent moss as bait.
- **Blossom's confession and Dasher's letter** ([[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]): [[Mary Stoutfellow]] paid [[SWOJ]] 180gp for the three scarecrows and gave them the enchanted [[Sickle of Storms]]. [[Blossom Zuber]] confessed that the scarecrows had been summoned by a devil ring -- a ring with a three-pointed symbol matching the [[The God Catcher|God Catcher]], given to her by an aging tiefling with thin-rimmed glasses at [[Cassalanter Villa]]. The ring crumbled to dust when removed. Later, after finding [[Dasher Zuber|Dasher]] alive as a wererat with the [[Shard Shunners]] in the [[The God Catcher|God Catcher]], [[SWOJ]] delivered his farewell letter to [[Blossom Zuber|Blossom]]. In gratitude, she gave them the [[Gravity Goblet]] prototype and an invitation to a [[Cassalanter Villa|Cassalanter]] party.
