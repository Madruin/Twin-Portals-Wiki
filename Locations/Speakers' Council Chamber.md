# Speakers' Council Chamber

A chamber near the top of the Radiant Citadel where the Speakers for the Ancestors meet. Contains a platform with ancient ward controls that can shield the city from intruders, prevent teleportation, and control the citadel's movement. Located near the top soul conduit pylon.

**Type:** government building
**First appearance:** Episode S02E33
**Total appearances:** 1 episodes

## Notable Events




- **S02E33:** Vincent Trench provides Xae a list of her six siblings (all alive) and intelligence about the Speakers' council chamber ward controls in exchange for a future favor sealed by geas
