# Guard Post

An underground guard station at the entrance to the tunnels leading down into the chasm beneath [[Neverwinter]]. [[SWOJ]] had to bluff their way past the guards here while escorting the [[Manshoon Clone (Man-Two)|Manshoon clone]] on his secret mission.

**Type:** Encounter Area / Military Post
**Location:** Chasm tunnels, beneath [[Neverwinter]]
**First appearance:** [[S02E16 - Episode Forty Six LIVE (S02E16)|Episode S02E16]]

## Description

The guard post is a small, poorly maintained stone structure approximately 100 feet from the main tunnel entrance into the chasm. Inside are torches, exploring supplies, ropes, climbing tools, weapons, and armor. A large trapdoor leads further down into the depths. The station is staffed by five soldiers -- three seated and two standing near the trapdoor -- who challenge anyone approaching. Each guard has a personal chest by their post.

## Notable Events

- **[[S02E16 - Episode Forty Six LIVE (S02E16)|S02E16]]:** [[SWOJ]] approaches the guard post with the [[Manshoon Clone (Man-Two)|Manshoon clone]], who insists on keeping a low profile. [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] takes the lead, claiming they are there "for the delivery." The lead guard, a woman, is suspicious because another group claiming the same thing had just passed through. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] shows the [[The Stone of Golorr]] to persuade the guards, though the Manshoon clone is alarmed by this breach of secrecy. The party successfully bluffs their way through and descends into the chasm tunnels.
