# Shadowfell (Deep)

The far reaches of the [[Shadowfell]], the polar opposite of the Deep Wilds and the farthest point from the borderlands. [[SWOJ]] traveled here via a portal from a secret shrine of the [[Raven Queen]] to pursue [[Rowan Crooks]] and the [[Jolly Gents]]. The Deep Shadowfell is characterized by murky green clouds, towering spires of rock extending into bottomless crags, and an oppressive stillness far heavier than the party's previous visit to the plane.

**Type:** Plane / Region
**Region:** [[Shadowfell]]
**First Appearance:** [[S02E21]]

## Description

The Deep Shadowfell is a landscape of barren mesas perched on massive rock spires, connected by stone and rope bridges, all shrouded in murky green clouds. Lightning-like blasts occasionally flash through the bottomless crags below. All souls pass through the Shadowfell on their way to their final destinations, making it a nexus of spiritual transit.

Dominating the sky is a colossal opaque black sphere -- a false dark sun that appeared roughly ten days before [[SWOJ]]'s arrival. Much larger than a normal sun, it absorbs souls that would normally pass through the [[Raven Queen]]'s [[Fortress of Memories]], effectively cutting off the natural flow of the dead. Wyvern patrols regularly leave the dark sun, scouring mountaintops and crags to attack any who move before returning. Time feels indistinct here -- everything is permanently shadowy with no true day or night cycle.

The [[Raven Queen]] later revealed that within the dark sun lies [[Zhentil Keep]], [[Rowan Crooks]]'s floating castle base, which had been ripped from [[Faerun]]. A piece of the castle -- a section of library with curating spirits -- broke off and fell into the darkness below about four days before [[SWOJ]]'s arrival.

## Notable Events

- **Reunion with the Shadar-Kai:** Upon arriving, [[SWOJ]] was greeted by [[Idae]], [[Mari]], and [[Blish]], the three Shadar-Kai bards they had met on their previous visit to the Shadowfell. The trio had been sent by the [[Raven Queen]] to meet [[SWOJ]], and they arrived drumming and singing songs about the party's exploits. [[Xae]] ran toward them for an enthusiastic high-five with [[Idae]] ([[S02E21]]).
- **Intelligence Briefing:** The Shadar-Kai explained that the dark sun was siphoning souls and that wyvern patrols guarded it. They pointed [[SWOJ]] toward the Dark Market -- a place where interdimensional travelers gather to trade -- as a potential way to reach the dark sun, and they revealed the location of the [[Raven Queen]]'s sepulchre containing a soul stone ([[S02E21]]).
- **Soul Stone Quest:** The [[Raven Queen]] instructed [[SWOJ]] to take a soul stone from her sepulchre, located inside a trap door atop a nearby mountain. Each member had to leave a sacrifice on the altar -- a memory they had never shared with another soul. The [[Raven Queen]] believed the soul stone could be used to prevent [[Rowan Crooks]]'s escape ([[S02E21]]).
- **The Bag Man Callback:** When the Shadar-Kai asked for tales, [[SWOJ]] recounted their earlier adventure befriending the Bag Man -- a story the Shadar-Kai had warned them about on their previous visit ([[S02E21]]).
