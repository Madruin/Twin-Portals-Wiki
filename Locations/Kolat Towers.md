# Kolat Towers

The Zentarim base of operations -- a pair of towers with seven floors containing traps, a flesh golem, summoning circles, and a portal to the Necromancer's sanctum at the top.

**Type:** building
**First appearance:** Episode S01E22
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Notable Events




- **S01E22:** City Watch raids the Kolat Towers; Necromancer's simulacrum (iron mask) rushes to defend
- **S01E22:** Ramalia Haventree (Harper agent) meets them at the dock with intel: Zentarim have the second Eye, Kolat Towers are the Zentarim base
- **S01E23:** Return through portal to Kolat Towers; find the shield is down (rain getting in)
- **S01E23:** SWOJ escapes as the Kolat Towers explode behind them
- **S01E24:** Reporters arrive at the Short Rest asking about the Kolat Towers explosion
