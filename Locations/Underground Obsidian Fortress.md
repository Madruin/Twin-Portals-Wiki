# Underground Obsidian Fortress

An ancient fortress carved from obsidian, located deep beneath [[Castle Naerytar]] in the [[Mere of Dead Men]]. The fortress predates the surface castle, which was built on top of its ruins. Described by [[Bronk]] as "geroconic in nature," it serves as the [[Cult of the Dragon]]'s main staging area and houses the private lair of two black dragon twins. The keep contains a massive dragon hoard, a cauldron of dragon tea, one of the five [[Masks of Dragon Kind]], and is protected by dragon-themed traps.

**Type:** Fortress / Dragon Lair
**Region:** [[Mere of Dead Men]], beneath [[Castle Naerytar]]
**First Appearance:** [[S02E28]]

## Description

The obsidian fortress sits at the bottom of a long tunnel system accessed through excavation holes in [[Castle Naerytar]]'s belly. The approach passes through mud-saturated caverns where mummified corpses protrude from the walls, an underground lake of opaque chalky mud patrolled by grasping undead hands, and a broader cavern network where [[Cult of the Dragon]] members haul spoil materials on wheeled skiffs.

The fortress keep itself is a hollowed-out structure of smooth obsidian with narrow cracks providing hand and footholds for climbing. Its main door is a heavy metal barrier with an intricate locking mechanism requiring two handles to operate. Dragon mouth spouts flank the door about fifteen feet up, serving as decorative receptacles. A small window near the top of the keep (roughly forty to fifty feet up) provides the only alternative entrance -- though it is protected by a sonic screech trap and an acidic mist trap.

Inside the keep lies a massive dragon hoard of gold and relics, plus a large steaming cauldron producing a sweet-smelling concoction that [[Bronk]] calls "dragon tea." The two black dragon twins use this as their private sanctum, and one of the five [[Masks of Dragon Kind]] is reportedly kept within the hoard.

## Notable Events

- **Bronk's Intelligence:** The disillusioned cultist [[Bronk]] guided [[SWOJ]] through the tunnels, sharing that the fortress was the cult's main staging area. He revealed the existence of the dragon hoard, the [[Masks of Dragon Kind|Mask of Dragon Kind]], and a vulnerable window into the keep -- while growing increasingly disillusioned with the cult, comparing it to an MLM ([[S02E28]]).
- **Xae's Bat Infiltration:** [[Xae]] transformed into a bat and squeezed through the small window into the keep, triggering a sonic screech trap that dealt 5d6 thunder damage and deafened her. This was immediately followed by an acidic mist trap in the tunnel, dealing 4d8 acid damage. She transformed back to halfling form and discovered the dragon hoard, cauldron, and empty lair inside ([[S02E28]]).
- **Dragon Tea Discovery:** Inside the keep, [[SWOJ]] found the steaming cauldron of "dragon tea" -- a mysterious sweet-smelling concoction. The party debated using the [[Masks of Dragon Kind|Mask of Dragon Kind]] to control the two black dragons, and considered tipping the cauldron or pouring a Potion of Protection from Evil and Good into it ([[S02E28]]).
- **Undead Lake Crossing:** On the approach, [[SWOJ]] had to navigate past an eighty-foot underground lake of opaque muddy water, where undead hands reached for anyone on the narrow path. Cultists dealt with them routinely by kicking at the hands or shooting firebolts ([[S02E28]]).
- **Cult Infiltration:** [[SWOJ]] disguised themselves in stolen cult robes and dragon masks (red, white, and blue varieties) to move through the fortress area undetected. [[Scoot]] wore one robe with [[Xae]] hiding underneath ([[S02E28]]).
