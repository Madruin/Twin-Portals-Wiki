# Underground Obsidian Fortress

A fortress carved from obsidian located deep beneath Castle Nehri Tar. Serves as the Cult of the Dragon's main headquarters. Contains the dragon keep with the dragon hoard, the cauldron of dragon tea, and the Mask of Dragon Kind. Features dragon mouth spouts and sonic screech traps.
