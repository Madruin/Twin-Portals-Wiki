# Castle Ward Dungeon

A high-security anti-magic prison in the [[Castle Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]], located in a large fortress near [[Castle Waterdeep]]. [[SWOJ]] was imprisoned here for approximately a week after being arrested by [[Dagult Neverember|Lord Neverember]]'s forces following the chaos at the [[Cassalanter Theatre|theater]] and the loss of the city's gold.

**Type:** prison
**First appearance:** [[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|Episode S01E27]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episodes

## Description

The dungeon lies deep underground beneath a fortress building in the Castle Ward, Waterdeep's governmental district. A side staircase descends far below street level into a dank, wet facility designed for political prisoners, spies, and other high-value detainees -- a maximum-security facility unlike the city's ordinary jails. Upon entry, prisoners are processed through a hallway where all gear, tools, and personal effects are confiscated. Approximately ten feet further down the corridor, an anti-magic field takes effect, erasing all magical auras and preventing spellcasting entirely. The cells are small stone rooms without traditional bars, sealed instead by heavy doors with only small grates for visibility. The cells face each other across a hallway, arranged three on one side and two on the other. The light never changes underground, making it impossible to track the passage of time.
