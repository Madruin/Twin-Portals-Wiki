# Frewn's Brews

A tavern on [[Trollskull Alley]] directly across the street from [[Trollskull Manor]]. Originally operated by the hostile [[Emmek Frewn]] as a rival establishment, the tavern was later taken over by [[Floon Blagmaar]], who renamed it **Froon's Brews** -- leaning into [[SWOJ]]'s persistent mispronunciation of his name. Despite a rocky start with gaudy renovations and mounting debt, the tavern eventually became a thriving neighborhood fixture thanks to financial investment from the party.

**Type:** Tavern / Restaurant
**Region:** [[Trollskull Alley]], [[North Ward]], [[Waterdeep]]
**First Appearance:** [[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]

## Description

Frewn's Brews sits directly across the street from [[Trollskull Manor]], making it the closest business to SWOJ's headquarters. Under [[Emmek Frewn]]'s ownership, the establishment was a source of friction -- Frewn viewed the party as competitors and actively worked to undermine them. After Frewn's departure, [[Floon Blagmaar]] took over and gave the place gaudy renovations that quickly put him deep in debt. The party invested 1,000 gold (with [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] throwing an additional 500 gold through the window) in exchange for parking privileges, and Floon was described as a changed man afterward. By Season 2, the tavern had become a functioning neighborhood gathering spot where regulars like Ted and Phyllis were willing to donate supplies to SWOJ before dangerous missions.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]:** [[Emmek Frewn]], the tavern's original owner, visits [[Trollskull Manor]] and offers 150 gold to buy it from [[SWOJ]]. The party refuses, and Frewn becomes openly hostile and antagonistic.
- **[[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]:** [[Dasher Zuber]] reveals that Emmek Frewn hired the [[Shard Shunners]] wererat gang to infest [[Trollskull Manor]] with rats at 50 gold per night, sabotaging the party's home. SWOJ negotiates a truce with the gang to fake the sabotage instead.
- **[[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]]:** During the Season 1 finale downtime montage, the tavern has been taken over by [[Floon Blagmaar]] and renamed Froon's Brews. Floon is struggling with debt from overbuilding. [[SWOJ]] invests 1,000 gold (plus an additional 500 from Scoot) in exchange for parking privileges. Floon is described as "incredibly gracious" and appears to have "turned a leaf."
- **[[S02E05 - Episode Thirty Five (S02E05)|S02E05]]:** After SWOJ returns from a three-year time displacement, Froon's Brews is still operating on [[Trollskull Alley]]. [[Floon Blagmaar|Froon]] confirms hearing crunching and thumping sounds heading north on the night [[Fala Lefaliir]] was kidnapped from [[Corellon's Crown]], providing a crucial clue about the abduction.
- **[[S02E06 - Episode Thirty Six (S02E06)|S02E06]]:** Before departing [[Waterdeep]] northward on their mission to find [[Anya Ermbrusk|Anya Irmbrusk]] in [[Amphail]], SWOJ stops at Froon's Brews. Patrons Ted and Phyllis donate two health potions for the journey.
- **[[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]:** During the Season 2 finale homecoming, SWOJ visits Froon's Brews and discovers it is now thriving -- a testament to the party's early investment paying off. Floon serves the party food and drinks in celebration.
