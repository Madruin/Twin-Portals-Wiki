# The Moonhole

A huge round hole on a flat mountain face near Amphail, as if the bedrock was blown open from the inside. The entrance to the spiral cavern system leading underground. Heavily guarded with approximately twelve archers, magical runes, and spikes. The cult has fortified it against intrusion.

**Type:** dungeon_entrance
