# Zadio City Music Hall

The Zadio City Music Hall is a prominent theater in [[Waterdeep]], located on Copper Street near [[Delzorn Way]], not far from [[Trollskull Manor]]. Built on the site of [[Zadio Neverember]]'s ancient amphitheater, the Music Hall conceals the entrance to the [[Vault of Dragons]] in its floor. It was the site of SWOJ's climactic infiltration and the decisive battle against [[The Xanathar]].

**Type:** theater
**First appearance:** [[S01E25 - Episode Twenty Five|Episode S01E25]]

## Description

The Zadio City Music Hall is one of Waterdeep's more upscale theaters, hosting matinees and evening performances of dance and song. The building is an older structure with a large arched double door wide enough to accommodate stage props and equipment -- the only entrance and exit. High windows line the sides of the main atrium, but there is no separate stage door or backstage entrance. The [[Harpers]] hold special recognition at the Music Hall, though this influence does not always extend to its staff. Beneath the theater floor lies a sealed trap door leading down into ancient Malarkan dwarven ruins and, ultimately, the [[Vault of Dragons]]. The theater was originally an open-air amphitheater built by [[Zadio Neverember]] approximately 500 years before the campaign.
