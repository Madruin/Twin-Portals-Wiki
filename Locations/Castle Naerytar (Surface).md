# Castle Naerytar (Surface)

The above-ground level of [[Castle Naerytar]], a ruined swamp fortress in the [[Mere of Dead Men]]. The surface features crumbling outer walls, a collapsed observatory tower, and a main gate, with the castle's bailey dominated by two massive excavation pits dug by the [[Cult of the Dragon]]. During [[SWOJ]]'s assault on the castle, [[Little Joan]] and the [[Ripples]] operated from the surface while the party descended into the depths below.

**Type:** fortress
**First appearance:** [[S02E28 - Episode Fifty Eight (S02E28)|Episode S02E28]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Description

The surface of Castle Naerytar is a ruin of stone and mud, its walls roughly forty feet tall enclosing a rectangular bailey approximately one hundred feet across. A collapsed wizard's tower with a broken observatory telescope sits at one corner, providing cover amid the rubble. Two large excavation holes, each about forty feet in diameter, pierce the belly of the castle and extend deep underground. During the cult's occupation, thirty to forty cultists worked the pits, hauling skiffs loaded with muddy soil up from the depths using mold earth spells. Guards patrolled the walls while the excavation continued day and night.

After the underground battle that destroyed the cult's ritual and unleashed the trapped dragons, a massive chasm split the earth from east to west, tearing through the castle and the surrounding swamp. The surface became a smoking ruin as the freed dragons scoured the remains with fire, ice, and acid.
