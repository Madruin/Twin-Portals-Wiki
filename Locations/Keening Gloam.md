# Keening Gloam

A roiling, churning maelstrom of darkness that extends in all directions near the Radiant Citadel. Some scholars believe it is slowly moving closer. Silent at a distance but noisy up close.

**Type:** ethereal landmark, hazard
**First appearance:** Episode S02E34
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Notable Events




- **S02E34:** Xae activates the ward as the Citadel enters the Keening Gloam
- **S02E35:** The Citadel exits the Keening Gloam and arrives at the Wall of the Faithless
- **S02E35:** Spectral ravens fade as the Citadel passes through the Keening Gloam
