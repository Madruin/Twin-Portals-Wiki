# Ancient Waterdeep (992 D.R.)

Ancient Waterdeep in 992 DR is the version of the city that SWOJ was transported to by the [[Stone of Golorr]] during their quest to locate the [[Vault of Dragons]]. Roughly 500 years before the present day, Waterdeep was a smaller, more archaic settlement built over ancient [[Malarkan]] dwarven ruins. The city had recently suffered a catastrophic flood that poisoned its water supply, and its residents -- a mix of dwarves and humans -- were struggling to cope. SWOJ arrived here after the Stone challenged them to prove their will, and they worked alongside local allies to descend into the vault beneath [[Zadio Neverember]]'s amphitheater.

**Type:** city
**Region:** [[Sword Coast]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E25 - Episode Twenty Five|S01E25]]

## Description

Waterdeep in 992 DR is recognizably the same city but far less developed than the modern version. The architecture is more Greek or archaic in style, with stone buildings of ancient design. Dwarves still have a prominent presence, having built downward while humans built upward above them. The city includes landmarks that would persist into the modern era -- notably the amphitheater that would become the [[Zadio City Music Hall]], located near Trollskull Manor a couple blocks to the west near the High Road. At the time of SWOJ's visit, a devastating flood had recently struck the city, poisoning the water supply and damaging the sewer system. The Malarkan dwarven ruins beneath the surface were accessible through excavation beneath the amphitheater, leading down to the vault with its massive adamantine doors inscribed with Dwarvish runes. Inside, embossed iron frescoes depicted ancient Waterdeep defended by hooded wizards animating stone statues against invading goblins and sahuagin. Tapestries showed the city in an even older state, hinting at the deep layers of history beneath the streets.
