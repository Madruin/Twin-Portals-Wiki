# Reading Room (Sanctum)

The Reading Room is a secluded chamber in [[The Necromancer]]'s [[Necromancer's Extra-Dimensional Sanctum|extra-dimensional sanctum]], accessed by a winding staircase descending from the central antechamber. It served as a study and meeting space for the Zhentarim occupants of the sanctum.

**Type:** room
**Location:** [[Necromancer's Extra-Dimensional Sanctum]], beneath [[Kolat Towers]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E23 - Episode Twenty Three|Episode S01E23]]

## Description

A roughly twenty-by-twenty-foot square room located one level below the sanctum's central antechamber, reached by a winding staircase that descends about twenty feet. Narrow slit windows look out onto the purple void of the extra-dimensional space beyond. The walls are lined with bookshelves, and eight cushioned chairs surround a brazier of green flame at the room's center. When SWOJ arrived during [[S01E23 - Episode Twenty Three|S01E23]], the room appeared recently abandoned -- books lay scattered about as though readers had fled in a hurry when the Zhentarim forces were called back to [[Kolat Towers]].

The green flame brazier is enchanted with a form of magical surveillance. It tracks the movements of anyone in the room, flickering toward whoever stands closest. [[Xae|Xaerine]] detected the enchantment and warned the party that the brazier appeared to be watching them, though the exact school of magic was never determined. The green flame is reminiscent of torches found in [[Xanathar's Lair]].

Among the scattered volumes, the party discovered a spell book disguised with a cover titled "Flumph Mating Rituals," depicting two tentacled sky-jellyfish with intertwined tentacles. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] identified the ruse with a high investigation roll, revealing that the dragon-hide-bound book contained advanced arcane spells, including rituals for the Find Familiar spell. [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] slipped the book into [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]'s bag of holding while [[Xae|Xaerine]] distracted the watchful brazier.
