# Trade Ward

The **Trade Ward** is one of the commercial districts of [[Waterdeep]], mentioned alongside the [[Dock Ward]] as one of the poorer areas of the city experiencing an uptick in gang violence at the start of the campaign.

**Type:** District
**First appearance:** [[S01E01 - Episode One|Episode 1]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

The Trade Ward is a district in Waterdeep that serves as one of the city's commercial centers. At the time the campaign begins, it is described as one of the poorer districts alongside the Dock Ward, suffering from rising crime and gang violence that the [[City Watch]] has been struggling to contain. The violence has been occurring both at night and during the daytime, representing a significant deterioration in public safety.

## Notable Events

- **Rising Gang Violence** ([[S01E01 - Episode One|Episode 1]]): At the opening of the campaign, the DM establishes that Waterdeep has seen an uptick in crime, with gang violence plaguing the Dock Ward and the Trade Ward in particular. The City Watch has been having a hard time controlling the situation, with disturbances occurring day and night. This backdrop of instability sets the stage for the party's early adventures.

## Connected Characters

- [[Volothamp Geddarm]] - encountered the party in Waterdeep during this period of unrest
- [[Durnan]] - proprietor of the [[Yawning Portal]] in the city
- [[Floon Blagmaar]] - his kidnapping occurs during this crime wave
