# Yawning Portal

Very old tavern in the Castle Ward, built in the shell of an ancient wizard's tower. Circular, multi-story auditorium. Contains a 20-foot-wide well in the center that descends into Undermountain. Barkeep charges 20 gold for entry into the well. One of the safer taverns in the city.
