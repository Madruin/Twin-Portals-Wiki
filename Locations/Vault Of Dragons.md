# Vault Of Dragons

The Vault of Dragons is a vast underground chamber beneath the [[Zadio City Music Hall]] in [[Waterdeep]], concealed within ancient Malarkan dwarven ruins. The vault held the stolen dragon horde of 500,000 gold dragons, embezzled by former Open Lord [[Dagult Neverember]] and hidden away from the citizens of Waterdeep. It was guarded by [[Aurinax]], an adult gold dragon who assumed the disguise of a dwarf atop a mountain of gold coins. Recovering the horde was the driving quest of Season 1.

**Type:** dungeon
**First appearance:** [[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four|Episode S01E24]]

## Description

The Vault of Dragons is accessed through a trap door in the floor of the [[Zadio City Music Hall]], leading down into dwarven-carved tunnels dating back to the Malarkan era. The approach to the vault passes through corridors protected by an enchanted tapestry and guarded by black oozes. The vault chamber itself is enormous, dominated by a massive pile of gold coins upon which [[Aurinax]] sat disguised as a dwarf. The [[Stone of Golorr]] served as the key to locating the vault, its cryptic voice challenging SWOJ to prove their worth. [[Zardoz Zord]] and other factions raced against SWOJ to reach the horde, and the [[Cassalanter Family|Cassalanters]] sought it to fund their deal with [[Asmodeus]].

- **[[S01E25 - Episode Twenty Five|S01E25]]:** The [[Stone of Golorr]] speaks to SWOJ in a vision, challenging them to prove their will and "face me at my prime in the Vault of Dragons"; SWOJ is transported to an ancient version of Waterdeep where they meet [[Zadio Neverember]] at his amphitheater and learn the vault lies beneath it
- **[[S01E25 - Episode Twenty Five|S01E25]]:** Dwarves are found excavating beneath the amphitheater, tracking poisoned water to its source in the ruins below
- **[[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]]:** SWOJ descends into the vault through the trap door beneath the [[Zadio City Music Hall]], navigating past enchanted tapestries and black oozes
- **[[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]]:** SWOJ meets [[Aurinax]], who rises from his dwarf disguise into his true adult gold dragon form; they parlay with evidence from [[Renaer Neverember]] proving [[Dagult Neverember]]'s embezzlement, convincing Aurinax to release the gold
- **[[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]]:** [[Mr. E]] trades a "dragon piss" elixir for a giant ruby from [[Aurinax]]
- **[[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]]:** While loading gold into a bag of holding, [[The Xanathar]] attacks with bugbears, triggering the climactic battle that spills from the vault into the theater above
- **[[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]]:** SWOJ flies on dragons [[Mellafarne|Zellophane]] and [[Aurinax]] to [[Castle Waterdeep]], where the [[Masked Lords]] accept the recovered horde and award SWOJ a 50,000 gold finder's fee (10,000 each); [[Volo]] later seeks funding for his book, citing his role in helping them find the vault
- **[[S02E01 - Episode Thirty One (S02E01)|S02E01]]:** The Season 2 premiere recaps how SWOJ "raced against the biggest players in the Waterdeep criminal underworld to uncover the hidden Vault of Dragons" and recovered the stolen gold
