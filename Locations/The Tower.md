# The Tower

A tall stone structure at the center of the Dynamo's pocket dimension, covered in vines controlled by the Body Taker. The climactic battle takes place at its summit. Surrounded by water at its base.

**Type:** 
**First appearance:** Episode S02E04
**Total appearances:** 1 episodes

## Notable Events




- **S01E05:** Find Nat in the tower clutching the talisman; she gives it to the party
- **S01E05:** Children find a glowing talisman (shriveled hand clutching a heart) under the tower floorboards, triggering the haunting
- **S01E23:** Find Squiddly with Urstul Floxin near the tower exit
- **S01E23:** Cast Shatter to destroy the tower's shield rune
- **S02E04:** SWOJ rides Craw and Gaw up the tower to confront the Body Taker
- **S02E04:** Xae wild shapes into a velociraptor and delivers the killing blow, sending the Body Taker off the tower
- **S02E20:** Ylka discovers the star staircase descending to a Raven Queen shrine beneath the tower
