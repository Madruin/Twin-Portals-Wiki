# Waterdeep

The City of Splendors, the biggest city on the Sword Coast. Population approximately 300,000. Experiencing an uptick in gang violence, especially in the Dock Ward and Trade Ward. Post-war economy after the dragon wars four years prior.

**Type:** city
**First appearance:** Episode S01E01
**Total appearances:** 7 episodes

## Description

The City of Splendors, the biggest city on the Sword Coast. Population approximately 300,000. Experiencing an uptick in gang violence, especially in the Dock Ward and Trade Ward. Post-war economy after the dragon wars four years prior.

## Notable Events




- **S01E03:** Party falls through a green portal into a psychic/illusory version of Waterdeep that smells of cherry
- **S01E04:** Meet Mara, reporter for Waterdeep Wazoo newspaper
- **S01E10:** Jarlaxle explains Bregan D'aerthe's goal to establish drow in Waterdeep
- **S01E14:** Time speeds up: watch Waterdeep grow from settlement to city over centuries
- **S01E14:** Scoot, Ylka, and Lark are transported to prehistoric Waterdeep during Winter Solstice
- **S01E20:** Discussion of whether to incorporate real-world pandemic events into the Waterdeep story (tongue in cheek)
- **S01E24:** SWOJ washes out at the mud flats on Waterdeep's west coast
- **S01E25:** The Stone transports SWOJ's consciousness 500 years into the past to 992 DR Waterdeep
- **S01E25:** They meet Rosetta Stonemeroe, a dwarven historian, in ancient Waterdeep
- **S01E29:** SWOJ visits God Catcher statue and discovers the central figure is Halaster Blackcloak, founder of Waterdeep
- **S01E29:** SWOJ flies on dragons Zellophane and Aurinax to Castle Waterdeep
- **S02E05:** Antediluvian Jake teleports everyone to Waterdeep via teleportation circle
- **S02E05:** Volo returns and explains the war between Waterdeep and Neverwinter over Cassalanter diamond mines
- **S02E06:** SWOJ departs Waterdeep northward with Ziraj and Craw, stopping at Froon's for health potions
- **S02E06:** Hare Company yields and reveals they deserted after Waterdeep's defeat at Red Larch
- **S02E14:** Jarlaxle provides political exposition: Neverember rules from Hall of Justice, war with Waterdeep continues, the Masks control city affairs, Renaer leads the resistance
- **S02E20:** Final war council reviews threats: Waterdeep (dragons), Luskan (barbarians), Amphail (army), Cult of the Dragon
- **S02E25:** Dream scenario: battle of the bands in Waterdeep against Bregan D'aerthe and others
- **S02E31:** Zardos Zord appears at the docks and agrees to carry messages to Waterdeep requesting reinforcements
- **S02E38:** SWOJ travels by caravan from the Spiral Cavern to Waterdeep alongside refugees, with reports of mysterious portals opening across the Sword Coast
