# Private Study (Sanctum)

The Private Study is the deepest chamber in [[The Necromancer]]'s [[Necromancer's Extra-Dimensional Sanctum|extra-dimensional sanctum]], located at the bottom of a second descending staircase below the [[Reading Room (Sanctum)|Reading Room]]. It served as the Necromancer's personal workspace for arcane rituals and contained his most guarded possessions, including the [[Mask of the Cultivator]].

**Type:** room
**Location:** [[Necromancer's Extra-Dimensional Sanctum]], beneath [[Kolat Towers]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E23 - Episode Twenty Three|Episode S01E23]]

## Description

The study is a richly appointed room furnished with purple couches, a plush lavender carpet, and slit windows revealing the surrounding void. Books and arcane materials cover the space, marking it as a site for advanced magical study and ritual work. The room is distinctly personalized to [[The Necromancer]]'s tastes.

At the center of the room stands a pedestal vault sealed with an arcane rune-inscribed lock, requiring both a key and knowledge of the magical glyphs to open. On the far side of the room, a menacing suit of armor stands over a lectern holding a large black spell book, gripping a red-and-gold great axe in a decorative pose. The armor is in fact an animated guardian trap -- touching the spell book triggers the construct to attack with the great axe. [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] tested the axe before the trap was sprung and found it locked in an iron grip.

When [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] touched the spell book on the lectern, the animated armor attacked. The party managed to restrain it with [[Xae|Xaerine]]'s magic net while [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] picked the vault's arcane lock. Inside the vault's drawer, he found a ledger recording financial accounts for the Thann and Irmbrusk noble families. A bookmark inside listed five names: Mert, Coralus Thann (checked), Tharduke Starbuckler, Jelen Irmbrusk (checked), and [[Grim Wavesilver]] -- [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]'s father. Behind a false back in the drawer, [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] discovered the [[Mask of the Cultivator]], a plain skeletal face-shaped mask. Putting it on transformed him into an unrecognizable six-foot figure in purple robes with a flat, genderless voice.
