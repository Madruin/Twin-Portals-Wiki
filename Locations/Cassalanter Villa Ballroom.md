# Cassalanter Villa Ballroom

The grand second-floor ballroom of [[Cassalanter Villa]] in [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Sea Ward]], where the Cassalanter family hosts their lavish Founders Day masked ball. A spectacular room with 25-foot ceilings, enormous floor-to-ceiling windows draped in crimson curtains, and a live string and harpsichord ensemble, the ballroom becomes the site of a dramatic cultist attack when figures in red leather armor and golden grinning masks crash through the windows during the soiree.

**Type:** Interior / Event Hall
**Ward:** [[Sea Ward]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E09 - Episode Nine|S01E09]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Description

The Cassalanter Villa Ballroom occupies the second floor of the villa, accessed by a grand staircase from the foyer. The room features 25-foot-high ceilings and is surrounded by huge windows running floor to ceiling, hung with rich red crimson curtains. A live string band and harpsichord provide music for the guests.

During the Founders Day soiree, the ballroom hosts a masked ball where attendees wear gold, silver, and porcelain masks -- a rack of masks is available at the entrance for guests to select from. Nobles and dignitaries perform traditional Waterdhavian dances including waltzes, pavanes, and other courtly forms. Private seating areas line the sides of the ballroom for more intimate conversations. The banquet hall is located in the opposite direction on the same floor.
