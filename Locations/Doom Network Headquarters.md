# Doom Network Headquarters

The secret base of operations for the [[Doom Raiders|Doom Network]] in [[Waterdeep]], where the reformed criminal organization coordinates its activities. [[SWOJ]] visits the headquarters in Season 2 after returning from their three-year absence, receiving the mission to find [[Anya Ermbrusk]], the missing stag lass of [[Amphail]], from [[Skemo]] and other Doom Network operatives.

**Type:** Hideout / Criminal Base
**First appearance:** [[S02E05 - Episode Thirty Five (S02E05)|S02E05]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Description

The Doom Network Headquarters serves as the central command post for the Doom Raiders' operations in Waterdeep. The location is kept secret -- when [[Rowan Crooks]] mentions taking someone to "our secret base," the party reacts with surprise and excitement. The base is secure enough that the Doom Raiders conduct sensitive intelligence briefings and assign missions from within its walls. Access appears restricted, with operatives like [[Radecere]] serving as gatekeepers who are protective of allowing outsiders too close to their operations.

## Notable Events

- **[[S02E05 - Episode Thirty Five (S02E05)|S02E05]]:** [[SWOJ]] visits the Doom Network Headquarters after returning to [[Waterdeep]] following their three-year absence; they receive the mission to travel north and find [[Anya Ermbrusk]], the missing stag lass of [[Amphail]]
- **[[S02E05 - Episode Thirty Five (S02E05)|S02E05]]:** [[Rowan Crooks]] offers to bring [[Fetch]] to the secret base for investigation, sparking a territorial dispute with [[Radecere]] over operational security
- **[[S02E06 - Episode Thirty Six (S02E06)|S02E06]]:** SWOJ departs Waterdeep heading north along the Long Road, having received their job from the Doom Raiders along with leads pointing toward [[Amphail]]; [[Tagga]]'s cure (mothweed from the [[Cryptwood]]) becomes a secondary objective for the journey
