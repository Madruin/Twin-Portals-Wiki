# Resistance Base

A natural earthen cavern beneath Castle Never's dungeons, where a small creek flows underground. Contains workstations for artisans, maps, books, and a war room. Accessible only through unmarked tunnels with small wall markings. Home to Renaer and various resistance members.

**Type:** hidden base
