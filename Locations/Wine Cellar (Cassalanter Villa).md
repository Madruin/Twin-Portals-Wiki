# Wine Cellar (Cassalanter Villa)

The **Wine Cellar** is a large rectangular room in the basement of [[Cassalanter Villa]], filled with shelves and wine casks. It contains a hidden passage leading deeper underground toward the [[Shrine Of Asmodeus (Cassalanter Villa)|Shrine of Asmodeus]]. [[SWOJ]] explored it during their daytime infiltration in [[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four|S01E24]].

## Description

A big rectangular room lined with shelves and at least 30 large casks of wine along the south wall, with varying levels of dust. The cellar is accessed from an anti-chamber at the bottom of the basement stairs, through a door to the right (slightly ajar). The room is unnaturally cold for a wine cellar. Along the far end of the south wall, a secret stone door is concealed in the brickwork, identifiable by a draft blowing through its outline and a notch in a nearby rock that serves as the release mechanism. A large sun symbol of the goddess [[Siamorphe]] is painted next to the opening mechanism. The door swings open quietly on top and bottom hinges, revealing a hallway and stairs descending further underground toward the [[Lower Vault (Cassalanter Villa)|Lower Vault]] and ultimately the shrine.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four|S01E24]]:** [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] entered the wine cellar with the [[Cassalanter]] children ([[Terenzio Cassalanter|Terenzio]] and [[Elzerina Cassalanter|Elzerina]]) during a game of hide and seek. His sailor instincts (rolling a 23 on perception) immediately detected a draft and identified the secret door. While [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] picked the locks on the main [[Vault (Cassalanter Villa)|vault]] door across the anti-chamber, Scoot distracted the children with a sleight-of-hand magic show, then opened the secret passage behind their backs. The children were excited about the discovery and crept down cautiously. Scoot also stashed bottles of wine into his bag of holding.
