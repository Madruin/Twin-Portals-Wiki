# Library (Cassalanter Villa)

The **Library** is a richly adorned second-floor room in [[Cassalanter Villa]], filled with books, magical music jars, and a large portrait of the [[Cassalanter]] patriarchs. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] explored it during SWOJ's daytime infiltration in [[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four|S01E24]], using detect magic to investigate its contents.

## Description

A grand, Beauty-and-the-Beast-style library on the second floor of the villa, accessed through large open double doors off the main hallway near the ballroom. The room smells of old parchment and features tall shelves of colorful leather-bound books with rolling ladders for reaching the higher shelves. A 10-foot tall renaissance-style portrait of [[Victoro Cassalanter|Victoro]] and [[Amalia Cassalanter|Amalia]] dominates one wall, its eyes seeming to follow viewers as they move around the room. On the southwest shelf sit about ten glass jars containing swirling lights, each corked and labeled with names like "Canticle of the Silver Chalice," "Garen's Dragon Ward," "Your Beardy Face," and "Wolfgar and the Crystal Shard." These jars radiate faint evocation and divination magic and hum, buzz, or rattle faintly when undisturbed.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four|S01E24]]:** [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] went upstairs specifically seeking a library and found it on the second floor. She cast detect magic and spent up to 10 minutes exploring, climbing ladders and scanning the shelves. The main magical items were the ten glass jars containing recorded musical performances. When Ylka uncorked the "Canticle of the Silver Chalice" jar, the room filled with the singing of a 30-to-40-person choir performing a hymn to the goddess [[Siamorphe]], praising her calmness and peacefulness. Ylka was overcome by the music and swayed along, lost in the performance. The choral singing was audible throughout the house, which alerted the Cassalanter guards to someone being upstairs. [[Nana]] later mentioned hearing the music jars and suspected someone was hiding in the library.
