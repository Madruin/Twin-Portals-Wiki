# Tagga's Apartment

[[Tagga]]'s modest home in the [[Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]], near [[Candle Lane]], where [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]'s mother lives before relocating to [[Trollskull Manor]]. The apartment becomes the site of a [[Xanathar Guild]] break-in early in Season 1, marking the first time the Guild directly threatens SWOJ's loved ones and setting the stage for Tagga's move to the safety of Trollskull Alley.

**Type:** Residence
**Ward:** [[Dock Ward]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]
**Total appearances:** 3 episodes

## Description

Tagga's apartment is a small dwelling in the Dock Ward, described as a cramped space that Scoot barely inhabits due to his sailor lifestyle. The building sits near [[Candle Lane]] in one of Waterdeep's rougher neighborhoods. Neighboring residents include [[Bacto And Juba|Bacto and Juba]], a tabaxi couple and musicians who play at all hours -- neighbors Tagga secretly enjoys despite complaining about the noise.

The apartment is humble but functional, reflecting Tagga's practical nature. When SWOJ first visits, Tagga brings a massive homemade pie, establishing the domestic warmth that defines her character throughout the campaign.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]:** [[Tagga]] arrives at the apartment bearing a massive homemade pie and meets the rest of [[SWOJ]] for the first time, sharing useful local information about the [[Dock Ward]] neighborhood
- **[[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]]:** After receiving a warning arrow signed "Y" that they are being followed, SWOJ rushes to check on Tagga; they find the apartment door open, the interior ransacked, and crow feathers scattered about -- the [[Xanathar Guild]]'s calling card
- **[[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]]:** An amethyst [[sending stone]] is discovered at the ransacked apartment, a clue left behind during the Guild's search
- **[[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]]:** SWOJ learns that [[Pinker]], a kenku ally, rescued Tagga from the Xanathar agents before they could harm her and hid her in a smuggler's closet inside the [[Honorable Knight]] statue tenements
- **[[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]:** With the apartment no longer safe from Xanathar Guild reprisals, Tagga agrees to relocate to [[Trollskull Manor]] to help run the tavern, permanently leaving the Dock Ward residence behind
