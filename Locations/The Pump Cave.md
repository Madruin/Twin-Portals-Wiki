# The Pump Cave

A hidden cave on the cliffside near the [[High Road]], roughly a day's travel north of the [[Mere of Dead Men]]. The cave contains a magical pumping device operated by [[Cult of the Dragon]] members that drains swamp water from the Mere, likely to expose underground ruins and the [[Underground Obsidian Fortress]] beneath [[Castle Naerytar]]. [[SWOJ]] discovered it while traveling south from [[Neverwinter]] on a dragon-hunting mission.

**Type:** Cave
**Region:** [[High Road]], near the [[Mere of Dead Men]]
**First Appearance:** [[S02E27]]

## Description

The cave sits about twenty feet below the edge of a sea cliff, accessed by a rickety wooden staircase that switchbacks down the cliff face. The cave mouth is roughly fifteen feet wide, with a five-foot-by-five-foot stream of brackish brown water blasting out of the cliff side from the pump's action. A narrow path runs alongside the water channel into the cave interior.

Inside, a ten-foot magical pump sits in the water, damming it behind the device and shooting it out at high pressure. The water contains very fine gray clay characteristic of swamp beds, confirming it originates from the [[Mere of Dead Men]]. The pump is tended by three robed figures wearing dragon masks -- one red, one white, and one blue -- who periodically make arcane gestures over the apparatus to rev it up. The cave extends into a tunnel behind the pump, eventually connecting to the swamp itself.

The pumping sound carries on the wind as a rhythmic whooshing mixed with a mechanical pumping cadence, audible from the [[High Road]] above.

## Notable Events

- **Xae's Reconnaissance:** [[Xae]] transformed into a raven and scouted the cliff edge, discovering the cave and its occupants. She observed the three masked cultists operating the pump before flying back to report. She correctly deduced that the cultists were draining the [[Mere of Dead Men]] to access something beneath the swamp ([[S02E27]]).
- **Scoot's Diplomatic Approach:** [[Scoot]] descended the rickety staircase and attempted a friendly greeting, announcing his presence by shuffling his feet and calling out "Hello." The white-masked cultist was somewhat talkative, but the blue-masked one was stone-cold hostile and the red-masked one hissed. When threatened, [[Scoot]] backed away with arms wide ([[S02E27]]).
- **Swamp Water Analysis:** [[Ylka]] identified the fine gray clay in the water as characteristic of swamp beds, confirming the pump was draining the [[Mere of Dead Men]] from miles away. This gave [[SWOJ]] their first clue about the [[Cult of the Dragon]]'s excavation activities ([[S02E27]]).
- **Destruction Debate:** The party debated their options -- [[Ylka]] proposed using Shatter to destroy the pump, collapsing the tunnel to trap the cultists inside with rising floodwater. Others suggested following the water tunnel as an alternate route into the swamp, bypassing any surface dragons. Ultimately, [[SWOJ]] chose to continue south along the [[High Road]] toward [[Castle Naerytar]] ([[S02E27]]).
