## Notable Events




- **S01E29:** Renaer Neverember departs for Neverwinter fearing inter-city conflict
- **S02E05:** Volo returns and explains the war between Waterdeep and Neverwinter over Cassalanter diamond mines
- **S02E14:** Jarlaxle asks SWOJ to put in a good word with Neverwinter's leadership for his trading interests
- **S02E14:** Lark obtains the Neverwinter Nights periodical and reads about the Buzzards adventuring group
- **S02E15:** Jack reveals: Masks send a wizard with an aboleth stone to the chasm monthly on the full moon; a Manshoon clone is involved in Neverwinter politics
- **S02E16:** SWOJ descends into the Neverwinter chasm with the Manshoon clone (Man-two)
- **S02E17:** The chasm erupts catastrophically, releasing demons and magical creatures into Neverwinter
- **S02E18:** The Crown of Neverwinter is found on Lissandra's skeleton -- a mithril crown with silver, red, and blue gemstones
- **S02E18:** SWOJ holds the Crown of Neverwinter, uncertain whether to give it to Dagult or Renaer Neverember; Lark declines to try it on
- **S02E19:** SWOJ emerges from Castle Neverwinter with the Crown of Never and captive Erstel Fluxen
- **S02E19:** SWOJ escapes on Jack and Jill, flying back toward Neverwinter with the crystals
- **S02E20:** SWOJ returns to Neverwinter with the Amphail crystals
- **S02E20:** The chasm-sealing ritual succeeds -- Neverwinter is saved
- **S02E26:** SWOJ exits the Shadowfell through the Shard of Night portal to Neverwinter
- **S02E30:** SWOJ flies to Neverwinter and sees three airships under construction: Hammerhead Destroyer, Ethereal Galleon, and Clockwork Kraken
- **S02E30:** SWOJ gives the spelljammer helm to Little Joan and sends the Ripples to Neverwinter
- **S02E33:** Concord Jewel is set to return automatically so the Neverwinter army can use it
