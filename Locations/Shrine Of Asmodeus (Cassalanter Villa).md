# Shrine Of Asmodeus (Cassalanter Villa)

A massive underground shrine beneath Cassalanter Villa. Features a church-like layout with pews, red carpet, wrought iron candelabras, a 10-foot diameter stone bowl of fire, a 30-foot statue of Asmodeus, crumbling balconies on both sides, bloody cells, and a natural cave opening. The third Eye of the Stone of Golorr was kept on a pedestal in the fire bowl.
