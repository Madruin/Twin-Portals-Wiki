# Shrine Of Asmodeus (Cassalanter Villa)

The **Shrine of Asmodeus** is a massive underground temple hidden beneath [[Cassalanter Villa]], serving as the secret heart of the [[Cassalanter]] family's [[Asmodeus]] worship. [[SWOJ]] discovered it during their infiltration of the villa in [[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four|S01E24]] and fought cultists here to retrieve the third [[Eye of the Stone of Golorr]].

## Description

The shrine is built like an underground church, with a long central aisle flanked by pews and lined with a red carpet. Wrought iron candelabras provide flickering dim light throughout. The walls are decorated with statues and paintings depicting demonic scenes of debauchery and violence. Two crumbling stone balconies overlook the main floor from either side, about 15 feet apart, each connected by staircases to the ground level. At the far end stands a massive 30-foot statue of [[Asmodeus]], a horned figure reaching nearly to the ceiling. At the base of the statue sits a 10-foot diameter stone bowl filled with fire, with a small pedestal at its center where the third Eye was kept. Bloody cells line parts of the space, and a natural crack in the corner wall leads to a cave with an underground pool and stream. The air is warm from the fire, with a persistent draft blowing through the natural opening.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four|S01E24]]:** [[Xae]] scouted the shrine from the balcony and spotted the marble (the third Eye) sitting on the pedestal in the fire bowl, along with three crimson-robed cultists patrolling the hall below. SWOJ split into two groups to flank the cultists. During the ensuing fight, [[Xae]] knocked the Eye off its pedestal with a thrown dagger, and the party killed three Asmodeus cultists (satyr-like creatures). [[Amalia Cassalanter]] then arrived in pursuit, hasted, as the party fled through the natural crack in the shrine wall into an underground cave and stream, escaping with the third Eye.
