# Sea Maiden's Faire

A traveling carnival fleet of three ships operated by [[Zardoz Zord]] (secretly [[Jarlaxle]]) in [[Waterdeep]]'s harbor. One ship has a unicorn figurehead that seemed to look at [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xaerine]]. Fake workers aboard sing in unison. The ships house [[Nim]], a broken automaton, and serve as the base for [[Pog Waggletop]] and the [[Sea Plum Crew]].

**Type:** Fleet / Carnival
**First appearance:** [[S01E03 - Episode Three|S01E03]]

## Description

The Sea Maiden's Faire consists of three ships docked in Waterdeep's harbor, functioning as both a carnival attraction and a front for [[Bregan D'Aerthe]] operations. In the psychic dreamscape version of Waterdeep, the ships appeared crewed by eerily synchronized workers. In the waking world, the Faire served as a meeting place for various contacts and allies.
