# Beast Of Erua

The Beast of Erua was a colossal undead creature that lurched through the [[Shadowfell]], standing at least one hundred feet tall with four castle-like legs and exposed bones where its skin had rotted away. Hundreds of stolen [[Wish Stones|wish stones]] from the [[Brigganocks]] were embedded in its scales, fueling its tireless march. A stone fortress -- the [[Rose Knight's FortressMausoleum|Rose Knight's Fortress]] -- was lashed to its spine with three massive vine-like trunks, and its ribcage housed a laboratory, teleportation circle, and the podling creation operation of one of [[Manshoon]]'s clones. The Beast was originally slain by the legendary hero [[Teriador]] the Rose Knight, but was reanimated through the stolen [[Dynamo Of Possibility]], which was installed in its skull chamber.

**Type:** Creature / Mobile Lair

## Description

The Beast of Erua resembled a skeletal rhinoceros-camel hybrid of mountainous proportions. Its four legs were so massive they looked like castle towers, and large patches of skin were missing, exposing bone and sinew beneath. Strigoi -- bat-like creatures with proboscis tongues -- circled it through the black mist of the Shadowfell. The creature's scales were studded with hundreds of glowing wish stones stolen from the [[Brigganocks]], each one a crystallized unfulfilled wish that powered the Beast's relentless movement.

Atop its spine sat the [[Rose Knight's FortressMausoleum|Rose Knight's Fortress]], a stone edifice overgrown with vines. Inside the [[Beast Of Erua's Ribcage|ribcage]], a vast internal chamber contained a wizard's laboratory, workshop, stone teleportation circle, mulch beds where podlings grew from hands sticking out of the ground, and glowing pods holding captured prisoners. A spiral staircase ascended through the trachea to the skull chamber, where the malfunctioning [[Dynamo Of Possibility]] crackled with green lightning.
