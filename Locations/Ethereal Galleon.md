# Ethereal Galleon

The Ethereal Galleon was one of three airships constructed at the [[Neverwinter Dry Docks]] under [[Renaer Neverember]]'s direction, alongside the [[Hammerhead]] and the Clockwork Kraken. Built using spelljammer helm technology with spider silk rigging contributed by [[Quorlith The Starweaver|Corlith the Starweaver]], the Ethereal Galleon served as [[Silver Waves Of Justice (SWOJ)|SWOJ]]'s primary vessel during the assault on the [[Concord Jewel]] and the [[Radiant Citadel]].

**Type:** Airship / Vehicle
**First appearance:** [[S02E30 - Episode Sixty (S02E30)|S02E30]]

## Description

The Ethereal Galleon was SWOJ's flagship during the final campaign against [[Manshoon]] and the [[Cult Of The Dragon]]. It was crewed by volunteers including [[Squidly]], [[Fel'Rekt Lafeen]], [[Shaleheart]], [[Urstul Floxxin]], and [[Fioleth]], alongside [[Neverwinter Forces|Neverwinter soldiers]].

The ship was sabotaged by [[Krom]] and other agents during a coordinated attack on [[Castle Never]], but was repaired in time for the assault. During the fleet's passage through the thunderstorm over the [[The Moonhole|moonhole]], the Ethereal Galleon survived the periton rider attack that destroyed the [[Hammerhead]]. The ship descended into the [[Spiral Cavern]] and was transported to the [[Ethereal Plane  Deep Ethereal|Deep Ethereal]] via the [[Concord Jewel]], where the [[Radiant Citadel]] appeared as a distant gleaming light.
