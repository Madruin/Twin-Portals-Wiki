# Zhentil Keep

Zhentil Keep is a ruined castle floating on an island at the center of a massive sphere of darkness deep within the [[Shadowfell]]. It served as [[Rowan Crooks]]' secret base of operations, where the souls of the [[Jolly Gents]] and the surrounding Shadowfell were harvested through three giant obelisks to fuel a soul engine. The keep was the site of SWOJ's climactic assault on Crooks and ultimately collapsed when the party destroyed his phylactery, plummeting from the sky and crashing into a canyon below.

**Type:** floating fortress
**Region:** [[Shadowfell]]
**First appearance:** [[S02E20 - Episode Fifty (S02E20)|S02E20]] (revealed by tablet)
**Destroyed:** [[S02E25 - Episode Fifty Five LIVE (S02E25)|S02E25]]

## Description

The keep sits atop a floating island suspended within an enormous sphere of impenetrable darkness. The island itself is anchored by three massive obelisks made of the same material as the [[Soul Stone]], each several stories tall and connected to the island by enormous chains five feet in width. These obelisks channel the souls of the Shadowfell inward, absorbing them in swirling currents of energy that course through the chains and into the fortress. The sphere acts as a gravitational trap, pulling objects toward its center.

The fortress comprises a ruined outer wall encircling several structures: the central keep tower (eight stories tall, with a crumbled upper section), [[The Bailey]] courtyard with its training grounds and armory, a library with collapsed walls, caverns below containing clone tanks and laboratories, and a throne room vault on the upper floors. The keep tower contains [[Rowan Crooks]]' office on the sixth floor adjacent to the throne room, heavily trapped hallways protecting the vault, and a teleportation circle in the Bailey courtyard. Green torches light the walls, and sentries patrol the battlements, though the garrison was relatively modest compared to the fortress's size.

Below the keep lie extensive caverns housing Crooks' secret operations: clone tanks containing suspended copies of Rowan Crooks, notes detailing his travel to the [[Radiant Citadel]], and the [[Hungering Shard]] -- a weapon Crooks planned to use in a broader scheme. [[Friar Truck]] was revealed to have convinced the original Rowan Crooks to stage the initial assault on Zhentil Keep, and a secret clone eventually replaced the real Crooks.

## Notable Events

- **[[S02E20 - Episode Fifty (S02E20)|S02E20]]:** [[Lark]] reads [[Antediluvian Jake]]'s tablet, revealing that the Jolly Gents were killed by [[Manshoon]], their souls harvested by [[Rowan Crooks]], and that his secret base is Zhentil Keep in the Shadowfell. This becomes SWOJ's ultimate destination.
- **[[S02E22 - Episode Fifty Two LIVE (S02E22)|S02E22]]:** SWOJ approaches Zhentil Keep on a flying trash carpet, emerging from the sphere of darkness to see the floating island with its three soul-draining obelisks, chained and hovering in a triangle formation. They land on one of the massive chains and infiltrate the fortress, entering [[The Bailey]] disguised as Jolly Gents. [[Lark]] receives critical intel from [[Ann Jane Flip-Flap]] during a sparring match on the Bridge of Destiny.
- **[[S02E23 - Episode Fifty Three (S02E23)|S02E23]]:** SWOJ infiltrates the keep interior, navigating trapped hallways and discovering [[Rowan Crooks]]' laboratories in the caverns below, including clone tanks with suspended copies of Crooks and notes about his plans involving the [[Hungering Shard]] and the [[Radiant Citadel]].
- **[[S02E24 - Episode Fifty Four (S02E24)|S02E24]]:** [[Rowan Crooks]] returns to the Bailey and addresses roughly 50 assembled Jolly Gents near the soul engine. SWOJ confronts Crooks and the battle escalates. The keep's structure begins to fail as SWOJ targets the obelisks and the soul engine, triggering a catastrophic sky-falling battle sequence as Zhentil Keep breaks apart.
- **[[S02E25 - Episode Fifty Five LIVE (S02E25)|S02E25]]:** SWOJ defeats [[Rowan Crooks]] atop the crumbling fortress and traps him in the [[Soul Stone]]. The destruction of Crooks' phylactery causes the keep to lose whatever magic held it aloft, and Zhentil Keep plummets from the sky and crashes into a canyon. A few surviving Jolly Gents are found in the rubble. SWOJ stands in the ruins as the Jolly Gents arc reaches its conclusion.
