# Underground Caverns

A network of natural tunnels and chambers beneath [[Zhentil Keep]], accessed via a spiral staircase hidden behind the throne room through a secret door. The caverns extend seven stories down from the main floor, growing wetter and danker as they descend, eventually opening into a narrow stone tunnel. The network contains multiple distinct areas including the trash room, a bioluminescent mushroom cavern, and passages leading deeper into the floating castle's infrastructure.

**Type:** Dungeon / Cavern Network
**Region:** [[Zhentil Keep]] (within the [[Shadowfell]])
**First Appearance:** [[S02E23]]

## Description

The staircase down winds through solid stone for seven stories before opening into narrow, very wet, untraveled dungeon-style tunnels. The first door off the main passage leads to the trash room -- a garbage-filled chamber inhabited by a contented [[Otyugh]] that fed on the refuse. Beyond, the main tunnel curves gently downward for several hundred feet before opening into a large cavern dimly lit by bioluminescent fungi in blue, pink, and green hues.

The mushroom cavern contains an underground lake of very clear, still water, surrounded by fields of sentient mushrooms that communicate through spores. The fungi are carnivorous given enough time -- anything that lingers eventually becomes food -- but they respond to [[Xae]]'s Speak with Plants spell, cheerfully declaring "We grow! Join us!" A narrow path winds clockwise around the lake, and a mysterious pumping sound emanates from machinery somewhere underwater.

## Notable Events

- **Escape from the Throne Room:** [[SWOJ]] fled down the spiral staircase after their break-in of [[Rowan Crooks]]'s office was noticed by guards. A commanding female voice above ordered the search, not realizing the party had found the secret door behind the throne ([[S02E23]]).
- **The Trash Room Discovery:** The party found the otyugh's garbage chamber first. [[Xae]] sensed the creature was very happy in its home, leading to a brief debate about capturing one for composting, and a more practical discussion about using otyugh poison against dragons ([[S02E23]]).
- **Talking Mushroom Encounter:** [[Xae]] used Speak with Plants to communicate with the bioluminescent fungi. The mushrooms asked her to "destroy the corruption" deeper in the caverns. [[Xae]] used her druidic ability to shift the difficult mushroom terrain into ordinary ground, creating a path. [[Ylka]] tiptoed through on an acrobatics check of 15 ([[S02E23]]).
- **Mud Zombie Ambush:** While investigating the underground lake, [[Ylka]] was yanked into the water by a humanoid figure encased in mud that fell from the saturated ceiling. [[Xae]] used Druidcraft to solidify the mud around [[Ylka]], and [[Scoot]] pulled her free during the subsequent combat ([[S02E23]]).
- **Mushroom Intelligence:** The mushrooms shared that food (living things) passed through regularly but moved too fast for them to catch. They offered to let the party stay and "join" them -- a polite invitation to become nutrients ([[S02E23]]).
