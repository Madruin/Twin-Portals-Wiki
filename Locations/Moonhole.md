# Moonhole

An approximately 80-foot opening in the top of the Sword Mountains that leads several hundred feet down to the spiral cavern. Guarded by hidden archers, ballistae, periton riders, and a blue dragon generating a thunderstorm. The main entry point to reach the Concord Jewel.
