# Grinda's Lighthouse

A weathered lighthouse at the far end of [[Mistshore]], home to the eccentric antiquarian [[Grinda Garloth]]. The building doubles as a personal workshop and storage facility for rare magical artifacts, most notably the [[Apparatus of Kwalish]] -- a lobster-shaped submersible capable of deep-sea exploration. The lighthouse sits at the point where [[Deepwater Harbor]] curves around, surrounded by the run-down shanties and repurposed ship wreckage that define the Mist Shore neighbourhood. Inside, a large wooden floor opens mechanically to reveal a hidden dock beneath, allowing the Apparatus to be launched and recovered directly from the water. [[Grinda Garloth]]'s mouse familiar [[Anton]] operates the lever that controls the trapdoor mechanism.

**Type:** lighthouse, workshop
**Region:** [[Mistshore]], [[Waterdeep]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E06 - Episode Six|Episode 6]]
**Total appearances:** 4 episodes

## Description

The lighthouse occupies the very tip of [[Mistshore]], a downturned stretch of harbour lined with wrecks of old ships repurposed into sheds and shanties. The front door opens directly into a cozy interior where [[Grinda Garloth]] greets visitors in her purple robes. The main floor conceals a mechanical trapdoor that splits open to reveal an underground dock and waterway connecting to [[Deepwater Harbor]]. The Apparatus of Kwalish is stored in this dock when not in use. Grinda refers to the establishment as "Grinda Garlott's Antiquities Emporium," though it functions primarily as her home and workshop rather than a shop.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E06 - Episode Six|S01E06]]:** [[SWOJ]] travels to [[Mistshore]] on behalf of [[Zardoz Zord]], delivering a lavender-scented handkerchief to [[Grinda Garloth]] in exchange for use of the [[Apparatus of Kwalish]]. Grinda reveals that someone resembling [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] visited the previous night and borrowed the Apparatus, though Ylka has no memory of this -- the first sign of the [[Ylka Doppelganger]]. [[Anton]] activates the trapdoor mechanism and the party descends into the submersible to explore [[Deepwater Harbor]].
- **[[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]:** After exploring the [[The God Catcher|God Catcher]] cavern and finding the Apparatus still inside, [[SWOJ]] pilots it through the underwater passage back to [[Mistshore]] and docks it at the lighthouse. [[Grinda Garloth]] runs out to greet them and takes custody of the Apparatus again. During the return, she shares lore about aboleths, including one named [[Golorr]].
- **[[S01E10 - Episode Ten|S01E10]]:** [[SWOJ]] discusses the possibility of borrowing the Apparatus again for another dive, noting they kept it far longer than originally agreed and likely owe Grinda for the extended loan. [[Zelifarn]] in human form points out the submersible is now visible beneath [[The Eyecatcher]] at the docks.
- **[[S02E05 - Episode Thirty Five (S02E05)|S02E05]]:** [[Grinda Garloth]] appears at [[Trollskull Manor]] representing the Warlocks and Witches of Waterdeep guild. She offers to install a teleportation circle in the manor's basement and volunteers to do another dive with the Apparatus if [[SWOJ]] can locate the ship they are searching for. She also examines a mysterious silver-glowing object brought back from the [[Shadowfell]], identifying powerful time magic associated with it.
