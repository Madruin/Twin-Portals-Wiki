# Kelemvor's Cathedral

A grand stone cathedral hidden deep beneath [[Castle Naerytar (Surface)|Castle Naerytar]], reached through the halls of [[The Raven Queen]]. It serves as a sacred site of [[Kelemvor]], god of the dead, housing his floating silver scales and acting as a waystation for souls passing to the afterlife. [[SWOJ]] arrived here after completing the divine trials in the [[Shrouded Citadel]] and fought the [[Cult of the Dragon]] for control of the cracked phylactery.

**Type:** Temple
**Region:** Beneath [[Castle Naerytar (Surface)|Castle Naerytar]], [[Mere Of Dead Men]]

## Description

The cathedral is a vast, ancient hall of worked stone with rubble strewn around its base, suggesting great age and partial ruin. At its center float [[Kelemvor]]'s silver scales -- a divine artifact that judges and sorts the souls of the dead, diverting them to their proper resting places. When SWOJ entered, the cracked phylactery from the dracolich ritual was also present, and the scales were actively working to redirect trapped dragon souls away from the phylactery's corrupting influence. The chamber is large enough for dragons to burst through its walls and engage in aerial combat.

## Notable Events

- **[[S02E29 - Episode Fifty Nine LIVE (S02E29)|S02E29]]:** After completing the [[Trial Of Jergal]] and [[Trial Of Orcus]] in the [[Shrouded Citadel]], SWOJ becomes incorporeal spirits and rises through the earth. They return to physical form in a hallway of [[The Raven Queen]], then follow it into the cathedral. There they find [[Kelemvor]]'s floating silver scales and help divert trapped souls away from the cracked phylactery.
- **[[S02E29 - Episode Fifty Nine LIVE (S02E29)|S02E29]]:** The two black dragons burst into the cathedral and seize the phylactery. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] casts Shatter and [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] casts Daylight on it, but both are knocked unconscious by acid breath. [[Amalia Cassalanter]]'s disguise as Velia is burned away by moonlight, revealing her true identity. One dragon kills the other to fuel the dracolich transformation. [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] bags the phylactery, but Amalia uses telekinesis to fight for it. Ylka summons allied dragons with the Mask of Dragon Kind. [[The Ripples]] blow open a chasm to the surface, and SWOJ escapes on dragonback while Amalia flees eastward atop the partially transformed dracolich.
