# The Shadowfell

Dark, rain-soaked plane of existence on the other side of the stone border wall from the Feywild. Home of the Beast of Erua, Strigoi, and Brighanox.

**Type:** plane
**First appearance:** Episode S02E01
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Notable Events




- **S02E01:** An earth-shaking roar from the Shadowfell direction aligns with the compass
- **S02E01:** The party eats the magic mushroom (blessed effect) and charges into the Shadowfell
- **S02E02:** Brighanox dig a tunnel to the Shadowfell border for the party
- **S02E20:** Lark reads Antediluvian Jake's tablet: Jolly Gents killed by Manshoon, souls harvested by Rowan Crooks, secret base is Zentil Keep in the Shadowfell
- **S02E20:** SWOJ is launched through the portal and arrives in the Shadowfell
- **S02E22:** SWOJ arrives at Zentil Keep on a flying trash carpet, approaching through the Shadowfell
- **S02E26:** SWOJ exits the Shadowfell through the Shard of Night portal to Neverwinter
