# Brigganock's Tunnels

An underground network of tunnels beneath the borderlands between [[The Feywild]] and [[The Shadowfell]], home to the [[Brigganocks]] -- tiny mouse-like humanoid creatures who collect and preserve [[Wish Stones|wish stones]], crystallized unfulfilled wishes. The tunnels are remarkably well-engineered, with channels dug to catch rainwater and walls festooned with glowing silver and gold crystals. SWOJ was led here for safety after being attacked by [[Strigoi]] on the surface, and learned crucial information about the wish stones fueling the [[Beast Of Erua]] before the Brigganocks dug them a passage into the Shadowfell.

**Type:** Dungeon / Settlement
**First appearance:** [[S02E01 - Episode Thirty One (S02E01)|S02E01]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Description

The tunnels were accessed through a "hidey hole" in the surface that dropped into a glowing cavern. Despite being underground, the space was remarkably dry thanks to carefully engineered drainage channels. The walls shimmered with crystals that cast silver and gold light throughout the low-ceilinged chambers. Long tunnels branched away from the main antechamber, lined with curious Brigganocks carrying tiny pickaxes and accompanied by hovering lights that bounced from creature to creature. The Brigganocks used a chittering one-word language that could be understood through speak with animals magic. The tunnels connected to the Shadowfell border, and the Brigganocks were able to dig new passages when needed.
