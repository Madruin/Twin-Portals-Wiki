# Fields Of Glory

The **Fields of Glory** is a giant colosseum in [[Waterdeep]], described as one of the city's most famous landmarks. It is located in the [[Sea Ward]] along Lion Street, near the city watch post, and hosts city-sanctioned gladiatorial trials that draw large audiences.

**Type:** Landmark / Arena
**First appearance:** [[S01E10 - Episode Ten|Episode 10]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

The Fields of Glory is a massive colosseum situated prominently in the Sea Ward of Waterdeep. It hosts large-scale gladiatorial trials that are officially sanctioned by the city, making them a popular spectacle that draws big audiences. The events are described as not particularly bloody, suggesting they are more sport than blood sport. The arena sits along Lion Street, near the city watch post, making it a major landmark used for navigation in the city.

## Notable Events

- **Investigating the Cult of Asmodeus** ([[S01E10 - Episode Ten|Episode 10]]): After the [[Cult of Asmodeus]] attacked the [[Cassalanter Villa|Cassalanter]] event, the cultists were arrested and taken to the city watch post on Lion Street near the Fields of Glory. [[Victoro Cassalanter]] told [[SWOJ]] that the guard station was located on Lion Street near the Fields of Glory, and offered to use his influence to get them access to interrogate a captured cultist -- a woman who had previously tried to kill [[Lark]]. [[Terry]] later drove the party down [[Del Zorn Street]] into the Sea Ward, passing the Fields of Glory on the way to the watch post, which was a towering building with small windows just beyond the arena. [[Willifort Crowell]] was waiting for them at the door.
