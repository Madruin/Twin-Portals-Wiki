# Waterdeep Sewers

The Waterdeep Sewers are a vast underground tunnel network running beneath the city of [[Waterdeep]], serving as both a municipal drainage system and a hidden thoroughfare for criminal organizations. The [[Xanathar Guild]] maintains its lair deep within the sewers, and the tunnels are also home to the [[Shard Shunners]] wererat gang. [[SWOJ]] has ventured into the sewers on multiple occasions -- first to rescue [[Floon Blagmaar]], later to investigate disappearances near the [[Spouting Fish]], and eventually to access the [[Xanathar's Lair]] itself.

**Type:** Underground tunnel network
**First appearance:** [[S01E02 - Episode Two|Episode S01E02]]
**Total appearances:** 4 episodes

## Description

The sewers form an extensive labyrinth beneath Waterdeep's streets, with entrances accessible from various locations across the city. The tunnels are marked with yellow signs that lead toward the [[Xanathar Guild]]'s territory -- a detail revealed by the captured kenku [[Pinker]] in S01E02. The [[Xanathar Guild]] symbol, a circle with ten spokes, appears on the sewer walls near areas under their control. The tunnels connect to larger underground complexes including the [[Xanathar's Lair]] and [[Skullport]], and also link to underwater passages leading to caverns beneath the [[The God Catcher|God Catcher]]. Behind [[Trollskull Manor]], a sewer entrance leads to rat-infested tunnels used by the [[Shard Shunners]].

## Notable Events

- **Rescue of Floon Blagmaar** ([[S01E02 - Episode Two|S01E02]]): [[SWOJ]] entered the sewers following [[Pinker]]'s clue to "follow the yellow signs." They found [[Nihiloor]], a mind flayer, torturing [[Floon Blagmaar]] and defeated an intellect devourer before rescuing Floon.
- **Spouting Fish disappearances** ([[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]): Investigating missing persons from the [[Spouting Fish]] tavern, [[SWOJ]] descended into the sewers beneath the establishment. They discovered [[Xanathar Guild]] symbols on the walls and fought three grell -- floating brain creatures with paralyzing tentacles. [[The Black Viper]] appeared to assist them, and they rescued approximately fifteen captives including [[Harold]].
- **Trollskull sewer exploration** ([[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]): [[SWOJ]] explored the sewers behind [[Trollskull Manor]], following rat tracks through the tunnels to an underwater passage. This route led to a cavern beneath the [[The God Catcher|God Catcher]], where they found the [[Apparatus of Kwalish]] and encountered [[Dasher Zuber]] and the [[Shard Shunners]] wererat gang.
- **Infiltration of the Xanathar's Lair** ([[S01E16 - Episode Sixteen|S01E16]]): [[Remallia Haventree]] led [[SWOJ]] and guest character [[Thaelin]] through the sewers to a hidden entrance into the [[Xanathar's Lair]]. The party descended into the [[Hall Of Eyes]] to steal the Eye of the [[Stone of Golorr]].
