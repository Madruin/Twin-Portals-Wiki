# Quorlith's Cave

A large cave near the docks of [[Neverwinter]] where [[Quorlith The Starweaver|Quorlith the Starweaver]], a massive giant spider with teal and peacock-feather coloring, nests with her eggs.

**Type:** cave
**Location:** Cliffside near the [[Neverwinter]] docks
**First appearance:** Episode S02E31
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Description

The cave mouth is set into the mountainside cliffs near the [[Neverwinter]] docks, noticeably larger than the other caves in the area and heavily covered in spiderwebs. It extends at least thirty feet inward with a slight bend before opening into [[Quorlith The Starweaver|Quorlith]]'s nesting area. The cave connects to deeper tunnels that run further into the mountain, though a cave-in blocked [[Quorlith The Starweaver|Quorlith]] from pursuing the egg thief who fled through them.

[[Quorlith The Starweaver|Quorlith the Starweaver]] is a massive spider with teal and peacock-feather coloring, dozens of eyes the size of beach balls, who communicates through clicking sounds and waving her spinnerets and legs. She is an ally of [[SWOJ]] and the people of [[Neverwinter]], though her cave is given a wide berth by the dock workers.

## Notable Events

- During Episode S02E31, a fire broke out at the [[Neverwinter]] docks. While [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] helped organize bucket brigades, [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] noticed the webbed cave mouth and heard frantic clicking from inside -- recognizing it as [[Quorlith The Starweaver|Quorlith]].
- [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] entered the cave with dancing lights and found [[Quorlith The Starweaver|Quorlith]] about thirty feet in, in a panicked state. Using speak with animals, [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] learned that a man in black had set the fire as a distraction and stolen a bundle of [[Quorlith The Starweaver|Quorlith]]'s eggs -- ranging from small to about two feet in size.
- The egg thief had fled deeper into the tunnels, causing a cave-in behind him to prevent [[Quorlith The Starweaver|Quorlith]] from following, as she was too large to fit through the collapsed passage.
- [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] cleared the cave-in, allowing [[Quorlith The Starweaver|Quorlith]] to fill the tunnel behind the party as they pursued the thief deeper into the mountain.
- The party caught up to the egg thief, who threatened to destroy the eggs with a volatile orange vial. After a tense standoff, the party recovered the eggs and captured the thief using polymorph (turning him into a beetle in a jar). Interrogation revealed he was working for operatives based out of [[Amphail]], sent to steal a stone from the castle and bring it to the [[Spiral Cavern]].
