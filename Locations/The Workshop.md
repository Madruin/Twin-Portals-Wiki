# The Workshop

The Workshop is a work area discovered inside the ribcage of a massive creature, situated opposite the laboratory across the ribs. It contained the tools and research materials of whatever entity had been conducting experiments within the creature's remains.

**Type:** Dungeon / Work Area
**Location:** Inside the ribcage, [[Kryptgarden Forest]] area
**First appearance:** [[S02E03 - Episode Thirty Three (S02E03)|Episode S02E03]]

## Description

The Workshop sits on one side of the ribcage interior, directly across from a magical laboratory on the other side. It contains desks, shelves, compounds, and notes -- the working materials of an unknown researcher or alchemist. The area around both the Workshop and the laboratory features disturbing mulch beds: piles of dirt and refuse with hands sticking out of them, and bodies slowly growing upward from the wrists as if being cultivated like plants, reminiscent of the opening credits of Westworld.

## Notable Events

- **[[S02E03 - Episode Thirty Three (S02E03)|S02E03]]:** [[Xae]], scouting ahead in animal form, discovers the Workshop while exploring the ribcage interior. She spots it across the ribs from a magical laboratory that contains an overgrown teleportation circle. The party fights off animated giant hands and other constructs growing from the mulch beds throughout the area. [[Lark]] takes fire bolt shots at the animated hands while [[Xae]] provides healing with Balm of the Summer Court.
