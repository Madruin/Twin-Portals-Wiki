# Hammerhead

The Hammerhead Destroyer was one of three airships constructed at the [[Neverwinter Dry Docks]] under [[Renaer Neverember]]'s direction, alongside the [[Ethereal Galleon]] and the Clockwork Kraken. Built using spelljammer helm technology with spider silk rigging contributed by [[Quorlith The Starweaver|Corlith the Starweaver]], the Hammerhead served as part of the [[Neverwinter Military]]'s fleet during the assault on the [[Concord Jewel]] and the [[Radiant Citadel]].

**Type:** Airship / Vehicle
**First appearance:** [[S02E30 - Episode Sixty (S02E30)|S02E30]]

## Description

One of three airships in [[Neverwinter]]'s fleet, the Hammerhead was crewed by [[Neverwinter Forces|Neverwinter soldiers]] in red uniforms. It was destroyed during a devastating attack by [[Malar The Beast Lord's Followers|barbarian periton riders]] while the fleet flew through a thunderstorm en route to the [[Concord Jewel]].

After the fleet reached the [[Radiant Citadel]], the crashed Hammerhead's wreckage came to rest at the bottom of the Citadel, roughly sixty feet from [[Manshoon]]'s combined soul pylon. [[Sholeh]], an ancient brass dragon, guarded surviving soldiers near the wreckage. In the final battle, [[SWOJ]] used the crashed Hammerhead as a battering ram against the soul pylon, with [[Zelifarn]]'s lightning breath powering up the spelljamming helm.
