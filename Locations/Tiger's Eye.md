# Tiger's Eye

A private investigation office on [[Trollskull Alley]] run by [[Vincent Trench]], a mysterious figure who is secretly a rakshasa with a tiger's face beneath his human disguise. The Tiger's Eye is one of the key businesses on the alley and becomes an important resource for SWOJ throughout their adventures, serving as a place to gather intelligence, hire investigative services, and shelter during dangerous times.

**Type:** Shop (Private Investigator's Office)
**Region:** [[Trollskull Alley]], [[Waterdeep]]
**First Appearance:** [[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]

## Description

The Tiger's Eye has no windows and minimal exterior markings -- just a large black-painted sign with two orange cat's eyes on it. Inside, the decor is unexpectedly lavish: velvet chairs, tasseled curtains, paintings, exotic hookahs, and a tiger's pelt on the ground. Despite looking more like a lounge than an office, [[Vincent Trench]] operates it as a legitimate private investigation business, offering his services to paying clients. The office sits near [[Corellon's Crown]] and across from [[Trollskull Manor]], making Trench a constant presence in the alley's daily life. In later episodes, [[Terry]] the awakened horse is seen regularly coming in and out of Trench's office, having developed his own relationship with the detective.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]:** SWOJ discovers the Tiger's Eye while exploring [[Trollskull Alley]] for the first time. They note the mysterious sign with cat's eyes and no windows. Inside, they meet [[Vincent Trench]], who presents himself as a private investigator. [[Fala Lefaliir]] identifies him as her favorite conversationalist. The party considers hiring him or subcontracting through the [[Zuber Family|Zubers]].
- **[[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]:** During the [[Trollskull Alley]] fireball attack on SWOJ's grand opening night, the explosion hits near Vincent Trench's office. [[Jenks]] is found burned but alive against the wall near his door. A witness reports hearing something land in a barrel near Trench's door -- a key piece of evidence in tracking the bomber. SWOJ later consults [[Vincent Trench]] about the attack, and he provides useful intelligence.
- **[[S01E12 - Episode Twelve|S01E12]]:** The episode opens with SWOJ leaving Vincent Trench's office, stepping back into the charred remains of [[Trollskull Alley]] at nighttime as they continue investigating the fireball.
- **[[S01E16 - Episode Sixteen|S01E16]]:** While flying patrol over [[Trollskull Alley]], a member of SWOJ spots a hooded figure on the roof of Vincent Trench's building who appears to be watching them before dematerializing.
- **[[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]]:** SWOJ brings the [[Doom Raiders]] musicians to Vincent Trench's office, cramming six people into the small space. Trench comments on [[Scoot]]'s appreciation of dragon piss. Before SWOJ departs on a dangerous mission, Vincent gives them a stern nod and says he expects to see them again.
- **[[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]]:** During a time skip, [[Terry]] is seen spending time at Vincent Trench's office, coming in and out of the door and developing his own independent reputation as a figure connected to SWOJ.
- **[[S02E08 - Episode Thirty Eight (S02E08)|S02E08]]:** SWOJ encounters [[Vincent Trench]] operating a branch of his private eye business in the [[Radiant Citadel]]. They find his sign hanging above a door with blackout curtains on the main thoroughfare. The party hires him to investigate [[Xae]]'s missing siblings, and he agrees to report back in a tenday. He also answers [[Zelifarn]]'s question about the Preserve of the Ancestors for free. The party bribes him with crab cakes from [[Sid Squid]]'s.
- **[[S02E12 - Episode Forty Two (S02E12)|S02E12]]:** SWOJ notes they need to check back in with [[Vincent Trench]] regarding information about [[Xae]]'s siblings, as the tenday period should be up.
- **[[S02E33 - Episode Sixty Three (S02E33)|S02E33]]:** SWOJ passes by the location of Vincent Trench's office in the [[Radiant Citadel]] while navigating the city. [[Xae]] spots a puff of smoke reminiscent of Trench and notes that his office is on the other side of the building they are investigating.
- **[[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]:** Back in [[Waterdeep]], SWOJ discovers that [[Vincent Trench]] has disappeared from the Tiger's Eye along with [[Fala Lefaliir]] from [[Corellon's Crown]]. His office is empty with no answer at the door, adding to the sense of instability in the city heading into Season 3.
