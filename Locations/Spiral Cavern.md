# Spiral Cavern

A deep underground cave system beneath the Sword Mountains. Contains Arcturia's lair with transmutation experiments, an enchanted mirror, moth weed gardens, and the Concord Jewel vessel. Formed from jutting layers of basalt.

**Type:** cavern, dungeon, underground
**First appearance:** Episode S02E07
**Total appearances:** 5 episodes

## Notable Events




- **S02E07:** SWOJ enters the spiral cavern beneath the Sword Mountains following Farron
- **S02E19:** Erstel Fluxen reveals Manshoon operates from Amphail and commands the Spiral Cavern forces
- **S02E19:** Meeting with Circle of Dusk druids Leah and Shala who provide intel on the Spiral Cavern
- **S02E31:** Interrogation of cicada-Crom reveals Zentarim involvement, Lieutenant Crowell, spiral cavern defenses, and plans to use the Radiant Citadel
- **S02E32:** SWOJ descends into the spiral cavern, spotting cultists loading soul stones around the jewel
- **S02E37:** The damaged Concord Jewel crash-lands in the Spiral Cavern on the Sword Coast, where allies are stationed receiving refugees
- **S02E38:** SWOJ travels by caravan from the Spiral Cavern to Waterdeep alongside refugees, with reports of mysterious portals opening across the Sword Coast
