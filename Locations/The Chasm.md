# The Chasm

A great rift in the southeast part of Neverwinter that opened when Mount Hotenow erupted 30 years ago. Previously spawned demonic horrors before being sealed by Lord Neverember. Rumors of people performing arcane rituals or throwing things into it.

**Type:** landmark
**First appearance:** Episode S02E14
**Total appearances:** 1 episodes

## Notable Events




- **S02E15:** Jack reveals: Masks send a wizard with an aboleth stone to the chasm monthly on the full moon; a Manshoon clone is involved in Neverwinter politics
- **S02E15:** Renaer introduces Fumbles/Fumblewick, a gnome city guard resistance contact at the Slinky Serpent tavern who has accompanied caravans into the chasm
- **S02E16:** The Hungry Elder, an aboleth-like viper fish, is revealed to maintain the chasm's seal using green stones of power
- **S02E17:** The chasm erupts catastrophically, releasing demons and magical creatures into Neverwinter
- **S02E17:** Party pushes toward the chasm district wall and witnesses an enormous kaiju creature
- **S02E19:** Mellafarne explains she needs the Crown of Never plus crystals from Amphail to seal the chasm
- **S02E20:** The chasm-sealing ritual succeeds -- Neverwinter is saved
- **S02E20:** Dagult and Renaer agree to share the Crown of Never for the chasm-sealing ritual
