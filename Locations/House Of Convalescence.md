# House Of Convalescence

A healing facility on the [[Radiant Citadel]], staffed by a dedicated cast of healers who make their quarters near the [[Auroral Diamond]]. The diamond's energy amplifies their healing abilities, allowing them to offer powerful restorative services to the weak and injured. The House of Convalescence is one of the key institutions of the Citadel's egalitarian society.

**Type:** Building (healing facility)
**First appearance:** [[S02E08 - Episode Thirty Eight (S02E08)|S02E08]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Description

The House of Convalescence is a healing institution situated near the [[Auroral Diamond]] at the heart of the [[Radiant Citadel]]. As [[Sax Bandon]] explained to SWOJ upon their arrival, the auroral diamond amplifies healing energy throughout the Citadel, and the healers stationed at the House of Convalescence harness this amplification to treat the sick, wounded, and weak. The facility represents one of the pillars of the Citadel's communal society, where food is largely free and healing is available to all in need.

During the shadow creature crisis that began in [[S02E09 - Episode Thirty Nine (S02E09)|S02E09]], injured citizens were taken to the House of Convalescence for treatment after the attacks at [[Trill]]'s sugar mill in the [[Preserve Of The Ancestors]].
