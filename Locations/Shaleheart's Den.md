# Shaleheart's Den

An underground cavern deep within the [[Neverwinter Chasm]] where [[Shaleheart]] the bulette lives with her three children. SWOJ encountered Shaleheart while descending into the chasm in search of the seal keeping the rift stable. The friendly landshark mother provided the party with a fire-resistant scale for protection against the underground river of fire and gave them directions through the subterranean passages -- cross the river of fire, then ride the river of water.

**Type:** Lair
**First appearance:** [[S02E16 - Episode Forty Six LIVE (S02E16)|S02E16]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

The den was located along the path through the Neverwinter Chasm, past a guard post and an [[Anti-Magic Rune Trap]] that destroyed the [[Manshoon Clone (Man-Two)|Manshoon clone (Man-Two)]] accompanying the party. The cavern was home to Shaleheart and her three young bulette children. Shaleheart was a friendly creature who communicated with the party and offered aid, providing one of her fire-resistant scales as protection and directions through the dangerous underground environment. The den sat along a route that led deeper toward the [[River Of Fire]], the lair of [[Arcanthos]] the ancient red dragon, and ultimately the [[Subterranean Lake]] where the [[The Hungry Elder|Hungry Elder]] maintained the chasm's seal.
