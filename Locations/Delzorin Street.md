# Delzorin Street

A street at the southern end of [[Trollskull Alley]] in the [[North Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]]. Delzorin Street serves as the southern exit from the alley and becomes significant during the investigation of the [[Trollskull Alley]] fireball attack, when a dark figure is seen fleeing south along this route after stealing something from the body of the dead gnome [[Dalekar|Dalcar]].

**Type:** Street
**Region:** [[North Ward]], [[Waterdeep]]
**First Appearance:** [[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]

## Description

Delzorin Street runs along the southern boundary of [[Trollskull Alley]], providing one of the main access routes to the alley from the broader [[North Ward]]. It connects the residential and commercial neighborhood around the alley to the wider street network of [[Waterdeep]]. During the fireball investigation, multiple witnesses and party members reference the street as the escape route used by suspicious figures fleeing the scene.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]:** After the catastrophic fireball explosion devastates [[Trollskull Alley]] during the grand opening of [[Trollskull Manor|The Short Rest]], an eyewitness reports seeing a dark figure in black leather kneel over the body of a dead gnome ([[Dalekar|Dalcar]]), take something from the body, and flee south toward Delzorin Street. The [[Trollskull Alley Irregulars]] later confirm they followed the burned [[Zhentarim]] agent south and saw him meet up with [[Hrabbaz]], who took him into [[Gralhund Villa]] through the back door. SWOJ tells the city watch they intend to pursue the figure who fled south.
- **[[S01E12 - Episode Twelve|S01E12]]:** The investigation continues from the previous episode. SWOJ heads north from [[Trollskull Alley]] to find [[Renaer Neverember]], while the trail of evidence from the Zhentarim agent who fled south via Delzorin Street leads the party to the [[Gralhund Villa]] and [[Saradune Street]], uncovering the connection between the Gralhund family and the fireball attack.

## Connected Characters
