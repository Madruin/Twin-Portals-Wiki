# Docks Ward Guardhouse (Jail Cell)

> **This location is part of the [[Docks Ward Guardhouse]].** See that page for full details on SWOJ's imprisonment, the Stone of Golorr visions, and the trial presided over by [[Laeral Silverhand]].

The jail cells are located in the basement of the [[Docks Ward Guardhouse]] in [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Dock Ward]]. [[SWOJ]] was held here after their arrest by [[Captain Hyustus Stagget]]. While imprisoned overnight, the [[The Stone of Golorr]] activated, sending [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]], [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]], and [[Lark]] on a shared vision journey through the prehistory of Waterdeep, while [[Xae]] remained asleep.
