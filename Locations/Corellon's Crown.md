# Corellon's Crown

An herbalist shop on [[Trollskull Alley]] run by [[Fala Lefaliir]], located next to [[Trollskull Manor]]. Corellon's Crown is one of the first businesses SWOJ encounters when they take ownership of the manor and begin building their community on the alley. [[Fala Lefaliir|Fala]] is a druid and skilled herbalist who becomes a trusted neighbor and friend to the party.

**Type:** Shop (Herbalist/Apothecary)
**Region:** [[Trollskull Alley]], [[Waterdeep]]
**First Appearance:** [[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]

## Description

Corellon's Crown sits directly adjacent to [[Trollskull Manor]] on [[Trollskull Alley]], making it the closest business to SWOJ's home base. The shop sells herbs, potions, and remedies, and is run by [[Fala Lefaliir]], who introduces herself as an herbalist when SWOJ first explores the alley. Fala mentions that her favorite conversationalist is [[Vincent Trench]] from the [[Tiger's Eye]] next door. The shop serves as an anchor of the Trollskull Alley community alongside the other local businesses.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]:** SWOJ meets [[Fala Lefaliir]] for the first time while exploring [[Trollskull Alley]]. She introduces herself and her shop, and the party learns that Corellon's Crown sits right next to the manor. Fala identifies [[Vincent Trench]] at the [[Tiger's Eye]] as her favorite conversationalist.
- **[[S01E10 - Episode Ten|S01E10]]:** SWOJ visits Corellon's Crown seeking healing supplies after a rough outing. They find the shop shuttered and closed, suggesting Fala keeps limited hours. The party refers to it as "the apothecary" when planning their route through the neighborhood.
- **[[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]:** During the [[Trollskull Alley]] fireball attack, [[Fala Lefaliir]] witnesses someone fleeing the scene from her position at Corellon's Crown. She reports to SWOJ that she saw a figure running south after the explosion. A bystander named [[Jenks]] is found injured near [[Vincent Trench]]'s door after the blast, and Fala's eyewitness account helps SWOJ begin tracking the bomber.
- **[[S02E07 - Episode Thirty Seven (S02E07)|S02E07]]:** SWOJ discovers that [[Fala Lefaliir|Fala]] has been taken from Corellon's Crown, kidnapped from the herbalism shop as part of a larger pattern of disappearances. The party speculates that her druid nature may have made her a target, and they resolve to bring her back.
- **[[S02E08 - Episode Thirty Eight (S02E08)|S02E08]]:** SWOJ confirms their intention to rescue [[Fala Lefaliir|Fala]] and bring her back to Corellon's Crown when they return to Waterdeep.
