# Wavesilver Household

The Wavesilver Household is the family home of the Wavesilver noble family in [[Waterdeep]], a minor noble house involved in the shipping trade. It is the childhood home of [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]], who ran away as a teenager to live at [[The Splintered Stair]] with [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]. After the events of Season 1, [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] reconciled with his parents and returned to the household, where his mother [[Esvelle Wavesilver|Isvel]] trained him in the arts of vigilantism.

**Type:** residence
**First appearance:** [[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|Episode S01E29]]

## Description

The Wavesilver family is a minor noble house in Waterdeep whose fortunes are tied to the shipping business. Early in the campaign, [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]'s father [[Grim Wavesilver]] was described as stern and demanding, charging exorbitant interest on family credit. By the end of Season 1, however, the family's shipping business was booming -- pirate attacks had slowed, weather had improved, and the household was seeing rapid prosperity. The Wavesilvers commissioned a new trade vessel named the Silver Plum in honor of SWOJ's heroic efforts. [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]'s mother, [[Esvelle Wavesilver|Isvel]], secretly operated as the [[Black Viper]], a vigilante heroine, and was eager to pass on her skills to her son.
