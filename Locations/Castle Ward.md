# Castle Ward

The wealthiest and most heavily guarded district of [[Waterdeep]], Castle Ward is the seat of the city's government and home to its most powerful institutions. It contains [[Castle Waterdeep]], the [[Palace Of Waterdeep|Palace of Waterdeep]], the [[The God Catcher|God Catcher]] statue, and the [[Yawning Portal]] tavern, making it the backdrop for many pivotal moments in SWOJ's adventures.

**Type:** District
**First appearance:** [[S01E01 - Episode One|Episode One (S01E01)]]
**Total appearances:** 6 episodes

## Description

Castle Ward is where the palace and nobility of [[Waterdeep]] reside. It has the highest concentration of city watch guards, making it the safest area of the city. The district houses major landmarks including [[Castle Waterdeep]] where the [[Masked Lords]] convene, the [[The God Catcher|God Catcher]] -- a massive statue shaped like a figure reaching skyward -- and the [[Yawning Portal]] tavern with its well descending into [[Undermountain]]. Beneath the district run the [[Waterdeep Sewers|sewers]], which contain secret entrances to the [[Xanathar's Lair|Xanathar's lair]]. Castle Ward also contains a high-security anti-magic dungeon used for political prisoners.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E01 - Episode One|S01E01]]:** The adventure begins at the [[Yawning Portal]] tavern in Castle Ward, where the four adventurers first meet and are hired by [[Volothamp Geddarm|Volo]] to find [[Floon Blagmaar]]
- **[[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]:** SWOJ discovers they are banned from the [[Yawning Portal]] for 20 days; they investigate the [[Waterdeep Sewers|sewers]] and fight grell with help from [[The Black Viper]]
- **[[S01E06 - Episode Six|S01E06]]:** SWOJ follows smuggler tunnels from [[Deepwater Harbor]] up into the [[The God Catcher|God Catcher]] statue in Castle Ward, where they fight a wererat colony, free imprisoned sailors, and confront an [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] doppelganger performing a blood ritual on the statue's palm
- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** [[Laeral Silverhand]] reveals that a secret stair in the sewers below Castle Ward leads to the [[Xanathar's Lair]], marked by a symbol of a circle with ten spokes; SWOJ is commissioned to infiltrate the lair
- **[[S01E16 - Episode Sixteen|S01E16]]:** [[Remalia Haventree]] leads SWOJ through the Castle Ward sewers to the hidden entrance into the [[Xanathar's Lair]] for the heist to steal the Eye of the [[Stone Of Golorr|Stone of Golorr]]
- **[[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]]:** SWOJ is arrested and imprisoned in a high-security anti-magic dungeon in Castle Ward for about a week before [[Esvelle Wavesilver|the Black Viper]] breaks them out
