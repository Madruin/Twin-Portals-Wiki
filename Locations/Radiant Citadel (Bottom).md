# Radiant Citadel (Bottom)

The underside and base level of the [[Radiant Citadel]], a ruined landscape suspended above [[Kelemvor's Wall]] (the Wall of the Faithless). The bottom of the Citadel served as the staging ground for [[Manshoon]]'s final bid for godhood during the Season 2 finale. A massive crystal spire connects the Citadel's base to the wall below, and the wreckage of the crashed [[Hammerhead]] ship lies scattered across the debris field.

**Type:** Ruins / Battlefield
**Region:** [[Radiant Citadel]]
**First Appearance:** [[S02E35]] (referenced), [[S02E36]] (explored)

## Description

The bottom of the Radiant Citadel is a blasted landscape of wreckage, debris, and ancient ruins. A towering crystal spire extends downward from the Citadel toward [[Kelemvor's Wall]], and gravity behaves strangely near it -- visitors feel their stomachs lurch, and it becomes possible to walk up its surface. At the foot of the spire sits a colossal hundred-foot-tall skeleton on a throne, holding a pair of giant silver scales. The area around the skeleton contains the remnants of an old ashlike ruin city.

The crashed [[Hammerhead]] ship rests roughly sixty feet from the combined soul pylon, its front half still sputtering with residual energy. The ground is marshy, wet, and full of debris from the ongoing battle. [[Sholeh]], the ancient brass dragon, guards the surviving soldiers from the crash near the wreckage. [[Zelifarn]] circles overhead, breathing lightning on enemy forces alongside griffin riders and several pigeons.

## Notable Events

- **Arcturia's Soul Ritual:** [[Arcturia]], [[Xae]]'s long-lost shape-shifting sister, set up a ritual at the base of the giant skeleton to summon an army of imprisoned souls from [[Kelemvor's Wall]]. She carved two large circles beneath the silver scales and channeled blue fire for approximately ten minutes while [[SWOJ]] defended her from [[Manshoon]]'s spectral forces ([[S02E36]]).
- **Ghost Battle at the Spire:** While [[Arcturia]] performed her ritual, [[Manshoon]]'s green spectral forces -- ghosts with soul crystals at their hearts -- attacked. [[Scoot]] raged into melee, [[Xae]] conjured eight woodpeckers to shatter the crystals, and [[Ylka]] cast Spirit Guardians (little pirates with cannons). A bone colossus formed from the giant skeleton's remains, animated by [[Manshoon]]'s necrotic energy ([[S02E36]]).
- **Scoot Throws Xae:** In a memorable combat moment, [[Scoot]] grabbed [[Xae]] and hurled her eighty feet at the bone colossus. [[Xae]] cast Thunderwave from within the creature, and later unleashed Faerie Fire from inside its bony mass. The army of souls eventually tore the colossus apart ([[S02E36]]).
- **Army of the Faithless:** The ritual succeeded, summoning hundreds of warrior spirits from the wall. Their leader, a ten-foot-tall orc woman ghost with a crescent moon earring and a toothy dolphin tattoo, declared "No gods -- we will not be prisoners again!" before leading her forces up the crystal spire alongside [[SWOJ]] ([[S02E36]]).
- **Final Confrontation with Manshoon:** At the base of the Citadel, [[SWOJ]] found [[Manshoon]] channeling the combined soul pylon -- a towering spire of spirits bound in soul stones. [[Scoot]] engaged him directly while [[Xae]], [[Ylka]], and [[Lark]] raced to the crashed [[Hammerhead]] to use it as a battering ram against the pylon. [[Zelifarn]]'s lightning breath was needed to power up the spelljamming helm ([[S02E36]]).
- **Raven Queen's Warning:** Before descending, the [[Raven Queen]] communicated through her ravens atop the Citadel, warning that [[Manshoon]]'s ambition reached even into her domain, and offering [[SWOJ]] power in exchange for eternal fealty -- which they politely declined ([[S02E35]]).
