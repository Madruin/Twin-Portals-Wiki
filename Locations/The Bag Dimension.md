# The Bag Dimension

An infinite demiplane of astral space containing a lattice of floating bags of holding connected by thick ropes (one to two feet wide). Gravity is relative to the ropes. Has faint astral purple light. Anchored by the Bag Man's soul since the first bag of holding was created.
