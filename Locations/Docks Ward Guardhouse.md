# Docks Ward Guardhouse

The Docks Ward Guardhouse is a City Watch station in [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Dock Ward]] where [[SWOJ]] was imprisoned following their arrest by [[Captain Hyustus Stagget]]. The guardhouse contains a basement prison with cells, and served as the site of a dramatic trial presided over by [[Laeral Silverhand]], the Open Lord of Waterdeep herself, after the originally scheduled corrupt magistrate was replaced.

**Type:** City Watch Station / Prison
**Ward:** [[Dock Ward]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|Episode S01E15]]

## Description

The Docks Ward Guardhouse is a City Watch facility with a basement level containing prison cells. The upper level includes an atrium and stairways, while the basement holds the cells where prisoners are kept. The guardhouse served as both a holding facility and impromptu courtroom during SWOJ's trial. [[Captain Hyustus Stagget]] operates out of this station and used his position to secretly warn and assist SWOJ during their imprisonment.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]:** [[Captain Hyustus Stagget]] arrests [[SWOJ]] and takes them to the Docks Ward Guardhouse following their nighttime escapades involving the [[The Stone of Golorr]] and a confrontation with [[House Cassalanter]] agents.
- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** While imprisoned in the basement cells, [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]], [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]], and [[Lark|Lark]] are bewitched by the [[The Stone of Golorr]] and experience a shared vision journey through the prehistory of Waterdeep, while [[Xae]] remains asleep.
- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** [[Captain Hyustus Stagget]] warns [[SWOJ]] in the dead of night that the presiding magistrate for their trial is corrupt, having been paid off by the [[House Cassalanter|Cassalanters]]. He helps them smuggle their bag of holding to [[Terry]] to hide the Stone of Golorr.
- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** The corrupt magistrate Umberto Stonemero is unexpectedly replaced by [[Laeral Silverhand]], the Open Lord of Waterdeep, who presides over the trial. During the proceedings, intellect devourers attack the courtroom, bursting out of witnesses including [[Harold]] from the [[Spouting Fish]]. [[Laeral Silverhand]] removes SWOJ's manacles and they help fight off the creatures.
- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** After the chaos subsides, [[Laeral Silverhand]] effectively frees [[SWOJ]] and takes them to the [[Haventree Estate]], a [[Harpers]] safehouse near the [[The God Catcher|God Catcher]].
