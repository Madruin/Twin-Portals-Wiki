# Zaka's Tomey Tower

Zaka's Tomey Tower (also called Zaka's Tome Tower) is a stone wizard's tower deep in the snowy woods of [[Prehistoric Waterdeep]], belonging to [[Zaka]] Bailey Brew, grandmother of [[Gorge Baileybrew]]. The tower houses Zaka's vast collection of tomes -- mostly books of solstice carols -- and was the site of the climactic battle of the Winter Solstice holiday special when the evil wizard [[Hans Von Gruber]] took it over and held the [[Gorge Baileybrew|Bailey Brew]] family captive inside.

**Type:** tower
**Region:** [[Prehistoric Waterdeep]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]

## Description

The tower is a traditional stone wizard's tower with crenellations on top rather than a pointed roof. It stands about 40 feet tall in a clearing in the woods, roughly 400 feet from where SWOJ encountered the [[Krampus]] in the forest. The entrance consists of thick wooden double doors up a small flight of stairs, with heavy stone knockers. A single window sits about 40 feet up, with warm light visible from within. The stone blocks are old enough to be scalable, with cracks suitable for climbing, though the exterior is covered in snow and ice.

Inside, the tower is a single open space roughly 30 feet in diameter with a vaulting high ceiling and no internal levels. The walls are lined with shelves holding Zaka's tome collection. The interior features a harpsichord, a small throne toward the back, and during SWOJ's visit it was filled with clockwork, glass, and metal toys -- solstice gifts that [[Hans Von Gruber]] had gathered. A magical bell near the throne grants ethereal wings and flight when struck.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]:** [[Gorge Baileybrew]] tells SWOJ that his grandmother [[Zaka]] has a tower deep in the woods and that his sister [[Zuzu]] may have been taken there; the party sets out through the snowy forest to find it
- **[[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]:** SWOJ arrives at the tower following the [[Krampus]]'s blood trail to the double doors; inside they find the Bailey Brew family playing happily with toys under an enchantment, with a figure disguised as [[Grandfather Niklas]] watching from a small throne
- **[[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]:** The disguised grandfather reveals himself as [[Hans Von Gruber]], a gaunt vampire-like wizard with an 80s-style haircut, when SWOJ sees through the deception; he rings the magical bell, gains ethereal wings, and initiates combat
- **[[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]:** A chaotic battle erupts inside the tower: [[Scoot]] fastball-specials [[Lark]] upward toward Von Gruber, [[Ylka]] casts Shatter as cracked solstice bells, the [[Krampus]] summons eight gingerbread pain deer from its satchel, and [[Zaka]] (still enchanted) attacks [[Ylka]] with a candy cane shiv while defending Hans
- **[[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]:** Von Gruber throws the solstice tree down from the ceiling, which shatters his concentration and frees [[Gorge Baileybrew|Gorge]], [[Navi]], and [[Zuzu]] from his charm; Von Gruber flees to the rooftop with his pain deer and sleigh
- **[[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]:** [[Lark]] lands a critical hit with his rapier for 38 damage, knocking Von Gruber off the sleigh and incapacitating him against the crenellations; the battle ends and Von Gruber confesses he was cast out by his clan and only wanted a family
- **[[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]:** [[Lark]] wraps Von Gruber in the Fleece of [[Navi]]'s Dad, melting his frozen tears and warming him; the Bailey Brew family loads the remaining toys into the sleigh and prepares to deliver solstice gifts across the countryside before dawn
