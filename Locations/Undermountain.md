# Undermountain

The legendary mega-dungeon beneath [[Waterdeep]], Undermountain is a vast, seemingly endless labyrinth of tunnels, chambers, and magical anomalies created by the mad wizard [[Halaster Blackcloak]]. Accessible through the well in the [[Yawning Portal]] tavern, it looms as a constant presence beneath the city and becomes central to [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]'s personal quest to free her imprisoned siblings.

**Type:** Dungeon / mega-dungeon
**First appearance:** [[S01E01 - Episode One|Episode One (S01E01)]]
**Total appearances:** 4 episodes

## Description

Undermountain is a massive complex of underground ruins, tunnels, and dungeon levels sprawling beneath [[Waterdeep]]. It was carved from ancient dwarven ruins by [[Halaster Blackcloak]], one of the founders of the city. While [[Waterdeep]] was built upward, Halaster descended downward, forming his own wizardly domain and rarely being seen again. The entrance is the well inside the [[Yawning Portal]] tavern, where [[Durnan]] charges 20 gold for passage down. The dungeon is home to all manner of creatures and serves as the base of operations for Halaster and his apprentices, including [[Arcturia]], [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]'s sister and a powerful transmutation wizard.

- **[[S02E35 - Episode Sixty Five (S02E35)|S02E35]]:** At the [[Wall Of The Faithless]], SWOJ encounters [[Arcturia]], [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]'s sister and a powerful transmutation wizard who works for [[Halaster Blackcloak]] in Undermountain; she was sent by Halaster to break the Wall
- **[[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]:** While visiting the [[Yawning Portal]], [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] hears a mysterious voice from Undermountain saying "come home" on the melody of the wind
