# Vault (Cassalanter Villa)

The **Vault** is a heavily locked and trap-protected room in the basement of [[Cassalanter Villa]], across the anti-chamber from the [[Wine Cellar (Cassalanter Villa)|Wine Cellar]]. Despite its imposing security, the vault was completely empty when [[SWOJ]] broke in during [[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four|S01E24]], serving as a decoy or already-emptied storage.

## Description

A small rectangular chamber of smooth hewn stone, sealed behind a heavy vault door secured with three large iron locks. The door has a big handle next to the middle lock. The vault is protected by a magical cold damage trap that triggers when the door is opened, blasting anyone in range with intense magical cold energy dealing 30 cold damage. Inside, the chamber is completely empty. The vault sits in a basement anti-chamber that is unnaturally cold, accessed by stairs from the main floor. Across the anti-chamber, the [[Wine Cellar (Cassalanter Villa)|wine cellar]] door stands slightly ajar.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four|S01E24]]:** While [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] explored the wine cellar with the children, [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] picked all three iron locks (rolling 15, 21, and 27 on dexterity checks) with [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]'s familiar [[Mimsy]] keeping watch from the doorway. When Lark pulled the vault door open, a blast of magical cold energy slammed into him, knocking him back against the opposite wall and dealing 30 cold damage on a critical fail constitution saving throw. Ylka immediately cast cure wounds to stabilize him (restoring 6 HP). The vault was completely empty inside -- a frustrating discovery after the painful trap. The real treasure and the path to the [[Shrine Of Asmodeus (Cassalanter Villa)|Shrine of Asmodeus]] lay through the secret passage in the wine cellar instead.
