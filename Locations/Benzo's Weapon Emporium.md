# Benzo's Weapon Emporium

Benzo's Weapon Emporium is a kobold-run blacksmith shop in the [[Night Market]] of the [[Radiant Citadel]]. The shop is operated by a team of enthusiastic kobolds who work with all kinds of exotic and fantastic metals -- colored metals, stained metals, and other unusual materials. They draw a crowd of onlookers who watch them work. It was here that [[Scoot]] brought his great axe to be examined and learned that its blade is forged from star metal.

**Type:** shop
**Region:** [[Night Market]], [[Radiant Citadel]]
**First appearance:** [[S02E08 - Episode Thirty Eight (S02E08)|S02E08]]

## Description

Benzo's Weapon Emporium sits among the bustling stalls and tents of the Night Market in the Radiant Citadel's trade district. The kobold blacksmiths work with a dazzling variety of metals beyond ordinary steel, producing weapons and items from colored and stained metals that look like something out of a fantastical forge. Their skilled work draws a crowd of spectators. The shop operates on a contract basis, crafting items from whatever materials customers bring in rather than mining or sourcing their own. Despite their expertise with exotic metals, the kobolds noted that star metal is rare enough that they had not encountered it in the Radiant Citadel before.

## Notable Events

- **[[S02E08 - Episode Thirty Eight (S02E08)|S02E08]]:** [[Scoot]] searches the Night Market for a blacksmith who might identify the material of his great axe; he rolls a 16 on investigation and finds the kobold blacksmith shop working with exotic metals
- **[[S02E08 - Episode Thirty Eight (S02E08)|S02E08]]:** The kobolds swarm around [[Scoot]]'s axe in excitement, examining it and remarking on its lightweight construction and the core running through the middle; they identify the material as star metal -- something that fell from the sky rather than being dug from the earth
- **[[S02E08 - Episode Thirty Eight (S02E08)|S02E08]]:** The kobolds tell [[Scoot]] that star metal is rare, especially in the purity and quantity present in his axe; when asked where to find more, they suggest talking to someone very rich or someone above the law with connections, as they have not seen it in the Radiant Citadel themselves
- **[[S02E08 - Episode Thirty Eight (S02E08)|S02E08]]:** One of the kobolds identifies the shop as "Benzo's Weapon Emporium" and confirms they primarily do contract work, crafting from whatever materials people bring them
