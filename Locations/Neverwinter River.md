# Neverwinter River

The Neverwinter River is a geothermally heated river flowing through the city of [[Neverwinter]]. Its warm waters, fed from the direction of [[Mount Hotenow]], give the city its name and flow approximately 150 feet below the [[Sleeping Dragon Bridge]].

**Type:** Geographic Feature / River
**City:** [[Neverwinter]]
**First appearance:** [[S02E15 - Episode Forty Five (S02E15)|Episode S02E15]]

## Description

The Neverwinter River runs through the heart of [[Neverwinter]], its balmy waters heated by geothermal activity originating from [[Mount Hotenow]] to the east. The river sits far below the city's bridges -- approximately 150 feet below the [[Sleeping Dragon Bridge]] -- and flows out toward the ocean. The [[Dolphin Bridge]] and [[Sleeping Dragon Bridge]] both span the river at different points in the city.

## Notable Events

- **[[S02E15 - Episode Forty Five (S02E15)|S02E15]]:** After [[SWOJ]]'s confrontation on the [[Sleeping Dragon Bridge]], the body of [[16 String Jack]] floats away in the Neverwinter River toward the ocean, carried by its warm current hundreds of feet below.
