# Lower Vault (Cassalanter Villa)

A hidden secondary vault beneath [[Cassalanter Villa]], accessed through a secret door in the wine cellar. The lower vault contains shelves of silver bars and other stored valuables, and connects via a spiral staircase to the deeper [[Cassalanter Villa Crypt|underground crypt]] and [[Shrine of Asmodeus (Cassalanter Villa)|Shrine of Asmodeus]].

**Type:** room
**First appearance:** [[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four|S01E24]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episodes

## Description

A cold, dark storage room accessed through a secret passage discovered by [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] behind the wine cellar (found with a natural 20 perception check). The vault is stocked with heavy silver bars on shelves, along with bags and boxes of other valuables. The space appears relatively undisturbed. A draft of warm air rises from a spiral staircase on the far side of the room, leading further downward toward the Cassalanters' secret shrine. The temperature contrasts sharply with the cold cellar above -- a warm current flowing up from the fiery shrine below.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four|S01E24]]:** During [[SWOJ]]'s infiltration of the villa while playing "ghosts" with the Cassalanter children, the party descends to the cellars. After [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] picks three iron locks on the main vault (which was empty but trapped with a cold damage blast dealing 30 damage to Lark), [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] discovers a secret door leading to this lower vault. Scoot immediately begins eyeing the silver bars as gym weights, then stashes five bars into his bag of holding. The party notices a warm draft coming from the spiral staircase and descends further, discovering the [[Shrine of Asmodeus (Cassalanter Villa)|underground shrine to Asmodeus]] below. Lark later promises the Cassalanter children they will find their silver bars by the hedges outside.
