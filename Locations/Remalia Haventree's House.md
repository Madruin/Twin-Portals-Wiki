# Remalia Haventree's House

[[Remalia Haventree]]'s estate is a Harper safehouse in [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Castle Ward]], situated near [[The God Catcher]]. Described as a beautiful manor in a long row of elegant old houses reminiscent of Copenhagen, the estate serves as a secure meeting place for [[The Harpers]] and their allies. [[SWOJ]] uses the house as a staging ground at several critical points during the [[The Stone of Golorr]] quest and the broader conflict against [[Waterdeep]]'s rival factions.

**Type:** Safehouse / Noble Estate
**Ward:** [[Castle Ward]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|Episode 15]]

## Description

The Haventree Estate is a shuttered, well-guarded manor staffed by [[Laeral Silverhand]]'s private agents and Harper operatives. The house features private rooms, pillows and refreshments for guests, and enough space to shelter allies in need. [[Remalia Haventree]] uses it as both her personal residence and a base for Harper operations in Waterdeep. The estate sits near the towering statue known as [[The God Catcher]], one of the Walking Statues of Waterdeep.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** [[Laeral Silverhand]] brings [[SWOJ]] to the Haventree Estate near [[The God Catcher]] after their trial in the [[Dock Ward]]. [[Xae|Xaerine Rumblestride]] is left here sleeping under the protection of Laeral's agents while the rest of the party proceeds with other business.
- **[[S01E16 - Episode Sixteen|S01E16]]:** [[Xae|Xaerine Rumblestride]] awakens at the estate and meets [[Remallia Haventree]], who has been watching over her. The crystal unicorn reveals its name is [[Lyrian]] and creates a green portal that teleports Xae to [[Trollskull Manor]]. Remallia then leads SWOJ through the sewers to a secret entrance into [[Xanathar's Lair]] for their heist mission.
- **[[S01E22 - Episode Twenty Two|S01E22]]:** [[Remalia Haventree]] meets SWOJ at the docks after they visit [[Zelifarn]] in the harbor. She provides intelligence that the [[Zhentarim]] possess the second Eye of the [[The Stone of Golorr]] and that the [[Kolat Towers]] are the Zentarim base. She gives them a sending stone for future contact.
- **[[S01E25 - Episode Twenty Five|S01E25]]:** SWOJ assembles the three pieces of the [[The Stone of Golorr]] at the house. The Stone ensorcels [[Remalia Haventree|Remalia]] and her guards [[Bricks]] and [[Bracks]], then transports SWOJ's consciousness 500 years into the past to 992 DR [[Ancient Waterdeep (992 D.R.)|ancient Waterdeep]], revealing the location of the [[Vault Of Dragons]].
- **[[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]]:** SWOJ awakens breathless on the floor of the estate after the time-travel vision. They break the ensorcellement on Remalia, Bricks, and Bracks by hitting them. [[Terry]] arrives with Pass Without Trace ability. The party departs with their Harper allies to infiltrate [[Zadio City Music Hall]] and enter the [[Vault Of Dragons]].
- **[[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]]:** After the Cassalanter Villa showdown, [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] alerts the Harpers that [[Bricks]] and [[Bracks]] were traitors. Remalia is freed from house arrest and coordinates the Harper cleanup effort.
