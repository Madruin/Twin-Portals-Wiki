# Blue Alley

Legendary magical dungeon hidden within the city of Waterdeep. An urban legend that turned out to be real. Contains bizarre traps and challenges including a sticky staircase, a laughing mouth riddle, rooms that induce madness, a dining room with a statue inscribed 'utter madness pure rubbish,' and a final egg-shaped mirror room with a crystal unicorn on a pedestal.
