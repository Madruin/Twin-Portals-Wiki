# Amphail

A town north of Waterdeep where the Right of the Staglass ceremony is held. Anya Irmbrusk went missing there. SWOJ's destination for multiple quest objectives.

**Type:** town
**First appearance:** Episode S02E05
**Total appearances:** 6 episodes

## Description

A town north of Waterdeep where the Right of the Staglass ceremony is held. Anya Irmbrusk went missing there. SWOJ's destination for multiple quest objectives.

## Notable Events




- **S02E05:** The Doom Network assigns SWOJ to find missing noblewoman Anya Irmbrusk (the Staglass) in Amphail
- **S02E05:** Ziraj the Hunter joins as guide for the journey north to Amphail
- **S02E06:** Party arrives in military-occupied Amphail and parts ways with Ziraj
- **S02E06:** Multiple disappearances in Amphail linked to mysterious mists from the Cryptwood causing blackouts
- **S02E07:** SWOJ attends the Rite of the Stag Lass festival in Amphale
- **S02E19:** Erstel Fluxen reveals Manshoon operates from Amphail and commands the Spiral Cavern forces
- **S02E19:** SWOJ flies to Amphail on dragonback
- **S02E20:** SWOJ returns to Neverwinter with the Amphail crystals
- **S02E20:** Final war council reviews threats: Waterdeep (dragons), Luskan (barbarians), Amphail (army), Cult of the Dragon
- **S02E30:** Flying to the Moonhole near Amphail, they discover the town has been devastated by dragonfire
- **S02E30:** They meet Renaer Neverember at Castle Never and report on the dracolich, Amalia Cassalanter, and Amphail's destruction
- **S02E38:** Refugees from the Radiant Citadel are given space to homestead in Amphail
