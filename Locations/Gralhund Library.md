# Gralhund Library

The **Gralhund Library** is a large, high-ceilinged wood-paneled room inside [[Gralhund Villa]], serving as the private library of the [[Gralhund Family|Gralhund family]]. It became a key objective for [[SWOJ]] during their raid on the villa, as it housed a mysterious locked book guarded by an [[imp]] in disguise.

**Type:** Room (interior)
**First appearance:** [[S01E12 - Episode Twelve|Episode 12]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

The library is an impressive fifty-by-fifty-foot chamber with high ceilings and wood-paneled walls. Sliding wooden ladders on rails provide access to the extensive collection of books lining the shelves. Comfortable chairs and divans are arranged throughout for reading. Paintings depicting the lineage of the Gralhund family hang above the room, though several faces have been scratched out or removed with white-out, hinting at disowned or disgraced family members.

At the center of the room stands a lectern holding a large, beautifully embossed book bound in metal and leather. The book is secured shut with a keyhole latch, its metal casing wrapping entirely around the binding. A giant tarantula -- roughly a foot in diameter -- perched atop the book as its guardian, which was actually a shapeshifted imp.

## Notable Events

- **The Imp Guardian** ([[S01E12 - Episode Twelve|Episode 12]]): During the raid on [[Gralhund Villa]], [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] sprinted into the library and attempted to sweep the giant tarantula off the book with a broom. The spider caught the broom with one leg, then transformed into a small imp with red wings and a scorpion-like stinger tail, setting the broom ablaze. [[Lark]] shot the imp with a crossbow, knocking it off the book, but the imp stung [[Xae|E]] with its venomous tail, dealing poison damage.
- **Stealing the Book** ([[S01E12 - Episode Twelve|Episode 12]]): [[Xae|E]] grabbed the heavy metal-bound book from the lectern and crashed through a library window to escape before the [[City Watch]] arrived, telling [[Lark]] to blame it on an intruder. Lark covered for E by telling the arriving guards that someone had taken a book and fled out the window. Earlier, [[Yalah Gralhund]] had been found upstairs wearing a silver chain with the key to the book, which E snatched before leaping away.
