# Alterdeep

Alterdeep is an illusory or psychic mirror version of [[Waterdeep]] that [[SWOJ]] was transported to through a green portal. This eerie doppelganger city served as an early and recurring mystery in the campaign, with the party returning to confront its horrors multiple times across Season 1 and into Season 2.

**Type:** Extraplanar / Illusory City
**First appearance:** [[S01E03 - Episode Three|Episode S01E03]]

## Description

Alterdeep resembles [[Waterdeep]] but with deeply unsettling differences. The city smells of cherry rather than the usual salt and refuse of the [[Dock Ward]]. It is perpetually shrouded in fog, and green-yellow portals flicker in the sky throughout the city like eerie fireworks, with at least six or seven visible within a mile radius. There is no [[City Watch]] presence.

The inhabitants are zombie-like citizens of various races who shuffle through the streets in unnaturally large numbers, all wearing identical grey, patchy, poor-quality clothing. They speak in unison, repeating hollow pleasantries such as "lovely day" and "lovely weather" even in the middle of the night. They completely ignore screams and disturbances around them.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E03 - Episode Three|S01E03]]:** [[SWOJ]] falls through a green portal into Alterdeep for the first time and encounters the shuffling, identical citizens. They explore the fog-shrouded streets, discover the cherry smell, and meet [[Zardoz Zord]], who tells them he also does not belong here. They hear screams in the distance and encounter a brain monster (intellect devourer) before eventually escaping.
- **[[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]:** The party recalls the cherry smell and their experiences in Alterdeep, piecing together clues about what happened to them.
- **[[S01E17 - Episode Seventeen|S01E17]]:** The cherry smell returns, connecting back to the Alterdeep mystery as events escalate around the [[Crystal Unicorn]].
- **[[S02E37 - Episode Sixty Seven LIVE (S02E37)|S02E37]]:** The faint odor of cherry returns as the party is pulled through space into another reality, echoing their earlier Alterdeep experiences.
