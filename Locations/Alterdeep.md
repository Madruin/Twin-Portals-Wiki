# Alterdeep

An illusory or psychic version of Waterdeep that the party was transported to through a green portal. Smells of cherry instead of salt and refuse. Populated by zombie-like citizens who shuffle in unison, speak in identical phrases ('lovely day,' 'lovely weather'), and wear matching grey patchy clothing. No City Watch present. Green portals flicker in the sky throughout the city.
