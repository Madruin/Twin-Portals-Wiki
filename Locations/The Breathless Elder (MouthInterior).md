# The Breathless Elder (Mouth/Interior)

The mouth and interior of the Breathless Elder -- a colossal dead viper fish deity drifting in [[The Far Realm]] beyond [[The Wall]] of the multiverse. This cavernous corpse served as a lair for [[Shoonie]], a cooperative [[Manshoon]] clone, and housed the gem-like power source that tethered bronze dragon spirits to eggs growing on the Elder's back. SWOJ entered through the creature's mouth to sever the tethers, only to be betrayed by [[Rowan Crooks]] inside. After a catastrophic explosion killed the party, they awoke as ghosts in this space and had to flee through the ribcage to escape soul-hunting whistlers.

**Type:** Dungeon / Extraplanar Corpse Interior
**First appearance:** [[S02E11 - Episode Forty One (S02E11)|S02E11]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Description

The interior of the Breathless Elder was a vast organic space, part corpse and part dungeon. The mouth opened into a cavernous chamber large enough for combat between multiple parties. Deeper inside, the creature's ribcage formed a skeletal tunnel that SWOJ squeezed through to escape. The Elder's angler-fish-like appendage held a gem that served as the power source for the dragon-spirit tethering system. Dragon spirits were bound to eggs growing on the Elder's exterior like barnacles, and severing the gem released a massive burst of energy. The space was haunted by whistlers -- soul-eating entities that hunted the Far Realm.
