# Hole In The Wall

A halfling-sized tavern built into the base of [[The God Catcher]] statue in [[Waterdeep]]. The windows sit only three to four feet off the ground, and the low ceilings make it a tight squeeze for taller visitors. The interior extends from a cozy wooden-walled front room back into the stone of the God Catcher itself, with carved stone alcoves lining the walls. A secret back room connects to the [[Shard Shunners]]' lair deeper inside the statue. The tavern serves [[Stoutfellow Farm|Stout Fellow]] wine and is a regular gathering spot for Waterdeep's halfling community.

**Type:** restaurant, tavern
**Region:** [[Castle Ward]], [[Waterdeep]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E08 - Episode Eight|Episode 8]]
**Total appearances:** 5 episodes

## Description

The Hole In The Wall occupies a small space at street level within the base of the God Catcher, one of Waterdeep's famous Walking Statues. Its sign hangs beside a low entrance, and everything about the place is scaled for halflings -- the ceiling, the windows, the furniture. Inside, the room is cozy and intimate, with only a handful of patrons on a typical visit. The decor transitions from wooden walls near the entrance to rough-hewn stone deeper in, where alcoves carved into the God Catcher's base hold boots and personal items. The tavern has a connection to the [[Shard Shunners]], a halfling wererat gang led by [[Dasher Zuber]] who operate from chambers deeper inside the statue.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]:** [[SWOJ]] first visits the Hole In The Wall while searching for [[Dasher Zuber]]. [[Tula]] at [[Zuber Farm]] had revealed that Dasher frequented the tavern. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] enters alone on a covert mission, posing as a lovelorn woman looking for a curly-haired halfling she met outside the tavern. [[Bonnie Hilltopple]], the bartender, serves her [[Stoutfellow Farm|Stout Fellow]] wine and promises to keep an eye out. The visit establishes the tavern's connection to Dasher and the halfling underworld beneath the God Catcher.
- **[[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]:** Later in the same episode, after [[SWOJ]] discovers [[Dasher Zuber]] living as a wererat with the [[Shard Shunners]] inside the God Catcher, they negotiate a truce. Dasher provides 100gp and a farewell letter to his mother [[Blossom Zuber]]. [[Mary Stoutfellow]] also arranges a Stout Fellow wine distribution deal through the tavern.
- **[[S01E10 - Episode Ten|S01E10]]:** [[SWOJ]] stops at the Hole In The Wall during their rounds through [[Waterdeep]]. [[Vaniel]], a halfling patron and regular, discusses concerns about lycanthropy with the party -- a nod to the [[Shard Shunners]]' wererat activity in the area.
- **[[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]:** [[Vaniel]] from the Hole In The Wall attends the grand opening of [[The Short Rest]] tavern on [[Trollskull Alley]]. He is among the ten or more people killed when a catastrophic fireball explosion devastates the street, becoming one of the tragedy's named victims.
- **[[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]]:** During the Season 1 finale downtime montage, [[SWOJ]] mentions the Hole In The Wall and its back-room connection to the God Catcher interior. The tavern has become a familiar landmark as the party reflects on their Waterdeep adventures before departing for the [[Feywild]].
- **[[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]:** The Hole In The Wall is listed among locations [[SWOJ]] revisits during their homecoming tour of [[Waterdeep]] after a three-year absence, checking in on the places and people that defined their early adventures.
