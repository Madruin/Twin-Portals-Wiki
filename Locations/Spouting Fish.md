# Spouting Fish

The Spouting Fish is a tavern in [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Dock Ward]], owned by [[Eunissa]]. It became the center of a neighborhood crisis when residents began vanishing in the night, including Eunissa's husband [[Harold]]. The tavern's cellar connects via a trap door and grate to the city sewers, which served as the route by which victims were abducted by creatures lurking below.

**Type:** Tavern
**Ward:** [[Dock Ward]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E04 - Episode Four|Episode S01E04]]

## Description

The Spouting Fish is a well-known neighborhood tavern in the Dock Ward, situated near the homes of many local residents including [[Tagga]] (Scoot's mother), who lives across the street. The tavern is a gathering spot where neighbors meet for morning coffee and socializing. Beneath the main floor, a small washroom contains a trap door leading to a cellar, which in turn connects to a metal grate opening into Waterdeep's sewer system. This hidden passage was exploited by subterranean creatures to abduct sleeping residents from the surrounding area.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]:** [[SWOJ]] learns from [[Tagga]] that [[Harold]] and other neighbors near the Spouting Fish have gone missing in the night. The party travels to the tavern to investigate, discovers the trap door and sewer access in the cellar, and descends into the sewers to search for the missing people.
- **[[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]:** After rescuing the kidnapped residents from tentacular horrors in the sewers below, [[SWOJ]] ushers the survivors back up through the Spouting Fish cellar, marking their first victory under the name Silver Waves of Justice.
- **[[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]]:** The Spouting Fish erupts in celebration as dozens of rescued people are reunited with their loved ones. [[Ylka]] sings "A Taxi Full of SWOJ" at the top of her voice while the neighborhood celebrates inside.
- **[[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]]:** [[SWOJ]] checks in at the Spouting Fish to report further developments to [[Captain Hyustus Stagget]] and the City Watch, including that [[Tagga]] has also gone missing.
