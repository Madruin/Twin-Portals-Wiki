# Neverwinter Streets

The streets of [[Neverwinter]], which erupted into chaos when demons and aberrations poured through from the chasm. [[SWOJ]] fought through waves of monsters here while trying to protect civilians.

**Type:** Urban / Combat Zone
**City:** [[Neverwinter]]
**First appearance:** [[S02E17 - Episode Forty Seven (S02E17)|Episode S02E17]]

## Description

The streets of [[Neverwinter]] are normally a bustling urban environment, but during the chasm eruption they became a warzone. Rubble from the chasm breach littered the ground, and the air was filled with a gut-wrenching stench from the demons. Chokers oozed up from the rubble while a massive winged frog demon (resembling a Hezrou) crashed down from above. The party could see at least ten more creatures further down the street, with crashing and rumbling in every direction.

## Notable Events

- **[[S02E17 - Episode Forty Seven (S02E17)|S02E17]]:** When the chasm beneath [[Neverwinter]] erupts, demons and aberrations pour into the streets. [[SWOJ]] emerges from shelter to find chokers oozing from the rubble and a hulking winged frog demon crashing into the ground. [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] engages the chokers with his star metal axe, obliterating one with fire damage, but is grappled and choked by two more. [[Xae]] shifts into panther form to claw at the creatures restraining Scoot. The frog demon savages [[Xae]] with claws and a bite for 41 total damage, forcing her back into halfling form. [[Lark]] scouts ahead and reports the streets are overrun in every direction. [[SWOJ]] fights to rescue a family trapped amid the chaos before the frog demon is eventually driven off.
