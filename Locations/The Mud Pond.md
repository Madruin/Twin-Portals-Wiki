# The Mud Pond

The Mud Pond is a hazardous underground swamp area between the main shaft and the obsidian fortress in the [[Ygglimar]] region. Filled with treacherous mud and guarded by mud mummies, it proved a dangerous obstacle for the combined forces trying to reach the fortress.

**Type:** Environmental Hazard / Swamp
**Location:** Underground, near the obsidian fortress in [[Ygglimar]]
**First appearance:** [[S02E27 - Episode Fifty Seven (S02E27)|Episode S02E27]]

## Description

The Mud Pond is a stretch of deep, cursed mud lying between the main underground shaft and the obsidian fortress. The area is infested with mud mummies -- undead creatures that lurk within the muck. Some patches of raised ground exist outside the worst of the mud, providing limited shelter. The mud itself carries a magical curse: contact can inflict mud rot, which causes the victim's body to slowly turn to mud, dealing ongoing necrotic damage that can only be removed by curse-breaking magic.

## Notable Events

- **[[S02E27 - Episode Fifty Seven (S02E27)|S02E27]]:** [[SWOJ]] and their allies (approximately 20 people including [[Little Joan]]'s group) reach the Mud Pond area. The mud mummies are identified as a threat, and the party must decide whether to leave their allies in a secret camp on raised ground outside the muck while [[SWOJ]] sneaks into the fortress.
- **[[S02E28 - Episode Fifty Eight (S02E28)|S02E28]]:** While crossing through the Mud Pond, [[Xae]] fails a constitution saving throw and contracts mud rot, taking 3d6 damage per round as her body begins turning to mud. She attempts to counter it with Balm of the Summer Court healing but cannot shake the curse. [[Bronk]] is also afflicted and deteriorates rapidly. [[Xae]] and Bronk are both reduced to half speed as the mud rot sets in.
