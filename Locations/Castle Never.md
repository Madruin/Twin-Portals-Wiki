# Castle Never

The largest building in Neverwinter, a massive towering castle on the north side of the river. Dark and abandoned, filled with ghosts. Lower levels are undergoing renovation/construction. Lord Neverember does not use it as his seat of power.

## Notable Events




- **S02E14:** They cross the Sleeping Dragon Bridge and observe Castle Never (dark, abandoned), the Hall of Justice, and the Moonstone Mask (floating island inn)
- **S02E15:** SWOJ takes shelter in the excavation areas beneath Castle Never
- **S02E15:** SWOJ reunites with Renaer Neverember in the resistance base beneath Castle Never
- **S02E18:** Guard captain Tresa escorts SWOJ to Castle Never; a halfling carpenter gives them a tour of the lower levels
- **S02E18:** Lord Dagult Neverember hires SWOJ to retrieve the Crown of Neverwinter from Castle Never for 5,000 gold each plus property
- **S02E19:** SWOJ emerges from Castle Neverwinter with the Crown of Never and captive Erstel Fluxen
- **S02E30:** They meet Renaer Neverember at Castle Never and report on the dracolich, Amalia Cassalanter, and Amphail's destruction
- **S02E30:** Saboteurs attack Castle Never -- explosion damages the Ethereal Galleon at the dry docks
- **S02E31:** SWOJ secures the soul stone of Rowan Crooks in the aftermath of the Castle Never attack
