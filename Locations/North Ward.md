# North Ward

The North Ward is the wealthy residential district of [[Waterdeep]] where [[Trollskull Manor]] and [[Trollskull Alley]] are located. Home to old noble families and well-to-do merchants, the ward serves as [[SWOJ]]'s home base throughout the campaign. It is patrolled by the magistar [[Ulkoria Stonemarrow]], a powerful shield dwarf archwizard who keeps a tight watch over the district.

**Type:** City District
**City:** [[Waterdeep]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E04 - Episode Four|Episode 4]]

## Description

The North Ward sits between Del Zord Street and Ceradue Way in the upper reaches of Waterdeep. It is a respectable neighborhood of beautiful old mansions and well-kept streets, attracting a mix of nobles, prosperous merchants, and artisans. [[Trollskull Manor]], the converted tavern that [[Volo]] gives to SWOJ, sits in [[Trollskull Alley]] within the ward. The alley's businesses — including the various shops and vendors — are considered trustworthy by the local city watch. [[Captain Hyustus Stagget]] of the [[Dock Ward]] recommends SWOJ introduce themselves to [[Ulkoria Stonemarrow]] when they first head to the ward.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]:** [[Volo]] offers SWOJ [[Trollskull Manor]] as payment for rescuing [[Floon Blagmaar]], describing it as a beautiful old mansion in the North Ward that has fallen into disrepair. The party names themselves the Silver Waves of Justice (SWOJ) and takes a Zuber ride with [[Terry]] to investigate their new home.
- **[[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]]:** SWOJ arrives in the North Ward and begins exploring [[Trollskull Manor]] and [[Trollskull Alley]]. [[Captain Hyustus Stagget]] tells them to say hello to [[Ulkoria Stonemarrow]], the magistar who keeps a tight watch over the district. They meet local business owners and neighbors.
- **[[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]:** [[Ulkoria Stonemarrow]] mentions knowing many of the Trollskull Alley business owners from her rounds in the North Ward, vouching for them as trustworthy people.
- **[[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]:** [[Bacto And Juba|Bacto and Juba]], local silk merchants, approach SWOJ about an exclusive trade deal, noting that the party is conveniently close to them in the North Ward.
- **[[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]]:** During the Founder's Day festival, people from across Waterdeep — including the North Ward — gather at the celebration grounds, mixing social classes in a festive atmosphere.
- **[[S02E06 - Episode Thirty Six (S02E06)|S02E06]]:** The North Ward is mentioned in context of SWOJ's ongoing operations and the neighborhood surrounding [[Trollskull Manor]].
