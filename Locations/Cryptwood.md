# Cryptwood

The **Cryptwood** is a forest near the [[Sword Mountains]] and [[Amphail]]. It is the only known source of [[mothweed]], a rare purple moss used to treat spore illnesses.

## Overview

The Cryptwood is home to the [[Circle of Dusk]], a druid circle connected to the [[Emerald Enclave]]. The druid [[Shala]] mentored [[Leah]] in the forest near Amphail. During Season 2, [[Arcturia]] (one of [[Halaster Blackcloak]]'s apprentices) operated in the Cryptwood, using transmutation magic to transform people and control swarms of butterflies before escaping aboard a [[Concord Jewel]].

When [[SWOJ]] returned to [[Waterdeep]] after a three-year absence, they found [[Tagga]] ill with a spore sickness that required mothweed from the Cryptwood for treatment, motivating their journey north.
