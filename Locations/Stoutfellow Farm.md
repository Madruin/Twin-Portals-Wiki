# Stoutfellow Farm

Stoutfellow Farm is a vineyard and farm in the [[Undercliff]] region just east of [[Waterdeep]], run by [[Mary Stoutfellow]]. The farm came under attack by enchanted scarecrows sent by a rival, and [[SWOJ]] was hired through the [[Emerald Enclave]] to deal with the threat. After resolving the crisis, the Stoutfellow Vineyard became a commercial ally of SWOJ, supplying wine that the party helped distribute to taverns across Waterdeep.

**Type:** Farm / Vineyard
**Region:** [[Undercliff]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E07 - Episode Seven|Episode S01E07]]

## Description

Stoutfellow Farm sits about an hour's travel east of Waterdeep in the Undercliff, with a sign marking the path to the neighboring [[Zuber Farm|Zuber Orchard and Meadery]] branching off nearby. The farm includes a cottage farmhouse where the Stoutfellow family lives, along with vineyards and orchard grounds. The Stoutfellows produce wine that has gained popularity in Waterdeep's taverns. Their competitive relationship with the neighboring [[Zuber Farm]] and its owner [[Blossom Zuber]] became a source of conflict when Blossom used an enchanted ring to send magical scarecrows against the farm at night.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]:** [[Leah]] brings an [[Emerald Enclave]] quest to [[SWOJ]]: scarecrows have been attacking Stoutfellow Farm at night. The party travels to the Undercliff and arrives at the farm as evening approaches.
- **[[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]:** At midnight, [[SWOJ]] confronts and destroys three enchanted scarecrows, setting fire to a section of fallow ground in the process. The Stoutfellow workers and family are grateful for the intervention.
- **[[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]:** [[Mary Stoutfellow]] thanks [[SWOJ]] the following morning, presenting them with an enchanted sickle found among the scarecrow remnants and paying 60 gold per scarecrow (180 gold total) as their first official paycheck.
- **[[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]:** [[SWOJ]] confronts [[Blossom Zuber]] at the neighboring farm about the scarecrow attacks. Blossom confesses she used an enchanted ring to send the scarecrows in order to undermine the Stoutfellows' wine business. The party negotiates a deal: Blossom stops the attacks and provides information about [[Dasher Zuber]]'s disappearance in exchange for SWOJ keeping her involvement secret.
- **[[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]:** [[Ylka]] composes a song titled "A Sack of Stout Fellow" in honor of the victory, and Mary offers SWOJ a discount contract on Stoutfellow wines.
- **[[S01E09 - Episode Nine|S01E09]]:** The Stoutfellow Vineyard is referenced as a trusted ally of SWOJ, alongside the [[Shard Shunners]] and [[Renaer Neverember]], as the party builds its network across Waterdeep.
