# Nameless Seas

The pirate-infested waters surrounding [[Lantan]] and [[Devil's Rock]], south of the [[Sword Coast]]. These chaotic seas are home to [[Driftwood]], a massive floating settlement built from shipwrecks and debris, and rumored to contain a permanent whirlpool that serves as the entrance to the [[Deep Dragon Temple]]. [[SWOJ]] sailed here during [[Season 2]] seeking the temple as part of their quest to confront the draconic threats menacing the [[Sword Coast]].

**Type:** Sea / Ocean Region
**Region:** South of the [[Sword Coast]]
**First Appearance:** [[S02E09]]

## Description

The Nameless Seas are treacherous, chaotic waters dotted with whirlpools, shifting currents, and ever-present pirate activity. Piracy has been a longtime problem in the region -- anyone with enough coin can usually buy safe passage, but the waters remain unpredictable. The seas are navigable primarily by those who know the local hazards or have established relationships with the pirate factions operating out of [[Driftwood]].

[[Driftwood]] itself is a floating island constructed from pieces of ship, driftwood, and massive chunks of flotsam, all lashed together into a sprawling piratical settlement with multiple sailing ships moored around it. It serves as a neutral ground of sorts for the region's sailors and pirates.

The rumored permanent whirlpool that leads to the [[Deep Dragon Temple]] proved elusive -- even the dolphins [[Xae]] communicated with had no idea where it was, and the local pirates at [[Driftwood]] were vague about its location. When [[SWOJ]] asked [[Jarlaxle]]'s people directly, the response was unhelpful -- the whirlpool was described as "a shadowy thing on the horizon" that no one could reliably locate.

## Notable Events

- **Arrival at Driftwood:** [[SWOJ]] sailed into the Nameless Seas and encountered [[Driftwood]], the floating pirate settlement. They initially tried to get dolphins to guide them to the whirlpool entrance to the [[Deep Dragon Temple]], but the dolphins delivered them to [[Driftwood]] instead ([[S02E09]]).
- **Whirlpool Search:** The party's primary goal was finding the permanent whirlpool entrance to the [[Deep Dragon Temple]], but it proved frustratingly elusive. [[Xae]]'s dolphins had no knowledge of it, and asking locals at [[Driftwood]] yielded vague directions. [[Scoot]] asked a pirate directly, but the response was merely that the waters were chaotic with whirlpools everywhere ([[S02E09]]).
- **Pirate Negotiations:** [[SWOJ]] had to navigate pirate politics in the region, debating whether they had enough coin to bribe their way through. The pirate factions controlled passage and information, making direct approaches to the [[Deep Dragon Temple]] difficult without local cooperation ([[S02E09]]).
- **Dragon Investigations:** The Nameless Seas served as the broader setting for [[SWOJ]]'s investigation into the dragon cult's activities, with connections to [[Lantan]], [[Devil's Rock]], and the underwater [[Deep Dragon Temple]] that featured in multiple subsequent episodes ([[S02E09]]--[[S02E14]]).
