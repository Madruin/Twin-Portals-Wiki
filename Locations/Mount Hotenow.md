# Mount Hotenow

A volcano located northeast of [[Neverwinter]] whose catastrophic eruption approximately thirty years ago devastated the city, sending lava flows down the river and destroying half of its structures. The eruption also opened [[The Chasm]], a massive rift into subterranean caverns that unleashed demonic creatures. [[Lord Dagult Neverember]] is credited with rebuilding the city in the aftermath and sealing the Chasm.

**Type:** Geographic Feature
**Region:** Near [[Neverwinter]], [[Sword Coast]]

## Description

Mount Hotenow is an active volcano whose eruption remains one of the defining events in Neverwinter's recent history. The lava flows traveled down the river through the city, leveling entire districts and killing untold numbers of inhabitants. The eruption's force also cracked open the earth on the city's southeast side, creating [[The Chasm]] -- a great rift descending into deep caverns that spawned monstrosities. The river flowing from the volcano's direction still runs warm and steamy, a lingering reminder of the geothermal forces beneath the surface.
