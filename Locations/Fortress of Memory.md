# Fortress of Memory

The **Fortress of Memory** is the divine seat of the [[The Raven Queen|Raven Queen]] (also known as the Raven Mother, the Madam of Feathers, and the Queen of Memory) in the [[Shadowfell]]. It is where souls and their stories are collected and preserved after death.

## Overview

The Fortress of Memory serves as the final destination for wayward souls collected by the Raven Queen's [[Shadar-kai]] servants -- [[Blish]], [[Mari]], and [[Idae]] -- who gather both the souls and their life stories to deliver to the fortress. Murals in the [[Raven Queen Shrine]] beneath the [[Shard of Moonlight]] depict souls and memories flowing toward the Fortress of Memory in the sky.

The dark sun in the Shadowfell disrupted the natural order by siphoning souls passing through, preventing them from reaching the Fortress of Memory.
