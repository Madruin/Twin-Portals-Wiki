# Skullport

Skullport is an underground port city hidden beneath [[Waterdeep]], buried inside the mountain that the city is built upon. Described as a "hive of scum and villainy," the lawless settlement serves as the primary base of operations for [[The Xanathar]], the beholder crime lord whose guild controls nearly the entire town. SWOJ's ally [[Thaelin]] ventured into Skullport to purchase potions before the party's infiltration of the [[Xanathar's Lair]].

**Type:** Underground city
**Region:** Beneath [[Waterdeep]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E16 - Episode Sixteen|S01E16]]

## Description

Skullport occupies a vast cavern system inside the mountain beneath Waterdeep, accessible through the city's sewer network and hidden passages. The Xanathar Guild maintains outposts throughout Waterdeep above, but Skullport is the guild's true seat of power. The port attracts criminals, smugglers, and anyone with "lawless activity to do," as most law-abiding citizens avoid the settlement entirely due to its danger and the Xanathar's iron-fisted control.

The town contains merchants willing to deal in goods that surface vendors will not, including potions and magical contraband. [[Thorvin Twinbeard]], a dwarf engineer enslaved in the Xanathar's lair, was building a crushing device intended for use in Skullport during the events of S01E16.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E16 - Episode Sixteen|S01E16]]:** [[Thaelin]], a tiefling warlock and guest party member, travels to Skullport to buy supplies for SWOJ's heist on the [[Xanathar's Lair]]. He purchases one potion of restoration and three greater healing potions for 600 gold pieces. Meanwhile, [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] visits the [[Emerald Enclave]] on the surface to learn Pass Without Trace, and [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] practices Find Familiar, summoning a hawk.
- **[[S01E16 - Episode Sixteen|S01E16]]:** [[Remallia Haventree]] leads SWOJ through the sewers beneath Waterdeep to a hidden entrance into the Xanathar's lair, passing near Skullport's territory. The party learns that the Xanathar controls "almost the whole town" and that people avoid it unless they have criminal business.
