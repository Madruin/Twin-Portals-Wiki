# Trollskull Manor

A once-rundown four-story estate on [[Trollskull Alley]] in [[Waterdeep]]'s [[North Ward]], given to [[SWOJ]] by [[Volothamp Geddarm|Volo]] as payment for rescuing [[Floon Blagmaar]] and [[Renaer Neverember]]. The party renovated the building and opened it as **The Short Rest**, a tavern that became their headquarters and the social heart of the neighborhood. Haunted by the ghost [[Lif Lindgren]], staffed by [[Tagga]], [[Nat]], [[Jenks]], and [[Pog Waggletop]], and home to a resident silver dragon ([[Zelifarn|Zellophane]]) on its rooftop roosts, Trollskull Manor is the single most important location in the Twin Portals campaign.

**Type:** Tavern / Headquarters
**Also known as:** The Short Rest (tavern name)
**First appearance:** [[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]
**Total appearances:** 20+ episodes

## Description

Trollskull Manor is a tall, narrow townhouse with a taproom on the ground floor, living quarters on the upper floors, a tower on the northwest corner, a dusty attic above the third floor, and a deep cellar below. When SWOJ first arrived, the building was dilapidated and haunted -- the ghost [[Lif Lindgren]] cooked eggs and slammed doors to communicate, and a hag named [[Esther]] lurked in the attic with her cauldron.

After clearing out the hag and befriending Lif, the party spent several episodes renovating the manor with help from guilds including the [[Fellowship Of Innkeepers]], the [[Guild of Watermen]], and the [[Watchful Order Of Magisters]]. [[Alcoria Stonemarrow]] (also called [[Ulkoria Stonemarrow]]) warded the building and registered [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] and [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] as magic users. [[Emmek Frewn]], a rival tavern owner, tried to buy the manor for 150gp and was refused.

The tavern opened as **The Short Rest** in [[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]], with a grand opening party attended by [[Jarlaxle|Zardoz Zord]], [[Fel'Rekt Lafeen|Fel'Rekt]], the [[Doom Raiders]], and much of the neighborhood. The celebration was cut short by a devastating fireball explosion on [[Trollskull Alley]] that killed ten or more people, including [[Voniel]], and badly injured [[Tagga]].

The cellar contains a scrying pool through which [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] later contacted [[The Necromancer]], and [[Mordecai Gralhund]]'s secret laboratory. [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]'s urn was kept on the mantle after his death at the hands of the [[Xanathar]].

During SWOJ's three-year absence in the [[The Shadowfell|Shadowfell]], [[Lif Lindgren|Lif]] kept the tavern running with help from [[Tagga]], [[Nat]], and [[Jenks]] -- the latter two growing from street urchins into competent teenagers. Dragon roosts on the roof became operational, visited by [[Zelifarn]]. When SWOJ returned in [[S02E05 - Episode Thirty Five (S02E05)|S02E05]], they found Tagga bedridden with a spore illness and [[Grinda Garloth|Grinda Garloff]] offering to establish a teleportation circle.

## Key Areas

- **Attic:** A dusty storage space above the third floor where [[Esther]] the hag lurked with her cauldron. The party confronted and defeated her here during their initial exploration of the manor in [[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]].
- **Cellar:** The basement level containing ale and wine storage, [[Lif Lindgren|Lif]]'s hidden workshop with his personal effects and magic sword, a scrying pool [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] used to contact [[The Necromancer]], and [[Mordecai Gralhund]]'s secret laboratory.
- **Tower:** A tower on the northwest corner of the building. The [[Trollskull Alley Irregulars|street children]] found [[Esther]]'s hag talisman under the floorboards here. The tower also opens to a purple void demi-plane.
