# Trollskull Manor

Rundown estate in the North Ward given to the party by Volo as payment. Has a taproom on the ground floor.

**Type:** tavern, tavern/headquarters
**First appearance:** Episode S01E04
**Total appearances:** 12 episodes

## Description

Rundown estate in the North Ward given to the party by Volo as payment. Has a taproom on the ground floor.

## Notable Events




- **S01E04:** Volo gives the party the deed to Trollskull Manor as payment for rescuing Floon and Renaer
- **S01E05:** Party arrives at Trollskull Manor for the first time to inspect their new property
- **S01E06:** Morning at Trollskull Manor with ghost Lif cooking eggs for the party
- **S01E07:** Alcoria Stonemarrow wards Trollskull Manor and registers Xae and Ylka as magic users
- **S01E07:** Emmek Frewn tries to buy Trollskull Manor for 150gp and is refused
- **S01E15:** SWOJ heads to Trollskull Manor to prepare; mission: infiltrate Xanathar's Lair at dawn
- **S01E16:** Crystal unicorn creates a green portal teleporting Xae to Trollskull Manor
- **S01E17:** Party arrives at midnight in Trollskull Manor with Lark's ashes and hat
- **S01E17:** Larian calls 'Gather around me' -- Scoot, Ylka, and Xae touch the unicorn and are teleported to Trollskull Manor in rainbow light
- **S01E18:** Brainstorming selling Trollskull Manor to fund the resurrection
- **S01E21:** They discover the hag from Trollskull Manor (now one-armed) serving Malar with captured dryads and caged animals
- **S02E38:** SWOJ returns to Trollskull Manor and reunites with Lief, Grim, Aspell, Tega, Simon, Marie Polvich, and others
- **S02E38:** Massive celebration at Trollskull Manor on God's Night during Marpenoth, attended by Antediluvian Jake, Angie, Pinker, Sid Squid, Zardoz Zord, Volo, Doom Raiders, and Ramalia
