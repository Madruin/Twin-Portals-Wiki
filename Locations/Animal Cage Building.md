# Animal Cage Building

A large building in the [[Deep Wilds]] of the [[Feywild]], roughly three times the size of the adjacent [[The Hag Of Trollskull Manor|hag]]'s shack, used to hold animals as sacrifices to [[Malar|Malar the Beast Lord]].

**Type:** building
**Location:** Clearing in the [[Deep Wilds]], [[Feywild]], near the [[Pool of Eternal Spring]]
**First appearance:** Episode S01E21
**Total appearances:** 1 episodes

## Description

The building sits about thirty feet from [[The Hag Of Trollskull Manor|the hag]]'s shack in a small forest clearing, roughly three times larger than the shack itself. Inside are eighteen cages made of thick wood with heavy wooden bars, each with a designated door side. The cages held various captive animals -- a boar, a hyena, a hawk, a giant rat, a baboon, and others -- as well as one [[Third Dryad Sister|dryad]] who had been tied up and left unconscious in a cage at the far end. The floor is marked with blood stains and scratch marks, evidence that animals were regularly dragged out of the cages to be used as sacrifices.

The building was part of [[The Hag Of Trollskull Manor|the hag]]'s operation in service to [[Malar]], who was corrupting the [[Pool of Eternal Spring]] and striking against [[Lurue]], the goddess of unicorns. The caged animals were being used to feed a growing bloodlust that [[Malar]]'s disciples were spreading through the forest, killing wildlife on sight.

## Notable Events

- During Episode S01E21, [[SWOJ]] tracked sounds of distressed animals to this clearing while searching for [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]], who had been kidnapped and delivered to [[Malar]].
- [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] scouted the clearing and discovered [[The Hag Of Trollskull Manor|the hag]] in her shack, cooking her heart-stone in a cauldron with her one remaining arm, alongside two bound [[Tricayla|dryad]] [[Argentia|sisters]].
- The party ambushed [[The Hag Of Trollskull Manor|the hag]] -- [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] used dissonant whispers through the shack window, driving the hag outside into [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]'s magic net. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] then pulled the hag's heart from her chest, rendering her helpless. Meanwhile, [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] slipped inside and freed the two dryads, [[Tricayla]] and [[Argentia]].
- [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] then entered the animal cage building, finding the eighteen cages and the [[Third Dryad Sister|third dryad sister]] unconscious at the far end. She freed the dryad first, then [[Tricayla]] and [[Argentia]] rushed in to revive their sister with magical goodberries.
- [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] opened all eighteen cages and every terrified animal immediately fled into the forest. The freed [[Third Dryad Sister|dryad]] revealed that the animals were not being given bloodlust -- they were feeding it, suggesting the bloodlust was a separate force or entity.
- [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] hung [[The Hag Of Trollskull Manor|the hag]] from a tree in her net, then placed her inside one of the reinforced wooden cages, tilting it so the door faced the ground to prevent escape. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] kept the hag's heart as leverage, offering it back only if the hag helped defeat [[Malar]] -- an offer the hag refused with a promise to kill them all someday.
