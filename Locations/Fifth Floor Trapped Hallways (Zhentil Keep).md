# Fifth Floor Trapped Hallways (Zhentil Keep)

The fifth floor of [[Zhentil Keep]]'s central tower is a dusty, low-ceilinged corridor roughly a hundred feet long, lined with closed doors and unlit sconces. It appears undisturbed and shows no signs of foot traffic. The hallway is laced with shadow threads -- near-invisible magical tripwires of shadowy energy that materialize from the air when disturbed. These threads coil around anything they contact, draining warmth and binding victims in place. Among the debris on the floor lie remnants of an ancient battle: arrowheads, diadems, pieces of mithril, and scattered treasure left behind long ago.

**Type:** corridor
**Parent location:** [[Zhentil Keep]]
**First appearance:** [[S02E22 - Episode Fifty Two LIVE (S02E22)|S02E22]]

## Description

The hallway sits on the fifth floor of the keep tower, accessible from below via the hollowed-out interior of the tower. The ceiling is low enough that a flying ladder barely fits through the opening, and riders must duck to avoid scraping it. Green torches elsewhere in the keep do not illuminate this floor -- it is completely dark, requiring magical light to navigate safely.

The shadow threads span the corridor at various angles and heights, forming a lattice that is invisible to normal sight. They only become visible in the presence of strong magical light such as a radiant weapon or [[Sunblade]]. When triggered, a thread materializes, coils around the victim, and saps body heat while anchoring them in place. The threads seem to regenerate: disturbing one can cause additional threads to materialize nearby. [[Scoot]]'s enchanted axe and [[Ylka]]'s [[Sunblade]] both proved capable of revealing and cutting through them.

Scattered on the floor among the threads are relics from a past conflict: a solid mithril brooch bearing the Jolly Gents insignia, and a pair of old black boots that turned out to be remarkably comfortable and well-fitted -- later identified as magical boots found by [[Lark]].

## Notable Events

- **[[S02E22 - Episode Fifty Two LIVE (S02E22)|S02E22]]:** SWOJ reaches the fifth floor by riding a flying ladder powered by [[Scoot]]'s rock of flyingness. [[Lark]], surfing at the front of the ladder, hits the first shadow thread, which coils around his arm and drains warmth before he rips it free. He then narrowly avoids a second thread with an acrobatic duck. [[Xae]], in tressym form, uses her ability to detect invisibility but cannot perceive the threads until [[Scoot]]'s glowing axe reveals the full lattice. The party cuts through the threads using [[Scoot]]'s axe and [[Ylka]]'s [[Sunblade]], clearing a path to the doors beyond.
- **[[S02E22 - Episode Fifty Two LIVE (S02E22)|S02E22]]:** [[Lark]] discovers and pockets a mithril Jolly Gents brooch and finds a pair of magical boots among the battle debris on the hallway floor, despite [[Xae]]'s frantic attempts to keep him moving. The party reaches the door to [[Rowan Crooks]]' office on the sixth floor, which [[Lark]] picks with a 24 on his thieves' tools check.
