# Renaer Neverember's Apartment

The personal residence of [[Renaer Neverember]], a wealthy nobleman and close ally of SWOJ. Located somewhere in the vicinity of the [[Short Rest]] in [[Waterdeep]], Renaer's apartment serves as a place where SWOJ can call on their friend for aid. The party visits here during their quest to resurrect [[Lark]], seeking a diamond large enough to fuel the ritual.

**Type:** Building
**Region:** [[Waterdeep]]
**First Appearance:** [[S01E22 - Episode Twenty Two|S01E22]]

## Description

Renaer keeps his fortune fluid through gemstones rather than gold, making his apartment a repository of valuable stones. The residence is close enough to [[Trollskull Alley]] that SWOJ can visit relatively easily. Renaer is generous with his wealth when it comes to helping his friends, and the apartment represents one of the few places in Waterdeep where SWOJ can count on unconditional support.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E22 - Episode Twenty Two|S01E22]]:** SWOJ visits Renaer at his apartment to ask for help obtaining a fist-sized diamond needed for [[Lark]]'s resurrection. [[Zelifarn]] had suggested Renaer as one of the wealthiest people they know. [[Xae]] holds up her small fist to demonstrate the size needed, and Renaer agrees immediately, saying he keeps gemstones as a way of keeping his fortune liquid. He provides the diamond without hesitation, asking only that they explain how Lark's soul ended up in a hat.
- **[[S01E22 - Episode Twenty Two|S01E22]]:** In exchange for the diamond, SWOJ presents Renaer with a ledger of financial misdeeds committed by his father [[Dagult Neverember]], which they recovered from a dragon's lair beneath Waterdeep Harbor.
