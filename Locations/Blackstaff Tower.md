# Blackstaff Tower

The headquarters of [[Waterdeep]]'s most powerful wizards, Blackstaff Tower is a well-known arcane institution in the city. [[Laeral Silverhand]] recommends SWOJ drop her name there if they need magical assistance, though she warns them not to trust the wizards with anything particularly valuable.

**Type:** Tower
**Region:** [[Waterdeep]]
**First Appearance:** [[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]

## Description

Blackstaff Tower houses the wizards who serve as one of [[Waterdeep]]'s key defensive and arcane resources. The tower's mages can be enlisted for aid through the right connections, though [[Laeral Silverhand]] implies they are not entirely trustworthy when it comes to valuable items. In addition to their role defending the city, the wizards of Blackstaff Tower work alongside the Griffin Riders as part of Waterdeep's military apparatus.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** After SWOJ's encounter with [[Laeral Silverhand]], she tells them they can drop her name at Blackstaff Tower to receive help from the wizards, but advises them not to trust the mages with anything particularly valuable.
- **[[S02E31 - Episode Sixty One (S02E31)|S02E31]]:** [[Renaer Neverember]] promises to speak with the wizards of Blackstaff Tower and the Griffin Riders about mustering a Waterdeep force to aid in the conflict, after SWOJ requests additional military support from the south.

## Connected Characters
