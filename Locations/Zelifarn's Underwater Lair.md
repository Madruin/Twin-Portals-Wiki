# Zelifarn's Underwater Lair

[[Zelifarn]]'s physical dragon hoard, located beneath the surface of [[Deepwater Harbor]] in the [[Docksward]] district of [[Waterdeep]]. The lair sits among the shipwrecks and debris of the harbor floor, where Zelifarn served as the self-appointed guardian of the harbor. The hoard contained the [[Neverember Ledger]], a key piece of evidence in [[The Stone of Golorr]] storyline.

**Type:** Lair
**First appearance:** [[S01E22 - Episode Twenty Two|S01E22]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

Zelifarn maintained an underwater lair at the bottom of [[Deepwater Harbor]], nestled among the sunken ships and accumulated debris of one of [[Waterdeep]]'s busiest waterways. As the self-appointed guardian of the harbor, the bronze dragon accumulated a hoard that included various treasures and at least one item of significant political importance -- a ledger belonging to the [[Neverember]] family.

The lair was accessible only by diving deep beneath the harbor surface. [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]], the party's strongest swimmer, made the dive alone to visit Zelifarn and explore the hoard during the party's quest to gather the components needed for [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]'s resurrection.
