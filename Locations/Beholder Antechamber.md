# Beholder Antechamber

An oval-shaped antechamber before the throne room containing the decomposed corpse of a dead beholder, picked clean by spiders over 20+ years. Among the remains: a ghostly pan flute, a vine-wrapped staff, a shadow inkwell, a vulture quill, gold coins, and beholder teeth. After the throne room, the ghost of Talgath the Undying appears here.
