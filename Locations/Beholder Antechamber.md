# Beholder Antechamber

An oval-shaped hallway in [[Castle Never]] that serves as the antechamber to the [[Castle Never Throne Room|throne room]], littered with the decomposed corpse of a dead beholder that has been picked clean by spiders over twenty-plus years. [[SWOJ]] looted magical treasures from the remains before encountering the ghost of [[Talgath The Undying]], the beholder's spectral counterpart.

**Type:** encounter area
**First appearance:** [[S02E18 - Episode Forty Eight LIVE (S02E18)|Episode S02E18]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episodes

## Description

The antechamber is an oval-shaped room positioned between the upper stairwell and the throne room of [[Castle Never]]. At its center lies the corpse of a beholder -- a massive floating aberration now long dead, its tongue lolled out, all eye stalks hollowed out, and its body extensively chewed by the colony of small spiders that infest the upper castle. The creature has been dead at least twenty years, preserved by the sealed, airless environment. Beneath and around the corpse, a small hoard of treasures accumulated: gold coins half-hidden under the beholder's tongue, crushed boxes, and several magical artifacts.

After [[SWOJ]] explored the throne room beyond and disturbed the throne, the ghost of the beholder -- [[Talgath The Undying]] -- materialized in this chamber, floating above its own corpse. The ghost appeared as a spectral beholder with a missing central eye and hollowed-out eye stalk pits, bound to this room by some invisible barrier.
