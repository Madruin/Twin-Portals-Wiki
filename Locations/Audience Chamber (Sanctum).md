# Audience Chamber (Sanctum)

The Audience Chamber is a large hall within [[The Necromancer]]'s [[Necromancer's Extra-Dimensional Sanctum|extra-dimensional sanctum]], functioning as a presentation stage and gathering space. It is connected to the sanctum's central antechamber and leads down to the [[Private Study (Sanctum)|Private Study]] via a backstage staircase.

**Type:** room
**Location:** [[Necromancer's Extra-Dimensional Sanctum]], beneath [[Kolat Towers]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E22 - Episode Twenty Two|Episode S01E22]]

## Description

The Audience Chamber is the largest room in the sanctum, featuring a raised podium or stage at one end where [[The Necromancer]] originally addressed SWOJ during their first encounter. Rows of benches provide seating for an audience, and a sparring area occupies part of the floor. A door behind the stage leads to a short staircase descending about ten feet, which connects to the lower levels of the sanctum.

The chamber is one of four paths branching from the central antechamber -- the others leading to the laboratory (where [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] was resurrected), the staircase down to the [[Reading Room (Sanctum)|Reading Room]], and the portal back to [[Kolat Towers]]. The central antechamber itself features a large Zhentarim symbol (a winged snake) on the floor, which [[Mookie]] identified as a trap.

A hallway near the central antechamber displays a collection of steel and iron masks of various sizes and shapes -- tragic and comic designs, all grotesque in nature. During their exploration, each member of SWOJ selected a mask: [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] chose a Phantom of the Opera-style three-quarter mask, [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] picked one with a long pencil mustache, [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] found an angler-fish-toothed Hannibal Lecter-style lower face mask, and [[Xae|Xaerine]] selected an oversized flower-shaped mask. Each mask delivered a small zap of damage upon being worn.
