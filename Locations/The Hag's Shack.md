# The Hag's Shack

A rundown shack in [[Malar's Realm (Afterlife)|Malar's Realm]], the afterlife domain of the Beast Lord [[Malar]]. This was the base of operations for [[The Hag Of Trollskull Manor|Esther]], the hag previously defeated by SWOJ in the attic of [[Trollskull Manor]], who had been banished to this plane and entered [[Malar]]'s service. The shack served as her workshop for cooking a heartstone and as a prison for two captured [[Tricayla|dryad]] [[Argentia|sisters]].

**Type:** Building
**First appearance:** [[S01E21 - Episode Twenty One|S01E21]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

The shack was a small, roughly 15-by-20-foot structure in terrible disrepair, situated on the slopes of a mountain in [[Malar's Realm (Afterlife)|Malar's Realm]]. Inside, the furnishings were sparse and grim: a ramshackle bed, a table covered in scratches, a warped shelf lined with containers full of body parts, and a large cauldron in the center of the room. [[The Hag Of Trollskull Manor|The hag]] -- now missing the arm that [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] had cut off during their first encounter in [[Trollskull Manor]]'s attic -- stood over the cauldron, repeatedly dipping her remaining hand in to check on a small heart-shaped stone she was cooking. This was her heartstone, the source of a hag's power.

Two [[Tricayla|dryads]] -- [[Tricayla]] and [[Argentia]] -- lay bound and gagged on the floor, captured as part of the hag's service to [[Malar]]. A third sister, the [[Third Dryad Sister]], was imprisoned in the adjacent [[Animal Cage Building]], a much larger structure three times the shack's size containing 18 wooden cages of animals destined for sacrifice to the Beast Lord.
