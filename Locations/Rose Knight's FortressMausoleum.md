# Rose Knight's Fortress/Mausoleum

A stone fortress lashed to the spine of the [[Beast Of Erua]] with three massive vine-like trunks. Built as a mausoleum for the legendary hero [[Teriador]] the Rose Knight after he slew the Beast, it was later corrupted by [[Manshoon]]'s forces and overrun with plant growth. SWOJ entered the fortress during their hunt for a Manshoon clone, only to have the floor collapse beneath them into the Beast's ribcage.

**Type:** Dungeon
**First appearance:** [[S02E03 - Episode Thirty Three (S02E03)|S02E03]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

The fortress was a large stone edifice built into the back of the Beast of Erua, festooned with large vines and overgrowth. It was accessed through an arched gateway from the Beast's spine. Inside, an antechamber contained [[Teriador]]'s sarcophagus and a tapestry depicting his legendary battle against the Beast of Erua. A hallway led deeper into the structure, where the party found Teriador's reanimated corpse -- actually a podling replica wearing his chain mail armor and wielding his ruby-pommeled sword, its hand covered in rings of sky-blue stone. The floor was structurally compromised, and when the battle began, it collapsed into the [[Beast Of Erua's Ribcage|Beast's ribcage]] below.
