# Candle Lane

Narrow, dark alley in the Dock Ward, less than 10 feet wide. Contains a magical floating flame street lamp. Location of the Zhentarim warehouse.

**Type:** 
**First appearance:** Episode S01E01
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Notable Events




- **S01E01:** Party discovers the Zhentarim warehouse on Candle Lane with three fresh corpses inside
- **S01E01:** Lark meets Jelaster the Harper, who directs them to Candle Lane
- **S01E03:** Xaerine maps lightning convergences to near Candle Lane and the Splintered Stair
