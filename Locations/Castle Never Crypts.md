# Castle Never Crypts

The underground crypts beneath Castle Never, accessed via the map found in the throne. Contains side chambers for old royal families and courts. At the far end are two doors: gold scales (Tyr) and silver scales (Kelemvor), leading to Lord Halueth Never's tomb and Lady Lissandra Never's tomb respectively.
