# Castle Never Crypts

The ancient burial chambers beneath [[Castle Never]] in [[Neverwinter]], final resting place of the castle's founding royal families and their courts. [[SWOJ]] descended into the crypts following a secret map found beneath the throne in the castle's upper levels, searching for the [[Crown of Neverwinter]].

**Type:** dungeon
**First appearance:** [[S02E18 - Episode Forty Eight LIVE (S02E18)|Episode S02E18]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episodes

## Description

The crypts lie deep below ground level, accessed via a secret path revealed by a map hidden in a compartment beneath the stone throne in [[Castle Never Throne Room|the throne room]] above. The route passes through a trap door and a concealed archway opened by pressing specific stones. The air below is damp and stagnant, with no sign of the volcanic ash that blankets the upper floors -- instead, the smell is wet and dank, sealed away for centuries.

A long flat hallway stretches through the crypt level, lined with small side chambers where members of old royal families and courts have been interred. The chambers grow progressively older the deeper one goes. At the far end of the hallway stand two sealed doors: one bearing golden scales (the symbol of [[Tyr]], god of justice) and one bearing silver scales (the symbol of [[Kelemvor]], god of the dead). Behind the gold door lies the ornate tomb of [[Lord Halueth Never]], ringed by nine silver swords and adorned with golden filaments, statues, and tapestries. Behind the silver door rests the simple stone coffin of [[Lady Lissandra Never]], unadorned save for silver-lit chandeliers that ignite magically when the chamber is opened.
