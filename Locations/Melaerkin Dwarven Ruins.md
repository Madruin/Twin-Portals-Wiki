# Melaerkin Dwarven Ruins

Ancient dwarven ruins located beneath [[Waterdeep]], specifically underneath the [[Zardio City Music Hall]] (formerly a theater run by [[Zardio Neverember]]). The ruins connect to the vault complex where the [[Dragons of Waterdeep|dragon hoard]] is hidden, and served as the primary dungeon for the vault heist arc of Season 1.

**Type:** Dungeon / Ancient Ruins
**Location:** Beneath [[Waterdeep]], under the [[Zardio City Music Hall]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|Episode S01E26]]

## Description

The Melaerkin Dwarven Ruins are an extensive underground complex beneath Waterdeep dating back at least 500 years. The ruins feature dwarven inscriptions, trapdoors, and ancient stonework. [[Remallia Haventree|Remallia]] helped [[SWOJ]] locate the entrance by identifying the connection between the ruins and the Zardio City Music Hall. The ruins contain dwarven writing that [[Remallia Haventree|Remallia]] could read, including clues such as "a secret will part from the one's lips." These ruins were later connected to [[Halaster Blackcloak|Halaster]]'s deeper descent beneath the city.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]]:** [[SWOJ]] seeks out the Melaerkin Dwarven Ruins with [[Remallia Haventree|Remallia]]'s help. They identify the entrance beneath the [[Zardio City Music Hall]] and descend, finding dwarven inscriptions and trapdoors leading deeper into the vault complex.
- **[[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]]:** The heroes continue their descent into the dwarven ruins, pushing deeper toward the vault.
- **[[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]]:** The ruins are connected to the broader history of [[Halaster Blackcloak|Halaster]], who descended downward into the old dwarven ruins below Waterdeep while the city was built upward above them.

## Connected Characters

- [[Remallia Haventree]] — helped SWOJ locate and enter the ruins
- [[Zardio Neverember]] — historical Neverember who operated the theater above the ruins
- [[Halaster Blackcloak]] — descended into the deeper dwarven ruins beneath the city
