# Staghorn Flagon (Stag And Flag)

A busy three-story tavern in the heart of [[Amphail]], colloquially known as the **Stag and Flag**. Run by [[Gundark Gundarkson]], who also serves as the town's working mayor, the Staghorn Flagon is the central gathering place in Amphail and the first stop for SWOJ when they arrive in town seeking the missing noblewoman [[Anya Ermbrusk|Anya Irmbrusk]].

**Type:** Tavern
**Region:** [[Amphail]]
**First Appearance:** [[S02E06 - Episode Thirty Six (S02E06)|S02E06]]

## Description

The Staghorn Flagon is described as a three-story saloon-style building sitting in the heart of [[Amphail]]. The name is acknowledged in-universe as "a mouthful," with locals and travelers alike defaulting to the shorter "Stag and Flag." During SWOJ's visit, the tavern is decorated with paraphernalia for the upcoming [[Right of the Staglass]] festival -- a ceremony that would allow the noblewoman [[Anya Ermbrusk|Anya Irmbrusk]] to legally renounce her family ties. The town is under military occupation from the [[Waterdeep]]-[[Neverwinter]] conflict, and the tavern serves as the informal center of civic life, with [[Gundark Gundarkson]] pulling double duty as bartender and mayor from behind the bar.

## Notable Events

- **[[S02E06 - Episode Thirty Six (S02E06)|S02E06]]:** SWOJ arrives in [[Amphail]] after a combat encounter with the Hare Company on [[The Long Road|the Long Road]]. They visit the Staghorn Flagon and meet [[Gundark Gundarkson]], the bartender-mayor, who explains the situation: [[Anya Ermbrusk|Anya Irmbrusk]] went missing about a tenday ago while staying in town to prepare for the [[Right of the Staglass]]. Gundarkson also reveals that mysterious mists have been rolling in from the [[Kryptwood|Cryptwood]], causing blackouts and memory loss among the townspeople -- he himself blacked out behind the bar and woke up outside with an axe. Multiple townsfolk have disappeared, including Hanna's husband [[Juan]]. SWOJ uses the tavern as a base before heading into the Cryptwood to investigate.
- **[[S02E07 - Episode Thirty Seven (S02E07)|S02E07]]:** After SWOJ rescues the captives from [[Arcturia]]'s cavern beneath the [[Sword Mountains]], [[Gundark Gundarkson]] is grateful and offers rewards from the town coffers. The missing people are returned to Amphail.
- **[[S02E08 - Episode Thirty Eight (S02E08)|S02E08]]:** In a montage sequence, SWOJ returns to [[Amphail]] for the [[Right of the Staglass]] ceremony and collects quest rewards. The Stag and Flag is referenced as the party passes through town.
- **[[S02E30 - Episode Sixty (S02E30)|S02E30]]:** When SWOJ flies east toward [[Amphail]] later in Season 2, they discover the town has been devastated by dragonfire -- farms and civilian buildings destroyed. The fate of the Staghorn Flagon after the attack is unknown.
