# The Way Inn

The Way Inn is a travelers' rest stop situated on the border between the [[The Feywild|Feywild]] and the [[The Shadowfell|Shadowfell]], run by the bartender [[Dorvina]]. When SWOJ arrived during their Season 2 hunt for [[Manshoon]] clones, the inn was bustling with colorful patrons including the famous chronicler [[Volothamp Geddarm|Volo]], the gnome inventor [[Radecere]], and dwarven travelers from [[Sigil]]. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] performed music at the inn to earn room and board for the group before they crossed the stone border wall into the Shadowfell. The inn has a dark counterpart, the [[Shadow Way Inn]], which SWOJ discovered in the next episode filled with podling replicas of the real patrons.

**Type:** Tavern / Inn
**Region:** Feywild-Shadowfell borderlands
**First appearance:** [[S02E01 - Episode Thirty One (S02E01)|S02E01]]

## Description

The Way Inn sits in the liminal borderlands where the Feywild meets the Shadowfell, serving as a waypoint for travelers moving between the two planes. The compound includes the main tavern building with a common room, bar, and guest quarters. A stone border wall nearby marks the boundary between the Feywild and Shadowfell territories -- crossing it means leaving the relative safety of the Feywild behind.

The inn attracts an eclectic mix of planar travelers: scholars, inventors, adventurers, and wanderers from realms as far-flung as Sigil. [[Dorvina]] tends bar and keeps the peace. The patrons share stories and legends of the surrounding lands, including tales of the [[Beast Of Erua|Beast of Erua]] and the Rose Knight [[Teriador]] who supposedly slew it.

## Notable Events

- **[[S02E01 - Episode Thirty One (S02E01)|S02E01]]:** SWOJ arrives at The Way Inn during their Feywild journey with members of the [[Jolly Gents]]. They meet [[Volothamp Geddarm|Volo]] the chronicler, [[Radecere]] the rock gnome inventor (creator of the Dynamo of Possibility), and [[Ly Angestunne]] and [[Tyjit Skesh]], two dwarves from Sigil. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] performs music at the inn to earn room and board for the group.
- **[[S02E01 - Episode Thirty One (S02E01)|S02E01]]:** An earth-shaking roar sounds from the direction of the Shadowfell, and SWOJ's compass -- crafted from [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]'s gemstone and blood -- aligns with it. [[Dorvina]] and the patrons speak of the [[Beast Of Erua|Beast of Erua]], a legendary creature said to dwell in the Shadowfell, and the Rose Knight [[Teriador]] who once defeated it. The party eats a magical mushroom (granting a blessed effect) and charges through the stone border wall into the Shadowfell.
- **[[S02E02 - Episode Thirty Two (S02E02)|S02E02]]:** Three [[Shadar-kai]] -- [[Blish]], [[Mari]], and [[Idae]] -- direct SWOJ toward the Shadow version of The Way Inn for shelter. Inside the [[Shadow Way Inn]], the party discovers plant-based podling replicas of the patrons they met at the real Way Inn, including fake versions of Volo, Radecere, and Dorvina.
