# Dolphin Bridge

The Dolphin Bridge is a bridge in [[Neverwinter]], located east of the [[Sleeping Dragon Bridge]]. It spans the [[Neverwinter River]] and served as a key landmark during [[SWOJ]]'s time in the city.

**Type:** Bridge
**City:** [[Neverwinter]]
**First appearance:** [[S02E15 - Episode Forty Five (S02E15)|Episode S02E15]]

## Description

The Dolphin Bridge is one of several bridges crossing the [[Neverwinter River]] in the city of [[Neverwinter]]. It sits to the east of the [[Sleeping Dragon Bridge]] and connects different districts of the city. The bridge is part of the route leading toward the southeast gate.

## Notable Events

- **[[S02E15 - Episode Forty Five (S02E15)|S02E15]]:** Guards pursuing [[SWOJ]] speculate that the party might be heading toward the Dolphin Bridge as an escape route eastward. Later, [[SWOJ]] loops around west over the Dolphin Bridge, which they had previously crossed the day before, then winds back toward the southeast gate of [[Neverwinter]].
