# Dessarin River Valley

The Dessarin River Valley is a broad geographical region through which the [[Dessarin River]] flows, running near and eventually underneath [[Waterdeep]]. [[SWOJ]] traveled through this valley on their journey north from Waterdeep toward [[Amphail]] during Season 2.

**Type:** Region / Geographic Feature
**First appearance:** [[S02E06 - Episode Thirty Six (S02E06)|Episode S02E06]]

## Description

The Dessarin River Valley is a well-traveled route along the main highway leading north from [[Waterdeep]]. The road runs near the Dessarin River and passes through increasingly hilly, wooded terrain with occasional rock outcroppings as it approaches [[Amphail]]. The [[Sword Mountains]] are visible to the northwest. The valley was compared by the DM to the St. Louis River Valley near Duluth for the benefit of local listeners. Small hamlets like [[Rassalantar]] dot the road, serving as rest stops for travelers.

## Notable Events

- **[[S02E06 - Episode Thirty Six (S02E06)|S02E06]]:** [[SWOJ]] travels north through the Dessarin River Valley with [[Ziraj The Hunter|Ziraj]] guiding from the rear. The road forks near the foothills of the [[Sword Mountains]], where a Waterdeep guard presence steers travelers east toward [[Amphail]] due to the western road being impassable from conflict. [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] keeps watch while [[Lark]] peppers [[Ziraj The Hunter|Ziraj]] with questions about his past lives during the four-day journey.
