# Kuo-Toa Shrine Room

A wide rectangular chamber found in the tunnels beyond [[Xanathar]]'s collapsed lair, serving as a makeshift shrine to a deity invented by the [[Kuo-Toa]] inhabiting the area.

**Type:** shrine
**Location:** Tunnels beneath [[Waterdeep]], beyond the collapsed lair
**First appearance:** Episode S01E17
**Total appearances:** 1 episodes

## Description

The shrine room is a wide rectangular chamber with a large open door on the far end. At its center stands a crude idol resembling a strange octopus-like god with ten tentacles, cobbled together from garbage, bones, hair, feathers, and other debris. The idol matches the talismans worn by the [[Kuo-Toa]] themselves and appears to be one of their self-invented deities -- a well-known trait of the species, who lost their sanity after being enslaved by [[Mind Flayer|mind flayers]] thousands of years ago.

The room was heavily damaged by the explosion that collapsed [[Xanathar]]'s lair, with large chunks of the ceiling caved in and piles of rubble scattered throughout. Blood stains mark the floor, and the space has a dark purple aesthetic with a creepy atmosphere. A wide trail -- roughly fifteen feet across, about the width of a [[Beholder|beholder]] -- had been cleared through the rubble toward the far door, suggesting something massive was dragged through the room.

## Notable Events

- During Episode S01E17, [[SWOJ]] discovered this room while pursuing a trail through the tunnels after the collapse of [[Xanathar]]'s lair. Six unarmed and unarmored [[Kuo-Toa]] were found praying at the shrine, scrawnier and more submissive than the warriors the party had fought earlier.
- When the party entered the room, the [[Kuo-Toa]] spotted them and fled in terror to the corners of the room, cowering as if the adventurers were gods. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] tried showing her [[Deep Sashelas]] pendant, but the fish people only hissed and shrank further away.
- [[Flutterfoot Zipswiggle]] and [[Jarlaxle|J.B. Nevercott]] accompanied the party through this area. The trail through the rubble suggested the injured or dead [[Xanathar]] had been dragged by the [[Kuo-Toa]] toward the deeper tunnels beyond.
- The party noted the faint smell of cherry-scented zyquil in the area, which they recognized from a previous dream encounter.
