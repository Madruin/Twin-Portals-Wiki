# Throne Room / Vault (Zhentil Keep)

A large chamber on the sixth floor of [[Zhentil Keep]], adjacent to [[Rowan Crooks]]' office. The room served as both a throne room and a vault, containing an old gold and stone throne surrounded by piles of treasure, weapons, and armor -- including SWOJ's confiscated gear. It was guarded by a soul armor construct that animated from the scattered equipment and chains when intruders entered. A secret door behind the throne concealed a spiral staircase descending into the caverns below the fortress.

**Type:** room
**Parent location:** [[Zhentil Keep]]
**First appearance:** [[S02E22 - Episode Fifty Two LIVE (S02E22)|S02E22]]

## Description

The throne room is roughly 20 by 40 feet, accessible through [[Rowan Crooks]]' office via a locked door on the sixth floor (beyond the [[Fifth Floor Trapped Hallways (Zhentil Keep)|trapped hallways]]). The room contains an old gold and stone throne set about 30 feet back, with coins, armor, and weaponry strewn across the floor. Multiple chandeliers hang from the ceiling -- one large and several smaller -- suspended by chains that also line the walls. SWOJ's stolen equipment, including [[Lark]]'s old red hat and his sickle, was piled in bags near the throne.

The office antechamber features [[Rowan Crooks]]' personal desk with diagrams of the three obelisks and soul-conducting conduits, a copper ball phylactery containing the shade of [[Antediluvian Jake]], several of Crooks' hats on wall hooks, and a spinning copper orb hovering in a cradle that served as Jake's anchor to the material world.

Behind the throne lies a secret door leading to a spiral stone staircase that descends into the underground caverns housing the [[Soul Engine]], clone laboratories, and phylactery storage. [[Antediluvian Jake]]'s shade pointed out this passage from the schematics on Crooks' desk.

## Notable Events

- **[[S02E22 - Episode Fifty Two LIVE (S02E22)|S02E22]]:** After navigating the [[Fifth Floor Trapped Hallways (Zhentil Keep)|trapped hallways]], SWOJ reaches the sixth floor. [[Lark]] picks the locked door (rolling 24) and the party enters Crooks' office, discovering diagrams of the obelisk system and the shade of [[Antediluvian Jake]] tethered to a floating copper orb on the desk. Jake reveals that SWOJ's gear is in the vault and that a staircase behind the throne leads downward.
- **[[S02E22 - Episode Fifty Two LIVE (S02E22)|S02E22]]:** [[Xae]] scouts the throne room in tressym form and spots the party's stolen gear near the throne. When the full party enters, the scattered armor, chains, and treasure animate into a soul armor guardian -- a ten-foot humanoid construct of chained armor plates surrounding a core of pale blue soul energy, wielding a massive great mace. Chains link up behind the party, blocking the door. SWOJ battles the guardian, with [[Xae]] opening with Thunder Wave after transforming back into halfling form.
- **[[S02E23 - Episode Fifty Three (S02E23)|S02E23]]:** After defeating the soul armor guardian, SWOJ collects their gear in the stairwell. Guards led by a commanding female voice break into Crooks' office above, but do not discover the secret door behind the throne. The party descends the spiral staircase into the caverns below, carrying [[Antediluvian Jake]]'s copper orb with them.
