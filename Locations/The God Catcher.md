# The God Catcher

The God Catcher is a massive ancient statue in [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Castle Ward]], depicting a figure reaching skyward with one outstretched hand. An 80-foot diameter stone globe floats magically about 50 feet above the open palm. The statue contains internal tunnels, a spiral staircase running up through the leg and arm, and serves as a hideout for the [[Wererats Of The God Catcher (Shard Shunners)|Shard Shunners]] wererat gang and cultists performing blood rituals. Several inns and taverns, including the [[Hole in the Wall]], are built into its base near the border of the [[North Ward]] and Castle Ward, close to [[Trollskull Alley]].

**Type:** Landmark / Statue
**Ward:** [[Castle Ward]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E06 - Episode Six|Episode S01E06]]

## Description

The God Catcher is one of Waterdeep's many famous statues, located in the Castle Ward near the border with the North Ward and Sea Ward. A narrow trail carved into the statue's arm leads up to the outstretched hand, which is wide enough to walk along safely. The interior contains a wooden spiral staircase winding up through the leg into the upper reaches. Inside, [[SWOJ]] discovered rooms inhabited by wererats (the [[Wererats Of The God Catcher (Shard Shunners)|Shard Shunners]]), as well as a stone chamber covered in bloody handprints and occult symbols depicting three triangles in a hoof-like shape, with braziers and paintings of demonic figures performing blood rites. The palm of the hand, open to the sky, became the site of a deadly confrontation with a blood pool and dead bodies.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E06 - Episode Six|S01E06]]:** [[SWOJ]] emerges inside the God Catcher statue after traveling through an underwater passage from the harbor to the Castle Ward. They discover a trail of blood leading up the internal spiral staircase.
- **[[S01E06 - Episode Six|S01E06]]:** The party encounters [[Wererats Of The God Catcher (Shard Shunners)|Shard Shunner]] wererats inhabiting the tunnels and discovers a cult chamber with bloody handprints, occult hoof-shaped symbols, and dark ritual imagery on the walls.
- **[[S01E06 - Episode Six|S01E06]]:** [[SWOJ]] ascends to the palm of the God Catcher's hand, finding a pool of blood and two dead bodies. They confront [[Fel'Rekt Lafeen]], a drow assassin disguised as a clone of [[Ylka]], who had been executing members of [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]'s old crew from the Sea Plumber.
- **[[S01E06 - Episode Six|S01E06]]:** [[Terry]] follows [[SWOJ]] to the base of the God Catcher and contacts them via sending stone. During the battle, [[Terry]] is killed by the Ylka-clone, his body found run through beneath his cart at the statue's base.
- **[[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]:** The recap references [[SWOJ]] launching themselves from the God Catcher's hand (about 40 feet up) while chasing the Ylka-clone, and discovering [[Terry]]'s death at the base.
- **[[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]:** [[SWOJ]] returns to the God Catcher's base level to visit the [[Hole in the Wall]] halfling tavern. They note similarities between the wererat-infested tunnels here and skittering sounds heard elsewhere.
- **[[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]:** [[Fel'Rekt Lafeen]] is recognized again at the God Catcher, appearing in disguise before revealing his true drow form and offering information to [[SWOJ]].
- **[[S01E09 - Episode Nine|S01E09]]:** The God Catcher's underground areas are referenced as containing tunnels similar to other wererat hideouts, with the same hoof-shaped cult symbol appearing on walls.
- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** [[Laeral Silverhand]] takes [[SWOJ]] from the [[Docks Ward Guardhouse]] to the [[Haventree Estate]], described as a [[Harpers]] safehouse near the God Catcher.
- **[[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]]:** [[SWOJ]] revisits the God Catcher statue, recalling the wererat hideout inside.
