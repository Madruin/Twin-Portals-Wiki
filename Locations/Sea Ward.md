# Sea Ward

A wealthy district of [[Waterdeep]] home to noble estates, temples, and high society. The [[Cassalanter Villa]] is located in the Sea Ward, as is the [[Temple of Selune]] where [[Agorna]] provides healing services.

## Notable Locations

- [[Cassalanter Villa]] -- Estate of the [[Cassalanter Family  Cult Of Asmodeus|Cassalanter family]]
- [[Temple of Selune]] -- Temple where [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] receives healing
