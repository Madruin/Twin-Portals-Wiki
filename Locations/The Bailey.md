# The Bailey

The Bailey is the main courtyard of [[Zhentil Keep]], the floating fortress in the [[Shadowfell]] that serves as [[Rowan Crooks]]' secret base of operations. When SWOJ infiltrated the keep disguised as [[Jolly Gents]], the Bailey was the bustling center of activity -- a well-maintained compound filled with dozens of Gents training, sparring, and socializing around a central teleportation circle. It was here that [[Lark]] fought [[Ann Jane Flip-Flap]] on the Bridge of Destiny and received critical intelligence about the keep's layout.

**Type:** courtyard / fortress compound
**Region:** [[Zhentil Keep]], [[Shadowfell]]
**First appearance:** [[S02E22 - Episode Fifty Two LIVE (S02E22)|S02E22]]

## Description

The Bailey sits within the outer walls of Zhentil Keep, surrounding the central keep tower (an eight-story structure with a crumbled top). The courtyard is surprisingly well-maintained despite the fortress's general state of decay. Facilities include an eating hall, an obstacle course, an archery range, a training area, a garden filled with stone statues near the keep entrance, and the armory -- a red-roofed building stocked with camouflage cloaks bearing the Jolly Gents' forest camouflage pattern, as well as finely crafted bows, swords, knives, and guerrilla-fighting tools.

At the center of the Bailey stands a teleportation circle on a raised dais, used by Rowan Crooks to travel between the keep and destinations such as the [[Radiant Citadel]]. The Bridge of Destiny is a sparring platform -- a log set over a mud pit on two stands -- where Jolly Gents test their combat skills. Various buildings line the courtyard perimeter, connected by paths of trampled mud showing heavy foot traffic.

## Notable Events

- **[[S02E22 - Episode Fifty Two LIVE (S02E22)|S02E22]]:** SWOJ arrives at the Bailey after being escorted by [[Long Mary]] and [[Rambling Bill]], who caught them sneaking through the keep. The party convinces Bill to cooperate and subdues Long Mary in a hallway before reaching the courtyard. They observe dozens of Jolly Gents assembled to welcome [[Rowan Crooks]], who arrives via the teleportation circle accompanied by a halfling lieutenant.
- **[[S02E22 - Episode Fifty Two LIVE (S02E22)|S02E22]]:** The party dons Jolly Gents camouflage cloaks from the armory and attempts to cross the Bailey unnoticed. [[Lark]] is spotted by [[Ann Jane Flip-Flap]], who challenges him to spar on the Bridge of Destiny. During the fight, Ann Jane reveals under her breath that only she and [[Antediluvian Jake]] have free will among the Gents. She shares critical intel: Crooks' office is on the sixth floor next to the throne room, the vault is heavily trapped and only lieutenants can access it, and [[Little Joan]] may be developing free will. Lark deliberately loses the fight to avoid attention, tumbling into the mud.
- **[[S02E22 - Episode Fifty Two LIVE (S02E22)|S02E22]]:** SWOJ walks across the Bailey in disguise, receiving nods and waves from unsuspecting Jolly Gents. They pass the sparring area and training grounds, then enter the keep tower through the main entrance. [[Helena Van Der Graff]] orders them to help clean up for Crooks' arrival, inadvertently giving them cover to move freely inside.
- **[[S02E24 - Episode Fifty Four (S02E24)|S02E24]]:** The soul engine is lifted upwards towards the Bailey as [[Rowan Crooks]] addresses roughly 50 assembled Jolly Gents in the courtyard. A teleportation circle near the sphere marks where Crooks arrived. The confrontation between SWOJ and Crooks escalates in the Bailey before the keep begins to collapse.
