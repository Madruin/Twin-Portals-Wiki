# Malar's Realm (Afterlife/Astral Plane)

A wild, otherworldly forest realm belonging to the deity [[Malar]], the Beast Lord, located on the Astral Plane. [[SWOJ]] traveled here in spirit form after a [[The Necromancer|necromancer]] sent [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] and [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] into the afterlife to retrieve [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]'s soul, using Lark's majestic red hat as the vessel to bring them all home. The realm features a massive mountain with an enormous ash tree at its volcanic caldera summit, four seasonal streams, and increasingly intense red lightning beneath a swelling harvest moon.

**Type:** Wilderness / Divine Realm
**First appearance:** [[S01E21 - Episode Twenty One|S01E21]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

Malar's Realm presents as a vast, primordial landscape dominated by a single enormous mountain resembling a Yellowstone-scale volcano with gentle slopes. At the base, golden plains give way to a dense forest of unnaturally large trees -- some recognizable, others wholly alien -- that grow progressively larger ascending the mountainside. At the summit, a colossal ash tree rises from the volcanic caldera, framed against an oversized harvest moon.

The realm is organized by seasonal magic: the lower slopes feel like spring and summer, with life and faith energy flowing through the environment. Four streams run from the mountain, connected to the seasons. Deeper down, the mountain's base is shrouded in impenetrable shadow -- a pool of darkness concealing the [[Shadowfell]] below.

The atmosphere grows increasingly hostile as events unfold: red lightning strikes the mountaintop (a sign of dark magic rather than natural weather), thunder rolls more frequently, and the moon reddens as [[Malar]]'s influence intensifies. Vicious tribesfolk -- disciples of the Beast Lord -- stalk the forest, killing any animal on sight and leaving carnage in their wake.
