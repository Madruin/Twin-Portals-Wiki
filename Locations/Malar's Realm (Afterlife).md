# Malar's Realm (Afterlife/Astral Plane)

A wild, stormy forest realm belonging to the deity Malar. Features a mountain with an enormous ash tree at its volcanic caldera summit, four seasonal streams, and increasingly intense lightning and a reddening moon. The party enters via the necromancer's magic and exits through the hat's portal.
