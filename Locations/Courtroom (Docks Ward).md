# Courtroom (Docks Ward)

The courtroom within the [[Dock Ward]] guardhouse where [[SWOJ]]'s trial is held after their arrest during the Founders Day chaos. A modest room with a few rows of benches, a podium, and lecterns for judge and defendant, it becomes the site of dramatic revelations -- a [[doppelganger]] is exposed impersonating [[Captain Hyustus Stagget]], a [[grell]] crashes through the window with paralyzing tentacles, and an [[intellect devourer]] attempts to bore into party members' minds -- transforming a routine trial into a life-or-death battle.

**Type:** Interior / Judicial
**Ward:** [[Dock Ward]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

The courtroom is a relatively small, functional room inside the Docks Ward guard station -- not a grand vaulting chamber but a practical judicial space. It contains a few rows of wooden benches for spectators and witnesses, a podium, a lectern for the presiding judge, a lectern for the defendants, and a couple of chairs near the judge's position for witnesses and the clerk. Windows along one wall let in daylight, though these same windows become entry points for attacking creatures during the trial.

The guard station below the courtroom contains prison cells where SWOJ is held overnight, with their gear stored down the hall around a corner. The courtroom is accessed by stairs from the prison level.

## Notable Events

- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** [[Captain Hyustus Stagget]] visits SWOJ in their cell before the trial, warning them that the originally scheduled magistrate is corrupt and paid off by the [[Cassalanter]] family
- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** SWOJ discovers that the real Stagget has been replaced by a [[doppelganger]] -- the impersonator is exposed when its behavior and mannerisms don't match; the creature is revealed underneath when poison from the grell's tentacles interferes with its shapeshifting magic
- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** The corrupt magistrate Umberto Stonemero is replaced at the last moment by [[Laeral Silverhand]], the Open Lord of Waterdeep herself, who presides over the trial personally
- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** [[Bartolo]], a sympathetic guard, reads the charges against SWOJ; [[Tagga]] testifies as a character witness on behalf of the party; [[Laeral Silverhand]] expresses amusement that "Silver Waves of Justice" sounds like a political statement about her title
- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** A [[grell]] -- a floating brain-like creature with a beak and paralyzing tentacles, similar to those encountered in the [[Xanathar]]'s Lair -- smashes through the courtroom window, seizes the doppelganger-Stagget, and begins dragging it out
- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** SWOJ fights the grell in the courtroom while manacled; the creature paralyzes [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] with venomous tentacle barbs and targets [[Laeral Silverhand]] before being killed -- splattering brains across Scoot and the doppelganger-Stagget
- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** An [[intellect devourer]] attempts to bore into party members' minds during the chaos, dealing psychic damage and intruding on their consciousness; [[Jarlaxle|Zardos Zord]] guards the door to protect [[Tagga]] and other witnesses during the fighting
