# Teleportation Circle (Zhentil Keep)

A permanent teleportation circle located on a stone pedestal in [[The Bailey]] courtyard of [[Zhentil Keep]]. [[Rowan Crooks]] used it to travel to and from the fortress, including trips to the [[Radiant Citadel]]. During the sky-falling battle that destroyed the keep, the circle became a critical objective as Crooks attempted to reach it to escape. [[Ylka]] destroyed the pedestal with a shatter spell while diving through the air, though the magical glyphs persisted briefly, hovering where the platform had been.

**Type:** magical structure
**Parent location:** [[Zhentil Keep]], [[The Bailey]]
**First appearance:** [[S02E24 - Episode Fifty Four (S02E24)|S02E24]]
**Destroyed:** [[S02E24 - Episode Fifty Four (S02E24)|S02E24]]

## Description

The teleportation circle sits on a raised stone pedestal in the Bailey area of Zhentil Keep, roughly a hundred feet from the central sphere where [[Rowan Crooks]] addressed the assembled [[Jolly Gents]]. It is a standard permanent teleportation circle inscribed with arcane glyphs, used by Crooks as his primary means of transit between the fortress and other locations. Notes found in [[Friar Truck]]'s laboratory confirm that Crooks had been traveling via this circle to the [[Radiant Citadel]] in the Ethereal Plane, as part of his scheme to reach [[Kalimvoire's Wall]].

During the destruction of [[Zhentil Keep]], [[Little Joan]] held onto the pedestal as it tumbled through the sky, gripping a piece of [[Soul Stone]] in one hand and a metal hook near the circle's glyphs in the other, keeping herself anchored to the still-intact platform.

## Notable Events

- **[[S02E24 - Episode Fifty Four (S02E24)|S02E24]]:** After SWOJ confronts [[Rowan Crooks]] in [[The Bailey]] and the fortress begins to break apart, Crooks uses misty step to disengage from combat and moves toward the teleportation circle, intending to escape with the [[Hungering Shard]]. [[Xae]] encircles him in a wall of fire to slow his progress. [[Ylka]] then takes a running swan dive off the crumbling island, casting shatter at the teleportation circle while falling. The spell pulverizes the stone pedestal, but the magical glyphs remain hovering in the air briefly. [[Little Joan]], who had been holding onto the platform, gives a thumbs up as she tumbles away, unharmed. The destruction of the circle cuts off Crooks' escape route and forces the final confrontation.
