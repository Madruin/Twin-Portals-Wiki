# Trollskull Alley

A narrow, cobbled alley in [[Waterdeep]]'s [[North Ward]] where [[Trollskull Manor]] stands. Home to a cluster of small businesses and a tight-knit community that became deeply enmeshed with [[SWOJ]]'s adventures. The alley is the site of the catastrophic fireball explosion that marks a turning point in Season 1.

**Type:** Street / Neighborhood
**First appearance:** [[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]]
**Total appearances:** 11+ episodes

## Description

Trollskull Alley is a quiet side street lined with shops and residences. Key neighbors include [[Fela Lefaliir]] (herbalist at Corellon's Crown), [[Vincent Trench]] (private investigator whose office conceals a portal), and various other shopkeepers. The alley gives [[Trollskull Manor]] its name and serves as the community hub for the [[Trollskull Regulars]] and the [[Trollskull Alley Irregulars]].

The alley's character changed dramatically after the fireball explosion during the grand opening of The Short Rest ([[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]), which killed ten or more people and left scorch marks and devastation across the street. Despite this tragedy, the community rebuilt and the alley remained SWOJ's home turf throughout both seasons.
