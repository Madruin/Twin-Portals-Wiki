# Brigganocks

The Brigganocks are tiny mouse-like fey creatures that live in underground tunnels in the borderlands between the [[Feywild]] and the [[Shadowfell]]. They appear on the surface as dancing lights and their society centers on collecting and preserving [[Wish Stones]] -- crystallized unfulfilled wishes. The [[Strigoi]] steal their wish stones, which are then embedded into the [[Beast Of Erua]]'s scales to fuel its movement, making the Brigganocks indirect victims of the Season 2 opening arc's central conflict.

**First appearance:** [[S02E01 - Episode Thirty-One]]
**Total appearances:** 3 episodes (S02E01, S02E02, S02E05)

---

## Overview

The Brigganocks inhabit [[Brigganock's Tunnels]], a network of underground passages beneath the borderlands where the Feywild and Shadowfell meet. On the surface they manifest as dancing lights, but underground they live as a small community of mouse-like creatures devoted to the care and preservation of wish stones. These wish stones contain the unfulfilled wishes of individuals -- each one holding a person's deepest desire that can be heard when touched.

The Brigganocks' way of life came under threat from the Strigoi, bat-like creatures that raid the tunnels to steal wish stones. The stolen stones were embedded into the Beast of Erua's scales, powering the massive construct that terrorized the Shadowfell borderlands. When SWOJ investigated the Beast of Erua in the opening arc of Season 2, the Brigganocks' plight was part of the larger web of exploitation orchestrated through the stolen [[Dynamo Of Possibility]].

After the Beast of Erua was defeated and the party returned to [[Waterdeep]], the Brigganocks were referenced again as a possible lead for investigating the three-year time displacement SWOJ experienced. Their connection to wish stones and time magic made them persons of interest in understanding what had happened during the party's absence.
