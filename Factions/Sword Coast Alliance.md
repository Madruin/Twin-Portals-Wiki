# Sword Coast Alliance

A coalition of [[Waterdeep]], [[Baldur's Gate]], [[Neverwinter]], [[Luskan]], and allied states, formed in the wake of the shared threats posed by [[Manshoon]], the [[Cult of the Dragon]], and mysterious portals opening along the coast. The Alliance formally chartered the [[Guild Of The Silver Waves Of Justice]] in S02E38.

**First appearance:** Episode S02E38
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Overview

The Sword Coast Alliance represents one of the most significant political developments in the Twin Portals campaign -- the unification of formerly rival (and recently warring) city-states along the [[Sword Coast]] into a formal coalition. The Alliance comprises five major powers:

- **[[Waterdeep]]** -- The City of Splendors, governed by the [[Lords Of Waterdeep|Open Lord and Masked Lords]]
- **[[Baldur's Gate]]** -- A major trading city to the south
- **[[Neverwinter]]** -- The Shining Jewel of the North, led by [[Lord Dagult Neverember]]
- **[[Luskan]]** -- The northern port city, historically a haven for pirates and cutthroats
- Allied states -- Smaller settlements and territories along the coast

This coalition was forged from the ashes of conflict. [[Waterdeep]] and [[Neverwinter]] had been at war over the Cassalanter diamond mines for much of Season 2, with soldiers occupying [[Amphail]] and battles fought at [[Red Larch]]. The alliance became possible when the [[Masks]] mercenary faction withdrew from Neverwinter and both cities faced existential threats from [[Manshoon]]'s forces, the [[Dracolich (Half-Formed)|dracolich]], and the occupation of the [[Radiant Citadel]].

At the grand council meeting in S02E38, representatives from across the Alliance attended the ceremony where [[SWOJ]] received their official [[Guild Of The Silver Waves Of Justice|guild charter]]. The council also expressed concern about mysterious portals opening along the [[Sword Coast]] -- a thread that carries into Season 3.

## Activities

- **S02E38:** The Sword Coast Alliance convenes a grand council meeting attended by representatives from all member states; they formally charter the [[Guild Of The Silver Waves Of Justice]], granting SWOJ recruitment rights, petitioning privileges, safe passage, limited judicial immunity, and franchising rights; the council expresses concern about portals opening along the Sword Coast

## Connections

- Chartered the [[Guild Of The Silver Waves Of Justice]] through the [[Letters Of Charter (Guild Charter)|Letters of Charter]]
- Born from the end of hostilities between [[Waterdeep]] and [[Neverwinter]]
- The [[Neverwinter/Waterdeep Alliance|Neverwinter/Waterdeep Alliance]] reinforcements at the [[Radiant Citadel]] were an early manifestation of this cooperation
- Rising portal activity along the [[Sword Coast]] provides a new common threat for Season 3
