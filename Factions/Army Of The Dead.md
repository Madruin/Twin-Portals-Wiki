# Army Of The Dead

The Army of the Dead is a host of ancient souls raised from the [[Wall Of The Faithless]] by [[Arcturia]]'s ritual during the climactic battle against [[Manshoon]] at the [[Radiant Citadel]]. Manifesting as blue and white spectres wearing armor from forgotten civilizations, the army was led by an orc woman warrior ghost and marched up the crystal spire to engage Manshoon's forces, turning the tide of the final conflict.

**First appearance:** [[S02E36 - Episode Sixty-Eight]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

---

## Overview

During the final battle at the Radiant Citadel in the deep [[Ethereal Plane]], [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] persuaded Arcturia -- [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]'s sister and a powerful transmutation wizard in service to [[Halaster Blackcloak]] -- to perform a ritual at [[Kelemvor]]'s skeleton within the Wall of the Faithless. The ritual summoned an army of ancient souls who had been trapped in the Wall for untold ages. These spectres, glowing blue and white and clad in armor from civilizations long since forgotten, rose as a unified force and marched up the crystal spire toward the Citadel to fight Manshoon's occupying army. An orc woman warrior ghost led the spectral host. Their arrival represented a critical turning point in the battle, providing SWOJ and their allies with the reinforcements needed to break Manshoon's grip on the Citadel.
