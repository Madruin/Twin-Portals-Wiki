# Malar The Beast Lord's Followers

Fierce barbarian warriors devoted to [[Malar]], the Beast Lord -- a deity of hunting, lycanthropy, and blood. In Season 2, these followers appear as war-painted riders who mount peritons and attack ships with weighted nets, crowbars, and explosives. They are connected to both the older [[Cult of Malar]] encountered in Season 1 and the broader threat facing the [[Sword Coast]].

**First appearance:** Episode S02E32
**Total appearances:** 1 episode (see also [[Cult of Malar]] for Season 1 appearances)

## Overview

Malar the Beast Lord's followers are a militant force devoted to the deity of hunting, lycanthropy, and blood. While the [[Cult of Malar]] was first encountered in S01E21 within Malar's afterlife realm -- where wererats, a werewolf, and a hag corrupted the ash tree's waters with blood to ensnare the unicorn [[Lyrian]] -- a more organized and aggressive contingent appears in Season 2.

During [[SWOJ]]'s three-ship assault through the thunderstorm toward the [[Concord Jewel]] in S02E32, the party was attacked by periton riders -- fierce barbarians covered in war paint who chanted discordant melodies coordinating their attacks with the peritons' song. These warriors threw weighted nets to snare soldiers and boarded ships using crowbars and explosives, attempting to disable the fleet.

The periton riders were eventually intimidated by [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]'s bluff about the crew's ability to shift into the Ethereal Plane. The attack was devastating nonetheless -- the [[Hammerhead]] and its entire [[Neverwinter Forces|Neverwinter crew]] were lost during the engagement.

## Known Members

No individual members are identified by name. The faction includes:
- Barbarian periton riders armed with nets, crowbars, and explosives
- The older [[Cult of Malar]] included a werewolf, four wererats, and a hag

## Activities

- **S01E21:** *(as [[Cult of Malar]])* Followers of Malar sacrifice animals and corrupt the ash tree's waters with blood to ensnare the unicorn [[Lyrian]] in Malar's afterlife realm
- **S02E32:** Barbarian periton riders attack the three-ship fleet during the thunderstorm approach to the [[Concord Jewel]]; they throw weighted nets, board ships, and chant coordinated attack melodies; the [[Hammerhead]] is lost during the engagement

## Connections

- Serve [[Malar]], the Beast Lord -- deity of hunting, lycanthropy, and blood
- The [[Cult of Malar]] (S01E21) represents an earlier encounter with Malar's devotees
- The [[Reghed Barbarians]] are also barbarian riders allied with antagonist forces, though their connection to Malar is separate
- Their attack during S02E32 resulted in the loss of the [[Hammerhead]] and its [[Neverwinter Forces|Neverwinter crew]]
