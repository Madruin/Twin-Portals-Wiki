# Neverember's Forces

The military and law enforcement forces loyal to [[Lord Dagult Neverember|Lord Dagult Neverember]], the Lord Protector of [[Neverwinter]]. These forces are responsible for maintaining order in the city, defending against threats, and carrying out Neverember's directives -- including the arrest and interrogation of [[SWOJ]] and their subsequent commission to retrieve the [[Crown of Neverwinter]].

**First appearance:** Episode S02E16
**Total appearances:** 3 episodes

## Overview

Neverember's Forces represent the military apparatus of [[Neverwinter]] under the rule of [[Lord Dagult Neverember|Lord Dagult Neverember]], who rebuilt the city after Mount Hotenow erupted thirty years prior and sealed the original demonic rift in the chasm. His forces maintain law and order through a combination of city guards, soldiers, and officials operating from the [[Hall of Justice]] and [[Castle Never]].

SWOJ's relationship with Neverember's Forces was initially adversarial. When the party was discovered at a subterranean lake beneath [[Neverwinter]], Neverember's guards arrived in force and demanded their surrender (S02E16). [[SWOJ]] was arrested, brought to the [[Hall of Justice]], and subjected to a Zone of Truth interrogation by Neverember himself (S02E17). Once cleared of suspicion regarding [[Shimmer Brackenwell]]'s murder, Neverember released the party and returned their gear, enlisting them to help defend the city when the chasm erupted with shadow creatures.

The relationship shifted to a working alliance when Neverember formally hired SWOJ to retrieve the [[Crown of Neverwinter]] from [[Castle Never]], offering 5,000 gold each plus property as reward (S02E18). He warned them about the [[Masks]] mercenary force pulling out of the city, signaling growing instability.
