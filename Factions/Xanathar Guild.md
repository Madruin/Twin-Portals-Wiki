# Xanathar Guild

A powerful criminal organization operating from the sewers and underground tunnels beneath Waterdeep. Led by [[The Xanathar]] — a paranoid beholder — the guild is one of the two major criminal factions (alongside the [[Zhentarim]]) whose escalating gang war threatens the city throughout Season 1. The guild's operations include kidnapping civilians, breeding [[intellect devourers]] via a mind flayer's spawning pool, running gladiatorial death matches, and controlling the subterranean fortress of Skullport. Unbeknownst to most, the Xanathar itself was secretly being controlled by the mind flayer [[Nihiloor]], who was the true power behind the guild.

**First appearance:** [[S01E01 - Episode One]]
**Total appearances:** 11 episodes
**Status:** Defeated — The Xanathar was killed by SWOJ in [[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six]], and Nihiloor was defeated in [[S01E17 - Episode Seventeen]]. The lair was destroyed by smoke powder explosions.

## Overview

The Xanathar Guild is a sprawling criminal empire centered beneath Waterdeep's Castle Ward. The guild takes its name from its leader — a title passed between successive beholders who murder each other and assume the role. The current Xanathar was a relatively recent replacement, a fact not widely known.

The Xanathar is obsessively paranoid, trusting no humanoid and imagining conspiracies where none exist. The only creature it genuinely cares for is its pet goldfish [[Sylgar]], which is kept in a tank in the beholder's private sanctum. Guild members secretly replace the goldfish whenever it dies, without the Xanathar's knowledge.

The guild's true power, however, lay with [[Nihiloor]] — a mind flayer operating from a spawning chamber deep within the lair. Nihiloor created intellect devourers using an eldritch ritual and a green spawning pool, deploying them as sleeper agents planted inside kidnapped civilians. Nihiloor was ultimately revealed to be telepathically controlling the Xanathar's body itself.

The guild's operations are wide-ranging: kidnapping surface dwellers, deploying grell and intellect devourers as enforcers, running the Arena of Blood and Fortune for gladiatorial entertainment, and controlling Skullport — a hive of criminal activity deep beneath the city. Their territory in the sewers is marked with the Xanathar's symbol: a circle with ten radiating spokes, and yellow signs direct agents through the tunnels to the lair.

The guild's conflict with the [[Zhentarim]] escalated dramatically when the [[Stone of Golorr]] — a sentient artifact key to locating Lord [[Lord Dagult Neverember|Dagult Neverember]]'s hidden gold hoard — was stolen from the guild's possession by [[Dalekar|Dalcar]], a gnome spy. This theft triggered a cascading power struggle involving multiple factions across Waterdeep.

## Known Members

### Leadership
- [[The Xanathar]] — Beholder crime lord and nominal guild leader. Extremely paranoid, ego-driven, and obsessed with its pet goldfish [[Sylgar]]. Fires death rays, disintegration beams, paralysis rays, and fear rays. Secretly controlled by Nihiloor. Killed by SWOJ at the Zadio City Music Hall ([[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]]).
- [[Nihiloor]] — Mind flayer and the guild's true puppetmaster. Operates from a spawning chamber with a green pool that breeds intellect devourers. Controls the Xanathar's body telepathically. Previously encountered (unnamed) torturing [[Floon Blagmaar]] in the sewers ([[S01E02 - Episode Two|S01E02]]). Tackled into the spawning pool by [[Scoot Sparkles]] ([[S01E17 - Episode Seventeen|S01E17]]).

### Inner Circle
- [[Xibrinidas|Nar'l Xibrindas]] — Drow advisor who rose to the upper echelons of the guild despite drow normally being excluded. Personal advisor to the Xanathar. Secretly a [[Bregan D'Aerthe]] double agent working for [[Jarlaxle]]. Went missing in the lair; sought by both [[Thorvin Twinbeard]] and [[Fel'Rekt]]'s agents.
- [[Thorvin Twinbeard]] — Dwarf chief engineer. Maintains a workshop in the lair where he builds machines, including a crushing device for Skullport. Secretly a [[Harpers]] informant who provided intelligence about the Eye of the Stone hidden in [[Sylgar]]'s tank.

### Guild Personnel
- [[Ott Steeltoes]] — Dwarf fish keeper who tends to [[Sylgar]] the goldfish in the Xanathar's private sanctum. Compelled by [[Thaelin]]'s Suggestion spell to retrieve the Eye of the [[Stone of Golorr]] from a locked chest.
- [[Flutterfoot Zipswiggle]] — Gnome jester enslaved by the Xanathar as an entertainer. Wears a jester hat with golden eyes. Gave SWOJ dust of disappearance and warned them of the lair's dangers. Found dead after the lair collapse — his brain eaten by Nihiloor ([[S01E17 - Episode Seventeen|S01E17]]).
- [[Pinker]] — Kenku agent who wore a distinctive pink hat and bore the Xanathar symbol tattooed on his hand. Captured and released by SWOJ ([[S01E02 - Episode Two|S01E02]]). Later defected and rescued [[Tagga]] from the guild's retaliation ([[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]]). Imprisoned again in the arena cells but freed by [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] ([[S01E17 - Episode Seventeen|S01E17]]).
- Zemk — Duergar (gray dwarf) operative stationed in the sewers. Ash-white skin, white hair, red eyes. Could magically enlarge himself. Fought SWOJ with a greataxe and shield, refusing to surrender ([[S01E02 - Episode Two|S01E02]]).
- [[Thaelin]] — Former guild employee (tiefling warlock) who knew the lair's layout. Left the guild because "it wasn't quite what I thought I was going to be doing." Later returned as a guest PC to help SWOJ infiltrate the lair, ultimately sacrificing himself to deliver the Eye of the Stone and trigger the smoke powder explosion ([[S01E16 - Episode Sixteen|S01E16]]–[[S01E17 - Episode Seventeen|S01E17]]).

### Agents and Creatures
- Kenku agents — Multiple kenku operatives who served as the guild's foot soldiers. Four encountered at the Zhentarim warehouse on Candle Lane ([[S01E02 - Episode Two|S01E02]]); twelve more imprisoned in the arena cells beneath the lair ([[S01E17 - Episode Seventeen|S01E17]]).
- Bugbears — Guild shock troops who accompanied the Xanathar in the attack on the Zadio City Music Hall, screaming "For the Xanathar! For the guild!" in Goblin and Common ([[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]]). Several killed by [[Aurinax]] and SWOJ; two captured by the city watch.
- Grell — Floating brain-like creatures with paralyzing tentacles deployed as enforcers in the sewers ([[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]) and at the courthouse ([[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]).
- Intellect devourers — Brain-shaped creatures created by Nihiloor in the spawning pool. Deployed as sleeper agents inside kidnapped civilians, including [[Harold]] ([[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]). [[Terry]], one of Nihiloor's creations, defected and became an ally of SWOJ.
- Kuo-Toa — Two "Avatars of Blibdoolpoolp" used as arena champions in the Arena of Blood and Fortune ([[S01E17 - Episode Seventeen|S01E17]]).

## Activities


- **S01E01:** The Xanathar Guild is identified by [[Jalaster]] as one of the criminal organizations terrorizing Waterdeep, alongside the [[Zhentarim]] and the Black Network.
- **S01E02:** Xanathar Guild kenku agents attack the [[Zhentarim Warehouse|Zhentarim warehouse]] on Candle Lane, killing multiple Zhentarim agents in a bloody raid. They kidnap [[Floon Blagmaar]], mistaking him for [[Renaer Neverember]]. SWOJ tracks them into the [[Waterdeep Sewers|sewers]], following yellow signs, and encounters the duergar Zemk and a tattooed human operative. In the deepest chamber, they discover [[Nihiloor]] (unnamed at this point) torturing a half-orc and cradling an intellect devourer. [[Pinker]] the kenku is captured and reveals the guild's sewer operations.
- **S01E04:** SWOJ discovers Xanathar Guild symbols carved into the sewer walls beneath the Dock Ward while investigating missing persons at the [[Spouting Fish]]. Three grell — floating brain-like creatures deployed by the guild — are fought and defeated. Approximately fifteen kidnapped civilians are rescued.
- **S01E05:** The guild retaliates against SWOJ by ransacking [[Tagga]]'s apartment (Scoot's mother), leaving crow feathers as a calling card. However, [[Pinker]] — the kenku SWOJ previously released — has defected and rescues Tagga before the guild agents can harm her, hiding her in the Honorable Knight statue tenements.
- **S01E07:** The guild is mentioned as a growing threat by [[Alcoria Stonemarrow]], who warns about the connection between the guild, intellect devourers, and mind flayers.
- **S01E11:** [[Jarlaxle]] (as Zardos Zord) identifies [[Nihiloor]] by name as the guild's mind flayer, responsible for creating intellect devourers. He reveals that the [[Stone of Golorr]] was stolen from the guild's possession, triggering the escalating street violence between the Xanathar Guild and [[Zhentarim]].
- **S01E12:** SWOJ traces the Stone of Golorr's chain of possession: it was held by the Xanathar Guild, stolen by [[Dalekar|Dalcar]] (a gnome spy working for [[Lord Dagult Neverember]]), taken by a Zhentarim agent after the fireball explosion at Trollskull Alley, and brought to [[Gralhund Villa]] where [[Yala Gralhund]] now keeps it.
- **S01E15:** Intellect devourers — Xanathar creations planted as sleeper agents inside kidnapped civilians — attack the courthouse during SWOJ's trial, bursting out of spectators including [[Harold]]. A grell crashes through the courthouse window. [[Laeral Silverhand]] crushes the grell with telekinetic power, frees SWOJ, and provides extensive intelligence about the guild: the Xanathar's paranoid personality, the lair's location beneath the Castle Ward, the spawning pool, key personnel ([[Thorvin Twinbeard]], [[Xibrinidas|Nar'l Xibrindas]]), and the Eye of the Stone hidden in [[Sylgar]]'s fish tank. She commissions SWOJ to infiltrate the lair.
- **S01E16:** SWOJ infiltrates the Xanathar's lair with [[Remallia Haventree]] guiding them through the sewers and [[Thaelin]] (a former guild employee) navigating the interior. They encounter [[Flutterfoot Zipswiggle]] the enslaved gnome jester, pass through [[Thorvin Twinbeard|Thorvin]]'s workshop, and reach the private sanctum where [[Ott Steeltoes]] tends to [[Sylgar]]. Thaelin uses Suggestion to have Ott retrieve the Eye. [[The Xanathar]] arrives and attacks — firing a death ray at Thaelin (49 necrotic damage), a paralysis ray at Ylka, and a fear ray at Lark. SWOJ is captured and sent to the Arena of Blood and Fortune.
- **S01E17:** In the Arena, [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] frees [[Pinker]] and twelve other kenku prisoners from the cells below. JB Nevercott ([[Jarlaxle]] in disguise) warns SWOJ that the lair is rigged with smoke powder and reveals a secret exit (south wall, flaming eye symbol). Thaelin delivers the Eye to Xae and fires a Flame Bolt into a crystal chandelier, detonating the smoke powder and collapsing the lair. Beyond the rubble, SWOJ finds [[Flutterfoot Zipswiggle]] dead on a throne (brain eaten by Nihiloor) and reaches the spawning chamber, where [[Nihiloor]] is revealed to be the true puppetmaster — telepathically controlling the Xanathar's body. The Nihiloor-controlled Xanathar fires a disintegration beam that kills [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]], turning him to dust. Scoot tackles Nihiloor into the spawning pool. [[Lyrian]] the crystal unicorn teleports the surviving party to safety at [[Trollskull Manor]].
- **S01E26:** The Xanathar attacks the [[Zadio City Music Hall]] with a force of bugbears during SWOJ's recovery of gold from the Vault of Dragons. The beholder appears "glazed over" and emotionless — possibly still under residual influence from Nihiloor. Combined forces of SWOJ, [[Aurinax]] (in gold dragon form), [[Remallia Haventree]], [[Renaer Neverember]], and [[Bricks And Bracks|Bricks and Bracks]] fight back. Aurinax breathes fire on the Xanathar; Remallia casts fireball; the party hammers it with attacks. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]'s thrown dagger delivers the killing blow, and the Xanathar's corpse slides into the theater lobby for all to see. The remaining bugbears are captured by the city watch. The Xanathar Guild is effectively destroyed.
