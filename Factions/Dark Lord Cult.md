# Dark Lord Cult

The Dark Lord Cult is a group of cultists discovered by [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] operating inside the [[God Catcher]], a massive ancient walking statue in [[Waterdeep]]'s Castle Ward. The cultists imprisoned sailors and performed rituals "for the dark lord of Nexus," and at least one member wore [[House Wavesilver]] insignia -- a disturbing connection to [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]'s noble family.

**First appearance:** [[S01E06 - Episode Six|S01E06]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

---

## Overview

During SWOJ's early investigations in Season 1, the party explored the interior of the God Catcher -- the massive statue shaped like a figure reaching skyward with one outstretched hand. Inside its tunnels, they discovered cultists who had been imprisoning sailors and conducting occult rituals dedicated to "the dark lord of Nexus." The presence of House Wavesilver insignia on at least one cultist hinted at a connection between Lark's noble family and darker forces at work in the city.

The God Catcher itself proved to be a recurring location of intrigue: it also housed a wererat colony (the [[Shard Shunners]]) and hidden chambers containing statues of [[Halaster Blackcloak]] and his apprentices. The Dark Lord Cult's rituals in the palm of the statue's outstretched hand -- involving blood and imprisoned sailors -- represented one thread in the larger web of conspiracies running through Waterdeep's underbelly. Their invocation of "Nexus" and connection to Asmodean worship placed them within the broader [[Cult Of Asmodeus|Asmodean cult]] activity that culminated in the [[Cassalanter Family|Cassalanter]] arc.
