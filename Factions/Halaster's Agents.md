# Halaster's Agents

Individuals who serve [[Halaster Blackcloak]], the legendary mad wizard of [[Undermountain]]. The most prominent agent encountered by [[SWOJ]] is [[Arcturia]], Xae's sister and a powerful transmutation wizard, who was sent to break the [[Wall of the Faithless]].

**First appearance:** Episode S02E35
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Overview

Halaster's Agents are those who carry out the will of [[Halaster Blackcloak]], the immensely powerful and reputedly insane wizard who descended into [[Undermountain]] beneath [[Waterdeep]] ages ago. Though Halaster himself has not appeared directly in the campaign, his influence is felt through the actions of his agents -- most notably [[Arcturia]], [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]'s long-lost sister.

Halaster is described as "the most powerful wizard in the multiverse" by [[Arcturia]], who refers to him as "the master." His connection to [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]'s backstory runs deep: a portrait of Halaster was found in [[Xae's Locket]] at the [[Old Xoblob Shop]] (S01E29), and statues of Halaster and his apprentices were discovered inside the [[God Catcher]] (S01E29). In a dream sequence during S02E38, Xae hears the cryptic message "one sibling freed, five more and nine years remain," suggesting Halaster's hold over Xae's family is far from over.

Arcturia was sent to the [[Radiant Citadel]] to break the [[Wall of the Faithless]] on Halaster's behalf. However, after encountering SWOJ, she was persuaded to switch sides and join the party instead, becoming a key ally in the climactic battles of Season 2.

## Known Members

- [[Halaster Blackcloak]] -- The mad wizard of [[Undermountain]]; the master
- [[Arcturia]] -- Xae's sister; transmutation wizard and shapeshifter; sent to break the [[Wall of the Faithless]]

## Activities

- **S02E07:** [[Arcturia]] (referred to as "Arturia") serves Halaster in the [[Cryptwood]] near [[Amphail]], using transmutation magic to transform people and control swarms of butterflies; she escapes aboard a [[Concord Jewel]]
- **S02E35:** [[Arcturia]] arrives at the [[Wall of the Faithless]] on Halaster's orders to break it; initially hostile (casting disintegrate on [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]), she is eventually persuaded by SWOJ to join them instead
- **S02E36:** [[Arcturia]] performs a ritual at [[Kelemvor]]'s skeleton that raises an army of ancient souls from the Wall, proving instrumental in the final confrontation with [[Manshoon]]

## Connections

- [[Halaster Blackcloak]] operates from [[Undermountain]] beneath [[Waterdeep]]
- [[Arcturia]] is [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]'s sister, creating a deep personal connection to SWOJ
- Halaster's legacy is tied to the [[God Catcher]], where statues of him and his apprentices were found
- The cryptic dream message about "five more siblings" suggests ongoing Halaster storylines into Season 3
