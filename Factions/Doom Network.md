# Doom Network

The Doom Network is an organization in [[Waterdeep]] that contracts adventurers for jobs, operated by [[Skeemo|Skemo]] on behalf of the [[Doom Raiders]]. It represents the Doom Raiders' formalization of their operations within the moderate wing of the [[Zhentarim]], providing a structured way to assign missions and build relationships with adventuring parties across the city.

**First appearance:** [[S02E05 - Episode Thirty Five (S02E05)|S02E05]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes (S02E05, S02E07)

---

## Overview

By the start of Season 2, the [[Doom Raiders]] had evolved beyond a simple adventuring band and musical act into a more organized operation. The Doom Network served as their job-assignment arm -- a Waterdeep-based organization that matched adventuring parties with contracts and missions. This represented the practical fruit of [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]]'s 5,000 gold investment in the Doom Raiders at the end of Season 1.

Operating out of the [[Doom Network Headquarters]] in Waterdeep, the Network was run by [[Skeemo|Skemo]], one of the Doom Raiders' operatives. When SWOJ visited the headquarters, Skemo assigned them the mission to find [[Anya Irmbrusk]], a missing noblewoman known as the Staglass, in [[Amphail]]. Anya had traveled to Amphail to participate in the Right of the Staglass ceremony, seeking to renounce her noble family ties, but had disappeared about a tenday before SWOJ took the case.

The quest led SWOJ north along the Long Road with [[Ziraj The Hunter]] as their guide, deeper into Zhentarim-controlled territory and the broader conflict that would define Season 2. Upon completing the mission and finding Anya alive in a cavern with other captives, SWOJ collected their reward from the Doom Network during a montage sequence.
