# Lizardfolk Of The Mere

The native lizardfolk inhabitants of the [[Mere of Dead Men]], a vast swampland along the [[Sword Coast]]. Represented by the cleric [[Semu]], they worship [[Semuanya]] and have suffered greatly from the [[Cult of the Dragon]]'s occupation of their ancestral lands and the draining of the swamp.

**First appearance:** Episode S02E27
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Overview

The Lizardfolk of the Mere are the indigenous people of the [[Mere of Dead Men]], a treacherous swampland south of [[Neverwinter]]. They are devotees of [[Semuanya]], the lizardfolk deity, and have lived in the swamp for generations.

Their way of life was severely disrupted by the [[Cult of the Dragon]]'s occupation of [[Castle Naerytar]], their ancestral stronghold. The cult's activities drained parts of the swamp and displaced the lizardfolk from their homeland, forcing them to retreat to outlying settlements like the [[Swamp Castle]] roadhouse.

When [[SWOJ]] traveled south to investigate Cult of the Dragon activity, they encountered [[Semu]], a lizardfolk cleric of [[Semuanya]], at the [[Swamp Castle]]. Semu agreed to guide the party through the treacherous [[Mere of Dead Men]] to [[Castle Naerytar]], sharing knowledge of the swamp's dangers including [[Mud Mummies|undead creatures]] lurking in the muck. She asked SWOJ to look for lizardfolk relics in the castle's refuse piles -- artifacts of her people's heritage lost during the cult's occupation. Semu parted ways with the group at the castle perimeter, unwilling to venture closer to the cult's stronghold.
