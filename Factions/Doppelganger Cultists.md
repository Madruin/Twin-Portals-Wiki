# Doppelganger Cultists

The Doppelganger Cultists are a group of masked attackers led by [[Zilka]] who violently crashed the [[Cassalanter Family|Cassalanter]] party in Season 1. Wearing golden grinning masks and red leather armor and robes, the cultists swung through the ballroom windows, attacked nobles, painted blood patterns on the walls, and performed occult rituals. Their assault marked a dramatic escalation in the Waterdeep power struggles and introduced Zilka -- [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]'s doppelganger double -- as a recurring figure in the campaign.

**First appearance:** [[S01E09 - Episode Nine|S01E09]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

---

## Overview

During SWOJ's attendance at the Cassalanter party, the celebration was shattered when Zilka and her cultists crashed through the ballroom windows. At least 8-10 cultists participated in the attack, plus Zilka herself and three other figures who entered via the windows. The cultists wore distinctive golden grinning masks and red leather armor and robes, giving them a theatrical and menacing appearance.

Their actions went beyond simple violence: the cultists attacked nobles, painted blood patterns on the walls in what appeared to be ritual markings, and performed occult ceremonies during the chaos. Zilka, wielding a magical rapier with an infinity symbol on the hilt, fought [[Osvaldo]] alongside SWOJ but was ultimately knocked unconscious and captured.

Zilka proved to be a doppelganger impersonating Ylka's appearance -- a body double whose existence raised profound questions about Ylka's own identity. After capture, Zilka could not speak due to a magical effect and wore two rings: a silver Ring of Disguise Self and a brass ring of unknown function. She later escaped from city watch custody, only to be found impaled through the heart in the [[Gralhund Family|Gralhund]] Crypt in S01E13, where she used her dying moments to draw the symbol of [[Cult Of Asmodeus|Asmodeus]] and point upward to warn of cultists above.
