# Waterdeep Military

The armed forces of [[Waterdeep]], the City of Splendors. During Season 2, the military is engaged in a war with [[Neverwinter]] over the Cassalanter diamond mines, occupying [[Amphail]] and patrolling the roads between the cities. The war effort suffered a devastating defeat at [[Red Larch]], leading to desertions including the [[Hare Company]].

**First appearance:** Episode S02E06
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Overview

The Waterdeep Military represents the armed forces of one of the largest and most powerful cities on the [[Sword Coast]]. During the three-year time skip between Season 1 and Season 2, a conflict erupted between [[Waterdeep]] and [[Neverwinter]] over the Cassalanter diamond mines and the political power vacuum left by the fall of the [[Cassalanter Family]]. By S02E06, this conflict had escalated into open warfare.

Waterdeep soldiers occupied [[Amphail]], a strategic town between the two cities, and patrolled the [[Long Road]] and surrounding countryside. However, the military suffered a major defeat at the Battle of [[Red Larch]], a turning point that demoralized the army and led to significant desertions. The [[Hare Company]] -- a squad led by [[Jonas]] with wizard [[Nina Blackthorn]] -- was among those who deserted after Red Larch, turning to banditry on the roads they once patrolled.

The war between Waterdeep and Neverwinter eventually ended when the [[Masks]] mercenary faction withdrew from Neverwinter and both cities faced the existential threats of [[Manshoon]]'s forces and the chasm crisis. This rapprochement led to the [[Neverwinter/Waterdeep Alliance|Neverwinter/Waterdeep Alliance]] and ultimately the [[Sword Coast Alliance]].

In addition to the ground forces, Waterdeep maintains aerial military capabilities through the [[Griffin Cavalry Of Waterdeep]], who were present at the [[Spiral Cavern]] to assist with refugee reception (S02E37), and the regular [[Waterdeep City Watch]] for law enforcement.
