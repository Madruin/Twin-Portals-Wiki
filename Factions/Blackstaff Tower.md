# Blackstaff Tower

Blackstaff Tower is the wizards' guild and tower in [[Waterdeep]], a longstanding institution of arcane power on the [[Sword Coast]]. In the Twin Portals campaign, the tower is referenced as both a location and an organization that SWOJ's allies sought to recruit for the war effort against [[Manshoon]]'s forces.

**First appearance:** [[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes (S01E15, S02E31)

---

## Overview

Blackstaff Tower is one of Waterdeep's most powerful magical institutions. When [[Laeral Silverhand]] first took [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] under her wing as personal agents in Season 1, she told them they could drop her name at Blackstaff Tower for assistance -- though she cautioned them not to trust the wizards there with valuables.

The tower became strategically relevant again in Season 2 when SWOJ assembled allies for the assault on the [[Spiral Cavern]] and the [[Radiant Citadel]]. [[Jarlaxle  Zardos Zord|Zardos Zord]], departing for Waterdeep via [[Mellafarne]], promised to contact both Blackstaff Tower and the [[Griffin Cavalry Of Waterdeep|Griffin Riders]] to request military reinforcements. Waterdeep is described as home to the [[Masked Lords Of Waterdeep|masked lords]], Blackstaff Tower, and the Griffin Riders -- the city's major power centers that SWOJ hoped would send aid against the forces occupying the spiral cavern.

*No individual members of Blackstaff Tower are named in the transcripts. The institution is historically led by the Blackstaff, Waterdeep's archmage.*
