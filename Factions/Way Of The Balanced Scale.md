# Way Of The Balanced Scale

An order of metallic dragons dedicated to maintaining balance in the world, allied with [[Silver Waves Of Justice (SWOJ)|SWOJ]] through their connection to [[Mr. E]] and his sensei [[Mr. Ziyagi]]. The order's members serve as mounts, allies, and aerial support for SWOJ during the Neverwinter campaign. Their motto is "keep the balance."

**First appearance:** Episode [[S02E19|S02E19]] (as "Way Of The Balanced Scales"), [[S02E28|S02E28]] (as "Way Of The Balanced Scale")
**Total appearances:** 4+ episodes (S02E19, S02E28, S02E29, S02E30)

## Overview

The Way of the Balanced Scale (also referred to as "Way of the Balanced Scales") is an order of friendly dragons who share a philosophy of maintaining cosmic balance. They are connected to SWOJ through [[Mr. E]], the party's dragonborn warlock, and his late sensei [[Mr. Ziyagi]]. The order's dragons arrive at [[Neverwinter]] led by [[Mellafarne]] (an ancient bronze dragon) alongside Mr. E, providing critical aerial support during the escalating threats to the [[Sword Coast]].

Several members of the order were captured by the [[Cult of the Dragon]] and taken to [[Castle Nehri Tar]] for use in dracolich creation rituals, prompting SWOJ's rescue mission that forms a major arc of late Season 2.

## Connection to Mr. E and Mr. Ziyagi

[[Mr. E]] is a dragonborn warlock who trained under [[Mr. Ziyagi]], a sensei who was discussed during the Season 1 talkback panel as a character Robert made up as a joke that became integral to the story. After Mr. Ziyagi's death, Mr. E returned with a warlock spellbook. Mr. E later took up residence at [[Castle Waterdeep]], where he served as a dragon administrator, using the [[Dragon Staff of Ahghairon]] and a special elixir to vet incoming dragons.

Mr. E's connection to the dragon order led to the Way of the Balanced Scale providing military support to Neverwinter. He communicates with the dragons via a whisper device and personally leads their flights.

## Activities by Episode

- **[[S02E19]]:** [[Mr. E]] and [[Mellafarne]] arrive at Neverwinter with a flight of dragons -- [[Jack]], [[Jill (Iglemar)|Jill]], [[Chip]], and [[Dale]]. Their faction is introduced as "the Way of the Balanced Scales" with the motto "keep the balance." SWOJ rides the dragons to Amphail. During the infiltration of the Spiral Cavern, Chip and Dale serve as a diversion and are captured by barbarians and periton riders. Mr. E promises to return and rescue them.
- **[[S02E28]]:** SWOJ undertakes a mission to rescue the missing friendly dragons from [[Castle Nehri Tar]], where the [[Cult of the Dragon]] is performing dracolich creation rituals. [[Jill (Iglemar)|Jill]] is found held in a trance by the Cult; SWOJ breaks her free by reminding her of balance. She freezes the pursuing cultists to aid their escape. [[Jack]]'s soul is drained into a bronze phylactery by [[Velia Shadowmantle]] in the Sunken City.
- **[[S02E29]]:** The order's captured members' souls are at stake during the Trial of [[Jergal]]. Allied dragons -- including Jill -- arrive to rescue SWOJ at the climax of the episode.
- **[[S02E30]]:** The order's rescued dragons now serve as SWOJ's mounts for the return flight from Castle Nehri Tar. Three members serve as primary mounts: [[Jill (Iglemar)|Jill]] (carries Ylka and Xae, leads the formation), [[Grage]] (carries Scoot), and [[Argentis]] (carries Lark). The flight approaches the [[Moonhole]] near Amphail. SWOJ regroups in Neverwinter.

## The Dracolich Crisis

The Cult of the Dragon targeted members of the Way of the Balanced Scale for their dracolich creation rituals at Castle Nehri Tar. The cult's operation involved:
- Capturing dragons and holding them in magical trances.
- Draining dragon souls into phylacteries (as with Jack's soul being drained into a bronze phylactery).
- [[Velia Shadowmantle]] serving as the primary ritualist.

SWOJ's rescue mission disrupted the ritual and freed several dragons, though not without cost -- Jack's soul was lost in the process. The rescued dragons became key military assets for the defense of Neverwinter.
