# Reghed Barbarians

Northern barbarian clans from the icy reaches beyond the [[Sword Coast]], allied with [[Manshoon]]'s forces during the events of Season 2. The Reghed are present in the [[Spiral Cavern]] at [[Amphail]] with their peritons and are also massing near [[Luskan]] as a separate threat.

**First appearance:** Episode S02E19
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Overview

The Reghed Barbarians are fierce northern clans who have allied with [[Manshoon]] and his [[Zhentarim]] forces during the campaign's second season. Traditionally hailing from the frozen wilderness far north of the [[Sword Coast]], the Reghed's alliance with Manshoon represents a significant expansion of the villain's reach -- combining urban criminal networks with the raw military power of barbarian war bands.

When [[SWOJ]] and their allies scouted the [[Spiral Cavern]] beneath the [[Sword Mountains]] at [[Amphail]], they discovered a massive military staging operation. The Reghed Barbarians were among the assembled forces, alongside [[Zhentarim]] soldiers, [[Cult of the Dragon]] cultists, and other mercenaries. The barbarians had brought their peritons -- fierce flying creatures used as war mounts -- into the cavern, making them a potent aerial threat.

Most alarmingly, the Reghed captured [[Chip and Dale|Chip and Dale]], friendly dragons from the [[Way of the Balanced Scales]], near the cave entrance -- demonstrating their capability to neutralize even dragon-level threats. Beyond the Spiral Cavern operation, intelligence indicated that additional Reghed forces were massing near [[Luskan]], representing a separate axis of the larger threat to the [[Sword Coast]].
