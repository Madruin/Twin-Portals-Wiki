# Shadar-Kai

Shadow elves native to the [[Shadowfell]] who serve the [[The Raven Queen|Raven Queen]] (also called "the Raven Mother"). The Shadar-Kai are an elven people adapted to the dark mirror plane, acting as scouts, guides, and servants of their deity. Three recurring Shadar-Kai -- [[Blish]], [[Mari]], and [[Idae]] -- serve as SWOJ's primary contacts in the Shadowfell across multiple visits.

**First appearance:** Episode S02E02
**Total appearances:** 3 episodes

## Overview

The Shadar-Kai are encountered by [[SWOJ]] on two separate journeys into the [[Shadowfell]], with the same trio -- [[Blish]], [[Mari]], and [[Idae]] -- greeting the party each time. They serve as both warners and informants, providing crucial knowledge about the dangers of the Shadowfell and the activities of its denizens.

During SWOJ's first visit to the Shadowfell (early Season 2), the three Shadar-Kai appeared at the border between the Feywild and the Shadowfell, warning the party about the [[Beast of Erua|Great Beast of Erua]] -- a colossal creature that roamed the dark plane -- and ominously cautioning them to "beware the bag man." They directed SWOJ toward the Shadow version of [[The Way Inn]] for shelter.

When SWOJ returned to the Shadowfell later in Season 2 via the [[Raven Queen Shrine]] beneath the [[Shard of Moonlight]], the same three Shadar-Kai were waiting to greet them. This time, they provided critical intelligence about a new threat: a dark sun had appeared in the sky approximately ten days prior (revealed to be [[Zhentil Keep|Zentil Keep]], [[Rowan Crooks]]' floating fortress) and was absorbing the souls of the dead, disrupting the natural order of the Shadowfell. Wyvern patrols circled the dark sun, and a library had fallen from it to the ground below.

The Shadar-Kai also served as a link between SWOJ and their ally [[Mr. E]], who departed with the three shadow elves on a separate mission while SWOJ continued to the [[Sepulchre of the Raven Queen]] and eventually the [[Dark Market]].

## Known Members

- [[Blish]] -- One of three Shadar-Kai who greets SWOJ on both visits to the Shadowfell. A servant of the Raven Mother.
- [[Mari]] -- One of three Shadar-Kai who greets SWOJ on both visits to the Shadowfell. A servant of the Raven Mother.
- [[Idae]] -- One of three Shadar-Kai who greets SWOJ on both visits to the Shadowfell. Provides the most detailed briefing about the dark sun situation, explaining its soul-absorbing properties and the disruption to the natural order.

## Activities

- **S02E02:** SWOJ emerges from the [[Brigganock's Tunnels|Brighanox tunnels]] into the [[Shadowfell]]'s windswept darkness and encounters three Shadar-Kai -- [[Blish]], [[Mari]], and [[Idae]] -- who serve the Raven Mother. The shadow elves warn the party about the [[Beast of Erua|Great Beast of Erua]], explain that the Rose Knight's mausoleum sits atop the creature's back, and ominously caution them to "beware the bag man." They point SWOJ toward the Shadow version of [[The Way Inn]] for shelter.
- **S02E20:** SWOJ arrives in the [[Shadowfell]] via the [[Raven Queen Shrine]] portal beneath the [[Shard of Moonlight]] in [[Neverwinter]]. They are immediately greeted by the three familiar Shadar-Kai: [[Idae]], [[Mari]], and [[Blish]] -- the same trio from their earlier visit.
- **S02E21:** The Shadar-Kai brief SWOJ on the dire situation: a dark sun (actually [[Zhentil Keep|Zentil Keep]]) appeared in the Shadowfell sky approximately ten days ago and has been absorbing the souls of the dead, disrupting the natural order. Wyvern patrols circle the dark sun, and a library fell from it to the ground below. [[Mr. E]] departs with the Shadar-Kai on a separate mission, while SWOJ continues to the [[Sepulchre of the Raven Queen]].
