# Citadel Resistance

Civilians and fighters hiding in the cisterns and storm sewers beneath the [[Radiant Citadel]], resisting [[Manshoon]]'s occupation. When the soul conduit pylons were activated and the city's defenses fell, these survivors retreated underground rather than submit to the occupying forces. They provided critical information and support to [[SWOJ]] when the party arrived to liberate the Citadel.

**First appearance:** Episode S02E34
**Total appearances:** 1 episodes

## Overview

When [[Manshoon]]'s agents infiltrated the [[Radiant Citadel]] and activated the hidden [[Soul Conduit Pylons]], the city's defenses collapsed overnight. Guards were locked up or disappeared, the [[Speakers For The Ancestors|Speakers for the Ancestors]] were captured and imprisoned in soul stones, and an enforced curfew was imposed on the population. The concord jewels -- the Citadel's connections to civilizations across the multiverse -- were deactivated to prevent reinforcements from arriving.

In response, a group of civilians and fighters retreated into the cisterns and storm sewers beneath the Citadel, forming an impromptu resistance. These survivors maintained a hidden presence underground, gathering intelligence about the occupation and sheltering those who refused to comply with [[Manshoon]]'s regime. When [[SWOJ]] arrived aboard the [[Ethereal Galleon]] and descended through the storm sewers, they encountered these resistance fighters in a cistern, who shared vital information about the [[Clavager Pedestal]] and the state of the Citadel above.

The Citadel Resistance is distinct from [[The Resistance]] in [[Neverwinter]], though both share the common goal of opposing [[Manshoon]]'s influence.

## Known Members

Specific named members were not identified in the transcripts. The group consisted of ordinary citizens and fighters of the [[Radiant Citadel]] who chose to hide rather than submit to occupation.

### Allies

- [[SWOJ]] -- Arrived to liberate the Citadel and worked with the resistance fighters.
- [[Sholeh]] -- The elder speaker (and ancient brass dragon in disguise) who, once freed, protected civilian survivors at the bottom of the Citadel during the final battle.
- [[Sid Squid]] -- The crawfish vendor who created a distraction for SWOJ by pestering guards at the Preserve of Ancestors' doors.

## Activities

- **S02E34:** SWOJ descends through the storm sewers beneath the [[Radiant Citadel]] to avoid detection. After defeating [[Old Salty]] (a giant crawfish), they discover a cistern full of refugees and resistance fighters hiding from [[Manshoon]]'s forces. The resistance members share information about the [[Clavager Pedestal]] and the state of the Citadel above. A friendly water elemental from the sewers offers to carry SWOJ upward through the sewer system to reach the top of the Citadel, where the speakers are held prisoner.
