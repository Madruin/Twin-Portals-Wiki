# Sea Plum Crew

The crew of the [[The Sea Plum|Sea Plum]], a ship captained by [[Pog Waggletop|Pog Wageltop]] with [[Gildis]] as first mate. The Sea Plum was [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]'s ship before the campaign began, and its sinking in [[Deepwater Harbor]] led to one of SWOJ's earliest adventures. Several crew members became residents of [[Trollskull Manor]].

**First appearance:** Episode S01E06
**Total appearances:** 1 episode (as a faction; individual crew members appear throughout)

## Overview

The Sea Plum Crew are the sailors who served aboard the [[The Sea Plum|Sea Plum]], a vessel closely tied to [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]'s backstory. The ship's captain was [[Pog Waggletop|Pog Wageltop]], a goblin, with [[Gildis]], a human, serving as first mate. [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] himself was a former crewmate, and [[Taiga]] served as another crew member who later became an [[Emerald Enclave]] contact.

The Sea Plum's sinking in [[Deepwater Harbor]] became a key early adventure for [[SWOJ]]. [[Pog Waggletop|Pog]] sent a pigeon message requesting help investigating the wreck, and the party used [[Grinda Garloss]]'s [[Apparatus of Qualish|Apparatus]] to dive to the harbor floor. There they found the hull breached from outside and the cargo hold emptied. The bronze dragon [[Zelifarn]] led them to the wreck and then to a smuggler's cave, expanding the investigation.

After the Sea Plum's loss, several crew members became fixtures at [[Trollskull Manor]]. [[Pog Waggletop|Pog]] took up residence on the roof, tending a pigeon rookery that became the manor's communications hub -- and was devastated when most of his pigeons were killed in the Trollskull Alley fireball explosion (S01E11), though [[Sally]] the pigeon survived. [[Taiga]] stayed at [[House Phaulkonmere]] before eventually joining the [[Circle of Dusk]] druids.

The Sea Plum itself remained a powerful symbol throughout the campaign. It appeared in [[SWOJ]]'s dream sequence at the opening of S02E25, serving as an emblem of comfort, home, and the simple life the party had before their adventures escalated.
