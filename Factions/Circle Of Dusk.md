# Circle Of Dusk

A druidic circle that tends the [[Kryptwood]] near the [[Sword Mountains]], northwest of [[Amphail]]. The Circle of Dusk are guardians of the forest and its rare flora, including [[Moth Weed|mothweed]], a purple moss with powerful countermagic properties. Their glade served as a haven for travelers and refugees until the witch [[Arcturia]] disrupted the region with her transmutation magic.

**First appearance:** Episode S02E05
**Total appearances:** 6 episodes

## Overview

The Circle of Dusk is a small community of druids who have lived in the [[Kryptwood]] for generations, maintaining the balance of the dense coniferous forest between [[Amphail]] and the [[Sword Mountains]]. Led by [[Shala]], the circle tended a wide open clearing marked by old trees, moss, and druidic totems, centered around a great tree.

When [[Arcturia]] established herself in the [[Spiral Cavern]] beneath the mountains, her enchanted mirror began projecting memory-altering mists into the Cryptwood, causing blackouts and disappearances across the region. The druids themselves fell victim to Arturia's transmutation magic -- [[Shala]] was transformed into a badger, [[Ander]] became a talking riddle door, and [[Nuria]] and other members vanished. Only [[Farron]], actually the enchanted bronze dragon [[Zelifarn]], remained at the glade as an amnesiac survivor.

After [[SWOJ]] destroyed Arturia's mirror and freed the captives, the circle reconstituted. [[Leah]], an [[Emerald Enclave]] agent who had been sent to assist the druids, stayed behind to coordinate refugee transport to the [[Radiant Citadel]] and distribute mothweed to those afflicted by the spore illness. The circle later served as crucial intelligence operatives during the war preparations, scouting [[Zhentarim]] and [[Cult of the Dragon]] activity around [[Amphail]] and the [[Spiral Cavern]].

## Known Members

- [[Shala]] -- Lead druid of the circle. Mentored [[Leah]]. Transformed into a badger by [[Arcturia]]'s magic; later freed and resumed scouting operations near Amphail.
- [[Ander]] -- Druid transformed into a talking riddle door by Arturia's magic. Restored when the enchanted mirror was smashed.
- [[Nuria]] -- Youngest member of the circle. Described as nice by [[Farron]]. Went missing from the glade.
- [[Leah]] -- [[Emerald Enclave]] agent who was sent to help the circle. Mentored by Shala. Transformed into a six-eyed beast by Arturia; freed by [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] with mothweed. Later became a full druid of the circle, riding the giant stag beetle [[Craw]].
- [[Kingfisher]] -- A druid operating in the Cryptwood forests near Amphail during the later stages of Season 2.

### Associated

- [[Farron]] / [[Zelifarn]] -- An amnesiac soldier cared for by the druids, actually the bronze dragon Zellopharn under Arturia's enchantment. Learned to play a pipe flute from the druids.
- [[Craw]] -- A giant stag beetle ridden by the druids as a mount, particularly [[Leah]] and [[Shala]].

## Activities

- **S02E05:** The Circle of Dusk is mentioned as tending the [[Kryptwood]] near the [[Sword Mountains]], with knowledge of mothweed needed for [[Tagga]]'s illness.
- **S02E06:** SWOJ reaches the Circle of Dusk glade in the Cryptwood and finds it abandoned. All druids -- [[Shala]], [[Nuria]], [[Ander]], and Lea -- have gone missing. Only [[Farron]], an amnesiac soldier, remains, cared for by the now-absent druids. The mists from [[Arcturia]]'s enchanted mirror have been plaguing the region.
- **S02E07:** SWOJ enters the [[Spiral Cavern]] and discovers the druids transformed by Arturia's magic. [[Ander]] has become a talking riddle door; [[Shala]] was turned into a badger; [[Leah]] was transformed into a six-eyed beast. The party uses mothweed to reverse the transformations and smashes the enchanted mirror, freeing everyone.
- **S02E08:** [[Leah]] stays behind with the Circle of Dusk to coordinate refugee transport to the [[Radiant Citadel]] and ensure mothweed reaches those who need it. SWOJ returns to the Sword Mountains in a montage, rescues all captives, and delivers mothweed to cure [[Tagga]].
- **S02E19:** SWOJ flies to [[Amphail]] on dragonback and meets Circle of Dusk druids [[Leah]] and [[Shala]], now scouting the area with their giant stag beetle mount [[Craw]]. They provide intelligence about the [[Spiral Cavern]] and help stage a diversionary fire to cover SWOJ's infiltration.
- **S02E30:** SWOJ meets [[Kingfisher]], a Circle of Dusk druid operating in the Cryptwood near the devastated [[Amphail]]. Kingfisher reports that [[Neverwinter]] has marshalled military forces in response to the dragon threat and provides local intelligence about the fortified [[The Moonhole|Moonhole]].
