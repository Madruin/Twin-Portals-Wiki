# Doom Raiders

The Doom Raiders are a moderate subfaction within the [[Zhentarim|Zhentarim (Black Network)]], led by the half-elf [[Davil Starsong]]. Operating as both an adventuring band and a political faction, the Doom Raiders opposed [[Manshoon]]'s extremist leadership of the Zhentarim and eventually allied with [[Scoot Sparkles|SWOJ]] to resist the Necromancer's grip on the organization. After Manshoon's destruction in S02E36, the Doom Raiders filled the resulting power vacuum and became the new Zhentarim leadership in Waterdeep.

**First appearance:** [[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]
**Total appearances:** 8+ episodes across both seasons
**Affiliated with:** [[Zhentarim]]

---

## Overview

The Doom Raiders represent the Zhentarim's less extreme wing -- a faction that rejects Manshoon's authoritarian methods of assassination, necromancy, and brute domination in favor of a more pragmatic, locally rooted approach to the Black Network's operations. While still criminals by any measure, the Doom Raiders function as a recognizable adventuring band and musical act in Waterdeep, maintaining a public-facing presence that stands in sharp contrast to Manshoon's shadow armies and clone conspiracies.

The internal split within the Zhentarim is a defining tension of the campaign. Manshoon's division commands assassins, soldiers, [[Reghed Barbarians|Reghed barbarians]], dragon cultists, and undead forces. The Doom Raiders, by contrast, operate through personal relationships, mercantile deals, and cultural influence -- performing music at noble feasts, running a job-assignment network (the [[Doom Network Headquarters|Doom Network]]), and cultivating alliances with adventuring parties like SWOJ.

Their aesthetic is described as "adventurers" -- they dress the part rather than hiding behind masks or black leather. Davil Starsong, the group's leader, is called a "grandstander" who refuses to cover his face, embodying the Doom Raiders' philosophy of operating in the open rather than from the shadows.

### Notes on Members

- **Davil Starsong** is the face of the organization. [[Bonnie]], the doppelganger bartender at the [[Yawning Portal]], telepathically confirmed he was searching for the [[The Stone of Golorr]] when SWOJ first encountered him. He later explained the Zhentarim's internal power struggle directly to SWOJ and requested their financial backing.
- **Yagra** first appeared in S01E01, brawling with tattooed thugs at the Yawning Portal. She sent the mysterious "Y" note to SWOJ in S01E07, connecting them to the Zhentarim's moderate wing. She is described as sober and serious compared to the other bandmates.
- **Ziraj** served as Davil's crew member and was offered by Davil alongside Skeemo when negotiating the 5,000gp deal. He later guided SWOJ north from Waterdeep to Amphail in S02E05-S02E06.
- **Skeemo** operates the **Doom Network**, the Doom Raiders' job-assignment operation in Waterdeep. At the Doom Network headquarters, Skeemo assigned SWOJ the quest to find the missing noblewoman [[Anya Irmbrusk]] (the Staglass) in Amphail.

## Alliance with SWOJ

The relationship between the Doom Raiders and SWOJ is one of the campaign's most enduring alliances, evolving from a chance tavern meeting into a full wartime partnership.

### First Contact (S01E11)

SWOJ first encountered Davil Starsong at the [[Yawning Portal]] during their investigation of the [[The Stone of Golorr]]. Davil introduced himself and his band, boasting about the Doom Raiders' reputation across the Sword Coast. Bonnie's telepathic read confirmed he was also searching for the Stone. Davil attended the grand opening of The Short Rest tavern but fled when a griffin cavalry rider appeared overhead -- a sign of his faction's precarious position within the Zhentarim power structure.

### The Cassalanter Feast Infiltration (S01E27)

When SWOJ needed to infiltrate the Cassalanters' Founders Day feast -- a front for a devil-soul harvest -- the Doom Raiders provided their cover. The band had been booked as the lead musical act at the feast. SWOJ disguised themselves as additional members of the Doom Raiders, entering [[Cassalanter Villa]] alongside Davil, Yagra, and the rest of the band. Davil, true to form, refused to cover his face. The Doom Raiders performed while SWOJ moved through the event, eventually discovering the poisoned food and confronting [[Victoro Cassalanter]] in a battle against spined devils and imps.

### The 5,000 Gold Investment (S01E29)

In the Season 1 finale, following the Cassalanter affair, Davil Starsong made his pitch to SWOJ directly. He explained the Zhentarim's internal power struggle between the Doom Raiders and the Necromancer's (Manshoon's) dominant division. Davil requested a **5,000 gold piece investment** to finance Doom Raiders operations against Manshoon's forces, offering the services of his crew including Ziraj and Skeemo. SWOJ agreed, cementing a formal alliance with the moderate wing of the Zhentarim. This investment bore fruit across Season 2 as the Doom Raiders became reliable allies and operatives.

### The Doom Network (S02E05)

By Season 2, the Doom Raiders had formalized their operations through the **Doom Network**, a Waterdeep-based organization that contracted adventurers for jobs. At the Doom Network headquarters, Skeemo assigned SWOJ the mission to find the missing noblewoman Anya Irmbrusk (the Staglass) in Amphail -- a quest that led SWOJ north along the Long Road and deeper into the Zhentarim conflict.

## The Ethereal War and Capture (Season 2)

### Joining SWOJ's Fleet (S02E31)

When SWOJ assembled their allies for the final assault against Manshoon's forces at the [[Spiral Cavern]] and beyond, the Doom Raiders answered the call. They joined the crew aboard the Ethereal Galleon alongside other allies -- [[Squidly]], [[Fel'Rekt Lafeen|Thelrect Lafina]], [[Shaleheart]] the bulette, and [[Fioleth]] the Ragamuffin. Their role was characteristically musical: the Doom Raiders served as the second musical act aboard the ship, their performances helping to power the spelljamming helm that propelled the fleet. Ylka led them with her concertina in what was described as "very death metal polka."

### Captured by Manshoon (S02E36)

During the battle at the [[Radiant Citadel]] in the ethereal plane, the Doom Raiders were among those captured by Manshoon's forces and held in magical restraints at the bottom of the Citadel. Lark freed them alongside Squidly and Urstul Floxxin, and the Doom Raiders immediately rejoined the fight against the last Manshoon clone. Their liberation was part of the turning point in the final battle that ended with Manshoon's soul being sent to the [[Raven Queen]].

---

## Post-Manshoon: The New Zhentarim (S02E38)

With Manshoon permanently destroyed -- his soul claimed by the Raven Queen, ensuring he could never return -- the Zhentarim entered a power vacuum in Waterdeep. The Doom Raiders, having survived the final battle and maintained their local connections throughout the campaign, filled this void.

By the Season 2 finale, the Doom Raiders are described as the **new local Zhentarim leadership in Waterdeep**, operating from a public-facing office in the city. When [[Simon]], Scoot's father, returned from years lost at sea, he recounted being helped home by "Zhentarim-affiliated pirates" -- people sailing without official charters -- who checked in with the Doom Raiders. [[Tagga|Tega]], Scoot's mother, **winked knowingly** when asked about the Zhentarim, suggesting the organization under Doom Raiders leadership had evolved into something less threatening, or at least something Tega had quiet connections to.

The Doom Raiders performed music at the massive God's Night celebration at Trollskull Manor during Marpenoth, alongside [[Antediluvian Jake]], [[Ann Jane Flip-Flap|Angie]], [[Pinker]], [[Sid Squid]], [[Jarlaxle  Zardos Zord|Zardoz Zord]], [[Volo]], and [[Remalia Haventree|Ramalia]].

## Relationship to the Broader Zhentarim

The Zhentarim's internal division is one of the campaign's structural tensions. The two factions represent fundamentally different visions for the Black Network:

| | Manshoon's Division | Doom Raiders |
|---|---|---|
| **Leadership** | Manshoon (through clones and simulacra) | Davil Starsong |
| **Methods** | Assassination, necromancy, military conquest, soul manipulation | Mercantile deals, music, adventuring contracts, alliances |
| **Aesthetic** | Black leather armor, masks, winged snake sigils | Adventurer garb, open faces, public performances |
| **Bases** | [[Kolat Towers]], [[Spiral Cavern]], [[Radiant Citadel]] | [[Yawning Portal]], [[Doom Network Headquarters]], public office in Waterdeep |
| **Forces** | Soldiers, Reghed barbarians, periton riders, dragon cultists, undead | Band members, contracted adventurers, allied factions |
| **Outcome** | Destroyed (S02E36) | Ascendant -- new Zhentarim leadership in Waterdeep |

The Doom Raiders' rise to power after Manshoon's fall represents a thematic resolution: the moderate, community-rooted faction prevails over the authoritarian one, in part because of its willingness to build alliances rather than rule through fear.
