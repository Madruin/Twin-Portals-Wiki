# Emerald Enclave

A druidic organization dedicated to preserving the natural world and maintaining the balance between civilization and the wild. In the Twin Portals campaign, the Emerald Enclave operates as a nature-oriented faction that provides allies, quests, and connections to the [[Circle of Dusk]] druids. Their iconography -- including unicorn imagery -- resonates with [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]'s Feywild connections.

**First appearance:** Episode [[S01E03|S01E03]]
**Total appearances:** 5+ episodes (S01E03, S01E07, S01E15, S01E16, S02E07)

## Overview

The Emerald Enclave is a widespread druidic organization that operates across the [[Sword Coast]]. Unlike the political factions of Waterdeep, the Enclave focuses on the natural world -- protecting forests, wildlife, and the balance between nature and civilization. In SWOJ's story, the Enclave serves as a questgiver through their agent [[Leah]], a connection point for [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]'s druidic abilities, and a bridge to the [[Circle of Dusk]] druids who become important allies in Season 2.

The Enclave's unicorn iconography first appears when Xaerine recognizes a small wooden talisman -- shaped like a unicorn's head with seven star-shaped tears trickling from its eye, warm and pulsing -- as being from Emerald Enclave druidic imagery. This talisman acts as a compass, swinging toward [[Blue Alley]], and appeared in Xae's hand when she arrived in the psychic version of Waterdeep.

## Connection to the Circle of Dusk

The Emerald Enclave has a direct relationship with the [[Circle of Dusk]], a druid circle that operates in the [[Cryptwood]] forests near the [[Sword Mountains]] and [[Amphail]]. The Enclave sent [[Leah]] on a mission to help the Circle of Dusk druids near Amphail, where she was mentored by druid [[Shala]]. This connection proved crucial in Season 2, as the Circle of Dusk provided intelligence and support for SWOJ's operations against the [[Zhentarim]] and the [[Cult of the Dragon]].

The Circle of Dusk druids tend the [[Circle Of Dusk Glade|Cryptwood glade]] -- a wide open clearing with old trees, moss, and druidic totems featuring a great central tree. They serve the goddess [[Mielikki]] and were nearly wiped out when [[Arturia]] (the Witch of the Woods) captured and transformed them using enchanted magic. SWOJ freed the druids by destroying Arturia's mirror, after which Leah stayed behind to help coordinate refugee transport to the [[Radiant Citadel]].

## The Unicorn Talisman

The [[Unicorn Talisman]] is a key item connected to the Emerald Enclave's iconography:
- Small wooden talisman shaped like a unicorn's head with seven star-shaped tears trickling from its eye.
- Warm and pulsing with magical energy.
- Acts as a compass, swinging toward [[Blue Alley]].
- Appeared in [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]'s hand when she arrived in the psychic version of Waterdeep.
- Recognized by Xae as Emerald Enclave druidic imagery.
