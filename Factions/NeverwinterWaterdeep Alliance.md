# Neverwinter/Waterdeep Alliance

A combined force of soldiers from [[Neverwinter]] and [[Waterdeep]] who arrive as reinforcements aboard a hammerhead ship to support [[SWOJ]] at the occupied [[Radiant Citadel]]. Led by [[Nat]] and [[Janks]], the alliance brings 30 soldiers, 5 casters, and animal scouts.

**First appearance:** Episode S02E35
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Overview

The Neverwinter/Waterdeep Alliance represents a crucial moment in the campaign: the formerly warring cities of [[Neverwinter]] and [[Waterdeep]] setting aside their conflict to unite against the common threat of [[Manshoon]]'s occupation of the [[Radiant Citadel]]. This joint force arrived aboard a hammerhead ship just as [[SWOJ]] was deep in their mission to liberate the Citadel.

The reinforcement force was led by [[Nat]], a griffin rider from [[Waterdeep]] who communicates through sign language and coordinates aerial operations, and [[Janks]], a wizard in a distinctive blue hat who serves as the primary spellcaster among the allied forces. Together they brought 30 soldiers, 5 casters, and animal scouts -- a modest but critical addition to SWOJ's strength.

These reinforcements played an important role in the final episodes of Season 2, assisting with the descent through the [[Radiant Citadel]] and supporting SWOJ's confrontation with [[Manshoon]] at the [[Wall of the Faithless]]. The cooperation between Neverwinter and Waterdeep forces foreshadowed the formal [[Sword Coast Alliance]] established in S02E38.
