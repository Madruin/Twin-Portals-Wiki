# Griffin Cavalry Of Waterdeep

The Griffin Cavalry of Waterdeep is the city's aerial military force, patrolling the skies above [[Waterdeep]] on trained griffins. Across the campaign, they serve as both a symbol of civic authority and a military asset, ultimately providing air support and refugee assistance during the assault on the [[Spiral Cavern]] and the aftermath of the war against [[Manshoon]].

**First appearance:** [[S02E31 - Episode Sixty-One]]
**Total appearances:** 3 episodes (S02E31, S02E37, S02E38)
**Also known as:** Griffin Riders, Griffin Riders of Waterdeep

---

## Overview

The Griffin Cavalry represents one of Waterdeep's premier military institutions, an aerial force of riders mounted on griffins that patrols the city and can be deployed for combat operations along the [[Sword Coast]]. Their presence in the skies is iconic enough that the mere sight of a griffin cavalry rider overhead caused [[Davil Starsong]] to flee the Short Rest tavern during its grand opening in Season 1 -- a sign of the Zhentarim's wariness of Waterdeep's enforcement arm.

When [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] assembled their alliance for the final push against Manshoon's forces, the Griffin Cavalry was one of the key reinforcements they sought. [[Jarlaxle  Zardos Zord|Zardos Zord]], departing for Waterdeep via [[Mellafarne]], agreed to carry messages requesting military support from both the Griffin Riders and [[Blackstaff Tower]].

The cavalry answered the call. By the time the [[Concord Jewel]] crash-landed in the Spiral Cavern carrying SWOJ and refugees from the [[Radiant Citadel]], the Griffin Cavalry was already stationed there alongside [[Neverwinter Forces|Neverwinter soldiers]] and the [[Trollskull Regulars]], receiving refugees and securing the area. After the war's conclusion, Griffin Riders soared above the refugee caravan making its way south to Waterdeep, providing aerial escort for the journey home.
