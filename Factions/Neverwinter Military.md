# Neverwinter Military

The assembled armed forces of [[Neverwinter]], marshalled by [[Renaer Neverember]] in response to the growing dragon and Manshoon threats. The force consists of approximately 500 soldiers and 30 spellcasters, equipped with three airships built using spelljammer helm technology.

**First appearance:** Episode S02E30
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Overview

The Neverwinter Military represents the city's full military capability during the late Season 2 crisis. Under [[Renaer Neverember]]'s leadership, [[Neverwinter]] assembled a formidable fighting force in response to the [[Cult of the Dragon]]'s activities, the [[Dracolich (Half-Formed)|dracolich]] threat, and [[Manshoon]]'s growing power.

The force comprised approximately 500 soldiers and 30 spellcasters, a significant marshalling of military resources for a city still recovering from the chasm crisis. Most notably, three airships were constructed at [[Neverwinter]]'s dry docks using spelljammer helm technology, giving the military an unprecedented aerial capability. These ships -- including the [[Ethereal Galleon]] and the [[Hammerhead]] -- became the centerpiece of the assault on the [[Concord Jewel]] and ultimately the mission to the [[Radiant Citadel]].

The military also provided ongoing support throughout the late campaign, with soldiers stationed at the [[Spiral Cavern]] to guard refugees and support the evacuation effort from the [[Radiant Citadel]] in S02E37.
