# Vintners', Distillers', And Brewers' Guild

A Waterdeep trade guild overseeing the production and sale of wines, spirits, and ales throughout the city. The guild was represented by [[Hammond Kraddoc]] during the tavern licensing process for [[Trollskull Manor]].

**First appearance:** Episode S01E08
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Overview

The Vintners', Distillers', and Brewers' Guild is one of the many trade guilds that regulate commerce in [[Waterdeep]]. As the guild responsible for overseeing the production and distribution of alcoholic beverages, it holds significant influence over the city's tavern and hospitality industry. Any establishment wishing to serve drinks in Waterdeep requires the guild's approval.

The guild became relevant to [[SWOJ]]'s story during the licensing process for [[Trollskull Manor]]. When the party sought to convert the old manor into a functioning tavern (the Short Rest), they had to navigate the Waterdeep guild system, appearing before representatives from multiple trade organizations. [[Hammond Kraddoc]] represented the Vintners', Distillers', and Brewers' Guild at the licensing hearing, alongside representatives from the [[Guild Of Butchers]] ([[Justin Rask]]), the [[Guild Of Apothecaries]] ([[Fala Lefaliir]]), and the [[Fellowship Of Innkeepers]] ([[Broxley Fairkettle]]).

The licensing process, while bureaucratic, was ultimately successful, allowing SWOJ to open the Short Rest and establish [[Trollskull Manor]] as their home base for the campaign.
