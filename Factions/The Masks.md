# The Masks

A mercenary faction operating in [[Neverwinter]] under contract to [[Lord Dagult Neverember]]. All members wear distinctive black masks and black leather armor. The Masks served as the city's dominant security and military force during the war between Neverwinter and [[Waterdeep]], wielding significant political influence over Neverember and operating with carte blanche from the city guard. Behind their official role, they were secretly a front for the [[Zhentarim]], commanded by a clone of [[Manshoon]] operating out of [[Amphail]].

**First appearance:** [[S02E14 - Episode Forty Four (S02E14)|Episode S02E14]]
**Active episodes:** S02E14 through S02E20
**Status:** Dissolved -- withdrew from Neverwinter during the chasm crisis; their Zhentarim nature was subsequently exposed.

---

## Overview

The Masks rose to prominence in Neverwinter during the three-year gap between Season 1 and Season 2 (1493--1496 DR), a period marked by the war between Neverwinter and Waterdeep over the [[Cassalanter]] diamond mines. Officially, they were a mercenary company contracted by Lord Neverember to supplement Neverwinter's military strength. In practice, they became the dominant power behind the throne.

[[Renaer Neverember]] and [[The Resistance]] believed the Masks controlled things behind the scenes, manipulating Lord Neverember and directing city affairs to serve their true masters. The resistance operated from tunnels beneath [[Castle Never]], allied with the carpenters' guild and various other guilds opposed to the Masks' influence.

The Masks' true allegiance was to [[Manshoon]] and the [[Zhentarim]]. A Manshoon clone -- referred to by his agents as "the boss with the mask" or "Nancy" -- commanded the organization from the [[Spiral Cavern]] beneath Amphail, where the Zhentarim had mustered a formidable army including soldiers, assassins, barbarians, peritons, and [[Cult of the Dragon|dragon cultists]].

## Connection to the Zhentarim and Manshoon

The link between the Masks and the Zhentarim was gradually revealed across multiple episodes:

- **[[S02E14 - Episode Forty Four (S02E14)|S02E14]]:** [[Slippery Shim]] reported witnessing an assassin attack a Masks lieutenant on the road from the war front. When stabbed, the lieutenant did not bleed -- a detail suggesting the lieutenant was a Manshoon clone or other magical construct.
- **[[S02E15 - Episode Forty Five (S02E15)|S02E15]]:** [[16 String Jack]], a captured Jolly Gent, revealed that the Masks sent a wizard carrying a mysterious stone to the chasm every month on the full moon, and that the Jolly Gents believed one of the last Manshoon clones was involved in Neverwinter politics -- possibly operating behind one of those black masks. The wizard who didn't bleed when Jack stabbed him had dropped an [[Aboleth Stone]] whose eyes opened upon hitting the ground.
- **[[S02E18 - Episode Forty Eight LIVE (S02E18)|S02E18]]:** [[Urstul Floxxin]], a Zhentarim assassin encountered inside [[Castle Never]], was sent by Manshoon to retrieve the [[Crown of Never]]. During this episode, Neverember warned SWOJ that the Masks mercenary force was pulling out of the city entirely, potentially preparing to claim it for themselves.
- **[[S02E19 - Episode Forty Nine (S02E19)|S02E19]]:** Under interrogation, [[Urstul Floxxin]] confirmed that Manshoon ("the boss with the mask") operated from Amphail and commanded the forces mustered in the Spiral Cavern. SWOJ infiltrated the cavern and witnessed the Manshoon clone (the "Nancy" clone) conferring with an ancient black dragon in elf form, planning to consolidate forces and breach the mountain for aerial access. The extraction data explicitly identified the organization as "Zhentarim / The Masks," also referred to as the "Neverwinter Masks."

The Masks' monthly delivery of aboleth stones to the chasm seal was particularly insidious: while ostensibly maintaining the magical barrier that kept demons from flooding Neverwinter, this operation also gave the Zhentarim direct access to -- and control over -- the city's most critical vulnerability.

## How They Framed SWOJ

One of the Masks' most calculated moves was framing the Silver Waves of Justice for murder to neutralize them as a threat:

1. **The murder:** [[Shimmer Brackenwell]] (also known as [[Slippery Shim]]), a tawny tabaxi smuggler and information broker who had been providing intelligence to SWOJ, was murdered outside the [[Moonstone Mask]].
2. **The impersonation:** The killer wore a red hat and disappeared by vanishing into mist -- deliberately matching the description of [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark Wavesilver]], whose signature collapsible red hat was well-known. This framed SWOJ as the murderers.
3. **The manhunt:** Guards and Masks mobilized across the city with wanted posters. The Moonstone Mask became a crime scene. SWOJ was forced to use disguises and deception to move through Neverwinter while being hunted.
4. **The disappearance:** [[Fel'Rekt Lafeen]], the Bregan D'aerthe agent who had introduced SWOJ to Slippery Shim, disappeared the same night as the murder -- possibly killed as well, or possibly the one who impersonated Lark (he possessed a ring of disguise).

SWOJ was eventually cleared of the murder charges when they were arrested by Neverember after the chasm expedition ([[S02E17 - Episode Forty Seven (S02E17)|S02E17]]) and subjected to a Zone of Truth interrogation at the [[Hall Of Justice]].

## Key Operations

### The Chasm Convoys
Every month on the full moon, the Masks dispatched a small convoy led by a wizard carrying an [[Aboleth Stone]] down into the [[The Chasm|Neverwinter Chasm]] to feed the seal maintained by [[The Hungry Elder]], an enormous aboleth-like creature in a subterranean lake. This operation kept the chasm sealed and prevented demons from flooding the city -- but it also gave the Zhentarim leverage over Neverwinter's survival.

### Infiltration of the Hall of Justice
SWOJ obtained Masks uniforms (black leather armor, black masks, and black cloaks) from [[Squidly]] at the [[House Of A Thousand Faces]], the Harper base in Neverwinter. Disguised as Masks operatives, they infiltrated the [[Hall Of Justice]] and freed [[16 String Jack]] from the dungeon -- a prison break that occurred between S02E14 and S02E15.

### The War Front
The Masks maintained forces on the front lines of the Neverwinter-Waterdeep war, with lieutenants rotating back to the city monthly under cover of night. This military presence reinforced their political leverage over Lord Neverember.

## Legacy

The Masks' withdrawal from Neverwinter and the successful sealing of the chasm marked a turning point for the city. With their departure:

- The war between Neverwinter and Waterdeep ended, and both cities eventually joined the Sword Coast Alliance.
- [[Renaer Neverember]] emerged from the resistance to share power with his father [[Lord Dagult Neverember|Dagult]], jointly performing the Crown of Never ritual.
- The Zhentarim's forces regrouped at the Spiral Cavern in Amphail under Manshoon's direct command, shifting from political manipulation to open military buildup alongside the [[Cult of the Dragon]].
- Neverwinter began marshalling its own army -- eventually 500 soldiers and 30 spellcasters -- and constructing three airships for the coming conflict.
