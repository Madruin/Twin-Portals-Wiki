# The Masks

A mercenary faction working for Neverwinter that all wear black masks. Have significant sway over Lord Neverember and operate with carte blanche from the city guard. The resistance believes they control things behind the scenes. Send a lieutenant from the war front to Neverwinter monthly under cover of night.
