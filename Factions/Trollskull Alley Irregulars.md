# Trollskull Alley Irregulars

The Trollskull Alley Irregulars are a group of three orphan street urchins -- [[Nat]], [[Jenks]], and [[Squidly]] -- who were taken in by [[SWOJ]] and hired to help around [[Trollskull Manor]] for 10 gold pieces per tenday. What began as a simple employment arrangement grew into a genuine family bond, with [[Taga]] taking over their education and the children eventually calling her "mom." Over time the Irregulars expanded to include the [[Cassalanter Family|Cassalanter]] children, [[Terenzio Cassalanter|Terenzio]] and [[Elga Cassalanter|Elga]], who were placed under city protection after their parents' sentencing.

**First appearance:** [[S01E05 - Episode Five]]
**Named and hired:** [[S01E07 - Episode Seven]]
**Also known as:** The Irregulars, the Trollskull Trio

## Overview

The three urchins were first encountered by SWOJ during [[S01E05 - Episode Five|Episode 5]] at [[Taga]]'s ransacked apartment in the Dock Ward, where they had been sheltering. Nat, the fearless mute leader, found a hag's talisman hidden under the tower floorboards while Squidly was captured by [[Esther]] the hag and nearly cooked in a cauldron. After this harrowing introduction, SWOJ brought the children to [[Trollskull Manor]] where they were formally hired in [[S01E07 - Episode Seven|Episode 7]] as helpers around the tavern at 10 gold pieces per tenday.

The Irregulars quickly proved their worth as more than just tavern hands. They became SWOJ's eyes and ears on the streets of [[Waterdeep]], gathering intelligence and reporting on suspicious activity in the neighborhood. Their most dangerous assignment came during the [[Stone of Golorr]] investigation, when they tracked a burned [[Zhentarim]] agent to the [[Gralhund Villa]], resulting in [[Squidly]] being wounded by [[Hrabbaz]].

Under [[Taga]]'s care, the Irregulars received an education and a stable home life. By [[S01E29 - Episode Twenty-Nine|Episode 29]], Taga had become their surrogate mother, and the Cassalanter children -- whose souls had been forfeit in their parents' [[Cult of Asmodeus|devil contract]] -- joined the group after being placed under city protection.

During the three-year gap between Seasons 1 and 2, the Irregulars grew into teenagers and took on serious responsibilities. [[Nat]] and [[Jenks]] helped manage the [[Short Rest]] tavern alongside [[Leaf]], while [[Squidly]] became a [[Harpers|Harper]] agent operating out of the [[House of a Thousand Faces]] in [[Neverwinter]]. By Season 2, each had forged their own path: Nat became a griffin rider leading aerial forces, Squidly served as a scout and crew member aboard the [[Ethereal Galleon]], and Jenks continued supporting operations at the tavern.

## Known Members

### Original Trio
- [[Nat]] -- Half-elven girl, mute, communicates through sign language (interpreted by Jenks). Leader of the group. Later became a griffin rider and commanded aerial reinforcements.
- [[Jenks]] -- Half-orc boy, self-proclaimed "great wizard." Carries a stick with a continual flame candle as his "magic wand." Later helped manage the Short Rest.
- [[Squidly]] -- Tiefling boy with an eye patch and swashbuckling clothes. Later became a Harper agent and crew member aboard the Ethereal Galleon.

### Extended Members (Season 1 Epilogue)
- [[Terenzio Cassalanter]] -- Son of [[Victoro Cassalanter|Victoro]] and [[Amalia Cassalanter|Amalia]], placed under city protection after his parents' sentencing. Began attending classes with the Irregulars.
- [[Elga Cassalanter]] -- Daughter of Victoro and Amalia, twin sister of Terenzio. Befriended the Irregulars at Trollskull.

### Parental Figure
- [[Taga]] -- [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]'s mother. Took over the Irregulars' education and became their surrogate mother.

## Activities

### Season 1
- **S01E05:** SWOJ meets the three street urchins at Taga's ransacked apartment. Nat finds the hag's talisman; Squidly is captured by Esther the hag and nearly cooked in a cauldron.
- **S01E07:** The trio is formally hired as the Trollskull Alley Irregulars for 10 gold per tenday to help around the tavern.
- **S01E11:** The Irregulars are present during the fireball investigation at Trollskull Alley. Squidly finds the [[Necklace of Fireballs]] in a barrel and gives it to SWOJ.
- **S01E12:** The Irregulars report seeing the burned Zhentarim agent enter the [[Gralhund Villa]] with [[Hrabbaz]]. Squidly is wounded (arm gashed) during the surveillance. Nat takes watch at the Short Rest door.
- **S01E22:** Nat and Jenks report to SWOJ that Squidly has gone missing.
- **S01E29:** [[Taga]] has taken over the Irregulars' education; they call her "mom." The Cassalanter children ([[Terenzio Cassalanter|Terenzio]] and [[Elga Cassalanter|Elga]]) join their ranks after being placed under city protection.

### Season 2
- **S02E02:** SWOJ discovers Squidly's unfulfilled wish ("I wish my brother came home") crystallized as a glowing red wish stone embedded in the [[Beast of Erua]]. Through the stone, Squidly is seen at the Trollskull tavern hatching a scheme.
- **S02E05:** Nat and Jenks have grown into teenagers, helping manage the Short Rest alongside [[Leaf]]. Squidly's whereabouts are unknown at this point.
- **S02E14:** SWOJ reunites with Squidly, now a teenage [[Harpers|Harper]] agent at the [[House of a Thousand Faces]] in [[Neverwinter]]. He provides SWOJ with [[The Masks|Masks]] uniforms and disguises for an infiltration plan.
- **S02E31:** Squidly volunteers to join SWOJ's crew aboard the [[Ethereal Galleon]] for the assault on the moonhole.
- **S02E33:** Squidly is sent on a solo scouting mission, returning with intelligence about the invasion timeline, the pylons' effects, the curfew, and the dracolich's recent arrival.
- **S02E35:** Nat arrives aboard the hammerhead ship with reinforcements -- 30 soldiers, 5 casters, and animal scouts. She leads the aerial forces as a griffin rider, coordinating through sign language.
- **S02E36:** Squidly is found captured and restrained by [[Manshoon]]'s forces at the bottom of the Citadel. [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] frees him from magical restraints.

## Relationships

- **[[SWOJ]]** -- Employers and surrogate family. The Irregulars served as informants and helpers, and SWOJ in turn provided them shelter, income, and purpose.
- **[[Taga]]** -- Surrogate mother who educated and raised them.
- **[[Trollskull Manor]] / [[Short Rest]]** -- Their home base and the tavern they helped operate.
- **[[Harpers]]** -- Squidly became affiliated with the Harpers by Season 2.
- **[[Cassalanter Family]]** -- The Cassalanter children joined the Irregulars after their parents' downfall.

## Notes

> [!info] Naming
> The faction is referred to as both "Trollskull Alley Irregulars" (their formal name from S01E07) and simply "The Irregulars" (the shorthand used from S01E29 onward). The name is a reference to the Baker Street Irregulars from Sherlock Holmes.
