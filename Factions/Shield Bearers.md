# Shield Bearers

The guard force and questing order of the [[Radiant Citadel]], serving dual roles as the city's defenders and as an adventuring company that sends crack teams on missions beyond the city. The Shield Bearers are open to recruiting new members and play a vital role in protecting the Citadel's inhabitants.

**First appearance:** Episode S02E09
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Overview

The Shield Bearers are the [[Radiant Citadel]]'s combined military and adventuring organization. Unlike purely defensive guard forces, the Shield Bearers also function as a questing group, dispatching elite teams outside the city to address threats, gather intelligence, and explore the wider multiverse.

[[SWOJ]] learned about the Shield Bearers during their first visit to the [[Radiant Citadel]] in S02E09. The [[Clavagers]] -- the Citadel's administrative officials -- suggested notifying the Shield Bearers if SWOJ didn't return from their mission within three days, indicating that the Shield Bearers serve as the Citadel's emergency response force. The Clavagers also noted that the Shield Bearers are accepting of new members, suggesting an open recruitment policy for capable adventurers.

Given the later occupation of the [[Radiant Citadel]] by [[Manshoon]]'s forces (S02E33-S02E36), the fate of the Shield Bearers during the invasion is uncertain. The city's defenders would have been among the first to confront the occupying forces, and the [[Citadel Resistance]] that formed in the cisterns beneath the city may include surviving Shield Bearers.
