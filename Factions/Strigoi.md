# Strigoi

Bat-like creatures that inhabit the [[The Shadowfell|Shadowfell]] borderlands near the [[Feywild]]. The Strigoi serve as raiders and enforcers in the territory surrounding the [[Beast Of Erua]], stealing [[wish stones]] from the [[Brigganocks]] and embedding them in the Beast's scales to fuel its movement.

**First appearance:** [[S02E01 - Episode Thirty One (S02E01)|S02E01]]

## Overview

The Strigoi are hostile creatures encountered by [[SWOJ]] at the start of Season 2 as the party crossed from the [[Feywild]] into the [[The Shadowfell|Shadowfell]]. They attacked the party on the surface before the [[Brigganocks]] led SWOJ to safety in their underground tunnels.

The Strigoi work alongside the podlings -- plant-based duplicates of captured people -- as part of the operation centered on the [[Beast Of Erua]]. They raid [[Brigganock's Tunnels|Brigganock tunnels]] to steal wish stones, which are then embedded into the Beast's scales to power the colossal undead creature's movement. Their raids grew more frequent as the Beast's roars intensified.

[[Fritz]], a staff member at the [[Shadow Way Inn]], revealed that the Strigoi and podlings cooperated: they stole wish stones, took people prisoner, and the prisoners would later "wander back, but not normal, not as themselves" -- replaced by podling duplicates.

## Activities

- **[[S02E01 - Episode Thirty One (S02E01)|S02E01]]:** Strigoi attack SWOJ on the surface of the Shadowfell borderlands
- **[[S02E02 - Episode Thirty Two (S02E02)|S02E02]]:** SWOJ learns from [[Rackjack]] and [[Fritz]] that the Strigoi steal wish stones and work with podlings; [[Squidly]]'s wish stone was stolen by Strigoi and embedded in the Beast

## Connections

- [[Shadow Way Inn]] -- The podlings who staffed the inn worked alongside the Strigoi
