# Guild Of Butchers

A Waterdeep trade guild representing butchers and meat merchants throughout the city. The guild was involved in the tavern licensing process for [[Trollskull Manor]].

**First appearance:** Episode S01E08
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Overview

The Guild of Butchers is one of the many trade guilds that regulate commerce in [[Waterdeep]]. As a guild with jurisdiction over the sale and preparation of meat, the Butchers' Guild had a stake in the licensing of taverns and eating establishments, since any establishment serving food required approval from relevant trade guilds.

The guild became relevant to [[SWOJ]]'s story when the party sought to license [[Trollskull Manor]] as a tavern. [[Justin Rask]], a representative of the Guild of Butchers, participated in the licensing hearing alongside representatives from other guilds including the [[Guild Of Apothecaries]], the [[Fellowship Of Innkeepers]], and the [[Vintners', Distillers', And Brewers' Guild]].
