# Guild Of The Silver Waves Of Justice

The officially chartered guild of [[SWOJ]], formally recognized by the [[Sword Coast Alliance]] in the Season 2 finale (S02E38). The charter grants the party recruitment rights, petitioning privileges, safe passage, limited judicial immunity, and franchising rights across the allied cities of the Sword Coast.

**First appearance:** Episode S02E38
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Overview

The Guild of the Silver Waves of Justice represents the culmination of [[SWOJ]]'s journey from a ragtag group of adventurers to a formally recognized institution on the [[Sword Coast]]. Chartered during the grand council meeting in S02E38, the guild was officially recognized by the [[Sword Coast Alliance]] -- a coalition of [[Waterdeep]], [[Baldur's Gate]], [[Neverwinter]], [[Luskan]], and allied states.

The [[Letters Of Charter (Guild Charter)|guild charter]] was presented by [[Ramalia Haventree]] of the [[Harpers]] during a ceremony attended by representatives from across the alliance. The charter grants SWOJ a suite of official privileges:

- **Recruitment rights** -- the ability to formally recruit new members
- **Petitioning privileges** -- the right to speak directly to nobles and lords
- **Safe passage** -- recognized travel rights across allied territories
- **Limited judicial immunity** -- legal protections when acting in the interest of the people
- **Franchising rights** -- the ability to establish satellite operations

[[Terry]], the party's beloved awakened horse companion and intellect devourer ally, was invited as the first official member beyond the four founding members ([[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]], [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]], [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]], and [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]).
