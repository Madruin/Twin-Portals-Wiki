# Raven Queen Followers

Devotees and servants of [[The Raven Queen|the Raven Queen]], a powerful deity associated with death, fate, memory, and the transition between worlds. Her followers maintain shrines, temples, and sacred sites across the planes, including the [[Raven Queen Shrine]] beneath the [[Shard of Moonlight]] in [[Neverwinter]] and the [[Sepulchre of the Raven Queen]] atop a mountain spire in the [[Shadowfell]]. The [[Shadar-Kai]] -- shadow elves native to the Shadowfell -- are her most prominent servants.

**First appearance:** Episode S02E20
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Overview

The Raven Queen is a deity who stands in opposition to [[Kelemvor]]'s [[Wall of the Faithless]], a massive barrier in the afterlife that traps the souls of those who worshipped no deity in life. Through her followers and agents, the Raven Queen works to dismantle this wall and ensure the proper passage of souls between worlds.

Her influence in Season 2 manifests through several key elements:

**The Raven Queen Shrine** beneath the [[Shard of Moonlight]] in [[Neverwinter]] serves as a portal gateway to the [[Shadowfell]]. Decorated with crow tapestries and guarded by undead (a [[Giant Skeletal Raven Beast]] and [[Zombies]]), this shrine became SWOJ's primary route to the Shadowfell when they pursued [[Rowan Crooks]] and [[Zhentil Keep|Zentil Keep]].

**The [[Sepulchre of the Raven Queen]]** is a sacred tomb atop a mountain spire in the deep Shadowfell. Visitors must make sacrificial offerings at the altar to enter. Inside, shadow guides navigate visitors through a whispering hallway and a massive chamber of raven-masked statues, ultimately providing a [[Soul Stone]] to those deemed worthy. A tempting magical pendant containing a trapped entity tests visitors' resolve.

**Divine Intervention** -- The Raven Queen personally tested SWOJ through a dream sequence after their victory over [[Rowan Crooks]], challenging them with shadow versions of themselves. She granted divine gifts: enchanted [[Raven Feathers]] to [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] (banishing undead once per week), soul-anchoring magic on [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]'s hat (preserving his soul if he dies), and power over the dead to the entire party. At the [[Radiant Citadel]], the Raven Queen sent a flock of spectral ravens through [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] to banish the two-headed dracolich and destroy the top soul pylon. Ultimately, [[Manshoon]]'s soul was sent to the Raven Queen as punishment after his defeat.

## Known Members

The Raven Queen's followers are not a conventional membership organization but rather a network of servants and devotees across the planes:

- [[The Raven Queen]] -- The deity herself, who directly intervenes at critical moments.
- [[Shadar-Kai]] -- Shadow elves native to the [[Shadowfell]] who serve the Raven Queen (also called "the Raven Mother"). See the [[Shadar-Kai]] page for details.
- Shadow guides within the [[Sepulchre of the Raven Queen]] who lead worthy visitors to the [[Soul Stone]].

## Activities

- **S02E20:** [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] discovers a star staircase beneath the [[Shard of Moonlight]] leading to a [[Raven Queen Shrine|shrine of the Raven Queen]]. The shrine contains crow tapestries, undead guardians, and a portal to the [[Shadowfell]]. SWOJ battles a [[Giant Skeletal Raven Beast]] and [[Zombies]] before being launched through the portal, arriving in the [[Shadowfell]] where they are greeted by three [[Shadar-Kai]].
- **S02E21:** SWOJ climbs the mountain spire to the [[Sepulchre of the Raven Queen]] in the deep Shadowfell. Each party member makes a sacrificial offering at the altar: [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] gives his durags, [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] sings a personal song, [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] whispers a secret, and [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] reveals her middle name. Inside, shadow guides lead SWOJ through the whispering hallway and chamber of raven-masked statues. They resist a tempting magical pendant and receive a [[Soul Stone]] from the shadow guides.
- **S02E25:** After defeating [[Rowan Crooks]] atop [[Zhentil Keep|Zentil Keep]], the [[The Raven Queen|Raven Queen]] orchestrates a dream sequence testing SWOJ's worthiness. The party faces shadow versions of themselves and prevails. The Raven Queen acknowledges their worthiness.
- **S02E26:** The Raven Queen's emissary addresses SWOJ about the [[Wall of the Faithless]] and her opposition to it. [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] receives enchanted [[Raven Feathers]] that banish undead once per week. [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] is taken for a private audience and returns with his hat enchanted to anchor his soul.
- **S02E29:** SWOJ returns to physical form in a hallway associated with the Raven Queen after completing the divine trials beneath [[Castle Naerytar]], now imbued with power over the dead.
- **S02E34:** [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] communes with the Raven Queen during the battle atop the [[Radiant Citadel]]. She sends divine intervention -- a massive flock of spectral ravens that banishes the two-headed dracolich and destroys the top [[Soul Conduit Pylons|soul pylon]].
- **S02E35:** The Raven Queen communicates with Scoot, warning about [[Manshoon]]'s ambitions to achieve godhood. Her spectral ravens fade as the Citadel passes through the [[Keening Gloam]].
- **S02E36:** [[Manshoon]]'s soul is sent to the Raven Queen as punishment after his defeat, ensuring he cannot return.
