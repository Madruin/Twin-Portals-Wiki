# Bailey Brew Family

The Bailey Brew Family is a dwarf family living in prehistoric [[Waterdeep]], encountered by [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] during the holiday special episode. The family -- father [[Gorge Bailey Brew]], and daughters [[Navi]] and [[Zuzu]] -- ran a homestead near [[Zedford Falls]] until the vampire wizard [[Hans Von Gruber]] seized [[Zaka's Tome Tower]] and captured Zuzu. SWOJ rescued the family and defeated Von Gruber through combat and an act of kindness involving the [[Fleece Of Navi-Dad]].

**First appearance:** [[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

---

## Overview

In a holiday-themed adventure set in prehistoric Waterdeep (a riff on *It's a Wonderful Life*), SWOJ encountered the Bailey Brew homestead near the settlement of Zedford Falls. Gorge Bailey Brew, the dwarf patriarch, was in distress -- his daughter Zuzu had been taken by Hans Von Gruber, a vampire wizard who had seized Zaka's Tome Tower, an ancient repository of knowledge. Navi, the other daughter, remained at the homestead. SWOJ traveled to the tower, confronted Von Gruber and his Pain Deer constructs, and ultimately rescued the family. The encounter culminated in an act of kindness using the Fleece of Navi-Dad, and Gorge eventually helped deliver toys via a magical sleigh.
