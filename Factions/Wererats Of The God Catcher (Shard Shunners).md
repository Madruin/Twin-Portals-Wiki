# Wererats Of The God Catcher (Shard Shunners)

> [!note] Redirect
> This is an alternate name for the [[Shard Shunners]]. See that page for the full article.

A colony of wererats and werecreatures living inside the [[God Catcher]] statue in [[Waterdeep]]. The gang is formally known as the Shard Shunners and is led by [[Vaniol]], a wereferret. Members include [[Dasher Zuber]], [[Kittle]], and [[Vixby]]. They were first encountered by [[SWOJ]] during [[S01E06 - Episode Six|Episode 6]] as an aggressive colony hostile to intruders, before being properly identified and negotiated with during [[S01E08 - Episode Eight|Episode 8]].
