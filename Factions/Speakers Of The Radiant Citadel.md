# Speakers Of The Radiant Citadel

The governing body of the [[Radiant Citadel]], an egalitarian council representing each of the founding cultures that settled the floating city approximately 200-300 years ago. Led by First Speaker [[Sholeh]] (also known as Shole), the council manages the Citadel's affairs including decisions about refugees, trade, ward controls, and the city's movement through the [[Deep Ethereal Plane]]. The Speakers' council chamber near the top of the Citadel contains ancient ward controls capable of shielding the city from intruders and preventing teleportation.

**First appearance:** Episode S02E08
**Total appearances:** 4 episodes

## Overview

The Speakers of the Radiant Citadel (also called the Speakers for the Ancestors) are the ruling council of one of the most remarkable cities in the multiverse. The [[Radiant Citadel]] is a floating city built around a colossal [[Auroral Diamond]] in the [[Deep Ethereal Plane]], connected to various locations on Toril via anchor points called [[Concord Jewel|Concord Jewels]]. The Speakers govern this cosmopolitan hub, where food is largely free, healing is amplified by the diamond, and diverse cultures from across the multiverse coexist.

Each Speaker represents one of the founding cultures that resettled the Citadel roughly 250 years ago, when 12 of at least 15 known Concord Jewels were recovered. The council operates on egalitarian principles, making collective decisions about the city's direction.

The Speakers' most devastating crisis came when [[Rowan Crooks]] -- a folk hero who had gained the council's trust -- introduced the [[Soul Conduit Pylons]] to the Citadel. After Crooks disappeared, [[Manshoon]] took over and weaponized the pylon infrastructure. When agents activated the pylons, the city's defenses collapsed. All Speakers were captured and imprisoned in soul stones near the top pylon on the [[Speakers' Platform]], leaving the Citadel leaderless and vulnerable to occupation.

[[SWOJ]] ultimately liberated the Speakers during their assault on the occupied Citadel, with [[Sholeh]] -- revealed to be an ancient brass dragon in disguise -- playing a crucial role in the city's defense and the final confrontation with Manshoon.

## Known Members

- [[Sholeh]] / Shole -- First Speaker and elder of the council. Revealed to be an ancient brass dragon in disguise. Imprisoned in a soul stone near the top pylon during the occupation. After rescue, she channeled the [[Auroral Diamond]]'s power to provide SWOJ with a compressed long rest and explained the history of [[Rowan Crooks]] and the pylons. Protected civilian survivors during the final battle against [[Manshoon]].

Other individual Speakers were not named in the transcripts. Fifteen Speakers total were freed from soul stone imprisonment by SWOJ.

### Associated Figures

- [[Sax Bandon|Saks Bandin]] -- A halfling scientist on the Radiant Citadel's welcoming committee. Reports incidents to Shole and is knowledgeable about the Concord Jewels and the Citadel's history.
- [[Vincent Trench]] -- A private detective (actually a rakshasa in disguise) operating from the [[Night Market]]. Provided intelligence about the Speakers' council chamber ward controls during the occupation.

## Activities

- **S02E08:** SWOJ arrives at the [[Radiant Citadel]] for the first time. The Speakers are referenced as the governing body, led by First Speaker [[Sholeh|Shole]]. [[Sax Bandon|Saks Bandin]], a member of the welcoming committee, explains the Citadel's egalitarian governance and promises to report the shadow creature attack to Shole.
- **S02E09:** [[Sax Bandon|Saks Bandin]] promises to report unprecedented shadow creature activity to [[Sholeh|Shole]], indicating the Speakers' role in managing security threats to the Citadel.
- **S02E33:** SWOJ returns to the [[Radiant Citadel]] to find it under [[Manshoon]]'s occupation. [[Vincent Trench]] reveals that [[Sholeh|Shole]] and all Speakers for the Ancestors have been missing since the [[Soul Conduit Pylons]] were activated two weeks prior. Their [[Speakers' Council Chamber|council chamber]] near the top conduit contains a platform with ancient ward controls that can shield the city, prevent teleportation, and control the Citadel's movement -- all currently deactivated by the occupiers. The Speakers are identified as representing each founding culture, with the council settled 200-300 years ago.
- **S02E34:** SWOJ assaults the top of the [[Radiant Citadel]] and reaches the [[Speakers' Platform]], where the Speakers are being held prisoner in soul stones near the soul pylon. SWOJ stealthily rescues [[Sholeh]], revealed to be an ancient brass dragon in disguise. Fifteen Speakers total are freed from soul stone imprisonment. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] reactivates the [[Concord Jewel|Concord Jewels]] to send displaced civilizations back to their home planes.
- **S02E35:** [[Sholeh]] channels the [[Auroral Diamond]] to provide SWOJ with a compressed long rest. She explains the history: [[Rowan Crooks]], a folk hero, arrived at the Citadel and built trust with the Speakers before introducing the [[Soul Conduit Pylons]], then mysteriously disappeared. [[Manshoon]] later corrupted the system Crooks built. Sholeh protects civilian survivors during the descent to confront Manshoon.
- **S02E36:** [[Sholeh]] protects civilian survivors at the bottom of the Citadel during the final battle against [[Manshoon]], shielding them from his forces while SWOJ confronts the villain directly. The Citadel is liberated.
