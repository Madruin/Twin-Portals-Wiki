# Geometric Shapes / Curators

Golden triangular entities that serve as the keepers and researchers of the [[Museum of the Multiverse]] and [[Stadium of Souls]] in the [[Far Realm]]. They telepathically interrogate captured souls about their names, abilities, and origins, studying mortal beings from across the multiverse.

**First appearance:** Episode S02E12
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Overview

The Geometric Shapes, also called the Curators, are enigmatic golden triangular entities encountered by [[SWOJ]] during their journey through the [[Far Realm]] in Season 2. They operate as the administrators and researchers of the [[Museum of the Multiverse]] and the [[Stadium of Souls]], vast Far Realm structures dedicated to cataloguing and studying mortal souls from across the planes.

The Curators communicate telepathically, interrogating captured souls about their identities, abilities, and places of origin. Their purpose appears to be the systematic study and collection of knowledge about mortal beings from every corner of the multiverse. They direct the [[Soul Collectors]] -- mechanical bell-shaped constructs that ride the [[Purple Moths / Seraphic Moths|Seraphic Moths]] to capture souls -- and oversee the gladiatorial combat in the Stadium of Souls.

## Known Members

No individual Curators are identified by name; they appear to function as a collective entity.
