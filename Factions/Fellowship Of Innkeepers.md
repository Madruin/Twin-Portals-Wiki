# Fellowship Of Innkeepers

The Fellowship of Innkeepers is one of [[Waterdeep]]'s many trade guilds, responsible for the licensing and regulation of taverns and inns throughout the city. [[SWOJ]] encountered the Fellowship when they sought to open their tavern, the [[Short Rest]], at [[Trollskull Manor]].

**First appearance:** [[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Overview

In [[Waterdeep]], operating a tavern requires navigating a complex web of guild approvals. The Fellowship of Innkeepers is one of several guilds that must sign off before a new establishment can legally serve the public. When SWOJ decided to convert [[Trollskull Manor]] into the [[Short Rest]] tavern, they had to go through the licensing process, which involved meeting with representatives from multiple guilds during [[S01E08 - Episode Eight|Episode 8]].

The Fellowship works alongside other guilds in the tavern licensing process, including the [[Guild of Apothecaries]], the [[Guild of Butchers]], and the [[Vintners', Distillers', and Brewers' Guild]]. Each guild has its own representative and its own requirements for approval, reflecting Waterdeep's famously bureaucratic approach to commerce.

## Known Members

- [[Broxley Fairkettle]] -- Representative of the Fellowship of Innkeepers, involved in the tavern licensing process for the [[Short Rest]].

## Related Guilds (Tavern Licensing)

The following guilds were also involved in SWOJ's tavern licensing process:

- [[Guild of Apothecaries]] -- Represented in the licensing process. [[Fala Lefaliir]], who runs [[Corellon's Crown]] on Trollskull Alley, is a member.
- [[Guild of Butchers]] -- Represented by Justin Rask.
- [[Vintners', Distillers', and Brewers' Guild]] -- Represented by Hammond Kraddoc.

## Activities

### Season 1
- **S01E08:** SWOJ goes through the tavern licensing process to open the Short Rest. [[Broxley Fairkettle]] serves as the Fellowship of Innkeepers' representative, alongside representatives from the other involved guilds. The licensing process is part of the broader events of the episode, which also involve the Shard Shunners and the investigation into [[Emmek Frewn]]'s sabotage.

## Relationships

- **[[SWOJ]]** -- Applied for and received tavern licensing from the Fellowship to operate the [[Short Rest]].
- **[[Short Rest]] / [[Trollskull Manor]]** -- The tavern that SWOJ licensed through the Fellowship and the other guilds.
- **[[Emmek Frewn]]** -- Owner of [[Frewn's Brews]], the competing tavern across the street from Trollskull Manor. His antagonism toward SWOJ provided context for the licensing subplot.
- **[[Waterdeep]]** -- The Fellowship operates within Waterdeep's guild-based economic system.

## Notes

> [!info] Waterdeep Guild System
> Waterdeep is famous for its extensive guild system, in which nearly every trade and profession has an associated guild that regulates standards, collects dues, and controls licensing. The Fellowship of Innkeepers is one of many such organizations, and navigating the guild bureaucracy is a rite of passage for any would-be business owner in the City of Splendors.
