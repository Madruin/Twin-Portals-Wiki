# The Resistance

An underground movement operating from tunnels beneath [[Castle Never]] in [[Neverwinter]], led by [[Renaer Neverember]]. The Resistance opposes the influence of [[The Masks]], a mercenary faction that has gained dangerous sway over Lord [[Dagult Neverember]] and city affairs. Working covertly with various guilds and agents, the Resistance provides a counterbalance to the authoritarian elements threatening Neverwinter's governance.

**First appearance:** Episode S02E14
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Overview

The Resistance formed during the three years that [[SWOJ]] was absent from the material plane (trapped in the Shadowfell/Feywild). During this time, [[Neverwinter]] remained locked in a costly war with [[Waterdeep]] over the [[Cassalanter Diamond Mines]], and [[The Masks]] -- a mercenary faction wearing distinctive black masks -- rose to power, gaining carte blanche from the city guard and significant influence over Lord [[Dagult Neverember]].

[[Renaer Neverember]], who had come to Neverwinter years earlier trying to oust his corrupt father, pivoted to leading an underground resistance when he realized the Masks posed an even greater threat. Operating from a network of tunnels and natural caverns beneath [[Castle Never]]'s abandoned dungeons, the Resistance maintained a war room and workspace where artisans and fighters coordinated their efforts. The carpenters' guild provided essential cover, using renovation work as a pretext for the underground activity.

The Resistance served as SWOJ's primary support network during their time in Neverwinter, providing safe harbor after the party was framed for the murder of [[Shimmer Brackenwell|Slippery Shim]] at the [[Moonstone Mask]]. Renaer's intelligence network kept the party informed of the Masks' movements and helped them plan the infiltration of the [[Neverwinter Chasm|chasm]], which was central to stopping the aboleth threat.

## Known Members

- [[Renaer Neverember]] -- Leader of the Resistance. Son of Lord [[Dagult Neverember]]. Has been hiding underground for three years. Provides intelligence, resources, and eventually dragon-hunting equipment to SWOJ.
- [[Laila]] -- A halfling knight wearing night regalia and a concealing helmet. Patrols the tunnels beneath [[Castle Never]] and serves as a scout for the Resistance. Discovered SWOJ in the excavation areas and led them to Renaer's base.
- [[Fumbles  Fumblewick|Fumbles (Fumblewick)]] -- A gnome city guard in Neverwinter who serves as a Resistance contact within the government. Has accompanied monthly caravans into the chasm and possesses detailed knowledge of what happens there. Can be found at the [[Slinky Serpent Tavern]].
- [[Squidly]] -- A former child NPC from Season 1, now a teenage [[Harpers|Harper]] agent working at the [[House of a Thousand Faces]] in Neverwinter. Provides SWOJ with Masks uniforms and disguises. While primarily a Harper operative, Squidly works closely with the Resistance.

### Allied Organizations

- The [[Harpers]] -- Operate the [[House of a Thousand Faces]] as their Neverwinter base.
- The Carpenters' Guild -- Provides cover for the Resistance's underground operations through renovation projects.
- [[Bregan D'aerthe]] -- [[Jarlaxle Baenre]]'s organization maintains a presence in Neverwinter and shares intelligence with SWOJ, though their relationship with the Resistance is pragmatic rather than formal.

## Activities

- **S02E14:** [[Jarlaxle Baenre|Jarlaxle]] provides political exposition about Neverwinter's situation: Lord [[Dagult Neverember|Neverember]] rules from the [[Hall of Justice]], the war with [[Waterdeep]] continues over the [[Cassalanter Diamond Mines]], [[The Masks]] control city affairs with troubling influence, and [[Renaer Neverember|Renaer]] leads an underground resistance against his father and the Masks. SWOJ arrives in Neverwinter aboard Jarlaxle's submersible.
- **S02E15:** After being framed for the murder of [[Shimmer Brackenwell|Slippery Shim]], SWOJ takes shelter in the excavation areas beneath [[Castle Never]]. [[Laila]], a halfling knight of the Resistance, discovers SWOJ hiding and leads them through the tunnels to the underground base. SWOJ reunites with [[Renaer Neverember]] in the Resistance war room beneath Castle Never. Renaer provides intelligence: someone impersonating [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] (wearing a red hat and vanishing into mist) murdered Slippery Shim to frame SWOJ. [[Fel'Rekt Lafeen|Felrecht]] has disappeared since the murder. Renaer introduces [[Fumbles  Fumblewick|Fumbles]], a gnome guard who is their contact within the city government, and explains how the carpenters' guild provides cover for their operations.
