# Deep Shadow Explorers Guild

The Deep Shadow Explorers Guild is a merchant guild operating a stall in the [[Dark Market]] that specializes in selling utility equipment for [[Shadowfell]] exploration. [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] purchased several items from them to prepare for their journey deeper into the Shadowfell.

**First appearance:** [[S02E21 - Episode Fifty-One]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

---

## Overview

Encountered during SWOJ's time in the Dark Market, the Deep Shadow Explorers Guild caters to adventurers and travelers venturing into the dangerous terrain of the Shadowfell. Their stall offered a range of practical equipment suited to the plane's oppressive darkness and treacherous landscapes: [[Goggles Of Night]] for seeing in the dark, [[Potions Of Spider Climb]] for scaling surfaces, ropes, pitons, crampons, and the mysterious [[Echoing Bell]] -- a bell that makes no audible sound in the material world but can be heard by entities in the ethereal plane and other parallel universes.

SWOJ made several purchases from the guild: [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] (as Mr. E) bought the Echoing Bell for 1,000 gold, [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] purchased Goggles of Night with a flip-up switch mechanism for 300 gold, and the party acquired two Potions of Spider Climb at 100 gold each.
