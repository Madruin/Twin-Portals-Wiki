# Harpers

**First appearance:** [[S01E01 - Episode One|S01E01]]
**Total appearances:** 12+ episodes across Seasons 1 and 2

## Overview

The Harpers are a secret network of spellcasters and spies who advocate equality and covertly oppose the abuse of power. Operating across the Sword Coast, they gather intelligence, place informants inside criminal organizations, and intervene when the balance between good and evil is threatened. Their symbol is a silver harp brooch, worn openly by members in cities where they operate publicly.

In the Twin Portals campaign, the Harpers serve as one of SWOJ's most important and enduring factional allies. From providing the party's very first investigative lead in Episode One to presenting them with an official guild charter in the Season 2 finale, the Harpers are woven throughout the story as a benevolent intelligence network that repeatedly equips, shelters, and fights alongside the Silver Waves of Justice.

[[Laeral Silverhand]], the Open Lord of Waterdeep, has deep personal ties to the Harpers -- she was a Harper herself in her younger days, and it is through her that SWOJ first gains access to Harper safehouses and intelligence. However, Laeral is careful to position SWOJ as her personal agents, operating "separate from the Masked Lords of Waterdeep, separate from the Harpers, separate from any other agency" (S01E15).

## Philosophy and Methods

- **Secrecy:** The Harpers prize discretion. They use code phrases, sending stones, and agents embedded in ordinary settings (taverns, boutiques) to communicate and gather intelligence.
- **Trust-based organization:** As one Harper tells Scoot, "We're an organization built on trust" -- though the betrayal of [[Bricks]] and [[Bracks]] reveals that trust can be exploited.
- **Non-lethal code:** In Neverwinter, [[Squidly]] explains that the Harpers "abide by a code that prohibits" kidnapping and coercive interrogation "unless there's some immediate risk of life" (S02E14).
- **Intelligence gathering:** The Harpers excel at placing informants in hostile organizations (e.g., [[Thorvin Twinbeard]] inside the [[Xanathar Guild]]) and maintaining networks of eyes and ears across cities.
- **Open operations in some cities:** While secretive in Waterdeep, the Harpers operate openly in Neverwinter, where they run the [[House of a Thousand Faces]] clothing boutique as their base.

## Known Members and Associates

### [[Remalia Haventree]] -- Senior Agent / Leader (Waterdeep)
**Race:** High elf | **Class:** Wizard | **Status:** Active

Remalia (also rendered as Ramalia or Remallia in transcripts) is the Harpers' most prominent agent in the campaign and the one Laeral Silverhand describes as someone she "would trust with my own life" (S01E15). She is an elf of noble bearing who operates from the [[Haventree Estate (Harper Safehouse)|Haventree Estate]] in Waterdeep's Castle Ward.

- **S01E01:** First mentioned by [[Jalaster]] at the Skewered Dragon as a Harper contact who frequents the Yawning Portal.
- **S01E15:** Laeral identifies her as her most trusted Harper agent and takes SWOJ to the Haventree Estate, which serves as a Harper safehouse.
- **S01E16:** Harbors [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] at her estate while the rest of the party prepares for the Xanathar lair heist. Personally leads SWOJ through the Waterdeep sewers to a secret entrance into the [[Xanathar's Lair]], providing tactical intelligence about the Festival of Blood and Fortune and the lair's layout. Departs after guiding them in, remaining on standby via sending stone.
- **S01E22:** Meets SWOJ at the Docksward docks after their return from the Feywild. Provides critical intelligence: the [[Zhentarim]] possess the second Eye of the [[The Stone of Golorr]], and the [[Kolat Towers]] are the Zhentarim's base of operations. Gives SWOJ a sending stone for communication.
- **S01E23:** Contacted via sending stone during the Kolat Towers infiltration. Leads a Harper strike force to the base of the towers to support SWOJ. Gets pinned down by Zhentarim forces but coordinates backup. Helps destroy the Kolat Towers' shield rune after Ylka shatters it.
- **S01E25:** Hosts SWOJ at her house to assemble the three Eyes of the Stone of Golorr. Gets ensorcelled (mind-controlled) by the Stone along with her guards [[Bricks]] and [[Bracks]] -- their eyes turn "opaque and blood red" as the Stone activates.
- **S01E26:** Freed from the ensorcellement when SWOJ physically hits her, breaking the spell. Accompanies SWOJ to the [[Zadio City Music Hall]] to access the [[Vault of Dragons]], disguising as ushers. Fights in the climactic theater battle against the [[The Xanathar|Xanathar]]: casts **Wall of Force** to contain the beholder, then launches a **Fireball** at the Xanathar and the Zhentarim on the theater balcony, blasting [[Manshoon]]'s position. Offers to call in more Harpers to guard the recovered gold.
- **S01E27:** Betrayed by [[Bricks]] and [[Bracks]], who hold knives to her throat and turn her over to the city watch outside the theater. She is "stunned" but "keeping silent" as the guards drag her forward. Taken to a separate prison from SWOJ and placed under house arrest.
- **S01E29:** Freed from house arrest after the Cassalanter plot is fully exposed. Coordinates the Harper effort at [[Cassalanter Villa]] alongside the unicorn [[Lyrian|Lerian]], overseeing cleanup and healing operations.
- **S02E20:** Participates in the final war council in Neverwinter. Mentions she has "stepped away from my business with the Harpers and been more involved in Waterdavian politics," working to bring an end to hostilities and ensure everyone is taken care of.
- **S02E38:** Represents the Harpers at the Masked Lords' council in the Season 2 finale. Presents SWOJ with their official Letters of Charter for the Guild of the Silver Waves of Justice, telling them: "Based on your actions over the years, we trust that you will use this to fight evil and promote justice wherever you go." Attends the God's Night celebration at Trollskull Manor.

### [[Jalaster]] -- Field Agent (Waterdeep, Dock Ward)
**Status:** Unknown after S01E01

A Harper agent stationed at the [[Skewered Dragon]] tavern in Waterdeep's Dock Ward. He is SWOJ's very first Harper contact, encountered in the series premiere.

- **S01E01:** Lark meets Jalaster (also heard as "Jelaster" or "Gelaster") at the Skewered Dragon. He provides information about [[Zhentarim]] activity on Candle Lane and directs SWOJ there to investigate. He also mentions [[Remalia Haventree]] as a more senior Harper who can sometimes be found at the [[Yawning Portal]].
- **S01E29:** Referenced in the talkback discussion: "Gelaster was the first of the Harpers that we met in episode one... a Schwaisty Harper who told you about Ramalia originally. Started that quest line long ago."

### [[Thorvin Twinbeard]] -- Deep Cover Informant (Xanathar Guild)
**Race:** Shield dwarf | **Status:** Unknown after S01E16

A dwarf engineer serving as the Xanathar's chief engineer while secretly feeding intelligence to the Harpers. He wears purple leather armor and has a big red beard.

- **S01E15:** Laeral Silverhand reveals to SWOJ that "in his upper echelons, including our informant, is a dwarf named Thorvin Twinbeard, his chief engineer. The Harpers pay him for information every so often."
- **S01E16:** SWOJ passes through Thorvin's workshop during the Xanathar lair heist. He is building a large crushing device for Skullport, watched by a small albino gazer. He asks the party to send [[Narl Xibrindas]] his way if they find him. He does not reveal his Harper affiliation to the party directly.

### [[Squidly]] -- Field Agent (Neverwinter)
**Race:** Tiefling | **Status:** Active

Originally one of the Trollskull Alley street urchins (the "Irregulars") alongside [[Nat]] and [[Jenks]], Squidly grew from a child NPC in Season 1 into a full Harper agent by Season 2. He wears a silver harp pin on his lapel and operates out of the [[House of a Thousand Faces]] in Neverwinter.

- **S01E22-23:** As a child, Squidly goes missing after infiltrating the Zhentarim. Found with [[Urstul Floxxin]] near the Kolat Towers, he reveals he pickpocketed the second Eye of the Stone of Golorr from Urstul -- a pivotal moment in the season's plot. Reunites emotionally with SWOJ: "You guys are the closest family I have now."
- **S02E14:** Now a teenager, Squidly operates as a Harper agent at the House of a Thousand Faces clothing boutique in Neverwinter. He excitedly reunites with SWOJ, introduces them to other Harper agents in the basement operations room (maps, jobs board, postings), and provides them with [[Masks]] uniforms and disguises for their infiltration plan. Explains the Harper code against coercive interrogation.
- **S02E17:** Seen helping people get to safety during the battle of Neverwinter, fighting alongside other Harpers.
- **S02E31 onward:** Volunteers to join SWOJ's crew aboard the Ethereal Galleon. Conducts a solo scouting mission to gather intelligence about an occupied city. Later captured by Manshoon's forces and freed by Lark.

### [[Bricks]] and [[Bracks]] -- Guards / Traitors
**Status:** Expelled from the Harpers

Two guards in gray armor and long cloaks who serve as Remalia Haventree's personal security detail. They initially appear loyal but are ultimately revealed as traitors working for the [[Cassalanters]].

- **S01E25:** Ensorcelled alongside Remalia by the Stone of Golorr. Freed when SWOJ hits them.
- **S01E26:** Accompany SWOJ and Remalia into the Vault of Dragons and the theater battle. Bricks scores a critical hit fighting bugbears.
- **S01E27:** Betray Remalia after the theater battle, holding knives to her throat and turning her over to the city watch, yelling "We have her, she's here. She's the one responsible. She did it." They were either working for the Cassalanters from the start or were blackmailed.
- **S01E29:** Scoot alerts the Harpers about the betrayal. A Harper confirms: "There are no longer members of the Harpers. They were traitors from the start." Bricks and Bracks are expelled and reported to be on the run.

### [[Laeral Silverhand]] -- Ally with Deep Harper Connections
**Race:** Human (Chosen of Mystra) | **Status:** Missing (as of late Season 2)

While not an active Harper agent during the campaign, Laeral is a former Harper and maintains deep connections to the organization. She leverages these connections to support SWOJ.

- **S01E15:** In her early days, Laeral was a Harper. She and her mother, the goddess Mystra, used their familial bond to defeat the evil wizard Manshoon by splicing into the Weave. She tells SWOJ: "The Harpers are generally trustworthy. I wouldn't make that a blanket statement, but I have one agent in particular named Ramalia Haventree who I know I would trust with my own life."
- **S01E15:** Takes SWOJ to the Haventree Estate (a Harper safehouse) and leaves Xae under Harper protection there.

## Bases of Operations

### [[Haventree Estate (Harper Safehouse)]] -- Waterdeep
Located in the Castle Ward near the [[The God Catcher|God Catcher]], this is Remalia Haventree's personal estate that doubles as a Harper safehouse. Described as a "really beautiful manner in a long line of manners" resembling Copenhagen row houses. SWOJ is taken here by Laeral Silverhand in S01E15 and returns multiple times. The Stone of Golorr is assembled here, and it serves as a staging point for the Vault of Dragons mission.

### [[House of a Thousand Faces]] -- Neverwinter
A clothing boutique in central Neverwinter, across the Dolphin Bridge, that serves as the Harpers' base of operations in the city. The main shopping floor features mirrors and mannequins that give the place its name. It also sells masks. The basement contains an operations room with maps, a jobs board with postings, and space for Harper agents to meet and plan. Squidly operates from here. The Harpers operate openly in Neverwinter, unlike their more secretive Waterdeep posture.

### [[Skewered Dragon]] -- Waterdeep (Dock Ward)
A tavern in the Dock Ward where Harper agent Jalaster is stationed. This is where SWOJ first encounters the Harpers in S01E01.

## Relationships with Other Factions

### SWOJ (Silver Waves of Justice)
The Harpers are SWOJ's most consistent factional ally. From Jalaster's first tip in Episode One to Remalia's guild charter in the Season 2 finale, the Harpers provide intelligence, shelter, combat support, and political backing. SWOJ is never formally inducted into the Harpers, but they operate in close coordination throughout both seasons.

### [[Laeral Silverhand]] / Open Lord of Waterdeep
Laeral has deep Harper roots and uses her Harper connections to support SWOJ, but she deliberately positions the party's mission as separate from any single faction. She trusts Remalia implicitly.

### [[Zhentarim]]
The Harpers and Zhentarim are natural adversaries. Harper intelligence identifies the Zhentarim's possession of the second Eye and the location of their base at the Kolat Towers. Remalia leads a strike force against the towers.

### [[Xanathar Guild]]
The Harpers have [[Thorvin Twinbeard]] embedded as a deep-cover informant inside the Xanathar Guild. Remalia knows the secret sewer entrance to the Xanathar's lair and provides tactical intelligence for SWOJ's infiltration.

### [[Cassalanters]]
The Cassalanters successfully infiltrated the Harper inner circle through Bricks and Bracks, who served as Remalia's personal guards. This betrayal leads to Remalia's arrest and SWOJ's imprisonment. The Harpers ultimately respond decisively, expelling the traitors and freeing Remalia once the Cassalanter plot is exposed.

### [[Bregan D'aerthe]]
The relationship is not antagonistic. Jarlaxle and Remalia both attend the Season 2 finale council, and both contribute to SWOJ's guild charter.

### [[Masks]]
The Harpers' Neverwinter operation at the House of a Thousand Faces exists in opposition to the Masks' authoritarian control of the city. Squidly provides intelligence and disguises to help SWOJ infiltrate the Masks.

## Key Items and Resources

- **Sending Stone:** Given to SWOJ by Remalia in S01E22. Used extensively throughout Episodes 22-26 for real-time coordination during missions. Referred to humorously as "Ramalia's burner."
- **Silver Harp Brooch:** The Harpers' identifying symbol. Worn openly by agents in Neverwinter; more discreetly in Waterdeep. Squidly wears one on his lapel.
- **Letters of Charter:** Presented by Remalia to SWOJ in S02E38, officially recognizing the Guild of the Silver Waves of Justice with rights across the Sword Coast Alliance.
- **Masks Uniforms and Disguises:** Provided by Squidly from the House of a Thousand Faces in S02E14 for SWOJ's infiltration of the Masks in Neverwinter.
