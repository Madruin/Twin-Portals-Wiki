# Speakers For The Ancestors

The governing council of the [[Radiant Citadel]], representing each founding culture that settled the city 200-300 years ago. Led by First Speaker [[Shole]], the council oversees the Citadel's affairs from their chamber near the top of the city. All speakers went missing after [[Manshoon]]'s soul pylons were activated, but were eventually rescued by [[SWOJ]].

**First appearance:** Episode S02E33
**Total appearances:** 1 episode (see also [[Speakers Of The Radiant Citadel]] for earlier references)

## Overview

The Speakers for the Ancestors are the democratic governing body of the [[Radiant Citadel]], a wondrous city that serves as a nexus point for civilizations across the multiverse. Each Speaker represents one of the founding cultures that settled the Citadel between 200 and 300 years ago, making the council a diverse assembly of leaders from across many worlds and traditions.

The council is led by First Speaker [[Shole]], who was first referenced in S02E09 when [[Saks Bandin]] promised to report a shadow creature attack to her. The [[Speakers' Council Chamber]] is located near the top of the Citadel, adjacent to the top soul conduit pylon, and contains a platform with ancient ward controls that can shield the city from intruders, prevent teleportation, and control the Citadel's movement.

When [[Rowan Crooks]] (secretly the final [[Manshoon]] clone) arrived at the [[Radiant Citadel]], he built trust with the Speakers and introduced the soul pylons that would later be weaponized. After Manshoon took over, all Speakers were captured and imprisoned in [[Soul Stones|soul stone crystals]], going missing when the pylons activated approximately two weeks before [[SWOJ]]'s arrival. Fifteen speakers were ultimately freed from soul stone imprisonment during SWOJ's rescue mission through the sewers to the top of the Citadel (S02E34).
