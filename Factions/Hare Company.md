# Hare Company

A band of [[Waterdeep Military|Waterdeep army]] deserters who turned to banditry along the [[Long Road]] after the devastating defeat at [[Red Larch]]. Led by [[Jonas]] with [[Nina Blackthorn]] as their wizard, the company was identifiable by their purple rabbit insignia. [[SWOJ]] convinced them to return to military duty.

**First appearance:** Episode S02E06
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Overview

The Hare Company was a squad of [[Waterdeep Military|Waterdeep soldiers]] who deserted after the city's forces suffered a crushing defeat against [[Neverwinter]] at the Battle of [[Red Larch]]. Demoralized and leaderless, the company turned to banditry on the [[Long Road]] between [[Waterdeep]] and [[Amphail]], ambushing travelers for supplies and coin.

Led by [[Jonas]], a plate-armored fighter and former Waterdeep soldier, the company included [[Nina Blackthorn]] as their illusionist wizard and at least three archers including [[Kai]]. They were identifiable by their distinctive purple rabbit insignia.

The Hare Company ambushed [[SWOJ]] along the Long Road during the party's journey north toward [[Amphail]]. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] used the [[Avian Circlet]] to spot the three hidden archers through a crow's eyes, and the resulting confrontation became a battle featuring the "Murderation" tactic -- a combination of Xae's summoned [[Giant Ravens]] and [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]'s crow effects.

Rather than simply defeating the deserters, SWOJ chose diplomacy. The party convinced [[Jonas]] and his company to abandon banditry and return to military service. As they parted ways, [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] gave Jonas a [[WWSD Bracelet]] -- standing for "What Would Scoot Do" -- as a reminder of their encounter.
