# Long John Mccron's Crew

A rival pirate crew led by [[Long John Mccron|Long John McRon]], captain of the [[Wyvern's Whale]]. The crew encountered [[SWOJ]] at [[Driftwood]] and later competed with them for treasure on [[Devil's Rock]], where they were attacked by skeletons in the Skull Dunes.

**First appearance:** Episode S02E09
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Overview

Long John McRon's Crew is a pirate outfit operating in the waters around [[Driftwood]], the floating island settlement that rests on the shell of the dragon turtle [[Kura Han]]. Their captain, [[Long John Mccron|Long John McRon]], is a colorful pirate with a wooden peg leg on one side and a magical animated water hook on the other -- a gift from a pirate queen, connected to his arm by glowing tattoos.

McRon's crew sails aboard the [[Wyvern's Whale]], a pirate ship complete with its own shanty band. [[SWOJ]] first encountered McRon at the [[Blubber Buckle]], a tavern in [[Driftwood]], where he offered passage to [[Devil's Rock]] for 500 gold. He knew of the legendary whirlpool near the island but had never seen it himself.

When SWOJ traveled to [[Devil's Rock]] to investigate, McRon and his crew also made landfall, seeking treasure. While exploring the island, the crew was caught in the Skull Dunes and attacked by skeletons. To make matters worse, [[One-Eyed Wilhelmina]]'s ship, the [[Crimson Cracken]], appeared offshore and began firing cannons at the island, adding to the chaos. SWOJ observed the carnage from higher ground.
