# Neverwinter Forces

Soldiers from [[Neverwinter]] who crew the three airships during [[SWOJ]]'s assault on the [[Concord Jewel]] and the [[Radiant Citadel]]. Identifiable by their red uniforms, these soldiers suffered heavy losses when the [[Hammerhead]] was destroyed during a periton rider attack.

**First appearance:** Episode S02E32
**Total appearances:** 1 episode (see also [[Neverwinter Military]] for broader military context)

## Overview

The Neverwinter Forces refer specifically to the soldiers from [[Neverwinter]] who served as crew aboard the three airships -- the [[Ethereal Galleon]], the [[Hammerhead]], and a third vessel -- during [[SWOJ]]'s assault through the thunderstorm to reach the [[Concord Jewel]] and travel to the [[Radiant Citadel]].

These soldiers wore distinctive red uniforms, earning them the nickname "red shirts." They were drawn from the larger [[Neverwinter Military]] force of 500 soldiers and 30 spellcasters that [[Renaer Neverember]] had marshalled in response to the dragon threat.

During the fleet's passage through the thunderstorm in S02E32, the ships were attacked by [[Malar The Beast Lord's Followers|barbarian periton riders]] who threw weighted nets, boarded vessels, and used explosives. The attack was devastating: the [[Hammerhead]] and its entire Neverwinter crew were lost. Some soldiers on the other ships were caught in periton nets during the assault. Despite these losses, the surviving forces pressed on to the [[Radiant Citadel]].
