# Cult Of Malar

The Cult of Malar is a group of followers devoted to Malar the Beast Lord, the god of the hunt, predators, and bestial savagery. In the Twin Portals campaign, they appear in two distinct forms: a corrupting cabal in Season 1 who targeted the unicorn [[Lyrian]], and fierce barbarian periton riders in Season 2 who served as part of [[Manshoon]]'s alliance during the war.

**First appearance:** [[S01E21 - Episode Twenty One|S01E21]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes (S01E21, S02E32)

---

## Overview

### The Ash Tree Corruption (Season 1)

In S01E21, [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] encountered a cell of Malar's followers operating in the wilderness near an ash tree sacred to the unicorn Lyrian. The cult included a werewolf, four wererats, and a hag, all working together to corrupt the ash tree's waters with blood sacrifices. Their goal was to ensnare and corrupt Lyrian through a dark ritual -- tainting the sacred waters that sustained her. SWOJ defeated the cultists and saved Lyrian, who would later reappear in the Season 2 finale alongside [[Laeral Silverhand]].

### Barbarian Periton Riders (Season 2)

By Season 2, followers of Malar resurfaced as the [[Barbarian Periton Riders]] -- fierce warriors covered in war paint who rode [[Peritons]] into battle. Connected to Malar the Beast Lord, these barbarian riders chanted discordant melodies that coordinated their attacks with the peritons' haunting song. Armed with weighted nets, crowbars, and explosives, they boarded enemy ships during the aerial naval combat around the [[Spiral Cavern]]. They served as part of the broader coalition of forces aligned with Manshoon's Zhentarim, alongside dragon cultists and mercenaries. See also: [[Malar The Beast Lord's Followers]].
