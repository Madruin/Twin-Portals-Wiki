# Cult Of The Dragon

A fanatical dragon-worshipping cult allied with [[Manshoon]]'s forces during the Season 2 conflict. Operating primarily out of [[Castle Naerytar]] in the [[Mere of Dead Men]], the cult conducted dracolich creation rituals, captured friendly dragons of the [[Way of the Balanced Scale]], and excavated ancient relics of [[Myrkul]]. Their leader, [[Velia Shadowmantle]], was ultimately revealed to be [[Amalia Cassalanter]] in disguise -- a Waterdeep noble seeking revenge.

**First appearance:** Episode S02E19
**Total appearances:** 9 episodes

## Overview

The Cult of the Dragon became a major antagonist force in the second half of Season 2. First mentioned during the war council at [[Neverwinter]] as one of several threats facing the [[Sword Coast]], the cult operated a sprawling base at [[Castle Naerytar]], an ancient ruin in the heart of the [[Mere of Dead Men]]. Their operations included draining the swamp using magical pumps to expose underground ruins containing relics of [[Myrkul]] (the former god of death), capturing dragons of the [[Way of the Balanced Scale]] for use in dracolich creation rituals, and maintaining an underground obsidian fortress with a dragon hoard and the powerful [[Mask of Dragon Kind]].

The cult worked in concert with [[Manshoon]]'s broader network, which also included the [[Zhentarim]] and barbarian forces. Their members wore distinctive black dragon masks and operated with military discipline. The cult's most devastating achievement was the partial creation of a dracolich -- a two-headed abomination formed when one of their black dragons killed its sibling to fuel the transformation. This creature later terrorized the [[Radiant Citadel]] during [[Manshoon]]'s occupation.

The cult was also involved in the occupation of the [[Radiant Citadel]], with a dragon cultist piloting the [[Concord Jewel]] used in the invasion and cult operatives working alongside the [[Zhentarim]] to maintain control of the city.

## Known Members

- [[Amalia Cassalanter]] / [[Velia Shadowmantle]] -- The cult cell leader at [[Castle Naerytar]], disguised as "Velia Shadowmantle." A Waterdeep noble seeking revenge. Oversaw dracolich creation rituals and fled eastward atop the partial dracolich. Later appeared at the [[Radiant Citadel]] during the final battle.
- [[Bronk|Bronch]] -- A disillusioned white-masked cultist at Castle Nehri Tar who provided SWOJ with critical intelligence about the cult's operations. Sacrificed himself during the dracolich ritual to buy SWOJ time.
- [[Semu]]'s oppressors -- Unnamed cultists who occupied [[Castle Naerytar]] and systematically drained the swamp, devastating the native lizardfolk.

### Allied Forces

- Two unnamed black dragons that served as leaders of the cult operation. One killed the other to fuel the dracolich transformation.
- The partial [[Dracolich (Partial)|dracolich]] -- A two-headed abomination that later terrorized the [[Radiant Citadel]].

## Activities

- **S02E19:** The [[Cult of the Dragon]] is identified during the war council at [[Neverwinter]] as one of four major threats (alongside Waterdeep's dragons, Luskan's barbarians, and [[Manshoon]]'s Amphail army). Dragon cultists are present in the [[Spiral Cavern]], participating in the military buildup.
- **S02E20:** The final war council reviews threats including the Cult of the Dragon. The chasm-sealing ritual succeeds, and SWOJ turns attention to the cult's southern operations.
- **S02E27:** SWOJ discovers a hidden cave on the road near the [[Mere of Dead Men]] containing a magical pump operated by three Cult of the Dragon members wearing [[Black Dragon Masks|black dragon masks]]. The pump drains swamp water to expose underground ruins. SWOJ destroys the pump, defeats the cultists, and enters the swamp. [[Semu]], a lizardfolk cleric, reveals the cult has established a base at [[Castle Naerytar]] and is excavating relics of [[Myrkul]].
- **S02E28:** SWOJ infiltrates [[Castle Naerytar]] and meets [[Bronk|Bronch]], a disillusioned cultist who reveals the leader is [[Velia Shadowmantle]] (a Waterdeep noble) and that the cult performs dracolich creation rituals on captured dragons. SWOJ discovers the [[Underground Obsidian Fortress]] containing a dragon hoard, the [[Mask of Dragon Kind]], and a cauldron of dragon tea. They free [[Jill (Ygglimar)|Jill]], a white dragon held in a trance, and witness [[Velia Shadowmantle|Velia]] draining [[Jack]]'s soul into a phylactery in the [[The Sunken City|Sunken City]].
- **S02E29:** [[Bronk|Bronch]] sacrifices himself as a distraction during the dracolich ritual. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] casts Shatter on the phylactery. The plaza collapses into the [[The Shrouded Citadel|Shrouded Citadel]]. Velia's disguise is burned away by moonlight, revealing her true identity as [[Amalia Cassalanter]]. One black dragon kills its sibling to fuel the dracolich transformation. Amalia flees eastward atop the partial dracolich. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] uses the stolen [[Mask of Dragon Kind]] to summon allied dragons for SWOJ's escape.
- **S02E30:** SWOJ reports [[Amalia Cassalanter]]'s identity and the dracolich threat to [[Renaer Neverember]]. The devastation of [[Amphail]] by dragonfire is discovered. Saboteurs -- likely cult-affiliated -- strike [[Castle Never]], damaging the [[Ethereal Galleon]] and attempting to steal the [[Soul Stone]].
- **S02E33:** A dragon cultist is found piloting the [[Concord Jewel]] during the occupation of the [[Radiant Citadel]]. The cultist reveals the invasion timeline and the role of pre-planted agents. The two-headed dracolich circles the occupied city, terrorizing its inhabitants.
- **S02E34:** [[Amalia Cassalanter]] is present at the top of the [[Radiant Citadel]] during the battle. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] polymorphs into a T-Rex and bites her. The two-headed dracolich is banished by the [[Raven Queen]]'s divine intervention through [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]].
