# Watchful Order Of Magisters

The official wizarding guild of [[Waterdeep]], formally known as the Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors. The Order is responsible for registering magic users, investigating magical threats, monitoring portal instability, and providing arcane services to the city's government. Their members serve as both regulators and protectors of Waterdeep's magical infrastructure.

**First appearance:** Episode [[S01E05|S01E05]] (mentioned), [[S01E07|S01E07]] (Alcoria's visit)
**Total appearances:** 3+ episodes (S01E05, S01E07, S01E11, S02E05, S02E38)

## Overview

The Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors is Waterdeep's magical authority -- a guild of wizards tasked with monitoring and regulating the use of magic within the city. All magic users in Waterdeep are expected to register with the Order, and their magisters investigate magical crimes and threats ranging from doppelgangers and intellect devourers to portal instability along the Sword Coast.

The Order interacts with SWOJ through two primary members: [[Alcoria Stonemarrow]], a stern dwarf magister who handles registration and investigation, and [[Grinda Garloff]], who specializes in portal research and teleportation circles.

## Alcoria Stonemarrow and Winston

[[Alcoria Stonemarrow]] is the Order's primary representative in SWOJ's story. A dwarf magister, she first visits [[Trollskull Manor]] accompanied by her shield guardian [[Winston]] -- a large magical construct that serves as both protector and assistant.

During her initial visit (S01E07), Alcoria:
- Carved a glyph of warding on Trollskull Manor's front door.
- Registered [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] and [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] as magic users.
- Discussed the threats of doppelgangers, intellect devourers, mind flayers, and the [[Xanathar Guild]].

She returned in S01E11 in a more official capacity as the magistrate investigating the fireball explosion at Trollskull Alley. Cold and stern, she took depositions from witnesses and SWOJ, cooperating with the party despite her dour demeanor.

By S02E38, Alcoria has completed the construction of a teleportation circle in the basement of Trollskull Manor during SWOJ's absence, providing the party with a permanent magical transit point.

## Portal Instability Research

[[Grinda Garloff]] represents the Order's research wing, specializing in the portal instability that becomes a central plot concern across the Sword Coast. In S02E05, she visits the [[Short Rest]] to discuss the growing number of unstable portals appearing along the coast and offers to establish a teleportation circle at Trollskull Manor.

This portal instability -- portals opening unpredictably along the Sword Coast -- becomes a major Season 2 concern addressed by the [[Masked Lords Of Waterdeep|Masked Lords]] in S02E38, where the council expresses alarm about the phenomenon.

## Magic User Registration

The Order maintains a registry of all known magic users in Waterdeep. When SWOJ first encounters the [[City Watch]], [[Captain Staget]] advises them to register their magic users with the Watchful Order of Magisters (S01E05). This registration is later completed by Alcoria during her visit to Trollskull Manor.
