## Activities




- **S01E01:** Zhentarim agents launch a drive-by crossbow attack on the tattooed thugs at the Yawning Portal
- **S01E01:** Party discovers the Zhentarim warehouse on Candle Lane with three fresh corpses inside
- **S01E02:** Renaer explains the kidnapping: Zhentarim took him and Floon from Fillet Lane, then Xanathar Guild attacked and took Floon thinking he was Renaer
- **S01E02:** Party discovers twelve additional corpses in the warehouse from two warring factions (Zhentarim vs Xanathar agents)
- **S01E11:** SWOJ meets Davil Starsong of the Zhentarim Doom Raiders at the Yawning Portal
- **S01E12:** Charmed by Ylka, the Zhentarim leader reveals: the Zhentarim stole the Stone from Dalcar, and Yala Gralhund double-crossed them and took it
- **S01E12:** Dead servants found in the pantry - the Zhentarim have been murdering Gralhund staff
- **S01E26:** Manshoon's simulacrum and Zhentarim lieutenants appear on the balcony; Manshoon casts Bigby's Hand on Scoot
- **S01E29:** Rowan reveals the Necromancer is Manshoon, founder of the Zhentarim
- **S02E19:** SWOJ disguises as Zhentarim and infiltrates the Spiral Cavern during a staged distraction
- **S02E38:** The Doom Raiders have filled the Zhentarim power vacuum in Waterdeep after Manshoon's defeat
- **S02E38:** Simon (Scoot's father) returns after years lost at sea, tells of being helped by Zhentarim-affiliated pirates and witnessing a divine tidal wave near Luskan
