# Lords Of Waterdeep

The ruling political body of [[Waterdeep]], consisting of the Open Lord and the [[Masked Lords Of Waterdeep|Masked Lords]]. During the events of the campaign, [[Laeral Silverhand]] serves as the Open Lord, having replaced [[Dagult Neverember]]. The Lords govern the city and direct its military and political affairs, including commissioning [[SWOJ]] as agents of the state.

**First appearance:** Episode S01E15
**Total appearances:** 1 episode (as "Lords Of Waterdeep"; see also [[Masked Lords Of Waterdeep]])

## Overview

The Lords of Waterdeep are the governing authority of the City of Splendors, one of the greatest cities on the [[Sword Coast]]. The political structure includes a single Open Lord -- the public face of the government -- and a council of [[Masked Lords Of Waterdeep|Masked Lords]] who govern anonymously to prevent corruption and assassination.

At the start of the campaign, [[Dagult Neverember]] had recently been deposed as Open Lord due to his conservative spending policies, disruptive tariffs, and alleged embezzlement of a vast hoard of golden dragons. He was replaced by [[Laeral Silverhand]], a daughter of the goddess Mystra, who proved immensely popular with the citizens.

The Lords of Waterdeep became directly relevant to SWOJ when [[Laeral Silverhand]] took personal control of the investigation into the Trollskull Alley fireball and the theft of the [[Stone of Golorr]]. She replaced a corrupt magistrate (who had been bought by the [[Cassalanter Family|Cassalanters]]), demonstrated immense telekinetic power by crushing a grell in the courtroom, freed SWOJ from custody, and commissioned them as her personal agents to infiltrate the [[Xanathar Guild|Xanathar's lair]].

The [[Masked Lords Of Waterdeep|Masked Lords]] later accepted SWOJ's return of the recovered gold and granted them a 50,000 gold finder's fee. They also revealed that [[Laeral Silverhand]] had gone missing since the spring equinox. By the Season 2 finale, two more Masked Lords had vanished, and the council -- now united under the [[Sword Coast Alliance]] -- granted SWOJ their official guild charter.
