# Guild Of Apothecaries

A Waterdeep trade guild overseeing herbalists, alchemists, and apothecary shops throughout the city. The guild's most notable member in [[SWOJ]]'s story is [[Fala Lefaliir]], who runs [[Corellon's Crown]] on [[Trollskull Alley]].

**First appearance:** Episode S01E08
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Overview

The Guild of Apothecaries is one of the many trade guilds that regulate commerce and professional standards in [[Waterdeep]]. The guild governs herbalists, potion-makers, and apothecary shops, ensuring quality and standards across the city's pharmaceutical trade.

The guild became relevant to [[SWOJ]]'s story through [[Fala Lefaliir]], a wood elf herbalist and guild member who operates [[Corellon's Crown]], an apothecary shop on [[Trollskull Alley]]. Fala was one of the party's earliest neighbors and allies after they acquired [[Trollskull Manor]]. The Guild of Apothecaries was also involved in the tavern licensing process when SWOJ sought to open their establishment, appearing alongside other trade guilds at the licensing hearing.
