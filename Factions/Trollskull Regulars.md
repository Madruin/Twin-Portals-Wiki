# Trollskull Regulars

The Trollskull Regulars are the loyal circle of allies, neighbors, and regular patrons who gathered around [[SWOJ]] and the [[Short Rest]] tavern on [[Trollskull Alley]]. Over the course of the campaign, this informal community became a steadfast support network that stood by SWOJ through the darkest times -- from the fireball attack to the final battle against [[Manshoon]]'s forces.

**First appearance:** [[S02E37 - Episode Sixty Seven LIVE (S02E37)|S02E37]] (as a named group)
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes (named as "Trollskull Regulars")

## Overview

While the [[Short Rest]] attracted many customers after its opening, a core group of neighborhood residents and friends became deeply intertwined with SWOJ's adventures. These individuals were not just patrons -- they were allies who contributed to the tavern's defense, helped run operations during SWOJ's absences, and ultimately mobilized as a unified force in the campaign's climax.

The term "Trollskull Regulars" is first used explicitly during the events of Season 2's finale, when this allied group appeared at the [[Spiral Cavern]] helping with the refugee effort alongside [[Neverwinter Forces|Neverwinter soldiers]] and the [[Griffin Cavalry of Waterdeep|Griffin Cavalry]]. They later arrived at [[Trollskull Manor]] through the new teleportation circle for the homecoming celebration, cementing their identity as SWOJ's core supporters.

The Regulars are known for their irreverent sense of humor -- they once tried to put googly eyes on the [[The Stone of Golorr]], which [[Taga|Tega]] put a stop to.

## Known Members

The Trollskull Regulars encompass the broader community of Trollskull Alley residents and SWOJ's close allies. While the exact roster is fluid, the following individuals are strongly associated with this group:

### Tavern Staff and Household
- [[Taga|Tega]] -- [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]'s mother and the tavern's cook. Managed the Short Rest and raised the [[Trollskull Alley Irregulars|Irregulars]].
- [[Leaf]] -- The poltergeist residing in Trollskull Manor who managed the Short Rest during SWOJ's three-year absence.
- [[Nat]] -- One of the original [[Trollskull Alley Irregulars]]. Grew up to help manage the tavern.
- [[Jenks]] -- One of the original Irregulars. Grew up to help manage the tavern alongside Nat.

### Trollskull Alley Neighbors
- [[Vincent Trench]] -- Private investigator who runs [[Tiger's Eye]] detective agency on Trollskull Alley. A recurring ally who provided portal services, forensic analysis, and intelligence.
- [[Fala Lefaliir]] -- Wood elf herbalist who runs [[Corellon's Crown]], the apothecary on Trollskull Alley.

### Close Allies
- [[Renaer Neverember]] -- Noble benefactor who loaned SWOJ 1000 gold for Trollskull Manor renovations and remained a steadfast ally.
- [[Terry]] -- SWOJ's telepathic horse companion (an intellect devourer), who became a celebrity and independent vigilante in Waterdeep.
- [[Marie Polvich]] -- An ally who popped out from behind the bar at Trollskull Manor when SWOJ returned for the homecoming.

### Family Members
- [[Grim Wavesilver]] -- [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]'s father, present at the homecoming.
- [[Aspell Wavesilver]] -- A Wavesilver family member, present at the homecoming alongside Grim.

## Activities

### Season 2
- **S02E05:** [[Leaf]] has been running the Short Rest during SWOJ's three-year absence. [[Nat]] and [[Jenks]] have grown into teenagers helping manage operations. The community has maintained the tavern and neighborhood in SWOJ's absence.
- **S02E37:** The Trollskull Regulars appear at the [[Spiral Cavern]], helping with the refugee effort alongside [[Neverwinter Forces|Neverwinter soldiers]] and the [[Griffin Cavalry of Waterdeep|Griffin Cavalry]]. They actively support the evacuation and defense operations.
- **S02E38:** The Trollskull Regulars arrive at Trollskull Manor through the new teleportation circle for the homecoming celebration. [[Tega]], [[Marie Polvich]], the [[Wavesilver|Wavesilver family]], and other core supporters gather to welcome SWOJ home. [[Renaer Neverember]] gives a toast. [[Terry]] arrives dramatically with a lightning bolt, leaping across rooftops.

## Relationships

- **[[SWOJ]]** -- The adventuring party at the center of the community. The Regulars' identity is defined by their loyalty to SWOJ.
- **[[Short Rest]] / [[Trollskull Manor]]** -- The tavern that serves as the community's gathering place.
- **[[Trollskull Alley Irregulars]]** -- The younger members of the community, many of whom grew up to be Regulars themselves.
- **[[Trollskull Alley]]** -- The North Ward street where the tavern and many Regulars' businesses are located.

## Notes

> [!info] Community, Not Organization
> Unlike formal factions such as the [[Harpers]] or [[Zhentarim]], the Trollskull Regulars are an organic community rather than a structured organization. Their unity comes from shared loyalty to SWOJ and the neighborhood they all call home.
