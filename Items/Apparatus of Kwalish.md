# Apparatus of Kwalish

The Apparatus of Kwalish is a mechanical submersible -- sometimes called a "lobster barrel" -- owned by [[Grinda Garloth|Grinda Garloss]], an elderly adventurer and treasure hunter who lives in a lighthouse at [[Mist Shore]] in [[Waterdeep]]. The device is stored beneath her [[Grinda's Lighthouse|lighthouse]], accessible through a hidden floor mechanism operated by her rat familiar [[Anton]].

[[SWOJ]] borrowed the Apparatus from Grinda to explore [[Deepwater Harbor]] underwater, investigating the sunken [[Sea Plum]]. Strangely, Grinda claimed that [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] had visited the previous night and already borrowed the Apparatus, though Ylka had no memory of this -- an early hint of the [[Ylka Doppelganger]] storyline. The submersible later appeared during SWOJ's sewer exploration beneath [[Trollskull Manor]], where the party found it in a cavern beneath the [[The God Catcher|God Catcher]]. After the adventure, the party returned it to Grinda, whose familiar Anton scurried inside to inspect its condition.
