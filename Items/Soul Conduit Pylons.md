# Soul Conduit Pylons

The **Soul Conduit Pylons** are two hidden pylons -- one at the top and one at the bottom of the [[Radiant Citadel]] -- that emit green light. Originally introduced by [[Rowan Crooks]] during his time as a folk hero at the Citadel, the pylons were later weaponized by [[Manshoon]] to collapse the city's defenses and enable his occupation.

## Description

When activated, the pylons knocked out the Citadel's defenses and destabilized the structure, causing it to shudder and wobble. The ward controls in the [[Speakers' Council Chamber|Speakers' chamber]] could potentially counteract them.

## History

[[Rowan Crooks]], after gaining the trust of the [[Speakers For The Ancestors|Speakers for the Ancestors]], introduced the Soul Conduit Pylons to the Citadel. After Crooks disappeared, [[Manshoon]] took over and weaponized the pylon infrastructure. When agents activated the pylons, the city's defenses collapsed, guards were captured, the Speakers were imprisoned in soul stones, and curfew was imposed.

During the battle atop the Radiant Citadel, [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] communed with the [[The Raven Queen|Raven Queen]], who sent divine intervention -- a massive flock of spectral ravens that banished the two-headed dracolich and destroyed the top soul pylon.
