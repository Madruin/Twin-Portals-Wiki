# Neverember Ledger

The Neverember Ledger is a financial record belonging to the [[Neverember]] family, discovered by [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] inside [[Zelifarn]]'s underwater dragon hoard beneath [[Deepwater Harbor]]. The ledger documented financial misdeeds committed by [[Dagult Neverember]] and proved to be a crucial piece of evidence in [[The Stone of Golorr]] investigation.

SWOJ traded the ledger to [[Renaer Neverember]] -- Dagult's son -- in exchange for a diamond worth 1,000 gold pieces, which they needed as a material component for the clone spell to resurrect [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]. Renaer later used a separate ledger to prove the [[Cassalanter Family  Cult Of Asmodeus|Cassalanters]]' embezzlement of city funds at the [[Vault Of Dragons]], adding financial crimes to the faction's offenses during the climactic events of the gold hoard recovery.
