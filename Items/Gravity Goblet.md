# Gravity Goblet

The **Gravity Goblet** is a novelty wine glass with a reverse-gravity enchantment, given to [[SWOJ]] by [[Blossom Zuber]] as a reward for their investigation of the enchanted scarecrows terrorizing [[Undercliff]] farms in [[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]. A prototype product from the [[Zuber Farm]] winery's gift line, the goblet became a quirky memento of the party's early adventures and their first brush with the [[Cassalanter Family  Cult Of Asmodeus|Cassalanter]] conspiracy.

## Description

The Gravity Goblet is a wine goblet with a spiraling pattern around its exterior, presented in a gaudy gift box with clear plastic paneling. It features a reverse-gravity enchantment: when wine is poured into the glass and the glass is tilted, the liquid flows in the opposite direction from what gravity would dictate. Tilting one way sends the wine the other way, making it a challenge to drink from -- a novelty that fits the [[Zuber Farm]]'s touristy, merchandise-heavy approach to winemaking.

[[Blossom Zuber]] described it as a prototype from a new product line not yet available on the Zuber Farm shelves, suggesting it was meant for their gift shop alongside Zuber-branded stuffed animals, pins, and postcards.

## History

After [[SWOJ]] resolved the scarecrow attacks on [[Stoutfellow Farm]] in [[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]] and [[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]], they confronted [[Blossom Zuber]] at the [[Zuber Farm]]. [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] and [[Scoot Sparkles]] pressured her into confessing that she had used a devil-linked conjuration ring -- given to her by a tiefling at [[Cassalanter Villa]] (later identified as [[Willifort Crowell]]) -- to summon enchanted scarecrows against her rival farm. The ring crumbled to dust when removed.

SWOJ struck a deal with Blossom: they would search for her missing son [[Dasher Zuber]] and keep her involvement secret, in exchange for cooperation and information. After the party later found Dasher alive as a wererat with the [[Shard Shunners]] and delivered his farewell letter to Blossom, she rewarded them with the Gravity Goblet -- a prototype from her winery's product line -- along with an invitation for two to an upcoming soiree at [[Cassalanter Villa]]. That invitation became SWOJ's entry point into the [[Cassalanter Family  Cult Of Asmodeus|Cassalanter]] storyline, one of the central threads of Season 1.
