# Charming Cane

A magical cane wielded by [[Victoro Cassalanter]] that produces a ball of light capable of charming a crowd. Used at the [[Founders Day]] feast to mesmerize attendees as part of the Cassalanters' devil-soul harvest scheme.

## Key Appearances

- **[[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]]:** [[Victoro Cassalanter]] uses the Charming Cane alongside his [[Rod of Rulership]] to control the crowd at the Founders Day feast
