# Necklace of Fireballs

The **Necklace of Fireballs** was the murder weapon used in the devastating bombing of [[Trollskull Alley]] during the grand opening of the [[Short Rest Tavern (Trollskull Manor)|Short Rest]] in [[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]. A mechanical [[Nim|nimble wright]] perched atop [[Phyllis]]'s flower shop hurled a bead from the necklace into the crowd below, killing ten or more people including [[Vaniel]] the halfling and injuring [[Tagga]]. The necklace was recovered by [[Jenks]] of the [[Trollskull Alley Irregulars]] and became a key piece of evidence in SWOJ's investigation into the attack, eventually leading them to the [[Gralhund Villa]] and deeper into the conspiracy surrounding the [[The Stone of Golorr|Stone of Golorr]].

## Description

The Necklace of Fireballs is a magical necklace strung with swirling, inky-colored beads of bright red that radiate a conjuration magic aura. Each bead can be detached and thrown, detonating in a fireball explosion on impact. When [[Jenks]] recovered the necklace from a barrel on [[Trollskull Alley]], only two beads remained -- the rest presumably expended or separated. [[Vincent Trench]] identified it by name after [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] showed it to him, confirming the beads must be broken off and thrown to activate.

## History

The attack on [[Trollskull Alley]] occurred during the grand opening celebration of SWOJ's tavern, the [[Short Rest Tavern (Trollskull Manor)|Short Rest]], in [[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]. The alley was packed with friends, neighbors, and allies -- including [[Jarlaxle|Zardos Zord]] and [[Fel'Rekt Lafeen]] from [[Bregan D'Aerthe]]. A gnome named [[Dalekar|Dalcar]], a spy working for [[Dagult Neverember]], was running through the alley carrying the [[The Stone of Golorr|Stone of Golorr]] when the fireball detonated. Dalcar was killed. A figure clad in dark leather armor was seen kneeling over his body to take something before fleeing south.

The actual bomber was a mechanical [[Nim|nimble wright]] positioned on the roof of [[Phyllis]]'s flower shop, overlooking the alley. The nimble wright hurled a bead from the necklace into the crowd below. Eyewitness [[Phyllis]] reported seeing the dark figure and hearing something plop into a nearby barrel. [[Jenks]], an orphan member of the [[Trollskull Alley Irregulars]], found the necklace with two remaining beads in that barrel and presented it to SWOJ.

[[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] cast detect magic on the necklace, confirming its conjuration aura. She then brought it to [[Vincent Trench]], the private investigator on [[Trollskull Alley]], who identified it as a Necklace of Fireballs and explained that someone wealthy, criminal, or foreign would likely possess such a weapon. The investigation into the attack's perpetrators led SWOJ to the [[Gralhund Villa]] in [[S01E12 - Episode Twelve|S01E12]], where they found the [[Zhentarim]] and the [[Gralhund]] family embroiled in the fight over the [[The Stone of Golorr|Stone of Golorr]].
