# Playwright's Dagger

The Playwright's Dagger is a magic stage dagger given to [[SWOJ]] by [[Harold]], a former actor and husband of [[Eunissa]], who owns the [[Spouting Fish]] tavern in the [[Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]]. The dagger creates an illusion of stabbing someone without actually harming them -- the illusion fades after about an hour. It was a prop from Harold's acting days, gifted in gratitude after SWOJ rescued him from the sewers beneath the Spouting Fish where he had been kidnapped by the [[Xanathar Guild]].

## Key Moments
- **[[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]:** [[Harold]] gives the party the Playwright's Dagger after SWOJ rescues him and approximately fifteen other missing residents from the [[Xanathar Guild]] sewers beneath the [[Spouting Fish]]

## Connected Characters
- [[Harold]] -- Original owner; gave the dagger to SWOJ as a gift
- [[Eunissa]] -- Harold's wife; her tavern was above the sewer entrance
- [[SWOJ]] -- Received the dagger after rescuing Harold
