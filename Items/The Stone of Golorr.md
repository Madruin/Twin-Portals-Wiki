# The Stone of Golorr

The **Stone of Golorr** (frequently mispronounced "Stone of Galore" by the party) is a sentient artifact containing the consciousness of an [[Zelifarn|aboleth]] named Golorr. It served as the central MacGuffin of Season 1, holding the key to locating [[Dagult Neverember]]'s embezzled hoard of 500,000 gold dragons hidden in the [[Vault Of Dragons]] beneath [[Waterdeep]]. Every major faction in the city -- the [[Xanathar Guild]], the [[Zhentarim]], the [[Harpers]], [[Bregan D'Aerthe]], and the [[Cassalanter Family  Cult Of Asmodeus|Cassalanters]] -- competed to possess it, driving the gang violence and political intrigue that consumed the campaign. The Stone was created in prehistoric times by [[Xae (Prehistoric)]], an ancient incarnation of the halfling [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]], who crushed a massive tentacle beast into an avocado-sized stone on the banks of a frozen river in what would one day become Waterdeep.

## Description

The Stone of Golorr is a small, roughly avocado-sized stone that fits in the palm of a hand. It has three empty eye sockets where gemstones -- referred to as the **Eyes** -- must be placed to unlock its full power. The Stone is sentient and can communicate with those who touch it, delivering vivid psychic visions. When [[Scoot Sparkles]] first made contact with it, he experienced visions of dolphins and a massive tentacle creature with teeth lurking in deep water -- imagery that foreshadowed the Stone's true nature as a compressed aboleth.

The Stone's consciousness belongs to **Golorr**, an aboleth -- an ancient, alien creature of the deep sea with psychic abilities. [[Grinda Garloth]] identified the name "Golorr" as belonging to an aboleth in [[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]. When the Stone was submerged in water during the time-travel sequence in [[S01E25 - Episode Twenty Five|S01E25]], it transformed back into its true aboleth form, which SWOJ had to defeat.

The Stone also possesses the power to **ensorcell** nearby creatures. When SWOJ assembled all three Eyes at [[Remalia Haventree's House|Remalia Haventree's house]], the Stone ensorcelled [[Remalia Haventree]] and her guards [[Bricks]] and [[Bracks]], putting them into a trance-like state that could only be broken by physical impact.

## History

### Prehistoric Origins

In the time before [[Waterdeep]] existed, during a Winter Solstice on the banks of a frozen river, [[Xae (Prehistoric)]] -- an ancient version of the halfling [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] wielding immense magical power -- pulled a thirty-foot tentacle beast resembling a lamprey with an enormous jaw from the icy water using a beam of bright green telekinetic light. She hauled the thrashing creature onto land with one hand, then clenched her fist to compress it. Over about twenty seconds the monster wrapped tighter and tighter into a sphere until it plunked down into her palm as a small stone. This was the creation of the Stone of Golorr -- an aboleth crushed into artifact form.

SWOJ witnessed this event during a time-travel vision granted by the Stone itself in [[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]. The players reacted with shock at the revelation that their companion Xae had an ancient origin stretching back to prehistoric times and had personally created the artifact at the center of their adventures.

### The Chase for the Stone (Season 1)

The Stone's journey through Season 1 was a chain of theft and betrayal:

1. **Xanathar Guild possession:** The [[Xanathar Guild]] initially held the Stone.
2. **Dalakhar's theft:** [[Dalekar|Dalakhar]], a gnome spy working for [[Dagult Neverember]], stole it from the Guild.
3. **The Trollskull Alley fireball ([[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]):** The fireball explosion that rocked [[Trollskull Alley]] killed Dalakhar. A [[Zhentarim]] agent took the Stone from his body.
4. **Gralhund Villa ([[S01E12 - Episode Twelve|S01E12]]):** The Stone was brought to [[Gralhund Villa]], where [[Yalah Gralhund]] double-crossed the Zhentarim and claimed it for herself.
5. **The Cult of Asmodeus:** The Stone passed to the [[Cassalanter Family  Cult Of Asmodeus|Cult of Asmodeus]] via [[Willifort Crowell]], the Cassalanters' doppelganger majordomo. In [[S01E13 - Episode Thirteen|S01E13]], a spined devil delivered the Stone to Crowell in a side alley in the [[City of the Dead]] during [[Feast of the Moon]] fireworks.
6. **The cart chase ([[S01E13 - Episode Thirteen|S01E13]]):** SWOJ pursued Crowell's ornate wagon down the [[High Road]] through the Feast of the Moon parade. [[Scoot Sparkles]] ultimately recovered the Stone during this dramatic chase.
7. **Laeral Silverhand's custody ([[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]):** After SWOJ's trial, [[Laeral Silverhand]] took custody of the Stone and commissioned SWOJ to locate the three Eyes needed to activate it.

### The Visions

While imprisoned in the [[Docks Ward Guardhouse (Jail Cell)|Docks Ward guardhouse jail cell]] in [[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]], the Stone activated during the night and gave each conscious party member a personal vision:

- **[[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]:** Saw his mother reading him a story, then a secret passage in his childhood bedroom.
- **[[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]:** Saw her own death and resurrection inside the [[Gralhund Crypt]], revealing her connection to the events there.
- **[[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]:** Had previously experienced visions of dolphins and a massive tentacle creature with teeth.

The Stone then transported Scoot, Ylka, and Lark back in time to [[Prehistoric Waterdeep]] during the Winter Solstice, where they witnessed Xae's creation of the Stone and eventually saw time fast-forward as the hamlet grew, burned, and rebuilt into Waterdeep. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] herself remained asleep clutching a crystal unicorn and did not travel with them.

### Assembling the Eyes and Opening the Vault

After all three Eyes were recovered, SWOJ assembled them at [[Remalia Haventree's House|Remalia Haventree's estate]] in [[S01E25 - Episode Twenty Five|S01E25]]. The completed Stone transported SWOJ's consciousness 500 years into the past to **992 DR**, where they met [[Rosetta Stonemarrow]] (a dwarven historian), [[Zadio Neverember]] (ancestor of the Neverember line and theater owner), and [[Melifarn]] (a young bronze dragon). They discovered the [[Vault Of Dragons]] beneath Zadio's amphitheater.

When the Stone got wet during the vault exploration, it **transformed back into its aboleth form** -- the creature that had been compressed into it by Prehistoric Xae millennia ago. SWOJ defeated the aboleth by using feather fall and druid craft wind to dry it out.

In [[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]], SWOJ opened the Vault of Dragons beneath the [[Zadio City Music Hall]], recovered the 500,000 gold hoard, and fought off attacks from [[The Xanathar]] (killed by [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]'s thrown dagger) and [[Manshoon]]'s simulacrum. The gold was returned to the city, and SWOJ received a 50,000 gold finder's fee.

## The Three Eyes

The Stone required three gemstones -- called **Eyes** -- to be placed in its empty sockets before it could reveal the location of the [[Vault Of Dragons]]. Each Eye was guarded by a different faction, and recovering them formed the core quest of the second half of Season 1.

### First Eye: The Xanathar's Lair

**Recovered in:** [[S01E16 - Episode Sixteen|S01E16]]--[[S01E17 - Episode Seventeen|S01E17]]

The first Eye was hidden inside [[Sylgar]] the goldfish's stuffed beholder doll in [[The Xanathar]]'s private lair deep within the [[Waterdeep Sewers]]. [[Laeral Silverhand]] identified its location and sent SWOJ to retrieve it. During the infiltration, a puppet-controlled [[Thaelin]] (Squidly's brother, under the Xanathar's control) handed [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] the red gemstone, then fired a flame bolt into a chandelier, igniting smoke powder that collapsed the lair. SWOJ escaped through a secret door revealed by [[Jarlaxle]]'s warning.

### Second Eye: The Kolat Towers

**Recovered in:** [[S01E23 - Episode Twenty Three|S01E23]]

[[Squidly]], one of the [[Trollskull Alley Irregulars]], had infiltrated [[Urstul Floxxin]]'s [[Zhentarim]] operation near the [[Kolat Towers]]. When SWOJ found Squidly with Urstul, the young tiefling revealed he had pickpocketed the second Eye from the Zhentarim operative. The [[Kolat Towers]] exploded behind them as they escaped.

### Third Eye: The Cassalanter Shrine

**Recovered in:** [[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four|S01E24]]

[[Jarlaxle|Zardos Zord]] tipped SWOJ off that the [[Cassalanter Family  Cult Of Asmodeus|Cassalanters]] held the third Eye. SWOJ infiltrated [[Cassalanter Villa]] during a Founder's Day feast, with [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] wild shaping into a golden retriever to get past guards and the [[Amalia Cassalanter|Cassalanter]] children letting the party inside to play. Deep beneath the villa, they discovered an underground [[Shrine Of Asmodeus (Cassalanter Villa)|shrine to Asmodeus]] with a 30-foot horned statue, bloody cells, and a fire bowl. The third Eye sat as a marble on a pedestal in the center of the fire bowl, guarded by three bearded devils.

After defeating the devils in a pitched battle, SWOJ struggled to retrieve the marble from the flaming bowl. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] ultimately shattered the bowl itself, and the party grabbed the Eye just as [[Amalia Cassalanter]] arrived, charmed [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]], and launched a devastating fireball. SWOJ escaped through an underground river, emerging at the mud flats on Waterdeep's west coast in possession of all three Eyes.
