# Book of Shadows

The Book of Shadows is a warlock spellbook carried by [[Mr. E]], which he acquired following the death of his sensei [[Mr. Ziyagi]]. Mr. E returned to the party in [[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]] bearing the book, marking his transition from monk to warlock and deepening the mystery surrounding Mr. Ziyagi's demise.

The book proved significant beyond its use as a spellcasting focus. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] later studied the Book of Shadows and discovered crucial information about [[Zylka]] -- learning that Zylka had been reanimated by the [[Gralhund Family|Gralhunds]] without a soul. This revelation was a key piece of the puzzle surrounding Ylka's origins as a clone created by her grandfather [[Mordecai Gralhund]].

## Key Moments
- **[[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]]:** [[Mr. E]] returns to the party carrying the Book of Shadows after [[Mr. Ziyagi]]'s death
- **[[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]]:** [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] studies the Book of Shadows and learns that [[Zylka]] was reanimated by the Gralhunds without a soul
