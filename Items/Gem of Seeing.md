# Gem of Seeing

The Gem of Seeing is a magical spy device that [[Manshoon]] (operating as [[Manshoon  The Necromancer|The Necromancer]]) secretly implanted in [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]'s temple during the clone spell resurrection. Through the gem, Manshoon was able to see and hear everything SWOJ experienced, effectively spying on the party from the moment Lark returned to life until the end of Season 1.

The gem's existence was revealed when [[Rowan Crooks]] and the [[The Jolly Gents|Jolly Gents]] arrived through a Feywild portal at [[Trollskull Manor]] on the summer solstice. Crooks surgically extracted the Gem of Seeing from Lark's temple and simultaneously revealed the Necromancer's true identity as Manshoon, founder of the [[Zhentarim]]. [[Friar Truck]] healed the wound on Lark's head afterward. The extracted gem was later used to craft a compass from Lark's blood that would track Manshoon clones across the planes, launching the Hunt for Manshoon in Season 2.
