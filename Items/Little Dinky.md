# Little Dinky

An extendable rod wielded by [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]], nicknamed "Little Dinky." This versatile tool has been used as a propulsion device, a billiard cue, and even a weapon when Lark lashed one of [[Rowan Crooks]]' silver knives to it as an "extendo shank."

## Notable Uses

- Used in the struggle to retrieve the [[Eye of Golorr]] from a fire bowl, employing billiard-style shots ([[S01E22 - Episode Twenty Two|S01E22]])
- Jammed into the soul engine atop [[Zhentil Keep]], destabilizing [[Manshoon]]'s floating fortress and causing it to collapse from the sky ([[S02E25 - Episode Fifty Five LIVE (S02E25)|S02E25]])
- Used as a propulsion rod to launch Lark at [[Rowan Crooks]] during the final confrontation
