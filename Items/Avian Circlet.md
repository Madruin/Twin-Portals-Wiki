# Avian Circlet

A magical circlet that grants the wearer the ability to see through a bird's eyes and communicate with birds via Speak with Animals. Worn by [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] and later woven into [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]'s hair for communicating with [[Sally]] and her bird network.

## Notable Uses

- [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] used it to spot three hidden [[Hare Company]] archers through a crow's eyes during an ambush on the [[Long Road]]
- [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] uses it to communicate with [[Sally]], [[Beaker]], and the bird intelligence network across [[Waterdeep]]
