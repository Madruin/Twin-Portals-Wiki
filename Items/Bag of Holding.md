# Bag of Holding

The **Bag of Holding** was [[SWOJ]]'s most frequently used magic item across Season 1 and into Season 2 -- a brown burlap satchel with a bright red sash, given to the party by [[Jarlaxle|Zardos Zord]] during the psychic version of [[Waterdeep]] in [[S01E03 - Episode Three|S01E03]]. It served as the party's universal storage for loot, evidence, bodies, and supplies until [[Scoot Sparkles]] shattered it in [[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]] to scatter [[Dagult Neverember]]'s embezzled gold across the city. In Season 2, the party's journey into [[The Bag Dimension]] revealed the deeper cosmology behind bags of holding and the entity known as [[The Bag Man]].

## Description

A small brown burlap satchel with a bright red sash matching [[Jarlaxle|Zardos Zord]]'s clothing. Like all bags of holding, it opens to an extradimensional space far larger than its exterior suggests -- roughly a foot and a half across at the opening. Items placed inside have no effect on the bag's weight. The bag can hold an enormous volume of material, though it has limits; when loaded with hundreds of thousands of gold pieces from the [[Vault Of Dragons]], the bag was described as overflowing and maxed out.

Every bag of holding is connected to [[The Bag Dimension]], a demiplane maintained by [[The Bag Man]] -- a primordial entity present when the first bag of holding was ever created, whose soul anchors every bag in existence.

## History

[[Jarlaxle|Zardos Zord]] gave the bag to [[Scoot Sparkles]] during the party's encounter on his ships at the [[Sea Maiden's Faire]] in [[S01E03 - Episode Three|S01E03]], suggesting they use it to contain whatever was powering the psychic anomaly affecting [[Waterdeep]]. Scoot carried the bag for the remainder of Season 1, and it quickly became the party's default repository for everything from posters and pies to corpses and cursed artifacts.

During the [[Xanathar's Lair]] collapse in [[S01E17 - Episode Seventeen|S01E17]], the party placed [[Thaelin]]'s body inside the bag after recovering it from the rubble -- a solemn moment that underscored the weight of losing their companion.

The bag saw its most dramatic use in [[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]], when SWOJ spent hours loading gold from the [[Vault Of Dragons]] into it with the help of [[Aurinax]] the gold dragon. [[Ornex]] and the party shoveled coins into the bag until it was overflowing. Later, when [[Manshoon]]'s forces and the [[Xanathar Guild]] attacked the [[Zadio City Music Hall]] above the vault, the bag became a contested prize -- with enemies demanding SWOJ drop it during the battle.

After the climactic fight, Scoot shattered the Bag of Holding to scatter [[Dagult Neverember|Neverember]]'s gold across [[Waterdeep]], ensuring no single faction could claim the hoard.

In Season 2, the party entered [[The Bag Dimension]] itself ([[S02E13 - Episode Forty Three LIVE (S02E13)|S02E13]]), where they met [[Fioleth]], a ragamuffin creature who collects items that fall through bags, and encountered [[The Bag Man]] -- the ancient entity whose soul binds all bags of holding together. By [[S02E21 - Episode Fifty One (S02E21)|S02E21]], bags of holding across the market had stopped working due to [[Arcanthos]]'s interference in the bag dimension, causing prices to plummet and the party to buy discounted replacements.
