# Dragon Staff of Ahghairon

The Dragon Staff of Ahghairon is a powerful artifact that allows dragons to bypass the dragon ward protecting [[Waterdeep]]. The staff was guarded by [[Aurinax]], the adult gold dragon who watched over the [[Vault Of Dragons]] while disguised as a dwarf. After [[SWOJ]] earned Aurinax's trust, the dragon offered the staff to [[Mr. E]], who accepted the responsibility and took up residence at [[Castle Waterdeep]] as the city's unofficial dragon administrator.

Using the staff alongside dragon piss-enhanced vision elixirs, Mr. E vetted incoming dragons seeking entry to the city. He held this post through the end of Season 1, ultimately leaving the Dragon Staff with his staff at Castle Waterdeep before stepping through a Feywild portal with SWOJ. The staff's stewardship was central to Mr. E's transformation from a [[Bregan D'Aerthe]] operative into a civic leader, and it became a symbol of his co-founding of the [[Way Of The Balanced Scale]] -- a dragon alliance dedicated to keeping the balance.
