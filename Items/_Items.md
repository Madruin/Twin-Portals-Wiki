# Items

Key items, artifacts, and magical objects from the Twin Portals campaign.

## Artifacts

- [[The Stone of Golorr]] — Aboleth artifact revealing the location of Neverember's dragon horde
- [[Hungering Shard]] — Soul stone connected to Manshoon's network and the Wall of the Faithless
- [[Crown of Neverwinter]] — Mithril crown of the city's rightful ruler

## Weapons

- [[Song Sword]] — Ylka's enchanted cold-steel blade, crafted by [[Mordecai Gralhund]]
- [[Necklace of Fireballs]] — The weapon used in the devastating Trollskull Alley bombing

## Wondrous Items

- [[Bag of Holding]] — SWOJ's universal storage, destroyed in S01E26
- [[Boots of Striding and Springing]] — Inherited by [[Scoot Sparkles]] from [[Thaelin]]
- [[Gravity Goblet]] — Reverse-gravity wine goblet from [[Zuber Farm]]
