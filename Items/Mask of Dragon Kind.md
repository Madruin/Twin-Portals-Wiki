# Mask of Dragon Kind

The Mask of Dragon Kind is a powerful artifact kept by the [[Cult Of The Dragon]] inside the [[The Dragon Keep|Dragon Keep]] of the [[Underground Obsidian Fortress]] beneath [[Castle Nehri Tar]]. Displayed in a glass case alongside a dragon hoard and a cauldron of dragon tea, the mask was used in the cult's dracolich creation rituals. The disillusioned cultist [[Bronk]] revealed its existence to [[SWOJ]] during their infiltration of the fortress.

[[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] and [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] discovered the mask during their exploration of the dragon keep. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] later used the Mask of Dragon Kind to summon [[Jill]] and two other allied dragons during the climactic battle at Castle Nehri Tar, turning the tide against the cult.
