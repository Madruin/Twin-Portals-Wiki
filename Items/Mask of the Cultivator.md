# Mask of the Cultivator

The Mask of the Cultivator is a magical mask associated with the [[Masked Lords Of Waterdeep]]. It was discovered by [[SWOJ]] inside a vault in [[The Necromancer]]'s [[Private Study (Sanctum)|private study]] beneath the [[Kolat Towers]]. When [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] put on the mask, it transformed him into an unrecognizable six-foot figure wearing purple robes with an altered voice -- and permanently gave him silver-tipped hair.

The mask was later traded to [[Jarlaxle]] in exchange for information about the Necromancer. Lark's silver-tipped hair from the mask became one of his defining physical features for the rest of the campaign, a visible reminder of his encounter with the Necromancer's secrets.

## Key Moments
- **[[S01E23 - Episode Twenty Three|S01E23]]:** Lark discovers the Mask of the Cultivator behind a false back in the Necromancer's vault and puts it on, transforming his appearance and permanently giving him silver-tipped hair
- **[[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four|S01E24]]:** SWOJ levels up to 5th level; Lark's silver-tipped hair from the mask is noted as a lasting change
- **[[S01E23 - Episode Twenty Three|S01E23]]:** SWOJ trades the mask to [[Jarlaxle]] for intelligence on the Necromancer
