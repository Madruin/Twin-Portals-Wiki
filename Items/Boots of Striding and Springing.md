# Boots of Striding and Springing

The **Boots of Striding and Springing** are a pair of magic boots originally worn by [[Thaelin]], the party's wizard, throughout Season 1. After Thaelin was killed in the [[Xanathar's Lair]] collapse in [[S01E17 - Episode Seventeen|S01E17]], the boots were recovered from his body and inherited by [[Scoot Sparkles]], who used them for the remainder of the campaign. The boots became one of SWOJ's most reliable pieces of equipment, granting enhanced jumping ability that Scoot used in combat and exploration alike.

## Description

The Boots of Striding and Springing are a pair of enchanted boots that increase the wearer's walking speed to 30 feet (if it is not already higher) and grant a dramatically enhanced jumping distance. The wearer can leap three times the normal distance, both vertically and horizontally. In D&D 5E, these boots require attunement.

Thaelin kept the boots as one of his attuned magic items alongside his pendant and magic ring. After passing to Scoot, the boots complemented the half-orc barbarian's already physical fighting style, allowing him to launch himself into enemies and make dramatic entrances.

## History

[[Thaelin]] wore the boots throughout his time with [[SWOJ]], using them as one of his key attuned items. During the party's capture and trial at the [[Blackstaff Tower]] ([[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]), their captors confiscated most equipment but allowed Thaelin to keep his boots and pendant -- items he was magically attuned to.

When Thaelin triggered the [[Xanathar's Lair]] collapse by firing a Flame Bolt into a rigged crystal chandelier in [[S01E17 - Episode Seventeen|S01E17]], he was killed by falling rubble. The party recovered his body along with the boots, a magic ring, and a potion of greater restoration. The boots passed to [[Scoot Sparkles]], who wore them from that point forward.

Scoot made immediate use of the boots in the same episode, using them to launch himself with extra force during the battle in the lair -- leaping at [[Nihiloor]] the mind flayer with the boots' enhanced spring power. He continued using them throughout the late Season 1 episodes and into Season 2 for dramatic jumps and extra mobility in combat.
