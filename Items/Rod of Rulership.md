# Rod of Rulership

A magical rod that compels obedience from those targeted. Originally wielded by [[Victoro Cassalanter]] at the [[Founders Day]] feast to control the crowd during the Cassalanters' devil-soul harvest. Taken by [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] after Victoro's defeat.

## Key Appearances

- **[[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]]:** [[Victoro Cassalanter]] uses the Rod alongside his [[Charming Cane]] on the crowd
- **[[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]]:** Taken by [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] after Victoro's defeat
