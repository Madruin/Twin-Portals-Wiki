# Crown of Neverwinter

The **Crown of Neverwinter** is a mithril crown set with a large silver gemstone and red and blue gems arranged in a wavy pattern, discovered on the skeleton of [[Lady Lissandra Never]] in the crypts beneath [[Castle Never]]. An ancient symbol of Neverwinter's founding bloodline, the crown became the focal point of a three-way political struggle between [[Dagult Neverember]], [[Renaer Neverember]], and the forces of [[Manshoon]] during Season 2. Its magical properties -- channeled through crystals from the [[Spiral Cavern]] beneath [[Amphail]] -- proved essential to sealing the catastrophic chasm that threatened to destroy the city.

## Description

The Crown of Neverwinter is forged from mithril and features a prominent silver gemstone at its center, flanked by red and blue gems set in a wavy pattern. It was found resting on the skeleton of [[Lady Lissandra Never]], wife of [[Lord Halueth Never]] and co-founder of [[Neverwinter]], inside her simple stone coffin in the crypts beneath [[Castle Never]]. The coffin bore the inscription: "Lissandra Never of the waves of silver."

The crown's magical properties allow a person of Neverember blood to channel its power through compatible crystal conduits. When combined with crystals harvested from the [[Spiral Cavern]] beneath [[Amphail]], it can generate massive sealing energy -- enough to close the demonic rift in the Neverwinter chasm. The ritual requires the wielder to physically wear the crown, and [[Melifarn|Mellafarne]] the bronze dragon served as the ritual's conductor, directing the crown's power through the crystals.

## History

### Origins and Burial

The Crown of Neverwinter belonged to the Never family, the founding rulers of the city. [[Lady Lissandra Never]], a follower of [[Kelemvor]] (the god of neutral judgment), was buried with the crown in a simple, unadorned crypt behind a silver-scaled door in the deepest level of [[Castle Never]]'s crypts. Her husband [[Lord Halueth Never]], a disciple of [[Tyr]], was interred in an ornate tomb behind a gold-scaled door directly opposite -- but the crown was not with him. The humble placement of the crown with Lissandra rather than in Halueth's grander tomb served as a kind of test: the true relic lay in the simpler resting place.

### The Hunt for the Crown (S02E18)

With the Neverwinter chasm erupting catastrophically and unleashing demons into the streets, both Neverembers recognized the crown's strategic importance. [[Dagult Neverember]] hired [[SWOJ]] to retrieve it from [[Castle Never]], offering 5,000 gold each plus property. Simultaneously, [[Renaer Neverember]] contacted SWOJ via whisper magic, asking them to find the crown for him instead and providing historical context about the Never family.

Inside [[Castle Never]], [[SWOJ]] navigated past [[Quorlith The Starweaver|Quorlith]] the giant spider in the throne room, discovered a hidden map beneath the stone throne, and descended into the crypts. At the end of a long hallway lined with ancient burial chambers, they found the two sealed doors -- gold scales for [[Tyr]] and silver scales for [[Kelemvor]].

[[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] opened the gold door to [[Lord Halueth Never's Tomb]], finding an ornate crypt with nine silver swords and an inscription urging visitors to swear an oath and "join him in rest." [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] entered the silver door to [[Lady Lissandra Never's Tomb]], finding the austere coffin. Scoot made the crucial connection between the inscription -- "waves of silver" -- and the party's own name, the Silver Waves of Justice. After struggling with the pull-lid coffin, [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] lifted the mithril crown from Lissandra's skeleton -- and the ghost of [[Lady Lissandra Never]] rose with long wavy silver hair and hollow eyes, unleashing a devastating banshee wail that dropped Scoot to zero hit points instantly.

### Political Crisis (S02E19)

SWOJ emerged from Castle Never with the crown and faced a dilemma: which Neverember to give it to. [[Urstul Floxxin|Erstel Fluxen]], a captured [[Zhentarim]] assassin, revealed that [[Manshoon]] had also sent agents to seize the crown, adding a third party to the struggle.

A war council convened with [[Dagult Neverember]], [[Renaer Neverember]], [[Jarlaxle|Zardoz Zord]], and [[Remalia Haventree]]. Father and son were in the same room for the first time in the campaign. [[Melifarn|Mellafarne]] the bronze dragon explained she could seal the chasm using the crown's power channeled through crystals from the [[Spiral Cavern]], but the ritual required someone of Neverember blood to wield the crown. SWOJ proposed that both Neverembers share the responsibility, and Dagult and Renaer reluctantly agreed.

### The Chasm-Sealing Ritual (S02E20)

After SWOJ retrieved the necessary crystals from [[Amphail]], the ritual was performed with Dagult and Renaer trading the Crown of Neverwinter back and forth while [[Melifarn|Mellafarne]] channeled its power through the crystal conduits. The ritual succeeded and the chasm closed, saving [[Neverwinter]] from destruction. The moment of cooperation between estranged father and son -- sharing the crown that symbolized their family's claim to the city -- was a turning point in their fractured relationship.
