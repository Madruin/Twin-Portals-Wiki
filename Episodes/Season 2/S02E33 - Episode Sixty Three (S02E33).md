## Summary

SWOJ hurtles through the deep ethereal plane aboard the Concord Jewel, with the Ethereal Galleon and the salvaged remains of the Clockwork Kraken in tow. Scoot's brontosaurus form hangs out the jewel's entrance, blocking it. Ylka drops the polymorph, returning Scoot to normal, and the party dumps the crates of empty soul stones overboard into the ethereal void. Fylith works on salvaging the Clockwork Kraken's mechanical claw arm, jerry-rigging it onto the Ethereal Galleon as a crude but functional crane. The pilot -- a dragon cultist -- is interrogated under threat of Scoot's axes, revealing the invasion happened about two weeks ago, that infiltrating agents activated two hidden soul conduit pylons which knocked out the city's defenses, and that more soul stone crystals were being demanded. Xae programs the Concord Jewel to arrive and immediately return, hoping the Neverwinter army can use it.

As the Radiant Citadel comes into view, it is nighttime and the normally luminous auroral diamond at its heart glows a dark, sickly red. Two green light conduits -- the pylons -- are visible at the top and bottom of the city. A half-formed dracolich, larger than before with two skeletal dragon heads improperly merged, circles the city terrorizing its inhabitants. The streets are empty under an enforced curfew, though lights glow in buildings. SWOJ transfers to the battered Ethereal Galleon and sends the jewel back on autopilot. The ship moves at less than half speed with its masts destroyed.

Approaching stealthily, SWOJ is spotted by a kobold from Benzo's Emporium. Xae wild shapes into a bird to follow it, discovering the kobold leads to the back door of Vincent Trench's office in the Night Market. Inside, the noir-styled detective (a rakshasa in disguise with striped hair) provides Xae with a list of her six siblings -- all still alive. For tactical intelligence, Trench demands a favor from Xae in exchange, sealed by a geas spell. He reveals that the Speakers' council chamber near the top conduit contains a platform with ancient ward controls that can shield the city and prevent teleportation. The speakers' leader Shole and all speakers have been missing since the pylons activated.

SWOJ returns to the ship and sends Squidly and Erstel Floxon on separate scouting missions. They learn: agents infiltrated weeks before the pylons were revealed; the pylons knocked out defenses and the agents instituted a curfew; guards were locked up or disappeared; most concord jewels fled; the dracolich arrived only days ago and began destroying buildings and terrorizing people; and the pylons are destabilizing the citadel itself, causing it to shudder and wobble in space.

The dracolich emerges from the top of the citadel and enters the Preserve of Ancestors, capturing the dragon incarnate -- a gemstone dragon entity -- inside an orb of force between its two malformed heads. The whole citadel shakes as the incarnate is ripped from the preserve. Seizing the opportunity with the preserve's guardian gone, SWOJ casts pass without trace and sneaks past the guards at the doors with help from Sid Squids, a crawfish vendor who distracts the sentries. Inside, they find Zellopharn's unconscious bronze dragon body surrounded by piles of soul stones, guarded by two heavily armored warriors enhanced with soul crystals -- one with golden crystals projecting a spectral lion head, the other with green crystals projecting a spectral crocodile head. Lark and Scoot ambush the guards while Xae attempts to restore Zellopharn's soul. She discovers the surrounding soul stones act as magnets, preventing a clean soul transfer. Xae casts thunder wave at level three, blasting the stones away from Zellopharn and damaging both allies and enemies. Scoot rages and rips the golden crystal from the lion guard's armor, triggering a terrifying lion roar that frightens Scoot and Lark. The lion guard flees in terror, and Zellopharn bites the crocodile guard. Lark finishes the crocodile guard, whose death triggers a poison explosion. With the stones cleared, Xae meditates and successfully channels Zellopharn's soul from the pendant back into his body. The bronze dragon lurches to life, coughing and stretching his wings. Zellopharn chases down the fleeing lion guard but returns, reporting the man renounced his ways. SWOJ and Zellopharn reunite with hugs.

**NPCs:**
- [[Vincent Trench]] — A noir-styled private detective operating from the Night Market on the Radiant Citadel. Actually a rakshasa in disguise with striped 'Cruella de Vil' hair. Provides Xae with a list of her six siblings (all alive) and intelligence about the Speakers' council chamber ward controls. Demands a favor from Xae sealed by a geas spell in exchange for tactical information. His office has Venetian blinds, jazzy background music, and the back door is accessed via a secret knock.
- [[Zelifarn]] — A bronze dragon ally of SWOJ whose soul was trapped in a crystal pendant worn by Xae. His body lies unconscious in the Preserve of Ancestors surrounded by soul stones used as storage. After Xae clears the stones with thunder wave and meditates to channel his soul back, Zellopharn lurches to life and immediately helps fight the remaining guard. He chases the fleeing lion guard but lets him go after the man renounces his ways.
- [[Squidly]] — A resourceful tiefling crew member. Sent on a solo scouting mission to gather intelligence about the occupied city. Returns with information about the invasion timeline, the pylons' effects, the curfew, and the dracolich's recent arrival. Suggests attacking the bottom conduit as a diversion while SWOJ enters the preserve.
- [[Urstul Floxxin]] — A former Zentarim member sent on a scouting mission alongside Squidly. Uses his Zentarim connections and knowledge to eavesdrop on guards and gather intelligence about the occupation. Returns with information complementing Squidly's findings.
- [[Sholeh]] — The leader of the Speakers for the Ancestors -- the governing council of the Radiant Citadel representing each founding culture. Has been missing along with all other speakers since the pylons were activated two weeks ago. Their council chamber near the top conduit contains the ward controls.
- [[Fioleth]] — The quartermaster and repair specialist aboard the Ethereal Galleon. Works on salvaging the Clockwork Kraken's claw arm and jerry-rigging it onto the galleon as a crude crane mechanism. Also helps with general ship repairs.
- [[Sid Squid]] — A crawfish vendor on the Radiant Citadel. Creates a distraction for SWOJ by pestering the guards at the Preserve of Ancestors' doors about reopening his business, allowing the party to sneak past unseen.
- [[Concord Jewel]] — A dragon cultist who was piloting the Concord Jewel. Interrogated by SWOJ under threat -- reveals the invasion timeline, the hidden pylons, and that agents had been infiltrating the city before the takeover. Brought along to the citadel since he cannot be hidden in the translucent jewel.
- [[Dragon Incarnate (Rose Quartz)]] — A gemstone dragon entity residing in the Preserve of Ancestors, connected to the citadel's ancient magic. Captured by the dracolich inside an orb of force suspended between its two heads. The citadel shakes violently when it is ripped from the preserve, suggesting the incarnate is integral to the city's stability.
