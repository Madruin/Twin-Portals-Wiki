## Summary

Lark returns from his private audience with the Raven Queen, revealing that his soul has been anchored to his hat as a safeguard -- if he dies, the hat preserves his essence. SWOJ continues south along the High Road and discovers a hidden cave containing a magical pump operated by three Cult of the Dragon members wearing black dragon masks. The pump is draining the swamp water from the Mere of Dead Men, exposing underground ruins the cult is excavating. The party destroys the pump and defeats the cultists, then follows the drainage tunnel into the Mere of Dead Men proper.

Emerging into the swamp, SWOJ encounters a roadhouse called 'Swamp Castle' run by lizardfolk. There they meet Semu, a lizardfolk cleric of Semuanya -- the lizardfolk deity of survival and propagation. Semu explains that the Cult of the Dragon has established a base at Castle Naerytar, an ancient ruin at the center of the swamp. The cult is systematically draining the swamp to reach underground ruins and relics of Myrkul, the former god of death. Semu's people have suffered greatly from the cult's presence and she agrees to guide SWOJ through the treacherous swamp toward the castle.

The journey through the Mere of Dead Men proves dangerous. SWOJ encounters will-o'-wisps -- floating orbs of spectral light that attempt to lure travelers to their doom -- and mud mummies that rise from the swamp muck to attack. Semu is invaluable as a guide but parts ways with the group at the edge of Castle Naerytar's perimeter, unwilling to go closer due to the dangers. Xae wildshapes into a bat to scout the castle from above, discovering massive excavation pits in the bailey, approximately 30 cultists inside, and five guards on the outer walls. The ruined wizard tower offers an unguarded entry point.

SWOJ instructs Little Joan to set up a secret camp with the Jolly Gents on nearby raised ground while the core party infiltrates the castle. Using Xae's pass without trace spell, they sneak through the ruined telescope tower and discover a nest of approximately 20 otyugh eggs. The eggs begin hatching, and Xae successfully befriends a baby otyugh through animal handling. The episode ends as the party is inside the castle walls with a trail of newborn otyughs following Xae, the dead body of the mother otyugh nearby -- killed by the cultists.
