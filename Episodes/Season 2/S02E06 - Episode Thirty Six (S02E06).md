# Episode Thirty Six (S02E06)

## Summary

SWOJ departs Waterdeep heading north on the Long Road with Ziraj the Hunter and the giant scarab Craw. They stop at Froon's Brews where Ted and Phyllis donate two health potions for the journey. Traveling through the North Ward and out the gate, they pass through the hamlet of Rassalanter before being ambushed on the road by the Hare Company -- a band of deserters from Waterdeep's army led by Jonas, a plate-armored fighter, with Nina Blackthorn serving as their wizard and illusionist, and Kai among the archers.

A major combat ensues. Xae uses the Avian Circlet to see through a crow's eyes, spotting three hidden archers. Scoot rages and charges into melee, Lark engages the fighters, Ylka unleashes Shatter and Dissonant Whispers, and Xae wild shapes into a giant centipede before summoning giant ravens. Ylka invents the 'Murderation' -- a combination of a murder of crows and a murmuration -- to devastating effect. The Hare Company eventually yields, revealing they are Waterdeep soldiers who deserted after a devastating defeat at Red Larch. SWOJ convinces them to return to duty rather than living as bandits. Ylka casts Yurtle's Trippy Tortoise Shell for a short rest, and the parties go their separate ways, with Lark gifting Jonas a WWSD bracelet.

Arriving in Amphail, the party finds the town under military occupation with soldiers from the Waterdeep-Neverwinter conflict. They part ways with Ziraj at the town entrance and visit a music shop run by Hanna, whose husband Juan -- a half-elf fiddler -- has gone missing along with other townspeople. Multiple disappearances are linked to mysterious mists that roll in from the Cryptwood, causing blackouts and memory loss. Ylka gets her concertina repaired and Xae finds the missing keys. Ylka buys a new orca-shaped ocarina for 200 gold.

At the Staghorn Flagon (Stag and Flag) tavern, they meet Gundarkson, the bartender-mayor of Amphail, who explains that Anya Irmbrusk went missing about a tenday ago. She had been staying in Amphail for nearly a month preparing for the Right of the Staglass -- a ceremony that would allow her to legally renounce her noble family ties. The mists come unpredictably from the Cryptwood and cause people to blackout and wake up in different places, sometimes with weapons in hand. SWOJ heads into the Cryptwood with ropes tied between them, uses the turtle shell to survive a mist encounter, and hears a low moaning sound and sees dead shredded birds along a path of crushed trees. They reach the Circle of Dusk's glade and meet Farron, an amnesiac former soldier being cared for by the druids, who have now all gone missing -- Shala (the leader), Nuria, Ander, and Lea. Farron joins the party with Ylka's explosive sword. As night falls, the mist returns with a mysterious witch's voice saying 'I know he's here somewhere' while butterflies drain the life from the surrounding vegetation and the great tree turns to stone. SWOJ camps overnight in the turtle shell and sets out for the cave in the Sword Mountain foothills the next morning, sending Craw back to town.
