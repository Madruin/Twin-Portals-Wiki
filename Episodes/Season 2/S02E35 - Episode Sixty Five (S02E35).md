## Summary

In the aftermath of the battle atop the Radiant Citadel, the Raven Queen communicates with Scoot, warning him about Manshoon's ambitions to achieve godhood. The spectral ravens begin to fade as the Citadel passes through the Keening Gloam, their divine power unable to sustain in this interdimensional void. The party takes stock of their situation: the top soul pylon is destroyed, the speakers are free, and Sholeh is rescued, but Manshoon and the bottom soul pylon remain a threat.

Allied reinforcements arrive aboard a hammerhead ship. Nat, a griffin rider who communicates through sign language, leads the force alongside Janks, a wizard in a distinctive blue hat. They bring approximately 30 soldiers, 5 casters, carrier pigeons (including one named Sally), owls, and a blood hawk. Sholeh, channeling the power of the Aural Diamond, provides the party with a compressed long rest -- healing and restoring their abilities in a fraction of the normal time. She explains the history of the soul pylons: they were introduced by Rowan Crooks, a folk hero who arrived at the Citadel and built trust before installing the pylons and then mysteriously disappearing. Manshoon later took over and weaponized the pylons.

SWOJ and their allies fly down the exterior of the Citadel aboard the hammerhead ship, accompanied by dragons and griffins. They rescue a floating school that had become detached during the chaos. Upon reaching the bottom, they discover that the remaining soul pylon has been dragged from its original position and combined with the destroyed top pylon's base, creating a single massive pylon. The Citadel then exits the Keening Gloam and arrives at the Wall of the Faithless -- a domain associated with Kelemvor, god of the dead.

The party is teleported to the surface of the Wall of the Faithless, where they encounter Arcturia, Xae's shape-shifting sister. Arcturia works for Halaster Blackcloak, the mad wizard of Undermountain, and has come to break the Wall. She is a transmutation wizard who shifts between forms -- at one point appearing as a cloud of butterflies and maggots. After tense negotiations and even combat (Arcturia casts disintegrate on Scoot, dealing massive damage), SWOJ persuades Arcturia to join their cause rather than work against them. Arcturia agrees and leads them to the base of an enormous skeleton -- Kelemvor's physical remains -- where she begins a ritual to summon souls trapped within the Wall.

Sholeh explains that Manshoon later took over and weaponized the pylon infrastructure that Crooks had built. His soul stone is a key item.
- [[Halaster Blackcloak]] — The mad wizard of Undermountain, Arcturia's employer. He sent Arcturia to the Wall of the Faithless. He is not present but his influence drives Arcturia's actions.
