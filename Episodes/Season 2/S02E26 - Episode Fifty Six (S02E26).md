## Summary

The episode opens with the Raven Queen's emissary addressing SWOJ after their trials in the dream sequence. The emissary speaks about the Wall of the Faithless -- a massive barrier in the afterlife built by the god Kelemvor to trap the souls of those who worshipped no deity. The Raven Queen opposes this wall and seeks allies in her cause. As a gift, she grants Scoot enchanted raven feathers that can banish undead once per week. The Raven Queen then takes Lark away temporarily for a private audience, and the party must continue without him.

SWOJ exits the Shadowfell through a portal at the Shard of Night and arrives in Neverwinter. They make their way to the Hall of Justice to meet with Renaer Neverember, their ally and patron. They hand over the Soul Stone containing Rowan Crooks for safekeeping, and Renaer provides them with specialized dragon-hunting equipment in preparation for their next mission: a net of binding, mirage marbles for creating illusory distractions, dragon fang arrows, and dragon hide cloaks for resistance against breath weapons.

The party departs Neverwinter heading south along the High Road toward the Mere of Dead Men, where Cult of the Dragon activity has been reported. Along the road they encounter bulettes -- massive burrowing land sharks -- that attack from underground. During the battle, SWOJ meets Shaleheart, a domesticated bulette ally who joins their cause. They also encounter Lochlina, a halfling herbalist who was being chased by the bulettes because they were attracted to her Lunaria herbs. Lochlina sells them three potions of greater healing before parting ways. The episode ends with the party continuing south toward the swamp.
