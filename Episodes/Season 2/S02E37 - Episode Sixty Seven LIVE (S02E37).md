## Summary

In this live penultimate episode of Season 2, SWOJ finds themselves aboard a Concord Jewel fleeing the crumbling Radiant Citadel after breaking the Wall of the Faithless. The episode opens in the aftermath of the previous battle, with the party aboard the jewel alongside Neverwinter soldiers, Renaer Neverember, and refugees. They quickly discover that imposter versions of themselves — Bizarro SWOJ — have been guiding refugees onto the jewel. These fakes are revealed to be illusions cast by giant frog-like creatures from beyond the Wall.

The bulk of the episode is a chaotic combat encounter against two of these frog creatures — a green one wielding a wand and a gray one — aboard the Concord Jewel. The frog creatures repeatedly smash the jewel's navigation console, causing reality to warp and shift the battlefield through multiple dimensions: first to a stone dungeon hallway filled with traps (reminiscent of Skull Rock), and then to the Bag Dimension where they spot an ancient red dragon in the distance. Throughout, Ylka uses Dimension Door and Shatter spells, Scoot attacks the green frog's wand with his star metal weapon, Lark performs daring acrobatic feats across ropes, and Xae casts Moonbeam, Faerie Fire, and Balm of the Summer Court.

Scoot ultimately destroys the navigation console to escape the Bag Dimension and the approaching dragon, snapping them back onto the Concord Jewel. The gray frog escapes through a rift in space. The damaged jewel crash-lands with tremendous force in the Spiral Cavern back on the Sword Coast, where Neverwinter soldiers, Trollskull Regulars, and the Griffin Cavalry of Waterdeep are waiting to receive refugees. The episode ends with the battered heroes crawling from the wreckage — Scoot kissing the ground, Lark taking a bow, and Ylka playing music with her last three hit points — as the crowd cheers for the Silver Waves of Justice.

### Key Events

- SWOJ discovers imposter 'Bizarro' versions of themselves guiding refugees onto a Concord Jewel — the fake Lark plays concertina poorly, the fake Scoot is chatty, and the fake Xae is standoffish

- Ylka and Xae use Dimension Door to teleport past traps to the console; Lark triggers traps that damage the frog creatures with saw blades and oil fire
- Scoot destroys the green frog creature's wand with his star metal weapon, killing the green frog in the process
- Reality warps again to the Bag Dimension, where an ancient red dragon is spotted approaching
- Scoot destroys the navigation console entirely, snapping the party back to the Concord Jewel on the material plane
- The gray frog creature escapes through a rift in space
- The damaged Concord Jewel crash-lands in the Spiral Cavern on the Sword Coast, where allies are stationed receiving refugees
