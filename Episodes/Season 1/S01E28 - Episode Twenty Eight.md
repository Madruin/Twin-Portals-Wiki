## Summary

Episode Twenty Eight is a behind-the-scenes conversation between Matias and Scott rather than a gameplay episode. The two hosts reflected on the journey of Twin Portals up to that point, discussing the production process, their lives outside the podcast, and the creative decisions that shaped the campaign. They shared their favorite video games, books, and media that influenced their storytelling sensibilities.

The conversation touched on the state of the campaign, with Matias and Scott reviewing the major story arcs including the Xanathar's lair, the Cassalanter plot, and the lingering threat of the Necromancer. They discussed DMing philosophy and techniques, including how they handled player-driven storytelling and improvisation within the framework of the Waterdeep: Dragon Heist adventure module.

Looking ahead, they speculated about potential settings and themes for the next arc of the campaign, mentioning possibilities like seafaring adventures and the Feywild. They also discussed their personal reading habits, recommending books like Dune, Ender's Game, and the Kingkiller Chronicles, as well as their favorite video games including Breath of the Wild, Death Stranding, and Hades.

The episode served as a breather between the climactic events of the Cassalanter arc and the upcoming Season 1 finale, giving listeners a glimpse into the real people behind the characters of SWOJ.

### Key Events

- Behind-the-scenes episode with no gameplay; Scott and Matias discuss the podcast and their lives
- Reflection on the campaign so far including the Xanathar arc, Cassalanter plot, and the Necromancer thread
- Discussion of DMing techniques and how player-driven storytelling shaped the campaign
- Speculation about future campaign settings including seafaring and the Feywild
- Discussion of favorite media: video games (Breath of the Wild, Death Stranding, Hades), books (Dune, Ender's Game, Kingkiller Chronicles)
