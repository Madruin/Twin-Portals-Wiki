## Summary

Episode Thirty was a special talkback panel titled 'Twin Portals Con 2021,' recorded with all cast members and a small live audience of friends and family. Rather than a gameplay session, the episode served as a retrospective on the entirety of Season 1 (Arc One) of the Twin Portals campaign, with the cast reflecting on their characters, favorite moments, and the creative process behind the show.

The panel discussed several loose story threads and dangling plot hooks from the campaign. They talked about Remalia Haventree and how Kendra kept pushing to learn more about her throughout the story. The relationship between the Necromancer and Lark's backstory was explored, including the revelation that Isvel (the Black Viper) being Lark's mother happened partly by accident through Justin's character creation. The origin of Mr. Ziyagi was discussed as a character Robert made up as a joke that became integral to Mr. E's story. The coincidence of Beth choosing the Gralhund family name for Ylka, which matched a family already written into the Dragon Heist adventure, was highlighted. Rowan Crooks was revealed to have been created by Justin, inspired by a name from a Duluth newspaper, and grew to become a central figure in the narrative.

Each cast member reflected on their character's journey and growth throughout the campaign. They discussed the collaborative nature of character backstory development and how the DM wove player-created elements into the larger Waterdeep: Dragon Heist framework. The conversation touched on how the source book offered four different villain paths and how the campaign incorporated elements from multiple paths rather than choosing just one.

The panel offered advice for aspiring actual play podcasters, discussing the importance of consistent scheduling, being open to improvisation, and creating a welcoming table environment. They discussed Xae's Solstice gifts to the party members, adding personal touches to the character relationships. Plans for Season 2 (Arc Two) were teased, with excitement about the Feywild setting that would carry the story forward.
