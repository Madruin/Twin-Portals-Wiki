## Summary

The episode begins with the party wrapping up at the Spouting Fish after the rescue. Captain Staget of the city watch advises them to register any magic users with the Watchful Order of Magisters. While traveling, the party receives a mysterious arrow with a note signed 'Y' warning them they are being followed. Alarmed, they rush to check on Scoot's mother Taga, only to find her apartment ransacked with crow feathers scattered about -- evidence of the Xanathar Guild seeking payback. They discover an amethyst sending stone and learn that Pinker, a kenku ally, has already rescued Taga and hidden her in a smuggler's closet within the Honorable Knight statue tenements.

With Taga safe, the party travels to the North Ward to inspect their new property, Trollskull Manor. The building is in disrepair but impressive in scale, with a taproom on the ground floor and multiple stories above including a tower. They encounter Lif, the ghost of a middle-aged elven bartender who formerly worked at the manor. Lif writes messages warning them: 'closing time,' 'she is coming,' and 'please save the children.' The party discovers three street urchin children -- Nat (a mute human girl and their leader), Jenks (a nine-year-old half-orc boy who fancies himself a wizard), and Squidly (a tiefling boy with an eye patch) -- who have been playing in the abandoned building.

In the cellar, the party finds Lif's personal effects including his diary, gold, a bartender's smock, and a magical short sword with the initials 'M.G.' (Mordecai Gralhund, Ylka's relative) that plays music and deals cold damage. The diary reveals Lif worked at the manor 95 years ago and discovered something terrible: the legend of Esther, a hag who ran an orphanage and ate children, was real and still haunted the building. When the children accidentally unearth a glowing talisman (a shriveled green hand clutching a heart) from under the tower's floorboards, the manor is pulled into a demi-plane. The hag Esther manifests with seven spectral children servants, attempting to cook Squidly in a cauldron. The party fights Esther in a chaotic battle -- Ylka throws the talisman into the boiling cauldron, which strips away the hag's ghostly form and makes her physical. Xae delivers the killing blow as a giant wolf spider, and the hag is destroyed. The spectral children are freed, the demi-plane collapses, and Lif appears one final time with a smile before fading. Volo arrives at the very end, apologizing for his lateness.
