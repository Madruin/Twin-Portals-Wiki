## Summary

Episode 21 marks the return to actual gameplay after a year-long hiatus. The DM's 'previously on' recap establishes that two months have passed in Waterdeep since SWOJ returned from the Xanathar's lair. Xae disappeared into the Fey with the unicorn Lyrian. Scoot remained at the Short Rest tavern, tending an urn of Lark's remains on the mantle. Ylka searched for information about her past and her resurrection, finding an inscrutable necromancer via a scrying pool in the Short Rest's deep cellar. The necromancer offered to return Lark to life by forming a copy of his body, but warned that without a soul the body would be empty. Lark's soul was either unwilling or unable to come home. Using Lark's majestic hat as a vessel, Scoot and Ylka agreed to let the necromancer send them into the afterlife.

The party (minus Ylka, who is absent this session) awakens in a strange otherworldly landscape -- a wild, stormy forest realm connected to the deity Malar, the Beast Lord. They meet a large colony of ants ruled by a queen who gives them purified water, and a mysterious figure named Rowan Crooks who explains the nature of this plane. They encounter a pack of wolves led by a mama wolf who bond with Xae and accompany the party. Ascending a mountain following a swelling river, they discover the hag from Trollskull Manor (now missing an arm from their previous fight) serving Malar. She has captured three dryads -- Tricayla, Argentia, and their sister -- and caged 18 animals for sacrifice. The party defeats the hag using Ylka's dissonant whispers and Scoot's magic net, extracts her heartstone, frees the dryads and animals, and cages the hag.

The hag reveals she plucked Lark's soul from Kelemvor's grasp and gave him to Malar as bait to trap Lyrian. At the mountain summit, they find an enormous ash tree at the center of a volcanic caldera, with four seasonal streams spiraling into it. Lark's body rests unconscious in the crook of the tree. Five bestial humanoids -- four wererats and a werewolf -- eat animal meat, their blood corrupting the water and turning the tree red. Lyrian approaches in a trance, eating blood-red ash berries from the werewolf. Xae initiates combat with moonbeam, which reverts the werewolf to human form. Ylka uses dissonant whispers and chill touch against the enemies. Scoot rages and kills the corrupted wolf-man but the unicorn gores Scoot, who fails a wisdom save and is transformed into a wolf under the unicorn's control. Meanwhile, Ylka sprints across the pool to Lark and places his hat on his head, waking him. Xae wild shapes into a giant goat, rams the corrupted Lyrian back into the pool, reverts to halfling form, and pours the purified water into the pool. Lightning from the moon strikes Lyrian, purifying her. The wererats flee.

In the emotional reunion, Lark hugs everyone. The hat's magic activates, pulling them through a wormhole back to the Short Rest cellar -- but Lark arrives as a soul trapped inside the hat, able to communicate telepathically. His body did not make the journey. Ylka still has the hag's heartstone in her pocket.

After being pushed into the pool by Xae (as a giant goat) and doused with purified water, she is struck by white moonlight and restored to her true form. She tells the party 'I'll find you. Be safe. - [[Kelemvor]] — God of the dead in the Forgotten Realms. The hag reveals she plucked Lark from Kelemvor's fist and gave him to Malar.
- [[Malar]] — The Beast Lord, a deity of hunting, lycanthropy and blood. The afterlife realm the party enters belongs to him. The hag serves him, capturing dryads and animals as sacrifices. His followers (wererats and a werewolf) corrupt the ash tree's water with blood to ensnare Lyrian.
