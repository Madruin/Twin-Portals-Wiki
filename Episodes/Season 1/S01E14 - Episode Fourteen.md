## Summary

In the Docks Ward guardhouse jail cell, the Stone of Galore activates during the night and gives each party member a personal vision. Lark sees his mother and a hidden secret passage. Ylka sees her own death and resurrection inside the Gralhund Crypt, revealing her connection to the events there. All three conscious members (Scoot, Ylka, and Lark) are then transported through time by the Stone's power to prehistoric Waterdeep during a Winter Solstice.

This special holiday episode sees the party meet Gorge Bailey Brew, a dwarf whose daughter Zuzu has gone missing near Zaka's Tome Tower. They encounter the Ghosts of Solstice Past (manifesting as snow dwarves) who deliver cryptic riddles and warnings about Krampus, a monstrous creature terrorizing the region. After fighting Krampus in the snowy woods, SWOJ reaches Zaka's Tome Tower and discovers the Bailey Brew family trapped under the spell of Hans Von Gruber, a vampire wizard who has taken over the tower.

The party battles Von Gruber and his Pain Deer (gingerbread reindeer constructs) in the tower. They ultimately defeat Von Gruber not through violence alone but through an act of kindness, using the Fleece of Navi-dad (a magical holiday item) to show him compassion. Afterward, they help the Bailey Brew family deliver toys across the region using a magical sleigh. During the flight, they witness a pivotal flashback: Xae pulling a tentacle beast from the river and crushing it into what becomes the Stone of Galore. Time then speeds up around them and they watch Waterdeep grow from a settlement into a city over centuries. They see an underground door with three rings before waking up back in the jail cell. The Stone now has three empty eye sockets visible, and Scoot discovers a glass dolphin from his vision is physically real inside his bag of holding.
