## Summary

Captured and stripped of their gear, SWOJ is brought to the Arena of Blood and Fortune within the Xanathar's lair. The arena is overseen by a drow announcer, a mind flayer, and the invisible Xanathar watching from above. Lark and Ylka are sent into the arena first to fight two Kuo-Toa champions called the Avatars of Blibdoolpoolp. Meanwhile, Scoot and Xae are held in cells below where they discover Pinker (a kenku) and twelve other kenku prisoners. Scoot trades his durag for Pinker's pink hat, forging an alliance with the kenku. Xae uses Mold Earth and Pinker picks locks to free the prisoners.

JB Nevercott -- secretly Jarlaxle Baenre of Bregan D'aerthe -- is in the audience and telepathically warns them via Message that the lair is rigged with smoke powder and there is a secret door on the south wall marked with a flaming eye symbol. The party stages a fake fight with the kenku in the arena, buying time. Scoot locates the flaming eye brick on the south wall. Thaelin then appears, seemingly puppet-controlled, and hands Xae a red gemstone (the Eye of the Stone of Golorr) before firing a Flame Bolt into a crystal chandelier, triggering a massive smoke powder explosion that collapses the lair.

SWOJ escapes through the secret door as the lair detonates around them. They awaken protected by a magical shield generated by the unicorn pendant. Thaelin is found dead, crushed by rubble. They loot his body (magic boots, magic ring, potion of greater restoration) and place his corpse in the bag of holding. The crystal unicorn guides them forward through collapsed tunnels. They pass a Kuo-Toa shrine room and discover Flutterfoot Zipswiggle dead on a throne, his brain eaten by the mind flayer. They reach the mind flayer's spawning chamber containing a green pool from which an intellect devourer crawls.

The mind flayer has been controlling the Xanathar's body, which floats above firing eye rays. The Xanathar blasts Xae with a death ray (27 necrotic damage) and later disintegrates Lark with its disintegration beam -- Lark crumbles to dust, his hat drifting down onto the pile of ashes as his last words are simply 'For justice.' Scoot rages and tackles the mind flayer into the spawning pool. Ylka inspires Scoot with bardic music. Xae transforms into a panther and sprints to the unicorn Larian, who calls 'Gather around me.' The surviving party members touch the unicorn and are teleported in a swirl of rainbow light to the Short Rest Tavern (Trollskull Manor), landing at midnight with Lark's ashes and hat beside them.
