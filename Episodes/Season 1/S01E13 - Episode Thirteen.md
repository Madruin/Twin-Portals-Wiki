## Summary

SWOJ picks up the trail of the Stone of Galore after the chaotic break-in at Gralhund Villa, where they discovered a bloodbath between Zhentarim and Gralhund forces. The city watch arrives at the villa and begins questioning the group, but they are interrupted when Yalah Gralhund secretly sends a nimblewright fleeing with the Stone of Galore and a handwritten note. Gazer-like creatures (floating eyeballs) pursue the nimblewright through Trollskull Alley, and Terry arrives with the SWOJ-mobile to give chase. SWOJ finds the note is a map of the City of the Dead pointing to the Gralhund Crypt.

During the Feast of the Moon festival, the party travels to the City of the Dead and locates the Gralhund Crypt. Inside, they find the nimblewright destroyed and "Zilka" (Ylka's doppelganger double) impaled through the heart with a sword. Before dying, Zilka draws the symbol of Asmodeus and points upward. On the upper level, SWOJ discovers a pentagram, two cultists, and a pair of spined devils receiving the Stone of Galore. A rooftop chase ensues across the City of the Dead during the festival fireworks, with Sally the pigeon providing aerial reconnaissance.

The chase leads to Willaford Crowell, the Cassalanter family's majordomo, who receives the stone from a devil atop a building. SWOJ pursues in a frantic cart chase down the High Road through the Feast of the Moon parade. Lark goes semi-lycanthropic under the moonlight, Xae casts Faerie Fire on the enemy wagon, and the party battles a bearded devil guard. Scoot ultimately recovers the Stone of Galore and experiences a vision of a dolphin and a tentacle monster. The chase ends when Captain Houston Staggit and the city watch arrest the entire party. They are taken to the Docks Ward guardhouse, where Staggit privately warns them that the presiding magistrate for their morning trial has been bought by the Cassalanters.
