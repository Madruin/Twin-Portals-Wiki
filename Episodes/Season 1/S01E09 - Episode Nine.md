## Summary

SWOJ prepares for the Cassalanter party. JB Nevercott the haberdasher delivers a fancy hat lined with lead foil (blocks thought-reading) and three pistols loaded with true sight rounds that reveal doppelgangers. Terry now inhabits a one-eyed golden retriever corpse. At Cassalanter Villa, they're received by tiefling majordomo Willifort Crowelle. Scoot wins 160gp gambling Tymora's Fancy with Victoro Cassalanter. Amalia Cassalanter privately hires SWOJ to find Dagult Neverember's hoard of golden dragons (gold coins) for 150gp/day, mentioning their late son Osvaldo. Zardoz Zord appears selling nimblewright automatons. Lark's parents Grim and Aswel Wavesilver arrive -- Grim is cold and disappointed, Aswel is supportive. In the ballroom masked ball, Zilka (the Ylka doppelganger) crashes through the windows with cultists wearing golden grinning masks and red leather armor. The cultists attack nobles and paint blood patterns on the walls. A musician is revealed as a green-skinned changeling when hit by a true sight pistol round. SWOJ chases Zilka up the building exterior via grappling hooks. On the rooftop, moonlight triggers Lark's wererat transformation (from the Shard Shunner bites), but Zay's Ratman comic quotes help him resist. In the attic, they discover Osvaldo Cassalanter -- a nine-foot-tall chained demonic humanoid trapped in a pentagram circle. They fight Osvaldo, temporarily teaming up with the unconscious Zilka. Victoro and Amalia burst in to restrain their son. SWOJ captures the unconscious Zilka and her magical rapier with an infinity symbol.
