# S3E08 - DM Sessions

[[_Season 3|Season 3]] | Episode 8 | Overall #76

## Summary

Matias leads a roundtable discussion with fellow Dungeon Masters Bailey Fazio and Anders Holtstrom about the craft and experience of DMing. Each shares their background: Matias has DMed Twin Portals for several years and is currently deep in world-building for Chrono CCG; Bailey has been DMing since around 2012-2013 when Matias introduced her through Duluth theater circles, and is currently running an all-women wizard's tower dungeon crawl; Anders has been playing D&D for about 30 years and just finished his first-ever complete campaign, moving into a round-robin monster-of-the-week format.

They describe their DM styles in three words: Anders says 'stories reveal truths,' Bailey focuses on 'storytelling, comfortability, and fun,' and Matias emphasizes narrative and player agency. The conversation covers a wide range of DMing topics including handling player agency, session preparation methods, managing large tables (Anders ran six players), the challenge of monthly play schedules causing memory gaps, the value of round-robin DMing formats, and the Monster Squad-inspired monster-hunting guild concept.

They discuss practical advice for new DMs, the importance of failing forward, how theater and improv backgrounds inform their DMing, balancing story preparation with improvisation, and the emotional weight of running long campaigns. Anders shares the satisfaction of finally completing a multi-year campaign for the first time in 30 years of DMing.

### Key Events

- Three DMs share their philosophies and approaches to running games
- Anders celebrates completing his first full campaign after 30 years of DMing
- Discussion of round-robin and monster-of-the-week formats as solutions to scheduling
- Practical session prep advice from experienced DMs
- Theater background as common thread among all three DMs
- Conversation aimed at inspiring new DMs and growing the DM community
