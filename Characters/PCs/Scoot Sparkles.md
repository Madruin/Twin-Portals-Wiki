# Scoot Sparkles

**Player:** Scott Hebert
**Race:** Half-Orc
**Class:** Barbarian (Path of the Totem Warrior -- Dolphin)
**Background:** Sailor
**Also known as:** Scoots, Scoot
**Party:** [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]]

## Description

Scoot Sparkles was a hulking half-orc barbarian with a sailor's bearing and a heart far gentler than his imposing frame suggested. He lived at the [[The Splintered Stair]], a dockside establishment in the [[Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]], alongside his elderly orc mother [[Tagga]]. His father, [[Simon]], served in the [[Waterdeep Military]] and was lost at sea for years before eventually finding his way home. Scoot wore a signature durag early in the campaign, which he later traded to the kenku [[Pinker]] in exchange for Pinker's iconic pink hat -- a headpiece he wore proudly from that point forward. He was childhood friends with [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]], and the bond between the two anchored much of the party's emotional core. Scoot named the adventuring party the [[Silver Waves Of Justice]], or SWOJ, and served as its moral compass -- a boisterous, loyal, and fiercely protective warrior who would throw himself into any danger for his friends.

## History

Scoot's story began in the [[Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]], where he lived a modest life at the [[The Splintered Stair]] with his mother [[Tagga]]. When [[Volothamp Geddarm|Volo]] hired the party to rescue [[Floon Blagmaar]] from the [[Xanathar Guild]], Scoot threw himself into the adventure with characteristic enthusiasm, bashing down doors and charging headlong into the [[Zhentarim]] warehouse and the [[Xanathar Guild]] sewers beneath the city. He proved his worth early, winning 160 gold pieces gambling against [[Victoro Cassalanter]] and forging an unlikely alliance with the kenku gang leader [[Pinker]] by trading his durag for Pinker's pink hat. When the party acquired [[Trollskull Manor]], Scoot eagerly helped renovate the decrepit tavern, and it was he who coined the name [[Silver Waves Of Justice]] for their fledgling adventuring company.

As the party pursued the [[The Stone of Golorr]] through the streets of [[Waterdeep]], Scoot's barbarian fury proved indispensable. He recovered the Stone itself and experienced a powerful vision -- a dolphin swimming alongside a vast tentacle monster beneath the waves. At third level, he chose the Path of the Totem Warrior with the dolphin as his spirit animal, reflecting his deep connection to the sea and his sailor heritage. When the trail led the party into the [[Xanathar's Lair|Xanathar's underground lair]], Scoot faced one of his most harrowing moments: after [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] was disintegrated by the [[The Xanathar]]'s beholder eye rays, Scoot tackled the mind flayer [[Nihiloor]] into a spawning pool in a desperate act of grief-fueled rage. He survived the [[Xanathar's Lair]] collapse that killed [[Thaelin]] and emerged from the rubble forever changed.

The quest to recover Lark's soul took SWOJ to the [[The Feywild]] and ultimately to the realm of [[Malar]], the Beast Lord, where Scoot helped retrieve his best friend's soul -- trapped inside Lark's hat. After Lark's resurrection via clone spell at the [[Kolat Towers]], Scoot stood guard over his friend's recovery. When the party tracked down the [[Vault Of Dragons]] and the hoard of gold belonging to [[Lord Dagult Neverember|Neverember]], Scoot made a characteristically selfless choice: he shattered their [[Bag of Holding]] to scatter the gold across the city rather than let it fall into the wrong hands.

In Season 2, the party traveled north to the [[Sword Mountains]] region and the town of [[Amphail]], where they investigated the mysterious [[Kryptwood]] and its memory-altering mist. Scoot was devastated to discover his former shipmate [[Leah]] had been transformed into a six-eyed beast by [[Arcturia]]'s transmutation magic. When the party freed her with moth weed, Scoot also secured enough of the herb to cure his mother [[Tagga]]'s debilitating spore illness back home. Aboard the [[Concord Jewel]], SWOJ traveled to the [[Radiant Citadel]] in the Deep Ethereal Plane, where Scoot's axe was identified by cobalt blacksmiths at Benzo's Weapon Emporium as being forged from star metal.

After SWOJ was killed and resurrected in new bodies following their encounter with [[Rowan Crooks]] (a [[Manshoon]] clone posing as the legendary [[Jolly Gents]] leader), Scoot received a steampunk anchor pipe maul from [[Fioleth]] in the [[The Bag Dimension]] -- a weapon that became his signature armament for the remainder of the campaign. The party's journey through [[Driftwood]], [[Devil's' Rock]], and the [[Deep Dragon Temple]] tested Scoot's endurance, and he fell into an acid pool during the treasure room fight but was rescued by his companions.

The campaign's climactic arc took SWOJ to the [[Neverwinter Chasm]], [[Zhentil Keep]] in the [[Shadowfell]], and ultimately back to the [[Radiant Citadel]] for the final confrontation with [[Manshoon]]. Scoot's connection to the [[Raven Queen]] deepened throughout Season 2 -- [[Zelifarn]], the bronze dragon ally, took up residence in Scoot's necklace and communed with him. During the dream sequence orchestrated by the [[Raven Queen]] after defeating [[Rowan Crooks]], Scoot and the party fought shadow versions of themselves and were deemed worthy. Atop the [[Radiant Citadel]], Scoot communed with the [[Raven Queen]] during the battle against the two-headed dracolich and received divine intervention: a massive flock of spectral ravens descended, banishing the dracolich and destroying the top soul pylon. When [[Arcturia]] cast disintegrate on him during tense negotiations at the [[Wall Of The Faithless]], Scoot survived through sheer toughness. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] polymorphed him into a giant ape to fight [[Manshoon]] directly in the final battle, and Scoot destroyed the navigation console aboard the [[Concord Jewel]] to escape the [[The Bag Dimension]] and the approaching ancient red dragon.

After the campaign's conclusion, Scoot returned to [[Trollskull Manor]] in [[Waterdeep]], where he was reunited with both his mother [[Tagga]] and his long-lost father [[Simon]], who had finally returned from his odyssey at sea. [[Aurinax]], an elder gold dragon, removed the frog creature curse that had been festering in Scoot's wounds. At the God's Night celebration, Scoot burned [[Rowan Crooks]] trading cards in the fire and expressed his desire to stay grounded at [[Trollskull Manor]], learning his mother's mysterious shadow skills.

## Relationships

- **[[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]** -- Childhood best friend. Their bond was the emotional anchor of the party. Scoot was devastated when Lark was disintegrated by the [[The Xanathar]] and never wavered in the quest to recover his soul.
- **[[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]** -- Fellow SWOJ member and trusted companion. Ylka polymorphed Scoot into a giant ape during the final battle against [[Manshoon]], demonstrating their deep tactical trust.
- **[[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]** -- Fellow SWOJ member. Scoot supported Xae through the revelations about her sister [[Arcturia]] and her connection to [[Halaster Blackcloak]].
- **[[Tagga]]** -- His elderly orc mother, who ran the [[The Splintered Stair]] in the [[Dock Ward]]. Scoot was deeply protective of her, especially when she fell ill with spore sickness. He traveled across the [[Sword Mountains]] to find moth weed to cure her.
- **[[Simon]]** -- His father, a [[Waterdeep Military]] sailor lost at sea for years. Simon's eventual return home was one of the campaign's most emotionally satisfying reunions.
- **[[Zelifarn]]** -- A bronze dragon ally who took up residence in Scoot's necklace. Their bond was spiritual and deeply personal, with Zelifarn communing with Scoot throughout Season 2.
- **[[Raven Queen]]** -- A deity of death and fate who tested SWOJ in a dream sequence and later granted Scoot divine intervention against the two-headed dracolich. Scoot served as her mortal conduit.
- **[[Pinker]]** -- The kenku gang leader from the [[Dock Ward]]. Scoot earned Pinker's loyalty by trading his durag for Pinker's pink hat, cementing an unlikely alliance.
- **[[Thaelin]]** -- A guest companion who died in the [[Xanathar's Lair]] collapse. Scoot carried the weight of Thaelin's sacrifice.
- **[[Mr. E]]** -- A drow ally who joined SWOJ on multiple adventures. Scoot respected Mr. E's combat prowess and dedication to the [[Way Of The Balanced Scales]].
- **[[Leah]]** -- Scoot's former shipmate, transformed by [[Arcturia]]'s magic and later freed by SWOJ with moth weed.
