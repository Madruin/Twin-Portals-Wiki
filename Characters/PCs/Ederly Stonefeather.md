# Ederly Stonefeather

**Player:** Bailey Fazio

**Class:** Wizard
**Campaign:** Icewind Dale (Season 3)
**Also known as:** Ederly
**Party:** Icewind Dale Party

## Description

A three-foot-tall burrowing owl person (Owlin) wizard who uses a quill as a spellbook. Bailey's PC for the Icewind Dale campaign launched in the Season 3 live shows. Ederly uses chromatic orb and magic missile in combat. During the tarrasque encounter, Ederly discovered a draconic shrine inscribed with "thank you for guarding the world's heart."
