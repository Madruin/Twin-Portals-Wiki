## Description

A human warlock interested in archaeology and theater, whose patron is the Luxon. Anders's PC for the Icewind Dale campaign launched in the Season 3 live shows. Mormo uses scorching ray and enhance ability in combat, contributing critical fire damage during the tarrasque encounter.
