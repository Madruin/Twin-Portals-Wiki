# Thaelin

**Player:** John Brofie
**Race:** Tiefling
**Class:** Warlock
**Status:** Deceased (killed in the [[Xanathar's Lair]] collapse; later appeared as a ghost named Thail)
**Also known as:** Thail (ghost form)
**Party:** [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]]

## Description

Thaelin was a tiefling warlock and the brother of [[Squidly]], a tiefling ally of SWOJ. He was a guest player character portrayed by John Brofie who joined the party for the climactic assault on the [[Xanathar's Lair]] beneath [[Waterdeep]]. Thaelin had formerly worked for the [[Xanathar Guild]] in [[Skullport]], giving him intimate knowledge of the lair's layout, secret passages, and defenses -- knowledge that proved essential to the heist's early success. He was a spellcaster who wielded Suggestion, Disguise Self, Phantasmal Force, and Flame Bolt, combining cunning deception with destructive firepower. His death during the lair collapse was one of the campaign's first major losses, and his sacrifice cast a long shadow over the party. In Season 2, Thaelin returned as a ghost called Thail, haunting the edges of the story and offering his spectral aid to the companions who had survived.

## History

Thaelin entered the campaign during the planning phase of SWOJ's most ambitious operation: the infiltration of the [[Xanathar's Lair]]. As [[Squidly]]'s brother and a former [[Xanathar Guild]] operative in [[Skullport]], Thaelin brought invaluable insider knowledge to the table. Before the heist, he visited [[Skullport]] to purchase supplies -- one potion of restoration and three potions of greater healing for 600 gold pieces -- demonstrating both his resourcefulness and his familiarity with the underground city's black market.

Inside the lair, Thaelin guided SWOJ through secret passages that bypassed the most heavily defended areas. His finest moment came when he cast Suggestion on [[Ott Steeltoes]], the dwarf fish keeper tending to the [[The Xanathar]]'s prized goldfish [[Sylgar]], compelling Ott to retrieve the Eye of the [[The Stone of Golorr]] -- a bright red gemstone locked in a chest in the [[The Xanathar]]'s private sanctum. The gambit worked, and the gem was secured before the beholder crime lord discovered the intrusion.

When the [[The Xanathar]] arrived in its sanctum, the situation deteriorated rapidly. The beholder's death ray struck Thaelin, dealing a devastating 49 points of necrotic damage that nearly killed him outright. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]], in weasel form, managed to pour a healing potion into Thaelin's mouth, reviving him. Thaelin escaped using Phantasmal Force and Disguise Self, demonstrating remarkable composure under fire.

The final chapter of Thaelin's life unfolded in the [[Xanathar's Lair]] arena during the party's second incursion. Something had gone terribly wrong: Thaelin appeared puppet-controlled, his actions no longer his own. In this compromised state, he handed [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] the red Eye gem -- a crucial act that ensured the party would retain the artifact -- and then triggered a catastrophic explosion by firing Flame Bolt into a crystal chandelier connected to smoke powder charges rigged throughout the lair. [[Jarlaxle]] had warned the party about the explosives. The resulting blast collapsed the lair. [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] was disintegrated by the [[The Xanathar]]'s eye ray moments before, and Thaelin was found dead under the rubble, crushed by the falling structure he had brought down.

The party recovered Thaelin's body from the wreckage, along with his [[Boots of Striding and Springing]] (later worn by [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]), his magic ring, and a potion. His body was placed in the party's [[Bag of Holding]] -- a solemn act that underscored the weight of his loss.

In Season 2, Thaelin's story continued beyond death. His ghost, now called Thail, appeared to the party during their early Season 2 adventures, able to possess living bodies and offer fragmentary guidance. Thail found [[Squidly]]'s wish stone on the Beast of Erua and said goodbye to his brother in an emotional reunion. He possessed the ability to cast fireball from a wish stone crystal, lending his spectral firepower to Season 2 battles. At one point, green lightning from the Dynamo absorbed the ghosts of Thail and [[Teriador]] the Rose Knight. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] carried Thail's soul crystal through the transition out of the Dynamo, preserving his spirit even as the physical world shifted around them.

## Relationships

- **[[Squidly]]** -- Thaelin's brother, a tiefling who became a loyal ally of SWOJ. Their bond transcended death: Thail's ghost found Squidly's wish stone and said a final goodbye. Squidly later joined SWOJ's crew for the final assault on the [[Radiant Citadel]], carrying his brother's memory forward.
- **[[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]** -- Fellow combatant during the [[Xanathar's Lair]] heist. Scoot inherited Thaelin's [[Boots of Striding and Springing]] after his death.
- **[[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]** -- Xae saved Thaelin's life during the heist by pouring a healing potion into his mouth while in weasel form. Thaelin repaid the debt by handing her the critical Eye gem moments before his death.
- **[[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]** -- Ylka carried Thail's soul crystal through dangerous transitions, preserving his spirit beyond death.
- **[[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]** -- Both Lark and Thaelin fell during the [[Xanathar's Lair]] battle, though Lark was later resurrected while Thaelin remained dead.
- **[[Xanathar Guild]]** -- Thaelin's former employer. His insider knowledge of the Guild's operations in [[Skullport]] made the lair infiltration possible.
- **[[Ott Steeltoes]]** -- The dwarf fish keeper whom Thaelin manipulated with Suggestion to retrieve the Eye gem. An unwitting pawn in the heist's most crucial moment.
- **[[Teriador]]** -- The Rose Knight, whose ghost was absorbed alongside Thail's by the Dynamo's green lightning in Season 2.
