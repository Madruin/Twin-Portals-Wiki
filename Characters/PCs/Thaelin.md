# Thaelin

**Player:** John Brofie (Beth's husband)
**Race:** Tiefling
**Class:** Warlock
**Status:** Deceased (killed in the [[Xanathar's Lair]] collapse; later appeared as a ghost named Thail)
**Also known as:** Thail (ghost form), "Fail like quail with a TH"
**Party:** [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]]

## Description

Thaelin was a tiefling warlock, [[Squidly]]'s older brother, and a guest player character portrayed by John Brofie -- the only non-performer among the show's guest players. He was average height or slightly under, with a reddish-tinted complexion similar to [[Squidly]]'s, two shortish horns, a tail that lashed beneath his cloak, and faint scaling around the edges of his face.

Thaelin had formerly worked for the [[Xanathar Guild]] in [[Skullport]], but left because, as he put it, "it's been all of a sudden not, not quite what I thought I was going to be doing." Crucially, [[The Xanathar]] did not yet know he had gone -- a fact that gave SWOJ a covert operative who could still pass as Guild. His intimate knowledge of the lair's layout, secret passages, and defenses proved essential to the heist's early success.

He was a spellcaster who wielded Suggestion, Disguise Self, Phantasmal Force, and Flame Bolt, combining cunning deception with destructive firepower. His death during the lair collapse was one of the campaign's first major losses, and his sacrifice cast a long shadow over the party. In Season 2, Thaelin returned as a ghost called Thail, haunting the edges of the story and offering his spectral aid to the companions who had survived.

## History

Thaelin entered the campaign during the planning phase of SWOJ's most ambitious operation: the infiltration of the [[Xanathar's Lair]]. [[Squidly]] introduced him at [[Trollskull Manor]], calling him "the best big brother" and vouching for him with a story about the time Thaelin pulled him out of the path of a real troll during [[Waterdeep]]'s Troll Tide festival. Thaelin offered to help repay what SWOJ had done for his brother, and his insider knowledge of the [[Xanathar Guild]]'s operations in [[Skullport]] made him an invaluable addition. Before the heist, he visited [[Skullport]] to purchase supplies -- one potion of restoration and three potions of greater healing for 600 gold pieces -- demonstrating both his resourcefulness and his familiarity with the underground city's black market.

[[Remallia Haventree]] led the party through the sewers to a hidden entrance. Inside the lair, Thaelin guided SWOJ through the Hall of Eyes -- evading beholder eye-stalk security cameras -- and through secret passages that bypassed the most heavily defended areas, including a secret rotating door that let them skip the audience chamber entirely. His finest moment came when he cast Suggestion on [[Ott Steeltoes]], the dwarf fish keeper tending to the [[The Xanathar]]'s prized goldfish [[Sylgar]], compelling Ott to retrieve the Eye of the [[The Stone of Golorr]] -- a bright red gemstone locked in a chest in the [[The Xanathar]]'s private sanctum. The gambit worked, and the gem was secured before the beholder crime lord discovered the intrusion.

When the [[The Xanathar]] arrived in its sanctum, the situation deteriorated rapidly. The beholder's death ray struck Thaelin, dealing a devastating 49 points of necrotic damage that nearly killed him outright. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]], in weasel form, managed to pour a healing potion into Thaelin's mouth, reviving him. Thaelin escaped using Phantasmal Force and Disguise Self, demonstrating remarkable composure under fire.

The final chapter of Thaelin's life unfolded in the [[Xanathar's Lair]] arena during the party's second incursion. Something had gone terribly wrong: Thaelin appeared puppet-controlled, his actions no longer his own. In this compromised state, he handed [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] the red Eye gem -- a crucial act that ensured the party would retain the artifact -- and then triggered a catastrophic explosion by firing Flame Bolt into a crystal chandelier connected to smoke powder charges rigged throughout the lair. [[Jarlaxle|JB Nevercott]] -- secretly [[Jarlaxle]] Baenre of [[Bregan D'aerthe]] -- had telepathically warned the party that the lair was rigged with smoke powder and pointed them to a secret door on the south wall marked with a flaming eye symbol. The resulting blast collapsed the lair. [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] was disintegrated by the [[The Xanathar]]'s eye ray moments before, and Thaelin was found dead under the rubble, crushed by the falling structure he had brought down.

The party recovered Thaelin's body from the wreckage, along with his [[Boots of Striding and Springing]] (later worn by [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]), his magic ring, and a potion of greater restoration. His body was placed in the party's [[Bag of Holding]] -- a solemn act that underscored the weight of his loss.

In Season 2, Thaelin's story continued beyond death. His ghost, now called Thail, first appeared to the party in the rain-soaked borderlands between the [[Feywild]] and the [[Shadowfell]], shepherding SWOJ to safety alongside the [[Brighanox]] after a [[Strigoi]] attack. Thail demonstrated his ability to possess living bodies by entering [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]], and later possessed [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] to cast major image, creating fog cover for the party's scarab-back approach to the [[Beast of Erua]].

Through Thail, SWOJ learned that [[Squidly]]'s wish stone -- a crystallized unfulfilled wish reading "I wish my brother came home" -- had been stolen by the [[Strigoi]] and embedded in the [[Beast of Erua]]'s scales. Atop the beast, [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] touched the glowing red stone and Thail entered it, reaching out to [[Squidly]] in a vision at the [[Trollskull Manor|Trollskull]] tavern, touching his brother's shoulder in a moment of peace before saying goodbye. [[Squidly]] later abandoned the [[Trollskull Manor]] crew altogether, devastated by the loss.

Inside the [[Beast of Erua]]'s ribcage, Thail cast fireball from his wish stone crystal during a battle against podlings, crawling hand swarms, and an animated knight, and scouted the staircase leading up through the beast's trachea. When the party reached the skull chamber and the malfunctioning [[Dynamo of Possibility]], green lightning erupted from the device and absorbed the ghosts of Thail and [[Teriador]] the Rose Knight. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] carried Thail's soul crystal through the transition out of the Dynamo, preserving his spirit even as the physical world shifted around them.

## Relationships

- **[[Squidly]]** -- Thaelin's younger brother, a tiefling who became a loyal ally of SWOJ. Squidly introduced Thaelin at [[Trollskull Manor]], calling him "the best big brother." Their bond transcended death: Thail's ghost found Squidly's wish stone -- "I wish my brother came home" -- and entered it to touch his brother's shoulder one last time before saying goodbye. Squidly abandoned the [[Trollskull Manor]] crew after Thaelin's death, devastated by the loss. He later rejoined SWOJ for the final assault on the [[Radiant Citadel]], carrying his brother's memory forward.
- **[[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]** -- Fellow combatant during the [[Xanathar's Lair]] heist. Scoot inherited Thaelin's [[Boots of Striding and Springing]] after his death. Scoot received a pistol from an agent of [[Fel'rekt Lafeen]] before the heist as part of a side mission to find [[Nar'l Xibrindas]] inside the lair.
- **[[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]** -- Xae saved Thaelin's life during the heist by finding him unconscious and pouring a healing potion into his mouth while in weasel form. Thaelin repaid the debt by handing her the critical Eye gem moments before his death.
- **[[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]** -- In Season 2, Thail possessed Ylka to cast major image for fog cover during the approach to the [[Beast of Erua]]. Ylka later carried Thail's soul crystal through the [[Dynamo of Possibility]] transition, preserving his spirit.
- **[[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]** -- Both Lark and Thaelin fell during the [[Xanathar's Lair]] battle, though Lark was later resurrected while Thaelin remained dead. In Season 2, Thail possessed Lark's body first among the party members, demonstrating his ability to enter living hosts.
- **[[Xanathar Guild]]** -- Thaelin's former employer. He left because things changed under [[The Xanathar]], but the beholder did not yet know he had gone -- giving SWOJ a covert insider advantage.
- **[[Ott Steeltoes]]** -- The dwarf fish keeper tending to [[The Xanathar]]'s prized goldfish [[Sylgar]]. Thaelin manipulated him with Suggestion to retrieve the Eye gem from a locked chest. An unwitting pawn in the heist's most crucial moment.
- **[[Jarlaxle]]** -- As [[Jarlaxle|JB Nevercott]], he sat in the arena audience and telepathically warned the party that the lair was rigged with smoke powder -- information Thaelin ultimately acted on when he fired Flame Bolt into the chandelier.
- **[[Flutterfoot Zipswiggle]]** -- A gnome jester enslaved by [[The Xanathar]] whom the party encountered during the infiltration. Flutterfoot gave them dust of disappearance and was later found dead on a throne, his brain eaten by a mind flayer.
- **[[Teriador]]** -- The Rose Knight, whose ghost was absorbed alongside Thail's by the [[Dynamo of Possibility]]'s green lightning in Season 2.
- **[[Remallia Haventree]]** -- Led SWOJ through the sewers to the hidden entrance of the [[Xanathar's Lair]].
