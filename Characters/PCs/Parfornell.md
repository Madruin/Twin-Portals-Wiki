# Parfornell

**Player:** Beth Brophy
**Race:** Kenku
**Class:** Cleric
**Campaign:** Icewind Dale (Season 3)
**Party:** Icewind Dale Party

## Description

A Kenku cleric who can only speak by mimicking phrases she has heard, a core trait of the Kenku race. Beth's second PC after [[Ylka Gralhund]], created for the new Icewind Dale campaign. Parfornell uses sacred flame and guidance in combat, and carries cat-sized beetle companions called Cat Beetles on her shoulders.
