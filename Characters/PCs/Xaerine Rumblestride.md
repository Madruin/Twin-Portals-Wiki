# Xaerine Rumblestride

**Player:** Kendra
**Race:** Lightfoot Halfling
**Class:** Druid
**Also known as:** Xae, Zay
**Party:** [[Silver Waves of Justice|SWOJ]]

## Description

Xaerine Rumblestride was a lightfoot halfling druid who arrived in [[Waterdeep]] through a mysterious portal into the [[City of the Dead]] with no memory of her past. She carried a crystal unicorn pendant containing a spirit named [[Larian]], which served as both a spiritual anchor and a conduit to powers she did not fully understand. Small in stature but vast in her connection to the natural world, Xae was a prolific wild shaper who took the forms of wolves, spiders, weasels, panthers, golden retrievers, velociraptors, crag cats, giant centipedes, bats, reef sharks, and countless other creatures throughout the campaign. She wore an [[Avian Circlet]] that allowed her to see through the eyes of crows, and she had a deep affinity for the [[Emerald Enclave]]. Her connection to the ancient wizard [[Halaster Blackcloak]] and her sister [[Arcturia]] placed her at the center of the campaign's deepest mysteries -- a halfling druid carrying the weight of an Asmodean contract and the fate of six imprisoned siblings on her shoulders.

## History

Xae's arrival in [[Waterdeep]] was itself a mystery. She stepped through a portal into the [[City of the Dead]] with no memory of who she was or where she had come from. The crystal unicorn pendant around her neck contained the spirit [[Larian]], who offered fragmentary guidance. She joined [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]], [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]], and [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] early in the campaign, bringing her druidic magic to bear against the [[Xanathar Guild]] and the [[Zhentarim]] as the party searched for [[Floon Blagmaar]] and later the [[Stone of Golorr]]. Her wild shape abilities made her an invaluable scout -- she could slip through cracks as a spider, reconnoiter as a bat, or fight as a panther when the situation demanded it.

A prehistoric vision provided the first clue to Xae's true nature: she saw herself pulling a tentacle beast from a river and crushing it into what would become the [[Stone of Golorr]]. This vision suggested a connection to the deepest history of [[Waterdeep]] and the ancient conflict between dragons and aboleths. Her visits to the [[Emerald Enclave]] to learn Pass Without Trace and her growing command of nature magic reflected a power that was rooted in something far older than any druidic circle.

The revelation of Xae's family connections reshaped the campaign. [[Arcturia]], a shape-changing witch who served [[Halaster Blackcloak]] as "the most powerful wizard in the multiverse," was revealed to be Xae's sister. Xae carried a locket containing a portrait of [[Halaster Blackcloak|Halaster]] himself, hinting at a familial connection to the mad wizard of [[Undermountain]]. Most dramatically, Xae signed a contract with [[Asmodeus]] -- the Lord of the Nine Hells -- to free six "brothers and sisters": [[Jhesiyra]], [[Rantantar]], [[Mayrin]], [[Trobriand]], [[Muiral]], and [[Arcturia]]. The contract gave her thirteen Founders Days to complete the task, binding her fate to a ticking cosmic clock.

In Season 2, Xae's quest to understand her past drove much of the narrative. When SWOJ traveled to the [[Sword Mountains]] and the town of [[Amphale]], Xae followed [[Arcturia|Arturia]] into the [[Spiral Cavern]], hearing her sister's telepathic message: "Follow me. Break the wall." Aboard the [[Concord Jewel]], Xae pursued Arcturia into the crystalline vessel before it warped SWOJ across time and space. At the [[Radiant Citadel]], Xae asked [[Vincent Trench]] to investigate her siblings, seeking answers about the six beings she was bound to free.

After the party's death and resurrection in new bodies at the hands of [[Rowan Crooks]], Xae received matching red-stained hand crossbows from [[Phylith]] in the [[Bag Dimension]]. She adapted quickly to combat situations, using her druidic magic in increasingly creative ways: commanding twenty baby otyughs as a stampeding distraction during the infiltration of [[Castle Nehri Tar]], scouting in bat form through the dragon keep (triggering a sonic screech trap and acid mist), and transforming into a reef shark to haul giant prawns during the Battle Prawns competition at [[Sid Squid]]'s.

Xae's magical abilities proved decisive in the campaign's climactic moments. She cast Daylight on the dracolich phylactery in the [[Sunken City]], conjured eight woodpeckers to shatter soul crystals in specters' chests at the [[Wall of the Faithless]], and summoned a water elemental to wash away guards atop the [[Radiant Citadel]]. Most critically, she reactivated the [[Concord Jewel]]s to send displaced civilizations back to their home planes and activated the protective ward over the [[Radiant Citadel]] as it plunged into the [[Keening Gloam]]. When Xae used Dimension Door alongside [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] to teleport past traps aboard the [[Concord Jewel]], and cast Moonbeam, Faerie Fire, and Balm of the Summer Court during the frog creature battle, her versatility was on full display.

The encounter with [[Arcturia]] at the [[Wall of the Faithless]] brought Xae's family arc toward resolution. [[Arcturia]], who had been working for [[Halaster Blackcloak]], was initially hostile but was eventually persuaded to join SWOJ's cause. Arcturia led the party to [[Kelemvor]]'s skeleton and performed the ritual that raised an army of ancient souls from the Wall. Xae's dream at the season's end -- "one sibling freed, five more and nine years remain" -- confirmed that the Asmodean contract's clock was still ticking. She heard a mysterious voice from [[Undermountain]] at the [[Yawning Portal]] saying "come home," and she threw a [[Feywild]] mushroom into the God's Night fire, resolving to seek her remaining siblings in [[Undermountain]].

## Relationships

- **[[Arcturia]]** -- Xae's sister, a transmutation wizard and shapeshifter who served [[Halaster Blackcloak]]. Their relationship was complex: Arcturia initially fled from Xae, then attacked SWOJ, and finally joined their cause at the [[Wall of the Faithless]]. Arcturia was one of the six siblings Xae was bound by contract to free.
- **[[Halaster Blackcloak]]** -- The mad wizard of [[Undermountain]], to whom Xae had a mysterious familial connection. She carried a locket with his portrait, and his influence shaped her destiny.
- **[[Asmodeus]]** -- The Lord of the Nine Hells, with whom Xae signed a binding contract to free her six siblings. The contract's thirteen-Founders-Day deadline drove much of Xae's urgency.
- **[[Larian]]** -- The spirit within Xae's crystal unicorn pendant, who provided guidance and served as a spiritual companion throughout the campaign.
- **[[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]** -- Fellow SWOJ member. Xae and Scoot shared a deep mutual loyalty, and Scoot supported her through every revelation about her mysterious past.
- **[[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]** -- Fellow SWOJ member. Ylka and Xae worked together seamlessly in combat, combining Dimension Door, polymorphs, and Moonbeam in coordinated tactics.
- **[[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]** -- Fellow SWOJ member. Lark's noble pragmatism complemented Xae's druidic instincts.
- **[[Emerald Enclave]]** -- The nature-focused faction with which Xae had strong ties. She visited them to learn spells and maintained connections to their druidic circles.
- **[[Vincent Trench]]** -- The private detective Xae hired to investigate her siblings at the [[Radiant Citadel]].
- **[[Jhesiyra]], [[Rantantar]], [[Mayrin]], [[Trobriand]], [[Muiral]]** -- Five of Xae's six siblings named in the Asmodean contract, still imprisoned at the campaign's end. Their fates remained unresolved, driving Xae toward [[Undermountain]].
- **[[Shaleheart]]** -- A friendly bulette ally whom Xae gifted lunaria flowers. Shaleheart and her children joined the final assault on the [[Spiral Cavern]].
