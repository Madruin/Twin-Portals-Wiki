# Mr. E

**Player:** Robert Lee
**Race:** Drow
**Class:** Rogue/Monk
**Also known as:** Mystery, Mr E
**Party:** [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]]

## Description

Mr. E was a drow rogue and monk who operated as an agent of [[Bregan D'Aerthe]], the infamous drow mercenary company led by [[Jarlaxle]]. Cool, capable, and sardonic, Mr. E was an expert lockpicker and self-described "home wrecker" -- his euphemism for burglar. He fought with flaming fists and dragon breath attacks, blending monastic discipline with roguish cunning. His sensei, [[Mr. Ziyagi]], was a figure Mr. E's player invented as a joke that became integral to the story. After Mr. Ziyagi's death, Mr. E returned to the party carrying a [[Mr. E]]. He wielded distinctive equipment including a "ring of fingering" (a ring that cast mage hand), a tooth communication device for whispering to allies at range, and a belt buckle that became his trademark. Over the course of the campaign, Mr. E evolved from a [[Bregan D'Aerthe]] operative following orders into a key ally of SWOJ and ultimately the dragon administrator of [[Waterdeep]], wielding the [[Dragon Staff of Ahghairon]] and coordinating with the [[Way Of The Balanced Scales]].

## History

Mr. E was introduced to SWOJ by [[Jarlaxle]] (operating under his human alias [[Zardoz Zord]]) during Season 1. His first mission with the party was infiltrating the [[Gralhund Villa]], where he demonstrated his exceptional skills by stealing a silver key from [[Yalah Gralhund]]'s neck and a locked book from the Gralhund library, guarded by an imp disguised as a tarantula. He escaped through a window with both prizes, establishing himself as the party's go-to infiltrator for high-stakes heists.

Mr. E's connection to dragons set him apart from the other party members. He traded a "dragon piss" elixir -- an alchemical compound brewed with the help of [[Fela Lefaliir]], the elven botanist on [[Trollskull Alley]] -- to [[Aurinax]], an adult gold dragon disguised as a dwarf who guarded the [[Vault Of Dragons]]. In exchange, Aurinax gave him a giant ruby the size of an ostrich egg. This transaction was not merely mercantile: it established a relationship of trust between Mr. E and the dragon community that would pay enormous dividends later. When [[Aurinax]] offered the [[Dragon Staff of Ahghairon]] -- a powerful artifact that allowed dragons to bypass the dragon ward protecting [[Waterdeep]] -- Mr. E accepted the responsibility. He took up residence in [[Castle Waterdeep]], serving as the city's dragon administrator and vetting incoming dragons with dragon piss-enhanced vision elixirs.

During the [[Founders Day]] battle at the [[Cassalanter Villa]], Mr. E fought alongside SWOJ against [[Victoro Cassalanter]] and his forces. In one of the campaign's most memorable combat moments, Mr. E, [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]], and [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] kicked the unconscious Victoro through his own portal into the temple of [[Asmodeus]]. Mr. E's flaming fists and dragon breath attacks proved devastating against the cultists.

In Season 2, Mr. E departed on his own missions but returned at critical junctures. After his sensei [[Mr. Ziyagi]] died, Mr. E came back carrying a [[Mr. E]] that [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] later studied, learning crucial information about [[Zylka]]'s origins. He accompanied SWOJ to [[Driftwood]], the floating pirate citadel, and then to [[Devil's' Rock]], where he helped navigate skeleton-infested beaches and trapped tunnels in search of the [[Deep Dragon Temple]]. A dragon known as [[Squidley]] -- who had saved Mr. E from a shipwreck years earlier -- resided in the temple and had fallen into an unconscious dream state, communicating through visions and urging Mr. E to "get help, find the temple."

Mr. E's most significant Season 2 contribution came during the [[Neverwinter]] arc. He arrived dramatically at [[Castle Neverwinter]] with [[Melifarn]] the bronze dragon and a flight of four other dragons -- [[Jack]] (silver), [[Jill]] (white), [[Chip]], and [[Dale]] -- representing the [[Way Of The Balanced Scales]]. He participated in the war council and flew to [[Amphail]] on dragonback with SWOJ to infiltrate the [[Spiral Cavern]], where [[Manshoon]]'s forces were massing. Inside the cavern, Mr. E used his ring of mage hand to communicate with the captured dragons [[Chip]] and [[Dale]], promising to return with reinforcements. When the [[Shadar-Kai]] -- elven natives of the [[Shadowfell]] who served the [[Raven Queen]] -- briefed SWOJ on the dark sun situation, Mr. E departed with them on a separate mission, his path diverging from the main party's journey to [[Zhentil Keep]].

## Relationships

- **[[Jarlaxle]] / [[Zardoz Zord]]** -- Mr. E's employer and the leader of [[Bregan D'Aerthe]]. Jarlaxle introduced Mr. E to SWOJ and sent him on missions that aligned with Bregan D'aerthe's interests. Their relationship was professional but marked by mutual respect.
- **[[Bregan D'Aerthe]]** -- The drow mercenary organization Mr. E served. His actions often advanced their intelligence-gathering goals while simultaneously helping SWOJ.
- **[[Aurinax]]** -- The adult gold dragon who guarded the [[Vault Of Dragons]]. Mr. E earned Aurinax's trust through the dragon piss trade and was entrusted with the [[Dragon Staff of Ahghairon]].
- **[[Mr. Ziyagi]]** -- Mr. E's sensei, whose death prompted Mr. E's return to the party with the [[Mr. E]]. A character Robert Lee invented as a joke that became an important narrative thread.
- **[[Squidley]]** -- A dragon in the [[Deep Dragon Temple]] who saved Mr. E from a shipwreck years before the campaign. Their bond drew Mr. E to [[Devil's' Rock]] and the temple.
- **[[Melifarn]]** -- An ancient bronze dragon and key ally. Mr. E arrived with Melifarn and the dragon flight at [[Neverwinter]], coordinating aerial operations for the [[Way Of The Balanced Scales]].
- **[[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]** -- Fellow combatant and trusted ally. Scoot and Mr. E fought side by side from the [[Gralhund Villa]] to the [[Spiral Cavern]].
- **[[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]** -- Mr. E and Lark shared a roguish kinship, working together on infiltrations and once disguising themselves as two people stacked in an eight-foot trench coat.
- **[[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]** -- Ylka studied Mr. E's [[Mr. E]], gaining crucial information about [[Zylka]]'s origins.
- **[[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]** -- Fellow SWOJ member with whom Mr. E shared multiple adventures.
- **[[Fela Lefaliir]]** -- The elven botanist who helped Mr. E establish a supply chain for dragon piss elixirs.
- **[[Way Of The Balanced Scales]]** -- The dragon-allied faction Mr. E represented in the late campaign. Their motto was "keep the balance."
