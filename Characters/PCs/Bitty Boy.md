# Bitty Boy

**Player:** Scott Hebert
**Race:** Human
**Class:** Barbarian
**Campaign:** Icewind Dale (Season 3)
**Also known as:** Bitty
**Party:** Icewind Dale Party

## Description

A muscular human barbarian who pulls a colorful wagon. Scott's second PC after [[Scoot Sparkles]], created for the new Icewind Dale campaign launched in the Season 3 live shows. Scott later transitioned to playing [[Rex]], a human bard warlock.
