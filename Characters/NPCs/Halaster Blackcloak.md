# Halaster Blackcloak

Halaster Blackcloak is the legendary co-founder of [[Waterdeep]] and an immensely powerful wizard who descended into [[Undermountain]] centuries ago. Though never encountered in person by [[SWOJ]], his influence pervades the campaign -- his apprentices are bound to [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]'s contract with [[Asmodeus]], and his agent [[Arcturia]] drives pivotal events across both seasons. [[Arcturia]] calls him "the most powerful wizard in the multiverse."

**First mentioned:** [[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]]
**Total appearances:** 6 episodes (referenced but never physically present)

---

## Description

Halaster worked alongside Ahghairon in the ancient days to found the city of [[Waterdeep]]. While the city was built upwards, Halaster descended downwards into the old Dwarven ruins below, forming his own wizardly lair in what became known as [[Undermountain]]. He was rarely seen after that. Rumors hold that he either left this world or went insane; there is no clear account of what happened to him after the city's founding, only that his magic was essential to its creation and that he once had a great wizard's tower.

His portrait appears in a locket that [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] found at the [[Old Xoblob Shop]] -- an old man with a quizzical look, a glimmer of piercing intellect, and wild eyes. The same face appears on the central statue in the [[The God Catcher|God Catcher]] chamber, flanked by figures in similar robes of different heights and ancestries, with a small placard reading his name.

Halaster commands six apprentices -- [[Jhesiyra]], [[Rantantar]], [[Lyrian]], [[Trobriand]], [[Muiral]], and [[Arcturia]] -- legendary wizards who were trapped or scattered across the planes. These are the six "brothers and sisters" that Xae's contract with [[Asmodeus]] requires her to free within thirteen Founder's Days.

## Connections and Influence

### The Apprentices and Xae's Contract

Xae signed a contract with an avatar of [[Asmodeus]] during the Founder's Day feast ([[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]]) to save the [[Cassalanters]] children. The contract's terms require Xae to free Halaster's six apprentices or forfeit her own soul. This obligation drives much of the campaign's planar exploration and is one of its most personal unresolved threads.

### Arcturia -- "The Master's" Agent

[[Arcturia]], Xae's sister, is a transmutation wizard who serves Halaster. When SWOJ confronted her in the [[Spiral Cavern]] beneath the [[Sword Mountains]] ([[S02E07 - Episode Thirty Seven (S02E07)|S02E07]]), she declared that she serves "the master -- the most powerful wizard in the multiverse" and told Xae telepathically to "follow me -- break the wall." Arcturia stated she would "help the master remember," implying Halaster has lost or fragmented memories.

Later, at the [[Wall Of The Faithless]] ([[S02E35 - Episode Sixty Five (S02E35)|S02E35]]), Arcturia arrived on Halaster's orders to break the Wall. She told SWOJ that "Halaster asked me not to get Kelemvor involved" and confirmed he was far away with no chance of arriving. Xae tried to persuade Arcturia that "Halaster isn't doing what's best for us," and the party encouraged Arcturia to think back to memories before Halaster's influence. Arcturia was eventually persuaded to join SWOJ's cause.

### The Voice from Undermountain

In the Season 2 finale ([[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]), while visiting the [[Yawning Portal]], Xae alone heard a strange voice on the wind saying "come home" -- emanating from [[Undermountain]]. That night, meditating on the connection, Xae dreamed of a cool, calm voice saying: "One sibling freed. Five more and nine years remain." She then saw a billowing red cloak that morphed into a sheet of cloth with many, many eyes on it. Xae also recalled visiting "a memory somewhat of Halaster's lab" during earlier adventures.

### Halaster's Agents

Halaster operates through proxies. [[Arcturia]] is his primary known agent, operating as part of [[Halaster's Agents]]. The [[Concord Jewels]] -- crystalline vessels connecting the material plane to the [[Radiant Citadel]] -- are tied to his apprentices. [[Fetch]], the fragment of [[Manshoon]]'s soul inside the [[Dynamo Of Possibility]], previously worked for [[Rantantar]], another of the six apprentices.
