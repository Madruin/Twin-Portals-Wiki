# Eunissa

Eunissa is the owner of the [[Spouting Fish]], a tavern in the [[Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]] known for its fried fish, kippered herring, and pickled fare. She is the wife of [[Harold]], a former actor. When Harold and several other neighborhood residents vanished overnight, Eunissa was frantic with worry -- and it was [[SWOJ]] who investigated the disappearances and rescued the missing people from the sewers beneath her tavern.

**First appearance:** [[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

Eunissa is described as an older woman, likely middle-aged or elderly, who runs the [[Spouting Fish]] alongside a young half-orc cook. When [[SWOJ]] first met her, she was sitting in a back booth going through papers, sweating, rubbing her hair, and looking very frazzled -- visibly distraught over her husband's disappearance. Despite her distress, she was welcoming and grateful for help, recognizing [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] as [[Tagga]]'s boy and remarking on how big he had grown. She is a familiar presence in the neighborhood, known to the residents of [[Trollskull Alley]] and the surrounding [[Dock Ward]] blocks.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]:** [[Tagga]] tells the party that their neighbor Eunissa's husband [[Harold]] went missing during the night, along with many other people from the neighborhood. The whole area is in an uproar. [[SWOJ]] takes a Zuber ride with [[Terry]] to the [[Spouting Fish]] to investigate. Inside the tavern, [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] introduces himself and [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] to Eunissa, who is visibly distraught. She explains that Harold was sleeping beside her, and when she woke in the morning he was gone. She heard a noise in the night and assumed he was getting into the kippered herring again, but the back door to the alley had been unlocked -- something she is certain she had locked the night before. She gives the party permission to investigate the back of the tavern, where they discover a trap door leading to a cellar and the sewers beneath. In the sewers, SWOJ finds [[Xanathar Guild]] symbols on the walls and fights three grell -- floating brain-like creatures with paralyzing tentacles. With the help of [[The Black Viper]], they rescue approximately fifteen missing people, including Harold. After the rescue, Eunissa offers SWOJ free fish and chips for life in gratitude.

## Relationships

- **[[Harold]]** -- Eunissa's husband, a former actor and enthusiastic kippered herring enthusiast. He was among the residents kidnapped through the sewers beneath the [[Spouting Fish]] and held captive by grell associated with the [[Xanathar Guild]]. After his rescue, Harold gave SWOJ the [[Playwright's Dagger]] in gratitude.
- **[[SWOJ]]** -- Eunissa's neighbors from [[Trollskull Alley]]. She knew [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] through his mother [[Tagga]] and welcomed the party warmly when they came to investigate Harold's disappearance. She offered them free fish and chips for life after they rescued the missing people.
- **[[Tagga]]** -- [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]'s mother and Eunissa's neighbor. Tagga was the one who first told the party about the disappearances, mentioning that Eunissa's husband had been getting into the kippered herring lately.
