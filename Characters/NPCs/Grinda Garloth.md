# Grinda Garloth

An elderly woman living in a lighthouse at [[Mistshore]], owner of the [[Apparatus of Kwalish]] (a lobster-shaped submersible barrel). A member of the [[Watchful Order Of Magists And Protectors]], Grinda aids [[SWOJ]] by lending them her submersible to investigate the sunken [[Sea Plum]] and later shares lore about aboleths, including one named Golorr.

**First appearance:** [[S01E06 - Episode Six|S01E06]]
**Total appearances:** 3 episodes
**Faction affiliations:** [[Watchful Order Of Magists And Protectors]]

## Role in the Story


- **S01E06:** At the lighthouse, Grinda reveals Ylka apparently visited the previous night and borrowed the Apparatus of Kwalish -- but Ylka has no memory of this
- **S01E06:** Zardos gives SWOJ a handkerchief to deliver to Grinda at her lighthouse in [[Mistshore]]
- **S01E08:** SWOJ returns the Apparatus of Kwalish to Grinda, who shares lore about aboleths including one named Golorr
- **S02E05:** Grinda visits about portal instability and offers a teleportation circle
