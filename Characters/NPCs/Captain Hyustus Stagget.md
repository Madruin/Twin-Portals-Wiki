# Captain Hyustus Stagget

Captain of the [[City Watch]] in the [[Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]]. A dutiful, mustachioed officer who became a recurring ally of [[Silver Waves of Justice|SWOJ]] during Season 1, ultimately murdered by the [[Cassalanter Family  Cult Of Asmodeus|Cassalanter]] agent [[Willifort Crowell (Doppelganger)|Willifort Crowell]] for trying to protect the party from a corrupt trial.

**First appearance:** [[S01E02 - Episode Two|S01E02]]
**Final appearance:** [[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]] (found dead; impersonated by doppelganger)
**Total appearances:** 4 episodes
**Status:** Deceased -- murdered by [[Willifort Crowell (Doppelganger)|Willifort Crowell]]
**Faction affiliations:** [[City Watch]]

## Description

Captain Hyustus Stagget was the City Watch captain in charge of the [[Dock Ward]]. He was a solemn, conscientious officer who took his duties seriously. In the transcript he is described stroking his mustache thoughtfully during conversations. He was a familiar face to [[Renaer Neverember]], who addressed him casually by first name, and he maintained professional relationships with figures across Waterdeep's institutions, including the magisters. He mentioned [[Ulkoria Stonemarrow]] as "an old friend" when advising the party to visit the [[North Ward]].

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E02 - Episode Two|S01E02]]:** Captain Stagget arrives at the [[Zhentarim Warehouse]] on [[Candle Lane]] with approximately thirteen men in raid formation to investigate reports of violence. He introduces himself as being "in charge of the Dock Ward." He is accompanied by a magister -- a shorter, bald man in a dark gray robe who watches the party critically. Stagget recognizes [[Renaer Neverember]], who vouches for [[Silver Waves of Justice|SWOJ]], and allows the party to leave. He expresses regret that the Watch did not bust up the Zhentarim hideout sooner and asks Renaer to put in a good word with his father [[Dagult Neverember]].
- **[[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]]:** At the [[Spouting Fish]] after SWOJ's rescue of missing persons from the sewers, Stagget checks in with the party as the City Watch responds to the commotion. He advises them that none of their names are registered as magic users and recommends they report to the [[Watchful Order Of Magisters|Watchful Order of Magisters]] to follow the Code Legal. He mentions that [[Ulkoria Stonemarrow]] in the North Ward is an old friend and a powerful archmage who keeps tight watch over that district.
- **[[S01E13 - Episode Thirteen|S01E13]]:** After SWOJ's chaotic cart chase down the [[High Road]] during the Feast of the Moon parade, Stagget arrives on a griffin and arrests the entire party. He takes them to the [[Docks Ward Guardhouse]] and informs them their trial will be in the morning. That night, around four in the morning, Stagget sneaks back to their cell -- sweating, dirty, with bags under his eyes from lack of sleep -- and privately warns the party that the presiding magistrate has been bought off by the [[Cassalanter Family  Cult Of Asmodeus|Cassalanters]]. He asks what happened on the streets, questions whether the party was poisoned, and offers to speak with the prison warden about getting them out, though he refuses to help them break the law outright. He tells the party where their gear is being stored before departing.
- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** After [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] picks the jail cell lock overnight, the party explores the prison basement and discovers two dead city watchmen bludgeoned to death. A figure appearing to be Stagget arrives, but it is actually [[Willifort Crowell (Doppelganger)|Willifort Crowell]], the Cassalanter majordomo and doppelganger, who had murdered the real Stagget and assumed his identity. At the trial, the impostor is exposed when [[Laeral Silverhand]] asks him to read the Gralhund note aloud -- something the real Stagget had already done -- and the doppelganger cannot read it. The false Stagget bolts for a window, is caught by a grell and [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]], and is restrained with manacles. The real Captain Stagget's body was among the dead found in the prison basement.

## Relationships

- **[[Renaer Neverember]]** -- Familiar acquaintance. Renaer addressed Stagget by his first name and Stagget deferred to Renaer's social standing, letting SWOJ go on his word.
- **[[Silver Waves of Justice|SWOJ]]** -- Developed into a quiet ally over the course of Season 1. Began as the officer investigating their activities, then gave them friendly advice, and finally risked his life to warn them about the Cassalanters' corruption.
- **[[Ulkoria Stonemarrow]]** -- Described as "an old friend." Stagget recommended SWOJ visit her when traveling to the North Ward.
- **[[Willifort Crowell (Doppelganger)|Willifort Crowell]]** -- His murderer. The Cassalanter doppelganger killed Stagget and impersonated him inside the [[Docks Ward Guardhouse]] to maintain control over SWOJ's trial.
- **[[Cassalanter Family  Cult Of Asmodeus|Cassalanter Family]]** -- Stagget discovered that the Cassalanters had bought off the magistrate assigned to SWOJ's trial and tried to warn the party, which cost him his life.
