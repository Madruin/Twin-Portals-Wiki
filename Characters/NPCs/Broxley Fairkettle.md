# Broxley Fairkettle

**Broxley Fairkettle** is a representative of the [[Fellowship of Innkeepers]] in [[Waterdeep]]. He visits [[Trollskull Manor]] as one of several guild representatives to discuss tavern licensing requirements with [[SWOJ]] as they prepare to reopen the manor as a functioning tavern.

**First appearance:** [[S01E07 - Episode Seven]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode
**Faction affiliations:** [[Fellowship of Innkeepers]]

## Description

Broxley Fairkettle is a guild representative responsible for overseeing innkeeper-related licensing in [[Waterdeep]]. He is one of roughly ten different guild representatives who visit [[Trollskull Manor]] over the course of a morning to discuss the requirements for reopening the establishment.

## Role in the Story

### Tavern Licensing at Trollskull Manor (S01E07)

- After [[SWOJ]] decides to reopen [[Trollskull Manor]] as a tavern, various guild contacts are sent to the manor to help with the licensing process ([[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]])
- Broxley arrives as part of a stream of roughly ten guild representatives who visit from early morning through highsun
- He represents the [[Fellowship of Innkeepers]], the guild responsible for overseeing tavern and inn operations in [[Waterdeep]]
- The players note his name alongside the other guild reps, with one joking about trying to remember all the names: "There was Broxley and Hammond"
- He visits alongside [[Hammond Kraddoc]] of the [[Vintners', Distillers', and Brewers' Guild]] and [[Justin Rask]] of the [[Guild of Butchers]], among others

## Relationships
