# Mellinore

Mellinore (also heard as "Maelanore") runs [[House Phaulkonmere]], a residence in [[Waterdeep]] where [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xaerine]] and [[Leah]] stayed before the start of the campaign. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xaerine]] had to leave after dangerous men came looking for her, prompting [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] to arrange new lodging for her at [[The Splintered Stair]].

**First appearance:** [[S01E01 - Episode One|S01E01]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

Mellinore is the person who runs [[House Phaulkonmere]], a residence in [[Waterdeep]]. Prior to the events of the campaign, both [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xaerine]] and [[Leah]] stayed there under Mellinore's care. When dangerous men came looking for [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xaerine]], she was forced to flee the house, and [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] arranged for her to move into [[The Splintered Stair]] in the [[Dock Ward]]. [[Leah]], a friend of Xaerine and former first mate of [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]'s ship the [[Sea Plum]], continued to stay at [[House Phaulkonmere]] under Mellinore's care before eventually joining the [[Circle Of Dusk]] druids.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E01 - Episode One|S01E01]]:** Referenced in [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xaerine]]'s backstory as the person who runs [[House Phaulkonmere]], where Xaerine and [[Leah]] had been staying. Xaerine was forced to leave after dangerous men came looking for her.
