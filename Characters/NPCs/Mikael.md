# Mikael

**Mikael** is a winged and horned tiefling or demon who frequents the [[Dark Market]] in the [[Shadowfell (Deep)|Shadowfell]], and one third of an inseparable trio alongside [[Lucinda]] and [[Santorini]]. Described as "very personable," Mikael is the most prominently named of the three when the group introduces themselves to newcomers. The trio hails from [[Avernus]] and operates as social greeters in the market, befriending new arrivals and directing them toward local establishments. The angel [[Ryloren|Rylerin]] considers them harmless but advises caution: "Just don't get too comfortable with them."

**First appearance:** [[S02E21 - Episode Fifty One (S02E21)]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

Mikael is roughly human-sized (comparable to [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]) with wings and horns, matching the appearance of his companions [[Lucinda]] and [[Santorini]]. The trio is described as extremely friendly, casual, and "weirdly relaxed and personable." They approach newcomers openly -- throwing arms around shoulders, slapping backs, and eagerly trading stories about the [[Shadowfell (Deep)|Shadowfell]]. They speak Common. An insight check by [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] (rolling 18) reveals that while they deliberately single out newcomers and try to get a read on them, they are not pickpocketing or stealing. Their demeanor carries a hint of the hustle -- like greeters who want to know what they can learn from you -- but nothing overtly threatening. The trio claims to be from [[Avernus]] and says this is their second trip to the Shadowfell, with roughly twenty days spent in the [[Dark Market]].

## Role in the Story

- **[[S02E21 - Episode Fifty One (S02E21)|S02E21]]:** Mikael, [[Lucinda]], and [[Santorini]] are the first denizens to approach [[Silver Waves of Justice|SWOJ]] upon their arrival in the [[Dark Market]]. They introduce themselves warmly and claim to be visitors from [[Avernus]] on their second trip to the Shadowfell. The trio shares information freely: they confirm no one has gone up to investigate the dark sun yet, point out the angel [[Ryloren|Rylerin]]'s stall (while keeping their distance due to "slightly different factions"), and strongly recommend [[The Nest]] as a place to stay and find tea. They note that "if it has a soul, it's a person" -- grinning rakishly as they say it. [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]], inherently wary of people who are instantly friendly, runs an insight check and detects the trio's pattern of singling out newcomers, though nothing malicious surfaces. [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] checks his belongings after physical contact and finds nothing missing. The trio asks SWOJ to "put in a good word for us" at [[The Nest]] before the party moves on. Later, when SWOJ mentions Mikael, Lucinda, and Santorini to [[Ryloren|Rylerin]], the angel confirms they have been in the market "for a long time" and are "okay," but cautions: "Just don't get too comfortable with them" -- a remark that carries extra weight coming from an angel who counts "demons, devils, aberrations" among his enemies.

## Relationships

- **[[Lucinda]]** and **[[Santorini]]** -- Mikael's constant companions in the [[Dark Market]]. The three function as a single social unit, always together when approaching newcomers and staying together at [[The Nest]].
- **[[Silver Waves of Justice|SWOJ]]** -- Newcomers whom the trio befriends in the [[Dark Market]], sharing drinks, conversation, and local knowledge.
- **[[Ryloren|Rylerin]]** -- The angel trader in the [[Dark Market]]. The trio keeps a respectful distance, citing "slightly different factions." Rylerin considers them okay but warns SWOJ not to get too comfortable with them -- notably, Rylerin lists demons among his enemies.
