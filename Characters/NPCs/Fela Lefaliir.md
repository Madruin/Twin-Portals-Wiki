# Fala Lefaliir

Fala Lefaliir is a wood elf herbalist who runs [[Corellon's Crown]], an apothecary shop on [[Trollskull Alley]] in the North Ward of [[Waterdeep]]. A member of the [[Guild Of Apothecaries]], Fala became one of [[SWOJ]]'s earliest friends and neighbors after the party acquired [[Trollskull Manor]]. Charming and enthusiastic, Fala sells potions, cultivates plants, and can speak with the flora growing around the alley. They were kidnapped during the three-year time displacement and later rescued from [[Arcturia]]'s spiral cavern beneath the [[Sword Mountains]], though their memories were left scrambled by the ordeal.

**First appearance:** [[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]
**Total appearances:** 7 episodes
**Faction affiliations:** [[Guild Of Apothecaries]]

## Description

Fala is described as a charming young wood elf with long, braided hair. They own and live in the two-story building that houses [[Corellon's Crown]], where they sell a variety of potions -- healing, water breathing, climbing, and animal friendship among them -- all brewed personally. Fala avoids dealing in exotic poisons or dangerous substances, preferring remedies that "help bridge the gap and help people communicate." They are also able to speak with plants using druidic magic and have tended to the vines and flora growing around [[Trollskull Alley]] for many years.

Fala is warm and community-minded, expressing genuine excitement when SWOJ moved into [[Trollskull Manor]] and volunteering to draw up landscaping plans for the building. They provided flower planters for the manor's windows and offered neighborhood guild rates for their services. Fala is also an avid reader of the Waterdeep broadsheets and recognized SWOJ from their early adventures, calling themselves "such a big fan."

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]:** SWOJ visits [[Corellon's Crown]] while getting to know their [[Trollskull Alley]] neighbors. Fala introduces themselves as a wood elf herbalist and member of the [[Guild Of Apothecaries]], shows the party a selection of potions, offers to draw up landscaping plans for [[Trollskull Manor]], and expresses enthusiasm about the alley's revival. They also recommend [[Vincent Trench]], calling him their favorite conversationalist. Fala asks about stabilizing a body when Lark raises the question, and directs them to chemists who could help.
- **[[S01E09 - Episode Nine|S01E09]]:** Fala has provided flower planters for the windows of [[Trollskull Manor]] as the tavern nears its grand opening, contributing to the building's improved appearance.
- **[[S01E10 - Episode Ten|S01E10]]:** [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] visits [[Corellon's Crown]] early in the morning about his lycanthropy condition -- he began turning into a silver ferret under the moonlight. Fala has not heard of were-ferrets but suggests he see a cleric of Selune for treatment.
- **[[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]:** One of the party members mentions returning an apothecary mug containing tea to Fala's shop, suggesting ongoing neighborly exchanges.
- **[[S02E05 - Episode Thirty Five|S02E05]]:** When SWOJ returns to [[Waterdeep]] after three years displaced in time, they discover that [[Corellon's Crown]] is dark and closed. A City Watch officer reveals that something large broke into the shop from the back, punching a ten-foot-wide tunnel through the wall heading north. Fala is gone. Xae casts Speak with Plants on the moss beds inside the shop and learns that something enormous -- possibly flying, as there was no rumbling until it arrived -- stomped through and took Fala away during the night. The party speculates that a dragon or similar creature may have been responsible and that Fala's druidic abilities may have made them a target.
- **[[S02E07 - Episode Thirty Seven|S02E07]]:** SWOJ finds Fala alive deeper in the [[Spiral Cavern]] beneath the [[Sword Mountains]], in areas the party did not fully explore during their confrontation with [[Arcturia]]. Fala's memories are described as "all over the place," scrambled by [[Arcturia]]'s transmutation magic and the memory-altering mist. The party speculates that Fala's herbalism expertise or druidic nature may have been why [[Arcturia]] targeted them specifically.
- **[[S02E08 - Episode Thirty Eight|S02E08]]:** The party discusses bringing Fala back to [[Waterdeep]] along with [[Leah]] and other rescued captives after the events in the [[Spiral Cavern]]. Fala's memories remain scrambled.

## Relationships

- **[[SWOJ]]** -- Fala is one of SWOJ's closest neighbors on [[Trollskull Alley]] and became a friendly ally early in their time at [[Trollskull Manor]]. They provided plants and planters for the manor, offered potion supplies, and expressed genuine excitement about the party's arrival. SWOJ later risked the journey north to rescue Fala after their kidnapping.
- **[[Vincent Trench]]** -- Fala's fellow [[Trollskull Alley]] neighbor and self-described favorite conversationalist. Fala recommended that SWOJ visit his detective agency, [[Tiger's Eye]].
- **[[Arcturia]]** -- Fala's captor. Arcturia kidnapped Fala from [[Corellon's Crown]] and held them in the [[Spiral Cavern]] beneath the [[Sword Mountains]], where her transmutation magic scrambled their memories. The party suspects Fala's druidic knowledge may have been the reason for the abduction.
- **[[Anya Ermbrusk]]** -- A fellow captive held in the [[Spiral Cavern]] alongside Fala. Both were found alive but with damaged memories.
