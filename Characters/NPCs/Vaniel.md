# Vaniel

Vaniel is a halfling patron of the [[Hole In The Wall]] tavern at the base of the [[The God Catcher|God Catcher]] in [[Waterdeep]]. He is a friendly, sociable figure who befriends [[Scoot Sparkles|SWOJ]] during their visits to [[Trollskull Alley]] and the surrounding neighborhood. He is killed in the devastating fireball explosion that rocks Trollskull Alley during the grand opening of [[Trollskull Manor  The Short Rest|The Short Rest]] in [[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]].

**Race:** Halfling
**First appearance:** [[S01E10 - Episode Ten|S01E10]]
**Last appearance:** [[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes
**Status:** Deceased -- killed in the Trollskull Alley fireball

---

## Description

Vaniel is a halfling who frequents the [[Hole In The Wall]], a halfling tavern located at the base of the [[The God Catcher|God Catcher]] statue. He is known to SWOJ through their visits to the tavern, which serves as a gathering place for the halfling community in the North Ward. He is described as a warm, approachable figure who enjoys wine and conversation.

In the transcript, his name is heard variously as "Vaniel," "Voniel," and "Bonneul" -- typical of the audio transcription variations across the podcast.

---

## Role in the Story

### Lycanthropy Concerns at the Hole in the Wall ([[S01E10 - Episode Ten|S01E10]])

Vaniel first appears when [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] and [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] visit the [[Hole In The Wall]] tavern. There, he discusses his concerns about **lycanthropy** with SWOJ -- a relevant topic given the party's recent encounters with the [[Shard Shunners]] wererat gang who had been operating out of a lair connected to the tavern's back rooms (discovered in [[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]). The conversation underscores the neighborhood's unease about the lycanthropic threat lurking beneath the God Catcher.

### The Fireball on Trollskull Alley ([[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]])

Vaniel attends the grand opening of [[Trollskull Manor  The Short Rest|The Short Rest]], SWOJ's newly renovated tavern on [[Trollskull Alley]]. He is seen outside celebrating with [[Dasher Zuber|Dasher]] and other neighbors, playing fiddle and dancing. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] brings him "a giant glass of wine," which he thanks her for deeply and sincerely. He also asks if he can take one of the promotional posters.

Shortly after, a catastrophic **fireball explosion** rocks Trollskull Alley, killing ten or more people. Vaniel is among the dead. In the aftermath, SWOJ recognizes his body among the fallen, along with broken violins nearby. The transcript captures the moment: "You recognize Bonneul. And the other three you don't recognize." [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] tells [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]], "Don't look."

Vaniel's death is one of the personal costs of the fireball -- transforming what should have been a celebration into a crime scene that launches the [[Stone of Golorr]] investigation arc. His death makes the attack feel immediate and personal to SWOJ, as he was not a stranger but a friend from the neighborhood.

- **[[Dasher Zuber]]** -- Fellow neighborhood figure; the two were seen dancing and playing fiddle together outside the Short Rest.
