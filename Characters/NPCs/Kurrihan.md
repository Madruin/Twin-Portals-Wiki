# Kurrihan

Kurrihan is an enormous [[dragon turtle]] that lives beneath [[Driftwood]], a lawless pirate settlement in the [[Nameless Seas]]. So massive that the entire floating island sits on his shell, anchored to him, Kurrihan is effectively the living foundation of the city. The dolphins and sea creatures in the area work for him, and travelers must throw a coin in the water as tribute when arriving or departing.

**First appearance:** [[S02E09 - Episode Thirty Nine (S02E09)|S02E09]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

Kurrihan (sometimes heard as "Kura Han") is a dragon turtle of staggering size -- large enough that the entire floating island of [[Driftwood]] rests on his shell. He is anchored to the island and helps navigate it in times of need. Many of the sea creatures in the surrounding waters work for him, including the dolphins that guide ships to [[Driftwood]]. As a condition of safe passage, all visitors to [[Driftwood]] must throw a coin into the water as tribute to Kurrihan upon arrival or departure.

[[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] was fascinated by Kurrihan and eager to study him for a possible future wild shape form. SWOJ borrowed [[Jarlaxle]]'s submersible, the [[Scarlet Marpenoth]], to visit him beneath the island. Xae interacted with Kurrihan in dolphin form and studied him closely. Kurrihan does not usually speak to guests, but he tolerated Xae's attention.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S02E09 - Episode Thirty Nine (S02E09)|S02E09]]:** [[Fel'Rekt Lafeen]] tells SWOJ about Kurrihan when they arrive at [[Driftwood]]. He explains that the dragon turtle lives beneath the floating island, that the sea creatures in the area work for him, and that visitors must throw a coin in the water as tribute. SWOJ borrows the [[Scarlet Marpenoth]] submersible to visit Kurrihan. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] transforms into a dolphin to interact with him and study him for a possible future wild shape. Kurrihan does not usually talk to guests, but he allows Xae's presence.
