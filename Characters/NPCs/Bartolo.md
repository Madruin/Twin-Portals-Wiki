# Bartolo

**Bartolo** is a [[City Watch]] guard in [[Waterdeep]] who serves in the [[Docks Ward Guardhouse|Docks Ward]]. A secret fan of [[SWOJ]], he nonetheless carries out his duties faithfully, including reading the formal charges against the party at their trial before [[Laeral Silverhand]].

**First appearance:** [[S01E13 - Episode Thirteen]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes
**Faction affiliations:** [[City Watch]]

## Description

Bartolo is a rank-and-file city watch guard who stands out from his peers through his genuine admiration for [[SWOJ]]. Despite being assigned to guard the party after their arrest, he remains friendly and approachable, openly admitting he is a fan of the group while acknowledging that the law must be followed. He carries himself with a degree of reluctance when it comes to treating SWOJ as criminals, but he never shirks his responsibilities.

## Role in the Story

### Arrest and Imprisonment (S01E13)

- After [[Captain Hyustus Stagget]] arrests SWOJ following the High Road chase during the [[Feast of the Moon]], the party is taken to the [[Docks Ward Guardhouse]] ([[S01E13 - Episode Thirteen|S01E13]])
- While the other guards maintain a professional distance, Bartolo introduces himself and tells the party he is a big fan, saying: "I'm a big fan of SWOJ, but I'm sorry, this is the law. I'm just supposed to keep the blood off the streets"
- He engages with the party, asking if they liked his prayer and expressing genuine warmth, even as his fellow guards watch his unusually friendly behaviour with suspicion

### The Trial (S01E15)

- At SWOJ's trial before [[Laeral Silverhand]], Bartolo serves as the court officer who reads the formal charges ([[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]])
- [[Laeral Silverhand|Laeral]] calls on him directly: "Let's hear the charges put forward, Bartolo, if you will," and he steps up to the lectern with a lengthy scroll
- He reads out the extensive list of charges against [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]], [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]], [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]], and [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]], including crimes against nobles, crimes against the city, crimes against the gods (tomb robbing), and crimes against citizens
- When [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] presents the Gralhund letter as evidence, Bartolo crosses the courtroom to collect it and deliver it to [[Laeral Silverhand|Laeral]], looking visibly uncomfortable with the proceedings but staying quiet
- He is present in the courtroom during the subsequent chaos when [[Willifort Crowell]] is exposed as a doppelganger

## Relationships

- **[[SWOJ]]** — A genuine fan of the party; tries to balance personal admiration with professional duty
- **[[Laeral Silverhand]]** — Serves as court officer under her authority during the trial
- **[[Captain Hyustus Stagget]]** — Fellow member of the city watch; part of the guard detail at the Docks Ward
- **[[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]** — Had a friendly conversation with Xae the night before the trial, noted by the other guards
