# Fioleth

Fioleth is a ragamuffin -- a unique species of creature native to the [[The Bag Dimension]]. Humanoid-sized, Fioleth is made up of mismatching clothing and many layers, with a raven-beak wooden mask covering their face and wheel-like prosthetic feet that allow them to travel along the ropes connecting bags. They collect and sort items that fall through bags of holding, maintaining a personal hideout called [[Fioleth's Junkyard]]. After leaving the bag dimension with SWOJ, Fioleth settled in [[Neverwinter]] as a craftsman before eventually rejoining the party as quartermaster and repair specialist aboard the [[Ethereal Galleon]].

**First appearance:** [[S02E13 - Episode Forty Three LIVE (S02E13)|S02E13]]
**Status:** Alive

## Description

Fioleth is a ragamuffin -- a creature composed of mismatching clothing piled in many layers, giving them a bulky, patchwork appearance. They wear a wooden mask shaped like a raven's beak over their face, and their feet are wheel-like prosthetics that let them roll along the thick ropes connecting the floating bags of holding in the bag dimension. Fioleth folds up into a pile of clothes to sleep. They identify as a "ragamuffin" species.

## Role in the Story

### The Bag Dimension (S02E13)

When SWOJ tumbled into a bag of holding after escaping the museum of the multiverse, they encountered Fioleth in the [[The Bag Dimension]] -- an infinite demiplane of astral space with a lattice of floating bags connected by ropes. Fioleth took the party to [[Fioleth's Junkyard]], their personal bag protected by an arcane gesture that keeps [[The Bag Man]] away. The junkyard also housed [[Oobledoop]], a healing gelatinous fungus.

Fioleth equipped SWOJ with weapons and gear from bags of stored items: [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] received a steampunk anchor pipe maul, [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] got a double-ended silver-plated dagger, [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] received matching red-stained hand crossbows with 40 bolts, and [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] got a long whip with 15-foot reach and a heater shield bearing the [[Gralhund]] family crest. In exchange, Fioleth asked the party to retrieve a golden turtle amulet from a nearby bag -- a memento important to [[The Bag Man]].

After SWOJ retrieved the amulet and the Bag Man went berserk, Fioleth was captured. The party rescued Fioleth from the Bag Man's lair and together they escaped through [[Jarlaxle Baenre|Jarlaxle]]'s twin portal bag.

### Neverwinter (S02E14)

Fioleth accompanied SWOJ ashore when they arrived in [[Neverwinter]] via the [[Eye Catcher]]. They planned to find work as a craftsman in the city, and were left behind when SWOJ continued their adventures.

### Quartermaster of the Ethereal Galleon (S02E31-S02E33)

By the time SWOJ rallied allies for their assault on the [[Moonhole]], Fioleth had been working for [[Neverwinter]]'s rebuilding efforts. They volunteered to rejoin the crew as quartermaster aboard the [[Ethereal Galleon]]. During the voyage through the [[Deep Ethereal Plane]] toward the [[Radiant Citadel]], Fioleth salvaged the [[Clockwork Kraken]]'s mechanical claw arm and jerry-rigged it onto the Ethereal Galleon as a crude but functional crane mechanism. They also handled general ship repairs, keeping the battered vessel operational despite its damaged masts.

## Key Relationships

- **[[Scoot Sparkles|SWOJ]]** -- Rescued Fioleth from [[The Bag Man]] and brought them out of the bag dimension; Fioleth later repaid the debt by serving as their quartermaster
- **[[The Bag Man]]** -- The entity that rules the bag dimension; Fioleth lived in hiding from the Bag Man before escaping with SWOJ
- **[[Oobledoop]]** -- A healing gelatinous fungus that resided in [[Fioleth's Junkyard]]
- **[[Squidly]], [[Fel'Rekt Lafeen]], [[Shaleheart]], [[Urstul Floxxin]]** -- Fellow crew members aboard the [[Ethereal Galleon]]
