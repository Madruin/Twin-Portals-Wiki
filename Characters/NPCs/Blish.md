# Blish

A [[Shadar-kai]] (shadow elf) who serves the [[Raven Queen|Raven Mother]] alongside his companions [[Mari]] and [[Idae]]. The trio operates in the [[Shadowfell]], collecting wayward souls and delivering them -- along with their stories -- to the [[Fortress of Memory]].

**First appearance:** [[S02E02 - Episode Thirty Two (S02E02)|S02E02]]
**Total appearances:** 3 episodes

## Description

Blish is one of three Shadar-kai encountered by [[SWOJ]] in the [[Shadowfell]]. He is notably twitchy and stutters when he speaks, introducing himself with a halting "I'm, I'm, I'm, I'm Blish." Despite his nervous demeanor, he is friendly and forthcoming -- sometimes too much so, prompting [[Mari]] to scold him for revealing too much information to strangers.

The trio wears a distinctive mishmash of leather, teeth, and spoons, and they carry small drums on their hips. Music is part of their religion in service to the [[Raven Queen|Raven Mother]], and they are keen to learn the stories of those they encounter so they can compose songs to bring back to their mistress. Blish in particular is enthusiastic about songwriting; during the trio's second encounter with SWOJ, he is noted to have written a song about the party's exploits.

Of the three Shadar-kai, [[Idae]] serves as the leader and primary speaker, [[Mari]] tends to be more guarded and cautious, and Blish is the most excitable and talkative -- though also the most easily flustered.

## Role in the Story

### First Encounter -- The Shadowfell Borderlands ([[S02E02 - Episode Thirty Two (S02E02)|S02E02]])

SWOJ emerged from the [[Brighanox]] tunnels into the windswept darkness of the [[Shadowfell]] and encountered three figures on the road. The Shadar-kai initially regarded the party with suspicion, sensing something mortal -- and something else -- about the group (reacting to [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]'s unusual spiritual nature). Once introductions were made, Blish stutteringly introduced himself and his companions.

The trio provided SWOJ with critical intelligence:
- They serve the [[Raven Queen|Raven Mother]] (also called the Madam of Feathers, Queen of Memory), collecting damned souls and their stories to deliver to the [[Fortress of Memory]]
- They use special crystals to trap wayward souls
- The [[Strigoi]] steal [[wish stones]] and embed them in the scales of the [[Beast of Erua]] to fuel its dark march
- The [[Rose Knight]] Teriador slew the Beast of Erua in ages past and built a mausoleum atop its corpse, but the beast has been reanimated by the wish stones' power
- They were traveling to the city of Toialis, south of the Lachuna and the Canyon of Crying, to collect wayward spirits
- They warned SWOJ to "beware the bag man" upon noticing [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]'s bag of holding

Before departing, Mari scolded Blish for telling the strangers too much. The trio asked to sing tales of SWOJ on their journey and to return what they learned to their mother. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] played a few chords on her concertina as a send-off, and the Shadar-kai regarded her intently, as if her music seemed somehow familiar. They played a backup beat on their hip-drums and walked away into the Shadowfell darkness.

### Second Encounter -- Return to the Deep Shadowfell ([[S02E20 - Episode Fifty (S02E20)|S02E20]] / [[S02E21 - Episode Fifty One (S02E21)|S02E21]])

Roughly three years later (in-world), SWOJ descended through the [[Raven Queen Shrine]] beneath the [[Shard of Moonlight]] and arrived in the deep Shadowfell via a portal. Crossing a stone bridge toward them were the same three Shadar-kai -- now singing a song about SWOJ's adventures, drumming an approaching rhythm. The trio was clearly expecting them; the [[Raven Queen|Raven Mother]] herself had sent them to meet the party.

[[Idae]] high-fived [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] and declared their arrival "fortuitous," while the other two drummed celebratory beats in the background. Blish was noted to have composed the SWOJ song himself and was eager for the party to hear it.

The Shadar-kai delivered urgent news:
- A [[Zhentil Keep (Dark Sun)|dark sun]] had appeared in the Shadowfell sky about ten days prior -- a massive opaque black sphere far larger than the normal sun
- The dark sun was siphoning souls passing through the Shadowfell, disrupting the natural order and preventing them from reaching the [[Fortress of Memory]]
- Wyvern patrols regularly departed the dark sun to scour the mountain crags and attack anyone who moved
- A piece of a castle (later identified as part of [[Zhentil Keep]]) had fallen from the dark sun to the ground below, appearing to be a section of library filled with books and spirit curators

The trio provided SWOJ with a charcoal map marking the locations of the [[Sepulchre of the Raven Queen]], the [[Dark Market]], the fallen library, and wyvern patrol routes. They instructed SWOJ to retrieve a [[soul stone]] from the sepulchre -- a gift from the Raven Mother to help trap the "false crux" (their quarry). Each party member would need to leave a personal sacrifice at the altar to enter.

Finally, the Shadar-kai claimed [[Mr. E]] as their companion, and the drow departed with them on a separate mission with a dreamlike, peaceful look in his eyes. The trio offered to share a meal before parting ways, then continued on their pilgrimage.

- **[[Mari]]** -- Fellow Shadar-kai; the cautious one who reins in Blish's tendency to overshare
- **[[Raven Queen|Raven Mother]]** -- Their mistress, the Queen of Memory, whom they serve by collecting souls and stories
- **[[SWOJ]]** -- The Shadar-kai are friendly and helpful toward the party, composing songs about their adventures and serving as guides in the Shadowfell
- **[[Mr. E]]** -- The drow who departed with the Shadar-kai on a separate mission during the second encounter
