# Obatha Merlywin

Obatha Merlywin is a magistar in [[Waterdeep]]'s governance structure, mentioned alongside [[Ulkoria Stonemarrow]] in the context of city governance and the [[Watchful Order Of Magisters|Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors]]. She is referenced when [[Captain Hyustus Stagget]] advises SWOJ to register their magic users.

**First appearance:** [[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

Obatha Merlywin is a magistar -- a title used for officials associated with [[Waterdeep]]'s magical governance. She is mentioned in the same context as [[Ulkoria Stonemarrow]], a shield dwarf magistar in the [[North Ward]], when [[Captain Hyustus Stagget]] advises the party about registering magic users with the [[Watchful Order Of Magisters|Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors]]. No physical description is provided; she appears to be a background figure in the city's administrative structure.
