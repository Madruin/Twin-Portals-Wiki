## Description

Leader of Bregan D'aerthe. Emerged from the bag of holding through connected twin portals with his drow soldiers. Traded information about the Necromancer for the Mask of the Lords and offered Zardos's entertainment troupe for SWOJ's celebration.

Drow captain of the Sea Maiden's Fair, a fleet that includes the Eyecatcher. Charming, calculating, and underdressed. Provides SWOJ with equipment, a new hat for Lark, and intelligence about Neverwinter. His ships are barred from trading in Neverwinter's port due to suspicion of being Waterdeep spies. Asks SWOJ to put in a good word with city leadership.
