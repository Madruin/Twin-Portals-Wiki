# Bonnie Hilltopple

Barmaid and waitress at the [[Yawning Portal]] tavern in [[Waterdeep]], later revealed to be a doppelganger with innate telepathic abilities. Bonnie is a trusted ally of [[SWOJ]] who uses her mind-reading powers to help the party gather intelligence. She is also a musician who plays double bass on the side.

**Race/Type:** Doppelganger (shapechanger)
**Occupation:** Barmaid at the [[Yawning Portal]]; musician
**First appearance:** [[S01E01 - Episode One|S01E01]]
**Total appearances:** 5 episodes

## Description

Bonnie works as a waitress and bartender at the [[Yawning Portal]], where she is known for serving her specialty berry concoction -- a passion fruit cocktail that nobody else orders. She is friendly and personable, particularly close with [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] and [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]. [[Durnan]], the tavern's owner, tolerates her chatting with patrons but occasionally scowls at her for lingering too long at tables.

Bonnie is a doppelganger by birth, possessing the innate ability to read thoughts and change her shape. She prefers the term "shape changer" over "doppelganger," noting that the latter has "an arcane ring to it." Unlike hostile doppelgangers such as [[Zylka]], Bonnie is benevolent and uses her abilities to help SWOJ. She describes doppelgangers as "a rare breed."

Outside of her work at the Yawning Portal, Bonnie plays double bass in a band and occasionally sneaks into social events to experience life beyond the tavern.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E01 - Episode One|S01E01]]:** Bonnie delivers drinks to SWOJ's table at the [[Yawning Portal]] during their first meeting. She serves her specialty berry concoction to [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]], who takes a particular liking to it. She listens in on the party's introductions and chats with [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]], drawing a scowl from [[Durnan]]. Later, she brings more rounds when [[Volothamp Geddarm|Volo]] joins the group to hire them.
- **[[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]:** Bonnie Hilltopple is identified as the bartender at the [[Hole In The Wall]], a halfling tavern at the base of the [[The God Catcher|God Catcher]] statue. She is also connected to the [[Shard Shunners]] who operate from the tavern's back rooms.
- **[[S01E10 - Episode Ten|S01E10]]:** SWOJ encounters Bonnie at the [[Cassalanter Villa]] party, where she is playing double bass in a band. She admits she stole an invitation to see "the noble life." When [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] confronts her, Bonnie openly confirms she is a doppelganger, saying she was born one. She reveals she can read thoughts and alerts SWOJ to [[Willifort Crowell]]'s suspicious behavior. She helps examine the captured [[Zylka]], who had been impersonating [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]. Bonnie confirms that "Bonnie" is her preferred name.
- **[[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]:** Bonnie is spotted leaving [[Jarlaxle]]'s ship, the [[Eye Catcher|Eyecatcher]], in the early morning, and informs SWOJ their ban from the [[Yawning Portal]] has been lifted. At the tavern, SWOJ recruits Bonnie to telepathically read the mind of [[Davil Starsong]], a [[Zhentarim]] agent of the [[Doom Raiders]] faction. Bonnie rolls a natural 20 and provides critical intelligence: Davil came to [[Waterdeep]] from [[Neverwinter]] specifically searching for the [[The Stone of Golorr]], and he has a connection to [[Yagra]]. She pretends to work the floor while eavesdropping telepathically, then pulls [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] aside on the balcony to share her findings. She serves SWOJ passion fruit cocktails and offers to help further on her night off. SWOJ also floats the idea of having Bonnie "telepathically scour the city" with them.
- **[[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]:** After SWOJ's long absence, Bonnie greets them at the door of the [[Yawning Portal]], letting them know [[Durnan]] has lifted the ban. She gives them a free (expired) berry drink -- noting it has been sitting in the back for three years because nobody else orders it.

## Relationships

- **[[Durnan]]** -- Her employer at the [[Yawning Portal]]. He tolerates her but is not above scowling at her for fraternizing with customers.
- **[[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]** -- The SWOJ member Bonnie is closest to. Xae is the one who discovers Bonnie is a doppelganger and the one Bonnie pulls aside to share telepathic intelligence.
- **[[Jarlaxle]]** -- Bonnie is seen leaving his ship the [[Eye Catcher|Eyecatcher]] in the morning, suggesting a social connection to the [[Bregan D'aerthe]] leader.
- **[[Zylka]]** -- A fellow doppelganger, though unlike Bonnie, Zylka is hostile and impersonated [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]. Bonnie helps SWOJ examine the captured Zylka.
- **[[Terry]]** -- SWOJ's intellect devourer ally. Bonnie and Terry are both telepathic beings allied with the party, and SWOJ sees them as complementary intelligence assets.

## Abilities

- **Telepathy / Mind Reading** -- Bonnie can read the surface thoughts of creatures near her, a natural doppelganger ability. She uses this to eavesdrop on [[Davil Starsong]]'s thoughts at the [[Yawning Portal]], rolling a natural 20 to extract detailed intelligence about the [[The Stone of Golorr]].
- **Shapeshifting** -- As a doppelganger, Bonnie can alter her physical form. She prefers to maintain a consistent appearance and identity.
