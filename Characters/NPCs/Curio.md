# Curio

**Curio** is a [[mimic]] who operates a merchant stall in the [[Dark Market]], a bazaar deep in the [[Shadowfell (Deep)|Shadowfell]]. Presenting himself as a friendly and enthusiastic vendor of armor, information, and rare goods, Curio conceals a covetous nature -- and a dangerous fixation on the [[Breath of the First Dawn]], a powerful artifact kept by the angel [[Ryloren|Rylerin]]. His attempt to manipulate [[Silver Waves of Justice|SWOJ]] into stealing the artifact backfires spectacularly when [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] chooses honesty over theft, resulting in Curio's capture and imprisonment in a jar inside Rylerin's cabinet.

**First appearance:** [[S02E21 - Episode Fifty One (S02E21)|S02E21]]
**Status:** Captured (imprisoned in a jar by [[Ryloren|Rylerin]])

## Description

Curio's true form is that of a mimic -- a shapeshifting creature that typically disguises itself as inanimate objects. In the [[Dark Market]], he takes the shape of an entire merchant stall, complete with displayed goods. A lamp on the edge of the stall opens to reveal a mouth full of teeth and a tongue, which he uses to speak. When animated, different parts of the stall wave and move independently. He can extend pseudopods to manipulate objects, such as using a pencil to mark a map. A guitar on the stall can split open down the middle to reveal another mouth and speak with his voice. Curio refers to himself in the third person and adopts a boisterous, salesman-like persona, eager to help customers and quick to boast about his ability to find anything.

When attempting to flee from [[Ryloren|Rylerin]], Curio's stall shrinks and bundles up into a small barrel with legs, sauntering toward the market's archway in an attempt to escape. After his capture, he is reduced to a jar-sized form with a single glaring eye visible through the glass -- described as having "the essence of a mimic."

## Role in the Story

- **[[S02E21 - Episode Fifty One (S02E21)|S02E21]]:** [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] discovers Curio's stall while exploring the [[Dark Market]]. Curio introduces himself and offers to sell armor, information, and rare items. When SWOJ explains they need to reach [[Zentil Keep]] (floating in the dark sun above the Shadowfell), Curio reveals he possesses a spell jamming helm -- salvaged from an ancient crashed [[Iambus]] ship -- capable of animating and flying any surface it is attached to. He prices it at 300,000 Faerunian gold coins, but when the party cannot afford it, he draws them behind a curtain for a private deal. He asks them to steal a small sealed clay jar from the cabinet of the angel [[Ryloren|Rylerin]], warning that the angel will not part with it willingly and that the jar must not be opened. He has been watching Rylerin and knows the cabinet is kept unlocked. [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] stays behind to question Curio further, learning that no one else has been asked to steal the jar yet. Meanwhile, Lark approaches Rylerin directly and honestly explains the situation. Upon hearing this, Rylerin glances at Curio's stall, which immediately begins to shrink and scuttle away as a barrel with legs. Rylerin says he will "have words with Curio later" and departs. He returns carrying a new jar containing something writhing inside -- Curio himself, reduced and imprisoned, with a single familiar eye glaring out. Rylerin gives SWOJ the spell jamming helm as a reward for their honesty, and reveals that the jar Curio wanted contained the [[Breath of the First Dawn]], though Rylerin appears frightened when contemplating what Curio would have done with it. The party apologizes to the imprisoned Curio and departs. The players note that by leaving the Dark Market (a designated safe haven), Curio made himself vulnerable -- "What a fool."

## Relationships

- **[[Ryloren|Rylerin]]** -- The angel trader whose artifact Curio coveted. Rylerin ultimately captured and imprisoned Curio after learning of his scheme. The two now share a jar-and-cabinet arrangement, with Curio trapped inside Rylerin's collection.
- **[[Silver Waves of Justice|SWOJ]]** -- Curio attempted to use the adventuring party as unwitting thieves, offering the spell jamming helm as incentive. His plan was foiled by Lark's honesty.
- **[[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]** -- The party member Curio specifically tried to recruit for the theft, noting his "skills." Ironically, it was Lark's decision to be forthright with Rylerin that sealed Curio's fate.
- **[[Spider Merchant]]** -- A fellow [[Dark Market]] vendor who operates nearby.
