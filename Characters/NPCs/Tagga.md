# Tagga

Tagga is [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]'s mother, an elderly orc woman who becomes one of SWOJ's most steadfast allies and the heart of their domestic life in [[Waterdeep]]. Beginning as a surprise visitor who brings pie to Scoot's apartment, Tagga quickly evolves into the operational backbone of [[Trollskull Manor]] -- managing the tavern, raising the [[Trollskull Alley Irregulars|Irregulars]], cooking for visiting dragons, and holding the home front together through every crisis. She endures being targeted by the [[Xanathar Guild]], survives the fireball that devastates Trollskull Alley, testifies bravely at SWOJ's trial, and fights through a debilitating spore illness during the three-year gap. By the Season 2 finale, her children Bennet and Janks have grown into teenagers, and hints of a secret past with the [[Zhentarim]] suggest there is more to this formidable orc matriarch than meets the eye.

**First appearance:** [[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]
**Total appearances:** 10 episodes
**Faction affiliations:** None confirmed (hints of [[Zhentarim]] connection)

## Description

Tagga is described as an elderly orc woman (sometimes referred to as half-orc) who lives in the [[Dock Ward]] of Waterdeep. She is physically imposing -- when tasked with keeping [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] in a room, she is described as "an immovable steel wall" that he cannot get around. Despite her intimidating stature, Tagga is warm, nurturing, and fiercely protective of her family and the growing community at Trollskull Manor.

She is a skilled cook who prepares massive pies and is enthusiastic about cooking large game beasts, including meals for visiting dragons. Her husband [[Simon]] works for the [[Waterdeep Navy]] and is frequently away at sea, leaving Tagga to manage the household alone. She has at least two younger children -- Bennet and Janks -- who by the Season 2 finale have grown into teenagers, with Bennet training as a [[Griffin Riders|Griffin Rider]] recruit and Janks studying as a wizard.

In the Season 2 finale, Tagga reveals hints of a secret past: she winks knowingly when asked about the Zhentarim, suggesting hidden shadow skills and a history she has never fully disclosed. She organized the homecoming party at Trollskull Manor and took charge of educating the Irregulars, who came to call her "mom."

## Role in the Story

### Season 1

- **S01E04:** Tagga arrives at Scoot's apartment in the [[Dock Ward]] bearing a massive homemade pie. She introduces herself to the rest of SWOJ, providing a warm domestic counterpoint to the party's dangerous adventures. She shares useful local information about the neighborhood.
- **S01E05:** SWOJ rushes to check on Tagga after learning the [[Xanathar Guild]] is targeting them. They find her apartment ransacked, the door open, with crow feathers scattered about -- the Xanathar Guild's calling card. An amethyst [[sending stone]] is discovered at the scene. They learn that [[Pinker]], a kenku ally, rescued Tagga from the Xanathar agents and hid her in a smuggler's closet inside the [[Honorable Knight]] statue tenements. SWOJ finds Tagga resting safely in Pinker's nest.
- **S01E07:** With Tagga no longer safe in the Dock Ward, she agrees to help run [[Trollskull Manor]] as a tavern. She moves in and begins managing the household, mentioning that [[Simon]] (Scoot's father) works for the [[Waterdeep Navy]]. She joins the group alongside the orphan Irregulars for the tavern renovation work.
- **S01E11:** Tagga helps prepare for the grand opening of the [[Short Rest]], unloading food and drinks from [[Terry]]'s cart. When the catastrophic fireball explosion rocks Trollskull Alley, Tagga is badly injured -- knocked off her feet and left leaning against the wall with difficulty breathing. Scoot rushes to find her, and [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] uses healing magic to stabilize her. Tagga crawls away from the blast zone alongside Janks. Despite her injuries, she comforts other wounded victims placed near her.
- **S01E15:** Tagga testifies as a character witness at SWOJ's trial, helping to establish their good reputation before the court. [[Jarlaxle|Zardos Zord]] guards the door to protect Tagga and other witnesses when courtroom chaos erupts. She is evacuated to safety during the fighting.
- **S01E22:** SWOJ returns from their journey to the [[Feywild]] carrying [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]'s soul in a red hat. They reunite with Tagga, [[Leaf]], and [[Terry]] at the [[Short Rest]]. Tagga is found upstairs and is looking well. She continues running supplies for the tavern.
- **S01E29:** Tagga has fully established herself as the domestic anchor of the Short Rest. She serves as cook at the tavern in Trollskull, experienced in cooking large game beasts and enthusiastic about preparing meals for visiting dragons. She has taken over the education of the [[Trollskull Alley Irregulars]], including the [[Cassalanter]] children, and they have all begun calling her "mom."

### Season 2

- **S02E05:** When SWOJ returns to Waterdeep after a mysterious three-year absence, they find Tagga bedridden at the [[Short Rest]] with a spore illness -- a shape-changing affliction that requires [[mothweed]] (a rare purple moss) for treatment. The mothweed grows only in the [[Cryptwood]] near the [[Sword Mountains]]. Finding a cure for Tagga becomes one of the party's primary motivations for venturing north.
- **S02E08:** During a montage sequence, SWOJ delivers the mothweed they gathered from the Cryptwood back to Tagga, curing her of the spore illness. Scoot is primarily driven by concern for his mother throughout this arc.
- **S02E38:** In the Season 2 finale, SWOJ returns to [[Trollskull Manor]] to find Tagga waiting alongside [[Simon]] (who has recently come home from sea), [[Lief]], [[Grim]], [[Aspell Wavesilver]], and others. Tagga organized the homecoming party. Her children Bennet and Janks are now teenagers -- Bennet is a [[Griffin Riders|Griffin Rider]] recruit and Janks is a wizard student. When asked about the [[Zhentarim]], Tagga winks knowingly, suggesting she has secret shadow skills and a hidden history she has never disclosed to SWOJ.

## Relationships

- **[[Scoot Sparkles]]** -- Tagga's son and the emotional core of their relationship. Scoot is fiercely protective of his mother, rushing to her side after the fireball and making her cure his top priority in Season 2. Tagga in turn anchors Scoot's life in Waterdeep.
- **[[Simon]]** -- Tagga's husband and Scoot's father. He works for the [[Waterdeep Navy]] and is frequently away at sea. He returns home by the Season 2 finale.
- **Bennet and Janks** -- Tagga's younger children (Scoot's siblings). By Season 2's end, Bennet is training as a Griffin Rider and Janks is studying wizardry.
- **[[Trollskull Alley Irregulars]]** -- The orphans who work at Trollskull Manor. Tagga took over their education and became a maternal figure to them; they call her "mom."
- **[[Leaf]]** -- The ghost of Trollskull Manor. He and Tagga share responsibility for managing the tavern during SWOJ's absences.
- **[[Pinker]]** -- The kenku who rescued Tagga from the Xanathar Guild, hiding her in the Honorable Knight statue tenements. A bond of gratitude connects them.
- **[[SWOJ]]** -- Tagga is the party's most constant civilian ally, providing a home base, meals, and emotional grounding throughout the campaign.
