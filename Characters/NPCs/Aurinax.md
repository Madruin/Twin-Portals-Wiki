# Aurinax

**Race:** Gold dragon (adult/elder)
**Also known as:** Oranax (transcription variant)
**Location:** [[Trollskull Manor]] rooftop (as of Season 2); formerly [[Vault Of Dragons]], then [[Castle Waterdeep]]
**Affiliation:** [[Way Of The Balanced Scales]], allies of [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]]

## Description

Aurinax is a gold dragon who spent centuries guarding [[Lord Neverember]]'s stolen hoard in the [[Vault Of Dragons]] beneath the [[Zadio City Music Hall]] in [[Waterdeep]]. In his humanoid guise he appears as a dwarf, and he can shapeshift between his dwarven form and his true massive gold dragon form at will. Aurinax is ancient, powerful, and measured in temperament -- slow to trust but fiercely loyal once his confidence is earned. He possesses innate spellcasting ability, including Remove Curse, and his gold dragon fire breath is devastating in combat. By the end of Season 2, he has settled into the role of a wise elder protector residing at [[Trollskull Manor]], watching over SWOJ and the dragons who roost on its roof.

## History

### Season 1: The Vault of Dragons

SWOJ first encountered Aurinax in [[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]], deep beneath the [[Zadio City Music Hall]] in the [[Vault Of Dragons]]. He was disguised as a dwarf, guarding the dragon hoard of 500,000 gold dragons that [[Dagult Neverember]] had embezzled from the city of [[Waterdeep]]. [[Mr. E]] broke the ice by offering Aurinax doses of his "dragon piss" elixir -- an alchemical vision-inducing compound -- and in return Aurinax traded him a giant ruby the size of an ostrich egg. This exchange, casual as it seemed, established a relationship of trust between Aurinax and SWOJ.

When [[Renaer Neverember]] arrived with a ledger proving the [[Cassalanter Villa|Cassalanters]]' embezzlement, Aurinax agreed to release the gold. As SWOJ loaded coins into a bag of holding, the [[Xanathar]] attacked with a force of bugbears. Aurinax transformed into his true gold dragon form for the first time, filling the theater with fire breath, crushing bugbears, and engaging the Xanathar directly. The massive battle also drew [[Manshoon]]'s simulacrum and [[Zhentarim]] lieutenants including [[Mookie Plush]] to the theater balcony. The chandelier eventually fell and took out Aurinax's remaining opponents, and [[Manshoon]] escaped in the chaos.

In [[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]], after SWOJ was freed from prison by [[Esvelle Wavesilver]] and infiltrated the [[Cassalanter Villa]] feast on Founders Day, [[Terry]] the telepathic horse brought dragon reinforcements. Aurinax and [[Melifarn]] (a bronze dragon) arrived flying over the villa, destroying the remaining imps with their breath weapons. Aurinax then transformed back into his dwarf form to follow SWOJ into the Cassalanter Villa basement.

In the Season 1 finale ([[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]]), Aurinax flew SWOJ alongside [[Zelifarn]] to [[Castle Waterdeep]] to meet the [[Masked Lords]]. There, Aurinax revealed he possessed the [[Dragon Staff of Ahghairon]], an ancient artifact that allowed dragons to bypass the dragon ward protecting [[Waterdeep]]. Rather than wield it himself -- acknowledging that as a dragon he could introduce old rivalries into the process -- Aurinax offered the staff to [[Mr. E]] as a neutral party. Mr. E accepted, becoming the city's unofficial dragon administrator. Aurinax stayed at [[Castle Waterdeep]], and [[Zelifarn]] began learning to polymorph from him so she could join SWOJ for dinner in humanoid form. [[Jarlaxle Baenre]] gave Aurinax a respectful bow before departing through the twin portals; Aurinax simply regarded him in silence.

### Season 2: Elder Protector at Trollskull Manor

By the Season 2 finale ([[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]), Aurinax had relocated to [[Trollskull Manor]], where he roosted on the rooftop alongside [[Melifarn]] and other visiting dragons. When SWOJ returned from breaking the Wall of the Faithless, [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] was gravely ill with a magical frog creature curse -- festering wounds with tadpole parasites growing inside them. Aurinax, in his full dragon form on the rooftop, took one look at Scoot, shapeshifted into his dwarven form, and diagnosed him immediately: "You are cursed, my friend." Using Remove Curse, Aurinax extracted four wriggling red tadpoles from Scoot's wounds, warning that if the curse progressed too far, victims might transform into frog creatures entirely.

Aurinax also served as a source of knowledge about the curse for the wider population, as SWOJ sought information from him about how people without a gold dragon at their disposal might heal from the same affliction.

During the God's Night celebration on the [[Trollskull Manor]] rooftop at the close of Season 2, Aurinax lit the ceremonial fire with his flame breath. As dawn rose over [[Waterdeep]], each member of SWOJ burned a meaningful item in that fire -- [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] burned Rowan Crooks trading cards, [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] burned his Jolly Gents outfit, and [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] threw in a Feywild mushroom, while [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] played her grandfather Mordecai's music.

## Relationships

- **[[Mr. E]]** -- Aurinax's closest bond among SWOJ. Mr. E earned his trust through the dragon piss trade and was entrusted with the [[Dragon Staff of Ahghairon]]. Aurinax is said to simply like Mr. E a lot, and that personal regard is the foundation of Mr. E's political influence as dragon administrator.
- **[[Zelifarn]]** -- A bronze dragon companion of SWOJ. Aurinax took on a mentorship role, teaching Zelifarn to polymorph so she could interact with SWOJ in humanoid form.
- **[[Melifarn]]** -- A bronze dragon ally who fought alongside Aurinax at the [[Cassalanter Villa]] and later roosted with him on the [[Trollskull Manor]] rooftop.
- **[[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]** -- Aurinax cured Scoot of a dangerous magical curse in the Season 2 finale, demonstrating both his healing power and his protective instinct toward SWOJ.
- **[[Terry]]** -- The telepathic horse who summoned Aurinax and [[Melifarn]] as reinforcements during the Founders Day battle.
- **[[Jarlaxle Baenre]]** -- The Bregan D'aerthe leader treated Aurinax with cautious respect; Aurinax regarded him with silent reserve.
- **[[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]]** -- By the end of Season 2, Aurinax has become a permanent protector and elder figure at the party's home base.
