# Quick Quill Quincy

**Quick Quill Quincy** is a member of the [[Jolly Gents]], the criminal organization operating out of [[Zhentil Keep]] in the [[Shadowfell]]. Named for his record-keeping duties, Quincy serves in a janitorial or maintenance capacity within the keep alongside [[Giggling Gus]].

**First appearance:** [[S02E22 - Episode Fifty Two LIVE (S02E22)]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode
**Faction affiliations:** [[Jolly Gents]]

## Description

Quincy is a rank-and-file [[Jolly Gents]] member stationed inside [[Zhentil Keep]]. His alliterative nickname -- "Quick Quill" -- suggests a role as a scribe or record-keeper within the organization. When [[Scoot Sparkles|SWOJ]] encounters him, he is mopping up mud in the lower levels of the keep's interior tower alongside [[Giggling Gus]], indicating he handles mundane upkeep tasks rather than combat or leadership duties.

## Role in the Story

During SWOJ's infiltration of [[Zhentil Keep]] in [[S02E22 - Episode Fifty Two LIVE (S02E22)|Episode 52]], the party enters the hollowed-out tower beyond [[The Bailey]] and faces a decision about how to ascend to the upper floors where [[Rowan Crooks]]' office lies. Below them is a churning garbage pit; above them, several stories of doorways. Behind them, the squeaking of mops signals the presence of Quincy and [[Giggling Gus]], cleaning up mud on the ground level.

The two Jolly Gents serve as background atmosphere -- part of the bustling activity within the keep that makes it feel like a living headquarters. SWOJ ultimately ignores them in favor of devising a plan to fly upward using a ladder and their enchanted rock, leaving Quincy and Gus to their mopping undisturbed.

## Relationships

- [[Giggling Gus]] -- Fellow [[Jolly Gents]] member; the two are seen working together on cleaning duty inside [[Zhentil Keep]]
- [[Rowan Crooks]] -- Serves under Crooks' operation at [[Zhentil Keep]], though likely without direct contact with the leadership
- [[Antediluvian Jake]] -- Nominally part of the same organization, though Jake's phylactery being held by Crooks suggests the true chain of command is complicated
