# Gorg Fallowfoot

Gorg Fallowfoot is a member of the [[City Watch]] serving as a crime scene investigator in [[Waterdeep]]. An enthusiastic fan of [[Silver Waves of Justice|SWOJ]], he encounters the party on [[Trollskull Alley]] while investigating the destruction at [[Corellon's Crown]] and eagerly asks for their autographs.

**First appearance:** [[S02E05 - Episode Thirty Five (S02E05)|S02E05]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode
**Faction affiliations:** [[City Watch]]

## Description

Gorg Fallowfoot is a City Watch guard who identifies himself as a crime scene investigator, though his self-important manner when announcing his title suggests he may be relatively new to the role or overstating his experience. He is starstruck upon meeting the members of SWOJ, whom he recognizes as the "heroes of Waterdeep." He wears a gauntlet that he eagerly peels back so the party can sign the leather underneath.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S02E05 - Episode Thirty Five (S02E05)|S02E05]]:** When SWOJ returns to [[Waterdeep]] after their three-year absence in the [[Shadowfell]], they find [[Trollskull Alley]] dramatically changed. While investigating the destruction at [[Corellon's Crown]], SWOJ encounters Gorg Fallowfoot, a City Watch crime scene investigator working the scene. Gorg is thrilled to meet the party, calling them the "heroes of Waterdeep" and noting that the city now has "new heroes" in the [[Doom Raiders]]. He excitedly asks for autographs, holding out his gauntlet for signatures. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] signs his gauntlet and he asks her to write "for Gorg" on it. He also asks Ylka to write new songs about SWOJ's adventures. When asked about his role, he puffs himself up and declares he is a crime scene investigator. SWOJ shares information about [[Fala Lefaliir]]'s kidnapping, and Gorg takes notes, promising to tell all the city guard that SWOJ is back.

## Relationships

- [[Ylka Gralhund]] -- Asked Ylka specifically to sign an autograph and dedicate it to him
