## Description

A nine-year-old half-orc boy who calls himself a great wizard. Carries a stick with a continual flame candle as his 'magic wand.' One of the three street urchins.

One of the three orphan children hired as the Trollskull Alley Irregulars.

One of the street urchins (Trollskull Trio). Present when they tell SWOJ about Squiddly's disappearance.

A teenager now, having grown up during the three-year gap. Helps manage the Short Rest tavern alongside Nat.


## Role in the Story

- **S01E05:** Meet three street urchin children: Nat (mute leader), Jenks (half-orc wizard wannabe), Squidly (tiefling with eye patch)
- **S01E22:** Nat and Jenks report that Squiddly has gone missing
- **S02E05:** Nat and Jenks have grown into teenagers helping manage the Short Rest
