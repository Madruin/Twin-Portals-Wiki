# Arcturia

Arcturia is one of [[Halaster Blackcloak]]'s six apprentices -- referred to as siblings -- and the sister of [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]. A powerful wizard of the School of Transmutation, she is a shapeshifter who can dissolve her form into swarms of butterflies and maggots, reform at will, and transmute other creatures into new shapes. She serves Halaster, whom she calls "the master" and describes as "the most powerful wizard in the multiverse."

Arcturia first appears as an unseen antagonist haunting the [[Spiral Cavern]] beneath the [[Sword Mountains]], and later resurfaces at the [[Wall Of The Faithless]] during the climax of Season 2, where she becomes a reluctant ally of [[Silver Waves of Justice|SWOJ]].

**First appearance:** [[S02E07 - Episode Thirty Seven (S02E07)|S02E07]]
**Total appearances:** 8 episodes

## Appearance

Arcturia's physical form is unstable, a side-effect of prolonged transmutation magic worked on herself. She typically appears wearing a yellow robe that "flickers and flutters and shifts into thousands of little butterflies and then back into a robe again." In her more humanoid shape she has "big iridescent wings and little bone spurs and kind of mottled grayish rainbow-ish skin." When agitated or silenced, the butterflies melt into maggots, and she collapses into a writhing pile of insects before reforming.

When Xae encounters her in a vision during [[S02E25 - Episode Fifty Five LIVE (S02E25)|S02E25]], Arcturia looks "less weird and butterfly-y" and instead resembles "a troubled elf woman," suggesting that her butterfly form is a magical alteration rather than her natural appearance.

On Xae's sibling info sheet, Arcturia is described as having "a shifting cloak of butterflies," being from the School of Transmutation, and being "perpetually exasperated."

## Personality

Arcturia is driven by loyalty to Halaster, though that loyalty appears to be enforced rather than freely given. She speaks mechanically at times -- "I must obey the master" -- and seems to wrestle internally with Halaster's control. She shows flashes of genuine emotion, especially toward Xae, and harbours regret over the pain she and the other siblings have caused. In a vision she murmurs, "How much power is too much power?" and "Sometimes I wish I could just forget all this, all the pain we've caused."

She is quick-tempered and dangerous when threatened. She cast disintegrate on [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] mid-negotiation at the Wall, and flung statues with telekinesis when pressed. Yet she also seems to long for freedom from Halaster's influence, asking Xae, "Can you teach me how to do it?" when she learns Xae broke free.

Once freed from Halaster's puppeteering, Arcturia becomes ambivalent about breaking the [[Wall Of The Faithless]]. After initially pursuing that mission at Halaster's command, she tells SWOJ that she thinks they "shouldn't break the wall" and that doing so would "release a lot of powerful magic into the world" and "could be pretty dangerous." She still possesses part of [[Rowan Crooks]]' soul, which she offers as a strategic asset against [[Manshoon]].

## Abilities

- **Transmutation magic** -- Her primary school. She transforms captives into beasts (turning [[Leah]] into a six-eyed creature, druid [[Shala]] into a badger, and [[Ander]] into a talking riddle door). She also maintains a self-transmutation that allows her to shift between humanoid form and a swarm of butterflies/maggots.
- **Disintegrate** -- Cast on [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] at the Wall of the Faithless for roughly 80 damage.
- **Telekinesis** -- Used to hold [[Zelifarn|Farron/Zelifarn]] in the air and to hurl statues at enemies.
- **Melf's Acid Arrow** -- Fired at [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] during the Wall encounter (missed).
- **Memory-altering mist** -- The mist blanketing the [[Kryptwood]] that scrambled memories originated from an enchanted mirror in the Spiral Cavern under her control.
- **Misty Step** -- Used to escape grapples and reposition.
- **Shapeshifting resistance** -- She shrugged off shatter damage, absorbed bludgeoning blows by flattening and reforming her body, and broke out of polymorph (chipmunk) almost immediately.
- **Concord Jewel** -- A large crystalline vessel she used to escape the Spiral Cavern. The butterflies streamed into the crystal, which lifted off the ground and floated away.
- **Portable Hole** -- She carries a portable hole that contained part of [[Rowan Crooks]]' soul stone. [[Manshoon]] later searched it during the battle at the [[Radiant Citadel (Bottom)|bottom of the Radiant Citadel]].

## Background

Arcturia is one of six siblings bound to [[Halaster Blackcloak]]. In [[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]], Halaster presents Xae with a document listing the six names -- [[Jhesiyra Kestellharp|Jhesiyra]], Rantantar, Mayrin, [[Trobriand]], [[Muiral]], and Arcturia -- along with an oath: "I swear to enchackle their souls by 13 Founders Days hence." Xae is the seventh, the one who escaped.

Arcturia and Xae share a deep bond. Xae has had dreams since Season 1 of "a sisterly figure who accompanied you closer than your other siblings." When Xae saw the butterflies in the Spiral Cavern, the memory struck a chord, and she immediately pursued them saying, "Hey guys, let's go find my sister."

## Role in the Story

### Season 1

- **[[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]]:** Arcturia is first named alongside the other five siblings when Halaster presents Xae with a list of names and a soul-binding oath. She does not appear in person.

### Season 2 -- The Spiral Cavern (S02E07-S02E08)

- **[[S02E07 - Episode Thirty Seven (S02E07)|S02E07]]:** SWOJ enters the [[Spiral Cavern]] beneath the [[Sword Mountains]] following [[Zelifarn|Farron]]. Arcturia is the source of the memory-altering mist flowing from an enchanted mirror deep in the cavern. She has transformed captives from the area into creatures -- [[Leah]] into a six-eyed beast, [[Shala]] into a badger, [[Ander]] into a riddle door -- and holds Farron (actually [[Zelifarn]] under enchantment) telekinetically in her laboratory.
- **S02E07:** SWOJ fights Arcturia in the bowl of the Spiral Cavern. She taunts Xae ("Do you like my little laboratory? It has been a pleasant project. Not like our wonders down below, of course, Sayrien. Not like the worlds we created.") and uses Constitution-draining magic on her. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] smashes the enchanted mirror with her Song Sword, breaking many of the illusions and freeing [[Zelifarn]], who transforms back into an adult-sized dragon.
- **S02E07:** Arcturia tells Xae, "I will help the master remember," and dissolves into butterflies. She retreats into the [[Concord Jewel]] -- a massive glowing crystal vessel -- which lifts off and floats away. Xae follows her briefly into the crystal and hears her whisper: "Follow me. Break the wall."
- **[[S02E08 - Episode Thirty Eight (S02E08)|S02E08]]:** [[Lark|Malark "Lark" Wavesilver]] mentions Arcturia by name while reporting to investigators in [[Waterdeep]], noting that the butterfly-being from the cavern was named Arcturia and expressing concern about the link SWOJ still has to her.

### Season 2 -- Return to the Spiral Cavern (S02E19)

- **[[S02E19 - Episode Forty Nine (S02E19)|S02E19]]:** SWOJ returns to the [[Spiral Cavern]] near [[Amphail]] as part of a war-council mission to retrieve crystals for [[Melifarn]]'s ritual. The party passes through "the first room where you encountered Arcturia as a sister" and revisits the overlook where they fought her and the Mirror. Arcturia is not present, but the location is still associated with her.

### Season 2 -- Halaster's Vision (S02E25)

- **[[S02E25 - Episode Fifty Five LIVE (S02E25)|S02E25]]:** During a vision/flashback inside Halaster's sanctum, Xae sees Arcturia among the siblings. She "looks less like weird and butterfly-y now" and resembles "a troubled elf woman." She mutters "How much power is too much power?" under her breath, questioning what Halaster is asking them to do. Later in the vision, Arcturia says, "Sometimes I wish I could just forget all this, all the pain we've caused." These moments suggest she has harboured doubts about Halaster's plans for a long time.

### Season 2 -- The Wall of the Faithless (S02E35-S02E36)

- **[[S02E35 - Episode Sixty Five (S02E35)|S02E35]]:** Arcturia summons SWOJ to the surface of the [[Wall Of The Faithless]]. She appears in her yellow butterfly-robe, greeting Xae: "Sister, you finally made it. Welcome, friends." Around her are craters and strange amalgam statues -- evidence of her experimentation.
- **S02E35:** She proposes breaking the Wall but SWOJ is wary. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] remembers "things going not super great last time we met Arcturia." When the party hesitates, her butterflies melt into maggots. Xae tells her that Halaster is not doing what is best for them, and Arcturia asks, "Is that why you left? ... Can you teach me how to do it?"
- **S02E35:** Xae rolls persuasion to convince Arcturia to join them, initially rolling a natural 1 (rerolled via Halfling Luck). After multiple rounds of persuasion checks, Scoot tries to snap her out of Halaster's control with a bop to the head. She responds mechanically -- "I must obey the master" -- and casts disintegrate on Scoot for roughly 80 damage (he survives). She continues attacking puppetlike, firing Melf's Acid Arrow at Ylka and telekinetically hurling statues.
- **S02E35:** SWOJ persists. Ylka casts a major image of Xae and Arcturia playing together as young sisters. The combined persuasion rolls (27, 26, 25) break through Halaster's hold. Arcturia "pulls as if there's some sort of veil or mask" from her head, becomes solid again, and embraces Xae. She says, "I remember. I want to tell you about these memories. I want you to remember too."
- **S02E35:** Freed from Halaster's direct control, Arcturia tells SWOJ she does not think they actually need to break the Wall -- it would be dangerous -- but they still must stop [[Manshoon]]. She reveals she has part of [[Rowan Crooks]]' soul stone and lays out three possible plans: assault the soul pylon directly, perform an ancient ritual to summon [[Kelemvor]], or petition the imprisoned souls of the Wall to rise up.
- **[[S02E36 - Episode Sixty Six (S02E36)|S02E36]]:** Arcturia performs her chosen ritual at the foot of [[Kelemvor's Skeleton]] on the Wall, carving two large circles below the god's silver scales. She explains that Manshoon is draining souls from the Wall into his pylon and will grow as strong as a god if unchecked. The ritual is interrupted by a green wave of [[Manshoon]]'s crystal-hearted spectral forces, but SWOJ defends her position. The ritual completes, raising an army of ancient souls -- blue and white spectres in armor from forgotten civilizations -- led by an orc woman warrior ghost. The army marches up the [[Crystal Spire]] to the [[Radiant Citadel]].
- **S02E36:** [[Manshoon]] searches Arcturia's portable hole for the soul stone of [[Rowan Crooks]] during the final battle.
- **S02E36:** When [[Kelemvor]] appears and asks SWOJ to vote on whether to break or preserve the Wall, Arcturia (now freed from Halaster) believes they should not break it, saying it would be dangerous. Xae reports that "Arcturia thinks that she has a chance to be freed, she said that we shouldn't break the wall."

### Season 2 Finale (S02E38)

- **[[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]:** After the climactic events at the Wall and the defeat of Manshoon, Xae looks around for Arcturia but cannot find her. Her whereabouts at the end of Season 2 are unknown.

## Relationships

- **[[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]** -- Sister and fellow apprentice of Halaster. Arcturia and Xae share the closest bond among the siblings. Arcturia calls her "Sayrien" (an older name) and recalls worlds they created together. Xae's persistence and love are ultimately what breaks Halaster's hold on Arcturia.
- **[[Halaster Blackcloak]]** -- Her master. She serves him under what appears to be magical compulsion. She refers to him as "the most powerful wizard in the multiverse." Once freed, she expresses fear of returning to Waterdeep because of "what he might do or say and about what kind of effect he might have on me."
- **[[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]** -- Antagonistic at first. She cast disintegrate on him during negotiations. Scoot, characteristically, took the hit, healed up, and kept trying to help.
- **[[Zelifarn]]** -- She held him captive in the Spiral Cavern, disguising him as the human "Farron" under enchantment.
- **Siblings** -- Part of a group of six Halaster apprentices: Jhesiyra, Rantantar, Mayrin, [[Trobriand]], [[Muiral]], and Arcturia. Xae is the seventh who escaped.

## Key Locations

- [[Spiral Cavern]] -- Her laboratory beneath the [[Sword Mountains]], where she kept an enchanted mirror that produced memory-altering mist across the [[Kryptwood]].
- [[Wall Of The Faithless]] -- Where she worked on experiments and eventually performed the ritual to raise an army of souls.
- [[Kelemvor's Skeleton]] -- The site of her ritual on the Wall.
