# Mellafarne

**Race:** Bronze Dragon (ancient)
**Also known as:** Melifarn, Mellifarne, Melopharn, Melafarn
**Affiliations:** [[Way Of The Balanced Scales]] (co-founder), [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] (ally), [[Deep Dragon Temple]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E25 - Episode Twenty Five|S01E25]]
**Total appearances:** 8 episodes

---

## Description

Mellafarne is an ancient bronze dragon, an ancestor or relative of [[Zelifarn]], and one of the most powerful allies [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] encounters across both seasons of the campaign. She first appeared five hundred years in the past during SWOJ's vision of 992 DR [[Waterdeep]], where she fought alongside the party as a young dragon in the [[Vault Of Dragons]]. In the present day, Mellafarne resides at the [[Deep Dragon Temple]], a site sacred to bronze dragons and historically connected to the ancient war between dragons and aboleth. She is a dragon of immense age and knowledge, capable of performing complex arcane rituals and wielding devastating lightning breath that crackles between her teeth when she grins.

Mellafarne's story intersects with SWOJ's at critical junctures. She befriended [[Mystery]] after rescuing him from a shipwreck and brought him to live at the [[Deep Dragon Temple]], where they became close companions. When the [[Breathless Elder]]'s enchantment spread across the bronze dragon lineage, Mellafarne fell into a psychic coma -- the same affliction that later struck [[Zelifarn]]. She communicated with [[Mystery]] through shared dreams, urging him to "get help, find the temple." After SWOJ destroyed the [[Breathless Elder]], Mellafarne awoke and immediately set about clearing the temple of the aberrant creatures that had infested it, destroying thousands of whistler larvae in the underground lake with her lightning breath. She then rallied a flight of dragons and co-founded the [[Way Of The Balanced Scales]] alongside [[Mystery]], becoming a central figure in the campaign's dragon alliance.

Mellafarne's most consequential role came at [[Neverwinter]], where she devised and performed the ritual to seal the chasm threatening to destroy the city. Using the [[Crown Of Never]] channeled through crystals recovered from [[Amphail]], she closed the rift and saved the city -- an act that cemented her status as one of the Sword Coast's great protectors.

## Role in the Story

### Season 1: The Vault of Dragons

- **[[S01E25 - Episode Twenty Five|S01E25]]:** When the [[Stone Of Golorr]] transports SWOJ's consciousness five hundred years into the past to 992 DR [[Waterdeep]], they discover the [[Vault Of Dragons]] beneath [[Zadio Neverember]]'s amphitheater. There they encounter Mellafarne as a young bronze dragon fighting [[black puddings]] in the vault. She allies with SWOJ during their battle against the aboleth that the Stone of Golorr transforms into when it gets wet. During the fight, [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] -- ensorcelled by the aboleth -- turns on Mellafarne with vicious mockery, calling her "an overgrown salamander." Mellafarne recognizes the ensorcellement and cries out "They're enslaved! Quick, snap them out of it!" before charging into the fray. She shrugs off the insult gracefully afterward: "It's okay! These things happen!"

- **[[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]]:** During the climactic battle at the [[Cassalanter Villa]] on Founders Day, [[Terry]] brings reinforcements. Mellafarne and [[Aurinax]] arrive flying over the villa as two streaks of light illuminated by fireworks, unleashing lightning and fire breath that incinerates the remaining imps swarming the sky.

### Season 2: The Enchanted Sleep and Awakening

- **[[S02E09 - Episode Thirty Nine (S02E09)|S02E09]]:** After [[Zelifarn]] collapses into a psychic coma at the [[Radiant Citadel]], [[Mystery]] explains to SWOJ that he was living at the [[Deep Dragon Temple]] with Mellafarne before being transported to them. Mellafarne had saved him from a shipwreck and taken him to the temple, where they became companions. But then things "started to get weird" -- Mellafarne fell into the same enchanted sleep afflicting [[Zelifarn]] and other bronze dragons. In their shared dreams, she told [[Mystery]] to "get help and find the temple." SWOJ recognizes Mellafarne as the same dragon they met five hundred years ago, confirming that she has lived for centuries and defeated aboleth at the temple in the distant past.

- **[[S02E11 - Episode Forty One (S02E11)|S02E11]]:** SWOJ arrives at the [[Deep Dragon Temple]] and climbs the cliff face through green mist. At the summit, they discover Mellafarne lying asleep on a massive stone plateau -- a bronze dragon afflicted by the [[Breathless Elder]]'s enchantment, one of many bronze dragons trapped in this psychic coma across the realms.

- **[[S02E19 - Episode Forty Nine (S02E19)|S02E19]]:** After SWOJ destroys the [[Breathless Elder]], Mellafarne awakens. She and [[Mystery]] destroy thousands of aberrant larvae in the lake at the base of the [[Deep Dragon Temple]], then rally other bronze dragons who have also recovered. Together with [[Mystery]], she co-founds the [[Way Of The Balanced Scales]] -- a dragon alliance -- and leads a flight of dragons ([[Jack]], [[Jill]], [[Chip]], and [[Dale]]) north to [[Neverwinter]]. There she reunites with SWOJ and explains that she can seal the [[Neverwinter Chasm]] using the [[Crown Of Never]] combined with crystals from the [[Spiral Cavern]] in [[Amphail]]. She sends SWOJ to retrieve the crystals while she prepares the ritual site, digging tunnels around the chasm with [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] and [[Shaleheart]].

- **[[S02E20 - Episode Fifty (S02E20)|S02E20]]:** Mellafarne reveals she discovered a green portal stone -- connected to the [[Jolly Gents]] -- at the underground lake near the ritual site, where the bones of the aboleth elder SWOJ fought five hundred years ago still lay. At a final war council, the multi-front threats facing the Sword Coast are reviewed. Mellafarne teaches the ritual to [[Dagult Neverember]] and [[Renaer Neverember]], who agree to share the responsibility of wearing the [[Crown Of Never]]. The ritual requires someone to return to the chasm every full moon to renew the seal. With the crystals placed around the chasm and the Neverembers trading the crown back and forth, Mellafarne channels its power through the crystals -- and the chasm closes, saving [[Neverwinter]].

### Season 2: Continued Alliance

- **[[S02E31 - Episode Sixty One (S02E31)|S02E31]]:** [[Zardoz Zord]] departs [[Neverwinter]] for [[Waterdeep]] via Mellafarne, who serves as his transport. He carries messages to the [[Masked Lords]], [[Blackstaff Tower]], and the [[Griffin Riders]] requesting military reinforcements against the [[Spiral Cavern]] forces.

- **[[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]:** In the Season 2 finale, Mellafarne is seen perched on the rooftop of [[Trollskull Manor]] alongside [[Aurinax]], both dragons having settled in at SWOJ's home. She is present at the God's Night homecoming celebration in her human form -- at peace, among friends, in a world finally freed from the [[Breathless Elder]]'s shadow.

## Relationships

- **[[Zelifarn]]** — Ancestor or relative. Both are bronze dragons, and both were afflicted by the [[Breathless Elder]]'s enchanted sleep. Mellafarne's presence at the [[Deep Dragon Temple]] and [[Zelifarn]]'s spiritual connection to the place (one of the twin portals led to an echo of his lair) confirm a deep lineage bond. By the Season 2 epilogue, both dragons roost together at [[Trollskull Manor]].
- **[[Mystery]]** — Close companion. Mellafarne rescued [[Mystery]] from a shipwreck and brought him to the [[Deep Dragon Temple]], where they lived together. When she fell into enchanted sleep, she communicated with him through shared dreams. After her awakening, they co-founded the [[Way Of The Balanced Scales]] and led the dragon flight together.
- **[[Aurinax]]** — Fellow dragon ally of SWOJ. The two fought together at the [[Cassalanter Villa]] in Season 1 and share the roost at [[Trollskull Manor]] by the Season 2 finale.
- **[[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]** — Worked together digging tunnels beneath [[Neverwinter]] with [[Shaleheart]] in preparation for the chasm-sealing ritual.
- **[[Shaleheart]]** — SWOJ's bulette companion who helped Mellafarne and [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] dig the ritual tunnels at [[Neverwinter]].
- **[[Dagult Neverember]] / [[Renaer Neverember]]** — Mellafarne taught them the [[Crown Of Never]] ritual to seal the chasm, a responsibility they share every full moon.
- **[[Zardoz Zord]]** — Served as his transport from [[Neverwinter]] to [[Waterdeep]] to carry messages requesting reinforcements.

## Key Items and Locations

- **[[Deep Dragon Temple]]** — Mellafarne's home and a site sacred to bronze dragons. Location of the twin portals and the underground lake where she destroyed thousands of aberrant larvae.
- **[[Crown Of Never]]** — The ancient royal crown of [[Neverwinter]]. Mellafarne devised and led the ritual using this crown and crystals from [[Amphail]] to seal the [[Neverwinter Chasm]].
- **[[Way Of The Balanced Scales]]** — The dragon alliance co-founded by Mellafarne and [[Mystery]], including dragons [[Jack]], [[Jill]], [[Chip]], and [[Dale]].
