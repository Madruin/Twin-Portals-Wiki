# Lyrian

Lyrian is a crystal unicorn spirit bound to [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]'s pendant. She first communicates with the party through dreams and visions, eventually revealing her name when Xae awakens at [[Remallia Haventree's Estate]]. She creates portals, projects magical shields, and serves as a guide and protector for SWOJ. During the Xanathar lair collapse, she teleports the surviving party to safety. Later, she is corrupted by [[Malar]] in the Feywild but restored by Xae using purified water and moonlight. By the Season 2 finale, she carries [[Laeral Silverhand]] into battle.

**First appearance:** [[S01E16 - Episode Sixteen|S01E16]] (reveals name)
**Total appearances:** 4 episodes

## Description

Lyrian manifests as a crystal unicorn -- a small figurine pendant that Xae carries around her neck. When active, she communicates telepathically and can project portals (green-hued), magical shields, and bursts of rainbow light. In her physical form in the Feywild, she appears as a white unicorn whose corruption by Malar temporarily turns her fur black and her horn into a source of red energy.

## Role in the Story

- **S01E16:** The crystal unicorn pendant reveals its name is Lyrian to Xae. Creates a green portal that teleports Xae from Remallia Haventree's estate to Trollskull Manor
- **S01E17:** Projects a magical shield that protects the party from the Xanathar lair collapse. Guides them through tunnels. Calls 'Gather around me' and teleports survivors to Trollskull Manor in rainbow light
- **S01E21:** Recap: Two months have passed since Lark's death. Xae vanished into the Fey with Lyrian, Scoot stayed at the Short Rest with Lark's urn, Ylka found a necromancer via scrying pool
- **S01E21:** The hag reveals she plucked Lark from Kelemvor's grasp and gave him to Malar as bait to trap Lyrian
- **S01E21:** Lyrian approaches in a trance and eats corrupted ash berries from the werewolf
- **S01E21:** Scoot kills the corrupted wolf-man but Lyrian gores him and transforms him into a wolf under her control
- **S01E21:** Xae (as giant goat) rams Lyrian into the pool, reverts, and pours the purified water -- moonlight strikes and restores Lyrian
- **S02E38:** Twin portals appear on the rooftop: a teal-green one with an aboleth tendril and a rose-gold one through which Laeral Silverhand arrives riding the unicorn Lyrian, road-worn and armed
