# Pog Waggletop

Goblin captain of the [[Sea Plum]] and devoted pigeon keeper who became a permanent resident of [[Trollskull Manor]].

**Race:** Goblin
**First appearance:** [[S01E06 - Episode Six|S01E06]]
**Total appearances:** 10 episodes
**Faction affiliations:** [[Sea Plum Crew]]

## Description

Pog Waggletop is a small goblin who served as captain of the ship [[Sea Plum]], though he was famously hands-off in that role -- spending far more time tending to his pigeons and sending messages than actually captaining. The day-to-day running of the ship fell mostly to his first mate, [[Gildis]]. When first encountered at the [[Sea Maiden's Faire]], Pog wore a trench coat covered in pockets that visibly flapped with living birds inside, and had a parrot perched on his shoulder.

After the Sea Plum sank under mysterious circumstances in [[Deepwater Harbor]], Pog and the surviving crew relocated to [[Trollskull Manor]], where he settled in as a permanent resident. He established a pigeon roost on the roof of the manor and became the de facto keeper of birds for the [[Silver Waves of Justice|SWOJ]]. He is fiercely protective of his pigeons and deeply devoted to [[Sally]], his most notable bird, who served as a messenger pigeon even before the crew came ashore.

Pog is a homebody at heart -- when [[Trollskull Manor]] was renovated and reopened as the [[Short Rest|Short Rest tavern]], he stayed on to help run the place alongside [[Tagga|Taga]] (Scoot's mother). He refuses to allow cats into the building out of concern for his birds, once turning away [[Leah]]'s cat Mr. Fluffstopples at the door. He is enthusiastic about the idea of building a proper rookery and aviary at the manor, and by the Season 1 finale, he had become a fixture on the roof -- described as having "a green exclamation mark above his head," always ready with a quest or a message.

During the fireball explosion that devastated [[Trollskull Alley]], most of Pog's pigeons were killed. He was devastated -- weeping profusely and clawing at his face in grief when he saw the dead birds being swept into a bin. [[Sally]] survived the blast, badly burned on one side, and was rescued by [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] and returned to Pog, who vowed to build a memorial to the fallen pigeons and grow a new flock starting with Sally.

When SWOJ returned to Waterdeep after three years in the Shadowfell, Pog and Taga were absent from the Short Rest's main floor, having retreated upstairs -- a notable change from their usual presence. Scoot asked after both of them immediately upon arriving, a sign of how integral Pog had become to the household.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E06 - Episode Six|S01E06]]:** Pog sends a pigeon message to [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] reporting that the [[Sea Plum]] has sunk in [[Deepwater Harbor]] under mysterious circumstances. The party travels to the [[Sea Maiden's Faire]] on the docks where they find Pog among his wet, depressed-looking crew. He is described as a small goblin in a trench coat with flapping pockets full of birds and a parrot on his shoulder. He greets Scoot warmly and introduces them to [[Zardoz Zord]]. Pog is desperate to know what happened to the crew, especially his first mate [[Gildis]], and feels extremely guilty that Gildis has gone missing. He identifies [[Sally]] as the pigeon who delivered the message.
- **[[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]:** [[Sally]] the pigeon is discovered roosting on the roof of [[Trollskull Manor]], having followed the party home from the Sea Plum. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] uses Speak with Animals to converse with Sally, who is cantankerous but agrees to stay. The party discusses getting Pog an aviary at the manor, noting he would be better suited to pigeon-keeping than captaining a ship. Sally reports on vermin activity in the building.
- **[[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]:** The [[Sea Plum Crew]] arrives at [[Trollskull Manor]], including Pog and [[Simon]] (Scoot's father). Pog has come to check on Sally and is glad she has moved into the roof. He wears his big coat, visibly containing birds. [[Leah]] mentions that Pog turned her away when she tried to bring her cat Mr. Fluffstopples to the manor, saying "this goblin fellow" had a good reason not to allow cats in the building. Pog enthusiastically suggests building a rookery when he overhears the idea. Lif tells him to shut up. Later in the evening, most of the Sea Plum crew leave to find other lodging, but Pog stays behind at the manor.
- **[[S01E09 - Episode Nine|S01E09]]:** Pog helps prepare brunch at [[Trollskull Manor]] alongside [[Tagga|Taga]], making steak and fresh berries on a beautiful cloudy morning as the manor prepares for the day.
- **[[S01E10 - Episode Ten|S01E10]]:** Pog is mentioned briefly in a passing reference -- a player suggests bringing him along for an investigation.
- **[[S01E12 - Episode Twelve|S01E12]]:** In the aftermath of the fireball explosion at [[Trollskull Alley]], Pog comes shuffling out of the blasted-open doors of [[Trollskull Manor]] asking for Sally. When he sees her alive (though badly burned), he is relieved, but then notices the other dead pigeons being swept into a bin and breaks down -- weeping profusely and clawing at his face in grief. [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] consoles him, promising that Pog will create a lovely memorial to the fallen pigeons and grow another family starting with Sally. Pog takes Sally and thanks the party. Before SWOJ leaves to investigate, Scoot tells Pog to let everyone inside know they are heading out. Pog agrees to hold down the fort.
- **[[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four|S01E24]]:** Pog and [[Tagga|Taga]] are inside the main floor of the [[Short Rest|Short Rest tavern]] on the morning of the first of Tarsakh, wringing their hands and wondering what to do about a large crowd that has gathered outside the doors before the inn has opened.
- **[[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]]:** During the Season 1 finale downtime montage, Pog is described as a permanent fixture on the roof of the renovated [[Trollskull Manor]], staying up there "all the time with the green exclamation mark above his head" -- a video game reference to his constant readiness with messages and tasks. [[Sally]] has meanwhile expanded her influence over the city's pigeon population, with her birds now looking "a little bit tough" as she extends her territory.
- **[[S02E05 - Episode Thirty Five (S02E05)|S02E05]]:** When SWOJ returns to Waterdeep after three years in the Shadowfell, there is no sign of Taga or Pog on the main floor of the Short Rest. Scoot immediately asks [[Lif]] where Taga and Pog are, and Lif points upstairs toward Taga's bedroom. Sally's roost is still visible on the roof with birds coming and going, though Sally herself is not spotted.
- **[[S02E25 - Episode Fifty Five LIVE (S02E25)|S02E25]]:** During a dream sequence orchestrated by the [[The Raven Queen|Raven Queen]], SWOJ finds themselves aboard the [[Sea Plum]] in a peaceful vision. Pog appears at the helm, barking orders -- a surreal sight that surprises the party, with one member exclaiming "Pog's dead!" before realizing he is right there. The dream Pog is alive and well, commanding his ship as it should have been.

## Relationships

- **[[Sally]]** -- Pog's most prized pigeon. Sally originally served as a messenger bird for the Sea Plum and later roosted at Trollskull Manor on her own. When Sally survived the fireball explosion, Pog was overjoyed and vowed to rebuild his flock around her. Sally became an increasingly powerful figure in her own right, eventually commanding a network of pigeons across Waterdeep.
- **[[Simon]]** -- Scoot's father, who arrived at Trollskull Manor alongside Pog as part of the Sea Plum crew.
- **[[Tagga|Taga]]** -- Scoot's mother. Pog and Taga worked together as the primary caretakers of Trollskull Manor, cooking meals and managing the household while SWOJ was away on adventures.
- **[[Scoot Sparkles]]** -- Pog's former crewmate on the Sea Plum. Scoot is the member of SWOJ closest to Pog, greeting him warmly when reuniting and consoling him after the pigeon deaths. Scoot asks after Pog by name when returning to the Short Rest.
- **[[Gildis]]** -- First mate of the Sea Plum, who handled the actual captaining duties. Pog felt extreme guilt when Gildis went missing after the ship sank.
- **[[Leah]]** -- Fellow Sea Plum crew member and Emerald Enclave contact. Pog turned away her cat to protect his birds, and she was mildly exasperated but respected his reasoning.
- **[[Lif Lindgren|Lif]]** -- The ghost of Trollskull Manor. Lif tells Pog to shut up when Pog gets overexcited about the rookery idea, suggesting a familiar if sometimes testy cohabitation.
- **[[Sea Plum Crew]]** -- Pog was captain of the crew in name, though his hands-off style meant others did most of the work. After the ship sank, the crew scattered, but Pog remained at Trollskull Manor.
