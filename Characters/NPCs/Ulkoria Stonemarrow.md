# Ulkoria Stonemarrow

Ulkoria Stonemarrow is an elder shield dwarf magister of the [[Watchful Order Of Magisters|Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors]], specializing in abjuration magic. A centuries-old fixture of the [[North Ward]], she serves as [[Waterdeep]]'s frontline magical authority -- registering magic users, warding buildings, investigating magical crimes, and coordinating with the [[City Watch]] on arcane threats.

**First appearance:** [[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]] (mentioned)
**Total appearances:** 5 episodes
**Faction affiliations:** [[Watchful Order Of Magisters|Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors]]

## Description

Ulkoria Stonemarrow is an older dwarf woman described as wise, poised, and stoic, with a dour and businesslike manner that occasionally cracks into dry humor. She wears the gray robes of the [[Watchful Order Of Magisters|Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors]] and is always accompanied by her shield guardian [[Winston]], a ten-foot-tall magical construct that stands watch with its arms crossed while she works.

Ulkoria's magical specialty is abjuration -- the school of defensive and protective magic. She routinely carves glyphs of warding into buildings across the [[North Ward]] and can teleport short distances. She formerly owned [[Trollskull Manor]] roughly 300 years before SWOJ took possession of it, and she has kept a watchful eye on the property ever since, returning annually to check on the spiritual activity tied to [[Esther|the hag Esther]].

Her office is located on the [[High Road]] at the Watchful Order's [[North Ward]] post. She maintains a network of contacts among the local guilds and businesses of [[Trollskull Alley]], and she is well-acquainted with the neighborhood's residents. Despite her stern professionalism, Ulkoria is sympathetic to SWOJ's mission and offers practical help at every turn -- from guild contacts to crime scene cooperation to the construction of a teleportation circle in [[Trollskull Manor]]'s basement.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]]:** [[Captain Hyustus Stagget]] advises SWOJ to register their magic users with the [[Watchful Order Of Magisters|Watchful Order of Magisters]]. Ulkoria Stonemarrow is mentioned as a shield dwarf magistar in the [[North Ward]].

- **[[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]:** Ulkoria visits [[Trollskull Manor]] with her shield guardian [[Winston]]. She has already carved a glyph of warding onto the front door before SWOJ arrives, explaining that she periodically checks on the manor due to its history with [[Esther|the hag Esther]]. She reveals she once owned the establishment roughly 300 years ago. Inside the tavern, she registers [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] and [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] as magic users, noting Ylka's unusual decomposing-but-not-undead condition with professional curiosity. Ulkoria takes a deposition from Ylka about an unauthorized use of charm magic on a [[Wavesilver Family|Wavesilver]] servant. She shares intelligence about [[Xanathar Guild]] activity, including the use of [[Nihiloor|mind flayers]] and intellect devourers to infiltrate Waterdeep's upper echelons, and reports of doppelganger activity concentrated in the Castle Ward. She offers to connect SWOJ with local guilds -- the Splendid Order of Carpenters, the Fellowship of Innkeepers, the Steelers and Brewers Guilds, and the Guild of Butchers -- to help restore the manor. When departing, she teleports outside and calls [[Winston]] to accompany her back down the alley.

- **[[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]:** After a devastating fireball explosion rocks [[Trollskull Alley]] during the grand opening of [[Trollskull Alley The Short Rest|the Short Rest]], Ulkoria arrives as the lead magistrate to investigate the crime scene. She works alongside a [[City Watch]] sergeant, coordinating guards to cordon off the alley, inspecting the blast area, and taking depositions from witnesses and SWOJ. Her cold, efficient demeanor contrasts with the emotional chaos of the aftermath.

- **[[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]]:** After SWOJ defeats the [[Cassalanters]] in the crypt beneath [[Cassalanter Villa]], the [[City Watch]] arrives with Ulkoria coordinating the cleanup operation. When [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] warns her that members of the city watch were secretly working for the [[Cassalanters]], she assures SWOJ that her crew are trustworthy -- "They're from my district" -- and promises to sweep the city watch ranks for Cassalanter agents using their financial ledgers. She helps arrange the secure transport of the recovered gold to [[Castle Waterdeep]], working with [[Aurinax]] to move it via the bag of holding.

- **[[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]:** During SWOJ's extended absence from Waterdeep, Ulkoria completed the construction of a permanent teleportation circle in the basement of [[Trollskull Manor]], connecting it to the [[Shard of the Moon]]. The party discovers this upon their homecoming.
