# Craw

A giant scarab beetle who became a loyal companion of [[Scoot Sparkles]] and [[SWOJ]] after being rescued from the [[Dynamo Of Possibility]], and who later served as a mount for the [[Circle Of Dusk]] druids near [[Amphail]].

**First appearance:** [[S02E02 - Episode Thirty Two (S02E02)]]
**Total appearances:** 7 episodes

## Description

Craw is a horse-sized (medium) giant scarab beetle, originally one of a pair of scarabs -- Craw and [[Gaw]] -- kept at the [[Shadow Way Inn]] in the [[Shadowfell]]. The beetles were fitted with two-person saddles and used as mounts. Despite their terrifying appearance, with pincers that open and shut, both scarabs were described as very friendly. Craw was the calmer and more determined of the two, while Gaw was more skittish but still cooperative.

Craw cannot fly despite being a true scarab. He is affectionate and dog-like in temperament, pressing in for scratches and sometimes bowling people over with enthusiasm. [[Scoot Sparkles]] in particular shares a deep bond with Craw, having rolled well on an early animal handling check that established their connection. Craw responds to Scoot's voice and seeks out physical affection in the form of "scarab licks" -- described as mandible nuzzles.

After a three-year time displacement, Craw appeared noticeably older and more "creaky," having been used as a pack animal at [[The Short Rest]] during the intervening years. By the time of the Amphail campaign, Craw was also described as a "giant stag beetle," suggesting his appearance may have shifted somewhat over time or the terms were used interchangeably.

## Role in the Story

- **S02E02:** [[Fritz]], a staff member at the [[Shadow Way Inn]], entrusts SWOJ with two giant scarab beetles named Craw and Gaw, fitted with two-person saddles. The party rides them toward the [[Beast Of Erua]], climbing one of its massive legs. Craw is described as "very determined and calm." [[Scoot Sparkles]] and [[Xaerine Rumblestride]] ride Gaw, while [[Ylka Gralhund]] and [[Malark Wavesilver]] ride Craw.
- **S02E03:** SWOJ rides the scarabs atop the Beast of Erua to the [[Rose Knight's FortressMausoleum]]. [[Malark Wavesilver]] stays behind with Gaw and Craw while others explore the fortress interior. [[Ylka Gralhund]] takes the reins from [[Scoot Sparkles]] when the party splits up.
- **S02E04:** Inside the [[Dynamo Of Possibility]]'s pocket dimension, SWOJ discovers Craw hiding in a sarcophagus room in the underground tunnels. Scoot reconnects with Craw and introduces him to the Tuba (an animated musical instrument ally). SWOJ rides Craw and Gaw up [[The Tower]] to confront [[The Body Taker]] in the climactic battle. During the fight, Gaw is killed. Craw mourns his companion with a loud, keening clicking from atop the tower. Craw then climbs down to rescue the party, carrying Scoot, the unconscious [[Malark Wavesilver]], and the velociraptor-form [[Xaerine Rumblestride]].
- **S02E05:** After the Dynamo collapses, Craw emerges from the [[Beast Of Erua]] with the party. Scoot checks on Craw, who goes in for a scratch but seems melancholy after the loss of Gaw. Scoot declares "We're going to find a spot for Craw" and tries to bring him through the teleportation circle to [[Waterdeep]]. However, during transit, Craw, [[Volothamp Geddarm]], and [[Radecere]] are displaced and vanish. Scoot laments the loss but remains confident Craw is safe. Later, Craw arrives in Waterdeep separately due to the time displacement effect -- appearing older and having served as a pack animal at [[The Short Rest]] during the three-year gap. Scoot rushes out to reunite with Craw, who is overjoyed and gives him "scarab licks." The party decides to take Craw along on their journey north.
- **S02E06:** Craw travels with SWOJ and [[Ziraj The Hunter]] north from Waterdeep along [[The Long Road]] toward [[Amphail]]. In the dense [[Kryptwood]], Craw has difficulty navigating the narrow paths, crunching through branches as he pushes forward. At the [[Circle Of Dusk Glade]], [[Farron]] (the human guise of [[Zelifarn]]) walks up to Craw and bonds with him instantly, scratching him in the same places Scoot does. Unable to navigate the deeper forest, Craw is sent back to Amphail when the party heads toward the [[Sword Mountains]] cave.
- **S02E08:** During a montage sequence covering SWOJ's return from the [[Radiant Citadel]], Scoot expresses a desire to find Craw and either ride him or [[Zelifarn]] back to Waterdeep.
- **S02E19:** Craw reappears near [[Amphail]], now serving as a mount for [[Leah]] and [[Shala]] of the [[Circle Of Dusk]], who have been scouting the area around the [[Spiral Cavern]]. When SWOJ arrives on dragonback, Craw crashes through the brush and immediately bowls over [[Scoot Sparkles]], going in for scratches in a joyful reunion. The druids have been riding Craw while monitoring [[Manshoon]]'s military buildup.

## Relationships

- **[[Scoot Sparkles]]:** Craw's primary bond. Scoot established a strong animal handling connection early on and consistently serves as Craw's caretaker, giving scratches and treating him like a beloved pet. Their reunions are always emotional.
- **[[Gaw]]:** Craw's companion scarab from the Shadow Way Inn. The two traveled together until Gaw's death in the battle atop the tower inside the Dynamo. Craw mourned Gaw with loud keening clicks.
- **[[Farron]] / [[Zelifarn]]:** Farron bonded instantly with Craw at the Circle of Dusk glade, scratching him in the same places Scoot does. Craw accepted him immediately.
- **[[Leah]] and [[Shala]]:** The Circle of Dusk druids who took Craw into their care after the party left Amphail, using him as their scouting mount near the Spiral Cavern.
