# Justin Rask

**Justin Rask** is a representative of the [[Guild of Butchers]] in [[Waterdeep]]. A distinctive and somewhat unsettling figure, he visits [[Trollskull Manor]] alongside other guild representatives to discuss tavern licensing with [[SWOJ]].

**First appearance:** [[S01E07 - Episode Seven]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode
**Faction affiliations:** [[Guild of Butchers]]

## Description

Justin Rask is described as a dead-eyed and slack-jawed humanoid who is notably tall and covered in scars, the likely marks of his trade as a butcher. Despite his intimidating appearance, he is very business-like and professional in his dealings. The players jokingly note the coincidence of him sharing a first name with Justin, the player who portrays [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]].

## Role in the Story

### Tavern Licensing at Trollskull Manor (S01E07)

- Justin Rask visits [[Trollskull Manor]] as one of roughly ten guild representatives who come to discuss the requirements for reopening the establishment as a tavern ([[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]])
- He represents the [[Guild of Butchers]], the guild overseeing meat supply and butchery in [[Waterdeep]]
- The DM introduces him after [[Broxley Fairkettle]] and [[Hammond Kraddoc]], prompting a joke from the players: "Justin, how are we ever going to remember that?" (referencing the real-life player Justin)
- Despite his unsettling physical appearance -- dead-eyed, slack-jawed, tall, and scarred -- he conducts himself in a straightforward, business-like manner

## Relationships

- **[[SWOJ]]** — Visits the party to discuss butchery and meat supply licensing for [[Trollskull Manor]]
