# Nana

An older tiefling woman employed as the nanny to the [[Cassalanter Villa|Cassalanter]] children, [[Elzerina Cassalanter|Elzerina]] and [[Terenzio Cassalanter|Terenzio]]. She is a no-nonsense caretaker who scolds the children for playing instead of studying, unwittingly providing cover for [[SWOJ]]'s infiltration of the villa.

**First appearance:** [[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

Nana is an older tiefling woman who serves as the live-in nanny for the Cassalanter children. She is strict and business-like, scolding Elzerina and Terenzio for playing games when they should be studying, and threatening to tell their father [[Victoro Cassalanter|Victoro]] about their misbehavior. She spends her own time crocheting and watching her stories in the upstairs playroom. Her exasperated refrain -- "I don't get paid enough for this" -- captures her weary attitude toward the unruly Cassalanter children.

## Role in the Story

- **S01E24:** While SWOJ infiltrates [[Cassalanter Villa]] under the guise of playing "ghosts" (hide and seek) with the Cassalanter children, Nana nearly exposes the intrusion by catching [[Elzerina Cassalanter|Elzerina]] running through the halls
- **S01E24:** Elzerina hastily explains to Nana that they are "just playing hide and seek," while [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] uses Minor Illusion to mimic [[Terenzio Cassalanter|Terenzio]]'s voice from hiding, convincing Nana that both children are accounted for
- **S01E24:** Nana scolds the children, telling them their father will be upset they have been playing all afternoon, then retreats upstairs to her crocheting and stories after being satisfied the children are heading to study

## Relationships
