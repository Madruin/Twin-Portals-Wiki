# Jarlaxle

Leader of Bregan D'aerthe. Mentioned in connection with doppelganger potions and crew disguises.

**First appearance:** [[S01E26]]
**Total appearances:** 12 episodes

## Description

Leader of Bregan D'aerthe. Mentioned in connection with doppelganger potions and crew disguises.

The infamous drow leader of the Bregan D'aerthe, disguised as the human Zardos Zord. Captain of the Sea Maiden's Fair fleet. Found at the Blubber Buckle tavern in Driftwood conducting business with halflings. Offers SWOJ passage to Devil's Rock as a detour on his way north to the Sword Coast. Previously introduced Mystery to SWOJ.

The famous drow mercenary leader of Bregan D'aerthe. SWOJ visits him and he allows them to speak with Arcanthos, an ancient red dragon trapped in a bag of holding.

The drow leader of Bregan D'aerthe, present at the Masked Lords' council as himself with his eye patch. Gives a toast to SWOJ and jokes about also being late to drink.


## Role in the Story

- **S02E09:** Party finds Jarlaxle (as Zardos Zord) at the Blubber Buckle tavern in Driftwood
- **S02E09:** Long John McRon offers passage for 500 gold; SWOJ chooses to ride with Jarlaxle instead
- **S02E20:** Visiting Jarlaxle and speaking to Arcanthos (ancient red dragon) through a bag of holding
