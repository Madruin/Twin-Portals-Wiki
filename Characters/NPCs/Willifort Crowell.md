# Willifort Crowell

Willifort Crowell is a **doppelganger** who served as the majordomo (chief servant) of [[Cassalanter Villa]] and a key operative of the [[Cassalanter Family  Cult Of Asmodeus|Cassalanter family]]. He later resurfaces as a **[[Zentarim (Zhentarim)|Zentarim]] lieutenant** operating out of [[Amphail]].

**First appearance:** [[S01E09 - Episode Nine|S01E09]]
**Last known appearance:** [[S02E31 - Episode Sixty One (S02E31)|S02E31]] (mentioned by name)
**Species:** Doppelganger (poses as a tiefling)
**Status:** Captured (Season 1); active as Zentarim lieutenant (Season 2)
**Faction affiliations:** [[Cassalanter Family  Cult Of Asmodeus|Cassalanter Family]], [[Zentarim (Zhentarim)]]

---

## Description

In his tiefling guise, Crowell is an aging tiefling who wears thin-rimmed glasses pulled from his pocket. He presents himself as a refined, professional household servant. His appearance matches the description of the tiefling who gave [[Blossom]] the devil ring.

As a doppelganger, Crowell possesses the ability to assume the form of other humanoids, which he uses to devastating effect -- most notably impersonating [[Captain Hyustus Stagget]] of the city watch.

## Role in the Story

### Season 1: The Cassalanter Conspiracy

#### The Cassalanter Villa Party ([[S01E09 - Episode Nine|S01E09]]--[[S01E10 - Episode Ten|S01E10]])

Crowell first appears receiving SWOJ at the door of [[Cassalanter Villa]] during a party hosted by [[Victoro Cassalanter]] and [[Amalia Cassalanter]]. He manages guests, checks weapons at the door, and directs visitors. SWOJ immediately notes that he matches the description of the tiefling connected to [[Blossom]]'s devil ring, marking him as suspicious.

#### Suspicion Grows ([[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]])

Following the events at the villa, SWOJ openly discusses their suspicions about Crowell. [[Captain Hyustus Stagget]] himself indicates that the Cassalanter majordomo is likely involved in the conspiracy.

#### The Stone of Golorr Chase ([[S01E13 - Episode Thirteen|S01E13]])

During the investigation of the [[Gralhund Crypt]] in the [[City of the Dead]], SWOJ discovers a handoff in progress -- cultists and spined devils transferring the [[The Stone of Golorr]]. A rooftop chase ensues across the City of the Dead during [[Feast of the Moon]] fireworks. [[Sally]] the pigeon provides aerial reconnaissance and spots Crowell waiting in a side alley with an ornate unmarked wagon, a bearded devil guard, and a valet.

A spined devil delivers the Stone to Crowell. He takes it, looks at SWOJ, and declares: *"It is time for the tides to turn on the Silver Waves of Justice. Kill them."* He then steps into the wagon and flees down the [[High Road]] through the Feast of the Moon parade, triggering a cart chase.

SWOJ is ultimately arrested by [[Captain Hyustus Stagget]] after the chase and taken to the [[Docks Ward Guardhouse]].

#### The Trial and Unmasking ([[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]])

While imprisoned awaiting trial, SWOJ picks their cell lock and explores the prison basement. They discover two dead city watchmen bludgeoned to death -- including the **real** [[Captain Hyustus Stagget]]. The "Captain Stagget" who had been interacting with them in prison was actually Crowell in disguise.

When confronted, the false Stagget reveals himself as Willifort Crowell in his doppelganger form. During the subsequent trial -- presided over by [[Laeral Silverhand]], the Open Lord of [[Waterdeep]], who replaces the corrupt Cassalanter-bought magistrate -- Crowell is exposed when he cannot read the [[Gralhund Family|Gralhund]] note presented as evidence. He attempts to flee through a window but is caught by a grell and [[Scoot Sparkles]]. He is restrained with manacles and taken into custody.

#### Aftermath ([[S01E16 - Episode Sixteen|S01E16]])

SWOJ discusses the implications of Crowell's impersonation, debating whether Captain Stagget was always a doppelganger or whether Crowell replaced him at some point.

### Season 2: Zentarim Operations

#### The Neverwinter Incident ([[S02E31 - Episode Sixty One (S02E31)|S02E31]])

Crowell resurfaces by name during the interrogation of [[Krom]], a [[Zentarim (Zhentarim)|Zentarim]] agent polymorphed into a cicada by [[Ylka Gralhund]]. Krom reveals that a **Lieutenant Crowell**, operating out of [[Amphail]], sent his team to steal the soul stone from [[Castle Never]]. Krom describes Crowell as a Zentarim lieutenant directing operations in the [[Sword Mountains]] region, including plans involving the [[Spiral Cavern]] defenses and the [[Radiant Citadel]].

This reveals that Crowell either escaped custody or was released, and has shifted his operations from the Cassalanters in [[Waterdeep]] to the Zentarim network based out of Amphail.
