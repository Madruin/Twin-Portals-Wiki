## Description

Ylka's relative (possibly grandfather or ancestor) whose initials 'M.G.' are on the magic sword found in Lif's chest. Helped Lif find work at Trollskull Manor.

Ylka's grandfather, a wizard who invented the soul-binding ritual used by Jake and Angie. His old lab is in a walled-off section of the Trollskull basement. Turned against his moral code involving demons, put Ylka into a hundred-year death-sleep for unknown purposes.


## Role in the Story

- **S01E05:** Discover Lif's hidden workshop in the cellar with diary, magic sword (initials M.G. for Mordecai Gralhund), and personal effects
- **S01E05:** Ylka recognizes the sword's melody as one her grandfather sang and the initials as her relative Mordecai
- **S02E38:** Antediluvian Jake and Angie Fliplap perform Mordecai Gralhund's soul-binding ritual, binding their souls to objects to end their undead immortality
