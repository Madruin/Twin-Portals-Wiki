# Dalekar

Dalekar (also spelled Dalakhar or Dalcar) was a gnome spy employed by [[Lord Dagult Neverember|Dagult Neverember]] to keep tabs on his estranged son [[Renaer Neverember]] and to recover the [[Stone of Golorr]]. He was killed in the fireball explosion at [[Trollskull Alley]], an event that triggered the gang war between the [[Xanathar Guild]] and the [[Zhentarim]] and set the central mystery of Season 1 into motion.

**First appearance:** [[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]] (mentioned by Renaer)
**Death:** [[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]] (killed in the fireball explosion)
**Total appearances:** 3 episodes

---

## Description

Dalekar was a gnome who operated as a low-key spy and agent. According to [[Renaer Neverember]], he had been tailing Renaer for several months, flitting around outside windows and waiting for him outside buildings. He kept a low profile and did not carry any fancy equipment. When his body was found after the fireball, he had sewage-stained boots (suggesting he had been traveling through the sewers), a dagger, and a [[House Neverember|Neverember]]-branded pouch containing five gemstones.

---

## Role in the Story

### The Spy ([[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]])

Renaer Neverember first mentions Dalekar by name when meeting with [[SWOJ]] at [[Trollskull Manor]]. He warns the party that a spy named Dalakhar has been following him, seeking the [[Stone of Golorr]] on behalf of his father [[Lord Dagult Neverember|Dagult Neverember]]. This is the first indication that Dagult is actively working to recover his embezzled gold from afar using agents in [[Waterdeep]].

### The Fireball ([[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]])

Dalekar is killed in the fireball explosion that devastates [[Trollskull Alley]] during the grand opening of [[The Short Rest]]. His body is found at the blast center -- a dead gnome carrying a dagger, with sewage-stained boots and a Neverember-branded pouch holding five gemstones. A dark figure in black leather is seen taking something from his body and fleeing south, suggesting Dalekar had the [[Stone of Golorr]] in his possession at the time of his death. Over 10 people are killed in the explosion, including [[Voniel]].

### Identification and the Chain of Possession ([[S01E12 - Episode Twelve|S01E12]])

[[Renaer Neverember]] identifies the dead gnome as Dalekar (referred to as "Dalcar" in conversation), his father's spy. Renaer notes he had not seen Dalekar in more than a tenday and assumed he had gone off the trail. According to Renaer, Dalekar had no connection to the [[Xanathar Guild]] and was loyal to Dagult, though as a spy he may have had various contacts. Renaer agrees to hide at [[The Short Rest]] for his own safety now that Dalekar is dead.

Through investigation at [[Gralhund Villa]], SWOJ pieces together the full chain of possession of the [[Stone of Golorr]]: the [[Xanathar Guild]] held the Stone, Dalekar stole it from them, the [[Zhentarim]] (specifically [[Urstul Floxxin]]) took it from Dalekar's body after the fireball, and [[Yalah Gralhund]] double-crossed the Zhentarim and seized it for herself.

## Relationships

- **[[Lord Dagult Neverember|Dagult Neverember]]** -- Dalekar's employer. Dagult sent him to track Renaer and recover the Stone of Golorr.
- **[[Renaer Neverember]]** -- Dalekar's surveillance target. Renaer was aware of Dalekar following him and identified his body after the fireball.
- **[[Xanathar Guild]]** -- Dalekar stole the [[Stone of Golorr]] from the Xanathar Guild's possession, triggering the gang war.
- **[[Urstul Floxxin]]** / [[Zhentarim]] -- A Zhentarim agent took the Stone from Dalekar's body after the fireball explosion.
