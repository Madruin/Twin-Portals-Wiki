# Blossom Zuber

Blossom Zuber is the wealthy halfling matriarch of [[Zuber Farm]], the largest winery in [[Undercliff]], just east of [[Waterdeep]]. Driven by a desire to maintain her family's monopoly on the wine trade, she secretly used a devil-linked ring to summon enchanted scarecrows against her rival, [[Stoutfellow Farm]]. When [[SWOJ]] confronted her, she broke down and confessed, revealing a connection between the ring and [[Cassalanter Villa]] that would draw the party deeper into Waterdeep's noble intrigue.

**First appearance:** [[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Description

Blossom is a finely dressed halfling who carries herself with a haughty, aristocratic air. She wears a poofy white shirt and several silver rings on her hands, and her office at the Zuber Farm is described as gaudy and lavishly decorated. She considers herself a very influential figure in Undercliff's society, and she draws a clear social distinction between the gentry and "the common folk." Despite her airs, she is genuinely distraught over the disappearance of her son [[Dasher Zuber]], and she is also a talented artist -- she drew the missing poster for Dasher herself.

The Zuber Farm is a large, flashy operation focused exclusively on wine, featuring a touristy brewery and distillery with a gift shop stocked with Zuber-branded goods, stuffed animals, pins, and postcards. Their current product line is themed around [[Zardoz Zord]] and the Sea Maiden's Faire.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]:** [[SWOJ]] travels to [[Undercliff]] after accepting a scarecrow quest from [[Leah]]. After meeting [[Mary Stoutfellow]] at [[Stoutfellow Farm]], they visit the neighboring [[Zuber Farm]] and meet Blossom, who presents herself as a concerned mother searching for her missing son [[Dasher Zuber]], offering a 500 gold reward. She claims the scarecrows have not attacked her farm but expresses worry they might. During the night, [[Scoot Sparkles]] spots a flash of light emanating from the direction of the Zuber Farm at chest level -- a suspicious clue that the scarecrows may be originating from Blossom's side of the road.
- **[[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]:** After defeating the scarecrows overnight, SWOJ visits Blossom at the Zuber Farm. [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] confronts her about the scarecrows, and [[Scoot Sparkles]] backs him up with his greataxe. Blossom initially threatens them, claiming to have "friends in very high places" and invoking the [[House Wavesilver|Wavesilver]] name. When Lark spots a silver ring bearing the same three-pointed hoof symbol from [[The God Catcher]] and presses her, she retreats to her private office and breaks down weeping. She confesses that she used the ring to summon the scarecrows to sabotage the Stout Fellows' business and maintain the Zuber monopoly on Waterdeep's wine trade. The ring was given to her by an aging tiefling in a black suit with thin-rimmed glasses at a soiree at [[Cassalanter Villa]] -- a description later confirmed to match [[Willifort Crowell]], the Cassalanters' majordomo. When she removes the ring to hand it over, it crumbles to dust, radiating a dissipating aura of conjuration magic. SWOJ strikes a deal: they will search for Dasher and keep her involvement with the scarecrows secret, in exchange for her cooperation and information about the tiefling. Later, after finding Dasher alive as a wererat with the [[Shard Shunners]], SWOJ delivers his farewell letter to Blossom. She cannot verify the letter's authenticity but accepts it, and as a reward gives them the Gravity Goblet -- a prototype wine glass with a reverse-gravity enchantment that the Zubers are developing. She also provides an invitation for two to an upcoming soiree at Cassalanter Villa, happy to avoid attending herself, which becomes SWOJ's entry point to the Cassalanter storyline.

## Relationships

- **[[Dasher Zuber]]** -- Her son, about 20 years old, who resented the family's sophisticated lifestyle and preferred spending time with common folk in Waterdeep. His disappearance left Blossom distraught.
- **[[Tula]]** -- Bartender at Zuber Farm who serves as a point of contact for visitors.
- **[[Mary Stoutfellow]]** -- Owner of the rival [[Stoutfellow Farm]]. Blossom used the devil ring to sabotage her competitor's business.
- **[[Willifort Crowell]]** -- The aging tiefling at [[Cassalanter Villa]] who gave Blossom the conjuration ring. She described him without recognizing him by name, though she was apparently a regular guest at Cassalanter events.
- **[[Victoro Cassalanter]]** -- In a later episode, Victoro mentions being good friends with the Zubers and enjoying their wine, suggesting the Cassalanters cultivated a close social relationship with Blossom's family.
- **[[SWOJ]]** -- The party who confronted her, extracted her confession, and struck a deal to find Dasher in exchange for her cooperation and secrecy.
