# Blossom Zuber

Farmer at the neighboring Zuber Farm in Undercliff. Her son Dasher has gone missing. Has a bartender named Tula working for her.

**First appearance:** [[S01E07]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Description

Farmer at the neighboring Zuber Farm in Undercliff. Her son Dasher has gone missing. Has a bartender named Tula working for her.

Confesses to using a ring with a three-pointed devil symbol (same as God Catcher) to summon the scarecrows. The ring was given to her by an aging tiefling in a black suit with thin-rimmed glasses at Cassalanter Villa. The ring crumbled to dust when removed.


## Role in the Story

- **S01E07:** SWOJ travels to Undercliff and meets Mary Stout Fellow and Blossom Zuber
- **S01E07:** Blossom Zuber's son Dasher is missing
- **S01E08:** Blossom Zuber confesses the scarecrows were summoned by a devil ring given by a tiefling from Cassalanter Villa
- **S01E08:** Meet Dasher Zuber as a wererat living with the Shard Shunners gang
- **S01E08:** Deliver Dasher's letter to Blossom; receive Gravity Goblet prototype and Cassalanter party invitation
