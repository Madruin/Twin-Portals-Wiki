## Description

A nine-year-old boy who is a fragment of Manchun's soul, representing his innocence and childhood. Has a crescent moon glow on his heart. Merges with Teriador's soul to gain enough power to transport SWOJ out of the collapsing Dynamo. Previously worked for the wizard Rantantar.

The nine-year-old fragment of Manchun's soul, now merged with Teriador. His soul is captured in a crystal shard by Rowan Crooks.

- **S02E04:** SWOJ meets Fetch, a nine-year-old fragment of Manchun's soul representing his innocence

- **S02E04:** Fetch absorbs Teriador the Rose Knight's soul, merging with it to gain escape power
- **S02E04:** Fetch transports SWOJ out of the collapsing Dynamo back to the Great Beast's skull
- **S02E05:** SWOJ exits the Dynamo back into the Great Beast's skull with Fetch now controlling the vines
- **S02E05:** Rowan Crooks and the Jolly Gents arrive and capture Fetch's soul in a crystal shard
- **S02E05:** Ylka attacks Rowan to protect Fetch but Lark deflects; Xae briefly takes the shard before surrendering it
- **S02E05:** Radisare examines the Dynamo and casts Geas on Rowan to safeguard Fetch
