# Beaker

Beaker (full name Beaker Bratzie) is a raven who serves as a lieutenant in [[Sally]]'s bird intelligence network across [[Waterdeep]]. Introduced by Sally to [[SWOJ]] as an overwatch operative, Beaker accompanied the party on their infiltration of [[Cassalanter Villa]] during the [[Founders Day]] feast and later returned as reinforcement during the climactic theater battle at the [[Zadio City Music Hall]].

**First appearance:** [[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four|S01E24]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Description

Beaker is a raven with a laconic, loyal personality. His full name is Beaker Bratzie, though Sally tells the party to "just call him Beaker." He is part of the sprawling avian network that [[Sally]] organized across [[Trollskull Alley]] and greater [[Waterdeep]], which includes pigeons, crows, ravens, hawks, and eagle bodyguards. When Sally first points him out, Beaker does not even look at the party -- he just "kind of chills" and watches the proceedings. When [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] uses Speak with Animals to introduce herself, Beaker's response is simple: "Whatever Sally wants. Sally won't ever steer you wrong." He is motivated by the promise of unlimited eggs to bring back to Sally.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four|S01E24]]:** After reporters arrive at [[The Short Rest]] asking about the [[Kolat Towers]] explosion, [[Sally]] introduces Beaker to [[SWOJ]] as someone who can provide aerial overwatch for their mission. Sally describes him as "a crone by the name of Beaker Bratzie" (likely meaning "crow"), and says he can watch over the party from above, though she is unsure how good he is with "people's stuff." [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] casts Speak with Animals through her Avian Circlet and recruits Beaker, promising unlimited eggs. Beaker accompanies the party as they head to [[Cassalanter Villa]], providing reconnaissance from the sky as [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] wild shapes into a golden retriever and the party infiltrates the estate.
- **[[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]]:** During the climactic battle against the [[Xanathar]] at the [[Zadio City Music Hall]], Beaker arrives as reinforcement from [[Sally]]'s network. As [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] directs her conjured birds to peck at the chandelier's rope, Beaker comes crashing through a window to join the assault, leading the other birds in attacking the massive chandelier hanging over the theater. The birds eventually succeed in bringing it down on the [[Xanathar]], contributing to the beholder's defeat. The DM notes that "Sally herself is not here" but Beaker is "one of her wingmen."

## Relationships

- **[[Sally]]** -- Beaker's boss and the head of the bird network. He is completely loyal to Sally and follows her orders without question, stating "Whatever Sally wants."
- **[[Ylka Gralhund]]** -- Ylka communicates with Beaker through Speak with Animals via her Avian Circlet, serving as the party's liaison with Sally's network.
- **[[Xaerine Rumblestride]]** -- During the theater battle, Xae directs Beaker and her conjured birds to attack the chandelier rope at the [[Zadio City Music Hall]].
- **[[SWOJ]]** -- Provides aerial overwatch support during the infiltration of [[Cassalanter Villa]] and reinforcement during the theater battle against the [[Xanathar]].
