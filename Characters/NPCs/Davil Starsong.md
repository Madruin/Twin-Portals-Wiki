## Description

Zhentarim agent and member of the Doom Raiders faction. Encounters SWOJ at the Yawning Portal. Bonnie reads his mind and determines he is searching for the Stone of Golorr. Flees the Short Rest opening when a griffin cavalry rider appears overhead.

Leader of the Doom Raiders, a moderate faction within the Zhentarim. Requested 5,000 gold from SWOJ to finance their operations against the Necromancer's main Zhentarim division. Offered the services of his crew including Ziraj and Skeemo.


## Role in the Story

- **S01E11:** SWOJ meets Davil Starsong of the Zhentarim Doom Raiders at the Yawning Portal
- **S01E11:** Bonnie telepathically reads Davil's mind - he's searching for the Stone of Golorr
- **S01E11:** Davil Starsong flees when griffin cavalry arrives
- **S01E29:** Davil Starsong requests and receives 5,000 gold investment from SWOJ for Doom Raiders operations
