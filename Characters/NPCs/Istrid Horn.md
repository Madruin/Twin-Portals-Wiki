# Istrid Horn

Dwarf member of the [[Doom Raiders]], a moderate subfaction within the [[Zhentarim|Zhentarim (Black Network)]] led by [[Davil Starsong]]. Istrid is named by the DM among the Doom Raiders' full roster when the band arrives at the [[Cassalanter Villa]] to perform at the Founders Day feast in [[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]].

**Race:** Dwarf
**Occupation:** Member of the [[Doom Raiders]]
**First appearance:** [[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]]
**Total appearances:** 1 confirmed episode (named); present by implication in all Doom Raiders group appearances
**Faction affiliations:** [[Doom Raiders]], [[Zhentarim]]

---

## Description

Istrid Horn is one of the core members of the Doom Raiders adventuring band. She is named in the transcript of [[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]] when the DM rattles off the full Doom Raiders roster at the gates of [[Cassalanter Villa]]: "There's Dabble Starsong. There is Istrid Horn, Skeemo Weirdbottle, Tashlyn Yafira, Ziraj, and then Yagra."

As a canonical member of the Doom Raiders, Istrid would have been present for the band's other collective appearances throughout the campaign -- performing at the [[Trollskull Alley]] celebration in [[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]], making music aboard the Ethereal Galleon during the war against [[Manshoon]] in Season 2, and performing at the God's Night celebration at [[Trollskull Manor]] in [[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]. However, she is not individually named or given dialogue in those scenes.

---

## Role in the Story

### The Cassalanter Feast Infiltration (S01E27)

When [[Scoot Sparkles|SWOJ]] needed to infiltrate the [[Cassalanter Family  Cult Of Asmodeus|Cassalanters']] Founders Day feast -- a front for a devil-soul harvest -- the [[Doom Raiders]] provided cover by allowing SWOJ to disguise themselves as additional band members. The full Doom Raiders roster, including Istrid Horn, arrived at the gates of [[Cassalanter Villa]] and gave their names to the guards one by one, stalling for time while SWOJ members blended in.

The Doom Raiders then moved toward the stage to set up and perform while SWOJ moved through the feast, eventually discovering the poisoned food and confronting [[Victoro Cassalanter]].

### Implied Group Appearances

As a member of the Doom Raiders, Istrid is implicitly present during the band's collective appearances:

- **[[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]]:** The Doom Raiders perform at the grand celebration in [[Trollskull Alley]] following the Cassalanter affair; [[Davil Starsong]] requests and receives a 5,000 gold investment from SWOJ.
- **[[S02E31 - Episode Sixty One (S02E31)|S02E31]] - [[S02E33 - Episode Sixty Three (S02E33)|S02E33]]:** The Doom Raiders join SWOJ's fleet aboard the Ethereal Galleon, serving as the second musical act to power the spelljamming helm during the assault on the [[Spiral Cavern]] and beyond.
- **[[S02E36 - Episode Sixty Six (S02E36)|S02E36]]:** [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] frees the Doom Raiders from magical restraints at the bottom of the [[Radiant Citadel]] during the final battle against [[Manshoon]].
- **[[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]:** The Doom Raiders perform at the God's Night celebration at [[Trollskull Manor]] and have filled the Zhentarim power vacuum in [[Waterdeep]] after Manshoon's defeat.
