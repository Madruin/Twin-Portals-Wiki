# Pinker

Pinker is a [[kenku]] who formerly worked for the [[Xanathar Guild]] before defecting to become a loyal ally of [[SWOJ]]. Recognizable by his distinctive pink hat, Pinker was first encountered as an enemy combatant during the rescue of [[Floon Blagmaar]] on [[Candle Lane]], but the party's decision to release him rather than kill him set in motion a chain of events that would see him repeatedly risk his life to help them. By the Season 2 finale, Pinker has become a fixture of the [[Waterdeep]] social scene around [[Trollskull Manor]], a friend who buys rounds of drinks and wears [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]'s durag as a badge of honor.

**Race:** Kenku
**First appearance:** [[S01E02 - Episode Two|S01E02]]
**Total appearances:** 6 episodes

---

## Description

Pinker is a kenku -- a corvid humanoid who cannot produce original speech and instead mimics phrases and voices he has heard. He wears a distinctive pink hat (his namesake) and has the [[Xanathar Guild|Xanathar]] symbol tattooed on his hand: a circle with ten spokes. Like all kenku, he communicates by repeating phrases in the voices of those who spoke them, which can make conversations with him unpredictable and sometimes comically confusing. He is a skilled lockpicker and possesses enough cunning to orchestrate an escape for over a dozen fellow kenku prisoners.

## Role in the Story

### Capture and Release (S01E02)

During [[SWOJ]]'s raid on the [[Xanathar Guild]] warehouse on [[Candle Lane]] to rescue [[Floon Blagmaar]], four kenku ambushed the party. After the fight, the party captured one alive -- a kenku wearing a pink hat whom [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] named "Pinker." Under questioning, Pinker repeated the phrase "follow the yellow signs in the sewers" and showed the Xanathar symbol tattooed on his hand (a circle with ten spokes), confirming the Guild's involvement in Floon's kidnapping.

Rather than kill him, [[SWOJ]] chose to release Pinker. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] sat beside him and sang every song she knew, filling his kenku memory with her music. [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] whispered "Xanatharim sends its regards" and "Zhentarim sends their regards" into his ear -- deliberately planting misleading phrases. The party gave Pinker a gold coin before letting him go. This act of mercy would prove pivotal.

### Rescuing Tagga (S01E05)

When the [[Xanathar Guild]] ransacked [[Tagga]]'s apartment in retaliation for SWOJ's interference -- leaving crow feathers as a calling card -- it was Pinker who intervened. He had defected from the Guild to protect [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]'s mother, spiriting [[Tagga]] away and hiding her in a smuggler's closet inside the [[Honorable Knight Statue]] tenements.

The party contacted Pinker through an amethyst sending stone found at Tagga's ransacked apartment. He guided them to the statue, dropped a rope ladder, and gave each party member a gold coin (echoing the one they had given him), indicating they should place it under their tongues as the kenku do. When asked what the Xanathar were looking for at the apartment, Pinker was solemn: "Pay back." He confirmed that the Guild was retaliating against SWOJ specifically. The party thanked him warmly, with Scoot declaring "Pinker is a good one."

### Passing Mention (S01E11)

SWOJ briefly recalls their encounter with Pinker at the Xanathar warehouse on Candle Lane while discussing the Guild's operations.

### The Arena of Blood and Fortune (S01E17)

When [[SWOJ]] was captured and brought to the [[Arena Of Blood And Fortune|Arena of Blood and Fortune]] beneath the Xanathar lair, [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] and [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] discovered Pinker and twelve other kenku imprisoned in the cells below the arena. Pinker produced his pink hat from the back of the crowd, put it on, and called out "Scoot!" in Scoot's own voice.

Pinker wanted to help, but the other kenku were reluctant. To win their cooperation, Scoot traded his beloved durag for Pinker's pink hat -- a sacrifice of real sentimental value. Pinker declared "I can make this work" and used his lockpicking skills to free all thirteen kenku from their cells. The kenku agreed to stage a fake fight with SWOJ in the arena, putting on a convincing show for the audience while buying time for the party to locate a secret escape route. Pinker led the kenku in the staged combat, even kicking Scoot to sell the performance. The alliance allowed SWOJ to find the flaming eye brick on the south wall and ultimately escape the Xanathar's lair.

### Remembering Pinker (S01E29)

SWOJ recalls meeting Pinker at the Candle Lane warehouse while discussing their complicated history with the [[Zhentarim]] and [[Xanathar Guild]].

### The Season Two Finale (S02E38)

At the massive God's Night celebration at [[Trollskull Manor]] during the festival of [[Marpenoth]], Pinker appeared among the guests from the [[Flute And Blackmore|Flute and Blackmore]]. He bought a round of drinks for everyone, saluted the party "in his pinkery way," and was wearing Scoot's durag -- the one traded back in the arena cells -- as a permanent memento of their bond.

## Relationships

- **[[Scoot Sparkles]]** -- Scoot named Pinker and advocated for his release. They share a deep mutual loyalty, symbolized by the durag-for-hat trade in the arena cells. Scoot considers Pinker "a good one."
- **[[Tagga]]** -- Pinker risked his life to rescue Scoot's mother from Xanathar Guild retaliation, hiding her in the Honorable Knight Statue tenements.
- **[[SWOJ]]** -- The party's mercy in releasing Pinker earned his lasting loyalty. He became one of their most reliable allies outside the core group.
- **[[Xanathar Guild]]** -- Pinker's former employers. He defected after SWOJ showed him kindness, and actively worked against the Guild by rescuing Tagga and aiding the party's escape from the arena.
