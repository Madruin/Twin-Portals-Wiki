# Krom

Krom is a [[Zentarim (Zhentarim)|Zentarim]] agent sent by **Lieutenant [[Willifort Crowell|Crowell]]** from [[Amphail]] to infiltrate [[Castle Never]] and steal the soul stone of [[Rowan Crooks]]. He is caught by [[Scoot Sparkles|SWOJ]] after stealing [[Quorlith The Starweaver|Corlith the Starweaver]]'s eggs during the chaos of an attack on Castle Never, and is **polymorphed into a cicada** by [[Ylka Gralhund]] before he can use a volatile potion to escape. Under interrogation via *speak with animals*, Krom provides critical intelligence about the [[Spiral Cavern]]'s defenses and [[Manshoon]]'s plans to use the [[Radiant Citadel]] as a base of multi-planar operations.

**First appearance:** [[S02E31 - Episode Sixty One (S02E31)|S02E31]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode
**Status:** Captured -- polymorphed into a cicada and turned over to [[Neverwinter]] guards
**Faction affiliations:** [[Zentarim (Zhentarim)|Zhentarim]] (Manshoon's division)

## Description

Krom is described as a "man in black" -- a Zentarim operative wearing the dark leather associated with [[Manshoon]]'s division of the Zhentarim. He carried a dagger, which he held to [[Quorlith The Starweaver|Corlith]]'s stolen eggs as a hostage threat, and a volatile orange potion that [[Scoot Sparkles]] caught mid-air after Krom was polymorphed.

In the transcript, his name is heard as "Crom" and "Chrome" due to audio transcription variations.

---

## Role in the Story

### The Castle Never Infiltration ([[S02E31 - Episode Sixty One (S02E31)|S02E31]])

During a devastating attack on [[Castle Never]]'s council room, Krom infiltrated the docks below the castle as part of the same coordinated assault. While SWOJ triaged wounded council members upstairs and secured the soul stone of [[Rowan Crooks]], explosions revealed that the [[Ethereal Galleon]] had been sabotaged from within.

At the docks, [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] discovered [[Quorlith The Starweaver|Corlith the Starweaver]] in distress -- the man in black had stolen her eggs and fled deeper into the caves. SWOJ pursued through the tunnels, clearing a cave-in with athletics and *mold earth* to let Corlith follow. They cornered Krom at a dead end where he held a dagger to the eggs as leverage.

Before he could act further, Ylka polymorphed him into a **cicada**. [[Scoot Sparkles]] caught his volatile orange potion as it fell mid-air, while the eggs bounced safely to the ground and Corlith scooped up her brood.

### Interrogation

SWOJ jarred the cicada and interrogated Krom using *speak with animals*. He revealed:

- His name is **Krom**, a [[Zentarim (Zhentarim)|Zentarim]] agent
- He was sent by a **Lieutenant [[Willifort Crowell|Crowell]]** operating out of [[Amphail]]
- His mission was to **steal the soul stone** from the castle
- The [[Spiral Cavern]] is being fortified with **dragons and peritons** as defenses
- The Zentarim leadership is planning to use the **[[Radiant Citadel]]** as a base for **multi-planar operations**
- There may be collaboration with **[[Manshoon]]**

This intelligence proved critical for SWOJ's strategic planning. The next morning, [[Renaer Neverember]] convened a strategy session where SWOJ planned their assault on the [[Moonhole]] and [[Spiral Cavern]] based in part on Krom's information. After interrogation, Krom was turned over to [[Neverwinter]] guards.

## Relationships

- **[[Willifort Crowell]]** -- Krom's commanding officer. Crowell, a Zentarim lieutenant operating from [[Amphail]], dispatched Krom to [[Castle Never]] to steal the soul stone.
- **[[Quorlith The Starweaver]]** -- Krom stole her eggs during the Castle Never attack, prompting SWOJ's pursuit through the caves. She recovered her eggs after his capture.
- **[[Ylka Gralhund]]** -- Polymorphed Krom into a cicada before he could use his potion or harm the eggs.
- **[[Scoot Sparkles]]** -- Caught Krom's volatile potion mid-air during the polymorph.
- **[[Manshoon]]** -- Krom serves the extremist wing of the Zhentarim under Manshoon's direction, in opposition to the [[Doom Raiders]].
