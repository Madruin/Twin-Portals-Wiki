# Giggling Gus

> *"Reminds you of Giggling Gus giggling."*
> -- The DM, describing a phylactery's eerie laughter in [[Friar Truck]]'s laboratory ([[S02E23 - Episode Fifty Three (S02E23)|S02E23]])

**First appearance:** [[S02E22 - Episode Fifty Two LIVE (S02E22)|S02E22]]
**Total appearances:** 3 episodes
**Faction affiliations:** [[The Jolly Gents]]
**Status:** Unknown -- soul likely bound in a phylactery

---

## Overview

Giggling Gus is a top-ranking member of the [[The Jolly Gents|Jolly Gents]], known for his distinctive giggling laugh. Though never directly confronted by [[SWOJ]], Gus is glimpsed at [[Zhentil Keep]] performing mundane duties and is referenced in connection with the soul-harvesting operation run by [[Friar Truck]]. He also appears in a [[The Raven Queen|Raven Queen]]-orchestrated dream sequence depicting the Jolly Gents' legendary assault on [[Xanathar's Lair|the Xanathar's lair]], where he fights alongside [[Antediluvian Jake]].

---

## Description

Gus is identified as "one of the tops" among the Jolly Gents -- a high-ranking member of the organization. His most notable characteristic is his distinctive giggling laugh, which is recognizable enough that even objects associated with his soul reproduce the sound.

---

## Role in the Story

### Zentil Keep (S02E22)

When [[SWOJ]] infiltrates [[Zhentil Keep]] disguised in Jolly Gents cloaks, they spot Gus and [[Quick Quill Quincy]] mopping and cleaning inside a hollowed-out tower near the garbage pit. The two are not directly engaged; SWOJ passes by them while navigating toward [[Rowan Crooks]]' office on the upper floors.

### The Phylactery Laboratory (S02E23)

During the battle against [[Friar Truck]] in his phylactery laboratory beneath Zhentil Keep, [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] knocks a phylactery off the altar and hears a giggling noise as it rolls away. The DM describes the sound as reminiscent of "Giggling Gus giggling," and the players note they have never actually met him face to face despite his reputation. This suggests Gus's soul -- like those of over a hundred other Jolly Gents -- was harvested and bound into one of Friar Truck's phylacteries as part of [[Manshoon]]'s soul-harvesting operation.

### The Raven Queen's Dream (S02E25)

After [[SWOJ]] defeats [[Rowan Crooks]] atop Zhentil Keep and the fortress collapses, the [[The Raven Queen|Raven Queen]] pulls the party into a dream sequence as a test of their worthiness. One of the dream scenarios recreates the Jolly Gents' legendary assault on [[Xanathar's Lair|the Xanathar's lair]] -- a famous exploit from the Gents' heyday as folk heroes. In this vision, Gus and [[Antediluvian Jake]] lead the charge alongside the rest of the Jolly Gents, with SWOJ stepping into the role of [[Rowan Crooks]] to direct the assault. The dream version of Gus represents the original, uncorrupted Jolly Gents at the height of their heroic reputation, before [[Manshoon]]'s takeover.

## Relationships

- **[[The Jolly Gents]]** -- Top-ranking member of the organization.
- **[[Antediluvian Jake]]** -- Fellow high-ranking Gent. The two appear together leading the charge in the Raven Queen's dream of the Xanathar assault.
- **[[Quick Quill Quincy]]** -- Seen mopping alongside Quincy at Zhentil Keep during SWOJ's infiltration.
- **[[Rowan Crooks]]** -- Served under Crooks, who was secretly the 14th [[Manshoon]] clone. Gus's soul was likely harvested as part of Crooks' operation.
- **[[Friar Truck]]** -- The demonic cleric who maintained the phylactery laboratory where Gus's soul was presumably bound.
