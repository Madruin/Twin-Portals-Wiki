# Dragon Incarnate (Rose Quartz)

A small dragon-shaped crystal creature made of many pieces of rose quartz fitted together magically -- an incarnate, a spiritual entity formed when many ancestral dragon spirits from a similar origin come together into a single crystalline form. It resides in the [[Preserve Of The Ancestors]] at the heart of the [[Radiant Citadel]] and appears to be integral to the citadel's magical stability.

**First appearance:** [[S02E08 - Episode Thirty Eight (S02E08)|S02E08]]
**Total appearances:** 5 episodes

## Description

The Rose Quartz Incarnate is roughly the size of a dog, composed of thousands of tiny rose quartz gemstones that fit together magically to form a draconic shape. It dwells in a small cave within the [[Preserve Of The Ancestors]], resting on a leaf-like pile of crystals. Despite its modest size, it is an entity of immense spiritual significance: it houses the collected ancestral spirits of dragons connected to the [[Radiant Citadel]].

Incarnates are a phenomenon unique to the Preserve. Each founding culture of the Radiant Citadel has its own incarnate -- for example, the people of Godsbreath are represented by a great obsidian eagle. The incarnates are formed when many spirits from a similar origin or lineage coalesce into a single stone or crystal creature. They serve as a living connection to the ancestors, capable of telepathic communication and possessing knowledge stretching back centuries. Residents of the Citadel visit their respective incarnates to commune with their forebears and learn about their heritage.

The Rose Quartz Incarnate is the draconic incarnate -- the vessel for ancestral dragon spirits. It communicates telepathically and possesses knowledge of draconic matters including the location of the [[Deep Dragon Temple]] and the network of [[Concord Jewel|Concord Jewels]] that connect the Citadel to the wider world. Its connection to the Citadel runs so deep that when the dracolich physically ripped it from the Preserve, the entire Citadel shook violently on its axis, suggesting the incarnate is structurally bound to the city's ancient magic.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S02E08 - Episode Thirty Eight (S02E08)|S02E08]]:** [[Zelifarn]], in his human guise as Farron, is drawn by a mysterious shadowy figure and a whistling sound toward a cave in the [[Preserve Of The Ancestors]]. Inside, [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] discovers the Rose Quartz Incarnate resting on a crystal pile. Zelifarn communes telepathically with the incarnate about draconic lineage and possible family connections. A farmer named [[Trill]] explains the nature of incarnates to the party -- that they form when many ancestral spirits from a similar place come together. When sounds of crashing come from [[Trill]]'s sugar mill, the incarnate stands up in alarm and confirms the disturbance is not normal. Zelifarn departs with [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] to investigate, leaving the incarnate behind in its cave.

- **[[S02E09 - Episode Thirty Nine (S02E09)|S02E09]]:** After [[Zelifarn]] collapses into a psychic coma following possession by a shadow creature, the Rose Quartz Incarnate emerges from its cave and approaches his unconscious body. It nudges him, inspecting what has gone wrong. [[Mystery]] tells the incarnate that its kin are in trouble, and it responds: "I can feel it -- something's wrong." When asked about the [[Deep Dragon Temple]], the incarnate confirms: "We know of the Deep Dragon Temple." It reveals that the closest [[Concord Jewel]] travels to [[Lantan]], and that the temple lies northeast of there. The incarnate then settles down over [[Zelifarn]]'s body, merging with him magically to protect and attempt to restore him, leaving a white crystal with him. [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] departs for the Deep Dragon Temple, entrusting Zelifarn to the incarnate's care.

- **[[S02E33 - Episode Sixty Three (S02E33)|S02E33]]:** When [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] returns to the [[Radiant Citadel]] during [[Manshoon]]'s occupation, they infiltrate the [[Preserve Of The Ancestors]] and witness the two-headed dracolich stomping out from within. Between its twin skeletal heads floats an orb of force, and trapped inside is the Rose Quartz Incarnate -- bound and unable to move. The dracolich rips the incarnate from the Preserve and flies upward toward the top of the Citadel. As it does, the entire Citadel shakes violently on its axis, making clear that the incarnate is not merely a resident of the Preserve but something fundamental to the city's stability.

- **[[S02E34 - Episode Sixty Four LIVE (S02E34)|S02E34]]:** [[Zelifarn]], freshly liberated by [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]], shares critical intelligence: the dracolich took the incarnate out of the Preserve and intends to feed on the dragon souls inside it to grow stronger. Later, during the battle atop the [[Radiant Citadel]], [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] witnesses cultists loading the crystals that made up the incarnate onto the bones of the dracolich in a binding ritual, fusing the ancestral dragon spirits to the undead creature's skeletal frame.

- **[[S02E35 - Episode Sixty Five (S02E35)|S02E35]]:** After the [[Raven Queen]]'s divine intervention destroys the dracolich in a massive flock of spectral ravens, the bones disintegrate -- but the rose quartz crystals remain. They rise into the air, swirl together magically, coalesce into a semi-draconic spectral shape, and fly back down toward the [[Preserve Of The Ancestors]], restored and free. [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] celebrates: "We defeated a dracolich and we freed the dragon incarnate."

## Relationships

- **[[Zelifarn]]** -- The incarnate shares a deep connection with the ancient bronze dragon. It communed with him telepathically about his draconic lineage, then physically merged with his unconscious body to protect and restore him during his long coma. Zelifarn was the first to be drawn to the incarnate's cave, suggesting a spiritual kinship between them.
- **[[Melifarn]]** -- The incarnate possesses knowledge of the [[Deep Dragon Temple]] where Melifarn resides, indicating awareness of the broader network of dragon ancestors and their sacred sites.
- **[[Mystery]]** -- The drow warlock communicated with the incarnate after Zelifarn's collapse, exchanging information about the crisis afflicting dragon-kind. The incarnate responded to Mystery's respectful gestures and provided the critical intelligence that directed [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] toward [[Lantan]] and the [[Deep Dragon Temple]].
- **[[Sholeh]]** -- As the elder speaker of the [[Radiant Citadel]] and an ancient brass dragon in disguise, Sholeh shares a draconic connection to the incarnate and the Preserve it inhabits.

## Key Locations

- **[[Preserve Of The Ancestors]]** -- The incarnate's home, a vast natural landscape within the auroral diamond at the heart of the [[Radiant Citadel]]. Contains many incarnates representing different founding cultures.
- **[[Radiant Citadel]]** -- The incarnate appears to be magically bound to the Citadel itself; removing it caused the entire structure to destabilize.
