# Bacto And Juba

Bacto and Juba are a tabaxi couple who live next door to [[Tagga]] in her apartment building near the [[Dock Ward]]. Worldly musicians who play at all hours of the night, they are friendly neighbors whom Tagga secretly enjoys despite her complaints about the noise. [[Scoot Sparkles]] turns to them for help when his mother goes missing.

**First appearance:** [[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

Bacto and Juba are tabaxi -- cat-like humanoids -- who share an apartment next door to [[Tagga]]. They are musicians and creative people who practice their craft at all hours, much to the audible (though privately appreciated) chagrin of their neighbor. Bacto appears fragile in build. Juba is more expressive and emotional, placing a comforting hand on [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]'s shoulder when she hears the bad news about Tagga. They describe themselves as well-traveled, having "sailed the world," and speak with a philosophical worldliness, offering Scoot both sympathy and gentle wisdom about the hard life of adventuring.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]]:** When [[SWOJ]] discovers [[Tagga]]'s apartment ransacked with crow feathers -- evidence of the [[Xanathar Guild]] seeking payback -- [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] decides to check in with the neighbor his mother is friendliest with. He knocks on Bacto and Juba's door. Bacto answers first and is shocked to hear Tagga is missing. He confirms they have been practicing music all day and heard nothing unusual. He recalls that Tagga came home around noon and offered them extra pie. Bacto then calls Juba over, and she becomes visibly worried, placing her hand on Scoot's shoulder and urging him to contact the city watch. Juba offers empathetic counsel, telling Scoot "We've sailed the world. We know life. Life you've chosen -- it's not an easy one. But still, there's an easier way, my friend." Scoot thanks them and asks that if Tagga returns, they tell her he is searching for her.

## Relationships

- **[[Tagga]]** -- Next-door neighbors and friends. Tagga complains about their late-night music but secretly enjoys their company and creativity. She shares her cooking with them, offering extra pie on the day she goes missing.
- **[[Scoot Sparkles]]** -- Tagga's son, whom they know well enough to greet by name. They offer him genuine concern and comfort when Tagga disappears.
