# Zelifarn

**Race:** Ancient Bronze Dragon
**Also known as:** Zellopharn, Zellophane, Zelopharne, Farron, Ferrin
**Affiliations:** [[Way Of The Balanced Scales]], [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] (ally), [[Bregan D'Aerthe]] (through [[Jarlaxle]])
**First appearance:** [[S01E06 - Episode Six]]
**Total appearances:** 22+ episodes across Seasons 1 and 2

---

## Description

Zelifarn is an ancient bronze dragon and one of [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]]'s most enduring allies, appearing across both seasons of the campaign in a variety of forms and circumstances. He is the self-appointed guardian of [[Deepwater Harbor]] in [[Waterdeep]], where he maintains an underwater lair among the shipwrecks and debris of the harbor floor. In his natural form, he is an imposing adult-to-ancient sized bronze dragon capable of unleashing devastating chain lightning breath. He can also assume a human guise -- most notably the amnesiac soldier "Farron" during the [[Kryptwood]] arc -- and was learning to polymorph from the gold dragon [[Aurinax]] during the Season 1 downtime so he could join [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] for dinner at [[The Short Rest]].

Zelifarn is loyal, protective, and deeply bonded to the party -- particularly to [[Scoot Sparkles]], who wore a necklace housing Zelifarn's soul during the latter half of Season 2. He has a somewhat humorous reputation at the table due to his name, which the podcast's audio transcription rendered in wildly varying ways: "Zellopharn," "Zellophane," "Zelepharine," "cell phone," and "Zell a farm," among others. Despite this running gag, Zelifarn's role in the story is serious and consequential -- he is central to the dragon-related mythology of the campaign, connected to the ancient conflict between bronze dragons and aboleth, and instrumental in the final liberation of the [[Radiant Citadel]].

## History and Role in the Story

### Season 1: Harbor Guardian and SWOJ Companion

**First Meeting (S01E06):** SWOJ first encountered Zelifarn while exploring [[Deepwater Harbor]] in [[Grinda Garloth]]'s [[Apparatus Of Kwalish]]. The bronze dragon served as the self-appointed guardian of the harbor and led the party to the wreck of the [[Sea Plum]], whose cargo hold had been emptied and hull breached from the outside. He then guided them to a [[Smuggler's Cave]] beneath the city. During this initial meeting, the players memorably debated his name's pronunciation -- "Zellophane with an F" -- establishing a running joke that would persist for years.

**Intelligence and Hoard (S01E10, S01E22):** When SWOJ visited Zelifarn at the docks in his human form, he pointed out a submersible beneath the [[The Eyecatcher]] belonging to [[Grinda Garloth]] and provided intelligence about [[Jarlaxle]]'s operations with the [[Bregan D'Aerthe]]. This intelligence proved crucial as SWOJ boarded the Eyecatcher and met Jarlaxle in his Zardos Zord disguise. Later, when [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] dived down to Zelifarn's [[Zelifarn's Underwater Lair|underwater lair]], he discovered a [[Neverember Ledger]] among the dragon's hoard -- a key piece of evidence in the [[The Stone of Golorr]] storyline.

**Season 1 Finale (S01E29):** Zelifarn flew [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] to [[Castle Waterdeep]] alongside [[Aurinax]] to meet the Masked Lords and turn over the recovered Neverember gold. During the extended downtime montage, Zelifarn received a roost on the [[Trollskull Manor]] roof and a custom dragon-shaped chair. He was learning to polymorph from [[Aurinax]] so he could join the party for meals in humanoid form. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] even built him a nest. This domestic interlude established Zelifarn as not just an ally but a member of the extended SWOJ household.

### Season 2: Enchantment, Coma, and Liberation

**The Three-Year Gap (S02E05):** When SWOJ returned to [[Waterdeep]] after a three-year time displacement in the [[Shadowfell]], they found [[The Short Rest]] changed dramatically. Dragon roosts on the tavern were now operational, and Zelifarn -- referred to as "a silver dragon" visiting the roosts -- was still in the area, though the party's priorities had shifted to multiple urgent quest threads.

**Farron: The Amnesiac Soldier (S02E06-S02E07):** SWOJ traveled north to [[Amphail]] to investigate disappearances linked to mysterious mists from the [[Kryptwood]]. In the [[Circle Of Dusk]] glade, they found Farron -- an amnesiac former soldier with a head injury, a pipe flute taught to him by the druids, and an intuitive bond with the scarab [[Craw]]. He carried [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]'s [[Sword Of Illusory Explosions|explosive sword]], which cobalt blacksmiths at [[Benzo's Weapon Emporium]] later identified as an illusion weapon whose "explosions" were just flashy runes.

Farron guided SWOJ into the [[Spiral Cavern]] beneath the [[Sword Mountains]], where they discovered the cave was the lair of [[Arcturia]], [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]'s sister, who had been using transmutation magic to transform people and generate the memory-altering mist. When [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] smashed the enchanted mirror at the heart of the cavern with her [[Song Sword]], the illusion magic shattered and Farron was revealed as Zelifarn -- immediately transforming back into an adult-sized bronze dragon. He then carried the entire party to board the [[Concord Jewel]] as [[Arcturia]] retreated aboard it, and the crystal vessel launched them all into the Deep Ethereal Plane.

**The Radiant Citadel and the Shadow Creatures (S02E08-S02E09):** At the [[Radiant Citadel]], Zelifarn -- in his human guise as Farron/Ferrin -- was drawn by a mysterious shadowy figure and a whistling sound toward the [[Preserve Of The Ancestors]] inside the auroral diamond. There he communed telepathically with the [[Rose Quartz Incarnate]], a crystal creature formed from many ancestral dragon spirits, discovering possible family connections and draconic lineage.

The encounter turned deadly when shadow creatures attacked [[Trill]]'s sugar mill. Zelifarn unleashed chain lightning for massive damage, but one of the creatures possessed him by flowing into his mouth, causing him to go rigid and emit the same eerie whistling. [[Mystery]] -- who arrived mid-battle by literally falling from midair onto Zelifarn's back -- used spectral tentacles to rip the creature out. Despite being saved, Zelifarn collapsed into a psychic coma, not physically injured but under deep ensorcellement. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] had a terrifying vision of dark storm clouds with dragons floating adrift, drawn toward a massive behemoth creature with an angler-fish jaw -- the [[Breathless Elder]]. The [[Rose Quartz Incarnate]] settled over Zelifarn to protect him, and he was left in her care at the [[Radiant Citadel]] while SWOJ departed on the long journey to the [[Deep Dragon Temple]] to find answers.

**The Soul in the Necklace (S02E11, S02E24-S02E26):** At the [[Deep Dragon Temple]], SWOJ found [[Melifarn]] -- Zelifarn's ancestor or relative -- also asleep atop a stone plateau, afflicted by the same enchantment. One of the twin portals at the temple led to a translucent, empty version of Zelifarn's lair, confirming his spiritual connection to the place. After the catastrophic events at [[Zhentil Keep]] -- where SWOJ defeated [[Rowan Crooks]] and destroyed the soul engine -- Zelifarn's soul had been separated from his body and placed into a crystal pendant. [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] wore this necklace, and Zelifarn would commune with him from within it. During the [[Raven Queen]]'s dream sequence test in S02E25, Zelifarn briefly manifested, cheering on the party as they fought shadow versions of themselves.

By S02E26, [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] wore the pendant and could communicate with Zelifarn by grasping it. He could hear what was happening around the party, responding with draconic snoring when sleeping and offering encouragement when awake. This was an intimate, bittersweet arrangement -- their powerful dragon ally reduced to a voice in a crystal, his body lying unconscious in a hostile-occupied city light-years away.

**Testimony at the Trial of Jergal (S02E29):** Even as a disembodied soul, Zelifarn played a crucial role. During the [[Trial Of Jergal]] in the [[The Shrouded Citadel]] beneath [[Castle Naerytar (Ruins)|Castle Nehri Tar]], Zelifarn provided testimony that helped SWOJ win the case for captured dragon souls to be sent to the [[Preserve Of The Ancients]] rather than consumed by the dracolich ritual. His firsthand knowledge of draconic matters proved decisive.

**Contact via Sending Stone (S02E30):** As SWOJ flew east from the ruins of Castle Nehri Tar on allied dragons, they contacted Zelifarn via sending stone to discuss the possibility of reaching the [[Radiant Citadel]] through the [[Concord Jewel]].

**The Assault on the Radiant Citadel (S02E32-S02E33):** During SWOJ's three-ship assault through the thunderstorm to reach the [[Moonhole]], Zelifarn's soul pendant -- now worn by [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] -- glowed urgently. He reached out psychically to convey that the [[Preserve Of Ancestors]] was under attack. This intelligence prompted SWOJ to fly ahead without waiting for the ground army.

The liberation of Zelifarn is one of the campaign's most emotionally satisfying sequences. SWOJ sneaked into the occupied [[Radiant Citadel]], gathered intelligence from [[Vincent Trench]], and infiltrated the [[Preserve Of Ancestors]] with the help of [[Sid Squid]]'s distraction. Inside, they found Zelifarn's unconscious bronze dragon body surrounded by piles of [[Soul Stones]], guarded by two heavily armored warriors enhanced with soul crystals -- one projecting a spectral lion head, the other a spectral crocodile. The soul stones acted as magnets, preventing a clean soul transfer.

[[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] cast thunder wave at third level, blasting the stones away from Zelifarn's body and damaging everyone in the blast radius. [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] raged and ripped the golden crystal from the lion guard's armor, triggering a terrifying roar. With the stones cleared, Xae meditated and channeled Zelifarn's soul from the pendant back into his body. The ancient bronze dragon lurched to life, coughing and stretching his wings, and immediately helped fight the remaining guard. He chased down the fleeing lion guard but showed mercy when the man renounced his allegiance to [[Manshoon]]. SWOJ and Zelifarn reunited with hugs.

**The Final Battle (S02E34-S02E36):** Once freed, Zelifarn shared critical intelligence: the two-headed dracolich had claimed the Dawn Incarnate crystals, the speakers were imprisoned at the top of the Citadel, and the concord jewels had been deactivated. Though weakened from his long imprisonment, he provided strategic information that guided SWOJ's assault plan.

Zelifarn accompanied SWOJ during the descent down the [[Radiant Citadel]], flying alongside the hammerhead ship and allied griffins. During the climactic battle at the bottom of the Citadel against [[Manshoon]], Zelifarn circled overhead, breathing lightning at the soul pylon and carrying carrier pigeons between forces to coordinate the multi-front assault. He fought alongside the army of ancient souls raised from the [[Wall Of The Faithless]], contributing to the final liberation of the Citadel.

**Epilogue (S02E38):** In the Season 2 finale, Zelifarn was seen perched on the rooftop of [[Trollskull Manor]] alongside [[Aurinax]], attending the God's Night homecoming celebration in his human form -- back where he belonged, at home with SWOJ.

## Relationships

- **[[Scoot Sparkles]]** -- The closest personal bond. Scoot wore Zelifarn's soul pendant as a necklace for much of Season 2, and the two communed spiritually. Scoot visited Zelifarn's underwater lair, dove into the harbor to see him, and was the first to embrace him upon his liberation. Their relationship is one of mutual loyalty and deep affection.
- **[[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]** -- Xae built Zelifarn a nest at [[Trollskull Manor]], wore his soul pendant, and was the one who meditated to channel his soul back into his body. She served as the psychic conduit for his restoration.
- **[[Aurinax]]** -- The adult (later elder) gold dragon who served as Zelifarn's mentor in polymorphing. The two dragons share a roost at [[Trollskull Manor]] and were present together at the God's Night celebration.
- **[[Melifarn]]** -- An ancestor or relative -- a bronze dragon who resides at the [[Deep Dragon Temple]]. Both were afflicted by the [[Breathless Elder]]'s enchanted sleep. [[Mystery]] originally lived with Melifarn before the temple fell.
- **[[Rose Quartz Incarnate]]** -- A crystal creature formed from ancestral dragon spirits. Communed telepathically with Zelifarn about draconic lineage and protected his unconscious body at the [[Radiant Citadel]] during his long coma.
- **[[Sholeh]]** -- The elder speaker of the [[Radiant Citadel]], herself an ancient brass dragon in disguise. The two ancient dragons fought side by side during the Citadel's liberation.
- **[[Mystery]]** -- The drow warlock who literally fell onto Zelifarn's back during the sugar mill battle and saved him by ripping the shadow creature from his body. Mr. E later helped coordinate the [[Way Of The Balanced Scales]] dragon alliance.
- **[[Jarlaxle]]** -- Connected through the [[Bregan D'Aerthe]] network. Zelifarn provided intelligence about Jarlaxle's operations at the [[The Eyecatcher]] in Season 1.
- **[[Arcturia]]** -- [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]'s sister and the witch who enslaved Zelifarn, transforming him into the amnesiac human "Farron" using her enchanted mirror in the [[Spiral Cavern]].

## Key Items

- **[[Zelifarn's Underwater Lair]]** -- His hoard beneath [[Deepwater Harbor]], containing the [[Neverember Ledger]] among other treasures.
- **[[Zellopharn's Soul Pendant]]** -- A dragon-shaped crystal pendant containing Zelifarn's soul after his body fell comatose. Worn first by [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]], then by [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]. Used as the conduit to restore his soul to his body.
- **[[Sword Of Illusory Explosions]]** -- The "exploding sword" Zelifarn carried in his Farron guise. Actually an illusion weapon with runes that created the appearance of explosions.
- **[[Farron's Pan Flute]]** -- A pipe flute taught to Zelifarn/Farron by the [[Circle Of Dusk]] druids during his amnesia.
