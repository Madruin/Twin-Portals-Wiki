# Squidly

A tiefling orphan turned [[Harpers|Harper]] agent. One of the three street urchins known as the [[Trollskull Alley Irregulars]], and brother of [[Thail]].

**First appearance:** [[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]]
**Total appearances:** 18 episodes
**Faction affiliations:** [[Trollskull Alley Irregulars]], [[Doom Raiders|Doom Network]], [[Harpers]]

## Description

Squidly is a tiefling who first appeared as a young boy wearing an eye patch and swashbuckling clothes, one of three street urchins -- alongside [[Nat]] and [[Jenks]] -- who made their home in the abandoned [[Trollskull Manor]]. After [[Silver Waves of Justice|SWOJ]] took ownership of the manor, the trio was hired as the [[Trollskull Alley Irregulars]] for 10 gold per tenday, running errands and gathering intelligence across [[Waterdeep]]. During these early days, Squidly was nearly cooked alive by [[Esther]], a spectral hag who haunted the manor's attic, and was later wounded by [[Hrabbaz]] while tailing a [[Zhentarim]] agent to the [[Gralhund Villa]]. His resourcefulness became evident when he infiltrated [[Urstul Floxxin]]'s operation at the [[Kolat Towers]] and managed to pickpocket the second Eye of the [[The Stone of Golorr]] from the Zhentarim operative, returning it to SWOJ.

During SWOJ's three-year absence in the Shadowfell, Squidly grew into a teenager and found work with the [[Doom Raiders|Doom Network]] as one of their agents. His brother [[Thail]] -- a ghost who traveled with SWOJ -- was able to reach out to Squidly one final time through a wish stone embedded in the [[Beast of Erua]]. The stone contained Squidly's unfulfilled wish: "I wish my brother came home." Through the stone, Thail touched his brother's shoulder in a vision and said goodbye before moving on.

By the time SWOJ reached [[Neverwinter]], Squidly had become a teenage [[Harpers|Harper]] agent operating out of the [[House of a Thousand Faces]], a clothing boutique that served as the Harpers' base in the city. He provided SWOJ with [[Masks]] uniforms and disguises for their infiltration of the [[Hall of Justice]], and was later seen helping Harpers evacuate civilians to safety during the catastrophic eruption of the [[The Chasm|chasm]]. When SWOJ assembled a crew for their assault on the [[Moonhole]], Squidly volunteered without hesitation, serving aboard the [[Ethereal Galleon]] through the perilous approach to the [[Sword Mountains]], the journey through the [[Deep Ethereal Plane]], and the liberation of the [[Radiant Citadel]]. He proved invaluable as a scout, gathering intelligence about the enemy occupation -- the invasion timeline, the pylons' effects, the curfew, and the dracolich's arrival -- and suggesting that an attack on the bottom conduit could serve as a diversion. During the final battle against [[Manshoon]], Squidly was captured and restrained by enemy forces at the bottom of the Citadel, but [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] freed him from magical restraints. After the campaign's conclusion, Squidly traveled home with SWOJ and moved into a room at [[Trollskull Manor]], delivering a letter from the [[Masked Lords]] to the party.

## Relationships

- **[[Thail]]** -- Brother. Thail died before Season 1 and appeared as a ghost traveling with SWOJ during Season 2. He was able to say goodbye to Squidly through a wish stone vision before his soul was absorbed by the [[Dynamo of Possibility]]. Squidly's deepest wish was for his brother to come home.
- **[[Nat]]** -- Fellow orphan and leader of the [[Trollskull Alley Irregulars]]. The three urchins grew up together on the streets of Waterdeep and later at Trollskull Manor.
- **[[Jenks]]** -- Fellow orphan and member of the [[Trollskull Alley Irregulars]]. The half-orc boy who fancied himself a wizard was Squidly's close companion throughout childhood.
- **[[Thaelin]]** -- Brother (also referred to as Thail in ghost form). A tiefling warlock who once worked for the [[Xanathar Guild]] and joined SWOJ as a guest PC in [[S01E16 - Episode Sixteen|S01E16]].
- **[[Harpers]]** -- Faction. Squidly became a Harper agent during the three-year time skip, operating from the [[House of a Thousand Faces]] in [[Neverwinter]].
- **[[Silver Waves of Justice]]** -- Allies and mentors. SWOJ hired the Irregulars, protected Squidly from the hag, and fought alongside him through the final campaign. He ultimately moved into [[Trollskull Manor]] as a member of their extended household.
- **[[Urstul Floxxin]]** -- Former adversary. Squidly infiltrated Urstul's Zhentarim operation and pickpocketed the second Eye of the Stone of Golorr from him. They later fought on the same side during the assault on the Radiant Citadel.
