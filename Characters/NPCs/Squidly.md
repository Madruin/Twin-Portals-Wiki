# Squidly

A tiefling boy with an eye patch and swashbuckling clothes. One of the three street urchins. Gets captured by the hag and placed in a cauldron.

**First appearance:** [[S01E05]]
**Total appearances:** 12 episodes

## Description

A tiefling boy with an eye patch and swashbuckling clothes. One of the three street urchins. Gets captured by the hag and placed in a cauldron.

One of the three orphan children hired as the Trollskull Alley Irregulars for 10gp per tenday.

Tiefling orphan wearing an eye patch. Member of the Trollskull Alley Irregulars.

Tiefling orphan member of the Trollskull Alley Irregulars. Wounded (arm gashed) by Hrabbaz while following the burned Zhentarim agent to the Gralhund Villa.

Thail's brother, also known as Nat'n Jenkson. His unfulfilled wish ('I wish my brother came home') was crystallized as a glowing red wish stone embedded in the Beast of Erua. Seen through the stone at the Trollskull tavern with regulars, hatching a scheme.

A former child NPC from Season 1, now a teenager working as a Harper agent at the House of a Thousand Faces in Neverwinter. Provides SWOJ with Masks uniforms and disguises for their infiltration plan.

A tiefling young man who volunteers to join SWOJ's crew aboard the Ethereal Galleon for the assault on the moonhole.

A tiefling crew member aboard the Ethereal Galleon. Part of the assembled crew during the approach to the Sword Mountains.

A resourceful tiefling crew member. Sent on a solo scouting mission to gather intelligence about the occupied city. Returns with information about the invasion timeline, the pylons' effects, the curfew, and the dracolich's recent arrival. Suggests attacking the bottom conduit as a diversion while SWOJ enters the preserve.

A tiefling ally of SWOJ who has been captured and restrained by Manshoon's forces at the bottom of the Citadel. Lark frees him from magical restraints during the battle.


## Role in the Story

- **S01E05:** Meet three street urchin children: Nat (mute leader), Jenks (half-orc wizard wannabe), Squidly (tiefling with eye patch)
- **S01E05:** Esther the hag manifests in the attic with a cauldron, attempting to cook Squidly
- **S01E12:** Trollskull Alley Irregulars report seeing the burned Zhentarim agent enter the Gralhund Villa with Hrabbaz; Squidly is wounded
- **S02E02:** They learn Squidly's wish stone was stolen by Strigoi and embedded in the Beast of Erua
- **S02E02:** Ylka touches a glowing red wish stone containing Squidly's wish
- **S02E02:** Thail enters the stone and reaches out to Squidly in a vision, saying goodbye
- **S02E14:** SWOJ reunites with Squidly, now a teenage Harper agent, who provides Masks uniforms and disguises
- **S02E31:** Allies rally to the crew: Squidly, Thelrect Lafina, Shaleheart, Fylith, Doom Raiders, Erstel Floxon
- **S02E33:** SWOJ sends Squidly and Erstel on scouting missions; they learn about the infiltration timeline, curfew, pylons' effects, and the dracolich's recent arrival
- **S02E36:** Lark frees Squidly, Urstal Flocks, and the Doom Raiders from magical restraints
