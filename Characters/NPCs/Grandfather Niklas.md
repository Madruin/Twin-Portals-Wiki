# Grandfather Niklas

**Grandfather Niklas** was the patriarch of the [[Gorge Baileybrew|Bailey Brew]] dwarven clan in [[Prehistoric Waterdeep]], encountered by [[SWOJ]] during the time-travel holiday adventure in [[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|Episode Fourteen]]. He manifested as one of the [[Ghosts Of Solstice Past]] -- a snow dwarf spirit tied to a magical hat -- and was recognized by his descendant [[Gorge Baileybrew|Gorge]] during the Winter Solstice crisis. He posed riddles to the party and warned them about dangers in the region. Later, the vampire wizard [[Hans Von Gruber]] disguised himself as Grandfather Niklas inside [[Zaka's Tomey Tower|Zaka's Tome Tower]], using the grandfather's appearance to deceive the Bailey Brew family.

**First appearance:** [[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

Grandfather Niklas appeared as a snow dwarf spirit with a snowy visage, a cheerful smile, and a big bushy white beard. He was one of nine [[Ghosts Of Solstice Past]] whose spirits stayed bound to magical hats that rested on the snow. When the hats were approached, the ghosts materialized as snow dwarves. Gorge immediately recognized him, exclaiming "Grandfather Niklas! Is that you?" -- indicating Niklas was a beloved and well-known ancestor of the Bailey Brew clan. He carried himself with authority and warmth, posing traditional Solstice riddles to those who sought his guidance.

His likeness was later stolen by [[Hans Von Gruber]], who assumed the grandfather's form inside [[Zaka's Tomey Tower|Zaka's Tome Tower]]. The disguised Von Gruber sat on a small throne playing the harpsichord and watching the enchanted Bailey Brew family with a cheerful smile -- a convincing imitation until the party grew suspicious and Von Gruber's true gaunt, vampiric appearance flickered through during combat.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]:** After [[SWOJ]] was transported to [[Prehistoric Waterdeep]] during the Winter Solstice by the [[Stone of Galore]], they met [[Gorge Baileybrew|Gorge Bailey Brew]], whose daughter [[Zuzu]] had been kidnapped and whose family's handmade toys had been stolen by an evil wizard.
- **[[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]:** In the snowy woods, the party encountered nine magical hats on the ground -- the [[Ghosts Of Solstice Past]]. When approached, the ghosts materialized as snow dwarves. Gorge recognized one immediately: "Grandfather Niklas! Is that you?" The grandfather spirit confirmed his identity, and Gorge told him that [[Zuzu]] had been taken by an evil wizard.
- **[[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]:** Grandfather Niklas looked the party over coolly and posed traditional Solstice riddles as a test, a custom Gorge called "the most Solstice riddles." The ghosts delivered cryptic warnings about [[Krampus]] and clues that would prove essential later in the battle at [[Zaka's Tomey Tower|Zaka's Tome Tower]], including a hint about "frost bite" that [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] remembered during the fight with Von Gruber.
- **[[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]:** Inside Zaka's Tome Tower, [[Hans Von Gruber]] had assumed Grandfather Niklas's appearance to maintain a false scene of family tranquility. The party found what appeared to be the grandfather sitting on a throne by the harpsichord, watching the Bailey Brew family play with clockwork toys. When [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] struck the disguised figure, "this visage of the grandfather flickers" and was replaced by Von Gruber's true form -- a gaunt man with fangs and an 80s-style haircut.
- **[[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]:** After Von Gruber's defeat, the real Grandfather Niklas appeared briefly by the harpsichord in the tower, and later the [[Ghosts Of Solstice Past]] decorated the Bailey Brew family's tree with little hats and ornaments as SWOJ helped deliver toys across the countryside by magical sleigh.
