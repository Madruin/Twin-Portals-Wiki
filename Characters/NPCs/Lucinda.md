# Lucinda

**Lucinda** is a winged and horned tiefling or demon who frequents the [[Dark Market]] in the [[Shadowfell (Deep)|Shadowfell]], and one third of an inseparable trio alongside [[Mikael]] and [[Santorini]]. The three are gregarious social operators who single out newcomers, befriend them immediately, and steer them toward local establishments like [[The Nest]]. Though their exact motives remain unclear, they appear genuinely friendly rather than malicious -- the angel [[Ryloren|Rylerin]] describes them as having "been here for a long time" and considers them "okay," while cautioning visitors not to get too comfortable with them.

**First appearance:** [[S02E21 - Episode Fifty One (S02E21)]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

Lucinda is roughly human-sized (comparable to [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]) with wings and horns, matching the appearance of her companions [[Mikael]] and [[Santorini]]. The trio is described as extremely friendly, casual, and "weirdly relaxed," approaching newcomers with open arms, throwing hands around shoulders, and eagerly swapping stories. They speak Common. Despite their warmth, an insight check by [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] reveals that they deliberately single out new arrivals to get a read on them -- their behavior carries a faint hustle quality, though they are not caught stealing or pickpocketing. The trio hails from [[Avernus]] and claims to be on their second trip to the Shadowfell, having spent roughly twenty days in the [[Dark Market]] by the time [[Silver Waves of Justice|SWOJ]] arrives.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S02E21 - Episode Fifty One (S02E21)|S02E21]]:** Lucinda, [[Mikael]], and [[Santorini]] approach SWOJ shortly after the party enters the [[Dark Market]]. They introduce themselves as friendly visitors from [[Avernus]], explain they have been in the market for about twenty days, and recommend [[The Nest]] as a good place to stay. They describe the [[Dark Market]] as a fascinating place full of interesting "beings" -- and grin rakishly when noting "if it has a soul, it's a person." The trio points out the angel [[Ryloren|Rylerin]]'s stall but keeps their distance from him, admitting they are from "slightly different factions" and are "just being polite." [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]], inherently wary of instantly friendly strangers, runs an insight check and detects that the trio has singled SWOJ out as newcomers and is trying to get a read on them, though nothing overtly malicious is happening. [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] checks his belongings after contact and finds nothing missing. The trio asks SWOJ to "put in a good word" for them at [[The Nest]] and bids them farewell. Later, when SWOJ mentions the trio to [[Ryloren|Rylerin]], the angel confirms they have been in the market "for a long time" and are "okay," but advises: "Just don't get too comfortable with them."

## Relationships

- **[[Mikael]]** and **[[Santorini]]** -- Lucinda's constant companions in the [[Dark Market]]. The three operate as an inseparable social unit, approaching newcomers together and staying together at [[The Nest]].
- **[[Silver Waves of Justice|SWOJ]]** -- Newcomers whom the trio befriends in the [[Dark Market]], sharing drinks and conversation and recommending [[The Nest]].
- **[[Ryloren|Rylerin]]** -- The angel trader in the [[Dark Market]]. The trio keeps a respectful distance from him, citing "slightly different factions." Rylerin considers them harmless but warns SWOJ not to get too comfortable with them.
