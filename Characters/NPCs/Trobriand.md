# Trobriand

One of [[Halaster Blackcloak]]'s six apprentices, referred to as siblings. Trobriand is among the trapped or scattered wizards that [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] is contractually bound to free as part of her pact with [[Asmodeus]].

**First mentioned:** [[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]]

## Overview

Trobriand is named in the document [[Halaster Blackcloak]] presented to [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] listing six apprentices -- [[Jhesiyra]], [[Rantantar]], [[Lyrian]], Trobriand, [[Muiral]], and [[Arcturia]] -- alongside an oath: "I swear to enchackle their souls by 13 Founders Days hence." Xae signed a contract with an avatar of [[Asmodeus]] during the Founder's Day feast to save the [[Cassalanters]] children, binding her to free all six siblings or forfeit her own soul.

Trobriand's current location and condition are unknown. Like the other apprentices, he was trapped or scattered across the planes by unknown circumstances. At the end of Season 2, five of the six siblings -- including Trobriand -- remained imprisoned, with only [[Arcturia]] having been freed.
