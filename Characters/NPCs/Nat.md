# Nat

A mute half-elven orphan who grew from a fearless street urchin into a griffin rider. Leader of the [[Trollskull Alley Irregulars]] and one of [[SWOJ]]'s most loyal allies.

**First appearance:** [[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]]
**Total appearances:** 7 episodes
**Faction affiliations:** [[Trollskull Alley Irregulars]], [[Griffin Riders of Waterdeep]]

## Description

Nat is a mute half-elven girl who communicates entirely through sign language and hand signals, typically interpreted by her companion [[Jenks]]. When the [[Silver Waves of Justice]] first encountered her in [[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]], she was around ten years old -- a small but fearless child leading a trio of street urchins who had taken up residence in the abandoned [[Trollskull Manor]]. Even then she displayed remarkable courage, venturing alone into the manor's haunted tower where she discovered a glowing talisman hidden under the floorboards. When the hag [[Esther]] manifested and pulled the building into a demi-plane, Nat fought back, managing to poke the spectral hag in the eye while the other children cowered.

After SWOJ claimed the manor, Nat and her companions were formally hired as the [[Trollskull Alley Irregulars]], earning 10 gold per tenday to run errands, gather information, and keep watch over [[Short Rest Tavern (Trollskull Manor)|the Short Rest]]. In this role she proved dependable and resourceful -- taking watch at the tavern door, helping follow leads through the city, and keeping tabs on the neighborhood.

Following SWOJ's three-year disappearance into the Shadowfell, Nat grew into an adolescent with shoulder-length hair and a confident, adventurous bearing. She helped [[Tagga]] and [[Jenks]] manage the Short Rest in the party's absence and developed a particular skill with the dragon roosts on the building's upper floors, communicating non-verbally with the visiting dragons and tending to their needs.

By the time of the final campaign arc, Nat had joined the [[Griffin Riders of Waterdeep]], arriving aboard a hammerhead ship at the [[Radiant Citadel]] in half-plate armor astride a griffin. She led aerial forces alongside [[Jenks]] -- now a proper wizard in a blue hat -- coordinating soldiers, casters, and animal scouts in the battle against [[Manshoon]]'s forces.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]]:** SWOJ discovers three street urchins living in [[Trollskull Manor]]: Nat (mute leader), [[Jenks]] (half-orc wizard wannabe), and [[Squidly]] (tiefling with eye patch). Nat is found in the tower clutching a glowing talisman she unearthed from the floorboards. She gives it to the party. During the battle with the hag [[Esther]], Nat pokes the hag in the eye while the other children are frozen with fear.
- **[[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]:** The three orphans are hired as the [[Trollskull Alley Irregulars]] for 10 gold per tenday. Nat communicates through hand signals to [[Jenks]], who negotiates terms with SWOJ on behalf of the group.
- **[[S01E12 - Episode Twelve|S01E12]]:** Nat is present at the Short Rest with [[Jenks]] and [[Squidly]] when SWOJ returns after the fireball investigation. She runs to the tavern door to take watch. The Irregulars report that they followed a burned [[Zhentarim]] agent south and saw him enter [[Gralhund Villa]] with [[Hrabbaz]].
- **[[S01E16 - Episode Sixteen|S01E16]]:** Nat and [[Jenks]] are upstairs at the Short Rest, roughhousing. [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] sends them downstairs to keep an eye on suspicious patrons. Nat rolls her eyes and relays the instructions to Jenks via hand gestures before they head down.
- **[[S01E22 - Episode Twenty Two|S01E22]]:** Nat and [[Jenks]] encounter SWOJ outside [[Renaer Neverember]]'s apartment and report that [[Squidly]] has gone missing from the group.
- **[[S02E05 - Episode Thirty Five (S02E05)|S02E05]]:** Three years have passed. Nat has grown into a teenager with shoulder-length hair and an adventurous look. She manages the dragon roosts atop the Short Rest, communicating non-verbally with visiting dragons. [[Tagga]] says she is proud of how Nat has grown. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] chases after her but has to look up at her now -- the dynamic between them has changed.
- **[[S02E35 - Episode Sixty Five (S02E35)|S02E35]]:** Nat arrives at the [[Radiant Citadel]] aboard a hammerhead ship as a [[Griffin Riders of Waterdeep|Griffin Rider]], wearing half-plate armor and riding a griffin. She signs a greeting to SWOJ and leads approximately 30 soldiers, 5 casters, and animal scouts (including the pigeon [[Sally]]) as reinforcements against [[Manshoon]]'s forces.

## Relationships

- **[[Jenks]]** -- Fellow orphan and her closest companion. Jenks serves as Nat's interpreter, translating her sign language for others. They grow up together at the Short Rest and later serve side-by-side in the final battle.
- **[[Squidly]]** -- The third member of their orphan trio. When Squidly disappears in Season 1, Nat and Jenks report his absence to SWOJ.
- **[[Tagga]]** -- [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]'s mother, who helps raise the children at the Short Rest. Tagga speaks of Nat with pride, noting her skill with the dragons.
- **[[Scoot Sparkles]]**, **[[Ylka Gralhund]]**, **[[Malark Wavesilver]]**, **[[Xaerine Rumblestride]]** -- The members of SWOJ, who hired Nat and the Irregulars, gave them a home, and watched them grow up. Xae in particular had a close bond with Nat as a child.
- **[[Lif Lindgren]]** -- The ghost of the Short Rest, who helped look after the tavern and the children.
