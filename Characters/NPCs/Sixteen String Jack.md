# Sixteen String Jack

## Description

Sixteen String Jack was a bard and core member of [[The Jolly Gents]]. His signature power summoned ghostly additional strings when he played, conjuring an accompanying phantom band of spectral musicians who jammed out on their own instruments around him -- hence the name "Sixteen String," because more strings appeared when he played. He had distinctively long fingers and fought with a lute, using bardic magic to impose disadvantage on enemies and deal area damage. [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] recognized him from obscure Jolly Gents comic books, where he was depicted as a "C-tier" member of the organization.

Jack was a complicated figure -- loyal enough to [[Rowan Crooks]] to carry out missions, but also independently operating in [[Neverwinter]] and gathering intelligence on [[The Masks]] before [[SWOJ]] caught up with him. He provided genuine information about the Masks' operations at the [[Neverwinter Chasm]], but ultimately betrayed SWOJ by screaming for guards rather than fully cooperating. Like many Jolly Gents, his soul was harvested and bound in a phylactery by [[Friar Truck]], raising the question of how much of his loyalty to Crooks was truly voluntary.

At some point before the events of Season 2, Jack stole [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]'s concertina -- the instrument that housed her grandfather [[Mordecai Gralhund]]'s disembodied soul. This theft made the conflict between Jack and Ylka deeply personal.

## Relationships

- **[[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]** -- Jack stole Ylka's concertina, making their rivalry intensely personal. During the lake battle, Jack taunted SWOJ by switching from his lute to Ylka's concertina, blasting spectral notes across the cavern. Ylka responded by redirecting the water to recover her instrument, then shattering the rock Jack stood on. She later mocked his phylactery by shaking it and threatening to "throw that guy in the river again."
- **[[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]** -- Lark recognized Jack from obscure Jolly Gents comic books and initially tried to bond with him as a fan. Jack was flattered by the recognition ("impressive that you can recognize me") and addressed Lark as "young Wavesilver" in the same archaic manner as [[Antediluvian Jake]]. Their rapport soured when Lark pressed him about Rowan Crooks being Manshoon.
- **[[Antediluvian Jake]]** -- Fellow core Jolly Gent. Jack and Jake fought together during the lake ambush and teleported away together with the stolen green power stones. Jack mocked Jake's lackluster combat performance during the battle. Jake was the wizard; Jack was the bard who led the ambush tactically -- described as "the leader this time around."
- **[[Little Joan]]** -- The third member of the lake ambush trio. Joan called out to Jack during the battle, saying "this is a waste of time," to which Jack replied, "No one's watching. Let's get rid of this trash."
- **[[Rowan Crooks]]** -- Jack served Crooks as part of the Jolly Gents, though like many members his soul was bound in a phylactery, leaving the extent of his free will uncertain.
- **[[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]** -- Scoot kicked Jack off a 150-foot cliff into the [[Neverwinter River]] after Jack's betrayal beneath the [[Sleeping Dragon Bridge]]. Jack hit a rock and floated away in the river, though he clearly survived to appear again at the lake.

## Role in the Story

### The Interrogation Beneath the Bridge (S02E15)

SWOJ first encountered Sixteen String Jack in [[Neverwinter]] after a prison break from the [[Hall Of Justice]]. They found him beneath the [[Sleeping Dragon Bridge]], where [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] recognized him from Jolly Gents comic books as a C-tier member of the organization. Jack was flattered by the recognition, noting Lark's "attention to detail" in identifying his distinctively long fingers.

Under interrogation, Jack provided valuable intelligence:
- [[The Masks]] sent a convoy to the [[Neverwinter Chasm]] every month on the full moon
- The convoy was led by a powerful wizard who had carte blanche to enter the city
- Jack had stabbed that wizard, who "didn't bleed" and seemed "made of something other than human body"
- When stabbed, the wizard dropped a small cloth holding a glowing stone -- "the stone had eyes that opened and looked at me" -- an [[Aboleth Stones|aboleth stone]]
- A [[Manshoon]] clone was involved in Neverwinter politics

When SWOJ pressed Jack about [[Rowan Crooks]] being Manshoon, Jack deflected ("I think you're way off base there") and began edging toward the cliff, gauging the drop. Finally, when SWOJ pushed harder, Jack betrayed them by screaming for guards: "Help! They're here! They have me!"

[[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] grappled Jack. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] cast Pass Without Trace to conceal the group. [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] kicked Jack off the 150-foot cliff into the [[Neverwinter River]], where he hit a rock and floated away in the current.

### The Lake Ambush (S02E16)

Jack reappeared alongside [[Little Joan]] and [[Antediluvian Jake]] at the subterranean lake beneath the [[Neverwinter Chasm]], where the three Jolly Gents ambushed [[SWOJ]]. Jack was described as "the leader this time around," arriving on a hovering rock that he used to try to smash the party's boat.

During the battle, Jack deployed his signature power: a haunting melody that caused ghostly band members to appear in the air around him, jamming out on their own instruments. This imposed disadvantage on [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] and [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]'s attacks. He also taunted [[Antediluvian Jake|Jake]] for his lackluster magic missile attacks.

The battle's emotional climax came when Jack dropped his lute and switched to [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]'s stolen concertina, blasting spectral notes that forced everyone to make dexterity saving throws. Ylka used water manipulation to redirect the flow, pushing Jack's rock while drawing her concertina toward the party's boat. She then shattered the rock Jack was standing on, cratering him into the ground.

As the fight turned against the Jolly Gents, [[Antediluvian Jake|Jake]]'s eyes turned white and opalescent; he dissolved into mist and reappeared on a floating force cage, holding up a green portal stone. Jake and Jack teleported away with the stolen green power stones. [[Little Joan]] attempted to follow with her own green stone but fell into the water instead.

### The Phylactery (S02E23)

When [[SWOJ]] infiltrated [[Zhentil Keep]] and discovered [[Friar Truck]]'s phylactery laboratory containing over a hundred bound Jolly Gents souls, Jack's phylactery was among them. During the fight with Friar Truck, one of the phylacteries was knocked off the altar and bounced away -- emitting "a twing of like a spectral band" that reminded the party of Sixteen String Jack.

After defeating Friar Truck, [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] scooped up the phylacteries from the altar and held them up to her ear. From one, she heard music -- Jack's phylactery. She shook it vigorously and joked about throwing "that guy in the river again," a callback to Scoot's cliff-kick in [[S02E15 - Episode Forty Five (S02E15)|S02E15]].

## Abilities

- **Phantom Band** -- Jack's signature bardic power. When he played, ghostly additional strings appeared and spectral musicians materialized around him, creating an accompanying phantom band. This imposed disadvantage on enemy attack rolls and could force dexterity saving throws when amplified through a more powerful instrument.
- **Concertina Blast** -- When Jack switched from his lute to [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]'s stolen concertina (a more powerful instrument), the spectral notes intensified, swirling around the entire cavern and forcing everyone to make dexterity saving throws.
- **Coiled Grappling Hook Apparatus** -- Jack and the other Jolly Gents at the lake wore grappling hook devices coiled on both arms, which they used to traverse the cavern ceiling.
