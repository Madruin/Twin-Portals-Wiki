# Myrkul

An ancient god of death in the Forgotten Realms, sometimes called the Lord of Bones. In the Twin Portals campaign, Myrkul's relics are the target of the [[Cult Of The Dragon]]'s excavation operations in the [[Mere Of Dead Men]] beneath [[Castle Naerytar]].

## Role in the Story

The [[Cult Of The Dragon]] established operations at [[Castle Naerytar]], digging massive excavation pits in the castle bailey to reach underground ruins containing relics of Myrkul. To aid their excavation, the cult set up a magical pump along the [[High Road]] to drain the [[Mere Of Dead Men]] and expose the buried ruins.

When [[SWOJ]] traveled south along the High Road in Season 2, they discovered the cult's drainage operation and destroyed the pump. The [[Raven Queen]] directed SWOJ to destroy these relics of Myrkul, framing the mission as part of her broader opposition to the unnatural manipulation of death and souls.

## Key Connections

- [[Cult Of The Dragon]] -- Excavating Myrkul's relics beneath Castle Naerytar
- [[Castle Naerytar]] -- Location of the cult's excavation for Myrkul's underground ruins
- [[The Raven Queen]] -- Opposes the use of Myrkul's relics; directed SWOJ to destroy them
- [[Mere Of Dead Men]] -- Swampland being drained by the cult to reach the buried ruins
