# Jalaster

**First appearance:** [[S01E01 - Episode One|S01E01]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode (referenced in 1 additional episode)
**Faction affiliations:** [[Harpers]]

## Description

Jalaster (also rendered as "Jelaster" or "Gelaster" in transcripts; full name Jalaster Silvermane) is a [[Harpers|Harper]] field agent stationed at the [[Skewered Dragon]] tavern in [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Dock Ward]]. He is described as a clean-shaven man in his early forties who appears drunk but is actually an undercover operative keeping watch on criminal activity in the ward. Beneath his casual outfit he wears leather armor, and his bearing is "world weary and weather worn and capable." He is not much of a dancer, though he gamely joins in when [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] and [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] invite him onto the floor at the Skewered Dragon.

## Role in the Story

Jalaster is the very first Harper agent that [[SWOJ]] encounters in the campaign. His tip about [[Candle Lane]] sets the party on the investigative trail that defines much of Season 1.

### [[S01E01 - Episode One|S01E01]]

While the party visits the [[Skewered Dragon]] looking for information about the kidnapped [[Floon Blagmaar]], [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] and [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] strike up music and dancing in the tavern. Jalaster, who had been leaning against the wall, lurches forward to join in. During their conversation on the dance floor, Lark asks about the recent gang violence in the city. Jalaster reveals that he is a Harper -- an agent of a secret organization that keeps an eye on the streets and works to stop criminal factions like the [[Zhentarim]], the [[Xanathar Guild]], and the Black Network from terrorizing the people of [[Waterdeep]].

He explains that the men in black leather armor are Zhentarim enforcers and that regular Zhentarim members can blend in without the distinctive uniform. He identifies the Zhentarim's icon as a winged snake diving downward. When Lark asks about joining the Harpers, Jalaster directs him to seek out [[Remallia Haventree|Ramalia Haventree]], a senior Harper who can sometimes be found at the [[Yawning Portal]].

Later in the evening, Jalaster beckons Lark to the tavern door with sudden urgency. He shares intelligence from a fellow Harper agent: five Zhentarim operatives have been seen coming in and out of a nearby alley called [[Candle Lane]], only a few blocks away in the Dock Ward. When Lark asks whether investigating would help his case as a prospective Harper recruit, Jalaster tells him it would be "a good way to get your foot in the door." Lark relays the information to the rest of the party, and SWOJ heads to Candle Lane, where they discover a warehouse marked with the Zhentarim's winged-snake symbol -- and three fresh corpses inside.

### [[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]] (Referenced)

During the episode's talkback segment, the hosts look back on the Harpers' role in the campaign. Jalaster is recalled as "the first of the Harpers that we met in episode one" and "a schwaisty Harper who told you about Ramalia originally." The hosts credit him with having "started that quest line long ago" -- the breadcrumb trail that led SWOJ from the Skewered Dragon to [[Remallia Haventree]], the [[Haventree Estate (Harper Safehouse)|Haventree Estate]], and ultimately to the heart of the Season 1 conspiracy.

## Relationships

- [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] -- Lark is the party member who engages Jalaster in conversation and receives both the Candle Lane intelligence and the referral to Remallia Haventree. Lark expresses interest in joining the Harpers based on Jalaster's encouragement.
- [[Remallia Haventree]] -- A senior Harper whom Jalaster directs Lark to contact. This referral proves pivotal: Remallia becomes one of SWOJ's most important allies across both seasons.
- [[Harpers]] -- Jalaster serves as a field agent monitoring criminal activity in the Dock Ward from his post at the Skewered Dragon.

## Locations

- [[Skewered Dragon]] -- The Dock Ward tavern where Jalaster is stationed and where he meets SWOJ.
- [[Dock Ward]] -- The ward of Waterdeep where Jalaster operates.
- [[Candle Lane]] -- The nearby alley Jalaster directs SWOJ to investigate based on Harper intelligence about Zhentarim activity.

## Notes

- Jalaster Silvermane is based on a character from the *Waterdeep: Dragon Heist* module, where he serves a similar role as a Harper contact in the Dock Ward.
- Despite being a one-scene character, Jalaster's two pieces of information -- the referral to Remallia Haventree and the tip about Candle Lane -- launch both the Harper alliance and the Zhentarim investigation that drive the entire first season.
