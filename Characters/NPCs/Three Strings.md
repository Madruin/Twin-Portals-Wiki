# Three Strings

Three Strings is the resident bard at the [[Yawning Portal]] tavern in [[Waterdeep]], named for his habit of playing a lute with only three strings. A fixture of the tavern's atmosphere across SWOJ's entire adventure, he begins as an underwhelming background performer and gradually becomes part of the tapestry of the party's homecoming.

**First appearance:** [[S01E01 - Episode One]]
**Total appearances:** 3 episodes

## Description

Three Strings is a lute-playing bard who serves as the regular musical entertainment at the [[Yawning Portal]]. He plays a lute with only three strings, apparently believing himself skilled enough to compensate for the missing string -- though his performances are described as off-key and not particularly impressive. Despite his limited talent, he is a consistent presence at the tavern, part of its ambient soundscape of yelling patrons, gambling, and drunken sing-alongs. Over the years he has learned [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka's]] songs and plays them for the regular crowd at the Yawning Portal.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E01 - Episode One]]:** When SWOJ first gathers at the [[Yawning Portal]], Three Strings provides the background music -- playing his lute with only three strings, off-key and not particularly good, though he thinks he is. His playing forms part of the ambient atmosphere before the bar fight with [[Yagra]] erupts.

- **[[S01E11 - Episode Eleven]]:** Three Strings is overshadowed when [[Davil Starsong]] stands on a table playing a lute and draws a massive crowd singing along to "The Wizard War." Three Strings sits in the corner, playing his own lute to nobody's attention, bewildered by being upstaged. When [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] leaps onto the balcony and joins Davil in a rousing duet, the crowd goes wild for them instead. Later, when someone mentions inviting musicians to [[Trollskull Manor|The Short Rest's]] grand opening, Three Strings is dismissively told to "play the secondary stage" -- and a door shuts behind him.

- **[[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38 - Season Two Finale]]:** In a warm callback to earlier episodes, Three Strings kicks up one of [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka's]] tunes at the [[Yawning Portal]] during SWOJ's homecoming visit. The entire crowd sings along, knowing the words by heart after years of performances. Ylka, swept up in the moment, swings herself up onto a table and joins in -- and this time Three Strings jumps up with her. The crowd goes wild. It is a celebratory moment showing how much has changed: the bard who once sat ignored in the corner is now performing Ylka's songs to a packed house, and when she returns, they rock the tavern together.

## Relationships

- **[[Ylka Gralhund]]** -- Fellow performer at the [[Yawning Portal]]. Over the years Three Strings has learned Ylka's songs and plays them regularly for the tavern crowd. By the Season 2 finale, the two share a triumphant joint performance.
- **[[Durnan]]** -- Owner of the [[Yawning Portal]], where Three Strings serves as the house bard.
- **[[Davil Starsong]]** -- Zhentarim agent and fellow lute player who completely upstaged Three Strings during a visit to the Yawning Portal.
