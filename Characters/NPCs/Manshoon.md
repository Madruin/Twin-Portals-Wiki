## Description

Zhentarim leader (or his simulacrum). Appears on the theater balcony during the battle with Zhentarim lieutenants. Casts Bigby's Hand against Scoot. Retreats after Remalia's fireball blasts the balcony.

The Necromancer's true identity, revealed by Rowan Crooks. Founder of the Zhentarim. Had planted a Gem of Seeing in Lark's head to spy on SWOJ. SWOJ set out through a Feywild portal to hunt him.

The last surviving clone of Manshoon, the primary antagonist. He is undead, wearing a half-mask over his face and possessing a metal arm. He has been channeling Rowan Crooks' soul pylon to ascend to godhood. He teleports around the battlefield, casts synaptic static and exhaustion effects. He desperately searches for Rowan Crooks' soul stone. Ultimately defeated when Kelemvor appears and his soul is sent to the Raven Queen.
