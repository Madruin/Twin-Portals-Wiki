# Krampus

A monstrous creature encountered by [[SWOJ]] during the Winter Solstice holiday special set in prehistoric [[Waterdeep]]. Allied with the vampire wizard [[Hans Von Gruber]], the Krampus fought the party in the snowy woods before the battle moved to [[Zaka's Tomey Tower]].

**First appearance:** [[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]
**Status:** Defeated

## Overview

The Krampus appeared during [[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|Episode Fourteen]], a holiday-themed adventure in which [[SWOJ]] was transported to a prehistoric version of Waterdeep during the Winter Solstice. The [[Ghosts Of Solstice Past]] -- supernatural entities manifesting as snow dwarves -- delivered cryptic riddles and warnings about the Krampus before SWOJ encountered the creature in the snowy woods.

After fighting the Krampus, the party tracked a blood trail to [[Zaka's Tomey Tower]], where they discovered the Bailey Brew family under an enchantment. Inside the tower, the Krampus summoned eight gingerbread pain deer from its massive satchel during the chaotic battle against [[Hans Von Gruber]]. The creature fought alongside the vampire wizard but was ultimately defeated by SWOJ.
