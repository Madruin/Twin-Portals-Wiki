# Mara

Mara Melora is a reporter for the [[Waterdeep Wazoo]], the city's populist newspaper. She has covered [[SWOJ]]'s exploits since their earliest days in the [[Dock Ward]], writing flattering exposes of the party's heroics and serving as their go-to journalist whenever they need a story published. Persistent, clever, and always scribbling in her notebook, Mara has remained a loyal media contact for [[SWOJ]] across multiple seasons of the campaign.

**First appearance:** [[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]
**Total appearances:** 6 episodes

## Description

Mara is an enterprising reporter who works for the [[Waterdeep Wazoo]]. She introduces herself simply as "Mara" when she first meets the party, though her full name -- Mara Melora -- is revealed later. She is observant and tenacious, often lingering after other reporters leave to press for the real story. She takes furious notes, sketches scenes in her notebook, and winks conspiratorially when granted exclusive scoops. Despite the party's occasional attempts to dodge her questions, Mara maintains a warm, mutually beneficial relationship with [[SWOJ]]: she gets exclusives, and they get favorable press coverage.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]:** Mara introduces herself to [[SWOJ]] outside the [[Yawning Portal]], identifying herself as a reporter for the [[Waterdeep Wazoo]] and asking questions about their exploits as "the Heroes of the Yawning Portal." The party gives her a brief interview before rushing off to investigate the [[Spouting Fish]]. They jokingly tell their [[Zuber]] driver [[Terry]] to bill the ride to Mara at the Wazoo's offices in the [[North Ward]].

- **[[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]:** The [[Waterdeep Wazoo]] publishes an article featuring a rough drawing of the party and an expose by Mara covering "SWOJ's latest sensation from the Dock Ward." The article appears below the fold but is described as very flattering.

- **[[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four|S01E24]]:** Mara arrives at the [[Short Rest]] alongside other reporters (with familiars [[Sally]] the pigeon and [[Beaker]] the raven) to ask about the [[Kolat Towers]] explosion. She is identified by her full name, Mara Melora, for the first time. While other reporters disperse, Mara lingers with a discerning look, pressing [[SWOJ]] about what really happened. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] pulls her aside and gives her the exclusive scoop: [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] was held captive by the [[Xanathar Guild|Zentarim]] and [[SWOJ]] destroyed the towers to rescue him. Mara furiously writes everything down, sketches [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] on his balcony, and confirms the story will run in that day's paper. She also provides intel about [[Terry]]'s habits, noting he drinks from a bucket at the [[Cranky Cobalt Avard]] on the High Road at least once a week.

- **[[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]]:** After [[SWOJ]] recovers the dragon horde from the [[Vault of Dragons]], the party plans their public reveal and specifically names Mara as someone they need to contact to cover the announcement. They discuss splitting up so someone can go fetch Mara from the Wazoo while others gather [[Mellafarne|Zellopharn]] the bronze dragon and [[Jarlaxle]].

- **[[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]]:** After [[SWOJ]] emerges from the [[Zadio City Music Hall]] following the battle with the [[Xanathar]], [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] yells out to the crowd calling for Mara. She responds from the crowd ("I'm here!"), and [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] announces to her that they have recovered the stolen dragon horde and that the [[House Cassalanter|Cassalanters]] are running a cult. Mara is stunned ("You found a dragon horde?"). The party also discusses bringing Mara along as they are taken into custody, hoping to use press coverage as protection.

- **[[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]:** When [[SWOJ]] returns to [[Trollskull Manor]] after their long Season 2 journey, Mara pops out from behind the bar as part of the homecoming welcoming party, alongside [[Tagga]], [[Simon]], [[Grim Wavesilver]], and other allies.

## Relationships

- **[[SWOJ]]** -- Mara serves as the party's preferred journalist and media contact, granted exclusive scoops in exchange for favorable coverage. The relationship is friendly and mutually beneficial.
- **[[Waterdeep Wazoo]]** -- Mara's employer, a [[Waterdeep]] newspaper with offices in the [[North Ward]].
- **[[Xaerine Rumblestride]]** -- Xae in particular cultivates the relationship with Mara, giving her the exclusive story about [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]'s rescue from the [[Kolat Towers]].
- **[[Scoot Sparkles]]** -- Scoot calls out to Mara in public to break major news stories, such as the recovery of the dragon horde.
- **[[Terry]]** -- Mara knows [[Terry]]'s habits around [[Waterdeep]], noting where and when he can be found drinking from a bucket.
- **[[Trollskull Manor]]** -- Close enough to [[SWOJ]] to be present at their homecoming party behind the bar.
