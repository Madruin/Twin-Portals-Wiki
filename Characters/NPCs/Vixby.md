# Vixby

Vixby is a member of the [[Shard Shunners]], a wererat gang operating in the sewers beneath [[Waterdeep]]. She is part of the tight-knit group that includes [[Vaniel]], [[Kittle]], and [[Dasher Zuber]], living in a hidden chamber beneath the [[The God Catcher|God Catcher]].

**First appearance:** [[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode
**Faction affiliations:** [[Shard Shunners]]

## Description

Vixby is a wererat and member of the Shard Shunners gang. Like the rest of the group, she bears a tattoo of a paw on her palm -- the gang's identifying mark. When SWOJ first encounters the Shard Shunners, Vixby and her companions are tense and ready to fight, only standing down when [[Dasher Zuber]] vouches for the adventurers.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]:** SWOJ follows rat tracks through the sewers behind [[Trollskull Manor]] to an underwater passage leading to the [[The God Catcher|God Catcher]] cavern. Inside a hidden chamber, they encounter [[Dasher Zuber]] and his wererat family -- [[Vaniel]], [[Kittle]], and Vixby. Dasher names Kittle and Vixby as the people he wants to be with, calling the Shard Shunners his "real family." When SWOJ presses the wererats about the rat infestation at their tavern, [[Vaniel]] reveals the gang was hired by [[Emmek Frewn]] to sabotage [[Trollskull Manor (Short Rest Tavern)|Trollskull Manor]]. SWOJ negotiates a truce: they will publicly complain about the rats while the Shard Shunners leave the tavern alone, allowing [[Emmek Frewn]] to keep paying the gang. Vixby and Kittle are present for this negotiation but let Vaniol and Dasher do most of the talking.

## Relationships

- [[Vaniel]] — Fellow Shard Shunner; the silver-furred wererat who serves as the group's leader and spokesman
- [[Kittle]] — Fellow Shard Shunner and close companion
- [[Dasher Zuber]] — Fellow Shard Shunner; a recent convert who chose to become a wererat to stay with the group
- [[Emmek Frewn]] — Employer; hired the Shard Shunners to infest Trollskull Manor
