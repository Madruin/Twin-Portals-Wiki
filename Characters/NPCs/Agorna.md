# Agorna

**Agorna** is a cleric at the [[Temple Of Selune]] in [[Waterdeep]]'s Sea Ward. She is the Wavesilver family's longtime cleric, having been present for ceremonies throughout [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]'s upbringing. When Lark contracts lycanthropy after being bitten by a wereferret, Agorna casts Lesser Restoration on him to help manage the curse, though she cautions that only a more powerful Greater Restoration could remove it entirely.

**First appearance:** [[S01E10 - Episode Ten]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

Agorna is a female cleric of [[Selune]], the goddess of magic and the moon. She has served at the Temple of Selune long enough to have known [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] since childhood, attending family ceremonies for the Wavesilver household. She is warm and familiar with Lark, recognizing him immediately, but also practical and knowledgeable -- she takes careful notes on his unusual condition, asks clinical questions about transmission and symptoms, and is honest about the limits of her own magic. She expresses genuine scholarly interest in studying Lark's wereferret curse, as she has never encountered one before. Her parting blessing -- "Blessings of Selune upon you" -- reflects her devotion to her goddess.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E10 - Episode Ten|S01E10]]:** After Lark is bitten by a wereferret and transforms during the previous night's full moon, SWOJ seeks help at the [[Temple Of Selune]], which is near the [[Wavesilver Mansion]] in the Sea Ward. Lark's family connection to the temple means he knows Agorna personally. She recognizes him and leads the group into a private side chamber to examine him. Lark explains his blood curse -- he transformed into a silvery ferret under the moonlight but managed to resist the feral urge to bite [[Scoot Sparkles]], something Agorna finds remarkable, noting that most lycanthropes lose their minds entirely during their first transformation. She explains that Lesser Restoration will not remove the curse but will make it manageable, allowing Lark to exert more control over his transformations. She notes that Greater Restoration could cure him completely but requires expensive material components she does not have on hand. Agorna casts Lesser Restoration, placing her hand on Lark's shoulder as divine energy fills the room and channels through her touch. The spell cleanses all traces of the wereferret transformation -- his hair returns to normal and he feels healthier than before, with even minor ailments eradicated. Lark attempts a celebratory backflip and crashes into a table, which Agorna takes in good humor. She also asks if she might study his condition at some point, showing academic curiosity about the rare wereferret strain. The group departs with Agorna's blessing.

## Relationships

- **[[Malark Wavesilver]]** -- The Wavesilver family's cleric; she has known Lark since he was a child and treats him with familiar warmth. She heals his lycanthropy symptoms with Lesser Restoration.
- **[[Selune]]** -- Agorna's patron goddess. She serves at Selune's temple and channels the goddess's divine magic.
- **[[Scoot Sparkles]]**, **[[Ylka Gralhund]]**, **[[Xaerine Rumblestride]]** -- Lark's companions who accompany him to the temple. Scoot asks creative questions about parasols and tattoos as lycanthropy countermeasures.
- **[[Wavesilver Mansion]]** -- The Wavesilver family estate is nearby in the Sea Ward, explaining Lark's longstanding connection to the temple and its clergy.
