# Aswel Wavesilver

Aswel Wavesilver is [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]'s mother and the wife of [[Grim Wavesilver]]. A member of [[House Wavesilver]], she is supportive and kind toward her son, contrasting sharply with Grim's cold and disapproving demeanor.

**First appearance:** [[S01E09 - Episode Nine|S01E09]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Description

Aswel is a noblewoman of [[House Wavesilver]] in [[Waterdeep]]. While the family's shipping business is in severe financial trouble -- described as "almost completely underwater" -- Aswel remains a warm and encouraging presence for Lark. She urges him to have patience with his father and expresses pride in SWOJ and the Silver Waves of Justice name. Despite wanting to help Lark financially, she cannot provide money due to the family's dire straits.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E09 - Episode Nine|S01E09]]:** Aswel arrives at the [[Cassalanter Villa]] party alongside her husband [[Grim Wavesilver]]. While Grim is cold and disapproving toward Lark, Aswel is supportive and kind, urging Lark to be patient with his father. She is proud of the Silver Waves of Justice name but cannot offer financial support due to the family's struggles.
- **[[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]:** SWOJ returns to [[Trollskull Manor]] and reunites with Aswel, Grim, and other allies during the homecoming celebration.

## Relationships

- **[[Malark Wavesilver]]** -- Her son. Aswel is supportive and encouraging of Lark's adventures.
- **[[Grim Wavesilver]]** -- Her husband. Aswel serves as an emotional counterbalance to Grim's harsh treatment of Lark.
- **[[House Wavesilver]]** -- Noble family in Waterdeep whose shipping business is failing.
