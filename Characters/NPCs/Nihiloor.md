# Nihiloor

A mind flayer (illithid) and the true power behind the [[Xanathar Guild]]. Nihiloor operated from a spawning chamber deep within the [[Xanathar's Lair]], where it used an eldritch ritual and a green spawning pool to create [[intellect devourers]] -- brain-shaped creatures deployed as sleeper agents planted inside kidnapped civilians. Nihiloor was secretly controlling [[The Xanathar]]'s body telepathically, making the beholder crime lord a puppet of the mind flayer's will. Nihiloor was defeated when [[Scoot Sparkles]] tackled it into its own spawning pool during the collapse of the Xanathar's lair.

**First encounter (referenced):** [[S01E02 - Episode Two|S01E02]] (sewer encounter, referenced in recap)

## Description

Nihiloor was a creature with purple skin, tentacles, and a black robe -- the distinctive appearance of a mind flayer. Mind flayers are aberrations from the Underdark who feed on the brains of sentient creatures and possess powerful psionic abilities including telepathy.

Nihiloor was a master of deception and mental domination. In [[Blue Alley]], it disguised itself as [[Durnan]], the bartender of the [[Yawning Portal]], with purple tendrils emerging from the false mutton chops -- a detail that connected to the mysterious warning the party had heard: "find me, free me, beware the bartender." It cast spells capable of putting [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] to sleep and only revealed its true form when the mirrors in the chamber shattered.

Its most significant power was the telepathic control it exerted over [[The Xanathar]] itself. The beholder crime lord, nominally the leader of one of Waterdeep's most dangerous criminal organizations, was in truth a puppet dancing on Nihiloor's psychic strings. This horrifying truth was not revealed until SWOJ reached the spawning chamber in [[S01E17 - Episode Seventeen|S01E17]].

Nihiloor also performed eldritch rituals using a magical focus and a green spawning pool to create intellect devourers. [[Laeral Silverhand]] explained that the intellect devourers required "some sort of magical focus to be conjured into existence" through a ritual that was "outside of my domain" -- suggesting the magic involved was deeply alien. The intellect devourers were deployed as sleeper agents inside kidnapped civilians, creating a network of unwitting operatives throughout Waterdeep.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E02 - Episode Two|S01E02]]:** *(Referenced in recap.)* SWOJ ventures into the [[Waterdeep Sewers|sewers]] beneath Waterdeep following yellow signs marked with the [[Xanathar Guild]]'s symbol. In the deepest chamber, they encounter a creature with purple skin and tentacles -- later identified as Nihiloor -- torturing a half-orc and cradling an intellect devourer. This is the party's first glimpse of the mind flayer pulling strings behind the guild, though they do not yet know its name or the full extent of its power.
- **[[S01E03 - Episode Three|S01E03]]:** Inside [[Blue Alley]], a legendary magical dungeon hidden within Waterdeep, Nihiloor appears disguised as [[Durnan]] in a mirror-lined egg-shaped chamber containing a crystal unicorn on a pedestal. Purple tendrils emerge from the false Durnan's mutton chops as the disguise begins to fail. Nihiloor casts spells including putting [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] to sleep. When [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] grabs the crystal unicorn and places it in the bag of holding, the mirrors shatter and the mind flayer is fully revealed in its black robe. The party is transported back to the [[Splintered Stair]] before the confrontation can escalate.
- **[[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]:** *(Nihiloor not present.)* [[Ulkoria Stonemarrow]] visits [[Trollskull Manor]] and warns SWOJ about the connection between the [[Xanathar Guild]], intellect devourers, and mind flayers, establishing that the guild's mind flayer is breeding these creatures as a serious threat to the city.
- **[[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]:** [[Jarlaxle]] (as [[Zardoz Zord]]) identifies Nihiloor by name for the first time during a meeting with SWOJ aboard the [[Eyecatcher]]. Jarlaxle explains that Nihiloor works for the Xanathar Guild and is responsible for creating the intellect devourers that have been terrorizing Waterdeep. He frames Nihiloor as a key threat in the escalating gang war.
- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** *(Nihiloor not present.)* [[Laeral Silverhand]] provides extensive intelligence about the Xanathar Guild after the courthouse attack, in which intellect devourers -- Nihiloor's creations -- burst out of spectators including [[Harold]]. Laeral reveals the existence of the spawning pool and explains that the mind flayer uses an eldritch ritual to create the intellect devourers, requiring "some sort of magical focus." She commissions SWOJ to infiltrate the lair and stop the threat. She suggests that destroying the power source or killing the mind flayer would put an end to the spawning.
- **[[S01E17 - Episode Seventeen|S01E17]]:** After the smoke powder explosions collapse the Xanathar's lair, SWOJ escapes through a secret door and pushes deeper into the ruins. They discover [[Flutterfoot Zipswiggle]] dead on a throne, his brain eaten by Nihiloor. Beyond lies the spawning chamber -- a room dominated by a green pool from which an intellect devourer crawls. Here the truth is finally revealed: Nihiloor has been telepathically controlling [[The Xanathar]]'s body, which floats above the pool firing eye rays at the party. The Nihiloor-controlled Xanathar blasts [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] with a death ray (27 necrotic damage) and then turns its disintegration beam on [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] -- Lark crumbles to dust, his hat drifting down onto a pile of ashes as his last words are simply "For justice." In a grief-fueled rage, [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] tackles Nihiloor into the spawning pool. [[Lyrian]] the crystal unicorn then teleports the surviving party to safety at [[Trollskull Manor]] in a burst of rainbow light.
- **[[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]]:** *(Nihiloor not present.)* When [[The Xanathar]] attacks the [[Zadio City Music Hall]] with a force of bugbears nine episodes after Nihiloor's defeat, the beholder appears "glazed over" and "emotionless" -- "not its gloating self that once was" -- possibly still under residual influence from Nihiloor's telepathic control, or perhaps simply broken without its puppetmaster. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]'s thrown dagger delivers the killing blow, ending the Xanathar Guild for good.

## Relationships

- **[[The Xanathar]]** -- Beholder crime lord and nominal leader of the [[Xanathar Guild]]. Nihiloor was the Xanathar's true puppetmaster, controlling the beholder's body telepathically from the spawning chamber. The Xanathar may not have been aware of this control. Even after Nihiloor's defeat, the Xanathar appeared "glazed over" and emotionless in its final battle.
- **[[Terry]]** -- An intellect devourer created by Nihiloor who defected from the mind flayer's control and became a loyal ally of SWOJ. Terry's very existence is a product of Nihiloor's spawning pool rituals.
- **[[Flutterfoot Zipswiggle]]** -- Gnome jester enslaved by the Xanathar Guild. Nihiloor ate Flutterfoot's brain after the lair began to collapse, and SWOJ found his corpse on a throne in the tunnels leading to the spawning chamber.
- **[[Scoot Sparkles]]** -- Half-orc barbarian who tackled Nihiloor into the spawning pool in a grief-fueled rage after watching [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] be disintegrated. This was the act that defeated Nihiloor.
- **[[Jarlaxle]]** -- Drow leader of [[Bregan D'Aerthe]] who identified Nihiloor by name and warned SWOJ about the mind flayer's role in the Xanathar Guild.
- **[[Laeral Silverhand]]** -- Open Lord of Waterdeep who provided intelligence about Nihiloor's spawning pool and commissioned SWOJ to infiltrate the lair and destroy the mind flayer's operation.
- **[[Thaelin]]** -- Former [[Xanathar Guild]] employee who returned to help SWOJ infiltrate the lair. In his final moments, Thaelin appeared "puppet-controlled" -- possibly by Nihiloor -- as he delivered the Eye of the [[The Stone of Golorr]] to [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] before triggering the smoke powder explosion.

## Faction Affiliations

- [[Xanathar Guild]] -- Nihiloor was the guild's true power, operating from behind the scenes while using [[The Xanathar]] as a puppet figurehead. The mind flayer's spawning pool and intellect devourer operations were the guild's most dangerous assets.
