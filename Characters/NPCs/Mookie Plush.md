# Mookie Plush

Mookie Plush is a [[Zhentarim]] lieutenant who operates out of the [[Kolat Towers]] alongside [[Urstul Floxxin]] and under the command of [[Manshoon|The Necromancer (Manshoon)]]. She serves as a guide and field commander for Zhentarim forces, and later appears on the battlefield during the climactic theater battle at the [[Zadio City Music Hall]].

**First appearance:** [[S01E22 - Episode Twenty Two]]
**Total appearances:** 3 episodes
**Faction affiliations:** [[Zhentarim]]

## Description

Mookie is a Zhentarim operative who carries herself with practical authority. At the [[Kolat Towers]], she distributes portal rings emblazoned with the letter "M" and guides visitors through the extra-dimensional sanctum with a matter-of-fact demeanor. During the tower siege in S01E23, she commands Zhentarim forces from an upper floor, issuing orders to wizards and directing the defense. At the [[Zadio City Music Hall]] battle, she appears armed with a crossbow alongside [[Manshoon]] on the theater balcony.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E22 - Episode Twenty Two|S01E22]]:** When SWOJ teleports into the [[Kolat Towers]] via the [[Gralhund Villa]] teleportation circle, they encounter [[Urstul Floxxin]] and Mookie. At the top of the tower, Mookie hands out three rings from a stone pedestal next to a standing arch portal, each bearing the letter "M." She instructs the party to put the rings on and step through the portal into [[Manshoon|the Necromancer's]] extra-dimensional sanctum. Inside the sanctum's eerie glass-walled corridors, Mookie tells the party "you'll get used to it" and leads them forward.

- **[[S01E23 - Episode Twenty Three|S01E23]]:** As SWOJ descends through the [[Kolat Towers]] after exploring the sanctum, they find Mookie commanding Zhentarim forces on an upper floor. She turns and gives orders to a wizard who vanishes and runs downstairs. Mookie confronts the party, saying "What are you doing here? We've been trying to leave for two hours now." She also pointed out a trap on a pressure plate during SWOJ's earlier ascent. When the party asks about [[Urstul Floxxin]], Mookie directs them downstairs, warning "you might get shot."

- **[[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]]:** During the massive battle at the [[Zadio City Music Hall]], [[Manshoon]] (or his simulacrum, wearing a mask) appears on the theater balcony and casts Bigby's Hand to crush [[Scoot Sparkles]]. Flanking him are Zhentarim lieutenants including Mookie Plush, armed with crossbows and overlooking the battle from above. When [[Remalia Haventree]] launches a fireball at the [[Xanathar]], the blast sends Manshoon and his lieutenants flying back from the balcony edge, effectively ending their involvement in the fight.

## Relationships

- [[Manshoon]] — Commander; Mookie serves as one of his Zhentarim lieutenants
- [[Urstul Floxxin]] — Fellow Zhentarim operative at the Kolat Towers
- [[Scoot Sparkles]] — Adversary; Mookie overlooks the theater battle where Manshoon attacks Scoot
- [[Remalia Haventree]] — Adversary; her fireball blasts Mookie and the other Zhentarim off the balcony
