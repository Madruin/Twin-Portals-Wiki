# Max

**Max** is a guard and messenger employed at [[Cassalanter Villa]], serving the [[Cassalanter Family|Cassalanter]] household. He is one of the few villa guards to interact directly with [[SWOJ]], recognizing them by sight and vouching for their entry to the Cassalanter soiree.

**First appearance:** [[S01E09 - Episode Nine|S01E09]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Description

Max serves as both a guard and messenger for the [[Cassalanter Family|Cassalanters]]. He previously delivered party invitations to [[SWOJ]] on the Cassalanters' behalf, making him familiar with the group before they arrived at the villa. He appears to be a practical, no-nonsense type -- willing to overrule a more cautious fellow guard when he knows the visitors are legitimate.

## Role in the Story

In [[S01E09 - Episode Nine|S01E09]], Max is stationed at the gate of [[Cassalanter Villa]] when [[SWOJ]] arrives for the Cassalanter soiree. When a fellow guard hesitates over the group's invitations, Max steps in and vouches for them, recognizing them as the same people he had delivered the invitations to. He tells the other guard to relax and waves [[SWOJ]] through, allowing them entry to the villa grounds.

In [[S01E10 - Episode Ten|S01E10]], Max reappears in the aftermath of the cultist attack on the Cassalanter ballroom. As the [[Cult of Asmodeus|cultists]] are being rounded up and the guards investigate the chaos, Max walks into the ballroom with a group of other guards. He is described as "the guard, messenger guy" for the Cassalanters, and he shakes his head at [[SWOJ]] while observing the aftermath of the incident, seemingly exasperated by the situation. The guards then proceed to lock up the conscious cultists and transport them to the [[Seaward Watch Post|Seaward city watch post]] for imprisonment.

## Relationships

- **[[Victoro Cassalanter]] and [[Amalia Cassalanter]]** -- Max's employers. He serves as a guard and messenger for their household.
- **[[SWOJ]]** -- Max is familiar with the adventuring party, having delivered their invitations to the Cassalanter soiree. He recognizes them on sight and vouches for them at the gate.
- **[[Willifort Crowell]]** -- Fellow member of the Cassalanter household staff. Willifort serves as majordomo while Max works as a guard and messenger.
