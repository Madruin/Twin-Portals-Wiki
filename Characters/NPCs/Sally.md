## Description

A pigeon from the Sea Plum, now roosting on the roof of Trollskull Manor. Ylka speaks to her via Speak with Animals.

Pigeon that survived the fireball explosion, badly burned on one side. Recovered by Xae and given to Pog Waggletop.

SWOJ's pigeon companion. Provides aerial reconnaissance during the rooftop chase across the City of the Dead, spotting the spined devils and Willaford Crowell.

A pigeon that Ylka communicates with via Speak with Animals. Has eagle bodyguards on the roof of Trollskull Manor.

A pigeon familiar belonging to one of the reporters.

A pigeon ally. Her 'wingmen' join the bird reinforcements during the theater battle, though Sally herself does not appear.

A bird (pigeon/raven) allied with SWOJ. Expanded her influence over the city's bird population with the Ravens of the Sea Ward out of the picture.

A carrier pigeon among the animal scouts brought by the hammerhead reinforcements. Used for communication between forces.

A flying creature (likely a bird or familiar) present at Trollskull Manor rooftop. Calls out in a 'pigeonie way' in the morning.
