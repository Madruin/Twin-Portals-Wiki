# Sally

A scrappy, wild-eyed pigeon originally from the [[Sea Plum]], Sally became a permanent resident of [[Trollskull Manor]] and eventually built a bird intelligence network spanning all of [[Waterdeep]], earning herself the self-appointed moniker "Vinnie the Fish."

**First appearance:** [[S01E06 - Episode Six|S01E06]]
**Total appearances:** 14 episodes

## Description

Sally is a pigeon with a fierce personality far larger than her small frame. Originally one of [[Pog Waggletop]]'s messenger pigeons aboard the [[Sea Plum]], she first appeared delivering a frantic message from Pog to [[Scoot Sparkles]] about the ship's sinking. Wild-eyed and missing feathers even before her worst ordeal, Sally took up residence on the roof of [[Trollskull Manor]], where she became a fixture of the [[Silver Waves of Justice|SWOJ]] household.

[[Ylka Gralhund]] is Sally's primary point of contact among the party, communicating with her through Speak with Animals and later through the Avian Circlet woven into her hair. Sally speaks in a gruff, no-nonsense manner and has an insatiable appetite for eggs. She is fiercely territorial and independent, once telling the party "Don't worry, you're under the protection of Sally and my organization."

Sally survived the devastating fireball that struck [[Trollskull Alley]] during the grand opening of [[The Short Rest]], though she was badly burned on one side and lost most of her feathers. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] rescued her and returned her to [[Pog Waggletop]], who nursed her back to health. Despite her injuries, Sally remained remarkably resilient and only grew tougher over time.

By mid-Season 1, Sally had organized an entire network of birds across Trollskull Alley and beyond, flanked by two eagle bodyguards and commanding pigeons, crows, ravens, and hawks. She adopted the alias "Vinnie the Fish" and ran her avian network like a mob boss, with lieutenants reporting in and delivering messages across the city. Her subordinate [[Beaker]], a raven, served as one of her key operatives. After the defeat of the [[Xanathar]] and the dismantling of the Ravens of the [[Sea Ward]], Sally expanded her influence across the entire city, with tough-looking pigeons patrolling the skies of Waterdeep on her behalf. She even developed silver talons and led her pigeons in devouring a Cassalanter imp spy.

Sally remained active through the events of Season 2, appearing among the animal scouts brought by the hammerhead reinforcements at the [[Radiant Citadel]], where she rode atop [[Sholeh]]'s horns alongside her pigeon entourage. Upon SWOJ's return to Waterdeep at the close of Season 2, Sally was back at [[Trollskull Manor]], calling out in her characteristic "pigeonie way" each morning.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E06 - Episode Six|S01E06]]:** Sally delivers a pigeon message from [[Pog Waggletop]] to [[Scoot Sparkles]] at [[Trollskull Manor]], alerting the party that the [[Sea Plum]] has sunk in [[Deepwater Harbor]]. Pog notes she has been acting "crazy" after eating and worries about her.
- **[[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]:** Sally is found roosting on the roof of [[Trollskull Manor]]. [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] tries to coax her down but fails; [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] casts Speak with Animals and lures her inside with food. Sally marches into the tavern demanding eggs and reports seeing pests coming in and out of the building. She refers to [[Leah]]'s cat as "Mr. Fluffstopples" with disdain.
- **[[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]:** [[Pog Waggletop]] visits [[Trollskull Manor]] and checks on Sally on the roof. He expresses happiness that she has moved in, calling her "Good old Sally."
- **[[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]:** After the fireball explosion devastates [[Trollskull Alley]] during [[The Short Rest]]'s grand opening, the party sees dead pigeons on the ground and cries out "Sally!" in alarm, fearing for her safety.
- **[[S01E12 - Episode Twelve|S01E12]]:** Sally is found alive but badly burned on one side, barely able to fly. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] chases and captures her, and [[Pog Waggletop]] takes her in to nurse her back to health, vowing to rebuild his pigeon family starting with Sally.
- **[[S01E13 - Episode Thirteen|S01E13]]:** During the rooftop chase across the [[City of the Dead]] on the night of the [[Feast of the Moon]], Sally leads a flock of pigeons that swarm and slow down two spined devils carrying the [[Stone of Golorr]]. Despite still being badly scorched and nearly featherless, she bombards them with beak and claws, showing a shocking awareness of SWOJ's mission.
- **[[S01E16 - Episode Sixteen|S01E16]]:** Sally has recruited two eagle bodyguards and established herself on the [[Trollskull Manor]] rooftop as the head of a bird organization. She introduces herself as "Vinnie the Fish" and assures [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] she has the manor "locked down." [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]'s hawk familiar develops a close fellowship with Sally, joining the network of birds that check in, deliver messages, and patrol the area.
- **[[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four|S01E24]]:** Sally surveys the crowd of reporters outside [[The Short Rest]] with a "pigeon-like scowl" and warns [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] via the Avian Circlet that the press has been tipped off about the [[Kolat Towers]] incident. When [[Zardoz Zord]] shoots an imp disguised as a crow, Sally's pigeons devour the imp's remains on the ground. Sally now has silver talons. She sends her lieutenant [[Beaker]], a raven, to provide overwatch for SWOJ's mission to [[Cassalanter Villa]].
- **[[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]]:** During the battle in the [[Zadio City Music Hall]], Sally's pigeon wingmen arrive as reinforcements, though Sally herself is not present.
- **[[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]]:** At the [[Cassalanter Villa]] [[Founders Day]] feast, the party wishes they had brought Sally along when they encounter suspicious birds that turn out to be devil spies.
- **[[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]]:** During the post-campaign downtime, [[Trollskull Manor]]'s attic is renovated into a roost for [[Zelifarn]], visiting dragons, and Sally's entourage. The pantry is expanded to supply eggs for Sally. With the Ravens of the [[Sea Ward]] now out of the picture, Sally expands her influence across the entire city, and tough-looking pigeons are frequently seen patrolling Waterdeep's skies.
- **[[S02E05 - Episode Thirty Five (S02E05)|S02E05]]:** After SWOJ's three-year absence in the [[Shadowfell]], Sally's roost is still visible on [[Trollskull Manor]]'s roof with birds coming and going, though Sally herself is not immediately seen. The party questions how long pigeons live. Xae notes it may be "Sally Junior."
- **[[S02E35 - Episode Sixty Five (S02E35)|S02E35]]:** Sally appears among the animal scouts brought by [[Nat]] and [[Jenks]] aboard the hammerhead reinforcement ship at the [[Radiant Citadel]]. She is described as a mangy-looking pigeon recognized by the party. She rides between [[Sholeh]]'s eyes atop the ancient brass dragon's horns, in fierce conversation with her pigeon entourage.
- **[[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]:** Upon SWOJ's return to [[Waterdeep]], Sally is back at [[Trollskull Manor]], flying around the rooftop. The next morning, she calls out "in a pigeonie way" as everyone prepares for the celebratory party.
