# Kai

Archer in the [[Hare Company]], a band of [[Waterdeep Military|Waterdeep army]] deserters turned bandits on the [[The Long Road|Long Road]]. One of three archers who ambushed [[SWOJ]] during their journey north toward [[Amphail]].

**First appearance:** [[S02E06 - Episode Thirty Six (S02E06)|S02E06]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode
**Faction affiliations:** [[Hare Company]], formerly [[Waterdeep Military]]

## Description

Kai was a former [[Waterdeep Military|Waterdeep soldier]] who served as an archer. After the army's devastating defeat at the Battle of [[Red Larch]] during the war with [[Neverwinter]], he deserted along with the rest of the [[Hare Company]] under [[Jonas]]'s leadership. The company turned to banditry along the [[The Long Road|Long Road]], ambushing travelers for food, medicine, and coin.

During the ambush on [[SWOJ]], Kai was one of three archers positioned on the valley walls above the road with longbows drawn, wearing leather armor. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] spotted all three archers using the [[Avian Circlet]] to see through a crow's eyes.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S02E06 - Episode Thirty Six (S02E06)|S02E06]]:** Kai participated in the [[Hare Company]]'s ambush of [[SWOJ]] on the [[The Long Road|Long Road]] between [[Waterdeep]] and [[Amphail]]. He was one of three hidden archers positioned on the valley walls, spotted by [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] through the [[Avian Circlet]]. During the ensuing battle -- which featured [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] raging into melee, [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]'s "Murderation" tactic, and [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] wild shaping into a giant centipede -- the archers fired on [[SWOJ]] from their elevated positions. After the [[Hare Company]] yielded, Kai was the last archer to come down from the ridge with his bow still drawn. [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] walked over to him separately, asked his name and his story, and verified that his account matched what [[Jonas]] and [[Nina Blackthorn]] had already told the party. Satisfied, Scoot lowered his axe and walked Kai over to the group. Along with the rest of the [[Hare Company]], Kai was convinced by [[SWOJ]] to abandon banditry and return to military service. The Hare Company traveled with [[SWOJ]] to [[Amphail]], where they headed for a tent bearing the company's purple rabbit insignia. Scoot gave each member of the company a respectful handshake before parting ways.
