# Thail

See [[Thaelin]]. "Thail" is the name used for Thaelin's ghost form after his death in the [[Xanathar's Lair]] collapse. He appeared to [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] in Season 2 as a spectral presence who could possess living bodies.
