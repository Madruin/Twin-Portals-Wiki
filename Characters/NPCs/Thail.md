# Thail

Ghost of a tiefling, former companion of SWOJ who died previously. Brother of Squidly (Nat'n Jenkson). Appeared to the party in the Shadowfell. Can possess living bodies.

**First appearance:** [[S02E01 - Episode Thirty One (S02E01)]]
**Total appearances:** 4 episodes

## Description

Ghost of a tiefling, former companion of SWOJ who died previously. Brother of Squidly (Nat'n Jenkson). Appeared to the party in the Shadowfell. Can possess living bodies.

Ghost of a tiefling, brother of Squidly. Possessed Lark's body. Found Squidly's wish stone on the Beast of Erua and entered it to say goodbye to his brother.

Ghost of a tiefling. Cast fireball from his wish stone crystal during the ribcage battle. Scouted the staircase leading up through the beast's trachea at episode's end.

A ghost whose soul is absorbed by green lightning from the Dynamo. His soul is preserved in a crystal that Ylka carries through the transition back to the Great Beast's skull.


## Role in the Story

- **S02E01:** The ghost of Thail appears to the party
- **S02E02:** Thail demonstrates he can possess living bodies by entering Lark
- **S02E02:** Thail enters the stone and reaches out to Squidly in a vision, saying goodbye
- **S02E03:** Thail casts fireball from his wish stone crystal
- **S02E03:** Thail scouts the staircase through the beast's trachea, hearing ominous sounds from above
- **S02E04:** Green lightning from the Dynamo absorbs the ghosts of Thail and Teriador the Rose Knight
- **S02E04:** Ylka brings Thail's soul crystal through the transition
