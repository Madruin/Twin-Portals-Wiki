# Fel'Rekt Lafeen

Fel'Rekt Lafeen is a drow trans man and lieutenant of [[Bregan D'Aerthe]], serving as [[Jarlaxle]]'s trusted agent in both [[Waterdeep]] and [[Neverwinter]]. A skilled operative armed with multiple firearms, Fel'Rekt acts as SWOJ's primary point of contact with the drow mercenary organization throughout both seasons of the campaign. Resourceful and loyal, he uses disguises and aliases to operate covertly in hostile cities, gathering intelligence and providing critical support to the party at key moments. His mysterious disappearance during the Neverwinter arc underscores the dangers of the political landscape the party navigates in Season 2.

**First appearance:** [[S01E06 - Episode Six|S01E06]]
**Total appearances:** 10 episodes
**Faction affiliations:** [[Bregan D'Aerthe]]

## Description

Fel'Rekt is a drow originally from [[Menzoberranzan]] who serves as a lieutenant in Jarlaxle's Bregan D'Aerthe. When first encountered in Season 1, he is found imprisoned by cultists inside the [[God Catcher]], armed with guns -- an unusual weapon choice that immediately marks him as distinctive among the NPCs of Waterdeep. As a Bregan D'Aerthe operative, he moves through the world with careful tradecraft, using disguises and aliases to conceal his drow identity in cities where dark elves are viewed with suspicion.

By the time SWOJ encounters him again in Season 2 at the floating island of [[Driftwood]], Fel'Rekt has become notably more armed, carrying four pistols and disguising himself as a human sailor with an eye patch hiding a glowing red drow eye. In [[Neverwinter]], he adopts the alias "Felrecht Laphine" (an anagram whose backward reading is "Left to Kerr") and appears at the [[Moonstone Mask]] disguised as a human with a braided beard and half-face mask, using a ring of disguise or shapeshifting magic.

Fel'Rekt carries himself with a deep sense of duty toward those he is assigned to protect. After the catastrophic fireball at the [[Short Rest]] opening, he is consumed with guilt, believing he failed in his responsibility to keep SWOJ safe. He stays behind to clean up the carnage and guard the tavern rather than fleeing like many others.

## Role in the Story

### Season 1

- **S01E06:** SWOJ discovers Fel'Rekt imprisoned by cultists inside the [[God Catcher]], a drow male armed with guns. The party rescues him, establishing their first connection to [[Bregan D'Aerthe]].
- **S01E07:** Fel'Rekt visits [[Trollskull Manor]] at noon as a formal Bregan D'Aerthe envoy. He reveals that his organization has been tracking SWOJ, shares intelligence about devil worship among [[Waterdeep]]'s nobles and the [[Wavesilver]] family crest connection, and reveals that [[Yagra]] was the one who sent the mysterious note signed "Y."
- **S01E11:** Fel'Rekt attends the grand opening of the [[Short Rest]] alongside [[Jarlaxle|Zardos Zord]]. When the catastrophic fireball explosion rocks Trollskull Alley -- killing ten or more people and badly injuring [[Tagga]] -- Fel'Rekt is deeply affected. He stays behind to help clean up and guard the tavern, feeling profoundly guilty that he failed in his duty to protect SWOJ.
- **S01E12:** Fel'Rekt returns to the Short Rest with [[Jarlaxle|Zardos Zord]]. He promises to defend the tavern while SWOJ investigates the [[Gralhund Villa]] to pursue the [[The Stone of Golorr]].
- **S01E16:** A carnival worker operating as Fel'Rekt's agent approaches SWOJ at the [[Sea Maiden's Faire]]. The agent gives [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] a pistol and asks the party to search for [[Narl Xibrindas|Nar'l Xibrindas]], a Bregan D'Aerthe spy embedded within the [[Xanathar Guild]]'s lair.

### Season 2

- **S02E09:** SWOJ reunites with Fel'Rekt at the [[Eyecatcher]] in [[Driftwood]]. He is disguised as a human sailor with an eye patch concealing a glowing red drow eye and is now armed with four pistols -- notably more weaponry than before. He greets the party and briefs them on Driftwood and the dragon turtle [[Kurrihan|Kura Han]] who lives beneath the floating island.
- **S02E14:** Operating under the alias "Felrecht Laphine," Fel'Rekt has infiltrated [[Neverwinter]] ahead of SWOJ, spending a tenday gathering intelligence. He meets the party at the [[Moonstone Mask]], disguised as a human with a braided beard and half-face mask, and introduces them to [[Slippery Shim|Shimmer Brackenwell]], a tabaxi smuggler and information broker. Fel'Rekt disappears later that night after a murder occurs at the Moonstone Mask.
- **S02E15:** Fel'Rekt's fate becomes a central mystery. [[Renaer Neverember]] reveals in the resistance base beneath [[Castle Never]] that Fel'Rekt has vanished since the night of [[Shimmer Brackenwell]]'s murder. SWOJ speculates he may have been killed, or may have been the one who impersonated [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] -- the murderer wore a red hat and vanished into mist. His ring of disguise makes him a suspect, but his loyalty makes foul play equally plausible.
- **S02E17:** Fel'Rekt resurfaces during the chasm eruption crisis in Neverwinter. He is found fighting alongside [[Teriador|Tari Dorr]], another Bregan D'Aerthe member, in the streets of the city against invading creatures from the chasm, acting on [[Jarlaxle]]'s standing orders to defend Neverwinter.
- **S02E31:** Fel'Rekt volunteers to join SWOJ's crew aboard the [[Ethereal Galleon]] for the assault on the moonhole. He serves as ballista crew, helping to operate the ship's heavy weapon.
- **S02E32:** During the periton attack and subsequent battles in the [[Sword Mountains]], Fel'Rekt works alongside [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] to operate the main ballista on the Ethereal Galleon, efficiently reloading the weapon between shots.

## Relationships

- **[[Jarlaxle]]** -- Fel'Rekt's commander and the leader of Bregan D'Aerthe. Fel'Rekt acts on Jarlaxle's orders throughout the campaign, serving as his primary liaison to SWOJ.
- **[[Scoot Sparkles]]** -- Close operational partnership. Fel'Rekt gives Scoot his first firearm (via his carny agent in S01E16) and later they fight side by side operating the Ethereal Galleon's ballista in the final arc.
- **[[SWOJ]]** -- Fel'Rekt serves as SWOJ's primary Bregan D'Aerthe contact, providing intelligence, protection, and material support across both seasons. His guilt after the fireball reveals genuine personal investment beyond mere duty.
- **[[Yagra]]** -- Fellow Bregan D'Aerthe operative. Yagra sent the "Y" note that initially drew SWOJ's attention, and Fel'Rekt reveals this connection when he visits Trollskull Manor.
- **[[Narl Xibrindas]]** -- A Bregan D'Aerthe spy embedded in the Xanathar Guild's lair. Fel'Rekt tasks SWOJ with locating him through his carny agent.
- **[[Slippery Shim|Shimmer Brackenwell]]** -- Fel'Rekt introduces SWOJ to this tabaxi smuggler and information broker in Neverwinter. Shimmer's murder and Fel'Rekt's subsequent disappearance become intertwined mysteries.
- **[[Renaer Neverember]]** -- Both serve as SWOJ allies in Neverwinter's fractured political landscape. Renaer is the one who reports Fel'Rekt's disappearance to the party.
