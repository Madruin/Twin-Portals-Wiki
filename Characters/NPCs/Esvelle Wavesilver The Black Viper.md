# Esvelle Wavesilver (The Black Viper)

Esvelle Wavesilver is a masked vigilante operating in [[Waterdeep]] under the identity of **the Black Viper**. She is the mother of [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] and wife of [[Grim Wavesilver]]. A skilled thief and fighter, she brings justice to the streets of Waterdeep from the shadows while maintaining her public role as a member of the noble but financially struggling [[House Wavesilver]].

**First appearance:** [[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]] (as the Black Viper)
**Identity revealed:** [[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]]
**Total appearances:** 7 episodes

---

## Description

As the Black Viper, Esvelle operates as a masked vigilante woman, fighting threats in Waterdeep's sewers and streets. Her signature calling card is a **black onyx gemstone rose**, which she leaves for those she aids. [[Laeral Silverhand]] describes her as "a vigilante bringing justice to the streets."

In her civilian identity, Esvelle is a supportive and kind mother who urges Lark to have patience with his father [[Grim Wavesilver|Grim]]. She is proud of [[SWOJ]] and the Silver Waves of Justice name. Despite her warmth, the Wavesilver family is in severe financial trouble -- their shipping business is "almost completely underwater" -- and she cannot provide money to Lark.

Esvelle is a master infiltrator, capable of breaking into a high-security anti-magic prison to free SWOJ from captivity.

> **Note on name:** Esvelle's name is rendered variously across episode transcripts as "Isvel," "Isabelle," "Aswel," and "Aspell" due to transcription of audio. These all refer to the same character.

## Role in the Story

### Season 1

- **[[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]:** First appears as the Black Viper -- a mysterious masked vigilante woman who helps SWOJ fight three grell in the sewers beneath the Spouting Fish tavern. Leaves a black onyx gemstone rose for [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] before disappearing into the night.
- **[[S01E09 - Episode Nine|S01E09]]:** Attends the Cassalanter party with her husband [[Grim Wavesilver|Grim]] (appearing under her civilian identity). Grim is cold and disapproving of Lark, but Esvelle is supportive and kind, urging Lark to have patience with his father. Proud of SWOJ and the Silver Waves of Justice name. Cannot give money due to the family's financial struggles.
- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** Discussed by name during SWOJ's meeting with [[Laeral Silverhand]] after the trial. The party recalls how the Black Viper previously helped them fight a mind flayer in the sewers. Laeral recognizes her as a vigilante and offers to put out feelers to recruit her for the Xanathar's Lair infiltration mission.
- **[[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]]:** Her identity is revealed -- Isabelle/Esvelle Wavesilver is the Black Viper and Lark's mother. She breaks SWOJ out of a high-security anti-magic prison where they have been held for about a week following the Xanathar battle. After the escape (through [[Vincent Trench]]'s portal to [[Trollskull Alley]]), she discusses the Cassalanter devil-worship plot with SWOJ, revealing that she and Grim already knew about the devil worship but did not know the terms of their deal involving the children's souls. She explains that Grim knows about her vigilante identity and that they kept it secret to protect Lark, though they suspected he was "following the family business regardless." She attends the Cassalanter Founders Day feast with Grim while SWOJ infiltrates separately.
- **[[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]]:** During the post-Season 1 downtime montage, Lark reconnects with his parents at the Wavesilver household and trains in vigilantism with his mother. Together they adopt the vigilante duo names **"the Black Viper and Silver Fair"** and create adventure comics based on their exploits.

### Season 1 Retrospective

- **[[S01E30 - Episode Thirty|S01E30]]:** In the Season 1 talkback panel, the cast discusses how Isvel/the Black Viper being Lark's mother happened partly by accident through Justin Peck's character creation and backstory choices, becoming a key narrative thread of the campaign.

### Season 2

- **[[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]:** Present at [[Trollskull Manor]] when SWOJ returns to Waterdeep in the Season 2 finale, reuniting with the party alongside [[Grim Wavesilver|Grim]] and other beloved NPCs.

## Behind the Scenes

The revelation that the Black Viper was Lark's mother emerged partly by accident through Justin Peck's character backstory choices. The D&D sourcebook *Waterdeep: Dragon Heist* includes the Black Viper as a pre-written NPC (Esvelle Rosznar); in Twin Portals, she was adapted into the Wavesilver family as Lark's mother, weaving the published adventure material into the original character's story.
