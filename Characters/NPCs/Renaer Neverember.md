# Renaer Neverember

> *"Spelled R-E-N-A-E-R."*

Renaer Neverember is the estranged son of [[Dagult Neverember]], the former Open Lord of [[Waterdeep]] and Lord Protector of [[Neverwinter]]. A handsome nobleman with hip-length red-gold blonde hair, Renaer carries a rapier and has proven himself one of [[Silver Waves of Justice|SWOJ]]'s most steadfast allies across both seasons of their adventures. What began as a rescue from a [[Zhentarim]] warehouse evolved into a deep, enduring partnership -- Renaer funding SWOJ's early ventures, providing critical intelligence, leading a resistance movement in [[Neverwinter]], and ultimately helping save the city from destruction.

**First appearance:** [[S01E02 - Episode Two|S01E02]]
**Total appearances:** 21+ episodes
**Faction affiliations:** [[House Neverember]], [[The Resistance]]
**Residence:** Near the [[North Ward|North Gate]] in Waterdeep (Season 1); tunnels beneath [[Castle Never]] (Season 2, early); [[Hall of Justice]] / [[Castle Never]] (Season 2, later)

## Description

Renaer is a young nobleman who has spent most of his adult life trying to distance himself from his father's legacy of corruption. Where [[Dagult Neverember]] is calculating and politically ruthless, Renaer is warm, generous, and genuinely affectionate toward his friends. When reunited with SWOJ after three years apart, he greets them with open warmth rather than political formality.

Physically, he is described as a handsome man with distinctive hip-length red-gold blonde hair. He is armed with a rapier and, when the situation demands, is willing to fight -- as demonstrated when he grabbed daggers to join the search for [[Floon Blagmaar]] and later attempted to stab the [[Xanathar]] in the eye during the theater battle. By Season 2, his hair color is described more simply as red.

Renaer's relationship with his father is strained but not irreparable. He came to Neverwinter specifically to try to oust Dagult from power, yet when the city faced annihilation from the chasm, father and son agreed to share the [[Crown of Neverwinter|Crown of Never]] ritual -- trading the crown back and forth to channel its magic and seal the rift. This pragmatic cooperation for the greater good defines Renaer's character: principled but practical.

---

## Background

Renaer is the heir to [[House Neverember]], one of the most powerful noble families on the Sword Coast. His father Dagult served as Open Lord of Waterdeep, where he imposed conservative spending policies and disruptive tariffs that damaged the city's economy. Dagult was eventually deposed and replaced by [[Laeral Silverhand]]. Before his removal, Dagult allegedly embezzled approximately 500,000 gold dragons and hid them somewhere in Waterdeep, employing the [[The Stone of Golorr]] as the key to their location.

Renaer is openly estranged from his father and wants nothing to do with the stolen gold or the political machinations surrounding it. He is aware of his father's spy network -- including the gnome agent [[Dalekar|Dalakhar/Dalcar]] who was tasked with tracking Renaer and recovering the Stone of Golorr. According to Renaer, Dagult had no direct connection to the [[Xanathar Guild]].

## Role in the Story

### Season 1 -- Waterdeep

#### The Rescue ([[S01E02 - Episode Two|S01E02]] -- [[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]])

Renaer's involvement with SWOJ begins when he and [[Floon Blagmaar]] are kidnapped from [[Fillet Lane]] by the [[Zhentarim]]. The [[Xanathar Guild]] then attacks the Zhentarim warehouse, taking Floon -- whom they mistake for Renaer -- deeper into the sewers. SWOJ finds Renaer hiding in a closet under the stairs at the Zhentarim hideout on [[Candle Lane]]. He explains the kidnapping, reveals the existence of the [[The Stone of Golorr]] and his father's hidden gold, and arms himself with daggers to join the search for Floon.

When [[Captain Hyustus Stagget|Captain Eustace Staggerd]] and the [[City Watch]] arrive, the captain recognizes Renaer and defers to his authority, allowing SWOJ to go free. By [[S01E03 - Episode Three|S01E03]], both Renaer and the rescued Floon await the party at [[The Splintered Stair]]. Their rescue earns SWOJ the deed to [[Trollskull Manor]] from [[Volothamp Geddarm|Volo]] in [[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]].

#### The Benefactor ([[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]] -- [[S01E12 - Episode Twelve|S01E12]])

Renaer becomes a financial supporter and intelligence source for SWOJ. He offers a 1,000 gold loan to help renovate [[Trollskull Manor]] and warns them about [[Dalekar|Dalakhar]], a spy following him on behalf of his father to find the [[The Stone of Golorr]].

After the devastating fireball explosion at [[Trollskull Alley]] in [[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]] -- which kills over ten people including Voniel -- Renaer meets SWOJ on the street in [[S01E12 - Episode Twelve|S01E12]]. He identifies the dead gnome found at the blast site as Dalcar, his father's spy, who had been asking around about SWOJ and may have been carrying the Stone of Golorr. Concerned for his own safety, Renaer agrees to hide at [[The Short Rest]] while SWOJ investigates the [[Gralhund Villa]].

#### Lark's Resurrection and the Vault ([[S01E22 - Episode Twenty Two|S01E22]] -- [[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]])

When [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] is killed and SWOJ needs a diamond for his resurrection, Renaer provides a diamond worth 1,000 gold from his apartment -- no questions asked.

In [[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]], Renaer arrives at the [[Vault of Dragons]] carrying a ledger that proves the [[Cassalanters]]' embezzlement. He helps load gold from the vault and fights alongside SWOJ in the chaotic battle at [[Zadio City Music Hall]], where [[Aurinax]] the gold dragon clashes with the [[Xanathar]], [[Manshoon]] appears on the balcony, and Renaer attempts to stab the Xanathar in the eye.

#### Departure ([[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]])

After the [[Cassalanters]]' trial and conviction, Renaer departs Waterdeep for [[Neverwinter]], fearing that the political fallout from the Cassalanter diamond business will spark war between the two cities. His departure marks the end of his direct involvement in Season 1.

### Season 2 -- Neverwinter

#### The Missing Years ([[S02E05 - Episode Thirty Five (S02E05)|S02E05]])

When SWOJ returns from the Shadowfell to discover three years have passed, they learn that Waterdeep and Neverwinter have gone to war over the Cassalanter diamond mines. Renaer Neverember has gone missing -- his whereabouts unknown.

#### The Resistance ([[S02E14 - Episode Forty Four (S02E14)|S02E14]] -- [[S02E15 - Episode Forty Five (S02E15)|S02E15]])

[[Jarlaxle Baenre|Jarlaxle]] fills in the gaps: Dagult Neverember rules from the [[Hall of Justice]], the war with Waterdeep continues, and [[The Masks]] control much of Neverwinter's affairs. Renaer has been leading an underground [[The Resistance|resistance]] against the Masks' influence, keeping an extremely low profile.

In [[S02E15 - Episode Forty Five (S02E15)|S02E15]], the halfling knight [[Laila]] leads SWOJ through tunnels beneath [[Castle Never]] to Renaer's secret base -- a war room in natural caverns beneath the castle dungeons where artisans and resistance members work. The reunion is warm and affectionate. Renaer, who has been hiding underground for three years, provides critical intelligence:

- [[Shimmer Brackenwell]] was murdered outside the [[Moonstone Mask]] by someone impersonating Lark (wearing a red hat, vanishing into mist)
- Shimmer's death was used to frame SWOJ, making them fugitives in Neverwinter
- [[Fel'Rekt Lafeen|Felrecht]] has disappeared
- The city guilds have divided loyalties
- [[Fumbles  Fumblewick|Fumbles/Fumblewick]], a gnome city guard, is a resistance contact at the [[Slinky Serpent Tavern]] who has accompanied caravans into [[The Chasm|the chasm]]

#### The Crown of Neverwinter ([[S02E18 - Episode Forty Eight LIVE (S02E18)|S02E18]] -- [[S02E20 - Episode Fifty (S02E20)|S02E20]])

A three-way political struggle emerges over the [[Crown of Neverwinter|Crown of Never]]. Dagult hires SWOJ to retrieve it from [[Castle Never]] for 5,000 gold each plus property. But Renaer also contacts SWOJ via whisper magic, revealing that he wants the crown too. He provides historical context about [[Lord Halueth Never]] and [[Lady Lissandra Never]] as the original founding family, and the [[Allegondar]] family who ruled after them. SWOJ retrieves the crown but faces a dilemma over which Neverember to give it to.

The crisis forces reconciliation. In [[S02E19 - Episode Forty Nine (S02E19)|S02E19]], Renaer brings halfling clerics to heal SWOJ after their emergence from [[Castle Neverwinter|Castle Never]]. A war council convenes with Dagult, Renaer, [[Zardoz Zord]], and [[Remalia Haventree]]. [[Melifarn|Mellafarne]] explains she can seal the chasm using the Crown of Never combined with crystals from the Amphail cavern, but the ritual requires someone of Neverember blood to wield the crown.

In [[S02E20 - Episode Fifty (S02E20)|S02E20]], Dagult and Renaer agree to share the ritual -- trading the crown back and forth while Mellafarne channels its power through the crystals. The chasm closes, saving Neverwinter. As a reward, Renaer offers SWOJ a choice of real estate: the [[Riverside Inn]] or the [[Shard of Moonlight]] (a floating wizard tower anchored above the city). SWOJ chooses the Shard of Moonlight.

#### Patron of Neverwinter ([[S02E26 - Episode Fifty Six (S02E26)|S02E26]] -- [[S02E31 - Episode Sixty One (S02E31)|S02E31]])

With the chasm sealed and the Masks' power broken, Renaer assumes a leadership role in Neverwinter's civic affairs. In [[S02E26 - Episode Fifty Six (S02E26)|S02E26]], he meets SWOJ at the [[Hall of Justice]], takes custody of the [[Soul Stone]] containing [[Rowan Crooks]] for safekeeping, and equips them for their dragon-hunting mission with specialized gear: a net of binding, mirage marbles, dragon fang arrows, and dragon hide cloaks.

By [[S02E30 - Episode Sixty (S02E30)|S02E30]], Renaer has overseen the construction of three airships (the Hammerhead Destroyer, the Ethereal Galleon, and the Clockwork Kraken) and marshalled 500 soldiers and 30 spellcasters for the coming battle against the dracolich threat. He receives SWOJ's report on the destruction of [[Amphail]] and [[Amalia Cassalanter]]'s role as a cult leader. During a saboteur attack on [[Castle Never]], Renaer is injured but stabilized by SWOJ.

In [[S02E31 - Episode Sixty One (S02E31)|S02E31]], Renaer is revived from unconsciousness and convenes a strategy session to plan the assault on the [[The Moonhole|moonhole]]. He stays behind to manage Neverwinter's civic functions while his father Dagult marshals the ground army of roughly 550 soldiers.

#### The Final Campaign ([[S02E37 - Episode Sixty Seven LIVE (S02E37)|S02E37]] -- [[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]])

In [[S02E37 - Episode Sixty Seven LIVE (S02E37)|S02E37]], Renaer leads Neverwinter soldiers in guarding refugees as they board the [[Concord Jewel]], standing outside the vessel with his troops to defend the evacuees.

At the Season 2 finale in [[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]], Renaer is present at the Masked Lords' council in Waterdeep alongside [[Jarlaxle Baenre|Jarlaxle]], [[Remalia Haventree]], and representatives from the Sword Coast Alliance. He gives a toast to SWOJ and offers to escort them back to Waterdeep from the Spiral Cavern. SWOJ is formally recognized with a Letters of Charter establishing the Guild of the Silver Waves of Justice.
