# Harold

Harold is [[Eunissa]]'s husband and a former actor who lives at the [[Spouting Fish]] tavern in the [[Dock Ward]] of [[Waterdeep]]. He is known locally for his fondness for kipper herring. Harold was among the many neighborhood residents who mysteriously vanished during a string of disappearances linked to the [[Xanathar Guild]], and was later rescued from the sewers by [[SWOJ]]. He gave the party the [[Playwright's Dagger]] in gratitude, a magic stage prop from his acting days. Harold was later revealed to have been implanted with an [[intellect devourer]] during his captivity, which emerged from his body during the chaotic courtroom battle at SWOJ's trial.

**First appearance:** [[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Description

Harold is a resident of the [[Dock Ward]] neighborhood surrounding the [[Spouting Fish]], the tavern owned by his wife [[Eunissa]]. Before settling down, he had a career as an actor, performing in stage shows that used minor magical props. He is frequently seen eating kipper herring, a habit strong enough that his personal smell is noticeable to those around him. At [[SWOJ]]'s trial, he was spotted in the courtroom gallery munching on kippered herrings, stinking up the entire room.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]:** Harold is reported missing by [[Tagga]] (Scoot's mother), who tells the party that he vanished in the night along with many others from the neighborhood around the [[Spouting Fish]]. [[Eunissa]] tells [[SWOJ]] that Harold was sleeping next to her and was gone when she woke, suspecting sleepwalking or kidnapping. The party investigates the tavern, discovers [[Xanathar Guild]] symbols in the sewers beneath it, fights three grell with help from [[The Black Viper]], and rescues Harold along with approximately fifteen other missing people. Harold tells the party he does not remember how he ended up underground, only that he woke up on the streets of [[Waterdeep]] as if sleepwalking. Grateful for the rescue, Harold gives them the [[Playwright's Dagger]], a magic stage dagger from his acting days that creates an illusion of stabbing someone without actually harming them. The illusion fades after about an hour.
- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** Harold appears as a spectator at [[SWOJ]]'s trial in the [[Dock Ward]] courtroom, eating kippered herrings and stinking up the room. When courtroom chaos erupts after the doppelganger [[Willifort Crowell]] is unmasked, several mind-controlled spectators -- including Harold -- begin attacking the city watch. [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] attempts to persuade the attacking crowd to stand down, and Harold responds, stopping for a moment and taking a few steps toward the party. His body then shudders and he collapses, as the [[intellect devourer]] that had been hiding inside his brain since his time in the sewers leaps out and attacks. Lark alerts [[Laeral Silverhand]] that the attacking spectators are hosts for intellect devourers, citing Harold as proof.
