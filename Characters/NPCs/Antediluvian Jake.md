# Antediluvian Jake

Member of the Jolly Gents. Teleports the group back to Waterdeep using a teleportation circle.

**First appearance:** [[S02E05 - Episode Thirty Five (S02E05)]]
**Total appearances:** 8 episodes
**Faction affiliations:** [[Jolly Gents]], [[The Jolly Gents]]

## Description

Member of the Jolly Gents. Teleports the group back to Waterdeep using a teleportation circle.

The lore keeper of the Jolly Gents adventuring party. Found waiting at the bottom of the Deep Dragon Temple. Possesses an obsidian tablet containing important information about the dragon-aboleth conflict.

The lore keeper of the Jolly Gents. Explains the history of the Breathless Elder, the aboleth-dragon conflict, and Manshoon's role in tethering dragon spirits. Waits in the Deep Dragon Temple while SWOJ proceeds through the portals.

One of the three Jolly Gents, a wizard. Drops an obsidian tablet to Lark and whispers 'we're all prisoners of Crooks' before teleporting away with Jack and the stolen green stones. Suggests the Jolly Gents may not be acting entirely of their own free will.

A historical figure connected to the Jolly Gents. His clay tablet reveals crucial backstory: the Jolly Gents were killed by Manshoon, Rowan Crooks harvests their souls, and the secret base is Zentil Keep floating above the Shadowfell.

A legendary Jolly Gents figure whose tablet SWOJ uses as a cover story. His phylactery (a copper orb) is found in Crooks' office on the sixth floor, suggesting he is trapped or controlled by Crooks.

Legendary Jolly Gents figure who appears in the dream sequence during the assault on the Xanathar's lair. Leads the charge alongside Giggling Gus.
