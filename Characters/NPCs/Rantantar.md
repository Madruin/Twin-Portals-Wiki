# Rantantar

One of [[Halaster Blackcloak]]'s six apprentices, referred to as siblings. Rantantar is among the trapped or scattered wizards that [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] is contractually bound to free as part of her pact with [[Asmodeus]].

**First mentioned:** [[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]]

## Overview

Rantantar is named in the document [[Halaster Blackcloak]] presented to [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] listing six apprentices -- [[Jhesiyra]], Rantantar, [[Lyrian]], [[Trobriand]], [[Muiral]], and [[Arcturia]] -- alongside an oath: "I swear to enchackle their souls by 13 Founders Days hence." Xae signed a contract with an avatar of [[Asmodeus]] during the Founder's Day feast to save the [[Cassalanters]] children, binding her to free all six siblings or forfeit her own soul.

[[Fetch]], the fragment of [[Manshoon]]'s soul trapped inside the [[Dynamo Of Possibility]], previously worked for Rantantar, suggesting Rantantar had some involvement with events in the [[The Shadowfell|Shadowfell]] or [[Undermountain]] before his imprisonment.

Rantantar's current location and condition are unknown. At the end of Season 2, five of the six siblings -- including Rantantar -- remained imprisoned, with only [[Arcturia]] having been freed.
