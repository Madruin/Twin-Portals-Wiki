# Shaleheart

A friendly female [[bulette]] (landshark) who became a loyal companion and ally of [[SWOJ|the Silver Waves of Justice]]. Originally encountered deep inside the [[Neverwinter Chasm]], Shaleheart is a mother of three and one of the party's most formidable non-humanoid allies.

**First appearance:** [[S02E16 - Episode Forty Six LIVE (S02E16)|S02E16]]
**Total appearances:** 10 episodes

---

## Description

Shaleheart is a massive, metallic-looking bulette -- a burrowing predator sometimes called a landshark. Unlike the feral bulettes SWOJ encounters elsewhere, she is intelligent and capable of speech. She has eight rows of teeth across the two sides of her jaws and can tunnel through earth and stone with ease, surfacing like a whale cresting the ocean. Her children travel with her, visible only as rumbling cobblestones when they move underground.

## History

### First Meeting in the Chasm (S02E16)

SWOJ first encountered Shaleheart in her den deep within the [[Neverwinter Chasm]] during their descent to find the seal keeping the chasm stable. She was living underground with her **three children**. Unlike the hostile creatures in the chasm, Shaleheart was friendly and helpful. She gave the party one of her **fire-resistant scales** for protection against the [[River Of Fire]] and provided directions through the tunnels: cross the river of fire, then ride the [[River Of Water]].

### Defence of Neverwinter (S02E17)

When the chasm erupted catastrophically, unleashing demons and shadows into the streets of [[Neverwinter]], Shaleheart came to the surface to help. A landshark was spotted coasting up out of the ground near the [[Chasm District Wall]] "like Free Willy style." [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] called out her name during the battle, and Shaleheart burst out of the rubble to land directly on the shadows that were draining [[Lord Dagult Neverember|Neverember]]'s strength, pulverising them with her massive jaws. Her children followed close behind, rumbling under the cobblestones. SWOJ asked her and the kids to hunt down any remaining shadowy creatures around town. She agreed, promising not to eat any people -- "unless they are shadowy." After the battle, [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] performed a song of rest, and Shaleheart and her children hummed along, providing a bass undertone.

### Digging Tunnels for the Ritual (S02E19 -- S02E20)

Shaleheart assisted [[Melifarn]] and [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] in digging tunnels beneath Neverwinter in preparation for the chasm-sealing ritual. While Melifarn channelled the [[Crown of Never]]'s power through the [[Amphail Crystals]] to seal the chasm, Shaleheart watched from a distance, keeping clear of the swirling energy of ice and fire.

### Remembered on the Road (S02E26 -- S02E27)

When SWOJ encountered wild bulettes on the [[High Road]] south of Neverwinter, [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] called out Shaleheart's name, hoping to calm them. The wild bulettes did not respond -- they lacked the intelligent look Shaleheart had when she spoke. Later, when the [[Jolly Gents]] grilled a slain bulette for breakfast, Xae could not bring herself to eat any because she kept thinking of Shaleheart. The roasted bulette was described as tasting "kind of like hazelnut and steak."

### Rally to the Ethereal Galleon (S02E31)

Shaleheart was among the allies who rallied to SWOJ's cause for the assault on the [[Moonhole]], alongside [[Squidly]], [[Fel'Rekt Lafeen]], [[Fioleth The Ragamoffin]], the [[Doom Raiders]], and [[Urstul Floxxin]].

### Battle of the Spiral Cavern (S02E32)

Shaleheart crawled onto the bottom of the [[Ethereal Galleon]] to join the mission into the [[Spiral Cavern]]. Her babies were **all grown up** by this point. Before departure, [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] hunted for [[Lunaria]] flowers and gifted them to Shaleheart, who was very appreciative.

During the battle inside the Spiral Cavern, Shaleheart climbed up on deck and eyed the ballista. When a dragon crashed into the ship, she lumbered out the broken back of the vessel and **burrowed into the cavern wall** toward the dragon, moving like a mole mound through the earth. She came up under the dragon and dislodged it from the wall. They tangled briefly before the dragon extricated itself, throwing Shaleheart down. Her fate after the impact was unclear as SWOJ rushed to board the portal ships.

### Name as an Alias (S02E28)

During the infiltration of [[Castle Naerytar]], one of the PCs used "Shale Heart" as a fake alias when confronted by a [[Cult of the Dragon]] officer. The cultist did not recognise the name.

## Relationships

- **[[Xaerine Rumblestride]]** -- Xae shares a special bond with Shaleheart, likely rooted in her connection to nature and the earth. She calls out to Shaleheart by name during bulette encounters, gifts her lunaria flowers, and refuses to eat roasted bulette out of fondness for her.
- **[[Melifarn]]** -- The ancient bronze dragon worked alongside Shaleheart and Xae to dig tunnels for the chasm-sealing ritual.
- **[[Scoot Sparkles]]** -- Scoot calls out to Shaleheart during the battle at the chasm district wall and later asks her to help hunt down shadowy creatures, telling her not to eat people.
- **Her Children** -- Three baby bulettes who travel with her underground. By [[S02E32 - Episode Sixty Two LIVE (S02E32)|S02E32]], they are described as fully grown. They helped with tunnel-digging work and assisted soldiers in widening passages in and out of the Spiral Cavern.
