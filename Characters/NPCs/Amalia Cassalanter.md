# Amalia Cassalanter

Noble matriarch of [[House Cassalanter]], one of the wealthiest and most politically connected families in [[Waterdeep]]. A powerful spellcaster, manipulator, and recurring villain across both Season 1 and Season 2 of the campaign. Together with her husband [[Victoro Cassalanter]], Amalia made a pact with [[Asmodeus]], Lord of Nessus, trading their children's souls for wealth and power. After being sentenced to life in prison at the close of Season 1, she escaped and reinvented herself as "[[Velia Shadowmantle]]," leading a [[Cult Of The Dragon]] cell dedicated to creating dracoliches and exacting revenge on Waterdeep. She was ultimately captured by [[Silver Waves Of Justice (SWOJ)|SWOJ]] at the [[Radiant Citadel]] during the Season 2 climax.

**First appearance:** [[S01E09 - Episode Nine|S01E09]]
**Total appearances:** 12+ episodes across Seasons 1 and 2
**Status:** Captured -- encased in water by a water elemental at the [[Radiant Citadel]] ([[S02E34 - Episode Sixty Four LIVE (S02E34)|S02E34]])
**Faction affiliations:** [[Cassalanter Family  Cult Of Asmodeus|Cassalanter Family / Cult of Asmodeus]], [[Cult Of The Dragon]]

## Appearance

Tall, with a Morticia Addams-like bearing. Often seen in elegant golden dresses befitting her station as a Waterdeep noblewoman. A large Renaissance-style portrait of Amalia and [[Victoro Cassalanter|Victoro]] hung in the [[Cassalanter Villa]] library, depicted as always watching visitors as they moved through the room.

During her time as "[[Velia Shadowmantle]]," she maintained a magical disguise that concealed her true identity until a moonbeam spell burned it away.

## Personality

Amalia presents a poised, gracious exterior -- warmly introducing herself to [[Silver Waves Of Justice (SWOJ)|SWOJ]] at the Cassalanter soiree, proudly showing off her children [[Terenzio Cassalanter|Terenzio]] and [[Elzerina Cassalanter|Elzerina]], and insisting guests call her by her first name. Beneath this aristocratic charm lies a desperate mother who entered into a diabolical bargain and a ruthless spellcaster willing to fireball intruders without hesitation. After her imprisonment and escape, that desperation hardened into vindictive ambition, driving her to lead a cult, oversee dracolich creation rituals, and ally with [[Manshoon]] -- all in pursuit of revenge against the city that imprisoned her.

## Abilities

Amalia is a powerful spellcaster with a diverse and dangerous repertoire:

- **Charm person** -- used against [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] in the underground shrine ([[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four|S01E24]])
- **Haste** -- cast on herself during the shrine confrontation
- **Fireball** (10d6 fire damage) -- devastated SWOJ in the shrine, dealing 29 damage and knocking [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] and [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] unconscious
- **Cone of cold** -- cast through a wall of fire at [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] and Xae during the [[Radiant Citadel]] battle ([[S02E34 - Episode Sixty Four LIVE (S02E34)|S02E34]])
- **Telekinesis** -- used to try to rip the phylactery away from Lark and fling him off the dracolich ([[S02E29 - Episode Fifty Nine LIVE (S02E29)|S02E29]])
- **Magical disguise** -- maintained her "[[Velia Shadowmantle]]" identity until dispelled by moonbeam

## Role in the Story

### Season 1: The Cassalanter Conspiracy

#### The Soiree and the Bargain ([[S01E09 - Episode Nine|S01E09]]--[[S01E10 - Episode Ten|S01E10]])

Amalia first appeared at the Cassalanter soiree, greeting SWOJ at the door in a golden dress and pulling them aside to the garden courtyard. She hired SWOJ to locate [[Lord Dagult Neverember|Dagult Neverember]]'s hidden hoard of golden dragons for a generous 150gp/day stipend, mentioning their "late son" [[Osvaldo Cassalanter|Osvaldo]]. She proudly introduced her children [[Terenzio Cassalanter|Terenzio]] and [[Elzerina Cassalanter|Elzerina]] to the party, watching them play with [[Terry]] the golden retriever with a warm, motherly grin.

When [[Zylka]] and golden-masked cultists crashed through the ballroom windows, the evening devolved into chaos. SWOJ discovered Osvaldo -- not dead but transformed into a nine-foot-tall chain devil -- imprisoned in a pentagram circle in the attic. Amalia and [[Victoro Cassalanter|Victoro]] burst into the attic, panicked and sweating with cuts and torn outfits, and begged SWOJ not to hurt him. As they laid hands on the pentagram to restrain Osvaldo, Amalia looked at Lark and said simply: "This is our son."

In the aftermath ([[S01E10 - Episode Ten|S01E10]]), Amalia and Victoro discussed their guilt with SWOJ, revealing that Osvaldo's transformation was a consequence of their pact with [[Asmodeus]]. The deal demanded the souls of their remaining children, Terenzio and Elzerina, unless a massive ransom of gold was paid to the archdevil. This desperation was the true reason behind their hiring of SWOJ.

#### The Shrine Confrontation ([[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four|S01E24]])

When SWOJ infiltrated [[Cassalanter Villa]] during the Founder's Day preparations -- with Xae disguised as a golden retriever to bypass guards -- and fought their way through the underground [[Shrine Of Asmodeus (Cassalanter Villa)|shrine to Asmodeus]], Amalia arrived just as [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] shattered the stone fire bowl (causing both Cassalanters psychic pain) and freed the third Eye of the [[The Stone of Golorr]].

Amalia confronted SWOJ in the shrine, framed in a golden dress as if she had just come from a ball. Before Xae could even warn her about the cultist infiltration, Amalia raised a wand and cast charm person -- ensnaring Scoot, who began calling her "Ramalia" in his befuddled state. She then hasted herself and unleashed a devastating fireball (10d6 fire damage, 29 damage) that knocked Lark and Xae unconscious. SWOJ barely escaped with the third Eye, fleeing through a natural cave passage to an underground river that washed them out near the mud flats on Waterdeep's west coast.

#### Pleading with Asmodeus ([[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]])

After the Cassalanters used their political influence to have SWOJ arrested by a city watch army under their control, and after SWOJ was broken out of prison by [[Esvelle Wavesilver]], the party infiltrated the Cassalanters' Founder's Day feast -- a front for a devil-soul harvest. While Victoro used his [[Charming Cane]] and [[Rod of Rulership]] on the crowd above, SWOJ fought their way into the temple of [[Asmodeus]] beneath the villa.

There they found Amalia holding her children Terenzio and Elzerina before a bowl of fire filled with golden coins, pleading with a semi-corporeal avatar of Asmodeus to accept gold instead of her children's souls. Amalia was overcome with emotion, desperate and incoherent. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] ultimately stepped forward and signed a contract with Asmodeus in place of the children -- agreeing to free six of [[Halaster Blackcloak]]'s apprentices within 13 Founder's Days or forfeit her own soul. A pillar of fire erupted from the altar, and the Cassalanter children were saved.

Amalia was described as regarding Xae "with some clarity for the first time" and seeming to grasp what had just happened. When the city watch and magisters arrived, she and Victoro were taken into custody.

#### Trial and Sentencing ([[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]])

Ylka delivered a legendary speech at the trial, and both Amalia and Victoro were sentenced to life in prison for their crimes and demonic dealings. Their children were placed under city protection and eventually joined the [[Trollskull Alley Irregulars]] at [[Trollskull Manor]].

### Season 2: The Return as Velia Shadowmantle

#### Escape and Reinvention

At some point between Season 1 and Season 2, Amalia escaped from prison. The circumstances of her escape remain unclear. By the time of Season 2, she had reinvented herself as "[[Velia Shadowmantle]]" and risen to lead a [[Cult Of The Dragon]] cell operating out of [[Castle Naerytar]] in the [[Mere of Dead Men]].

In [[S02E14 - Episode Forty Four (S02E14)|S02E14]], [[Jarlaxle Baenre|Jarlaxle]] revealed that the war between Waterdeep and Neverwinter over the Cassalanter diamond mines was still ongoing -- a lasting consequence of the family's far-reaching financial empire.

#### The Cult of the Dragon ([[S02E28 - Episode Fifty Eight (S02E28)|S02E28]])

A disillusioned white-masked cultist named [[Bronk]] provided SWOJ with intelligence about "Velia Shadowmantle." He described her as a Waterdeep noblewoman who hated the city and wanted revenge, and who had been steering the cult toward her personal ends rather than its broader purpose of serving dragons. Under her leadership, the cult performed dracolich creation rituals, draining the souls of captured dragons -- including [[Jack]], a silver dragon of the [[Way of the Balanced Scale]] -- into a bronze phylactery. The cult also kept a [[Mask of Dragon Kind]] in their underground fortress.

#### Unmasked ([[S02E29 - Episode Fifty Nine LIVE (S02E29)|S02E29]])

During the climactic battle in the Sunken City beneath Castle Naerytar, Amalia's magical disguise was burned away by moonbeam, revealing her true identity to SWOJ. She fought viciously for control of the phylactery, using telekinesis to rip it from Lark's grasp. When Lark flew past her and shouted "Amalia, you suck!" -- it threw her off just enough. Ylka summoned allied dragons using the [[Mask of Dragon Kind]], and the [[Ripples]] set off explosions above, opening a chasm to the surface. One of the two black dragons killed its sibling to fuel the dracolich transformation, beginning a horrific mutation.

As the sun rose and SWOJ escaped on allied dragonback, Amalia fled eastward atop the partially-transformed dracolich toward the [[Sword Mountains]], the creature grabbing her under its arm as they disappeared into the sunrise.

#### Reported to Renaer ([[S02E30 - Episode Sixty (S02E30)|S02E30]])

SWOJ met [[Renaer Neverember]] at [[Castle Never]] and reported that Amalia Cassalanter had escaped prison, assumed a new identity, and was leading the Cult of the Dragon. Renaer was stunned: "Amalia Castellanter, isn't she in prison?" The party confirmed she had somehow escaped and was now commanding a dracolich.

#### The Radiant Citadel ([[S02E34 - Episode Sixty Four LIVE (S02E34)|S02E34]]--[[S02E35 - Episode Sixty Five (S02E35)|S02E35]])

Amalia resurfaced allied with [[Manshoon]] at the [[Radiant Citadel]], commanding cultist forces and attempting to drain the Citadel's energy through soul pylons. She stood atop the two-headed dracolich near the pedestal, directing her guards and attempting to deactivate the concord jewel controls -- cursing under her breath when she found the pedestal locked.

SWOJ stealthily freed [[Sholeh]], the elder speaker (an ancient brass dragon in disguise), and fifteen other imprisoned speakers. When the battle broke into the open, Amalia cast cone of cold through a wall of fire at Ylka and Xae, and her guards formed a protective circle around her. She grew "increasingly angry" as SWOJ's plans unraveled her hold on the Citadel.

Ylka polymorphed into a Tyrannosaurus Rex, charged Amalia, bit her, and ragdolled her like a terrier shaking a toy. [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] communed with the [[Raven Queen]], who sent a massive flock of spectral ravens that banished the two-headed dracolich and destroyed the top soul pylon. Xae summoned a water elemental that swept through the temple ruins, washing away Amalia's remaining guards while parting around the T-Rex.

Amalia was ultimately encased in water by the water elemental and restrained. By [[S02E35 - Episode Sixty Five (S02E35)|S02E35]], SWOJ confirmed they had "Amalia Castellantern encased in water" as a prisoner, held while the party prepared for the final push against Manshoon and the second soul pylon below.

## Relationships

- **[[Victoro Cassalanter]]** -- Her husband and co-conspirator in the Asmodeus pact. Together they bore the guilt of Osvaldo's transformation and desperately sought Neverember's gold to save their remaining children. Both were sentenced to life in prison; Victoro's fate after Amalia's escape is unknown.
- **[[Osvaldo Cassalanter]]** -- Her eldest son, transformed into a chain devil as a consequence of the Asmodeus pact. Amalia rushed to his side in the attic, tearful and protective.
- **[[Terenzio Cassalanter]]** and **[[Elzerina Cassalanter]]** -- Her younger children, whose souls were at stake. Amalia pleaded with Asmodeus to spare them and was overcome with emotion when Xae's contract saved them. They were placed under city protection after the trial.
- **[[Asmodeus]]** -- The archdevil with whom Amalia and Victoro made their pact, trading their children's souls for wealth and power.
- **[[Manshoon]]** -- Her Season 2 ally at the [[Radiant Citadel]]. Their alliance involved seizing the Citadel and draining its energy through soul pylons.
- **[[Silver Waves Of Justice (SWOJ)|SWOJ]]** -- First hired them, then fought them. Amalia's relationship with SWOJ is one of the campaign's most enduring antagonisms, spanning from the Season 1 soiree through the Radiant Citadel climax.
