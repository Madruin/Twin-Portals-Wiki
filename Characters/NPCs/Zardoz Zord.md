# Zardoz Zord

Zardoz Zord is the flamboyant ringmaster of the [[Sea Maiden's Faire]], a traveling entertainment fleet. He wears red and yellow satin knickers, a harness hung with knives and vials, and a hat with a huge red plume. He first aids the party inside a psychic construct of [[Waterdeep]], later appears at the [[Cassalanter Villa]] party selling nimblewright automatons, and ultimately becomes a wartime ally on the Sword Coast.

**First appearance:** [[S01E03 - Episode Three|S01E03]]
**Total appearances:** 6 episodes

## Description

Zardoz Zord is a theatrical, larger-than-life figure who commands a fleet of three vessels moored in [[Waterdeep]]'s harbor -- the [[Sea Maiden's Faire]]. His ships carry automaton workers, a unicorn figurehead, and a broken halfling-sized construct called [[Nim]]. He is perceptive enough to recognize that the party did not belong in the psychic version of Waterdeep, and generous enough to equip them with a bag of holding and intelligence about the strange happenings in the city. Later, he sells nimblewright automatons at the [[Cassalanter Villa]] party for 1,000 gold each. By Season 2, he has become a strategic ally, participating in war councils and celebrations alongside [[SWOJ]].

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E03 - Episode Three|S01E03]]:** Inside a psychic/illusory version of [[Waterdeep]], Zardoz Zord recognizes the party as real people and offers them aid. He takes them to his ships, provides a bag of holding, and points them toward investigating lightning convergences near [[Candle Lane]]. Aboard his vessel they also discover [[Nim]], a broken automaton.
- **[[S01E06 - Episode Six|S01E06]]:** SWOJ travels to the [[Sea Maiden's Faire]] on the docks to find [[Pog Waggletop]] and meets Zardoz Zord, the enigmatic carnival master. He gives the party a handkerchief to deliver to [[Grinda Garloth]] at [[Mistshore]] in exchange for help investigating the sunken [[Sea Plum]].
- **[[S01E09 - Episode Nine|S01E09]]:** Zardoz Zord appears at the [[Cassalanter Villa]] party selling nimblewright automatons at 1,000 gold each.
- **[[S02E19 - Episode Forty Nine (S02E19)|S02E19]]:** Participates in the war council at [[Neverwinter]] alongside [[Dagult Neverember]], [[Renaer Neverember]], and [[Remallia Haventree]], helping plan strategy against the multiple threats facing the city.
- **[[S02E20 - Episode Fifty (S02E20)|S02E20]]:** Present at the final war council discussions about threats facing the Sword Coast.
- **[[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]:** Attends the massive God's Night celebration at [[Trollskull Manor]] during Marpenoth, helping run the bar alongside old friends and allies.
