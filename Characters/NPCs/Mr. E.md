## Description

Mr. E was a drow rogue and monk who operated as an agent of [[Bregan D'Aerthe]], the infamous drow mercenary company led by [[Jarlaxle]]. Cool, capable, and sardonic, Mr. E was an expert lockpicker and self-described "home wrecker" -- his euphemism for burglar. He fought with flaming fists and dragon breath attacks, blending monastic discipline with roguish cunning. His sensei, [[Mr. Ziyagi]], was a figure Mr. E's player Robert Lee invented as a joke that became integral to the story.

Mr. E wielded distinctive equipment including a "ring of fingering" (a ring that cast mage hand at range), a tooth communication device for whispering to allies at a distance, and a belt buckle that became his trademark. He was also known for his spectral tentacle ability, which he used to physically rip a shadow creature out of [[Zelifarn]]'s possessed body.

Over the course of the campaign, Mr. E evolved from a [[Bregan D'Aerthe]] operative following [[Jarlaxle]]'s orders into a key ally of SWOJ. He became the dragon administrator of [[Waterdeep]], wielding the [[Dragon Staff of Ahghairon]], and ultimately co-founded the [[Way Of The Balanced Scales]] alongside [[Mellafarne]] the ancient bronze dragon -- a dragon alliance whose motto was "keep the balance."

Robert Lee had never played D&D before joining the show as a guest player. He was recruited from Renegade Improv and described by the cast as an excellent character performer ([[S01E19 - Episode Nineteen|S01E19]]).

## History

### Season 1: Introduction and the Dragon Heist

Mr. E was introduced to SWOJ by [[Jarlaxle]] (operating under his human alias [[Zardoz Zord]]) during Season 1 at the Short Rest tavern ([[S01E12 - Episode Twelve|S01E12]]). His first mission with the party was infiltrating the [[Gralhund Villa]], where he demonstrated his exceptional skills by lockpicking the servants' entrance and navigating the pitched battle between Gralhund guards and Zhentarim agents inside. Mr. E stole a silver key from [[Yalah Gralhund]]'s neck while she slept and a locked book from the Gralhund library, which was guarded by an imp disguised as a tarantula. He escaped through a window with both prizes as the city watch arrived, establishing himself as the party's go-to infiltrator for high-stakes heists.

He continued working with SWOJ through the Gralhund Villa aftermath ([[S01E13 - Episode Thirteen|S01E13]]), returning with the stolen book and aiding the party despite being "still salty" about getting pranked during the heist.

### Season 1: The Vault of Dragons and Dragon Piss

Mr. E's connection to dragons set him apart from the other party members. After the death of his sensei [[Mr. Ziyagi]], Mr. E returned to the party carrying a [[Book of Shadows]] ([[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]]). With [[Fela Lefaliir]], the elven botanist on [[Trollskull Alley]], Mr. E had established a supply chain for "dragon piss" elixir -- an alchemical compound that granted dragon-enhanced vision.

At the [[Vault Of Dragons]], Mr. E traded a dragon piss elixir to [[Aurinax]], an adult gold dragon disguised as a dwarf who guarded the dragon horde ([[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]]). In exchange, Aurinax gave him a giant ruby the size of an ostrich egg. This transaction was not merely mercantile: it established a relationship of trust between Mr. E and the dragon community that would pay enormous dividends later. During the massive theater battle that followed, Mr. E fought bugbears with his trademark flaming fists while Aurinax breathed fire on the [[Xanathar]]'s forces.

### Season 1: Founders Day and the Dragon Staff

During the [[Founders Day]] battle at the [[Cassalanter Villa]], Mr. E fought alongside SWOJ against [[Victoro Cassalanter]] and his forces ([[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]]). In one of the campaign's most memorable combat moments, Mr. E, [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]], and [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] kicked the unconscious Victoro through his own portal into the temple of [[Asmodeus]] ([[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]]). Mr. E pocketed roughly 520 gold from the Cassalanter horde during the aftermath.

At [[Castle Waterdeep]], [[Aurinax]] offered the [[Dragon Staff of Ahghairon]] -- a powerful artifact that allowed dragons to bypass the dragon ward protecting [[Waterdeep]] -- to Mr. E ([[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]]). Mr. E accepted the responsibility and took up residence at [[Castle Waterdeep]], serving as the city's unofficial dragon administrator and vetting incoming dragons with dragon piss-enhanced vision elixirs.

### Season 2: Return and the Deep Dragon Temple

In Season 2, Mr. E departed on his own missions but returned at critical junctures. He reappeared dramatically by literally falling from midair onto [[Zelifarn]]'s back during a battle with shadow creatures at [[Trill]]'s sugar mill in the Preserve of the Ancestors ([[S02E09 - Episode Thirty Nine (S02E09)|S02E09]]). He had been teleported by unknown means. When one of the shadow creatures possessed Zelifarn by flowing into his mouth, Mr. E used spectral tentacles to reach into the dragon and rip the creature out, then hurled its lifeless body at the remaining shadow creature.

Mr. E explained that he had been living at the [[Deep Dragon Temple]] with a bronze dragon named [[Mellafarne]], who had saved him from a shipwreck and brought him to the temple. They became close companions, but then [[Mellafarne]] fell into an enchanted psychic sleep -- the same affliction later striking [[Zelifarn]] and other bronze dragons. In their shared dreams, Mellafarne communicated the message: "get help, find the temple." SWOJ recognized Mellafarne as the same bronze dragon they had met five hundred years in the past ([[S01E25 - Episode Twenty Five|S01E25]]).

Mr. E accompanied SWOJ to [[Driftwood]], the floating pirate citadel, and then to [[Devil's' Rock]], where they navigated skeleton-infested beaches (the Skull Dunes), trapped tunnels, and fought through the island's dangers to reach the [[Deep Dragon Temple]] ([[S02E10 - Episode Forty (S02E10)|S02E10]]). Inside the temple, Mr. E entered a meditative trance and then collapsed as the [[Breathless Elder]]'s whistling enchantment intensified -- his bond with the sleeping dragons making him vulnerable to the same psychic affliction ([[S02E11 - Episode Forty One (S02E11)|S02E11]]). [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] carried Mr. E's unconscious body on his back as the party pushed forward to discover [[Mellafarne]] lying asleep on the temple's massive stone plateau.

### Season 2: The Way of the Balanced Scales

After SWOJ destroyed the [[Breathless Elder]] and freed the bronze dragon spirits from their tethers, [[Mellafarne]] awoke. Together, Mr. E and Mellafarne destroyed thousands of aberrant whistler larvae in the underground lake beneath the [[Deep Dragon Temple]] with her lightning breath and his combat skills. They then rallied other recovering bronze dragons and co-founded the [[Way Of The Balanced Scales]] -- a dragon alliance dedicated to maintaining cosmic balance ([[S02E19 - Episode Forty Nine (S02E19)|S02E19]]).

Mr. E's most dramatic Season 2 entrance came at [[Neverwinter]], where he arrived at [[Castle Neverwinter]] riding [[Mellafarne]] at the head of a flight of four other dragons -- [[Jack]] (silver), [[Jill]] (white), [[Chip]], and [[Dale]] -- representing the [[Way Of The Balanced Scales]] ([[S02E19 - Episode Forty Nine (S02E19)|S02E19]]). He used his tooth communication device to amplify his voice as he swooped into the city. At the war council with [[Dagult Neverember]], [[Renaer Neverember]], [[Zardoz Zord]], and [[Remalia Haventree]], Mr. E and [[Mellafarne]] coordinated the strategy to seal the [[Neverwinter]] chasm.

Mr. E flew to [[Amphail]] on dragonback with SWOJ to infiltrate the [[Spiral Cavern]], where [[Manshoon]]'s forces were massing. Inside the cavern, when [[Chip]] and [[Dale]] were captured by barbarians and periton riders, Mr. E used his ring of mage hand to communicate with the captured dragons, promising to return with reinforcements ([[S02E19 - Episode Forty Nine (S02E19)|S02E19]]).

At [[Neverwinter]], Mr. E participated in the preparations for [[Mellafarne]]'s chasm-sealing ritual, coordinating with the dragon council while SWOJ retrieved crystals from the [[Spiral Cavern]]. He was observed carving "SWOJ + Mr. E" with a heart around it, reflecting his deep affection for the party ([[S02E20 - Episode Fifty (S02E20)|S02E20]]). He also performed naked sunrise yoga facing the sun, earning commentary from his companions.

### Season 2: Departure to the Shadowfell

When the [[Shadar-Kai]] -- elven natives of the [[Shadowfell]] who served the [[Raven Queen]] -- briefed SWOJ on the dark sun situation at [[Zhentil Keep]], Mr. E departed with the Shadar-kai ([[Idae]], [[Mari]], and [[Blish]]) on a separate mission ([[S02E21 - Episode Fifty One (S02E21)|S02E21]]). His path diverged from the main party's journey deeper into the [[Shadowfell]].

After Mr. E's departure, his legacy continued to echo through the campaign. The dragons of the [[Way Of The Balanced Scales]] remained active -- and vulnerable. [[Chip]], [[Dale]], [[Jill]], and [[Jack]] were captured by the [[Cult of the Dragon]] at [[Castle Naerytar]], prompting SWOJ's rescue mission ([[S02E28 - Episode Fifty Eight (S02E28)|S02E28]]). When the party encountered the captive [[Jill]] (also known as Iglemar), they invoked Mr. E's name and the Way of the Balanced Scales to break her trance.

By the Season 2 finale, [[Mellafarne]] was seen perched on the rooftop of [[Trollskull Manor]] alongside [[Aurinax]] in her human form, at peace among friends ([[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]). Mr. E's whereabouts during the finale are unconfirmed, though he was referenced as having been in town.

## Relationships

- **[[Mellafarne]]** -- An ancient bronze dragon and Mr. E's closest companion. Mellafarne rescued Mr. E from a shipwreck and brought him to the [[Deep Dragon Temple]], where they lived together. When she fell into the [[Breathless Elder]]'s enchanted sleep, she communicated with Mr. E through shared dreams, urging him to "get help, find the temple." After her awakening, they co-founded the [[Way Of The Balanced Scales]] and led the dragon flight to [[Neverwinter]] together. By the Season 2 finale, Mellafarne resided at [[Trollskull Manor]] alongside [[Aurinax]].
- **[[Jarlaxle]] / [[Zardoz Zord]]** -- Mr. E's employer and the leader of [[Bregan D'Aerthe]]. Jarlaxle introduced Mr. E to SWOJ and sent him on missions that aligned with Bregan D'Aerthe's intelligence-gathering interests. Their relationship was professional but marked by mutual respect.
- **[[Bregan D'Aerthe]]** -- The drow mercenary organization Mr. E served. His actions often advanced their goals while simultaneously helping SWOJ. [[Fel'Rekt Lafeen]], a Bregan D'Aerthe lieutenant, served as a contact throughout Mr. E's travels.
- **[[Aurinax]]** -- The adult gold dragon who guarded the [[Vault Of Dragons]]. Mr. E earned Aurinax's trust through the dragon piss trade and was entrusted with the [[Dragon Staff of Ahghairon]]. Aurinax was described as simply liking Mr. E "a lot" ([[S01E30 - Episode Thirty|S01E30]]).
- **[[Mr. Ziyagi]]** -- Mr. E's sensei, whose death prompted Mr. E's return to the party with a [[Book of Shadows]]. A character Robert Lee invented as a joke that became an important narrative thread. His connection to dragon philosophy influenced the founding of the [[Way Of The Balanced Scales]].
- **[[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]** -- Fellow combatant and trusted ally. Scoot carried Mr. E's unconscious body on his back through the [[Deep Dragon Temple]] when the [[Breathless Elder]]'s enchantment struck him down. They fought side by side from the [[Gralhund Villa]] to the [[Spiral Cavern]].
- **[[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]** -- Mr. E and Lark shared a roguish kinship, working together on infiltrations and once disguising themselves as two people stacked in an eight-foot trench coat. They jointly kicked [[Victoro Cassalanter|Victoro]] through his portal.
- **[[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]** -- Ylka studied Mr. E's [[Book of Shadows]] after [[Mr. Ziyagi]]'s death, gaining crucial information about [[Zylka]]'s origins.
- **[[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]** -- Fellow SWOJ member with whom Mr. E shared multiple adventures, from the [[Deep Dragon Temple]] to the [[Spiral Cavern]].
- **[[Fela Lefaliir]]** -- The elven botanist on [[Trollskull Alley]] who helped Mr. E establish a supply chain for dragon piss elixirs.
- **[[Way Of The Balanced Scales]]** -- The dragon alliance Mr. E co-founded with [[Mellafarne]]. Its members included dragons [[Jack]], [[Jill]], [[Chip]], and [[Dale]], who served as aerial support for the defense of the [[Sword Coast]].
- **[[Zelifarn]]** -- SWOJ's bronze dragon ally, related to [[Mellafarne]]. Mr. E literally fell onto Zelifarn's back upon his Season 2 return and ripped a shadow creature from the dragon's possessed body.
