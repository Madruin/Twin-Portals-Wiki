# Sylgar

[[The Xanathar]]'s beloved pet goldfish, kept in a tank in the beholder's private sanctum beneath Waterdeep. Despite being an ordinary goldfish, Sylgar is the only creature the paranoid Xanathar genuinely cares for. Guild members secretly replace the fish whenever it dies, without the Xanathar's knowledge. Sylgar's tank served as the hiding place for one of the Eyes of the [[The Stone of Golorr]] -- a bright red gem that SWOJ needed to unlock the [[Vault of Dragons]].

**First appearance:** [[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes
**Location:** [[The Xanathar]]'s private sanctum, [[Xanathar Guild]] lair beneath Waterdeep
**Associated faction:** [[Xanathar Guild]]

## Description

An ordinary goldfish kept in a tank in the Xanathar's private sanctum. The beholder is obsessively attached to Sylgar, and the fish is tended by a dedicated keeper, [[Ott Steeltoes]]. Guild members secretly replace Sylgar whenever it dies so that the Xanathar never realizes its pet has perished -- a necessary deception given the beholder's volatile temperament.

The fish tank also served a strategic purpose: one of the three Eyes of the [[The Stone of Golorr]] was hidden inside it, a fact discovered by [[Harpers]] informant [[Thorvin Twinbeard]] and relayed to [[Laeral Silverhand]].

## Role in the Story

- **S01E15:** [[Laeral Silverhand]] reveals to SWOJ that one Eye of the [[The Stone of Golorr]] has been located in the Xanathar's lair, inside Sylgar's fish tank -- intelligence provided by [[Harpers]] informant [[Thorvin Twinbeard]]. This discovery sets the stage for SWOJ's infiltration mission.
- **S01E16:** SWOJ reaches the Xanathar's private sanctum and finds Sylgar's tank tended by [[Ott Steeltoes]]. [[Thaelin]] casts Suggestion on Ott, compelling him to retrieve the Eye of the [[The Stone of Golorr]] -- a bright red gem -- from a locked chest in the sanctum. [[The Xanathar]] arrives shortly after and attacks the party.

## Connections

- [[The Xanathar]] -- Owner; the beholder's obsessive attachment to Sylgar is one of its few emotional vulnerabilities
