# Flo

Flo is a young servant at the [[Gralhund Villa]] who becomes an unlikely ally to [[SWOJ]] during the [[Zhentarim]] uprising against the [[Gralhund]] household. During the chaos of the villa raid, she steps forward as the bravest of the rescued servants, armed with a crowbar and ready to fight. SWOJ puts her in charge of leading the other servants to safety at the [[Trollskull Alley  The Short Rest|Short Rest]], and she later repays their trust by testifying at their trial.

**First appearance:** [[S01E12 - Episode Twelve|S01E12]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Description

Flo is described as "the young one" among the [[Gralhund Villa]] servants -- notably younger than the other staff. Despite her youth, she is braver and more resourceful than the older servants: when [[SWOJ]] asks if any of the rescued staff can fight, the others say no, but Flo speaks up and says she has a crowbar. She is decisive and confident, nodding "very strongly" when given instructions and taking charge of the group without hesitation.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E12 - Episode Twelve|S01E12]]:** During [[SWOJ]]'s infiltration of the [[Gralhund Villa]], the party encounters a group of nine servants who have been hiding from the [[Zhentarim]] uprising. Flo is among them and explains the situation -- the Zhentarim have turned on the Gralhunds, with six to ten agents stationed in the house trying to corner Lord and Lady Gralhund. When SWOJ asks if any of the servants can fight, Flo volunteers that she has a crowbar. SWOJ puts her "in charge of defense," advises the servants to remove or cover their Gralhund livery, and sends them to evacuate to the [[Trollskull Alley  The Short Rest|Short Rest]]. Flo accepts the responsibility and leads the group out through the great hall.
- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** At SWOJ's trial before [[Laeral Silverhand]], [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] mentions that a young servant named Flo can corroborate their account of the Gralhund Villa raid. [[Tagga]] steps outside the courtroom and brings Flo and several other Gralhund staff members in to testify. They confirm that the [[Silver Waves of Justice]] came into the villa while it was under attack by the Zhentarim, saved the servants, and protected them during their escape. Their testimony helps establish SWOJ's defense against the charges brought by [[Orond Gralhund]].

## Relationships

- **[[SWOJ]]** -- The adventuring party who rescued Flo and the other Gralhund servants during the villa raid. They trusted her with leading the evacuation and she repaid that trust by testifying on their behalf.
- **[[Gralhund Villa]]** -- Flo's place of employment. The Zhentarim uprising forced her and the other servants to flee.
- **[[Tagga]]** -- [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]]'s mother, who sheltered Flo and the other Gralhund servants at the Short Rest after their escape. Tagga brings Flo into the courtroom to testify at the trial.
