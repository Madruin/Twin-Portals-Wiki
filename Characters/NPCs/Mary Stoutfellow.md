## Description

Farmer at Stout Fellow Farm in Undercliff. Hires SWOJ to deal with the scarecrow attacks. Pays 60gp per scarecrow destroyed.

Pays SWOJ 180gp (60gp per scarecrow) and gives them the enchanted Sickle of Storms as reward. Also provides a wine discount deal.


## Role in the Story

- **S01E07:** Leah brings Emerald Enclave quest: scarecrows attacking Stout Fellow Farm
- **S01E07:** SWOJ travels to Undercliff and meets Mary Stout Fellow and Blossom Zuber
- **S01E08:** Mary Stout Fellow pays 180gp and gives Sickle of Storms for scarecrow mission
- **S01E08:** Receive 100gp and Dasher's farewell letter, plus Stout Fellow wine deal for Hole in the Wall
