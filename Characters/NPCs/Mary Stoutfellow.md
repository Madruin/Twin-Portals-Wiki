# Mary Stoutfellow

Mary Stoutfellow is the halfling co-owner of [[Stoutfellow Farm]], a winery and farm in [[Undercliff]] just east of [[Waterdeep]]. She hired [[SWOJ]] through the [[Emerald Enclave]] to deal with enchanted scarecrows that had been terrorizing her workers and threatening her livelihood. A warm and practical businesswoman, she became an early ally and wine supplier for the party's tavern at [[Trollskull Manor]].

**First appearance:** [[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Description

Mary is a heavy-set halfling woman with a wide, conniving grin and a welcoming demeanor. She is a hardworking farmer who rises early -- when she visits SWOJ the morning after the scarecrow battle, she is already wearing a grape-stained apron from working the fields. Despite her smaller operation compared to the neighboring [[Zuber Farm]], Mary is proud of her farm's products and is ambitious about expanding into ales as well as wine. She runs the farm alongside her husband, and her current wine line is themed around [[Zardoz Zord]] and the Sea Maiden's Faire.

Stoutfellow Farm is a busy operation during harvest season, with many halfling workers harvesting pumpkins and the last of the grapes. The farm includes a drinking hall where Mary entertains guests and conducts business.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]:** [[Leah]] brings an [[Emerald Enclave]] quest to [[SWOJ]]: scarecrows have been attacking [[Stoutfellow Farm]], injuring workers and threatening the harvest. SWOJ travels to [[Undercliff]] and meets Mary at her farm. She explains that enchanted scarecrows have been disguising themselves among the farm's real scarecrows and ambushing workers at night for the past few weeks, but the city guard refuses to investigate. She notes that her farm is small "compared to the Zubers" and suspects the [[Zuber Farm|Zuber family]] may have an interest in maintaining their wine monopoly, though she asks SWOJ not to tell the Zubers she said so. She offers to pay per scarecrow destroyed and promises a wine discount for their troubles. That night, SWOJ camps out and fights three scarecrows in the fields, defeating them all.
- **[[S01E08 - Episode Eight|S01E08]]:** The next morning, Mary visits SWOJ with rewards in hand. She pays them 180 gold pieces (60 gold per scarecrow) and gives them the Sickle of Storms -- an enchanted sickle recovered from the scarecrow remains, dripping with condensation and imbued with wind magic. She also offers a half-price wine discount if SWOJ agrees to work exclusively through Stoutfellow Farm for their tavern supply. [[Ylka Gralhund]] performs an ode to wine on her concertina to celebrate the deal, and Mary suggests the party's song title "A Sack of Stout Fellow" could become one of their new ale names. Mary promises to put in a good word with the [[Emerald Enclave]] for future work, and asks SWOJ to send a pigeon if any more scarecrows appear. She bustles off to get back to work, establishing a wine supply partnership that would serve [[Trollskull Manor]].

## Relationships

- **[[Blossom Zuber]]** -- Owner of the rival [[Zuber Farm]]. Mary suspects the Zubers of wanting to maintain a monopoly on the wine business, but asks SWOJ to keep her suspicions private to maintain friendly relations.
- **[[Leah]]** -- The [[Emerald Enclave]] contact who brought Mary's scarecrow problem to SWOJ's attention.
- **[[SWOJ]]** -- The adventuring party she hired to deal with the scarecrow attacks. They became her wine distribution partners for [[Trollskull Manor]].
- **[[Ylka Gralhund]]** -- Ylka performed a wine-themed song for Mary and negotiated the wine discount deal with enthusiasm.
