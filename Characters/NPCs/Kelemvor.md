## Description

God of the dead in the Forgotten Realms. The hag reveals she plucked Lark from Kelemvor's fist and gave him to Malar.

The god of death and judgment in the Forgotten Realms. Built the Wall of the Faithless to trap souls of the faithless. The Raven Queen opposes him on this matter.

The god of the dead in the Forgotten Realms, manifested as a set of floating silver scales in the grand cathedral. He judges and diverts the souls trapped in the phylactery, working with SWOJ to redirect them away from the dracolich ritual.

The god of the dead who manifests as a fifty-foot robed figure with silver scales. He appears at the climactic moment and presents the party with the choice to break or preserve the Wall of the Faithless. When SWOJ votes to break the Wall, he facilitates the release of all trapped souls.
