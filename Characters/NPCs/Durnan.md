# Durnan

Owner and barkeep of the [[Yawning Portal]] tavern in [[Waterdeep]]'s [[Castle Ward]]. Durnan is a retired adventurer of legendary toughness who has run the tavern for hundreds of years, seemingly bound to the establishment's very essence. He fights with a greatsword, sports notable mutton chops, and charges 20 gold for entry into the well that descends into [[Undermountain]]. He is not one for small talk, but he clearly keeps tabs on the comings and goings of [[Waterdeep]]'s adventuring community.

**First appearance:** [[S01E01 - Episode One|S01E01]]
**Total appearances:** 3 episodes

## Appearance and Personality

Durnan is a physically imposing figure who keeps a greatsword within easy reach at all times. He has prominent mutton chops. His demeanor is gruff and businesslike -- he communicates more through rough nods and a hand on his sword hilt than through conversation. Despite this taciturn exterior, he is fiercely protective of his tavern and the people inside it. He is willing to leap into combat at a moment's notice when the [[Yawning Portal]] is threatened.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E01 - Episode One|S01E01]]:** When a bar fight erupts between [[Yagra]] and five tattooed thugs, followed by a [[Zhentarim]] crossbow ambush from a dray outside, Durnan leaps over the bar counter wielding his greatsword and fights alongside [[SWOJ]] to defend the tavern. He kills multiple attackers during the chaotic battle, proving himself a fearsome combatant despite his role as a simple barkeep.
- **[[S01E03 - Episode Three|S01E03]]:** *(Durnan does not actually appear.)* Inside [[Blue Alley]], the mind flayer [[Nihiloor]] disguises itself as Durnan in a mirror-lined chamber, complete with purple tendrils emerging from mutton chops. The impersonation is eventually revealed when [[Nihiloor]] drops the disguise and attacks [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]. This moment ties back to the mysterious voice the party heard earlier warning them to "beware the bartender."
- **[[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]:** SWOJ discovers they have been banned from the [[Yawning Portal]] for 20 days as a consequence of the violence that occurred during the brawl in [[S01E01 - Episode One|S01E01]]. A [[Captain|City Watch officer]] enforces the ban at the door. *(Durnan is not present by name.)*
- **[[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]:** With their 20-day ban expired, SWOJ returns to the [[Yawning Portal]]. Durnan is behind the bar and greets them when they visit. [[Three Strings]] performs for the crowd, and [[Bonnie]] is working the floor. The visit is brief -- SWOJ has business with [[Davil Starsong]] of the [[Zhentarim]] [[Doom Raiders]], who is also present at the tavern.
- **[[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]:** When SWOJ returns to [[Waterdeep]] after breaking the Wall of the Faithless, they wonder whether they are still banned from the [[Yawning Portal]]. [[Bonnie]] meets them at the door and explains that Durnan heard they were back in town and told her to let them in. Inside, the tavern is packed with adventurers and strange music emanates from the well. Durnan nods roughly at SWOJ, one hand resting on his greatsword hilt -- the closest thing to a warm welcome he is likely to give. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] hears a mysterious voice from [[Undermountain]] saying "come home," and [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] performs a set alongside [[Three Strings]] for the crowd.

## Relationships

- **[[Bonnie]]** — One of his bartenders at the [[Yawning Portal]], who handles much of the customer-facing warmth that Durnan himself does not provide.
- **[[Three Strings]]** — Bard who regularly performs at the [[Yawning Portal]].
- **[[SWOJ]]** — Durnan has a complicated relationship with the Silver Waves of Justice. He fought alongside them in their very first adventure, then banned them from his tavern after the resulting chaos, but ultimately lifted that ban and showed them a grudging respect by the Season 2 finale.
