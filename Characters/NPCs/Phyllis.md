# Phyllis

Phyllis is the owner of a flower shop on [[Trollskull Alley]] in [[Waterdeep]] and a patron of [[Froon]]'s Brews. She served as a key eyewitness to the devastating fireball explosion that struck the alley during the grand opening of [[Trollskull Alley  The Short Rest|The Short Rest]], and later donated health potions to [[SWOJ]] alongside [[Ted]] before the party's departure northward.

**First appearance:** [[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]
**Total appearances:** 2 episodes

## Description

Phyllis runs a flower shop on [[Trollskull Alley]], making her one of the neighborhood's established merchants and a familiar face to [[SWOJ]]. She survived the fireball explosion that devastated the alley by sheltering inside her shop, emerging coughing from the broken window but otherwise uninjured. Her shop's rooftop played an unwitting role in the attack -- a nimble wright perched on top of it to hurl a bead from a [[Necklace of Fireballs]] into the crowd below.

By the time of [[SWOJ]]'s return to [[Waterdeep]] three years later, Phyllis is a regular patron at [[Froon]]'s Brews. Together with [[Ted]], she generously donates two health potions to the party before their journey north, earning a cheer from the tavern crowd and a promise from [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] to immortalize them in song.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]:** During the grand opening party for [[Trollskull Alley  The Short Rest|The Short Rest]], a fireball explosion devastates [[Trollskull Alley]], killing ten or more people. Phyllis survives inside her flower shop and emerges coughing from the shattered window. She provides critical eyewitness testimony to [[SWOJ]], reporting that she saw a dark figure kneel over the dead gnome's body, take something from it, and flee south down the alley. She describes the figure as badly burned and repeatedly glancing over his shoulder. After giving her account, she helps tend to the wounded. Her shop's rooftop is later identified as the spot where a nimble wright stood to throw the fireball bead, and a [[Necklace of Fireballs]] is found in a barrel near her building.
- **[[S02E06 - Episode Thirty Six (S02E06)|S02E06]]:** Phyllis and [[Ted]] are present at [[Froon]]'s Brews when [[SWOJ]] stops in before departing [[Waterdeep]] on the Long Road. The two donate two health potions (at 150 gold each) for the party's journey north. [[Froon]] rewards their generosity with a week of free drinks. [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] distributes the potions to [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] and [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]], and Ylka promises to write a verse about them. Ted and Phyllis receive a big cheer from the tavern crowd.

## Relationships

- **[[Ted]]** -- Fellow patron at [[Froon]]'s Brews; the two are always mentioned together and jointly donate potions to [[SWOJ]].
- **[[Froon]]** -- Owner of [[Froon]]'s Brews, the tavern Phyllis frequents. He compensates Ted and Phyllis with free drinks for their generosity.
- **[[SWOJ]]** -- The adventuring party Phyllis supports. She provided them with critical eyewitness information after the fireball attack and later donated health potions for their journey.
- **[[Trollskull Alley  The Short Rest|The Short Rest]]** -- The tavern run by [[SWOJ]] on [[Trollskull Alley]], near Phyllis's flower shop. The fireball struck during its grand opening.
