# Skeemo

Skeemo Weirdbottle is a member of the [[Doom Raiders]], the moderate subfaction of the [[Zhentarim]] led by [[Davil Starsong]]. He operates the **[[Doom Network Headquarters|Doom Network]]**, the Doom Raiders' formalized job-assignment operation in [[Waterdeep]], which contracts adventurers for missions. In [[S02E05 - Episode Thirty Five (S02E05)|S02E05]], Skeemo assigns [[Scoot Sparkles|SWOJ]] the quest to find the missing noblewoman [[Anya Irmbrusk]] (the Staglass) in [[Amphail]] -- a mission that launches SWOJ's northward journey in Season 2.

## Description

Skeemo is described as an alchemist and operative within the Doom Raiders crew. When [[Davil Starsong]] pitched his 5,000 gold investment deal to SWOJ in [[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]], he highlighted Skeemo's skills alongside those of [[Ziraj The Hunter|Ziraj]]: "You have access to Ziraj and his bowmanship, Skeemo and his alchemy, myself, and my many prolific talents."

His full name -- **Skeemo Weirdbottle** -- is given by the DM in [[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]] when the Doom Raiders identify themselves to the guards at [[Cassalanter Villa]] (heard in the transcript as "Schema Weirdbottle").

## Role in the Story

### The Cassalanter Feast Infiltration ([[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]])

When SWOJ needed to infiltrate the [[Cassalanter Family  Cult Of Asmodeus|Cassalanters]]' Founders Day feast -- a front for a devil-soul harvest -- the [[Doom Raiders]] provided their cover. SWOJ disguised themselves as additional members of the band. At the gates of [[Cassalanter Villa]], each Doom Raider gave their name to the guards: "There's Dabble Starsong. There is Istrid Horn, Skeemo Weirdbottle, Tashlyn Yafira, Ziraj, and then Yagra." Skeemo was present as part of the band lineup that performed at the feast while SWOJ moved through the event.

### The Doom Network and the Staglass Mission ([[S02E05 - Episode Thirty Five (S02E05)|S02E05]])

By Season 2, three years have passed during SWOJ's time in the Shadowfell. The [[Doom Raiders]] have formalized their operations through the **[[Doom Network Headquarters|Doom Network]]**, a Waterdeep-based organization that contracts adventurers for jobs -- funded by the 5,000 gold piece investment SWOJ made in [[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]].

At the Doom Network headquarters, Skeemo assigns SWOJ their first mission back in [[Waterdeep]]: find the missing noblewoman **[[Anya Irmbrusk]]**, known as **the Staglass**, before the Right of the Staglass ceremony in [[Amphail]]. Anya had traveled to Amphail to participate in the ceremony, seeking to renounce her noble family ties, but had disappeared about a tenday earlier.

This mission sets the trajectory for much of Season 2's early arc, sending SWOJ northward along the Long Road with [[Ziraj The Hunter|Ziraj]] as their guide and [[Craw]] as a companion, deeper into the [[Zhentarim]] conflict between the [[Doom Raiders]] and [[Manshoon]]'s forces.
