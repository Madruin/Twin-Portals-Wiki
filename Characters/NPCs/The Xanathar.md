# The Xanathar

A beholder crime lord and the nominal leader of the [[Xanathar Guild]], one of the two major criminal organizations terrorizing Waterdeep throughout Season 1. The Xanathar was an extraordinarily paranoid creature who trusted no humanoid and was prone to imagining conspiracies where none existed. The only being it genuinely cared for was its pet goldfish [[Sylgar]], kept in a tank in its private sanctum deep beneath the Castle Ward. Unbeknownst to the guild and to the Xanathar itself, the beholder was secretly being controlled telepathically by the mind flayer [[Nihiloor]], who was the true power behind the organization. The Xanathar was killed by SWOJ and their allies at the [[Zadio City Music Hall]] during the climactic battle for the Vault of Dragons, with [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]'s thrown dagger delivering the killing blow.

## Description

The Xanathar was a beholder -- a floating, spherical aberration with a massive central eye, a fanged mouth, and ten eyestalks, each capable of projecting a different magical ray. It was described as "the insane beholder crime lord, and leader of the gang that uses his own name," with the guild's very title being an expression of its enormous ego.

[[Laeral Silverhand]] briefed SWOJ extensively on the Xanathar's personality: it did not relate well to humanoids, did not trust them as a matter of course, and was prone to imagining conspiracies where none existed. The only creature the Xanathar truly trusted was its pet goldfish [[Sylgar]], for which it had "an insane love." Guild members secretly replaced the goldfish whenever it died, without the beholder's knowledge, to avoid its wrath.

The title "Xanathar" was passed between successive beholders who murdered each other and assumed the role. The current Xanathar was a relatively recent replacement, a fact not widely known.

In combat, the Xanathar deployed devastating eye rays including a death ray (dealing 49 necrotic damage to [[Thaelin]] in a single blast), a paralysis ray (used on [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]), a fear ray (used on [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]), and a disintegration beam (which turned Lark to dust in the spawning chamber). It could also turn invisible, as it did after capturing SWOJ in its sanctum.

The truth behind the Xanathar's behavior was darker than mere paranoia. [[Nihiloor]], a mind flayer operating from a spawning chamber beneath the lair, was telepathically controlling the beholder's body. This was revealed when SWOJ reached the spawning chamber in [[S01E17 - Episode Seventeen|S01E17]] and witnessed the Nihiloor-controlled Xanathar floating above the green pool, firing eye rays at the party. Even in its final appearance at the [[Zadio City Music Hall]], the Xanathar appeared "glazed over" and "emotionless" -- "not its gloating self that once was" -- possibly still under residual influence despite Nihiloor having been tackled into the spawning pool episodes earlier.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E01 - Episode One|S01E01]]:** [[Jalaster]] identifies the Xanathar Guild as one of the criminal organizations terrorizing Waterdeep, alongside the [[Zhentarim]]. The gang war between these two factions provides the backdrop for the entire first season.
- **[[S01E02 - Episode Two|S01E02]]:** The Xanathar Guild's agents -- four kenku including [[Pinker]] -- attack the [[Zhentarim Warehouse]] on Candle Lane and kidnap [[Floon Blagmaar]], mistaking him for [[Renaer Neverember]]. SWOJ tracks them into the sewers, following yellow signs marked with the Xanathar's symbol (a circle with ten radiating spokes). In the deepest chamber, they encounter [[Nihiloor]] (unnamed at this point) torturing a half-orc and cradling an intellect devourer -- the first hint of the mind flayer pulling strings behind the guild.
- **[[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]:** SWOJ discovers Xanathar Guild symbols carved into the sewer walls beneath the Dock Ward. Three grell -- floating brain-like creatures deployed by the guild -- are fought and defeated. Approximately fifteen kidnapped civilians are rescued from the guild's sewer operations.
- **[[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]]:** The guild retaliates against SWOJ by ransacking [[Tagga]]'s apartment (Scoot's mother), leaving crow feathers as a calling card. [[Pinker]], who has defected from the guild, rescues Tagga before the agents can harm her.
- **[[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]:** [[Ulkoria Stonemarrow]] warns SWOJ about the connection between the Xanathar Guild, intellect devourers, and mind flayers, establishing the threat the guild's operations pose to the city.
- **[[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]:** [[Jarlaxle]] (as Zardos Zord) identifies [[Nihiloor]] by name as the guild's mind flayer, responsible for creating intellect devourers. He reveals that the [[Stone of Golorr]] was stolen from the guild's possession, triggering the escalating street violence between the Xanathar Guild and the [[Zhentarim]].
- **[[S01E12 - Episode Twelve|S01E12]]:** SWOJ traces the Stone of Golorr's chain of possession: it was held by the Xanathar Guild, stolen by [[Dalekar|Dalcar]] (a gnome spy working for [[Lord Dagult Neverember|Dagult Neverember]]), taken by a Zhentarim agent after the fireball explosion at Trollskull Alley, and brought to [[Gralhund Villa]] where [[Yalah Gralhund]] now keeps it.
- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** Intellect devourers -- Xanathar Guild creations planted as sleeper agents inside kidnapped civilians -- attack the courthouse during SWOJ's trial, bursting out of spectators including [[Harold]]. A grell crashes through the courthouse window. [[Laeral Silverhand]] crushes the grell with telekinetic power, frees SWOJ, and provides extensive intelligence about the Xanathar: its paranoid personality, the lair's location beneath the Castle Ward, the spawning pool, key personnel ([[Thorvin Twinbeard]], [[Xibrinidas|Nar'l Xibrindas]]), and the Eye of the [[Stone of Golorr]] hidden in [[Sylgar]]'s fish tank. She commissions SWOJ to infiltrate the lair.
- **[[S01E16 - Episode Sixteen|S01E16]]:** SWOJ infiltrates the Xanathar's lair with [[Remallia Haventree]] guiding them through the sewers and [[Thaelin]] (a former guild employee) navigating the interior. They encounter [[Flutterfoot Zipswiggle]] the enslaved gnome jester, pass through [[Thorvin Twinbeard|Thorvin]]'s workshop, and reach the private sanctum where [[Ott Steeltoes]] tends to [[Sylgar]]. Thaelin uses Suggestion to have Ott retrieve the Eye of the Stone from a locked chest. The Xanathar itself arrives and attacks -- firing a death ray at Thaelin (49 necrotic damage, knocking him unconscious), a paralysis ray at Ylka, and a fear ray at Lark. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]], wild shaped into a weasel, pours a healing potion to revive Thaelin, who escapes using Phantasmal Force and Disguise Self. The Xanathar turns invisible as its minions rush in to capture the remaining party, sending them to the [[Arena of Blood and Fortune]].
- **[[S01E17 - Episode Seventeen|S01E17]]:** In the Arena, the Xanathar watches invisibly from above as SWOJ is forced to fight Kuo-Toa champions. When SWOJ's escape is discovered, the Xanathar rises rapidly from its seat demanding "What is this? Stop them." [[Thaelin]], appearing puppet-controlled by [[Nihiloor]], delivers the Eye of the Stone to Xae and fires a Flame Bolt into a crystal chandelier, detonating smoke powder charges that collapse the lair. Beyond the rubble, SWOJ finds [[Flutterfoot Zipswiggle]] dead on a throne (brain eaten by Nihiloor) and reaches the spawning chamber, where the horrifying truth is revealed: [[Nihiloor]] has been telepathically controlling the Xanathar's body. The Nihiloor-controlled Xanathar floats above the green spawning pool, firing eye rays. It blasts Xae with a death ray (27 necrotic damage) and then turns its disintegration beam on [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] -- Lark crumbles to dust, his hat drifting down onto a pile of ashes as his last words are simply "For justice." [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] rages and tackles Nihiloor into the spawning pool. [[Lyrian]] the crystal unicorn teleports the surviving party to safety at [[Trollskull Manor]].
- **[[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]]:** The Xanathar attacks the [[Zadio City Music Hall]] with a force of bugbears during SWOJ's recovery of gold from the Vault of Dragons. The bugbears scream "For the Xanathar! For the guild!" in Goblin and Common. The beholder appears "glazed over" and "emotionless" -- "not its gloating self that once was" -- possibly still under residual influence from Nihiloor. A massive battle ensues: [[Aurinax]] transforms into gold dragon form and breathes fire on the Xanathar; [[Remallia Haventree]] casts wall of force and fireball; [[Renaer Neverember]] attempts to stab the Xanathar in the eye; SWOJ hammers it with attacks. The beholder fires its eye beams into the wall of force, shattering it, and pushes back into the theater. Cornered and near death, the Xanathar falls eyeball-first through the main theater doors into the lobby, and [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]'s thrown dagger delivers the killing blow -- "the cherry on top" that tips it over. The Xanathar's corpse slides into the theater lobby with the dagger sinking into its back, visible to the assembled crowd of city watch, griffin cavalry, and citizens outside. The remaining bugbears are captured. The Xanathar Guild is effectively destroyed.
- **[[S02E25 - Episode Fifty Five LIVE (S02E25)|S02E25]]:** The Xanathar appears in a dream sequence orchestrated by the Raven Queen, in which SWOJ relives a surreal assault on the Xanathar's lair alongside the [[Jolly Gents]], with [[Antediluvian Jake]] and [[Giggling Gus]] leading the charge.

## Relationships

- **[[Nihiloor]]** -- Mind flayer and the Xanathar's true puppetmaster. Nihiloor operated from a spawning chamber with a green pool that bred intellect devourers, and controlled the Xanathar's body telepathically. The beholder may not have been aware of this control. Nihiloor was tackled into the spawning pool by [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] in [[S01E17 - Episode Seventeen|S01E17]].
- **[[Sylgar]]** -- The Xanathar's beloved pet goldfish, the only creature it genuinely trusted. Kept in a tank in the beholder's private sanctum. Guild members secretly replaced the fish whenever it died, without the Xanathar's knowledge. The Eye of the [[Stone of Golorr]] was hidden in a locked chest near Sylgar's tank.
- **[[Xibrinidas|Nar'l Xibrindas]]** -- Drow advisor who rose to the upper echelons of the guild as the Xanathar's personal advisor. Secretly a [[Bregan D'Aerthe]] double agent working for [[Jarlaxle]].
- **[[Thorvin Twinbeard]]** -- Dwarf chief engineer who maintained a workshop in the lair. Secretly a [[Harpers]] informant who provided intelligence about the Eye of the Stone hidden near Sylgar's tank.
- **[[Ott Steeltoes]]** -- Dwarf fish keeper who tended to Sylgar in the private sanctum.
- **[[Flutterfoot Zipswiggle]]** -- Gnome jester enslaved by the Xanathar as an entertainer. Found dead after the lair collapse, his brain eaten by Nihiloor.
- **[[Thaelin]]** -- Former guild employee who left because "it wasn't quite what I thought I was going to be doing." Returned as a guest PC to help SWOJ infiltrate the lair, and was the first target of the Xanathar's death ray.

## Faction Affiliations

- [[Xanathar Guild]] -- Nominal leader. The guild took its name from the beholder, and its symbol (a circle with ten radiating spokes) was marked throughout the sewer tunnels. The guild's operations included kidnapping civilians, breeding intellect devourers, running gladiatorial death matches in the Arena of Blood and Fortune, and controlling Skullport.
