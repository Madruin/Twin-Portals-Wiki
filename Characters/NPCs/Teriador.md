# Teriador

**Teriador**, known as **the Rose Knight**, is a legendary hero who originally slew the [[Beast Of Erua]]. Once a half-elf knight, Teriador's story spans centuries -- from his original heroic deeds, through death and ghostly existence, soul absorption, corruption as the Blight Knight, and ultimately resurrection in a drow body as a member of [[Bregan D'Aerthe]].

**First appearance:** [[S02E01 - Episode Thirty One (S02E01)|S02E01]] (mentioned)
**First physical appearance:** [[S02E03 - Episode Thirty Three (S02E03)|S02E03]] (ghost)
**Total appearances:** 8 episodes

## Description

Teriador was originally a half-elf and a legendary knight known as the Rose Knight. He slew the Beast of Erua in ages past and was entombed in a fortress with his armor and sword. By the time SWOJ encountered him, his ghost haunted the sarcophagus while his reanimated corpse -- a podling controlled by plant magic -- walked the halls wearing his armor.

After his soul was absorbed by the [[Dynamo Of Possibility]] and merged with [[Fetch]] (a fragment of [[Manshoon  The Necromancer|Manshoon]]'s soul), Teriador's soul was captured by Manshoon's forces. He was corrupted into **the Blight Knight** in the [[Stadium Of Souls]], possessed by [[The Body Taker]] entity that shared his fractured soul. In this form he alternated between his own rage at SWOJ's abandonment and the Body Taker's destructive impulses, wielding the Sword of Explosions and firing thorns and fireballs.

SWOJ calmed him through emotional persuasion -- [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] grappled him in a heartfelt bear hug, [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] placed a rose in his armor, and [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] created a distracting illusion. Through a resurrection console, Teriador was given a random drow body.

In his new drow form, Teriador was in very rough shape -- barely conscious with two soul fragments (his own and the Body Taker's) in a brand new body. After being captured by the [[Bag Man]] and rescued by SWOJ, he chose to stay aboard [[Jarlaxle]]'s ship [[Eye Catcher]] to learn drow ways and adapt to his new form. He eventually joined [[Bregan D'Aerthe]] and was encountered fighting alongside them during the chasm eruption in Neverwinter.
