**First appearance:** [[S01E04 - Episode Four]]

## Description

Famous author and adventurer. Gives the party the deed to Trollskull Manor as payment for rescuing Floon and Renaer.

Famous author. Arrives at the very end of the episode after the hag is defeated, apologizing for being late. Sent a paper crane message earlier saying he was delayed.

Famous chronicler and author encountered at The Way Inn. Was later captured and replaced by a podling in the Shadowfell.

Famous chronicler found captive in a pod inside the Beast of Erua. Amazed to learn he was inside the Beast of Erua and insisted on recording everything.
