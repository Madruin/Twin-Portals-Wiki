# Sarith Ab'Jean

Captain of the [[Hell Razor]], one of [[Jarlaxle]]'s three ships in the [[Sea Maiden's Faire]] fleet. A swashbuckling fellow with a neatly trimmed mustache, Sarith runs a tight ship and transported [[SWOJ]] from [[Driftwood]] to [[Devil's' Rock|Devil's Rock]] across the [[Nameless Seas]].

**First appearance:** [[S02E09 - Episode Thirty Nine (S02E09)|S02E09]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

Sarith Ab'Jean is the captain of the Hell Razor, one of three vessels in Jarlaxle's Sea Maiden's Fair fleet (alongside the [[Eye Catcher]] and a third ship). He cuts a dashing figure with a clearly trimmed mustache and a swashbuckling demeanor. He maintains strict discipline aboard his vessel. When [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] attempted to pester him with book-learned sailing knowledge and stories about knot-tying, Sarith politely redirected him to the first mate, who in turn passed him along to the next crew member, creating a chain of shunting until Lark ended up chatting with the deck swabby. Despite this, the Hell Razor under Sarith's command provided a smooth and communal three-day voyage, with the ships linking up via ladders between decks for shared meals, music, and dancing.

## Role in the Story

- **S02E09:** Sarith captains the Hell Razor as SWOJ sails from Driftwood to Devil's Rock at Jarlaxle's arrangement
- **S02E09:** [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] pesters Sarith with stories about sailing he read in books; Sarith redirects him down a chain of crew members until he ends up bothering the deck swabby
- **S02E09:** The three-day voyage aboard the Hell Razor is uneventful save for sightings of spectral green [[aboleth]] swimming in the waters and humming an ominous seven-note melody
