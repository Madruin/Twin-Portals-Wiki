# Charles Lurember

Charles Lurember is the male lead actor at the [[Zadio City Music Hall]], a dramatic and self-important performer encountered by SWOJ during their infiltration of the theater to access the [[Vault Of Dragons]].

**First appearance:** [[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

Lurember is a theatrical man with a large mustache and wavy silver hair. He carries himself with exaggerated gravitas, sitting in his dressing room with his fingers steepled in a swivel chair, waiting to dramatically reveal himself to visitors. His catchphrase -- or at least a running joke about his name -- is "Remember Lurember." He occupies the dressing room next door to the lead actress [[Westra Multimer]], and the two share a professional rivalry with underlying tension.

## Role in the Story

### Season 1

- **[[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six]]:** SWOJ infiltrates the [[Zadio City Music Hall]] disguised as ushers in order to reach the sealed trap door leading to the [[Vault Of Dragons]] beneath the theater. The trap door is located under the floor of [[Westra Multimer]]'s dressing room, so the party needs to get her out. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] devises a plan to move Westra into Lurember's dressing room next door. When Ylka knocks on his door, Lurember does not answer, so she opens it herself. Inside, a swivel chair dramatically turns to reveal Lurember with his fingers steepled, who declares: "Who is entering the dressing room of the great Charles Lurember?" Ylka tells him that Westra would like to use his room, and Lurember agrees with a casual wave, saying "She's welcome to, of course." He then swivels back around, muttering "Lurember, lurember," while Ylka gives him finger guns on the way out. Later, while SWOJ is breaking through the floor of Westra's former dressing room, bickering can be heard from Lurember's room where Westra has relocated. A sound check is called, and both actors leave for the stage, allowing SWOJ to lock the dressing room door and continue their excavation in peace.

## Relationships

- **[[Westra Multimer]]** -- Fellow actor at the [[Zadio City Music Hall]]. Westra is the lead actress; Lurember is the male lead. Their relationship involves professional tension and bickering once forced to share a dressing room.
- **[[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]** -- Briefly interacted when Ylka, disguised as an usher, requested that he allow Westra to use his room.
