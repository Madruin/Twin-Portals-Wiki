# Winston

Winston is a shield guardian -- a ten-foot-tall magical construct -- who serves as the loyal companion and protector of [[Ulkoria Stonemarrow]], the dwarf magister of the [[Watchful Order Of Magisters|Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors]]. He accompanies Ulkoria wherever she goes, standing silent watch with his arms crossed.

**First appearance:** [[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

Winston is a shield guardian, a large magical construct standing approximately ten feet tall. He has the appearance of a golem -- massive, silent, and still. When accompanying [[Ulkoria Stonemarrow]] on her rounds, Winston typically stands with his arms crossed, watching passersby with an unreadable stillness. He is too large to fit through standard doorways, so he waits outside while Ulkoria conducts business indoors. Despite his imposing size, he follows Ulkoria's commands obediently, trailing after her when she calls.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]:** When SWOJ returns to [[Trollskull Manor]], they find [[Ulkoria Stonemarrow]] carving a glyph of warding onto the front door. Standing beside her is Winston, a ten-foot-tall shield guardian with his arms crossed, silently regarding the approaching party. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] notes that the golem appears to be protecting the dwarf, and indeed Winston watches the party guardedly as they approach. When Xae peeks outside to offer Winston entry, he stays put -- he is too large to fit through the manor's door. When Ulkoria finishes her business inside and teleports to the street, she calls out "Come on, Winston," and the shield guardian follows her as the two make their way down the alley and depart.

## Relationships

- [[Ulkoria Stonemarrow]] -- Master and controller; Winston accompanies her on all her duties across [[Waterdeep]]
- [[Watchful Order Of Magisters|Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors]] -- Associated through Ulkoria's membership in the Order
- [[Trollskull Manor]] -- Has visited the manor alongside Ulkoria
