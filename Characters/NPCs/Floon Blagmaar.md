# Floon Blagmaar

A red-haired man described as having "more beauty than brains," Floon Blagmaar is the friend whose kidnapping brought the [[Silver Waves of Justice]] together. His full surname is spelled B-L-A-G-M-A-A-R, though the party has never once managed to call him by his correct name -- the persistent mispronunciation "Froon" eventually became the name of his own business.

**First appearance:** [[S01E01 - Episode One|S01E01]]
**Total appearances:** 10 episodes

## Description

Floon is a companion of the famous chronicler [[Volothamp Geddarm]], who characterizes him as a man of considerable good looks but limited intellect. He was a regular at the [[Skewered Dragon]] tavern in the Dock Ward of [[Waterdeep]].

After his rescue from the [[Xanathar Guild]], Floon settled into life on [[Trollskull Alley]], eventually taking over the tavern across the street from [[Trollskull Manor]] -- the establishment formerly run by the hostile [[Emmek Frewn]]. He renamed it **Froon's Brews**, leaning into the party's habitual mispronunciation of his name. Despite gaudy renovations, Floon quickly found himself deep in debt from overbuilding the place. [[Scoot Sparkles]] invested 1,000 gold and another party member contributed 500 gold in exchange for parking privileges, and Floon was described as "incredibly gracious" and a changed man after receiving the help.

By the time SWOJ returned from their long absence during Season 2, Froon's Brews had become a functioning neighborhood fixture on Trollskull Alley. Floon was the one who confirmed hearing crunching and thumping sounds heading north on the night [[Fala Lefaliir]] was kidnapped from [[Corellon's Crown]]. When SWOJ departed Waterdeep heading north, they stopped at Froon's, where patrons Ted and Phyllis donated health potions for the journey. By the Season 2 finale, Froon's Brews was described as thriving -- a testament to SWOJ's early investment paying off.

## Relationships

- **[[Volothamp Geddarm]]** -- Close friend. Volo hired the party to rescue Floon, describing him affectionately as having "more beauty than brains."
- **[[Renaer Neverember]]** -- Friend and drinking companion. The two were out together at the [[Skewered Dragon]] when they were kidnapped. Renaer was the intended target; Floon was taken by mistake because of his resemblance to Renaer.
- **[[Silver Waves of Justice]]** -- Rescued Floon from the sewers beneath Waterdeep, then became his neighbors on Trollskull Alley and financial backers of his tavern. Floon appeared as a witness at their trial and remained a loyal friend throughout the series.
- **[[Emmek Frewn]]** -- Previous owner of the tavern Floon took over on Trollskull Alley.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E01 - Episode One|S01E01]]:** [[Volothamp Geddarm]] hires the party at the [[Yawning Portal]] to find his missing friend Floon Blagmaar, who was kidnapped after leaving the [[Skewered Dragon]] tavern in the Dock Ward.
- **[[S01E02 - Episode Two|S01E02]]:** [[Renaer Neverember]] explains the kidnapping: the [[Zhentarim]] took both Renaer and Floon from Fillet Lane, then the [[Xanathar Guild]]'s kenku attacked and took Floon, mistaking him for Renaer. The party ventures into the sewers to find him.
- **[[S01E03 - Episode Three|S01E03]]:** Recap reveals the party found a mind flayer ([[Nihiloor]]) torturing Floon in the sewers and killed an intellect devourer. Floon is found safe at the [[Splintered Stair]] at the end of the episode alongside Renaer, holding [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]].
- **[[S01E04 - Episode Four|S01E04]]:** [[Volothamp Geddarm|Volo]] gives the party the deed to [[Trollskull Manor]] as payment for rescuing Floon and Renaer. The party names themselves the [[Silver Waves of Justice]].
- **[[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]:** Floon is established as the owner of Froon's Brews, the tavern across the street from Trollskull Manor. The players debate the "Floon vs. Froon" pronunciation and joke about his cranky entrepreneurial demeanor.
- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** Floon appears at SWOJ's trial before [[Laeral Silverhand]] as a witness and supporter. He is seen in the courtroom gallery with a "big, devious grin" as if ready to capitalize on the proceedings.
- **[[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]]:** During the Season 1 finale downtime montage, Floon receives a 1,000 gold investment from SWOJ for Froon's Brews in exchange for parking privileges. He is described as sheepish and chagrined, deeply grateful to the party for saving his life, but admits he has incurred significant debt from overbuilding his establishment. Scoot throws an additional 500 gold investment through his window.
- **[[S02E05 - Episode Thirty Five (S02E05)|S02E05]]:** After SWOJ returns to Waterdeep following a three-year time displacement, Floon is still running Froon's Brews on Trollskull Alley. He confirms hearing crunching and thumping sounds heading north on the night [[Fala Lefaliir]] was kidnapped from [[Corellon's Crown]].
- **[[S02E06 - Episode Thirty Six (S02E06)|S02E06]]:** SWOJ stops at Froon's Brews before departing Waterdeep northward. Patrons Ted and Phyllis donate health potions for the journey. Floon wishes them luck on the road.
- **[[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]:** During the Season 2 finale homecoming, SWOJ visits Froon's Brews and finds it thriving. Floon serves the party food and drinks, his business now successful thanks to their early investment.
