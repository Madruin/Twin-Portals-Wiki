## Description

Volo's missing friend. Described as having 'more beauty than brains' with red hair. Full name spelled out as Blagmaar (B-L-A-G-M-A-A-R). Was kidnapped after leaving the Skewered Dragon.

Taken by the Xanathar Guild's kenku from the warehouse. The kenku mistook him for Renaer Neverember. Presumably brought to the Xanathar's lair in the sewers.

Found at the Splintered Stair at the end of the episode, holding Scoot. Rescued from the sewers between episodes 2 and 3.

Man previously rescued by the party, mentioned in context of Volo's reward.

Friend of SWOJ. Appears at the trial as a witness/supporter.

Owner of Froon's Brews in Trollskull Alley. Received a 1,000 gold investment from SWOJ in exchange for parking privileges. Was in debt from overbuilding his establishment.


## Role in the Story

- **S01E01:** Volothamp Geddarm hires the party to find his missing friend Floon Blagmaar
- **S01E02:** Renaer explains the kidnapping: Zhentarim took him and Floon from Fillet Lane, then Xanathar Guild attacked and took Floon thinking he was Renaer
- **S01E02:** Renaer arms himself with daggers to join the search for Floon
- **S01E03:** Recap: party previously ventured into sewers, found mind flayer torturing Floon, killed an intellect devourer
- **S01E03:** Party transported back to the Splintered Stair where Floon and Renaer await them
- **S01E04:** Volo gives the party the deed to Trollskull Manor as payment for rescuing Floon and Renaer
