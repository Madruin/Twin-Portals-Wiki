# Malar

Malar the Beast Lord is a deity of hunting, lycanthropy, and blood in the Forgotten Realms. He is a malevolent god whose followers -- wererats, werewolves, hags, and war-painted barbarian riders -- carry out blood sacrifices and corruption rituals in his name. In the Twin Portals campaign, Malar serves as a major antagonist during Season 1's resurrection arc when [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] must enter his afterlife realm to recover [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]'s stolen soul, and his followers resurface as hostile combatants in Season 2.

**First appearance:** [[S01E21 - Episode Twenty One|S01E21]]
**Total appearances:** 3 episodes

## Description

Malar is never encountered directly in physical form, but his presence is felt through his domain and his devoted followers. His afterlife realm -- [[Malar's Realm (Afterlife)]] -- is a wild, stormy forest landscape of deep wilds, lit by increasingly violent lightning and a reddening moon. At its heart rises a mountain topped by a volcanic caldera containing an enormous ash tree fed by four seasonal streams. The realm operates on a lunar cycle: the corruption rituals within it reach their peak when the moon is full.

His followers are marked by bestial savagery. In Season 1, his cult comprised wererats, a werewolf, and a hag who captured dryads and animals as blood sacrifices to corrupt the ash tree's sacred waters. In Season 2, his devotees appeared as fierce barbarian periton riders covered in glowing war paint, chanting discordant melodies to coordinate their aerial attacks.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E21 - Episode Twenty One|S01E21]]:** SWOJ awakens in Malar's wild, stormy afterlife realm after being sent into the afterlife by [[Manshoon|the Necromancer]] to find [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]]'s soul. [[Rowan Crooks]] warns the party that "vicious predators" stalking the forest are "disciples of Malar, Malar the Beast Lord." Ascending the mountain, they discover [[The Hag Of Trollskull Manor]] (now one-armed from her previous fight with SWOJ) serving Malar -- capturing three dryads ([[Tricayla]], [[Argentia]], and their [[Third Dryad Sister|sister]]) and caging 18 animals for sacrifice. The hag reveals the central plot: "I plucked him out of Kelemvor's fist and gave him to Malar the Beast Lord. The bait was set." She stole Lark's soul from the god of the dead and delivered it to Malar as bait to lure the unicorn [[Lyrian]] into a trap. At the ash tree summit, five of Malar's followers -- four wererats and a werewolf -- corrupt the water with blood, turning the tree red and ensnaring Lyrian in a trance. The werewolf chants Malar's name as a battle cry: "Water into blood, earth into bones!" SWOJ defeats them, purifies Lyrian, and recovers Lark's soul.
- **[[S01E22 - Episode Twenty Two|S01E22]]:** Referenced in the "previously on" recap: "Under a blood moon at the pool of eternal spring, our heroes battled the minions of the Beast Lord Malar. They were able to recover the soul of Lark and rescue their corrupted unicorn friend Lyrian."
- **[[S02E32 - Episode Sixty Two LIVE (S02E32)|S02E32]]:** Malar's followers resurface during SWOJ's three-ship assault through the thunderstorm toward the [[Concord Jewel]]. Barbarian periton riders -- fierce warriors covered in glowing war paint -- ambush the fleet, throwing weighted nets and boarding ships. [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] recognizes their painted symbols and talismans as matching "the people that you fought in the Feywild when you were dealing with Malar, the Beast Lord." The [[Hammerhead]] and its entire [[Neverwinter Forces|Neverwinter crew]] are lost in the engagement.

## Relationships

- **[[Malark Wavesilver|Malark "Lark" Wavesilver]]** -- Lark's soul was stolen from [[Kelemvor]]'s grasp by the hag and delivered to Malar as bait to trap [[Lyrian]]. Lark was held unconscious in the crook of the ash tree in Malar's realm until SWOJ rescued him. The coincidence of Malar's name and Lark's full name "Malark" is a recurring source of confusion -- Lark's father [[Grim Wavesilver|Grim]] calls him "Malar" as a nickname.
- **[[Kelemvor]]** -- The god of the dead, rightful custodian of Lark's soul after his disintegration. The hag's theft of Lark from Kelemvor's grasp represents a direct affront to Kelemvor's authority over the dead, carried out in service to Malar.
- **[[The Hag Of Trollskull Manor]]** / **[[Esther]]** -- A hag who served Malar in his afterlife realm after SWOJ first killed her at [[Trollskull Manor]]. She captured dryads and animals as sacrifices and carried out Malar's scheme to trap Lyrian using Lark's soul as bait. When asked why she serves Malar, she answered: "What is evil? He helped me after you killed me."
- **[[Lyrian]]** -- The unicorn was the true target of Malar's trap. His followers corrupted the ash tree's sacred waters with blood to lure Lyrian into a trance, feeding her blood-red ash berries to corrupt and control her. She was transformed into a dark-furred unicorn with a black horn radiating red energy before SWOJ purified her.
- **[[Cult Of Malar]]** / **[[Malar The Beast Lord's Followers]]** -- Two distinct groups of Malar's devotees: a Season 1 cell of lycanthropes and a hag operating in his afterlife realm, and Season 2 barbarian periton riders who fought as part of [[Manshoon]]'s broader alliance.
- **[[Rowan Crooks]]** -- Encountered in Malar's realm, where he and the Jolly Gents were hunting the tribesfolk killing animals. He warned SWOJ about Malar's disciples and guided them through the wilds.
