# Santorini

**Santorini** is a winged and horned tiefling or demon who frequents the [[Dark Market]] in the [[Shadowfell (Deep)|Shadowfell]], and one third of an inseparable trio alongside [[Mikael]] and [[Lucinda]]. Named after a Greek island -- a fact that amuses the players when they hear it -- Santorini shares the group's extremely sociable, laid-back demeanor as they befriend newcomers to the market. The trio hails from [[Avernus]] and has been in the [[Dark Market]] for roughly twenty days by the time [[Silver Waves of Justice|SWOJ]] arrives. The angel [[Ryloren|Rylerin]] considers them harmless but warns against getting too comfortable with them.

**First appearance:** [[S02E21 - Episode Fifty One (S02E21)]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

Santorini is roughly human-sized (comparable to [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]) with wings and horns, matching the appearance of his companions [[Mikael]] and [[Lucinda]]. Like the rest of the trio, Santorini is extremely friendly, casual, and "weirdly relaxed," eager to throw an arm around a stranger's shoulder and trade stories. The three speak Common and present as gregarious social operators who seek out new arrivals. An insight check by [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] reveals their pattern of targeting newcomers, though they are not caught stealing or engaged in anything overtly harmful. The name "Santorini" -- evoking the famous Greek island -- draws out-of-character commentary from the players, who joke about an island being named after the demon.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S02E21 - Episode Fifty One (S02E21)|S02E21]]:** Santorini, [[Mikael]], and [[Lucinda]] approach SWOJ as the party enters the [[Dark Market]] for the first time. They introduce themselves as visitors from [[Avernus]] on their second trip to the Shadowfell. The trio recommends [[The Nest]] as a place to stay, points out the angel [[Ryloren|Rylerin]]'s stall while keeping their distance (citing "slightly different factions"), and confirms that no one has ventured up to investigate the dark sun yet. They share that they have been in the market for about twenty days. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] is fascinated by the trio, while [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] runs an insight check and detects that they are deliberately singling out newcomers to get a read on them -- reminiscent of greeters or con artists scoping new marks, though nothing malicious is confirmed. [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] verifies his belongings after the encounter and finds nothing taken. The trio asks SWOJ to "put in a good word" for them at [[The Nest]] before parting ways. Later, when SWOJ mentions the trio to [[Ryloren|Rylerin]], the angel says they have been around "for a long time" and are "okay," but adds: "Just don't get too comfortable with them."

## Relationships

- **[[Mikael]]** and **[[Lucinda]]** -- Santorini's constant companions in the [[Dark Market]]. The three always appear together and function as a single social unit when greeting newcomers.
- **[[Silver Waves of Justice|SWOJ]]** -- Newcomers whom the trio befriends in the [[Dark Market]], sharing information about the bazaar and recommending [[The Nest]].
- **[[Ryloren|Rylerin]]** -- The angel trader in the [[Dark Market]]. The trio avoids him, attributing the distance to "slightly different factions." Rylerin considers them harmless but warns SWOJ not to grow too comfortable with them.
