# Sergeant Crowley

Sergeant Crowley is a [[City Watch]] sergeant who assists [[Ulkoria Stonemarrow]] in investigating the devastating fireball explosion on [[Trollskull Alley]]. He coordinates the guards on the ground while Ulkoria handles the magical and forensic analysis of the crime scene.

**First appearance:** [[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode
**Faction affiliations:** [[City Watch]]

## Description

Sergeant Crowley is a City Watch sergeant with an investigative role. He arrives at the [[Trollskull Alley]] fireball crime scene alongside [[Ulkoria Stonemarrow]], functioning as the military law-enforcement counterpart to the magister's arcane authority. He is businesslike and somewhat territorial about his jurisdiction, expressing mild exasperation when Ulkoria involves SWOJ in the investigation, calling them "even more variables into an already complicated equation."

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E11 - Episode Eleven|S01E11]]:** After a fireball explosion kills ten or more people on [[Trollskull Alley]] during the grand opening of [[Trollskull Alley The Short Rest|the Short Rest]], the [[City Watch]] arrives in force to secure the scene. Six guards cordon off the alley, and Sergeant Crowley enters alongside [[Ulkoria Stonemarrow]]. While Ulkoria takes depositions from SWOJ and analyzes the magical evidence, Crowley works the physical crime scene -- ordering guards to clean up, set up the crime scene perimeter, and begin sketching and documenting the blast area. When he sees Ulkoria speaking with SWOJ, he walks over and objects, saying "Are you introducing even more variables into an already complicated equation here?" Ulkoria dismisses his concern with "just relax, you do yours," and Crowley returns to directing the guards. Together, Crowley and Ulkoria determine that the dead gnome was fleeing from pursuers -- likely three agents in black leather -- and that neither the gnome nor his pursuers anticipated the explosion, given the high number of casualties.

## Relationships

- [[Ulkoria Stonemarrow]] -- Works alongside the magister during the fireball investigation; their dynamic is professional but occasionally tense, with Crowley handling the City Watch side and Ulkoria the arcane
- [[City Watch]] -- Serves as a sergeant coordinating guards at crime scenes
- [[Silver Waves of Justice]] -- Views the party as complicating his investigation, though Ulkoria overrides his objections
