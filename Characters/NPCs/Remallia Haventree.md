# Remallia Haventree

Remallia Haventree is a high elf wizard and the leader of the [[Harpers]] in Waterdeep, one of SWOJ's most important and enduring allies across both seasons of the campaign. First mentioned in the series premiere and personally introduced in Season 1's mid-arc, she provides the party with intelligence, shelter, combat support, and political backing from the [[Haventree Estate (Harper Safehouse)|Haventree Estate]] through to the Season 2 finale, where she presents them with their official guild charter.

**First appearance:** [[S01E01 - Episode One|S01E01]] (mentioned); [[S01E09 - Episode Nine|S01E09]] (first on-screen appearance)
**Total appearances:** 15 episodes
**Faction affiliations:** [[Harpers]]

## Description

Remallia is a high elf of noble bearing who operates from the [[Haventree Estate (Harper Safehouse)|Haventree Estate]] in Waterdeep's [[Castle Ward]], near the [[The God Catcher|God Catcher]]. The Haventree name is well known in Waterdeep as a powerful house. [[Laeral Silverhand]], the Open Lord of Waterdeep, describes Remallia as someone she "would trust with my own life," and the party eventually learns that she is not merely a Harper agent but the leader of the Harpers outright.

As a wizard, Remallia is a formidable combatant. During the climactic battle at the [[Zadio City Music Hall]], she demonstrates high-level spellcasting, conjuring a **Wall of Force** to contain [[The Xanathar|the Xanathar]] and then launching a **Fireball** that blasts both the beholder and [[Manshoon]]'s [[Zhentarim]] forces on the theater balcony, forcing Manshoon to retreat. She is also tactically sharp, personally guiding SWOJ through the Waterdeep sewers to infiltrate the [[Xanathar's Lair]] and leading a Harper strike force during the [[Kolat Towers]] assault.

Beyond her martial capabilities, Remallia is warm, gracious, and lightly humorous. She cuts the crusts off sandwiches when asked, makes dry jokes about fighting pits, and greets the party with genuine affection after long absences. She endures betrayal and imprisonment with stoic composure, and by Season 2 she has transitioned from Harper leadership into Waterdavian politics, working to bring an end to hostilities and ensure the welfare of the people of the Sword Coast.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E01 - Episode One|S01E01]]:** [[Jalaster]], a Harper agent at the [[Skewered Dragon]] tavern, tells [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] to seek out Remallia Haventree if he wants to do some good. Jalaster describes her as a senior Harper who can sometimes be found at the [[Yawning Portal]]. Lark shares this information with the rest of SWOJ.
- **[[S01E09 - Episode Nine|S01E09]]:** Remallia meets SWOJ briefly outside the [[Cassalanter Villa]] party. She tells them she will send someone to be in touch and asks them to report any progress they hear, inviting them to visit any time.
- **[[S01E15 - Episode Fifteen|S01E15]]:** [[Laeral Silverhand]] identifies Remallia as her most trusted Harper agent and takes SWOJ to the [[Haventree Estate (Harper Safehouse)|Haventree Estate]], which serves as a Harper safehouse. Laeral leaves [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] under Harper protection there and tells the party to ask for Remallia when they return.
- **[[S01E16 - Episode Sixteen|S01E16]]:** Harbors [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] at her estate while the rest of the party prepares for the [[Xanathar's Lair]] heist. Greets SWOJ on the second level of the estate and personally leads them through the Waterdeep sewers to a secret entrance into the lair, providing tactical intelligence about the Festival of Blood and Fortune and the lair's layout. Departs after guiding them in, telling the party "I'll be listening" via sending stone.
- **[[S01E17 - Episode Seventeen|S01E17]]:** [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] attempts to contact Remallia via sending stone as SWOJ is being captured by bugbears in the [[Xanathar's Lair]], calling out "Ramalia, this is Lark. We're getting taken. Help us." The sending stone is ripped from his hand before he can finish.
- **[[S01E22 - Episode Twenty Two|S01E22]]:** Meets SWOJ at the Docksward docks after their return from the [[Feywild]]. She is waiting with city watch members keeping eyes on the pier. She provides critical intelligence: the [[Zhentarim]] possess the second Eye of the [[The Stone of Golorr]], and the [[Kolat Towers]] are the Zhentarim's base of operations. Gives SWOJ a sending stone for ongoing communication.
- **[[S01E23 - Episode Twenty Three|S01E23]]:** Contacted via sending stone throughout the [[Kolat Towers]] infiltration. Leads a Harper strike force to the base of the towers to support SWOJ but gets pinned down by Zhentarim forces. Coordinates backup via sending stone: when [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] shatters the shield rune, Remallia orders reinforcements to storm the towers. Her sending stone goes silent during a tense period, worrying the party, but she is later found with her Harper forces at the tower's ground floor, commanding wizards and gray-armored special forces.
- **[[S01E24 - Episode Twenty Four|S01E24]]:** Referenced in passing; the party uses the sending stone Remallia gave them and humorously refers to it as "Ramalia's burner." [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] confuses Remallia's name with [[Amalia Cassalanter]] while charmed.
- **[[S01E25 - Episode Twenty Five|S01E25]]:** Hosts SWOJ at her estate to assemble the three Eyes of the [[The Stone of Golorr]]. Her house is described as a beautiful manner in a row of Copenhagen-style row houses with shuttered windows. She provides the protective case for the Stone and has guards in city watch uniforms stationed at the estate (undercover Harpers). When the Stone activates, Remallia and her guards [[Bricks]] and [[Bracks]] are ensorcelled -- their eyes turn "opaque and blood red" as they loom over the unconscious party. SWOJ is transported 500 years into the past for a vision, and they awaken on the floor of Remallia's house with pillows and glasses of water nearby.
- **[[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]]:** Still ensorcelled from the Stone of Golorr, Remallia and her guards are freed when SWOJ physically strikes them; Remallia makes her wisdom save and staggers backward, saying "Thank you. I don't know how long I've been asleep." She accompanies SWOJ to the [[Zadio City Music Hall]] to enter the [[Vault of Dragons]], taking their cues from the party. In the vault, she helps navigate the ancient dwarven chambers. During the climactic theater battle against [[The Xanathar|the Xanathar]], she casts **Wall of Force** to contain the beholder, then launches a **Fireball** at the Xanathar and the Zhentarim on the theater balcony, blasting [[Manshoon]]'s position and forcing him to retreat. She offers to bring more Harpers to help guard the recovered gold.
- **[[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]]:** Betrayed by [[Bricks]] and [[Bracks]] as SWOJ emerges from the theater. The two guards hold knives to her throat and drag her before the city watch, yelling "We have her, she's here. She's the one responsible. She did it." Remallia is "stunned" but "keeping silent," looking at her betrayers and at SWOJ in a state of shock. She is taken to prison separately from the party and placed under house arrest.
- **[[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]]:** Freed from house arrest after the [[Cassalanters|Cassalanter]] plot is fully exposed. Coordinates the Harper healing and cleanup effort at [[Cassalanter Villa]] alongside the unicorn [[Lyrian|Lerian]], working through midnight. A Harper agent confirms to [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] that [[Bricks]] and [[Bracks]] have been expelled: "There are no longer members of the Harpers. They were traitors from the start."
- **[[S01E30 - Episode Thirty|S01E30]]:** Referenced in the talkback panel episode, where the cast discusses how [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Kendra]] kept pursuing the Remallia Haventree story thread across many episodes before the character finally appeared in person. The DM notes she asked about Remallia "probably like 20 times" before the Harper leader showed up.
- **[[S02E14 - Episode Forty Four (S02E14)|S02E14]]:** Referenced when SWOJ arrives at the [[House of a Thousand Faces]] in Neverwinter. The party recalls that Remallia was their last Harper contact, from around episode 25 of Season 1.
- **[[S02E19 - Episode Forty Nine (S02E19)|S02E19]]:** Participates in the war council at [[Neverwinter]] alongside [[Dagult Neverember]], [[Renaer Neverember]], and [[Zardoz Zord]], helping plan strategy against the multiple threats facing the city.
- **[[S02E20 - Episode Fifty (S02E20)|S02E20]]:** Reunites with SWOJ at the Neverwinter war council. Reveals she has "stepped away from my business with the Harpers and been more involved in Waterdavian politics," working to bring an end to hostilities and ensure everyone is taken care of. She engages with the party's intelligence briefing, reacting with surprise when they mention a possible clone of [[Manshoon]] and asking pointed questions of [[Dagult Neverember]]. She understands that SWOJ has significant influence with dragons and looks to them for help.
- **[[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]]:** Represents the [[Harpers]] at the Masked Lords' council in the Season 2 finale. Presents SWOJ with their official Letters of Charter for the Guild of the [[Silver Waves of Justice]], granting recruitment rights, petitioning privileges, safe passage, limited judicial immunity, and franchising rights across the Sword Coast Alliance. When asked what she hopes they will do with the charter, she tells them: "Based on your actions over the years, we trust that you will use this to fight evil and promote justice wherever you go." Attends the God's Night celebration at [[Trollskull Manor]].

## Relationships

- [[Laeral Silverhand]] -- Trusted by the Open Lord of Waterdeep with her life. Laeral describes Remallia as her most trusted Harper agent and entrusts SWOJ's safety to her.
- [[Scoot Sparkles]] -- Ally and friend. Scoot alerts the Harpers about the betrayal of Bricks and Bracks to help free Remallia from house arrest.
- [[Malark Wavesilver]] -- Ally. Lark is the primary point of contact via sending stone throughout Season 1 and often addresses Remallia directly in communications.
- [[Xaerine Rumblestride]] -- Harbored Xae at her estate while SWOJ prepared for the Xanathar lair heist. Kendra (Xae's player) was the one who kept asking about Remallia across many episodes before they met.
- [[Ylka Gralhund]] -- Ally. Ylka coordinates with Remallia during the Kolat Towers assault and her shattering of the shield rune allows Remallia's reinforcements to storm in.
- [[Bricks]] and [[Bracks]] -- Her personal guards in gray armor and long cloaks. They were ensorcelled alongside her by the Stone of Golorr and later betrayed her to the city watch, holding knives to her throat. Expelled from the Harpers as traitors who were likely working for the [[Cassalanters]] from the start.
- [[Jalaster]] -- Fellow Harper agent who first directed SWOJ to seek out Remallia in Episode One, starting the quest line that would define one of the party's most important alliances.
- [[Renaer Neverember]] -- Political ally. They sit together at the Neverwinter war council and both contribute to SWOJ's guild charter in the Season 2 finale.
- [[Jarlaxle Baenre]] -- Not antagonistic. Both attend the Season 2 finale council and contribute to SWOJ's charter.
- [[Manshoon]] -- Enemy. Remallia blasts his position with a Fireball during the theater battle, forcing his retreat.
- [[The Xanathar]] -- Enemy. Remallia casts Wall of Force to contain the beholder during the climactic theater battle.

## Key Items

- **Sending Stone** -- Given to SWOJ in [[S01E22 - Episode Twenty Two|S01E22]]. Used extensively through Episodes 22-26 for real-time coordination during missions. Humorously referred to as "Ramalia's burner" by the party.
- **Letters of Charter** -- Presented to SWOJ in [[S02E38 - Episode Sixty Eight - Season Two Finale (S02E38)|S02E38]], officially recognizing the Guild of the [[Silver Waves of Justice]].
