# Hammond Kraddoc

**Hammond Kraddoc** is a representative of the [[Vintners', Distillers', and Brewers' Guild]] in [[Waterdeep]]. He visits [[Trollskull Manor]] to discuss tavern licensing with [[SWOJ]], bringing along a young scribe named Ginny. He shares market intelligence about trending spirits in the city.

**First appearance:** [[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode
**Faction affiliations:** [[Vintners', Distillers', and Brewers' Guild]]

## Description

Hammond Kraddoc is a guild representative who handles beverage licensing and trade matters for taverns in [[Waterdeep]]. He is accompanied by a young female tattooed tiefling scribe named Ginny, who takes careful notes on everything he says. He is knowledgeable about the spirits trade and current market trends in the city.

## Role in the Story

### Tavern Licensing at Trollskull Manor (S01E07)

- Hammond visits [[Trollskull Manor]] as one of roughly ten guild representatives who come to discuss the requirements for reopening the establishment as a tavern ([[S01E07 - Episode Seven|S01E07]])
- He represents the [[Vintners', Distillers', and Brewers' Guild]] (abbreviated by the players as "the VDB"), the guild overseeing the production and sale of alcoholic beverages in [[Waterdeep]]
- He arrives accompanied by his scribe Ginny, a young female tattooed tiefling who records everything he says
- He discusses current beverage trends with [[SWOJ]], noting that Stoutheart Wine has become wildly popular across [[Waterdeep]] ever since [[Zardoz Zord]] started drinking it, and that it is beginning to out-compete the Zubers' main brewery
- The players take note of his name along with the other visiting guild reps, [[Broxley Fairkettle]] and [[Justin Rask]]
