# Bricks And Bracks

Two guards in gray armor and long cloaks who served as [[Remallia Haventree|Remalia Haventree]]'s personal security detail and [[Harpers|Harper]] allies. Initially appearing loyal, they were ultimately revealed as traitors working for the [[Cassalanters]]. They were expelled from the Harpers and are on the run following the Cassalanter downfall.

**First appearance:** [[S01E25 - Episode Twenty Five|S01E25]]
**Last appearance:** [[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]] (mentioned)
**Total appearances:** 4 episodes (S01E25, S01E26, S01E27, S01E29)
**Status:** On the run
**Faction affiliations:** [[Harpers]] (expelled), [[City Watch]], [[Cassalanters]] (secret)

## Description

Bricks and Bracks are a pair of guards who wear gray armor and long cloaks. They serve as [[Remallia Haventree|Remalia Haventree]]'s personal security, stationed at her estate in Waterdeep's Castle Ward. They were considered Harper allies and came from what the Harpers described as "reputable households" with clean backgrounds. Despite this, they were secretly working for the [[Cassalanters]] -- whether through ensorcellement, blackmail, or willing collaboration from the start.

## Role in the Story

### Ensorcelled by the Stone of Golorr ([[S01E25 - Episode Twenty Five|S01E25]])

When SWOJ assembled the three pieces of the [[Stone of Golorr]] at [[Remallia Haventree|Remalia]]'s house, the Stone activated and ensorcelled Remalia along with Bricks and Bracks. Their eyes turned "opaque and blood red" as the Stone took hold. The ensorcellement rendered them catatonic while SWOJ's consciousness was transported 500 years into the past to 992 DR Waterdeep.

### The Vault of Dragons and Theater Battle ([[S01E26 - Episode Twenty Six|S01E26]])

After SWOJ awoke from the Stone's vision, they broke the ensorcellement on Remalia, Bricks, and Bracks by physically hitting them. Bracks was being choked by Bricks while ensorcelled; SWOJ struck Bricks to free both of them. Once freed, Bricks and Bracks accompanied SWOJ, Remalia, and [[Mr. E]] to the [[Zadio City Music Hall]], where the party infiltrated the theater disguised as ushers to access the [[Vault of Dragons]] beneath it.

Inside the vault, Bricks and Bracks followed SWOJ's lead through the tunnels, navigating past an enchanted tapestry and black oozes. Bracks was nearly lost to a black ooze that latched onto his foot as the party crossed a bridge, but SWOJ managed to pull him free. When [[Aurinax]] questioned the group, he asked whether Bricks and Bracks were members of SWOJ, to which the party clarified they were allies but not formal members.

SWOJ left Bricks and Bracks to guard the remaining gold in the vault while the party went to find [[Zelifarn|Zellophane]]. When the [[Xanathar]] attacked with bugbears, a massive battle erupted in the theater. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] cast darkness over the group, plunging Bricks, Bracks, [[Mr. E]], [[Aurinax]], and several bugbears into magical blackness. During the fight, the Xanathar hit Bricks with a green beam that gave him the ability to fly. Despite fighting blind in the darkness, Bricks and Bracks held their own -- Bricks scored a critical hit on a bugbear and finished one off. The players jokingly referred to them as "red shirts."

### Betrayal at the Theater ([[S01E27 - Episode Twenty Seven|S01E27]])

As SWOJ emerged from the theater after defeating the Xanathar, they found a city watch army led by [[Victoro Cassalanter]]. Bricks and Bracks revealed their true allegiance, holding knives to [[Remallia Haventree|Remalia]]'s throat and dragging her before the city watch. Bracks was heard yelling: "We have her, she's here. She's the one responsible. She did it." Remalia was "stunned" but "keeping silent," looking at her betrayers in shock.

Their betrayal directly led to SWOJ's arrest and imprisonment in a high-security anti-magic dungeon beneath the [[Castle Ward Dungeon|Castle Ward]] for approximately one week. The episode summary describes them as having been "ensorcelled again by the Cassalanters," though whether they were genuinely mind-controlled or had been willing agents all along remained ambiguous.

### Aftermath and Expulsion ([[S01E29 - Episode Twenty Nine|S01E29]])

In the Season 1 finale, after the Cassalanter plot was fully exposed, [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] sought out the Harpers to report the betrayal. He found a half-orc Harper agent among those cordoning off the [[Cassalanter Villa]] and informed them about Bricks and Bracks. The agent confirmed the Harpers already knew:

> "There are no longer members of the Harpers. They were traitors from the start."

When Scoot pressed on how the Harpers' trust-based vetting failed, the agent explained that Bricks and Bracks' backgrounds had checked out: "Both come from reputable households. They had no reason to betray Ramali as far as we know." Scoot pointedly questioned the Harpers' reliance on trust alone: "It just seems like there should have been something other than trust. Like maybe some background checks or something."

By this point, Bricks and Bracks were described as being "on the lamb" -- fugitives who had fled after the Cassalanter downfall and were not seen again.

## Relationships

- [[Remallia Haventree|Remalia Haventree]] -- Their employer and the person they ultimately betrayed. They served as her personal guards and were trusted enough to accompany her on critical missions, making their treachery all the more devastating.
- [[Harpers]] -- They were allied with the organization and had passed background checks. Their betrayal exposed a vulnerability in the Harpers' trust-based vetting process.
- [[Cassalanters]] -- Their true masters. Whether through ensorcellement, blackmail, or willing collaboration, Bricks and Bracks served the Cassalanter interests by delivering Remalia to the city watch and helping frame SWOJ.
- [[Silver Waves of Justice]] -- SWOJ fought alongside Bricks and Bracks in the theater battle before being betrayed by them moments later.
