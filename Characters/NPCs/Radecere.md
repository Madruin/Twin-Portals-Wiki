# Radecere

Rock gnome inventor at The Way Inn. Creator of the Dynamo of Possibility, a device that imbues the suggestion of a soul onto inanimate objects.

**First appearance:** [[S02E01 - Episode Thirty One (S02E01)|S02E01]]
**Total appearances:** 3 episodes

## Role in the Story




- **S02E01:** The party meets Radecere at [[The Way Inn]] alongside Volo, Ly Angestunne, Tyjit Skesh, and bartender Dorvina
- **S02E03:** Radecere reveals his stolen Dynamo of Possibility is what animates the beast and creates podlings
- **S02E05:** Radecere examines the Dynamo and casts Geas on Rowan to safeguard Fetch
