# Navi

Navi is a young dwarf girl and the eldest daughter of [[Gorge Baileybrew]], living at the [[Bailey Brew Family|Bailey Brew]] homestead near [[Zedford Falls]] in [[Prehistoric Waterdeep]]. She and her family were encountered by [[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]] during a time-travel vision to the Winter Solstice in prehistoric times.

**First appearance:** [[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

Navi is a dwarf child, the older of [[Gorge Baileybrew]]'s two daughters. She is warm and trusting, bonding quickly with the party when they arrive at the Bailey Brew homestead. Her father describes her as "my eldest" when introducing her to SWOJ. She possesses a magical fleece made for her by Gorge -- a garment that radiates deep spiritual comfort and warmth -- which she generously gives to the adventurers to aid them in their quest. She explains that she calls it "the Fleece of Navi-dad" (her father made it for her), a name the party adopts with amusement.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]:** When SWOJ is transported to [[Prehistoric Waterdeep]] during the Winter Solstice by the [[Stone of Galore]], they meet [[Gorge Baileybrew]] at the Bailey Brew homestead. Gorge introduces Navi as his eldest daughter and explains that his younger daughter [[Zuzu]] has been taken to [[Zaka's Tomey Tower|Zaka's Tome Tower]] by the vampire wizard [[Hans Von Gruber]]. Gorge asks SWOJ to help rescue Zuzu while he stays to watch Navi.
- **[[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]:** Navi gives the party her fleece -- a magical garment her father made for her -- which provides a deep sense of gladness and warmth. She names it "the Fleece of Navi-dad." The fleece is used throughout the adventure: [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] wears it for comfort after being wounded, it is placed on a [[Ghosts Of Solstice Past|Ghost of Solstice Past]] (causing the snow dwarf to steam), and it is ultimately draped over [[Hans Von Gruber]] after his defeat as an act of kindness that helps break his hold over the [[Bailey Brew Family]].
- **[[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]:** Navi accompanies the party alongside Gorge as they travel toward [[Zaka's Tomey Tower|Zaka's Tome Tower]]. Along the way she cuddles close to [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] while listening to faint sleigh bells, saying "this is amazing" and expressing hope that they find [[Zuzu]]. When the [[Ghosts Of Solstice Past]] appear, Gorge instinctively shields Navi.
- **[[S01E14 - Episode Fourteen|S01E14]]:** After Von Gruber's defeat and Zuzu's rescue, Navi joins her family -- Gorge, Zuzu, and their ancestor [[Zaka]] -- on a magical sleigh to deliver toys across the region with SWOJ's help, capping the holiday adventure.

## Relationships

- **[[Gorge Baileybrew]]** -- Father. Gorge is fiercely protective of Navi, shielding her when threats appear. He made her the magical fleece she treasures.
- **[[Zuzu]]** -- Younger sister. Navi hopes throughout the adventure that SWOJ will rescue Zuzu from [[Hans Von Gruber]].
- **[[Bailey Brew Family]]** -- Member of the Bailey Brew clan of [[Prehistoric Waterdeep]]. A descendant named Zuzu Bailey Brew is later referenced in [[S01E25 - Episode Twenty Five|S01E25]] as the keeper of the [[Stone of Galore]] heirloom.
- **[[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]]** -- Navi bonds with Ylka during the journey, cuddling close to her while listening to sleigh bells.
- **[[Silver Waves Of Justice|SWOJ]]** -- The adventuring party who helped rescue her sister and defeat [[Hans Von Gruber]]. Navi's generous gift of the fleece proved instrumental in their victory.
