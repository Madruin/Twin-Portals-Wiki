# Esther

A spectral hag who haunted [[Trollskull Manor]], the first supernatural threat [[SWOJ]] faced in their new home. According to local legend -- and confirmed by [[Lif Lindgren]]'s diary -- Esther had run an orphanage in the building and preyed on the children in her care. Her dark power persisted as a ghostly curse anchored by a talisman hidden under the manor's tower.

**First appearance:** [[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode
**Status:** Destroyed

## Description

Esther was a hag who manifested as a spectral figure in [[Trollskull Manor]]'s upper levels. She commanded seven spectral children servants -- the ghosts of the orphans she had consumed in life. Her power was anchored by a shriveled green hand clutching a heart, a talisman buried under the floorboards of the manor's tower.

## Role in the Story

### [[S01E05 - Episode Five|S01E05]]

When the [[Trollskull Alley Irregulars|street urchins]] -- [[Nat]], [[Jenks]], and [[Squidly]] -- accidentally unearthed the glowing talisman from under the tower's floorboards, the manor was pulled into a demi-plane and Esther manifested with her seven spectral children servants. She attempted to cook [[Squidly]] in a cauldron. [[SWOJ]] fought her in a chaotic battle. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] threw the talisman into the boiling cauldron, which stripped away the hag's ghostly form and made her physical. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] delivered the killing blow in giant wolf spider form, and the hag was destroyed. The spectral children were freed, the demi-plane collapsed, and [[Lif Lindgren|Lif]] appeared one final time with a smile before fading -- his warnings heeded at last.

## Background

[[Lif Lindgren]]'s diary, discovered in a hidden workshop in [[Trollskull Manor]]'s cellar, revealed that Lif had uncovered the truth about Esther during his time working as a bartender at the manor approximately 95 years before [[SWOJ]]'s arrival. The legend of the child-eating hag was real, and her curse had persisted long after her original death, trapping Lif's ghost as well.

## Relationships

- **[[Lif Lindgren]]** -- Lif's diary contained the history of Esther, and his ghostly warnings ("she is coming," "please save the children") helped SWOJ prepare for the confrontation.
- **[[Trollskull Manor]]** -- The building Esther haunted, bound by her talisman.
- **[[Nat]], [[Jenks]], [[Squidly]]** -- The street urchins whose accidental discovery of the talisman triggered Esther's manifestation. Squidly was nearly cooked in her cauldron.
