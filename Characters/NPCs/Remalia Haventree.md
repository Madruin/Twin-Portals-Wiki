## Description

Harper ally, high elf wizard. Hosts SWOJ at her house while they assemble the Stone of Golorr. Gets ensorcelled by the Stone along with her guards.

Harper ally. Ensorcelled by the Stone of Golorr, freed by being hit. Casts wall of force and fireball during the theater battle against the Xanathar.

Betrayed by Bricks and Bracks to the city watch after the theater battle. Her fate during SWOJ's imprisonment is unclear.

High elf Harper ally. Released from house arrest after the Cassalanter plot was exposed. Helped coordinate the Harper effort at the villa.

Discussed during the talkback as a character Kendra kept asking about throughout the campaign. Harper ally whose arc provided important story momentum.
