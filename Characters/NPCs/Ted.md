# Ted

Ted is a patron at [[Froon]]'s Brews on [[Trollskull Alley]] in [[Waterdeep]]. Along with [[Phyllis]], he generously donates health potions to [[SWOJ]] as they prepare to depart the city heading north.

**First appearance:** [[S02E06 - Episode Thirty Six (S02E06)|S02E06]]
**Total appearances:** 1 episode

## Description

Ted is a regular at [[Froon]]'s Brews, the tavern on [[Trollskull Alley]] run by [[Froon]]. He and [[Phyllis]] are familiar enough with [[SWOJ]] to offer them a parting gift when the adventurers set out on their journey north along [[The Long Road]]. The potions they donate are valued at 150 gold each, and [[Froon]] compensates them by covering their drinks for a week. Ted and Phyllis receive a big cheer from the other patrons at the bar, and [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] promises to write a verse about them in SWOJ's next ballad, leaving them overcome with excitement.

## Role in the Story

- **[[S02E06 - Episode Thirty Six (S02E06)|S02E06]]:** Ted and [[Phyllis]] donate two health potions to [[SWOJ]] at [[Froon]]'s Brews before the party departs [[Waterdeep]] heading north with [[Ziraj The Hunter|Ziraj]]. [[Froon]] offers them free drinks for a week in return. [[Scoot Sparkles|Scoot]] distributes the potions to [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] and [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]], and Ylka promises to immortalize Ted and Phyllis in song.

## Relationships

- **[[Phyllis]]** -- Fellow patron at [[Froon]]'s Brews; the two are always mentioned together and jointly donate potions to SWOJ.
- **[[Froon]]** -- Owner of the tavern Ted frequents; compensates Ted and Phyllis for their generosity with a week of free drinks.
- **[[SWOJ]]** -- The adventuring party Ted supports by donating health potions for their journey.
