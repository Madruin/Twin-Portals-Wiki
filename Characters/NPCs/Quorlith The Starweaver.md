# Quorlith The Starweaver

A giant spider inhabiting the throne room of Castle Never. Eyes the size of beach balls, dozens of them. She has thousands of baby spiders and weaves constellations and star maps into her webs on the ceiling. Speaks via Xae's Speak with Animals spell. Accepts Lark's constellation watch as a gift and smashes the throne to reveal the hidden map and key. She has been living in the throne room for at least 30 years.
