# Quorlith The Starweaver

Quorlith the Starweaver is a massive giant spider who inhabits [[Castle Never]]. Originally found living in the [[Throne Room]] among thousands of baby spiders, she became a key ally of [[SWOJ]] and a surprisingly talented engineer who helped build [[Neverwinter]]'s airship fleet.

**First appearance:** [[S02E18 - Episode Forty Eight LIVE (S02E18)|S02E18]]
**Total appearances:** 3 episodes

## Description

Quorlith is an enormous giant spider with teal and peacock-colored coloring. She has dozens of eyes, each the size of a beach ball, and is accompanied by thousands of baby spiders that act almost as extensions of her will -- carrying objects and swarming in coordinated waves. She weaves intricate constellations and star maps into her webs on the ceiling of whatever space she occupies, demonstrating an intelligence and artistry far beyond that of a normal beast.

Quorlith communicates through clicking sounds and can be understood via the Speak with Animals spell (used by [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]]). She also appears to understand Common, though she prefers to speak in her own way. She values offerings and gifts, particularly those connected to the stars and constellations she loves to weave.

Her spider silk is stronger than steel cable, a property that proved invaluable in shipbuilding and emergency repairs.

## Role in the Story

### The Crown of Neverwinter (S02E18)

When [[SWOJ]] entered the [[Throne Room]] of [[Castle Never]] searching for the [[Crown of Neverwinter]], they encountered Quorlith living among her brood. She had been inhabiting the throne room for at least 30 years -- since the [[Neverember]] family took control of the city. [[Xaerine Rumblestride|Xae]] communicated with her via Speak with Animals, and Quorlith explained that a set of silver scales in the room bore the inscription "What must a ruler balance?" with the heavy side labeled "Power." She told them the scales had been out of balance for 30 years since the Neverembers took over.

Quorlith demanded an offering before she would help. After rejecting several attempts (including rations and the shadow inkwell from the [[Beholder Antechamber|beholder's hoard]]), [[Malark Wavesilver|Lark]] offered his constellation watch -- a gift that delighted the star-obsessed spider. In return, Quorlith smashed the throne itself, revealing a hidden compartment containing a key and a map to the crypts below where the Crown was hidden.

### Building the Fleet (S02E30)

By the time SWOJ returned to [[Neverwinter]] after their adventures in [[Amphail]], Quorlith had descended from the throne room to the docks and offered her services to [[Renaer Neverember]]. Using her silk -- stronger than steel cable -- and her surprising engineering talent, she was instrumental in helping Neverwinter construct three airships: the [[Ethereal Galleon]], the [[Hammerhead]], and the [[Clockwork Kraken]]. The ships were built in large part from her webs, which allowed construction to bypass many regular safety measures. Renaer credited her as responsible for much of the work, calling her a "surprisingly talented engineer."

### The Egg Theft (S02E31)

During the attack on [[Castle Never]] by agents of the [[Zentarim]], a man in black set fire to the [[Ethereal Galleon]] at the docks and stole Quorlith's eggs. [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] discovered Quorlith in distress at the [[Neverwinter Docks|cliff-side docks]], and SWOJ pursued the thief -- an agent named [[Krom]] -- through tunnels into [[Quorlith's Cave]]. The party cleared a cave-in so Quorlith could follow, and [[Ylka Gralhund|Ylka]] polymorphed the thief into a cicada before he could harm the eggs. Quorlith scooped up her brood safely.

After her eggs were recovered, Quorlith helped patch the damaged [[Ethereal Galleon]] with her spider silk, making the ship even more maneuverable than before. She then remained with her main brood at the docks as SWOJ departed with the fleet toward the [[Sword Mountains]].
