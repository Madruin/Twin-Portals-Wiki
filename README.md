# Twin Portals Wiki

Full episode transcripts for the **Twin Portals** podcast - a fifth edition Dungeons & Dragons actual play show from Duluth, Minnesota.

## About Twin Portals

Twin Portals is a D&D 5E actual play podcast created by **Scott Hebert** and **Matias Valero**. DM Matias leads a group of stage performers, improv artists, and musicians through adventures in Faerun and beyond.
